# <font style='color:green'>Challenge Evil</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_paladin_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one evil creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You challenge an evil creature to bring the fight to you, or suffer the consequences. You gain a +2 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ bonus on all melee attacks against the subject of the spell. At the end of its turn, if the target has not made at least one attack on you, it becomes _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_. If you move away from the target, the spell ends.

 #### Other
**Source:** APG
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
