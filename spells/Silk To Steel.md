# <font style='color:red'>Silk To Steel</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _witch_ 2, _bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _occultist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one scarf
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You imbue an ordinary _[[items/Mundane/Scarf|scarf]]_ (or similar piece of clothing) with the strength of steel without altering its weight or flexibility.
  Each round on your turn, you can decide to use the _scarf_ to defend yourself (gaining a +2 _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_ bonus to your Armor Class) or to attack as if the _scarf_ were a _[[items/Weapon/Whip|whip]]_.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Magic
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
