# <font style='color:blue'>Feeblemind</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _witch_ 5, _psychic_ 5, _mesmerist_ 5, _spiritualist_ 5, _medium_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a handful of clay, crystal, or glass spheres) {a handful of clay, crystal, or glass spheres}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; see text; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
Target creature's Intelligence and Charisma scores each drop to 1. The affected creature is unable to use Intelligence- or Charismabased skills, cast spells, understand language, or communicate coherently. Still, it knows who its friends are and can follow them and even protect them. The subject remains in this state until a _[[spells/Heal|heal]]_, _[[spells/Limited Wish|limited wish]]_, _[[spells/Miracle|miracle]]_, or _[[spells/Wish|wish]]_ spell is used to cancel the of the _[[spells/Feeblemind|feeblemind]]_. A creature that can cast arcane spells, such as a _[[classes/Sorcerer|sorcerer]]_ or a _[[classes/Wizard|wizard]]_, takes a -4 penalty on its saving throw.

 #### Other
**Source:** PFRPG Core
**Rating:** 4
**Bloodline:** Aberrant (Sorcerer) (11), Accursed (11);
