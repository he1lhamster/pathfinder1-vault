# <font style='color:green'>Fire of Entanglement</font> 
**School:** evocation 
_paladin_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 swift action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** special; see text
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Reflex partial; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
The next creature you attack using your smite evil class ability is wreathed in flames that impede its movement.
  Until the end of the spell's duration, the target is _[[conditions/Entangled|entangled]]_.
  If the target starts its turn in a square adjacent to you, it is considered to be _entangled_ to an immobile object (you) and cannot move. A target that saves against this spell is affected for only 1 round.

**Source:** APG
