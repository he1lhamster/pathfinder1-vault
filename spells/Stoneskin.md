 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 10 min./level or until discharged
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
The warded creature gains _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ to blows, cuts, stabs, and slashes. The subject gains DR 10/adamantine. It ignores the first 10 points of damage each time it takes damage from a weapon, though an adamantine weapon bypasses the reduction. Once the spell has prevented a total of 10 points of damage per caster level (maximum 150 points), it is discharged.
