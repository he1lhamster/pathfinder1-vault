# <font style='color:blue'>Burst Of Radiance</font> 
**School:** evocation [good, light] 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M/DF (a piece of flint and a pinch of silver dust) {a piece of flint and a pinch of silver dust}
 #### Effects
**Area:** 10-ft.-radius burst
**Range:** long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Reflex partial; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell fills the area with a brilliant flash of shimmering light. Creatures in the area are _[[conditions/Blinded|blinded]]_ for 1d4 rounds, or _[[conditions/Dazzled|dazzled]]_ for 1d4 rounds if they succeed at a Reflex save. Evil creatures in the area of the burst take 1d4 points of damage per caster level (max 5d4), whether they succeed at the Reflex save or not.

 #### Other
**Source:** Champions Of Purity
**Rating:** 4
**Potion:** Yes
