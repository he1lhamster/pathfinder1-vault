# <font style='color:red'>Imbue With Addiction</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 3, _alchemist_ 2, _investigator_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a dose of an addictive drug or substance) {a dose of an addictive drug or substance}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one living creature
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
The target immediately becomes addicted to the drug used as the material component during the casting of the spell. If the target was ever addicted to the drug at any point in the past, it takes a -4 penalty on its saving throw.

 #### Other
**Source:** Black Markets
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
