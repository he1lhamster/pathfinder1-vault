# <font style='color:orange'>Mind Swap</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 6, _wizard_ 6, _sorcerer_ 6, _witch_ 6, _summoner_ 5, _psychic_ 5, _mesmerist_ 5, _occultist_ 4, _spiritualist_ 4, _medium_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 hour/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell is similar to _[[spells/Possession|possession]]_, except you switch minds with the target, so instead of the target's soul sharing its body with yours and being unable to act, the target's mind moves to your body and can control it as if you each had cast _possession_ on the other.
