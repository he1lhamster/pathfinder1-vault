# <font style='color:green'>Entice Fey</font> 
**School:** conjuration (calling) 
_bard_ 6, _skald_ 6, _shaman_ 7, _druid_ 7, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 10 minutes
 **Components:** V, S, M (offerings worth 1,250 gp plus payment), DF {offerings worth 1,250 gp plus payment}
 #### Effects
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Effect:** one or two called fey, totaling no more than 12 Hit Dice, which can't appear more than 30 ft. apart
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as lesser _[[spells/Entice Fey|entice fey]]_, except that the spell's whimsical calling can produce a single fey of 12 Hit Dice or less, or two fey of the same kind whose Hit Dice total no more than 12.
