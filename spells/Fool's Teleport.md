# <font style='color:green'>Fool's Teleport</font> 
**School:** illusion (glamer) 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you and other touched willing creatures
**Range:** personal and touch
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); see text; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell instantly renders you _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Unseen|unseen]]_ as per _[[spells/Invisibility|invisibility]]_, but creates a visual and auditory display that makes it appear as if you had teleported away. You can affect one additional _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ or smaller creature or its equivalent per 3 caster levels (a Large creature counts as two _Medium_ creatures, a Huge creature counts as four _Medium_ creatures, and so forth). All creatures to be affected must be in contact with one another, and at least one of those creatures must be in contact with you. If a viewer identifies the spell with a successful Spellcraft check, she can attempt an immediate Will saving throw as if interacting with an illusion. With a successful Will save, the observer can correctly _[[spells/Identify|identify]]_ _[[spells/Fool's Teleport|fool's teleport]]_ for what it is; otherwise, she perceives the spell to be _[[spells/Dimension Door|dimension door]]_, teleport, or similar conjuration magic. A successful Will save doesn't negate the _invisibility_.

 #### Other
**Source:** Occult Origins
**Rating:** 3
