# <font style='color:orange'>Handy Grapnel</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _hunter_ 1, _ranger_ 1, _inquisitor_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (an arrowhead) {an arrowhead}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one ropelike object, length up to 50 ft. + 5 ft./level
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates (object); **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You cause the target rope to shrink and reshape itself into an arrow, bolt, or similar piece of ammunition, which you can shoot at any _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ or larger object. With a successful attack roll against an AC equal to 5 plus the hardness of the target object, the _[[spells/Handy Grapnel|handy grapnel]]_ strikes and embeds itself in that object with the strength of an iron _[[items/Weapon/Grappling hook|grappling hook]]_. As a move action, you can _[[spells/Command|command]]_ the rope to extend from the arrow. If you are within a distance equal to the length of the rope and have a hand free, the end of the rope swings directly into your hand. With another move action, you can _command_ the _handy grapnel_ to retract itself up to the _grappling hook_, pulling up any creature or object supported by the rope at a speed of 50 feet per round. Once embedded, the rope can be used for climbing or any other purpose a rope could serve, and it can bear up to 200 pounds per level of the caster at a time (maximum 1,000 pounds). If this weight limit is exceeded, the _handy grapnel_ comes loose and any creature or object supported by the rope falls.

 #### Other
