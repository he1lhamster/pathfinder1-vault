# <font style='color:orange'>Nauseating Dart</font> 
**School:** conjuration (creation) [poison] 
_witch_ 1, _druid_ 1, _hunter_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Effect:** one poisonous stinger
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude partial (see text); **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You spit a poisonous stinger around the size of a quill at a creature within range, as though you had fired a _[[items/Weapon/Dart|dart]]_ from a _[[items/Weapon/Blowgun|blowgun]]_. You must succeed at a ranged attack to hit your target. The stinger deals 1d2 points of piercing damage and poisons the target, causing it to become _[[conditions/Nauseated|nauseated]]_ for 1 round unless it succeeds at a Fortitude save.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Class Guide
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
