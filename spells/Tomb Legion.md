# <font style='color:red'>Tomb Legion</font> 
**School:** necromancy [evil] 
_arcanist_ 8, _wizard_ 8, _sorcerer_ 8, _cleric_ 8, _oracle_ 8, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 7 days or 7 months ; see text (D)
**Effect:** Three or more advanced mummies, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart; see text
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions like _[[spells/Shambler|shambler]]_, except that it calls into existence 1d4+2 advanced mummies rather than shambling mounds.
  Though the spell has _[[monsters/Fallen|fallen]]_ out of favor among the _[[npcs/Risen Guard|Risen Guard]]_ because the Ruby Prince frowns on the use of undead, this spell is popular among followers of the Whispering Way.

 #### Other
**Source:** Faction Guide
