# <font style='color:green'>Thought Shield IV</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_psychic_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 immediate action
 **Components:** V 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 round

 #### Description
This functions as _[[spells/Thought _[[spells/Shield|Shield]]_ III|thought _shield_ III]]_, but any creature that tries to read your thoughts while this spell lasts must succeed at a Will save or be _[[conditions/Stunned|stunned]]_ for 1d4 rounds. Additionally, if you succeed at your save against a _[[classes/Psychic|psychic]]_ crush spell, you take no damage. This spell can be undercast.

 #### Other
**Source:** Occult Adventures
**Rating:** 3
