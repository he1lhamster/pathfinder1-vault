# <font style='color:green'>Dancing Darkness</font> 
**School:** evocation [darkness, shadow] 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _witch_ 1, _magus_ 1, _bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, _shaman_ 1, _antipaladin_ 1, _psychic_ 1, _spiritualist_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)
**Effect:** Up to four spheres, all within a 10-ft.-radius area
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You create either up to four spheres of _[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_ that each reduce the illumination level by one step within a 20-foot-radius, or one dimly lit, vaguely humanoid shape. Each sphere of _[[spells/Dancing Darkness|dancing darkness]]_ must stay within a 10-foot-radius area of one another but can otherwise move as you desire (no concentration required): forward or back, up or down, straight or turning corners, or the like. The _darkness_ can move up to 100 feet per round. The effect winks out if the distance between you and it exceeds the spell's range. _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Dancing|Dancing]]_ shadows can be made permanent with a _[[spells/Permanency|permanency]]_ spell.

 #### Other
**Source:** Blood Of Shadows
**Rating:** 3
**Permanency:** Yes
**Potion:** Yes
