# <font style='color:blue'>Recentering Drone</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature/2 levels (no two of which may be more than 30 ft. apart)
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** concentration
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
You emit a _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Calming|calming]]_ subsonic drone that allows the targets to temporarily refocus and restore their balance when they're suffering from _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Debilitating|debilitating]]_ conditions. A target with the _[[conditions/Dazzled|dazzled]]_, _[[conditions/Fatigued|fatigued]]_, _[[conditions/Shaken|shaken]]_, or _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_ condition ignores the penalties of those conditions for the duration of this spell. A target with one of the following conditions is treated as having the associated lesser condition for the duration of the spell, as shown by the following table.
  
  <table><tbody><tr><th>Condition</th><th>Lesser Condition</th></tr><tr><td>_[[conditions/Blinded|Blinded]]_</td><td>_Dazzled_</td></tr><tr><td>_[[conditions/Exhausted|Exhausted]]_</td><td>_Fatigued_</td></tr><tr><td>_[[conditions/Frightened|Frightened]]_</td><td>_Shaken_</td></tr><tr><td>_[[conditions/Nauseated|Nauseated]]_</td><td>_Sickened_</td></tr></tbody></table>
  
  At the end of the spell's duration, the targets are again subject to the appropriate penalties if the original conditions affecting the target are still in effect.

 #### Other
