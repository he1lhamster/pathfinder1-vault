# <font style='color:red'>Soul Bind</font> 
**School:** necromancy 
_arcanist_ 9, _wizard_ 9, _sorcerer_ 9, _witch_ 9, _shaman_ 9, _cleric_ 9, _oracle_ 9, _psychic_ 9, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, F (see text) {see text}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** corpse
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** permanent
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You draw the soul from a newly dead body and imprison it in a black sapphire gem. The subject must have been dead no more than 1 round per caster level. The soul, once trapped in the gem, cannot be returned through _[[spells/Clone|clone]]_, _[[spells/Raise Dead|raise dead]]_, reincarnation, _[[spells/Resurrection|resurrection]]_, _[[spells/True Resurrection|true resurrection]]_, or even a _[[spells/Miracle|miracle]]_ or a _[[spells/Wish|wish]]_. Only by destroying the gem or _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Dispelling|dispelling]]_ the spell on the gem can one free the soul (which is then still dead).
  The focus for this spell is a black sapphire of at least 1,000 gp value for every HD possessed by the creature whose soul is to be bound. If the gem is not valuable enough, it shatters when the _[[spells/Binding|binding]]_ is attempted. (While creatures have no concept of level or HD as such, the value of the gem needed to trap an individual can be researched.)
