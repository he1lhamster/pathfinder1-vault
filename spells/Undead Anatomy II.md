# <font style='color:blue'>Undead Anatomy II</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _magus_ 5, _alchemist_ 5, _investigator_ 5, _spiritualist_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a piece of the creature whose form you plan to assume) {a piece of the creature whose form you plan to assume}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)

 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Undead Anatomy I|undead anatomy I]]_, except it also allows you to assume the form of a Tiny or Large corporeal creature of the undead type. If the form you assume has any of the following abilities, you gain the listed ability: _[[universal monster rules/Climb|climb]]_ 60 feet, fly 60 feet (good maneuverability), swim 60 feet, _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 60 feet, _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Blood Drain|blood drain]]_, DR 5/bludgeoning, _[[universal monster rules/Scent|scent]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Freeze|freeze]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Grab|grab]]_, mimicry, _[[universal monster rules/Pounce|pounce]]_, shadowless, _[[universal monster rules/Sound Mimicry|sound mimicry]]_, and _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_.
  In this form, you gain a +4 bonus on saves against mindaffecting effects, disease, poison, sleep, and stunning. If the form has a _[[curses/Vulnerability|vulnerability]]_ to an attack (such as sunlight), you gain that _vulnerability_.
  Tiny undead: If the form you take is that of a Tiny undead, you gain a +4 size bonus to your Dexterity, a -2 penalty to your Strength, and a +1 natural armor bonus.
  Large undead: If the form you take is that of a Large undead, you gain a +4 size bonus to your Strength, a -2 penalty to your Dexterity, and a +4 natural armor bonus.

 #### Other
