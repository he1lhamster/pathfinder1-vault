# <font style='color:purple'>Bloody Tears And Jagged Smile</font> 
**School:** necromancy 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you and see text
**Range:** personal and see text
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
Your eyes turn black and weep blood, and your teeth become jagged fangs. You gain a bite attack (1d3 points of damage if you are Small, 1d4 points of damage if you are _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_). You gain a +4 profane bonus on Intimidate checks and on your spell DCs for spells with the _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_ descriptor. You perceive creatures as if using _[[spells/Deathwatch|deathwatch]]_.
