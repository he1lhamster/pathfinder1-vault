# <font style='color:red'>Recoil Fire</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _witch_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (hoof shavings from a mule) {hoof shavings from a mule}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** 1 firearm
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (object); **Spell Resistance:** yes (object); 
 #### Description
The target firearm suddenly fires of its own volition, even if it is unloaded, generating a recoil so great that the wielder or carrier of the firearm must scramble to hold onto it and becomes _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ until the start of his next turn. If the wielder is already _flat-footed_, he is instead knocked _[[conditions/Prone|prone]]_. If the firearm was loaded, that ammunition is wasted.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Combat
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
