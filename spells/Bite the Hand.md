# <font style='color:orange'>Bite the Hand</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 4, _summoner_ 3, _unsummoner_ 3, _druid_ 3, _hunter_ 3, _inquisitor_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature summoned by a spell or spell-like ability
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
With a short _[[spells/Command|command]]_ and a wave of the hand, you compel the target creature to attack the being who summoned it, to the best of its ability. If the being who summoned it is not present, the creature acts normally according to its last task or instructions. This spell has no effect on _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Called|called]]_ creatures, summoned creatures not brought forth by spells or _[[universal monster rules/Spell-Like Abilities|spell-like abilities]]_ (such as a _[[classes/Summoner|summoner]]_'s eidolon), or bonded creatures not explicitly summoned, such as a _[[classes/Paladin|paladin]]_'s _[[spells/Mount|mount]]_ or _[[classes/Wizard|wizard]]_'s familiar.

 #### Other
**Source:** Pathfinder Society Field Guide
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
