# <font style='color:red'>Reveal Mirage</font> 
**School:** divination 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 3, _druid_ 2, _hunter_ 1, _ranger_ 1, _cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, F (a small prism) {a small prism}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 round

 #### Description
You can focus upon a single point within line of sight and discern if the terrain or geographic features of that point are illusory. This ability allows you a Will save to disbelieve a terrain-affecting illusion (glamer) effect from range as though you had interacted with it. In addition to affecting such spells as _[[spells/Hallucinatory Terrain|hallucinatory terrain]]_ and _[[spells/Mirage Arcana|mirage arcana]]_, this spell also allows you to disbelieve naturally occurring mirages.

 #### Other
**Source:** Osirion, Legacy Of Pharaohs
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
