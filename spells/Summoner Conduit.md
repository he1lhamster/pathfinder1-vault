# <font style='color:green'>Summoner Conduit</font> 
**School:** necromancy 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _witch_ 5, _summoner_ 4, _unsummoner_ 4, _cleric_ 4, _oracle_ 4, _warpriest_ 4, _psychic_ 5, _occultist_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (two flies) {two flies}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one summoned creature or eidolon
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You exploit the _[[conditions/Invisible|invisible]]_, mystic connection between the target creature and its _[[classes/Summoner|summoner]]_ to _[[spells/Harm|harm]]_ that _summoner_. Whenever the target's summoned creature is the target of a spell that deals damage, the target's _summoner_ is also considered to be a target of that spell. Such a _summoner_ gains his normal defenses against the spell, such as a saving throw and _[[universal monster rules/Spell Resistance|spell resistance]]_, and cannot be affected if he is on another plane of existence.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Combat
**Rating:** 3
