# <font style='color:blue'>Freedom</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_arcanist_ 9, _wizard_ 9, _sorcerer_ 9, _psychic_ 9, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) or see text
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
The subject is freed from spells and effects that restrict movement, including _[[spells/Binding|binding]]_, _[[spells/Entangle|entangle]]_, grappling, _[[spells/Imprisonment|imprisonment]]_, _[[spells/Maze|maze]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Paralysis|paralysis]]_, petrification, pinning, sleep, _[[spells/Slow|slow]]_, stunning, _[[spells/Temporal Stasis|temporal stasis]]_, and web. To free a creature from _imprisonment_ or _maze_, you must know its name and background, and you must cast this spell at the spot where it was entombed or banished into the _maze_.
