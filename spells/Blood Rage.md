# <font style='color:green'>Blood Rage</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a drop of blood from each creature to be affected) {a drop of blood from each creature to be affected}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one willing living creature per 2 levels, no two of which may be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
The targets of this spell become angrier as they fight, the pain of their wounds fueling their strength. An affected creature gains a cumulative +2 morale bonus to Strength and a cumulative -1 penalty to AC for every 5 points of damage it takes (maximum +10 Strength, -5 AC) for the duration of the spell. These bonuses last until the spell expires or the target falls _[[conditions/Unconscious|unconscious]]_.

 #### Other
**Source:** Orcs of Golarion
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
