# <font style='color:orange'>Light Prison</font> 
**School:** evocation [light] 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _magus_ 2, _bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, _inquisitor_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature/2 levels
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Reflex negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
Rays of light burst forth from your outstretched hand, encircling each target's space with a cage of light. If a target fails its Reflex save, it becomes enclosed in the _[[spells/Light Prison|light prison]]_. A creature enclosed in a _light prison_ can attack, cast spells, and otherwise act normally as long as it stays within the _light prison_. However, if a creature passes through the walls of a _light prison_ enclosing it, it takes 1d6 points of damage and is _[[conditions/Blinded|blinded]]_ for 1 round.
  Creatures do not receive saves to negate these effects. Once a creature passes through the walls of a _light prison_ surrounding it, the effect ends for that creature.

 #### Other
**Source:** Inner Sea Gods
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
