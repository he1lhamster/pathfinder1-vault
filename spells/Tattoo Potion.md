# <font style='color:orange'>Tattoo Potion</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _witch_ 2, _bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _alchemist_ 2, _investigator_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 minute
 **Components:** V, S, M (a potion to be tattooed, special inks worth 500 gp) {a potion to be tattooed, special inks worth 500 gp}
 #### Effects
**Range:** one potion
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Effect:** one spell tattoo
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
When you cast this spell, you mix special _[[items/Mundane/Tattoo|tattoo]]_ inks into a potion of your choice. Once you finish casting _[[spells/Tattoo Potion|tattoo potion]]_, the potion begins bubbling and fizzing-if no one drinks the potion within 1 minute of the spell being cast, the potion bubbles away into vapor and is destroyed.
  When a potion under the effects of _tattoo potion_ is imbibed, the effects of the potion do not occur. Instead, the potion transforms into a spell _tattoo_ (see page 16) on the drinker's chest _tattoo_ slot-if the drinker already has a magic _tattoo_ in this location, the _tattoo potion_ is wasted. Once the potion transforms into a spell _tattoo_, it remains in place permanently until it is used as a spell _tattoo_.

 #### Other
