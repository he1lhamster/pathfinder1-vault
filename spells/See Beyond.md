# <font style='color:orange'>See Beyond</font> 
**School:** divination [meditative] 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _witch_ 3, _shaman_ 3, _cleric_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 hour
 **Components:** V, M (soothing incense worth 300 gp) {soothing incense worth 300 gp}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 24 hours or until discharged

 #### Description
You attune your mind and your sight to the hidden world of spirits. You gain a +5 circumstance bonus on Perception checks; this circumstance bonus increases to a +10 circumstance bonus on Perception checks to find _[[conditions/Invisible|invisible]]_ creatures or objects, _[[universal monster rules/Incorporeal|incorporeal]]_ creatures or objects, or things that exist only on the Ethereal Plane. Additionally, at any time during the spell's duration, you can push your ethereal _[[spells/Vision|vision]]_ even further as a swift action. When you do, you can see through solid objects (as if using a _[[items/Ring/Ring of X-Ray Vision|ring of x-ray vision]]_) for 5 rounds. After this time, the spell ends.

 #### Other
**Source:** Divine Anthology
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
