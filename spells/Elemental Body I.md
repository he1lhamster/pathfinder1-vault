# <font style='color:green'>Elemental Body I</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _magus_ 4, _bloodrager_ 4, _alchemist_ 4, _investigator_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (the element you plan to assume) {the element you plan to assume}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 min/level (D)

 #### Description
When you cast this spell, you can assume the form of a Small air, earth, fire, or water elemental. The abilities you gain depend upon the type of elemental into which you change. Elemental abilities based on size, such as _[[universal monster rules/Burn|burn]]_, _[[spells/Vortex|vortex]]_, and _[[universal monster rules/Whirlwind|whirlwind]]_, use the size of the elemental you transform into to determine their effect.
  Air elemental: If the form you take is that of a Small air elemental, you gain a +2 size bonus to your Dexterity and a +2 natural armor bonus. You also gain fly 60 feet (perfect), _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 60 feet, and the ability to create a _whirlwind_.
  Earth elemental: If the form you take is that of a Small earth elemental, you gain a +2 size bonus to your Strength and a +4 natural armor bonus. You also gain _darkvision_ 60 feet, the push ability, and the ability to _[[universal monster rules/Earth Glide|earth glide]]_.
  Fire elemental: If the form you take is that of a Small fire elemental, you gain a +2 size bonus to your Dexterity and a +2 natural armor bonus. You gain _darkvision_ 60 feet, resist fire 20, _[[curses/Vulnerability|vulnerability]]_ to cold, and the _burn_ ability.
  Water elemental: If the form you take is that of a Small water elemental, you gain a +2 size bonus to your Constitution and a +4 natural armor bonus. You also gain swim 60 feet, _darkvision_ 60 feet, the ability to create a _vortex_, and the ability to breathe water.
