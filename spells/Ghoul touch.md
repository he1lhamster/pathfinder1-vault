# <font style='color:green'>Ghoul touch</font> 
**School:** necromancy 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _bloodrager_ 2, _spiritualist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (cloth from a ghoul or earth from a ghoul's lair) 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** living humanoid touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1d6+2 rounds
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
Imbuing you with negative energy, this spell allows you to paralyze a single living humanoid for the duration of the spell with a successful melee touch attack.
  A _[[conditions/Paralyzed|paralyzed]]_ subject exudes a carrion _[[universal monster rules/Stench|stench]]_ that causes all living creatures (except you) in a 10-foot-radius spread to become _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_ (Fortitude negates). A _[[spells/Neutralize Poison|neutralize poison]]_ spell removes the effect from a _sickened_ creature, and creatures immune to poison are unaffected by the _stench_. This is a poison effect.
