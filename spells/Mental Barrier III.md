# <font style='color:green'>Mental Barrier III</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_psychic_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 immediate action
 **Components:** V 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 round

 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Mental Barrier I|mental barrier I]]_, but the _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_ bonus to AC increases to +8. In addition, you are immune to mine thrust I, and you take half damage from _[[spells/Mind Thrust II|mind thrust II]]_, III, and IV (reduced to one-quarter on a successful Will save). This spell can be undercast.

 #### Other
**Source:** Occult Adventures
**Rating:** 3
