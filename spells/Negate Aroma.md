# <font style='color:red'>Negate Aroma</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_druid_ 1, _hunter_ 1, _ranger_ 1, _alchemist_ 1, _investigator_ 1, _occultist_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M/DF (a pinch of alum) {a pinch of alum}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature or object/level touched
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 hour/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
With a gesture, this spell allows you to dismiss even the foulest or most distinctive scents. When cast, the targeted creatures or objects lose all natural and unnatural odors. A creature under the effect of _[[spells/Negate Aroma|negate aroma]]_ cannot be tracked, located, or pinpointed by the _[[universal monster rules/Scent|scent]]_ special quality. In addition, this spell prevents the target creature from using the _[[universal monster rules/Stench|stench]]_ special ability and similar odor-based abilities (such as those possessed by troglodytes).
  _Negate aroma_ does not prevent the target from acquiring outside smells or odors. Dowsing the creature with a pungent substance effectively negates the benefits of the spell until the substance is neutralized or washed away.
