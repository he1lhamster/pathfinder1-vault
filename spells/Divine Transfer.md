# <font style='color:green'>Divine Transfer</font> 
**School:** necromancy 
_paladin_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** living creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
With a single touch, you transfer some of your life essence to the subject, transferring your hit points and your resolve. When you touch the subject you can transfer up to a number of hit points equal to your Constitution score to the target. These hit points _[[spells/Heal|heal]]_ the subject, but cannot raise the subject's hit points higher than its normal hit point total. In addition, the subject gains DR/evil equal to your Charisma bonus (if any) for the duration of the spell.

 #### Other
**Source:** APG
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
