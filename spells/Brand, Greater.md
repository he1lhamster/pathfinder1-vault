# <font style='color:orange'>Brand, Greater</font> 
**School:** transmutation [curse] 
_inquisitor_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** permanent
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions like _[[spells/Brand|brand]]_, except that it inflicts 1d6 points of damage when applied and cannot be removed, even temporarily. In addition, a greater _brand_ blazes as bright as a _[[items/Mundane/Torch|torch]]_ when brought within 30 feet of a visible symbol of your faith. While the symbol is blazing, the recipient is _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_.
  Like the effects of _[[spells/Bestow Curse|bestow curse]]_, a greater _brand_ cannot be dispelled, but it can be removed by any means that removes a _[[spells/Mark of Justice|mark of justice]]_.

 #### Other
**Source:** APG
**Rating:** 2
