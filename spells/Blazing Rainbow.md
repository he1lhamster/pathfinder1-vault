# <font style='color:green'>Blazing Rainbow</font> 
**School:** evocation [light] 
_druid_ 6, _hunter_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S, M (a drop of rainwater) {a drop of rainwater}
 #### Effects
**Range:** 0 ft. or 10 ft./level
**Duration:** 1 minute/level or until discharged (see text)
**Effect:** bow or bridge of light
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You create a spectrum of multicolored solid light that can be used for one of two effects.
  Bow: The spell creates a _[[items/Weapon/Longbow|longbow]]_ or _[[items/Weapon/Shortbow|shortbow]]_ appropriate for your size, and you are proficient in the use of this weapon. When drawn, the bow automatically creates a +1 seeking _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Brilliant Energy|brilliant energy]]_ arrow; it cannot fire any other arrows. A creature struck by one of these arrows is surrounded by _[[spells/Faerie Fire|faerie fire]]_ and _[[conditions/Dazzled|dazzled]]_ for 1 minute. This version of the spell ends when you have fired a number of arrows equal to your caster level.
  Bridge: The light forms a bridge or ramp 5 feet wide and up to 10 feet long per caster level. Creatures on the bridge are _dazzled_. Allies gain a bonus equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum 1) on checks, saves, and to their CMD to resist being moved from the bridge against their will.
  Enemies take a penalty equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum 1) checks, saves, and to their CMD to resist being moved from the bridge against their will.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Class Guide
**Rating:** 3
