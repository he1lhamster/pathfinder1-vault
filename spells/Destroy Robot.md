# <font style='color:orange'>Destroy Robot</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 6, _wizard_ 6, _sorcerer_ 6, _druid_ 5, _hunter_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, F/DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one robot (see below)
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude partial; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You attempt to destroy any one robot in range. When you cast this spell, your hand crackles with electricity. You must succeed at a ranged touch attack to affect the target.
  The target takes 12d6 points of damage + 1 point per caster level, or 3d6 points of damage + 1 point per caster level on a successful saving throw. A cyborg or _[[monsters/Android|android]]_ can be damaged by this spell, but takes half damage and gains a +4 bonus on the saving throw to resist the spell's effects.

 #### Other
**Source:** Technology Guide
**Rating:** 2
