# <font style='color:orange'>Sabotage Construct</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 6, _wizard_ 6, _sorcerer_ 6, _witch_ 6, _bard_ 5, _skald_ 5, _inquisitor_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** S, V 
 #### Effects
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will partial; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Confusion|confusion]]_, except that it affects only constructs. When you are rolling for a _[[conditions/Confused|confused]]_ construct's behavior, a result of 26-50 indicates the construct twitches spasmodically. On a successful Will save, the construct is _confused_ for only 1 round.

 #### Other
**Source:** People Of The River
**Rating:** 2
