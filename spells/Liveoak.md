# <font style='color:green'>Liveoak</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_shaman_ 7, _druid_ 6, _hunter_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 10 minutes
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** tree touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 day/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell turns an oak tree into a protector or _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Guardian|guardian]]_. The spell can only be cast on a single tree at a time; while _[[spells/Liveoak|liveoak]]_ is in effect, you can't cast it again on another tree. _Liveoak_ must be cast on a healthy, Huge oak. A triggering phrase of up to one word per caster level is placed on the targeted oak. The _liveoak_ spell triggers the tree into animating as a _[[monsters/Treant|treant]]_.
  If _liveoak_ is dispelled, the tree takes _[[spells/Root|root]]_ immediately wherever it happens to be. If released by you, the tree tries to return to its original location before taking _root_.
