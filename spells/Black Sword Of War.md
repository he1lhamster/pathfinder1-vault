# <font style='color:green'>Black Sword Of War</font> 
**School:** necromancy 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _hunter_ 3, _ranger_ 3, _cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, _antipaladin_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, F 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** piercing or slashing manufactured weapon touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless, object); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless, object); 
 #### Description
The target weapon turns a glossy black color and deals _[[universal monster rules/Bleed|bleed]]_ damage if it deals hit point damage to a creature. The amount of _bleed_ damage is equal to 1/2 your caster level (maximum _bleed_ 5).
