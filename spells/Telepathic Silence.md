# <font style='color:red'>Telepathic Silence</font> 
**School:** illusion (glamer) 
_psychic_ 4, _mesmerist_ 4, _occultist_ 4, _spiritualist_ 4, _medium_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Area:** 60-foot-radius emanation centered on a point in space
**Range:** long (400 feet + 40 feet/level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
Creatures within the area cannot send or receive telepathic messages, and anyone in the area casting a spell with a thought component must succeed at a concentration check (DC = 20 + twice the spell's level; this includes the increased concentration DC for casting spells with a thought component) or lose the spell. Furthermore, the thoughts of creatures within the area cannot be detected, located, or read with _[[universal monster rules/Thoughtsense|thoughtsense]]_, _[[spells/Detect Thoughts|detect thoughts]]_, or similar effects. Telepathic _[[spells/Silence|silence]]_ does not _[[feats/Hinder|hinder]]_ spells that target a creature's mind or that do not rely on communication, such as mind thrustOA.

 #### Other
**Source:** Monster Hunter's Handbook
**Rating:** 1
