# <font style='color:orange'>Sun's Disdain</font> 
**School:** transmutation [curse] 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _witch_ 2, _bloodrager_ 2, _shaman_ 2, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, _inquisitor_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a piece of glass)/DF {a piece of glass}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** permanent
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You alter a creature so the touch of the sun is hostile to it. The cursed creature gains _[[universal monster rules/Light Blindness|light blindness]]_ and is _[[conditions/Blinded|blinded]]_ for 1 round if exposed to bright light, such as sunlight or the _[[spells/Daylight|daylight]]_ spell. The cursed creature is _[[conditions/Dazzled|dazzled]]_ as long as it remains in areas of bright light, and it is treated as being particularly susceptible to sunlight for the purposes of resolving spells like _[[spells/Searing Light|searing light]]_, _[[spells/Sunbeam|sunbeam]]_, and _[[spells/Sunburst|sunburst]]_.

 #### Other
