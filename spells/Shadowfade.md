# <font style='color:orange'>Shadowfade</font> 
**School:** illusion (shadow) 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, _summoner_ 1, _unsummoner_ 1, _inquisitor_ 1, _mesmerist_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a blindfold) {a blindfold}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 minutes/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
In areas of _[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_, the target of _[[spells/Shadowfade|shadowfade]]_ is _[[conditions/Invisible|invisible]]_ to creatures using _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ to see. In areas of dim light, the target gains concealment against creatures using _darkvision_. This spell has no effect in areas of normal light or brighter, and is automatically dispelled if the target enters an area of bright light or takes a hostile action.
