# <font style='color:green'>Oracle's Vessel</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_oracle_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
The target gains all the benefits of your _[[classes/Oracle|oracle]]_'s curse, with none of the penalties. In some cases, this has no effect (for example, a _[[classes/Fighter|fighter]]_ with your haunted curse can't cast spells and doesn't gain any benefit from having additional spells known).
