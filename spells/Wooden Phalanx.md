# <font style='color:red'>Wooden Phalanx</font> 
**School:** conjuration (creation) 
_arcanist_ 9, _wizard_ 9, _sorcerer_ 9, _cleric_ 9, _oracle_ 9, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 hour/level (D)
**Effect:** three or more wood golems, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart (see text)
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You create 1d4+2 wood golems with the advanced template (Bestiary 164, 294). The golems willingly aid you in combat or battle, perform a specific mission, or serve as bodyguards. You can only have one _[[spells/Wooden Phalanx|wooden phalanx]]_ spell in effect at one time.
  If you cast this spell while another casting is still in effect, the previous casting is dispelled.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Magic
**Rating:** 1
