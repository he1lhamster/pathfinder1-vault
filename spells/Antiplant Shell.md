# <font style='color:red'>Antiplant Shell</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_druid_ 4, _hunter_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Area:** 10-ft.-radius emanation, centered on you
**Range:** 10 ft.
**Duration:** 1 min./level (D)
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
The _[[spells/Antiplant Shell|antiplant shell]]_ spell creates an _[[conditions/Invisible|invisible]]_, mobile barrier that keeps all creatures within the shell protected from attacks by plant creatures or _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Animated|animated]]_ plants. As with many abjuration spells, forcing the barrier against creatures that the spell keeps at bay strains and collapses the field.

**Source:** PFRPG Core
