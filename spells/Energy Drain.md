# <font style='color:blue'>Energy Drain</font> 
**School:** necromancy 
_arcanist_ 9, _wizard_ 9, _sorcerer_ 9, _shaman_ 9, _cleric_ 9, _oracle_ 9, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Effect:** ray of negative energy
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude partial; see text for enervation; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions like _[[spells/Enervation|enervation]]_, except that the creature struck gains 2d4 temporary negative levels. Twenty-four hours after gaining them, the subject must make a Fortitude saving throw (DC = _[[universal monster rules/Energy Drain|energy drain]]_ spell's save DC) for each negative level. If the save succeeds, that negative level is removed. If it fails, that negative level becomes permanent.
  An undead creature struck by the ray gains 2d4x5 temporary hit points for 1 hour.
