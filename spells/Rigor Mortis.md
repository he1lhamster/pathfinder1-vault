# <font style='color:green'>Rigor Mortis</font> 
**School:** transmutation [pain] 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 4, _magus_ 4, _shaman_ 4, _cleric_ 4, _oracle_ 4, _warpriest_ 4, _psychic_ 4, _occultist_ 4, _spiritualist_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M/DF (a knucklebone) {a knucklebone}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one living creature
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** instantaneous; see text
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude partial (see text); **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
The joints of a creature affected by this spell stiffen and swell, making movement painful and _[[spells/Slow|slow]]_. The target takes 1d6 points of nonlethal damage per caster level. Additionally, the target takes a -4 penalty to Dexterity and its movement speed decreases by 10 feet; these additional effects last for 1 minute per caster level, though another creature can spend 1 minute and attempt a DC 25 _[[spells/Heal|Heal]]_ check to end them early. A successful save halves the nonlethal damage and negates the penalty to Dexterity and movement.

 #### Other
