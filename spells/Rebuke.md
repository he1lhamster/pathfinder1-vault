# <font style='color:orange'>Rebuke</font> 
**School:** evocation [sonic] 
_inquisitor_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Area:** 20-ft.-radius burst centered on you
**Range:** 20 ft.
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude partial; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
Your wrathful words cause physical _[[spells/Harm|harm]]_ to your enemies.
  Your enemies take 1d8 points of damage per two caster levels (maximum 5d8) and are _[[conditions/Staggered|staggered]]_ for 1 round. Half of this damage is sonic damage, but the other half results directly from _[[spells/Divine Power|divine power]]_ and is therefore not subject to being reduced by _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ to sonic-based attacks. is especially devastating to foes who worship your god, inflicting 1d6 points of damage per caster level (maximum 10d6) and stunning them for 1d4 rounds. A successful Fortitude save halves the damage and negates the staggering or stunning effect.
  Enemies do not need to hear a _[[spells/Rebuke|rebuke]]_ to be harmed by it, but it is negated by magical _[[spells/Silence|silence]]_.
