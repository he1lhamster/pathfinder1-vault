# <font style='color:red'>Dread Bolt</font> 
**School:** evocation [evil] 
_cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous (1d4 rounds)
**Effect:** arrow-shaped projectile of evil energy
**Saving Throw:** Will partial (see text); **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You unleash a crackling missile of _[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_ from your _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Unholy|unholy]]_ symbol at any one target in range as a ranged touch attack.
  A good creature struck by _[[spells/Dread Bolt|dread bolt]]_ takes 1d8 points of damage per two caster levels (maximum 5d8). A good outsider instead takes 1d6 points of damage per caster level (maximum 10d6) and is _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_ for 1 round. A successful Will save reduces the damage to half and negates the _sickened_ effect. This spell deals only half damage to creatures that are neither good nor evil, and they are not _sickened_. The bolt has no effect on evil creatures.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Magic
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
