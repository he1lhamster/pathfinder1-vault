# <font style='color:green'>Heatstroke</font> 
**School:** evocation [fire] 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _druid_ 3, _hunter_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a drop of sweat) {a drop of sweat}
 #### Effects
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Effect:** ray
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude partial, see text; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
A wavering red ray projects from your finger. You must succeed on a ranged touch attack with the ray to hit your target.
  The ray inflicts 1d4 points of nonlethal damage, causing the target to suffer from _[[spells/Heatstroke|heatstroke]]_ as its body temperature dramatically increases. Except as noted above, this spell otherwise functions as _[[spells/Ray of Exhaustion|ray of exhaustion]]_.
  Characters wearing heavy clothing or armor of any sort take a -4 penalty on their saves.

 #### Other
**Source:** Sargava
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
