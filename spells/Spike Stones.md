# <font style='color:blue'>Spike Stones</font> 
**School:** transmutation [earth] 
_shaman_ 4, _druid_ 4, _hunter_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Area:** one 20-ft. square/level
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 hour/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Reflex partial; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
Rocky ground, stone floors, and similar surfaces shape themselves into long, sharp points that _[[spells/Blend|blend]]_ into the background.
  _[[spells/Spike Stones|Spike stones]]_ impede progress through an area and deal damage.
  Any creature moving on foot into or through the spell's area moves at half speed. In addition, each creature moving through the area takes 1d8 points of piercing damage for each 5 feet of movement through the spiked area.
  Any creature that takes damage from this spell must also succeed on a Reflex save to avoid injuries to its feet and legs. A failed save causes the creature's speed to be reduced to half normal for 24 hours or until the injured creature receives a cure spell (which also restores lost hit points). Another character can remove the penalty by taking 10 minutes to dress the injuries and succeeding on a _[[spells/Heal|Heal]]_ check against the spell's save DC.
  Magic traps such as _spike stones_ are hard to detect. A character with trapfinding can use the Perception skill to find _spike stones_.
  The DC is 25 + spell level, or DC 29 for _spike stones_. _Spike stones_ is a magic trap that can't be _[[conditions/Disabled|disabled]]_ with the Disable Device skill.
