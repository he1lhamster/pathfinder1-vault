# <font style='color:green'>Ear-Piercing Scream</font> 
**School:** evocation [sonic] 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _witch_ 1, _bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, _bloodrager_ 1, _inquisitor_ 1, _psychic_ 1, _medium_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous; see text
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude partial (see text); **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You unleash a powerful scream, inaudible to all but a single target. The target is _[[conditions/Dazed|dazed]]_ for 1 round and takes 1d6 points of sonic damage per two caster levels (maximum 5d6). A successful save negates the _[[spells/Daze|daze]]_ effect and halves the damage.
