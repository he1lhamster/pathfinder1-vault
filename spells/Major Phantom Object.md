# <font style='color:red'>Major Phantom Object</font> 
**School:** illusion (shadow) [shadow] 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _psychic_ 5, _mesmerist_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 10 minutes
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level (D)
**Effect:** illusory, unattended, nonmagical object, up to 1 cu. ft./level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as the _[[spells/Major Creation|major creation]]_ spell, except as noted above and the object created is a semi-real illusion. Any creature that interacts with the object may make a Will save, with success causing the object to cease to exist. A gnome casting this spell may make a Spellcraft check in place of any Craft check required to make a complex item.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Race Guide
**Rating:** 1
