# <font style='color:green'>Blood Scent</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 3, _bloodrager_ 3, _druid_ 3, _hunter_ 2, _ranger_ 2, _cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, _inquisitor_ 3, _antipaladin_ 2, _alchemist_ 3, _investigator_ 3, _psychic_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature/2 levels, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
You greatly magnify the target's ability to smell the presence of blood. The target is considered to have the _[[universal monster rules/Scent|scent]]_ universal monster ability, but only for purposes of detecting and pinpointing injured creatures (below full hit points). Creatures below half their full hit points or suffering _[[universal monster rules/Bleed|bleed]]_ damage are considered strong scents for this ability. Orcs and any creature under the effects of _[[spells/Rage|rage]]_ gain a +2 morale bonus on attack and damage rolls against creatures they can smell with this spell, or a +4 morale bonus if the target's blood counts as a strong _scent_.
