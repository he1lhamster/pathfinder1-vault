# <font style='color:red'>Sundered Serpent Coil</font> 
**School:** conjuration (creation) 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 3, _magus_ 3, _summoner_ 3, _unsummoner_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a snake scale) {a snake scale}
 #### Effects
**Area:** one 5-foot square
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions like _[[spells/Black Tentacles|black tentacles]]_, except it creates a Large decapitated snake, which erupts from the ground and grapples a creature you specify within its 5-foot reach. As a standard action, you can _[[spells/Command|command]]_ the snake to release its _[[conditions/Grappled|grappled]]_ target and direct it to attack a different creature.

 #### Other
**Source:** Monster Codex
