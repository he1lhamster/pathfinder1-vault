# <font style='color:green'>Widen Auras</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_antipaladin_ 2, _paladin_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)

 #### Description
The range of your _[[classes/Antipaladin|antipaladin]]_ or _[[classes/Paladin|paladin]]_ auras doubles. For example, if you're a _paladin_, your aura of courage affects allies within 20 feet instead of within 10 feet.
