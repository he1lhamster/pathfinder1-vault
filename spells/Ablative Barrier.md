# <font style='color:blue'>Ablative Barrier</font> 
**School:** conjuration (creation) [force] 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _magus_ 2, _summoner_ 2, _unsummoner_ 3, _bloodrager_ 2, _alchemist_ 2, _investigator_ 2, _psychic_ 3, _occultist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a piece of metal cut from a shield) {a piece of metal cut from a shield}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 hour/level or until discharged
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
_[[conditions/Invisible|Invisible]]_ layers of solid force surround and protect the target, granting that target a +2 armor bonus to AC. Additionally, the first 5 points of lethal damage the target takes from each attack are converted into nonlethal damage. Against attacks that already deal nonlethal damage, the target gains DR 5/-. Once this spell has converted 5 points of damage to nonlethal damage per caster level (maximum 50 points), the spell is discharged.
