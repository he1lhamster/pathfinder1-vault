# <font style='color:red'>Flash Fire</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of powdered fool's gold) 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** 1 firearm
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (object); see text; **Spell Resistance:** yes (object); see text; 
 #### Description
The firearm fires with a _[[feats/Blinding Flash|blinding flash]]_, even if the firearm is not currently loaded. Every creature within 15 feet of the creature carrying the weapon must succeed at a Fortitude save or be _[[conditions/Blinded|blinded]]_ for 1d4 rounds. The creature carrying the firearm takes a -4 penalty on this saving throw. If the firearm is loaded, the ammunition is wasted.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Combat
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
