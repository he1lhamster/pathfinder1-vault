# <font style='color:orange'>Deadly Finale</font> 
**School:** evocation [sonic] 
_bard_ 6, _skald_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** 1 creature/3 levels no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude partial; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You must have a bardic performance in effect to cast this spell.
  With a flourish, you immediately end your performance, dealing 2d8 points of sonic damage to each target. In addition, each target takes 3d6 points of _[[universal monster rules/Bleed|bleed]]_ damage for 1d6 rounds. A save negates the _bleed_ damage but not the sonic damage.

 #### Other
**Source:** APG
**Rating:** 2
