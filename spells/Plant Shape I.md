# <font style='color:blue'>Plant Shape I</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _alchemist_ 5, _investigator_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a piece of the creature whose form you plan to assume) {a piece of the creature whose form you plan to assume}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 min./level (D)

 #### Description
When you cast this spell you can assume the form of any Small or _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ creature of the plant type (see the Pathfinder RPG Bestiary).
  If the form you assume has any of the following abilities, you gain the listed ability: _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 60 feet, _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Constrict|constrict]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Grab|grab]]_, and poison. If the form you assume does not possess the ability to move, your speed is reduced to 5 feet and you lose all other forms of movement. If the creature has _[[curses/Vulnerability|vulnerability]]_ to an element, you gain that _vulnerability_.
  Small plant: If the form you take is that of a Small plant, you gain a +2 size bonus to your Constitution and a +2 natural armor bonus.
  _Medium_ plant: If the form you take is that of a _Medium_ plant, you gain a +2 size bonus to your Strength, a +2 enhancement bonus to your Constitution, and a +2 natural armor bonus.

 #### Other
**Source:** PFRPG Core
**Rating:** 4
