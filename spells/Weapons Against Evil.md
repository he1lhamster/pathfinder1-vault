# <font style='color:orange'>Weapons Against Evil</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_cleric_ 1, _oracle_ 1, _warpriest_ 1, _inquisitor_ 1, _paladin_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one weapon/level, no two of which can be more than 20 ft. apart
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates (harmless, object); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless, object); 
 #### Description
Each weapon this spell affects each shines with pale light that dimly illuminates a 5-foot square. These weapons also ignore the DR of evil creatures that have DR 5 or lower, as long as the _[[universal monster rules/Damage Reduction|damage reduction]]_ is not DR/epic.

 #### Other
**Source:** Inner Sea Gods
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
