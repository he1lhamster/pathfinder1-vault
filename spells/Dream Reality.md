# <font style='color:orange'>Dream Reality</font> 
**School:** illusion (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 6, _wizard_ 6, _sorcerer_ 6, _bard_ 5, _skald_ 5, _cleric_ 6, _oracle_ 6, _warpriest_ 6, _alchemist_ 5, _investigator_ 5, _psychic_ 6, _mesmerist_ 5, _occultist_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will disbelief; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You cause the target to perceive the world as if in a _[[spells/Dream|dream]]_. When the spell ends, the target forgets all identifying details of events that transpired while the spell lasted. The memories were not taken from it; rather, its mind never recorded the memories as real in the first place. If _[[spells/Modify Memory|modify memory]]_ is cast upon it before the spell ends, the target remembers these events. A target under the effect of _[[spells/Lullaby|lullaby]]_ before this spell is cast applies its penalty to the saving throw against this spell.

 #### Other
**Source:** Inner Sea Intrigue
**Rating:** 2
