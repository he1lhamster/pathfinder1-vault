# <font style='color:blue'>Plant Shape III</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_arcanist_ 7, _wizard_ 7, _sorcerer_ 7, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a piece of the creature whose form you plan to assume) {a piece of the creature whose form you plan to assume}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 min./level (D)

 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Plant Shape II|plant shape II]]_ except that it also allows you to assume the form of a Huge creature of the plant type. If the form you assume has any of the following abilities, you gain the listed ability: DR, _[[universal monster rules/Regeneration|regeneration]]_ 5, and _[[universal monster rules/Trample|trample]]_.
  Huge plant: If the form you take is that of a Huge plant, you gain a +8 size bonus to your Strength, a -2 penalty to your Dexterity, a +4 size bonus to your Constitution, and a +6 natural armor bonus.
