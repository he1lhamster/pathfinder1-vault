# <font style='color:green'>Mind Swap, Major</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_psychic_ 9, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 minute
 **Components:** V, S, M (diamonds worth 10,000 gp) {diamonds worth 10,000 gp}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature of your same race
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
The spell functions as _[[spells/Mind Swap|mind swap]]_, except as noted above. In addition, after 24 hours, instead of using each other's hit points, you each revert to your original hit points, modified by your new Constitution modifiers. This spell can be reversed only with _[[spells/Miracle|miracle]]_, _[[spells/Wish|wish]]_, or another major _mind swap_.

 #### Other
**Source:** Occult Adventures
**Rating:** 3
