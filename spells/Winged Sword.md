# <font style='color:red'>Winged Sword</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _magus_ 2, _bloodrager_ 2, _inquisitor_ 3, _paladin_ 2, _occultist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one melee weapon
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless, object); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless, object); 
 #### Description
The weapon targeted by this spell grows small feathered wings. For the duration of the spell, the weapon acts as if it had the _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Throwing|throwing]]_ weapon special ability. This spell can be used in place of _[[spells/Magic Stone|magic stone]]_ as a prerequisite for the _throwing_ weapon special ability.

 #### Other
**Source:** Arcane Anthology
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
