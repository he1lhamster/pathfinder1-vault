# <font style='color:orange'>Stunning Finale</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_bard_ 5, _skald_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** up to 3 creatures, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude partial; see text; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You must have a bardic performance in effect to cast this spell.
  With a flourish, you immediately end your bardic performance, assaulting the senses of the targets with your finale. Each target is _[[conditions/Stunned|stunned]]_ for 1 round. On a successful saving throw, a target is _[[conditions/Staggered|staggered]]_ for 1 round.

 #### Other
**Source:** APG
**Rating:** 2
