# <font style='color:orange'>Regenerate</font> 
**School:** conjuration (healing) 
_witch_ 7, _shaman_ 7, _druid_ 9, _cleric_ 7, _oracle_ 7, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 3 full rounds
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** living creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
The subject's severed body members (fingers, toes, hands, feet, arms, legs, tails, or even heads of multiheaded creatures), _[[conditions/Broken|broken]]_ bones, and ruined organs grow back. After the spell is cast, the physical _[[universal monster rules/Regeneration|regeneration]]_ is complete in 1 round if the severed members are present and touching the creature. It takes 2d10 rounds otherwise.
  _[[spells/Regenerate|Regenerate]]_ also cures 4d8 points of damage + 1 point per caster level (maximum +35), rids the subject of exhaustion and fatigue, and eliminates all nonlethal damage the subject has taken. It has no effect on nonliving creatures (including undead).
