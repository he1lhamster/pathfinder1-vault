# <font style='color:green'>Burst Of Glory</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_cleric_ 5, _oracle_ 5, _warpriest_ 5, _inquisitor_ 4, _paladin_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Area:** 10-ft.-radius burst, centered on you
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D; see text)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
Allies in the area of this spell at the time of casting gain a +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ bonus on attack rolls and saves against _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_ effects, plus 1 temporary hit point per caster level (maximum 20). You shine with a white or golden _[[items/Weapon/Radiance|radiance]]_ equal to a _[[items/Mundane/Torch|torch]]_ for the spell's duration, though you can dismiss the glow effect as a free action.

 #### Other
**Source:** Inner Sea Gods
**Rating:** 3
