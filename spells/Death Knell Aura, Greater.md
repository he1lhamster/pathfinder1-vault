# <font style='color:red'>Death Knell Aura, Greater</font> 
**School:** necromancy [death, evil] 
_arcanist_ 6, _wizard_ 6, _sorcerer_ 6, _witch_ 6, _cleric_ 6, _oracle_ 6, _warpriest_ 6, _inquisitor_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Area:** 20-ft.-radius emanation, centered on you
**Range:** 20 ft.
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Death Knell Aura|death knell aura]]_, except a _[[conditions/Dying|dying]]_ creature in the area cannot stabilize on its own and automatically takes 1 point of _[[universal monster rules/Bleed|bleed]]_ damage on its turn each round. An _[[universal monster rules/Incorporeal|incorporeal]]_ undead or living spirit traveling outside the body (such as a person using _[[spells/Astral Projection|astral projection]]_ or _[[spells/Magic Jar|magic jar]]_) within the aura at the start of its turn takes 1d8 points of damage.

 #### Other
**Source:** Horsemen Of The Apocalypse
**Rating:** 1
