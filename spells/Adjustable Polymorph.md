# <font style='color:orange'>Adjustable Polymorph</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 4, _magus_ 4, _bard_ 4, _skald_ 4, _summoner_ 4, _unsummoner_ 4, _shaman_ 4, _alchemist_ 4, _investigator_ 4, _psychic_ 4, _medium_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a small piece of doppelganger flesh) {a small piece of doppelganger flesh}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)

 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Alter Self|alter self]]_, except you can as a swift action alter the disguise (within the limitations of _alter self_).
  For example, you could transform yourself into a halfling, then a _[[monsters/Lizardfolk|lizardfolk]]_, then an elf, and so on. You can change your form a number of times equal to your caster level.
