# <font style='color:blue'>Illusory Maze</font> 
**School:** illusion (figment) 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _witch_ 2, _bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Area:** 20-ft.-radius spread
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will disbelief (if interacted with); **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell creates the illusion of a translucent, ever-changing _[[spells/Maze|maze]]_ of glass in the target area. Creatures in the area who believe the _maze_ to be real cannot move more than 5 feet continuously in any one direction and can't move diagonally, forcing them to take a circuitous route to their destination.

 #### Other
**Source:** Inner Sea Monster Codex
**Rating:** 4
**Potion:** Yes
