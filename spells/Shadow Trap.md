# <font style='color:green'>Shadow Trap</font> 
**School:** illusion (shadow) 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _witch_ 1, _bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, _bloodrager_ 1, _shaman_ 1, _cleric_ 1, _oracle_ 1, _warpriest_ 1, _antipaladin_ 1, _psychic_ 1, _mesmerist_ 1, _occultist_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You pin the target's _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Shadow|shadow]]_ to its current location, causing the target to become _[[conditions/Entangled|entangled]]_ and preventing it from moving farther than 5 feet from its original position, as if its _shadow_ were anchored to the terrain. Each round on its turn, the target can attempt a new saving throw to end the effect as a full-round action. A flying creature can only _[[feats/Hover|hover]]_ in place or fall while _entangled_ in this manner. This spell automatically fails when cast on a creature that doesn't throw a _shadow_, and it ends if the creature is entirely in an area with no illumination.

 #### Other
**Source:** Blood Of Shadows
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
