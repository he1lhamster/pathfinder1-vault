# <font style='color:green'>Resonating Word</font> 
**School:** transmutation [sonic] 
_arcanist_ 7, _wizard_ 7, _sorcerer_ 7, _bard_ 5, _skald_ 5, _psychic_ 7, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 3 rounds
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude partial; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You speak a terrible word of power, setting up potentially lethal vibrations in the chosen target. The target must save once each round on your turn, and the effects grow stronger for each saving throw the creature fails. On the first round, the target takes 5d6 points of sonic damage and is _[[conditions/Staggered|staggered]]_ for 1 round. A successful save halves the damage and negates the _staggered_ effect. On the second round, the target takes 5d6 points of damage and is _[[conditions/Stunned|stunned]]_ for 1 round. A successful save halves the damage and negates the stunning effect. On the third round, the target takes 10d6 points of damage and is _stunned_ for 1d4+1 rounds. A successful save halves the damage and negates the stunning effect. The _[[spells/Resonating Word|resonating word]]_ has no power after the third round, even if the spell's duration is increased.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Magic
**Rating:** 3
