# <font style='color:purple'>Possession, Greater</font> 
**School:** necromancy 
_arcanist_ 8, _wizard_ 8, _sorcerer_ 8, _witch_ 8, _psychic_ 8, _occultist_ 6, _spiritualist_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 hour/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Possession|possession]]_, but when you possess a host, you enter the host's body and your physical body vanishes. You are ejected to the closest empty square upon expiration of the spell or upon the host's death.

 #### Other
**Source:** Occult Adventures
**Rating:** 5
