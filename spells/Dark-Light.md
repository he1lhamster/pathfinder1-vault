# <font style='color:blue'>Dark-Light</font> 
**School:** evocation [light] 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _druid_ 3, _hunter_ 3, _cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M/DF (a pinch of Darklands earth) {a pinch of Darklands earth}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** living creatures without light sensitivity
**Area:** 20-ft.-radius burst
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude partial; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You evoke a powerful light that affects all sighted creatures that do not posses _[[universal monster rules/Light Sensitivity|light sensitivity]]_. Those that succeed on their saves are _[[conditions/Dazzled|dazzled]]_ for 1 round per level; those who fail are _[[conditions/Blinded|blinded]]_ for the same duration.
