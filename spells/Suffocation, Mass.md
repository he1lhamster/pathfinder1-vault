# <font style='color:green'>Suffocation, Mass</font> 
**School:** necromancy 
_arcanist_ 9, _wizard_ 9, _sorcerer_ 9, _witch_ 9, _psychic_ 9, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a vial containing a bit of the caster's breath) 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one living creature/2 levels (no two of which may be more than 30 feet apart)
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude partial; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Suffocation|suffocation]]_ except as noted above. Note that the duration of this spell is much longer, forcing those suffering from the effect to make far more Fortitude saves to stave off eventual _suffocation_.

 #### Other
**Source:** APG
