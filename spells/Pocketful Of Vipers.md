# <font style='color:red'>Pocketful Of Vipers</font> 
**School:** conjuration (summoning) 
_witch_ 3, _shaman_ 3, _druid_ 3, _hunter_ 3, _occultist_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S, M (a snake scale and fang) {a snake scale and fang}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** object touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 hour/level or until discharged (D)
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates (object); **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You set a magical ward upon the object touched, which must be a container such as a pouch, bag, backpack, or pocket. If any creature opens the container without first speaking a _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word, 1d3 summoned venomous snakes (Pathfinder RPG Bestiary 255) appear, _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Slithering|slithering]]_ out of the container and attacking that creature for 1 round/level before disappearing. They attack other creatures only if they themselves are attacked.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Intrigue
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
