#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a strip of kelp bound to the target), DF {a strip of kelp bound to the target}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
The target can move and attack _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Underwater|underwater]]_ normally without the usual penalties involved with _underwater_ combat, as if under the effects of _[[spells/Freedom of Movement|freedom of movement]]_. However, the target doesn't gain any protections against grapple, _[[universal monster rules/Paralysis|paralysis]]_, and magical effects that impede movement (such as _[[spells/Solid Fog|solid fog]]_, _[[spells/Slow|slow]]_, and web).
