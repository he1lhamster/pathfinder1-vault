# <font style='color:red'>Ardor's Onslaught</font> 
**School:** evocation 
_cleric_ 4, _oracle_ 4, _warpriest_ 4, _inquisitor_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Area:** 20-ft.-radius burst
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** instantaneous or 1d6 rounds; see text
**Saving Throw:** Will partial, see text; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You unleash the power of zealous _[[feats/Conviction|conviction]]_ to smite your enemies with a burst of pulsing metallic energy. Only creatures with a neutral alignment component are harmed by the spell. The spell deals 1d8 points of damage per 2 caster levels (maximum 5d8) to creatures whose alignment is neutral with no other alignment components (with the exception of neutral outsiders, who take 1d6 points of damage per caster level, maximum 10d6). Neutral outsiders are also _[[conditions/Nauseated|nauseated]]_ for 1d6 rounds. A creature can attempt a Will save to halve the damage and negate the _nauseated_ effect. The spell deals half damage against creatures that are chaotic neutral, lawful neutral, neutral evil, or neutral good, and such targets are not _nauseated_. On a successful Will save, such a creature reduces the damage to one-quarter the damage rolled.

 #### Other
**Source:** Planar Adventures
**Rating:** 1
