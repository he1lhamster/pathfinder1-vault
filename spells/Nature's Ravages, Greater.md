**School:** necromancy 
_witch_ 7, _cleric_ 8, _oracle_ 8, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 10 minute/HD of target
 **Components:** V, S, M/DF (dried maggots) {dried maggots}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** corpse of creature whose total number of HD does not exceed your caster level
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./level)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions like _[[spells/Nature's Ravages|nature's ravages]]_, except the target corpse ages a number of years equal to up to 1 year per caster level (maximum 20 years at 20th level).
