# <font style='color:orange'>Lucky Number</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _witch_ 1, _bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, _summoner_ 1, _unsummoner_ 1, _druid_ 1, _hunter_ 1, _cleric_ 1, _oracle_ 1, _warpriest_ 1, _inquisitor_ 1, _paladin_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one willing creature
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 24 hours or until discharged
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You are able to tweak tiny variables affecting a creature's immediate future in order to grant the target a _[[spells/Bit Of Luck|bit of luck]]_ at the right time. Roll a d20; once during the duration of _[[spells/Lucky Number|lucky number]]_, when the target creature rolls that result (regardless of what type of dice the target rolls), the creature has the option to either reroll the result or add a +2 luck bonus to the result. The creature must decide to use this ability before the success or failure of the original roll is known. A creature can have only one _lucky number_ at a time. If _lucky number_ is cast on a creature already affected by that spell, the new number replaces the previous one.

 #### Other
**Source:** Occult Mysteries
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
