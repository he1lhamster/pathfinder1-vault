# <font style='color:blue'>Shield Of Faith</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_cleric_ 1, _oracle_ 1, _warpriest_ 1, _inquisitor_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (parchment with a holy text written on it) {parchment with a holy text written on it}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 min./level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
This spell creates a shimmering, magical field around the target that averts and deflects attacks. The spell grants the subject a +2 _[[spells/Deflection|deflection]]_ bonus to AC, with an additional +1 to the bonus for every six levels you have (maximum +5 _deflection_ bonus at 18th level).
