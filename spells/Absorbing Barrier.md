# <font style='color:green'>Absorbing Barrier</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _summoner_ 4, _unsummoner_ 4, _bloodrager_ 4, _alchemist_ 4, _investigator_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** standard action
 **Components:** V, S, F 
 #### Effects
**Area:** sphere, 40-foot radius (S)
**Range:** medium (100 feet + 10 feet/level)
**Duration:** 1 hour/level or until discharged
**Saving Throw:** none (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** no (harmless); 
 #### Description
You ward all creatures and objects in the area from ranged weapons. The spell's area of effect is often a sphere, although you can shape it to be smaller than the maximum area or to exclude certain adjacent areas or objects (for example, if you are _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Warding|warding]]_ a specific structure, or if you are roughly _warding_ the front lines of a battlefield). This spell's area is also up to 40 feet in height, so flying creatures benefit as long as they are in this area of effect.Creatures and objects in the warded area gain _[[universal monster rules/Damage Reduction|damage reduction]]_ 20/magic against ranged weapons. Once the spell has prevented a total of 20 points of damage per caster level (maximum 200 points) to creatures and objects in the area, it is discharged.

 #### Other
**Source:** People of the Wastes
**Rating:** 3
