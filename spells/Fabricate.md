# <font style='color:blue'>Fabricate</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _psychic_ 5, _occultist_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** see text
 **Components:** V, S, M (the original material, which costs the same amount as the raw materials required to craft the item to be created) {the original material, which costs the same amount as the raw materials required to craft the item to be created}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** up to 10 cu. ft./level; see text
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You convert material of one sort into a product that is of the same material. Creatures or magic items cannot be created or transmuted by the _[[spells/Fabricate|fabricate]]_ spell. The quality of items made by this spell is commensurate with the quality of material used as the basis for the new fabrication. If you work with a mineral, the target is reduced to 1 cubic foot per level instead of 10 cubic feet. You must make an appropriate Craft check to _fabricate_ articles requiring a high degree of craftsmanship. Casting requires 1 round per 10 cubic feet of material to be affected by the spell.
