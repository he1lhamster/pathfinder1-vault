# <font style='color:orange'>Instant Summons, Greater</font> 
**School:** conjuration (summoning) 
_arcanist_ 8, _wizard_ 8, _sorcerer_ 8, _witch_ 8, _psychic_ 8, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (sapphires worth 1,000 gp each) {sapphires worth 1,000 gp each}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** up to one object per 3 caster levels, each weighing 10 lbs. or less whose longest dimension is 6 ft. or less
**Range:** see text
**Duration:** permanent or until discharged
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Instant Summons|instant summons]]_, except that you can target multiple objects. You must use a separate sapphire worth 1,000 gp for each one. For each item you target, you can touch a creature, granting that creature the ability to speak the special word for that item (each item has its own special word) while crushing the matched gem to call the item to hand. Only you or the touched creature can activate the gem or see the _[[spells/Arcane Mark|arcane mark]]_ upon it.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Intrigue
**Rating:** 2
