# <font style='color:green'>Whip Of Ants</font> 
**School:** conjuration (summoning) 
_arcanist_ 6, _wizard_ 6, _sorcerer_ 6, _witch_ 6, _bard_ 6, _skald_ 6, _summoner_ 6, _unsummoner_ 6, _druid_ 6, _hunter_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M/DF (a square of red cloth) {a square of red cloth}
 #### Effects
**Range:** 0 ft.
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D (see text)
**Effect:** whiplike swarm
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Whip Of Spiders|whip of spiders]]_, except it summons army ants, and any creature you strike with the _[[items/Weapon/Whip|whip]]_ takes swarm damage as if it were attacked by an army ant swarm (3d6 points of damage plus poison and _[[universal monster rules/Distraction|distraction]]_, Pathfinder RPG Bestiary 16). If you transform the _whip_ into an army ant swarm, it has 16 hit points.
