# <font style='color:green'>Moonrise Arrow</font> 
**School:** evocation [light] 
_magus_ 3, _cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, _inquisitor_ 3, _paladin_ 3, _occultist_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** up to 50 pieces of ammunition, all of which must be together at the time of casting
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates (harmless, object); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless, object); 
 #### Description
You cause ammunition, including shuriken, to glow with faint silvery light. The ammunition counts as magic and silver for the purpose of overcoming _[[universal monster rules/Damage Reduction|damage reduction]]_, in addition to the properties of any other special material the ammunition has. _[[universal monster rules/Incorporeal|Incorporeal]]_ undead and shapechangers take an additional 1d8 points of damage from such ammunition. The targeted ammunition sheds dim light in a 5-foot radius for 1 round after it is fired or thrown.

 #### Other
**Source:** AP 134
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
