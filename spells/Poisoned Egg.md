# <font style='color:red'>Poisoned Egg</font> 
**School:** transmutation [poison] 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _witch_ 1, _magus_ 1, _bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, _shaman_ 1, _cleric_ 1, _oracle_ 1, _warpriest_ 1, _inquisitor_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one egg
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** none (object); **Spell Resistance:** no (object); 
 #### Description
You transform the contents of a normal egg into a single dose of _[[poisons/Small Centipede Poison|small centipede poison]]_ (injury; save DC 11; frequency 1/round for 4 rounds; effect 1 Dex; cure 1 save). The poison reverts to a normal egg at the end of the spell's duration (the reverted egg substance is harmless unless the poisoned creature is vulnerable to eggs). The egg may be raw or cooked but must be whole and not empty when you cast the spell. When applying the _[[spells/Poisoned Egg|poisoned egg]]_'s contents to a weapon, the wielder has no chance of _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Poisoning|poisoning]]_ herself, as though she had the poison use class feature.

 #### Other
**Source:** Inner Sea Gods
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
