# <font style='color:orange'>Holy Whisper</font> 
**School:** evocation [good] 
_paladin_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Area:** cone-shaped burst
**Range:** 30 ft.
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates; see text; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You whisper a single word in the primordial language of good that is anathema to the minions of evil and strengthens the resolve of good creatures. Evil creatures within the burst must make a Fortitude saving throw or become _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_ for 1 round/ level. Evil outsiders with the evil subtype, evil-aligned dragons, and undead in the burst also take 2d8 points of damage if they fail their saves. Good-aligned creatures in the burst gain a +2 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ bonus on attack and damage rolls for 1 round.

 #### Other
**Source:** APG
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
