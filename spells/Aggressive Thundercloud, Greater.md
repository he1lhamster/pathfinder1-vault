# <font style='color:green'>Aggressive Thundercloud, Greater</font> 
**School:** evocation [electricity, sonic] 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 4, _magus_ 4, _druid_ 4, _hunter_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M/DF (a piece of a tree struck by lightning) {a piece of a tree struck by lightning}
 #### Effects
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Effect:** 5-ft.-diameter sphere
**Saving Throw:** Reflex negates and Fortitude negates (see text); **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Aggressive Thundercloud|aggressive thundercloud]]_, except it deals 6d6 points of electricity damage to any creature it strikes. The first creature damaged by the cloud is also _[[conditions/Stunned|stunned]]_ for 1 round (Fortitude negates); this is a sonic effect.
