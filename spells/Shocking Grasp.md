# <font style='color:green'>Shocking Grasp</font> 
**School:** evocation [electricity] 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _magus_ 1, _bloodrager_ 1, _occultist_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature or object touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
Your successful melee touch attack deals 1d6 points of electricity damage per caster level (maximum 5d6). When delivering the _[[spells/Jolt|jolt]]_, you gain a +3 bonus on attack rolls if the opponent is wearing metal armor (or is carrying a metal weapon or is made of metal).
