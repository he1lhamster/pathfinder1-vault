# <font style='color:green'>Charm Person, Mass</font> 
**School:** enchantment (charm) [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _witch_ 5, _bard_ 4, _skald_ 4, _shaman_ 5, _psychic_ 5, _mesmerist_ 4, _occultist_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one or more humanoid creatures, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 hour/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions like _[[spells/Charm Person|charm person]]_, except that mass _charm person_ affects a number of humanoid creatures whose combined Hit Dice don't exceed twice your level. If there are more potential targets than you can affect, you choose them one at a time until you reach the limit of HD you can affect. If you cast mass _charm person_ on only one creature, you ignore the spell's HD limit.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Intrigue
**Rating:** 3
