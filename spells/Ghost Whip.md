# <font style='color:green'>Ghost Whip</font>

_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _witch_ 2, _magus_ 2, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, _inquisitor_ 2, _psychic_ 2, _occultist_ 2, _spiritualist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Range:** 0 ft.
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Effect:** whip of flexible ectoplasm
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
A white, 15-foot-long lash of ghostly evanescence appears from your hand. This weapon is treated as a _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Ghost Touch|ghost touch]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Whip|whip]]_ with no enhancement bonus. You can wield it as a _whip_ as if you were proficient. Attacks with a _[[spells/Ghost Whip|ghost whip]]_ are resolved as _[[universal monster rules/Incorporeal|incorporeal]]_ touch attacks. The _whip_ affects only creatures you attack with it, passing through objects and other creatures in its path and thereby allowing you to ignore cover between you and your target. When a _ghost whip_ attack passes through total cover, the target is treated as having total concealment (50% miss chance). Against _incorporeal_ and undead creatures, a _ghost whip_ deals lethal damage and can be used to perform drag or reposition combat maneuvers (in addition to a _whip_'s normal disarm and _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_ maneuvers). A _ghost whip_ cannot be disarmed or sundered.
