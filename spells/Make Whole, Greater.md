# <font style='color:blue'>Make Whole, Greater</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _cleric_ 4, _oracle_ 4, _warpriest_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one object of up to 5 lb./level
**Range:** 10 ft.
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless, object); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless, object); 
 #### Description
This spell repairs 1d6 points of damage plus 1 point per level when cast on a construct creature (maximum 10d6+10).
  Greater _[[spells/Make Whole|make whole]]_ can fix destroyed magic items or technological items (items at 0 hit points or fewer), and restores the magic properties of the item if your caster level at least equal to that of the item. This spell otherwise functions as _make whole_.

 #### Other
**Source:** Technology Guide
**Rating:** 4
