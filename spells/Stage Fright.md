# <font style='color:green'>Stage Fright</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [emotion, fear, mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 3, _bard_ 3, _skald_ 3, _inquisitor_ 3, _antipaladin_ 3, _psychic_ 3, _mesmerist_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** 1 creature/level, no two of which may be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You fill your targets with the sudden _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_ of failure. A creature that fails its saving throw takes a -4 penalty on ability checks, skill checks, and any checks that require concentration (such as casting a spell in difficult circumstances or operating a complex device).

 #### Other
**Source:** Arcane Anthology
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
