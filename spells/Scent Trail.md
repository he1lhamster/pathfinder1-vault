# <font style='color:red'>Scent Trail</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a queen ant) {a queen ant}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature/level, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 hour/level
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
You, or a willing creature you touch, lay down a trail of scents that only the creatures you designate at the time of can detect. These creatures find it very easy to follow this trail.
  They gain a +20 competence bonus on any Survival checks made for the purpose of tracking the creature laying this trail.
  Subject creatures with the _[[universal monster rules/Scent|scent]]_ special ability gain a +10 competence bonus on Wisdom or Survival checks to follow the trail.
  In addition, you can leave _scent_ messages along the trail.
  Each word or emotion in this _[[spells/Message|message]]_ requires spending a move action in the same spot. Subject creatures seeking to understand this _message_ must make another DC 20 Survival check, with a -1 penalty for every word or emotion in the _message_, in order to understand it. Creatures cannot benefit from this smell if they cannot use, or do not have, a sense of smell. Creatures can use this spell to backtrack through a dungeon, _[[spells/Maze|maze]]_, or similar areas even if the creature laying down the trail crossed over the spot multiple times.
