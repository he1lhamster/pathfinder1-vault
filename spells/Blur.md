 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 min./level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
The subject's outline appears blurred, shifting, and wavering. This distortion grants the subject concealment (20% miss chance).
  A _[[spells/See Invisibility|see invisibility]]_ spell does not counteract the _[[spells/Blur|blur]]_ effect, but a _[[spells/True Seeing|true seeing]]_ spell does.
  Opponents that cannot see the subject ignore the spell's effect (though fighting an _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Unseen|unseen]]_ opponent carries penalties of its own).
