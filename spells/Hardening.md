# <font style='color:orange'>Hardening</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 6, _wizard_ 6, _sorcerer_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** item (volume no greater than 10 cubic feet/level)
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** permanent
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes (object); 
 #### Description
This spell increases the hardness of materials by 1 point per 2 caster levels. The hardening spell does not in any way affect _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ to other forms of _[[spells/Transformation|transformation]]_.This spell affects up to 10 cubic feet per level. If cast upon a metal or mineral, the spell affects 1 cubic foot per level.

 #### Other
