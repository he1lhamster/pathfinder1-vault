# <font style='color:red'>Summon Minor Ally</font> 
**School:** conjuration (summoning) 
_druid_ 1, _hunter_ 1, _ranger_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Effect:** 1d3 summoned creatures
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Summon Nature's Ally I|summon nature's ally I]]_, except you can _[[universal monster rules/Summon|summon]]_ 1d3 Tiny or smaller animals, such as bats, lizards, monkeys, rats, ravens, toads, or weasels. The summoned animals must all be the same type of creature.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Magic
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
