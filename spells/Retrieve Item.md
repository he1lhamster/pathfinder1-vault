# <font style='color:green'>Retrieve Item</font> 
**School:** conjuration (calling) 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 full round
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** an unattended object weighing 1 lb. or less whose longest dimension is 6 inches or less
**Range:** long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
**Duration:** permanent until discharged
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You call a specific nonliving item directly to your hand from a nearby location. First, you must cast the spell on the item while holding it. Thereafter, you can _[[universal monster rules/Summon|summon]]_ the item by speaking a special word (set by you when the spell is cast) and snapping your fingers. The item appears instantly in your hand.
  If the item is in the _[[spells/Possession|possession]]_ of another creature, the spell fails.

 #### Other
**Source:** Classic Treasures
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
