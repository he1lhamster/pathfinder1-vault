**School:** divination 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 3, _shaman_ 3, _druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, _ranger_ 2, _occultist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S, M 
 #### Effects
**Range:** close (25 feet + 5 feet/2 levels)
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level (D)
**Effect:** seed spies/4 levels (1)
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Insect Spies|insect spies]]_, except it creates wafting seeds with magical intellect instead of insects. The seeds can hear but not see. They fly at a speed of 10 feet (good maneuverability), but they can't crawl or _[[universal monster rules/Climb|climb]]_, although they can cling to rough, soft, or _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sticky|sticky]]_ creatures or surfaces.
