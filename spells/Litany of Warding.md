# <font style='color:green'>Litany of Warding</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_inquisitor_ 3, _antipaladin_ 2, _paladin_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 swift action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 round

 #### Description
With this litany, you become more aware of your opponents. You can make two additional attacks of opportunity this round. Furthermore, you gain a +2 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ bonus to AC against attacks of opportunity. While subject to this spell, the target cannot be the target of another spell that has the word "litany" in the title.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Combat
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
