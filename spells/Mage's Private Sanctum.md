# <font style='color:green'>Mage's Private Sanctum</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _psychic_ 5, _mesmerist_ 5, _spiritualist_ 5, _medium_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 10 minutes
 **Components:** V, S, M (a sheet of lead, a piece of glass, a wad of cotton, and powdered chrysolite) {a sheet of lead, a piece of glass, a wad of cotton, and powdered chrysolite}
 #### Effects
**Area:** 30-ft. cube/level (S)
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 24 hours (D)
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell ensures privacy. Anyone looking into the area from outside sees only a dark, foggy mass. _[[spells/Darkvision|Darkvision]]_ cannot penetrate it. No sounds, no matter how loud, can escape the area, so nobody can eavesdrop from outside. Those inside can see out normally.
  _[[spells/Divination|Divination]]_ (_[[spells/Scrying|scrying]]_) spells cannot perceive anything within the area, and those within are immune to _[[spells/Detect Thoughts|detect thoughts]]_. The ward prevents speech between those inside and those outside (because it blocks sound), but it does not prevent other communication, such as a _[[spells/Sending|sending]]_ or _[[spells/Message|message]]_ spell, or telepathic communication, such as that between a _[[classes/Wizard|wizard]]_ and her familiar.
  The spell does not prevent creatures or objects from moving into and out of the area.
  _[[spells/Mage's Private Sanctum|Mage's private sanctum]]_ can be made permanent with a _[[spells/Permanency|permanency]]_ spell.

 #### Other
**Source:** PFRPG Core
**Rating:** 3
**Permanency:** Yes
