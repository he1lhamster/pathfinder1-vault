# <font style='color:blue'>Flesh to Stone</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 6, _wizard_ 6, _sorcerer_ 6, _witch_ 6, _magus_ 6, _shaman_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (lime, water, and earth) {lime, water, and earth}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
The subject, along with all its carried gear, turns into a mindless, inert _[[spells/Statue|statue]]_. If the _statue_ resulting from this spell is _[[conditions/Broken|broken]]_ or damaged, the subject (if ever returned to its original state) has similar damage or deformities. The creature is not dead, but it does not seem to be alive either when viewed with spells such as _[[spells/Deathwatch|deathwatch]]_.
  Only creatures made of flesh are affected by this spell.
