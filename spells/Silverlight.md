# <font style='color:red'>Silverlight</font> 
**School:** evocation 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _bard_ 3, _skald_ 3, _druid_ 3, _hunter_ 3, _cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, _paladin_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** object touched (1)
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude partial (see text); **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
As _[[spells/Daylight|daylight]]_, except the object touched sheds bright silvery light in a 20-foot radius. In addition to providing illumination, the area of bright light is painful to creatures that are susceptible to alchemical silver. A creature with DR/silver that begins its turn within the light of this spell is automatically _[[conditions/Dazzled|dazzled]]_ and becomes _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_ unless it succeeds on a Fortitude save. These effects disappear 1 round after the target leaves the silverlight. A creature that leaves the area and then returns must make a new saving throw. This spell provides dim light up to 40 feet away, but only creatures within 20 feet are subject to the above e!ects.
