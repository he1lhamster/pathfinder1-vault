# <font style='color:green'>Veil of Positive Energy</font> 
**School:** abjuration [good] 
_paladin_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you or all creatures within 5 ft.; see text
**Range:** personal or 5 ft.; see text
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level (D)

 #### Description
You surround yourself with a _[[spells/Veil of Positive Energy|veil of positive energy]]_, making it harder for undead creatures to _[[spells/Harm|harm]]_ you. When under the effect of this spell, you gain a +2 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ bonus to AC and a+2 _sacred_ bonus on saves. Both of these bonuses apply only against attacks or effects created by undead creatures. You can dismiss this spell before its normal duration as a swift action on your turn to deal a number of points of positive energy damage equal to your level to all undead creatures within 5 feet of you.

 #### Other
**Source:** APG

**Potion:** Yes
