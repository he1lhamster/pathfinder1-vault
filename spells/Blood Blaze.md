# <font style='color:red'>Blood Blaze</font> 
**School:** transmutation [fire] 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _witch_ 2, _magus_ 2, _bloodrager_ 2, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, _antipaladin_ 1, _alchemist_ 2, _investigator_ 2, _psychic_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
The target gains a 5-foot-radius aura that causes the blood of creatures in that area to ignite upon contact with air. Any creature (including the spell's target) within the aura that takes at least 5 points of piercing, slashing, or _[[universal monster rules/Bleed|bleed]]_ damage from a single attack automatically creates a spray of _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Burning|burning]]_ blood. The spray strikes a creature in a randomly determined square adjacent to the injured creature. The spray deals 1d6 points of fire damage to any creature in that square, and 1 point of splash damage to all creatures within 5 feet of the spray's target, including the target of this spell. A creature can only create one spray of _burning_ blood per round. Creatures that do not have blood (including oozes and most constructs and undead) do not create blood sprays when attacked.
