**School:** conjuration (calling) 
_shaman_ 9, _druid_ 9, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 10 minutes
 **Components:** V, S, M (offerings worth 2,500 gp plus payment), DF {offerings worth 2,500 gp plus payment}
 #### Effects
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Effect:** up to three called fey, totaling no more than 18 Hit Dice, no two of which can appear more than 30 ft. apart
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
