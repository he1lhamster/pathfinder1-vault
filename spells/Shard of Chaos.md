# <font style='color:orange'>Shard of Chaos</font> 
**School:** evocation [chaos] 
_cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous (1d6 rounds)
**Effect:** dart-shaped projectile of chaotic energy
**Saving Throw:** Will partial (see text); **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You hurl a multicolored shard of congealed chaos from your holy symbol, affecting any one target in range as a ranged touch attack.
  A lawful creature struck by the shard takes 1d8 points of damage per two caster levels (maximum 5d8). A lawful outsider instead takes 1d6 points of damage per caster level (maximum 10d6) and is slowed (as _[[spells/Slow|slow]]_) for 1 round. A successful Will save reduces the damage to half and negates the _slow_ effect. The bolt has no effect on chaotic creatures. This spell deals only half damage to creatures that are neither lawful nor chaotic, and they are not slowed.

 #### Other
