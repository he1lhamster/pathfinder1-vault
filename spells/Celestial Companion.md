# <font style='color:green'>Celestial Companion</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _witch_ 2, _summoner_ 1, _unsummoner_ 1, _shaman_ 2, _druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, _ranger_ 2, _paladin_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF/M 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** animal companion, eidolon, familiar, or mount
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
You bestow _[[feats/Divine Protection|divine protection]]_ on your companion. The target gains _[[universal monster rules/Spell Resistance|spell resistance]]_ equal to 5 plus half your caster level against spells and spell-like effects produced by evil creatures. The target also gains a +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ bonus on saving throws and DR 5/evil or silver. Creatures under the effects of this spell often take on a celestial appearance, such as golden feathers or a _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Radiant|radiant]]_ aura, but they are not truly celestial creatures.

 #### Other
**Source:** Plane-Hopper's Handbook
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
