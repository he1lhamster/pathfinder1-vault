# <font style='color:red'>Abyssal Vermin</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 4, _cleric_ 4, _oracle_ 4, _warpriest_ 4, _antipaladin_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one vermin or vermin swarm
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates (see text); **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You infuse the target with power from the Abyss, granting it the fiendish creature simple template. The swarm gains DR 5/ good if it has at least 5 Hit Dice, or DR 10/good it if has at least 11 HD. It gains cold _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ 5 and fire _resistance_ 5 if it has at least 5 HD, or cold _resistance_ 10 and fire _resistance_ 10 if it has at least 11 HD. It gains _[[universal monster rules/Spell Resistance|spell resistance]]_ equal to its CR + 6. Once per day, it can smite good (as a swift action against a good opponent, the vermin adds its Charisma bonus to its attack rolls and its HD to its damage rolls until the opponent is dead). If the targeted vermin is under your _[[spells/Command|command]]_, it automatically fails its saving throw against this spell. This spell has no effect on vermin that already have the fiendish creature simple template.

 #### Other
**Source:** AP 78
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
