# <font style='color:blue'>Poisonous Cloud</font> 
**School:** conjuration (creation) 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 4, _magus_ 4, _summoner_ 4, _unsummoner_ 4, _bloodrager_ 4, _shaman_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M 
 #### Effects
**Range:** medium (100 feet + 10 feet/level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Effect:** cloud spreads in 20-foot radius, 20 feet high
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates (see text); **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
Poisonous cloud creates a bank of fog like that created by _[[spells/Fog Cloud|fog cloud]]_, except that the vapors are _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Toxic|toxic]]_. The cloud deals 1d2 points of Constitution damage per round to living creatures that fail their Fortitude saves. A creature that succeeds at its save but remains in the cloud must continue to attempt a new saving throw each round on your turn.Poisonous cloud can be made permanent with a _[[spells/Permanency|permanency]]_ spell cast by a caster of 13th level or higher, at a cost of 12,500 gp. A permanent poisonous cloud dispersed by wind re-forms in 10 minutes.
