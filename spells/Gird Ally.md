# <font style='color:green'>Gird Ally</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _witch_ 2, _summoner_ 2, _druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one summoned creature you control/level, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You create a magical field around summoned creatures you control that deflects attacks made against them. The targets gain a _[[spells/Deflection|deflection]]_ bonus to their AC equal to 1 + 1 for every 6 caster levels you possess (maximum +4 _deflection_ bonus at 18th level). A _[[classes/Summoner|summoner]]_ can target his eidolon with this spell.

 #### Other
