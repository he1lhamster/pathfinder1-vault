# <font style='color:orange'>Discovery Torch</font> 
**School:** evocation [light] 
_bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, _inquisitor_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** object touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
An object you touch emanates a 20-foot radius of bright light. The effect looks like a regular flame but creates no _[[universal monster rules/Heat|heat]]_ and uses no oxygen. Allies within the area of this light gain a +2 enhancement bonus on Perception and Sense Motive checks, as well as on Knowledge checks to _[[spells/Identify|identify]]_ monsters that are also within the area and their special powers and _[[universal monster rules/Vulnerabilities|vulnerabilities]]_. When an _[[classes/Inquisitor|inquisitor]]_ casts this spell, the light emanation doubles (40-ft.-radius emanation) while that _inquisitor_ has a judgment active. Light spells counter and dispel _[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_ spells of an equal or lower level.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Combat
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
