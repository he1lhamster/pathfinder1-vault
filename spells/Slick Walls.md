# <font style='color:orange'>Slick Walls</font> 
**School:** conjuration (creation) 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _magus_ 2, _bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _summoner_ 2, _unsummoner_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M 
 #### Effects
**Area:** 30-foot emanation centered on a point in space
**Range:** close (25 feet + 5 feet/2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You coat all walls and ceilings within the spell's area with a _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Slick|slick]]_ substance. The DC of all _[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_ checks for the affected surfaces increases by twice your caster level (maximum +20). Creatures cannot take 10 on _Climb_ checks while scaling the affected surfaces, even if they have a _climb_ speed or other ability that would normally allow taking 10. This spell has no effect on magical walls or surfaces, such as the sides of a hole created with create pitAPG.
