# <font style='color:green'>Comprehend Languages</font> 
**School:** divination 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _witch_ 1, _bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, _shaman_ 1, _cleric_ 1, _oracle_ 1, _warpriest_ 1, _inquisitor_ 1, _alchemist_ 1, _investigator_ 1, _psychic_ 1, _mesmerist_ 1, _occultist_ 1, _spiritualist_ 1, _medium_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M/DF (pinch of soot and salt) {pinch of soot and salt}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 10 min./level

 #### Description
You can understand the spoken words of creatures or read otherwise incomprehensible written messages. The ability to read does not necessarily impart insight into the material, merely its literal meaning. The spell enables you to understand or read an _[[monsters/Unknown|unknown]]_ language, not speak or write it.
  Written material can be read at the rate of one page (250 words) per minute. Magical writing cannot be read, though the spell reveals that it is magical. This spell can be foiled by certain _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Warding|warding]]_ magic (such as the _[[spells/Secret Page|secret page]]_ and _[[spells/Illusory Script|illusory script]]_ spells). It does not decipher codes or reveal messages _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Concealed|concealed]]_ in otherwise normal text.
  _[[spells/Comprehend Languages|Comprehend languages]]_ can be made permanent with a _[[spells/Permanency|permanency]]_ spell.
