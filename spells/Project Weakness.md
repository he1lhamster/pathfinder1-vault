# <font style='color:red'>Project Weakness</font> 
**School:** necromancy [curse, evil] 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 3, _cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** living creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** permanent
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You curse the target with the weaknesses of your kind of vampirism. The creature reacts to _[[items/Mundane/Garlic|garlic]]_, mirrors, ringing bells, sunlight, and so on as if it were a _[[monsters/Vampire|vampire]]_ of the same type as you. This cannot kill the target; anything that would kill it (such as a lengthy exposure to sunlight if you are a moroi _vampire_) renders it _[[conditions/Helpless|helpless]]_ until the curse or the harmful effect is removed. The target gains none of the benefits of being a _vampire_ (such as _[[universal monster rules/Fast Healing|fast healing]]_ or requiring special ways to be permanently killed), only the penalties.

 #### Other
**Source:** Blood Of The Night
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
