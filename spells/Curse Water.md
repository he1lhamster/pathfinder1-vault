# <font style='color:red'>Curse Water</font> 
**School:** necromancy [evil] 
_cleric_ 1, _oracle_ 1, _warpriest_ 1, _inquisitor_ 1, _antipaladin_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 minute
 **Components:** V, S, M (5 lbs. of powdered silver worth 25 gp) 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** flask of water touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (object); **Spell Resistance:** yes (object); 
 #### Description
This spell imbues a _[[items/Mundane/Flask|flask]]_ (1 pint) of water with negative energy, turning it into _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Unholy|unholy]]_ water (see Equipment). _Unholy_ water damages good outsiders the way _[[items/Mundane/Holy water|holy water]]_ damages undead and evil outsiders.
