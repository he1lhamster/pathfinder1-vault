# <font style='color:blue'>Arcane Concordance</font> 
**School:** evocation 
_bard_ 3, _skald_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a spent wand) {a spent wand}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Area:** 10-ft.-radius emanation centered on you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
A shimmering, blue and gold _[[items/Weapon/Radiance|radiance]]_ surrounds you, enhancing arcane spells cast by your allies within its area. Any arcane spell cast by a creature within the area gains a +1 enhancement bonus to the DC of any saving throws against the spell, and can be cast as if one of the following metamagic feats was applied to it (without increasing the spell level or casting time): _[[feats/Enlarge Spell|Enlarge Spell]]_, _[[feats/Extend Spell|Extend Spell]]_, _[[feats/Silent Spell|Silent Spell]]_, or _[[feats/Still Spell|Still Spell]]_ (you choose the metamagic feat when you cast _[[spells/Arcane Concordance|arcane concordance]]_).

 #### Other
**Source:** APG
**Rating:** 4
**Potion:** Yes
