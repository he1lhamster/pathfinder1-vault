# <font style='color:green'>Rend Body III</font> 
**School:** evocation 
_psychic_ 7, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 full-round action
 **Components:** S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude partial (see text); **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as per _[[spells/Rend Body I|rend body I]]_, but the target takes 1d8 points of damage per caster level you have (maximum 15d8), and if a limb is ripped from its body, the target is _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_ for as long as the _[[universal monster rules/Bleed|bleed]]_ damage persists. This spell can be undercast.

 #### Other
**Source:** Psychic Anthology
**Rating:** 3
