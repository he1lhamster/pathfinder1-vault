# <font style='color:orange'>Solidify Earth</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _shaman_ 2, _druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M, DF 
 #### Effects
**Area:** 10-foot cubes/level (2) (S)
**Range:** close (25 feet + 5 feet/2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** Reflex negates (see text); **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
Stone, dirt, and other types of earth within the area become more tightly packed. Burrowing creature (including those using _[[universal monster rules/Earth Glide|earth glide]]_) treat the area as difficult terrain. Any creature that begins its turn burrowing in the affected area becomes _[[conditions/Entangled|entangled]]_ for 1 round (Reflex negates). _[[universal monster rules/Tremorsense|Tremorsense]]_ and similar senses are ineffective within the affected area. Creatures with the earth mastery special ability (such as earth elementals) double their bonus on attack and damage rolls so long as both they and their targets are touching the area affected by this spell.
