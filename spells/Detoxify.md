# <font style='color:green'>Detoxify</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_witch_ 3, _shaman_ 3, _druid_ 3, _hunter_ 2, _ranger_ 2, _cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, _inquisitor_ 3, _paladin_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a dandelion stem) {a dandelion stem}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You remove a creature's ability to poison others, whether inherent or via poisoned weapons. Whenever an affected creature would inflict poison with an attack, spell, or other method (including auras and other constant methods), that poison is automatically neutralized. This does not grant the subject of the spell any _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ to poison itself.
