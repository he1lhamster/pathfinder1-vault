# <font style='color:red'>Suffocation, Instant</font> 
**School:** necromancy 

#### Casting
**Casting Time:** standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched, living (1)
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude partial; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions like _[[spells/Slay Living|slay living]]_, and kills by immediate _[[spells/Suffocation|suffocation]]_. Creatures that do not need to breathe are immune to this spell. The target is considered to have taken a dose of _[[items/Mundane/Pesh|pesh]]_ for the purpose of addiction and withdrawal effects.

 #### Other
**Source:** Dark Markets: A Guide to Katapesh
