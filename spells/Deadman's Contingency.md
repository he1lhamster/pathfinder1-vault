# <font style='color:red'>Deadman's Contingency</font> 
**School:** evocation 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 4, _bard_ 4, _skald_ 4, _cleric_ 4, _oracle_ 4, _warpriest_ 4, _psychic_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 10 minutes or more; see text
 **Components:** V, S, M (a scorpion's tail), F (ivory statuette of you worth 1,500 gp) {ivory statuette of you worth 1,500 gp}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** up to 1 hour/level plus 1d6 rounds; see text (D)

 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Contingency|contingency]]_, except as noted above. This spell also only comes into effect after your death and works only with certain spells. The companion spell triggers 1d6 rounds after your death. All decisions made involving the companion spell must be made when _[[spells/Deadman's Contingency|deadman's contingency]]_ is cast (including messages and recipients for spells like _[[spells/Magic Mouth|magic mouth]]_ or _[[spells/Sending|sending]]_). If the spell targets an object or appears in a certain location, it must target or be centered on your corpse. The following spells can be companion spells for _deadman's contingency_: _[[spells/Animate Dead|animate dead]]_ (animating your corpse as an uncontrolled skeleton or zombie), _[[spells/Disintegrate|disintegrate]]_, _[[spells/Fireball|fireball]]_, _[[spells/Gentle Repose|gentle repose]]_, _magic mouth_, _[[spells/Major Image|major image]]_ (with a duration of 3 rounds), _[[spells/Permanent Image|permanent image]]_, _sending_, _[[spells/Stinking Cloud|stinking cloud]]_, and _[[spells/Teleport Object|teleport object]]_.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Intrigue
**Rating:** 1
