# <font style='color:orange'>Magic Fang</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_summoner_ 1, _unsummoner_ 1, _druid_ 1, _hunter_ 1, _ranger_ 1, _spiritualist_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** living creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 min./level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
_[[spells/Magic Fang|Magic fang]]_ gives one natural weapon or unarmed strike of the subject a +1 enhancement bonus on attack and damage rolls. The spell can affect a slam attack, fist, bite, or other natural weapon.
  The spell does not change an unarmed strike's damage from nonlethal damage to lethal damage.
  _Magic fang_ can be made permanent with a _[[spells/Permanency|permanency]]_ spell.
