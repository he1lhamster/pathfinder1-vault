# <font style='color:red'>Fabricate Bullets</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S, M (1 pound of lead or other soft metal worth 2 gp) {1 pound of lead or other soft metal worth 2 gp}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** 1 pound of soft metal
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You convert 1 pound of lead into bullets. When you cast this spell, you decide whether you create normal _[[items/Weapon/Sling|sling]]_ bullets, firearm bullets, or firearm pellets. The spell creates two _sling_ bullets, 30 firearm bullets, or 10 uses of pellets.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Combat
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
