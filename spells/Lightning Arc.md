# <font style='color:green'>Lightning Arc</font> 
**School:** evocation [electricity] 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (fur and two glass figurines) {fur and two glass figurines}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** two creatures or objects which must be no more than 60 ft. apart
**Range:** long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Reflex half; **Spell Resistance:** yes (see text); 
 #### Description
You generate an arc of lightning between two targets. The lightning deals 1d6 points of electricity damage per caster level (maximum 15d6) to both targets and any creatures in a line connecting them. The spell fails if there is no line of effect between the targets. _[[spells/Lightning Arc|Lightning arc]]_ sets fire to combustibles and damages objects in its path. It can melt metals that have a low melting point, such as lead, gold, copper, silver, or bronze.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Magic
**Rating:** 3
