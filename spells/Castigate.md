# <font style='color:green'>Castigate</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [fear, language-dependent, mind-affecting, emotion] 
_inquisitor_ 2, _mesmerist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one living creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level or 1 round; see text
**Saving Throw:** Will partial; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You compel the target to beg for forgiveness. On a failed save, the target cowers with _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_. On a successful save, it is _[[conditions/Shaken|shaken]]_ for 1 round. Each round on its turn, a _[[conditions/Cowering|cowering]]_ subject may attempt a new save to end the effect. A creature who worships the same god as you takes a -2 penalty on its saving throw.
