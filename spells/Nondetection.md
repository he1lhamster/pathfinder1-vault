# <font style='color:orange'>Nondetection</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _summoner_ 3, _unsummoner_ 3, _hunter_ 4, _ranger_ 4, _inquisitor_ 3, _antipaladin_ 3, _alchemist_ 3, _investigator_ 3, _psychic_ 3, _mesmerist_ 3, _occultist_ 3, _spiritualist_ 3, _medium_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (diamond dust worth 50 gp) {diamond dust worth 50 gp}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature or object touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 hour/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless, object); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless, object); 
 #### Description
The warded creature or object becomes difficult to detect by _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_ spells such as clairaudience/clairvoyance, _[[spells/Locate Object|locate object]]_, and detect spells. _[[spells/Nondetection|Nondetection]]_ also prevents location by such magic items as crystal balls. If a _divination_ is attempted against the warded creature or item, the caster of the _divination_ must succeed on a caster level check (1d20 + caster level) against a DC of 11 + the caster level of the spellcaster who cast _nondetection_. If you cast _nondetection_ on yourself or on an item currently in your _[[spells/Possession|possession]]_, the DC is 15 + your caster level.
  If cast on a creature, _nondetection_ wards the creature's gear as well as the creature itself.
