# <font style='color:red'>Shapechanger's Gift, Greater</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_arcanist_ 7, _wizard_ 7, _sorcerer_ 7, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a piece of the creature whose form you plan to grant) {a piece of the creature whose form you plan to grant}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** willing living creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 hour/level
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Shapechanger's Gift|shapechanger's gift]]_ except the chosen form can be an animal (as per _[[spells/Beast Shape I|beast shape I]]_), elemental (as per _[[spells/Elemental Body I|elemental body I]]_), fey (as per _[[spells/Fey Form I|fey form I]]_*), humanoid (as per _[[spells/Alter Self|alter self]]_), monstrous humanoid (as per _[[spells/Monstrous Physique I|monstrous physique I]]_), or vermin (as per _[[spells/Vermin Shape I|vermin shape I]]_) and the chosen form can have a fly speed.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Wilderness
**Rating:** 1
