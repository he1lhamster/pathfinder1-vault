# <font style='color:orange'>Banshee Blast</font> 
**School:** necromancy [death, fear, mind-affecting, sonic] 
_arcanist_ 6, _wizard_ 6, _sorcerer_ 6, _witch_ 6, _spiritualist_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Area:** cone-shaped burst
**Range:** 30 ft.
**Duration:** instantaneous and 1 round/level (see text)
**Saving Throw:** Reflex half and Will negates (see text); **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You create a cone of spectral energy resembling screaming elven ghosts that deals 1d4 points of damage per caster level (maximum 15d4); a successful Reflex save halves this damage. Any creature that fails its Reflex save must succeed at a Will save or become _[[conditions/Panicked|panicked]]_ for 1 round/level.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Class Guide
**Rating:** 2
