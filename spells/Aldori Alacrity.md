# <font style='color:green'>Aldori Alacrity</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_magus_ 2, _bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _alchemist_ 2, _investigator_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (ginger root shavings) {ginger root shavings}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 minute/level

 #### Description
This spell's energy quickens your steps, allowing fancy footwork. You gain a +10-foot enhancement bonus to your speed and a +1 _[[feats/Dodge|dodge]]_ bonus to your AC. These bonuses do not stack with those granted by _[[spells/Haste|haste]]_ or similar effects. While under the effects of _[[spells/Aldori Alacrity|Aldori alacrity]]_, you can take 5-foot steps in difficult terrain.

 #### Other
**Source:** Adventurer's Guide
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
