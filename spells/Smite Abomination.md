# <font style='color:blue'>Smite Abomination</font> 
**School:** evocation 
_cleric_ 5, _oracle_ 5, _warpriest_ 5, _inquisitor_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 round/level

 #### Description
Drawing upon positive energy, you emulate some of a _[[classes/Paladin|paladin]]_'s power to smite undead. Choose one undead creature as your target.
  Your melee attacks against that target gain a bonus equal to your Charisma or Wisdom modifier, whichever is higher, on your attack rolls, and a bonus equal to your caster level on damage rolls. Your melee attacks also bypass the target's _[[universal monster rules/Damage Reduction|damage reduction]]_. These bonuses do not stack with the bonuses from a _paladin_'s smite.

 #### Other
**Source:** Inner Sea Gods
**Rating:** 4
