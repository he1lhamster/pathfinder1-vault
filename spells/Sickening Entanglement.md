# <font style='color:blue'>Sickening Entanglement</font> 
**School:** transmutation [poison] 
_shaman_ 2, _druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, _ranger_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Area:** plants in a 40-ft.-radius spread
**Range:** long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 min./level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Reflex partial and Fortitude negates (see  text); **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Entangle|entangle]]_, except the plants are coated with an irritating _[[items/Weapon/Sap|sap]]_ that creates a painful, itching rash on creatures that touch them. Any creature that enters the area or ends its turn there must succeed at a Fortitude save or be _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_ as long as it remains in the area and for 1d4 rounds afterward. This is a poison effect.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Class Guide
