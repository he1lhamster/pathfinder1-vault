# <font style='color:orange'>Sound Burst</font> 
**School:** evocation [sonic] 
_bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, _occultist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, F/DF (a musical instrument) {a musical instrument}
 #### Effects
**Area:** 10-ft.-radius spread
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude partial; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You blast an area with a tremendous cacophony. Every creature in the area takes 1d8 points of sonic damage and must succeed on a Fortitude save to avoid being _[[conditions/Stunned|stunned]]_ for 1 round. Creatures that cannot hear are not _stunned_ but are still damaged.

 #### Other
**Source:** PFRPG Core
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
