# <font style='color:orange'>Holy Ice Weapon</font> 
**School:** transmutation [cold, good, water] 
_cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, _inquisitor_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a flask of holy water, or 5 pounds of powdered silver worth 25 gp) {a flask of holy water, or 5 pounds of powdered silver worth 25 gp}
 #### Effects
**Range:** 0 ft.
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)
**Effect:** one ice weapon
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You create a masterwork weapon that has the qualities of ice and _[[items/Mundane/Holy water|holy water]]_. This weapon must be a simple weapon or your deity's favored weapon. The weapon deals normal damage for a weapon of that type, plus 1 point of cold damage per caster level (maximum 10). In addition, any creature struck by the weapon is also hit by _holy water_, dealing 1d4 points of damage if the creature is susceptible to _holy water_. If the weapon leaves your hands for more than 1 round, it melts and the spell ends.
  At caster level 6th, the weapon gains the _[[spells/Returning Weapon|returning weapon]]_ special ability when thrown, melting away after an attack and re-forming in your hand just before your next turn.

 #### Other
