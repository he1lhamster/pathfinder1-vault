# <font style='color:blue'>Defending Bone</font> 
**School:** necromancy 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _witch_ 2, _magus_ 2, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, _inquisitor_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, F (a Medium creature's skull or femur), DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 hour/level or until discharged

 #### Description
You animate a bone with necromantic energy, giving it the power to float near your body and interpose itself against physical attacks. This gives you _[[universal monster rules/Damage Reduction|damage reduction]]_ 5/bludgeoning.
  Once the bone has prevented a total of 5 points of damage per caster level (maximum 50 points), it is destroyed and the spell is discharged. This spell has no effect if you have _damage reduction_ from another source.

 #### Other
**Source:** Inner Sea Gods
**Rating:** 4
**Potion:** Yes
