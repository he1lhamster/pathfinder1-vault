**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You cause a living creature that is below 0 hit points but stabilized to resume _[[conditions/Dying|dying]]_. Upon casting this spell, you target a living creature that has -1 or fewer hit points. That creature begins _dying_, taking 1 point of damage per round. The creature can be stabilized later normally. This spell causes a creature that is _dying_ to take 1 point of damage.
