# <font style='color:orange'>Seeming</font> 
**School:** illusion (glamer) 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _bard_ 5, _skald_ 5, _psychic_ 5, _mesmerist_ 5, _occultist_ 5, _medium_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature per two levels, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 12 hours (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates or Will disbelief (if interacted with); **Spell Resistance:** yes or no; see text; 
 #### Description
This spell functions like _[[spells/Disguise Self|disguise self]]_, except that you can change the appearance of other people as well. Affected creatures resume their normal appearances if slain. Unwilling targets can negate the spell's effect on them by making Will saves or with _[[universal monster rules/Spell Resistance|spell resistance]]_.

 #### Other
**Source:** PFRPG Core
**Rating:** 2
