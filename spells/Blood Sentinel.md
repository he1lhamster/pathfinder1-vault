# <font style='color:red'>Blood Sentinel</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _alchemist_ 3, _investigator_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a life-sized animal sculpture of a familiar made of clay, cloth, stone, or wood, plus mandrake root worth 25 gp) {a life-sized animal sculpture of a familiar made of clay, cloth, stone, or wood, plus mandrake root worth 25 gp}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one animal sculpture
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no (harmless); 
 #### Description
After casting this spell, you pour a few drops of your blood and saliva into the mouth of the animal sculpture material component, temporarily giving it life. The animal is bonded to you as if it were a familiar, and it has the abilities of a familiar (use your caster level as your effective _[[classes/Wizard|wizard]]_ level to determine its abilities), but it does not grant you the _[[feats/Alertness|Alertness]]_ feat or the special ability of its animal type. If your _[[spells/Blood Sentinel|blood sentinel]]_ is slain, you take 2 points of Intelligence drain and the _blood sentinel_'s body becomes a destroyed _[[spells/Statue|statue]]_. This spell has no effect if you already have a familiar or another _blood sentinel_.

 #### Other
