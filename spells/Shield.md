 #### Description
_[[spells/Shield|Shield]]_ creates an _[[conditions/Invisible|invisible]]_ _shield_ of force that hovers in front of you. It negates _[[spells/Magic Missile|magic missile]]_ attacks directed at you. The disk also provides a +4 _shield_ bonus to AC. This bonus applies against _[[universal monster rules/Incorporeal|incorporeal]]_ touch attacks, since it is a force effect. The _shield_ has no armor check penalty or arcane spell failure chance.
