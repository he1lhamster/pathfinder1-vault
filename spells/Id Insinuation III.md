# <font style='color:green'>Id Insinuation III</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_psychic_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** three creatures
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** concentration + 1 round
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This functions as _[[spells/Id Insinuation I|id insinuation I]]_, except as noted above. In addition, on the first 2 rounds of the effect, the targets of this spell must roll twice to determine the result of their _[[conditions/Confused|confused]]_ conditions. You select which roll is used. This spell can be undercast.

 #### Other
**Source:** Occult Adventures
**Rating:** 3
