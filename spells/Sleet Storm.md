# <font style='color:blue'>Sleet Storm</font> 
**School:** conjuration (creation) [cold] 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 3, _magus_ 3, _bloodrager_ 3, _shaman_ 3, _druid_ 3, _hunter_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M/DF (dust and water) {dust and water}
 #### Effects
**Area:** cylinder (40-ft. radius, 20 ft. high)
**Range:** long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
Driving sleet blocks all sight (even _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_) within it and causes the ground in the area to be icy. A creature can walk within or through the area of sleet at half normal speed with a DC 10 Acrobatics check. Failure means it can't move in that round, while failure by 5 or more means it falls (see the Acrobatics skill for details).
  The sleet extinguishes torches and small fires.
