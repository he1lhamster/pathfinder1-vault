# <font style='color:green'>Unholy Aura</font> 
**School:** abjuration [evil] 
_cleric_ 8, _oracle_ 8, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, F (a tiny reliquary worth 500 gp) {a tiny reliquary worth 500 gp}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature/level in a 20-ft.-radius burst centered on you
**Range:** 20 ft.
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** see text; **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
A _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Malevolent|malevolent]]_ _[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_ surrounds the subjects, protecting them from attacks, granting them _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ to spells cast by good creatures, and weakening good creatures when they strike the subjects. This abjuration has four effects.
  First, each warded creature gains a +4 _[[spells/Deflection|deflection]]_ bonus to AC and a +4 _resistance_ bonus on saves. Unlike the effect of _[[spells/Protection From Good|protection from good]]_, this benefit applies against all attacks, not just against attacks by good creatures.
  Second, a warded creature gains SR 25 against good spells and spells cast by good creatures.
  Third, the abjuration protects the subjects from _[[spells/Possession|possession]]_ and mental influence, just as _protection from good_ does.
  Finally, if a good creature succeeds on a melee attack against a warded creature, the offending attacker takes 1d6 points of Strength damage (Fortitude negates).
