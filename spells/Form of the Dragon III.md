# <font style='color:blue'>Form of the Dragon III</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_arcanist_ 8, _wizard_ 8, _sorcerer_ 8, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a scale of the dragon type you plan to assume) {a scale of the dragon type you plan to assume}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 min./level (D)
**Saving Throw:** see below; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Form of the Dragon II|form of the dragon II]]_ save that it also allows you to take the form of a Huge chromatic or metallic dragon. You gain the following abilities: a +10 size bonus to Strength, a +8 size bonus to Constitution, a +8 natural armor bonus, fly 120 feet (poor), _[[universal monster rules/Blindsense|blindsense]]_ 60 feet, _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 120 feet, a _[[universal monster rules/Breath Weapon|breath weapon]]_, DR 10/magic, _[[universal monster rules/Frightful Presence|frightful presence]]_ (DC equal to the DC for this spell), and _[[universal monster rules/Immunity|immunity]]_ to one element (of the same type _[[spells/Form of the Dragon I|form of the dragon I]]_ grants _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ to).
  You also gain one bite (2d8), two claws (2d6), two wing attacks (1d8), and one tail slap attack (2d6). You can use the _breath weapon_ as often as you like, but you must wait 1d4 rounds between uses. All breath weapons deal 12d8 points of damage and allow a Reflex save for half damage. Line breath weapons increase to 100-foot lines and cones increase to 50-foot cones.
