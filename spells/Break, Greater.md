# <font style='color:red'>Break, Greater</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 6, _wizard_ 6, _sorcerer_ 6, _bloodrager_ 4, _psychic_ 6, _occultist_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S, M (an unbroken platinum tuning fork worth 100 gp) {an unbroken platinum tuning fork worth 100 gp}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** all Medium or smaller objects in a 30-ft. burst centered on you
**Range:** 30 ft.
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes (object); 
 #### Description
You release a burst of destructive energy. Each _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ or smaller object in the area gains the _[[conditions/Broken|broken]]_ condition unless it succeeds at a Fortitude saving throw. If a _broken_ object fails this save, it is instead destroyed. Magic items can be _broken_ by this spell, but not destroyed. Objects in your _[[spells/Possession|possession]]_ are not immune.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Intrigue
**Rating:** 1
