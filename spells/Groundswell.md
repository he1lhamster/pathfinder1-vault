# <font style='color:green'>Groundswell</font> 
**School:** transmutation [earth] 
_magus_ 2, _druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, _ranger_ 2, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
This spell allows the target to cause the ground to rise up beneath him. As a swift action, the target can cause the ground to rise 5 feet, while all adjacent squares are treated as steep slopes (Core Rulebook 428). The _[[spells/Groundswell|groundswell]]_ precludes flanking from creatures standing at lower elevations than the target. If the target moves after creating a _groundswell_, the ground returns to its normal elevation at the end of his turn; otherwise, it remains in place until the target moves or uses a swift action to return the ground to normal. A _groundswell_ cannot increase elevation of the ground beyond 5 feet.
