# <font style='color:green'>Goodberry</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_shaman_ 1, _druid_ 1, _hunter_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** 2d4 fresh berries touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 day/level
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
Casting _[[spells/Goodberry|goodberry]]_ makes 2d4 freshly picked berries magical. You (as well as any other _[[classes/Druid|druid]]_ of 3rd or higher level) can immediately discern which berries are affected. Each transmuted berry provides nourishment as if it were a normal meal for a _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ creature.
  The berry also cures 1 point of damage when eaten, subject to a maximum of 8 points of such curing in any 24-hour period.
