# <font style='color:orange'>Spit Venom</font> 
**School:** transmutation [poison] 
_witch_ 3, _shaman_ 4, _druid_ 3, _hunter_ 3, _cleric_ 4, _oracle_ 4, _warpriest_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V 
 #### Effects
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous; see text
**Effect:** one stream of venom
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude partial; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You spit a stream of venom at a target using a ranged touch attack. If the venom hits, it causes blindness for 1 round. The target must also save or be poisoned by _[[poisons/Black Adder Venom|black adder venom]]_; the DC in successive rounds of the poison is equal to the spell's DC.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Magic
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
