# <font style='color:blue'>Fog Cloud</font> 
**School:** conjuration (creation) 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _witch_ 2, _magus_ 2, _unsummoner_ 2, _shaman_ 2, _druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft. level)
**Duration:** 10 min./level
**Effect:** fog spreads in 20-ft. radius
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
A bank of fog billows out from the point you designate. The fog obscures all sight, including _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_, beyond 5 feet. A creature within 5 feet has concealment (attacks have a 20% miss chance).
  Creatures farther away have total concealment (50% miss chance, and the attacker can't use sight to locate the target).
  A moderate wind (11+ mph) disperses the fog in 4 rounds; a strong wind (21+ mph) disperses the fog in 1 round.
  The spell does not function _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Underwater|underwater]]_.
