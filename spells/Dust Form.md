# <font style='color:green'>Dust Form</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_witch_ 6, _druid_ 6, _hunter_ 6, _cleric_ 6, _oracle_ 6, _warpriest_ 6, _alchemist_ 5, _investigator_ 5, _psychic_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of dust gathered from a gravestone or sacred shrine) {a pinch of dust gathered from a gravestone or sacred shrine}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 round/level

 #### Description
Upon casting this spell, you keep your relative form, but you and your equipment become composed entirely of dust. While in this _[[spells/Dust Form|dust form]]_, you take no penalties for squeezing, and can move through spaces as if you were a creature three size categories smaller without penalty. You are also considered _[[universal monster rules/Incorporeal|incorporeal]]_, though any nonmagical attack you make deals half damage (50%). Magic attacks are unaffected, and you can still use your magic items and other equipment as normal. If the duration ends in a square that your normal space cannot occupy, you take 3d6 damage and are shunted to the nearest open space that you can normally occupy.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Combat
**Rating:** 3
