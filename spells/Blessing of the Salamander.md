# <font style='color:green'>Blessing of the Salamander</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_druid_ 5, _hunter_ 4, _ranger_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
When you cast this on a creature, its skin turns _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Slick|slick]]_ and clammy and it is able to _[[spells/Regenerate|regenerate]]_ damage each round. While under the effects of the spell, the creature gains _[[universal monster rules/Fast Healing|fast healing]]_ 5, fire _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ 20 and a +2 competence bonus to its Combat Maneuver Defense.

 #### Other
**Source:** APG
**Rating:** 3
