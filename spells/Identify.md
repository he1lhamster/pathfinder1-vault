# <font style='color:orange'>Identify</font> 
**School:** divination 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _witch_ 1, _bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, _summoner_ 1, _unsummoner_ 1, _alchemist_ 1, _investigator_ 1, _psychic_ 1, _occultist_ 1, _spiritualist_ 1, _medium_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (wine stirred with an owl's feather) 
 #### Effects
**Area:** cone-shaped emanation
**Range:** 60 ft.
**Duration:** 3 rounds/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Detect Magic|detect magic]]_, except that it gives you a +10 enhancement bonus on Spellcraft checks made to _[[spells/Identify|identify]]_ the properties and _[[spells/Command|command]]_ words of magic items in your _[[spells/Possession|possession]]_. This spell does not allow you to _identify_ artifacts.
