# <font style='color:orange'>Resize Item</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M 25
 #### Effects
**Targets:** weapon or armor touched (up to 25 pounds/level)
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 24 hours (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (object); **Spell Resistance:** yes (object); 
 #### Description
You alter a _[[spells/Magic Weapon|magic weapon]]_ or suit of armor to be up to two size categories larger or smaller. If the spell on a resized suit of armor ends while the armor is being worn, the armor falls off harmlessly. If the spell would cause an item to grow too large for the area containing it, its growth halts just before that point.Resize item can be made permanent with a _[[spells/Permanency|permanency]]_ spell for 7,500 gp, in which case the affected object is set at one specific new size within two steps of its original size.
