# <font style='color:green'>Seismic Fissure</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_shaman_ 7, _druid_ 7, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Range:** long (400 feet + 40 feet/level)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Reflex negates; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
With a thrust of tectonic energy, you _[[universal monster rules/Split|split]]_ the earth. Each creature standing in the area where the fissure opens must succeed at a Reflex saving throw to avoid falling into the rift. Those standing adjacent to the fissure when it is created must succeed at a Reflex save or be knocked _[[conditions/Prone|prone]]_ by tremors. This spell is effective only when cast on an area of dirt, earth, sand, or stone.

 #### Other
**Source:** Wilderness Origins
