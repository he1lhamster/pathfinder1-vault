# <font style='color:orange'>Hunter's Howl</font> 
**School:** necromancy [fear, mind-affecting, emotion] 
_hunter_ 1, _ranger_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Area:** 20-ft.-radius burst
**Range:** 20 ft.
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** none; 
 #### Description
With a single primal howl, you strike _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_ in your opponents.
  Until the end of the spell's duration, you treat those affected by it as if they were your favored enemy, gaining a +2 bonus on weapon attack and damage rolls against them, and a +2 bonus on Bluff, Knowledge, Perception, Sense Motive, and Survival checks against them. If an affected creature is already one of your favored enemies, it is _[[conditions/Shaken|shaken]]_ instead. This is a mindaffecting _fear_ effect.

 #### Other
**Source:** APG
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
