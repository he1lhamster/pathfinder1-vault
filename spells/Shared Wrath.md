# <font style='color:blue'>Shared Wrath</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting, emotion] 
_inquisitor_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a thorny vine) {a thorny vine}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** up to one creature/level, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 minute
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
This spell functions as wrath, except that you grant multiple creatures the bonuses against your designated foe. All subjects of _[[spells/Shared Wrath|shared wrath]]_ receive their bonuses against the same creature.

 #### Other
**Source:** APG
**Rating:** 4
