# <font style='color:blue'>Placebo Effect</font> 
**School:** illusion (phantasm) [mind-affecting] 
_psychic_ 2, _mesmerist_ 2, _medium_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, M (a sugar cube) {a sugar cube}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** Will disbelief (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** Yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
The subject temporarily ceases to feel the ill effects of a single ongoing affliction or condition from the following list: _[[conditions/Blinded|blinded]]_, cursed, _[[conditions/Dazed|dazed]]_, _[[conditions/Deafened|deafened]]_, diseased, _[[conditions/Fatigued|fatigued]]_, _[[conditions/Frightened|frightened]]_, _[[conditions/Nauseated|nauseated]]_, _[[conditions/Panicked|panicked]]_, _[[conditions/Paralyzed|paralyzed]]_, poisoned, _[[conditions/Shaken|shaken]]_, _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_, _[[conditions/Staggered|staggered]]_, or _[[conditions/Stunned|stunned]]_. If that affliction or condition has a duration, it is suspended until this spell expires. If the subject has multiple instances of the same type of affliction (such as multiple diseases), a single casting of this spell can suspend only one of them. _[[spells/Placebo Effect|Placebo effect]]_ doesn't remove or temporarily negate any damage that the affliction may have already caused, nor does it provide protection against receiving such conditions again.
