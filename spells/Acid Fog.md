# <font style='color:blue'>Acid Fog</font> 
**School:** conjuration (creation) [acid] 
_arcanist_ 6, _wizard_ 6, _sorcerer_ 6, _magus_ 6, _unsummoner_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (powdered peas and an animal hoof) {powdered peas and an animal hoof}
 #### Effects
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Effect:** fog spreads in 20-ft. radius, 20 ft. high
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
_[[spells/Acid Fog|Acid fog]]_ creates a _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Billowing|billowing]]_ mass of misty vapors like the _[[spells/Solid Fog|solid fog]]_ spell. In addition to slowing down creatures and obscuring sight, this spell's vapors are highly acidic. Each round on your turn, starting when you cast the spell, the fog deals 2d6 points of acid damage to each creature and object within it.
