# <font style='color:orange'>Life Shield</font> 
**School:** conjuration (healing) 
_druid_ 3, _hunter_ 3, _cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, _paladin_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 minute/level or until discharged

 #### Description
You surround yourself with a positive energy field that damages undead opponents. Each time an undead creature damages you with a melee attack, that creature takes an amount of positive energy damage equal to half the damage it dealt to you. This damage is calculated after applying your _[[universal monster rules/Damage Reduction|damage reduction]]_, resistances, and other defenses. Once this spell has dealt 5 points of damage per caster level (maximum 50), it is discharged.

 #### Other
**Source:** Undead Slayer's Handbook
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
