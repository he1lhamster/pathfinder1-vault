**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 4, _bard_ 4, _skald_ 4, _psychic_ 4, _mesmerist_ 4, _occultist_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Area:** several living creatures, no two of which may be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This functions as _[[spells/Hypnotism|hypnotism]]_, except it affects 2 Hit Die of creatures per caster level you have. You can make up to five requests (instead of a single request) to each creature affected by the spell, and the requests can be as long and as complicated as you desire (though they still must be reasonable).
