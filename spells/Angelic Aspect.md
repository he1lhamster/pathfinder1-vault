# <font style='color:green'>Angelic Aspect</font> 
**School:** transmutation [good] 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _cleric_ 5, _oracle_ 5, _warpriest_ 5, _paladin_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions like lesser _[[spells/Angelic Aspect|angelic aspect]]_, except you gain _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_, _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 60, _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ to acid 10, _resistance_ to cold 10, and DR 5/evil, and you sprout white feathered wings allowing you to fly at a speed of 30 feet with average maneuverability. In addition, your natural weapons and any weapons you wield are considered good-aligned for the purpose of overcoming _[[universal monster rules/Damage Reduction|damage reduction]]_.

 #### Other
