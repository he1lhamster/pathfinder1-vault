# <font style='color:red'>Steam Ray Fusillade</font> 
**School:** evocation [fire, water] 
_arcanist_ 7, _wizard_ 7, _sorcerer_ 7, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a scraping from a hydrothermal vent) {a scraping from a hydrothermal vent}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 round/level

 #### Description
For the duration of the spell, you can fire three rays of steam once per round as a standard action (and you also fire three such rays as part of casting the spell). These rays function similarly to the rays from _[[spells/Scorching Ray|scorching ray]]_ (you can fire them at close range, they're ranged touch attacks, they deal 4d6 points of fire damage each, and _[[universal monster rules/Spell Resistance|spell resistance]]_ negates them) except that they work both _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Underwater|underwater]]_ and above water without a caster level check for being a fire spell.

 #### Other
**Source:** Aquatic Adventures
**Rating:** 1
