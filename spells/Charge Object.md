# <font style='color:red'>Charge Object</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_psychic_ 1, _mesmerist_ 1, _occultist_ 1, _spiritualist_ 1, _medium_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 10 minutes
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** object touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** permanent
**Saving Throw:** none; see text; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You charge an item with minor _[[classes/Psychic|psychic]]_ energy. The item can be detected by the _[[spells/Detect _Psychic_ Significance|detect _psychic_ significance]]_ spell. If you _[[spells/Wish|wish]]_, you can imprint the item with your ownership history. Spells such as _[[spells/Object Reading|object reading]]_ a nd u ses o f t he p sychometry o ccult skill unlock (see page 196) can reveal any information about yourself you impart into the object, including your name, alignment, profession, and a summary of your experiences with the item. You can't impart false information into the object, but you can omit any details you'd prefer not to divulge. If the target object is already psychically charged, you can add more information to it, but you can't use _[[spells/Charge Object|charge object]]_ t o e rase existing _psychic_ information.
