# <font style='color:orange'>Heroic Invocation</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 9, _wizard_ 9, _sorcerer_ 9, _witch_ 9, _psychic_ 9, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 10 minutes
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one or more creatures, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 10 minutes /level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
Using worlds of ancient power, you bestow heroic powers on all creatures subject to this spell. They gain a +4 morale bonus on attack and damage rolls, gain 2d8+4 temporary hit points, and become immune to both _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_ and charm effects for the spell's duration. At the end of the spell's duration, the subjects become _[[conditions/Fatigued|fatigued]]_.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Combat
**Rating:** 2
