# <font style='color:blue'>Persistent Vigor</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_witch_ 4, _shaman_ 4, _cleric_ 4, _oracle_ 4, _warpriest_ 4, _inquisitor_ 4, _alchemist_ 4, _investigator_ 4, _psychic_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M/DF (ginseng root shavings) {ginseng root shavings}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)

 #### Description
You are filled with a _[[spells/Persistent Vigor|persistent vigor]]_. You gain _[[universal monster rules/Fast Healing|fast healing]]_ 2 and are immune to _[[universal monster rules/Bleed|bleed]]_ effects as well as the _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_ and _[[conditions/Nauseated|nauseated]]_ conditions. When you succeed at a saving throw against a disease or poison effect, that disease or poison effect ends (as if you had succeeded at enough saving throws to cure that effect).
  If you fail a saving throw against a disease or poison effect, you can instantly dismiss this spell to reroll that saving throw with a +4 bonus; you must take the result of this reroll, even if it is lower.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Class Guide
**Rating:** 4
