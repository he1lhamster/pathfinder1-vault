# <font style='color:orange'>Psychic Leech</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [emotion, mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 2, _magus_ 3, _bloodrager_ 3, _psychic_ 2, _mesmerist_ 2, _spiritualist_ 2, _medium_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one living creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will partial; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
The target experiences feelings of ennui and _[[curses/Lethargy|lethargy]]_, becoming _[[conditions/Fatigued|fatigued]]_ for the spell's duration. While the target is _fatigued_ and is within the spell's range, you gain a +2 enhancement bonus to Strength and Dexterity. On a successful save, the target is _fatigued_ for only 1d4 rounds and you receive no bonus. This spell has no effect if it targets a creature that is already _fatigued_.

 #### Other
**Source:** Occult Origins
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
