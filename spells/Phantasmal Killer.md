# <font style='color:orange'>Phantasmal Killer</font> 
**School:** illusion (phantasm) [fear, mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 4, _magus_ 4, _bloodrager_ 4, _psychic_ 4, _mesmerist_ 4, _spiritualist_ 4, _medium_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one living creature
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Will disbelief, then Fortitude partial; see text; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You create a _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Phantasmal|phantasmal]]_ image of the most fearsome creature imaginable to the subject simply by forming the fears of the subject's subconscious mind into something that its conscious mind can visualize: this most horrible beast. Only the spell's subject can see the _[[spells/Phantasmal Killer|phantasmal killer]]_. You see only a vague shape. The target first gets a Will save to recognize the image as unreal. If that save fails, the phantasm touches the subject, and the subject must succeed on a Fortitude save or die from _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_.
  Even if the Fortitude save is successful, the subject takes 3d6 points of damage.
  If the subject of a _phantasmal killer_ attack succeeds in disbelieving and possesses _[[universal monster rules/Telepathy|telepathy]]_ or is wearing a _[[items/Wondrous Item/Helm of Telepathy|helm of telepathy]]_, the beast can be turned upon you. You must then disbelieve it or become subject to its _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Deadly|deadly]]_ _fear_ attack.
