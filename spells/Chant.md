# <font style='color:black'>Chant</font> 
**School:** conjuration (creation) 
_cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, _paladin_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Area:** all allies and foes within a 40-ft. burst centered on you
**Range:** 40 ft.
**Duration:** concentration; maximum 5 min./level (see below)
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
As long as you _[[spells/Chant|chant]]_, you bring special favor upon your allies and bring disfavor to your enemies. You and your allies gain a +1 luck bonus on attack rolls, weapon damage rolls, saves, and skill checks while your foes take a -1 penalty on such rolls. You must _chant_ in a clear voice. Any interruption in your chanting, such as a failed concentration check, a _[[spells/Silence|silence]]_ spell or speaking or casting another spell, ends this spell. As an exception to the general rule, the effects of this spell stack with those of a _[[spells/Prayer|prayer]]_ spell if cast by a _[[classes/Cleric|cleric]]_ of your alignment and who worships the same deity as you.
