# <font style='color:blue'>Acid Pit</font> 
**School:** conjuration (creation) [acid] 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _summoner_ 4, _unsummoner_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (drop of acid), F (Fine shovel worth 10 gp) {drop of acid}{Fine shovel worth 10 gp}
 #### Effects
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round + 1 round/level
**Effect:** 10-ft.-by-10-ft. hole, 10 ft. deep/2 levels
**Saving Throw:** Reflex negates; see text; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Create Pit|create pit]]_, except that it places a 5-footdeep pool of acid at the bottom of the pit. The pit has a maximum depth of 100 feet. Creatures who fall into the pit take falling damage as normal (the acid counts as a yielding surface), plus 2d6 points of acid damage per round spent in contact with the acid. In addition, exposed items carried by a creature in the pit may be harmed. Refer to Table 9-2: Items Affected by Magical Attacks (Core Rulebook 216). Items are affected one at a time in the order listed on the table, and must make Fortitude saves after 3 consecutive rounds in the acid or gain the _[[conditions/Broken|broken]]_ condition. Objects with the _broken_ condition (regardless of how they became _broken_) must make a Fortitude saving throw each round spent within the acid or be destroyed. The walls of the pit are quite slippery and have a _[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_ DC of 30.

 #### Other
