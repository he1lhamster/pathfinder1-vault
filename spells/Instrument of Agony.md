# <font style='color:green'>Instrument of Agony</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, _inquisitor_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** weapon touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 minutes/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless, object), see text; **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless, object), see text; 
 #### Description
You cause a weapon to exude a palpable aura of divine fury. While wielding this weapon, a creature gains a +2 morale bonus on Intimidate checks. When an attack made using the targeted weapon hits, the wielder can spend a free action to _[[spells/Discharge|discharge]]_ the effect to inflict agony on the creature the weapon hit. If that creature has _[[universal monster rules/Spell Resistance|spell resistance]]_, it applies against this effect. If the creature fails a Will save, it is _[[conditions/Nauseated|nauseated]]_ for 1d4+1 rounds. If it succeeds at the saving throw, the creature is instead _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_ for 1 round. The _sickened_ condition created by the instrument is a mind-affecting effect.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Combat
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
