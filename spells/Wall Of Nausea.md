# <font style='color:green'>Wall Of Nausea</font> 
**School:** illusion (pattern) [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _bard_ 3, _skald_ 3, _psychic_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Effect:** transparent wall whose area is up to one 10-ft. square/level
**Saving Throw:** see text; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You create a transparent, shimmering wall through which creatures and objects appear to be wildly distorted to viewers. Any creature that passes through the wall is immediately assailed by overwhelming vertigo, becoming _[[conditions/Nauseated|nauseated]]_ for 1 round unless it succeeds at a Fortitude save; if _nauseated_, the creature must also succeed at a DC 12 Acrobatics check or fall _[[conditions/Prone|prone]]_. Creatures gain partial concealment (20% miss chance) against creatures on the other side of the wall.
  The wall must be vertical and rectangular. It need not touch the ground. The wall must be continuous and unbroken when formed. If its surface is _[[conditions/Broken|broken]]_ by any object or creature when it is cast, the spell fails.

 #### Other

**Potion:** Yes
