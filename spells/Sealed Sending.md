# <font style='color:green'>Sealed Sending</font> 
**School:** conjuration (creation) [language-dependent] 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Range:** unlimited
**Duration:** 1 hour/level or until discharged
**Effect:** one nonmagical parchment with a written message
**Saving Throw:** none (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** no (harmless); 
 #### Description
You create a scroll bearing a _[[spells/Message|message]]_ in any language you can speak, with a maximum length of 50 words. The scroll magically appears anywhere you have viewed at least once on the same plane of existence you are on now. When the spell ends or when the _message_ has been read completely, the scroll disintegrates, leaving no trace. This spell cannot be detected, traced, or blocked by any spell of a lower level.

 #### Other
**Source:** Inner Sea Intrigue
**Rating:** 3
