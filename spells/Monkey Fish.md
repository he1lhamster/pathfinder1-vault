# <font style='color:orange'>Monkey Fish</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _magus_ 1, _shaman_ 1, _druid_ 1, _hunter_ 1, _alchemist_ 1, _investigator_ 1, _psychic_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)

 #### Description
Your hands and feet alter to make you better at climbing and swimming. You gain a 10 foot _[[universal monster rules/Climb|climb]]_ speed and swim speed. This spell has no effect if you are wearing _[[classes/Medium|medium]]_ or heavy armor or carrying a _medium_ or heavy load.
