# <font style='color:red'>Symbol of Strife</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 9, _wizard_ 9, _sorcerer_ 9, _witch_ 9, _cleric_ 9, _oracle_ 9, _occultist_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 10 minutes
 **Components:** V, S, M (mercury and phosphorous, plus powdered diamond and opal worth a total of 15,000 gp) {mercury and phosphorous, plus powdered diamond and opal worth a total of 15,000 gp}
 #### Effects
**Range:** 0 ft.; see text
**Duration:** see text
**Effect:** one symbol
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Symbol of Death|symbol of death]]_, except all creatures within the radius of a _[[spells/Symbol of Strife|symbol of strife]]_ are compelled to attack the nearest conscious creaturefor 1 round per caster level (similar to the "attack nearest creature" result of the _[[spells/Confusion|confusion]]_ spell), even after leaving the symbol's area of effect. If no other creatures are visible, an affected creature can act normally. Once triggered, the symbol remains active for 10 minutes per caster level.
  _Symbol of strife_ can be made permanent with a _[[spells/Permanency|permanency]]_ spell by a caster of 18th level or higher for the cost of 25,000 gp.
