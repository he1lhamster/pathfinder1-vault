# <font style='color:orange'>Icicle Dagger</font> 
**School:** conjuration (creation) [cold] 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _witch_ 1, _summoner_ 1, _unsummoner_ 1, _bloodrager_ 1, _occultist_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Range:** 0 ft.
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Effect:** one icicle
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You create a masterwork _[[items/Weapon/Dagger|dagger]]_ out of ice. The _dagger_ deals 1 point of cold damage in addition to normal _dagger_ damage.
  If the _dagger_ leaves your hand for more than 1 round, it melts and the spell ends. At 6th level, the _dagger_ functions as a +1 frost _dagger_. At 11th level, it gains the returning property when thrown, melting away and reforming in your hand just before your next turn.
