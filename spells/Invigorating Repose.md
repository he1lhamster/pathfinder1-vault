# <font style='color:green'>Invigorating Repose</font> 
**School:** conjuration (healing) 
_cleric_ 5, _oracle_ 5, _warpriest_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 hour or until discharged

 #### Description
This spell enhances your spells that bring the dead back to life. If you cast _[[spells/Raise Dead|raise dead]]_, the target creature is restored to full hit points. If you cast _[[spells/Breath Of Life|breath of life]]_, that spell can revive a dead target as long as you cast it within a number of rounds equal to half your caster level, and the healing effect is treated as if the spell were empowered with the _[[feats/Empower Spell|Empower Spell]]_ feat. Casting any of these spells discharges the _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Invigorating|invigorating]]_ repose spell.

 #### Other
**Source:** Dwarves of Golarion
**Rating:** 3
