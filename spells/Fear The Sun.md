# <font style='color:green'>Fear The Sun</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _witch_ 2, _bloodrager_ 1, _shaman_ 2, _druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, _inquisitor_ 2, _antipaladin_ 1, _psychic_ 2, _occultist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a drow eyelash) {a drow eyelash}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** up to one creature/level, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
Each target that fails its saving throw gains _[[universal monster rules/Light Blindness|light blindness]]_, as per the universal monster rule. When exposed to bright light, affected targets are _[[conditions/Blinded|blinded]]_ for 1 full round and are _[[conditions/Dazzled|dazzled]]_ in successive rounds. If you cast this spell in the presence of bright light, any target that fails its save is _blinded_ immediately, and _dazzled_ starting at the beginning of its first turn.

 #### Other
**Source:** Blood Of Shadows
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
