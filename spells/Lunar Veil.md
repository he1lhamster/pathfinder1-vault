# <font style='color:red'>Lunar Veil</font> 
**School:** illusion (shadow) [darkness, shadow] 
_arcanist_ 7, _wizard_ 7, _sorcerer_ 7, _witch_ 7, _cleric_ 7, _oracle_ 7, _psychic_ 7, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Area:** 120-ft.-radius emanation
**Range:** long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
**Duration:** 10 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates, see text; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You conjure a tremendous area of _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Shadow|shadow]]_ to obscure moonlight within the area, creating something akin to a lunar eclipse. The light level is lowered by two steps (though unlike _[[spells/Deeper Darkness|deeper darkness]]_, areas of normal light or dimmer become normal _[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_ rather than supernaturally dark).
  Lycanthropes in the area must make a Will save every round or revert to their humanoid forms. In addition, lycanthropes attempting to assume animal or hybrid form within the affected area take a -5 penalty on Constitution checks to do so.
