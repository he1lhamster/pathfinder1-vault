# <font style='color:orange'>Burning Hands</font> 
**School:** evocation [fire] 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _witch_ 1, _magus_ 1, _bloodrager_ 1, _shaman_ 1, _occultist_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Area:** cone-shaped burst
**Range:** 15 ft.
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Reflex half; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
A cone of searing flame shoots from your fingertips. Any creature in the area of the flames takes 1d4 points of fire damage per caster level (maximum 5d4). Flammable materials _[[universal monster rules/Burn|burn]]_ if the flames touch them. A character can extinguish _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Burning|burning]]_ items as a full-round action.
