# <font style='color:blue'>Aura of Greater Courage</font> 
**School:** abjuration [emotion] 
_paladin_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Area:** 10-ft.-radius emanation centered on you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 10/minutes per level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
When you cast this spell you strengthen your _[[classes/Paladin|paladin]]_'s aura of courage. Until the end of its duration, all allies within that aura are immune to _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_ (magical or otherwise). If you do not have the aura of courage class feature, _[[spells/Aura of Greater Courage|aura of greater courage]]_ has no effect.

 #### Other
