# <font style='color:orange'>Life Current</font> 
**School:** conjuration (healing) [water] 
_shaman_ 3, _druid_ 3, _hunter_ 3, _ranger_ 3, _cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, _paladin_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Area:** current or portion of current up to 20 feet wide and 100 feet long
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round
**Saving Throw:** Will half; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You infuse the current with positive energy. At the start of their turn when the current moves them, living creatures in the current's area _[[spells/Heal|heal]]_ 1d6 hit points per 10 feet of the current's speed (maximum 6d6). Undead creatures in the current instead take an equal amount of damage.

 #### Other
**Source:** Aquatic Adventures
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
