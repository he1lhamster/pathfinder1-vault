#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Area:** The caster and all allies within a 50-ft. burst, centered on the caster
**Range:** 50 ft.
**Duration:** 1 min./level
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless);
