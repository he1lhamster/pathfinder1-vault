# <font style='color:red'>Shock Shield</font> 
**School:** abjuration [electricity, force] 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _magus_ 1, _bloodrager_ 1, _alchemist_ 1, _investigator_ 1, _occultist_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 minutes/level (D)

 #### Description
This spell creates an _[[conditions/Invisible|invisible]]_ _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_ similar to but not as strong as the _shield_ spell. This _shield_ hovers in front of you and negates _[[spells/Magic Missile|magic missile]]_ attacks directed at you. The disk provides a +2 _shield_ bonus to AC. This bonus applies against _[[universal monster rules/Incorporeal|incorporeal]]_ touch attacks. At any time, as a free action, you may dismiss your _shield_, at which point it deals 1d6 points of electrical damage to all creatures within a 5-foot burst, including you. A Reflex saving throw halves the damage.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Combat
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
