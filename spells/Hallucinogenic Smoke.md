# <font style='color:blue'>Hallucinogenic Smoke</font> 
**School:** conjuration [poison] 
_witch_ 4, _cleric_ 4, _oracle_ 4, _warpriest_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** S, M (tobacco leaves, ingested) {tobacco leaves, ingested}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 3 rounds/level or until discharged; see text
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude partial; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
Dark _[[monsters/Gray|gray]]_ smoke seeps from your eyes, ears, and mouth for the spell's duration, though the smoke doesn't significantly hamper your _[[spells/Vision|vision]]_. Upon casting the spell, you may immediately exhale the smoke in a 30-foot cone or do so in a later round as a standard action, ending the spell. Each living creature caught within the cone must succeed at a Fortitude save or be _[[conditions/Nauseated|nauseated]]_ for 1 round per caster level as it is wracked with waves of nausea and disorienting visions. If it succeeds at its Fortitude save, the creature is instead _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_ for 1d4 rounds. If you don't expel the smoke by the end of the spell's duration, you suffer the spell's effects instead. If you cast _[[spells/Augury|augury]]_ during the duration of _[[spells/Hallucinogenic Smoke|hallucinogenic smoke]]_, treat your caster level as 5 higher when determining the chance for a meaningful reply. Additionally, you know whether a result of "nothing" resulted from a failed or successful _augury_.

 #### Other
**Source:** Faiths & Philosophies
**Rating:** 4
