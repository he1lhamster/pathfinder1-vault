# <font style='color:orange'>Jitterbugs</font> 
**School:** illusion (figment) [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, _psychic_ 2, _mesmerist_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** short (25 ft. +5 ft. 2/levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You cause the target to perceive itself as being covered in _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Creeping|creeping]]_, crawling, stinging bugs. This causes the target to become jittery and unable to stay still, forcing it to constantly move and twitch. The target takes a -4 penalty on all Dexterity checks and Dexterity-based skill checks, and cannot take the delay, ready, or total defense actions.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Race Guide
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
