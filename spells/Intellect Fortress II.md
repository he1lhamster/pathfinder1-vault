# <font style='color:green'>Intellect Fortress II</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_psychic_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 immediate action
 **Components:** V 
 #### Effects
**Area:** 20-ft.-radius emanation centered on you
**Range:** 20 ft.
**Duration:** 1 round
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Intellect Fortress I|intellect fortress I]]_, but in addition, any mind-affecting effects that deal damage have their damage reduced by half (before any reduction due to a successful save or other effect). This applies to both hit point damage and ability score damage. This spell can be undercast.

 #### Other
**Source:** Occult Adventures
**Rating:** 3
