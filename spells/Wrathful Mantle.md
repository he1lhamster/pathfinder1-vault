# <font style='color:orange'>Wrathful Mantle</font> 
**School:** evocation [force, light] 
_cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, _paladin_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched or all creatures within 5 ft.; see text
**Range:** touch or 5 ft.; see text
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
A shimmering mantle of light shrouds the subject, light like a _[[items/Mundane/Torch|torch]]_. The subject of the spell gains a +1 _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ bonus on all saving throws per four caster levels (maximum +5 at 20th level). The subject can end the _[[spells/Wrathful Mantle|wrathful mantle]]_ at any time as a swift action to deal 2d8 points of force damage to all creatures within 5 feet.
