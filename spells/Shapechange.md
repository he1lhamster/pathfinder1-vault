# <font style='color:purple'>Shapechange</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_arcanist_ 9, _wizard_ 9, _sorcerer_ 9, _shaman_ 9, _druid_ 9, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, F (jade circlet worth 1,500 gp) {jade circlet worth 1,500 gp}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 10 min./level (D)

 #### Description
This spell allows you to take the form of a wide variety of creatures. This spell can function as _[[spells/Alter Self|alter self]]_, beast form IV, _[[spells/Elemental Body IV|elemental body IV]]_, _[[spells/Form of the Dragon III|form of the dragon III]]_, _[[spells/Giant Form II|giant form II]]_, and _[[spells/Plant Shape III|plant shape III]]_ depending on what form you take. You can change form once each round as a free action. The change takes place either immediately before your regular action or immediately after it, but not during the action.
