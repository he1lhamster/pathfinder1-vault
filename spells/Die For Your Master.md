# <font style='color:orange'>Die For Your Master</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _witch_ 5, _druid_ 5, _hunter_ 5, _antipaladin_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 immediate action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** your animal companion, familiar, or fiendish servant
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Bleed For Your Master|bleed for your master]]_, but it allows your companion or familiar to intercept any attack that targets you, including those that do not require attack rolls (e.g., _[[spells/Chain Lightning|chain lightning]]_, _[[spells/Hold Monster|hold monster]]_, and _[[spells/Magic Missile|magic missile]]_). Your companion or familiar cannot intercept effects that do not affect creatures of its type (e.g., _[[spells/Dominate Person|dominate person]]_).

 #### Other
**Source:** Animal Archive
**Rating:** 2
