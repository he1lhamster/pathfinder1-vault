# <font style='color:orange'>Bowstaff</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_hunter_ 1, _ranger_ 1, _inquisitor_ 1, _paladin_ 1, _occultist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 swift action
 **Components:** V 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one weapon (bow)
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless, object); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless, object); 
 #### Description
The bow that is touched takes on the rigidity and _[[feats/Toughness|toughness]]_ of forged steel, allowing it to be used as a melee weapon. The spell allows a _[[items/Weapon/Shortbow|shortbow]]_ to be used as a _[[items/Weapon/Club|club]]_ or a _[[items/Weapon/Longbow|longbow]]_ to be used as a _[[items/Weapon/Quarterstaff|quarterstaff]]_, although the bow retains its normal hit points and hardness. The bow's enhancement bonus, if any, applies on melee attack and damage rolls. Additional weapon special weapon qualities also apply to melee attacks if such qualities can be added to a melee weapon.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Combat
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
