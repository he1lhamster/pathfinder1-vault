# <font style='color:blue'>Hero's Defiance</font> 
**School:** conjuration (healing) 
_paladin_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 immediate action
 **Components:** V 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** instantaneous

 #### Description
The instant before you are reduced to 0 or fewer hit points, you can _[[spells/Expend|expend]]_ a use of your lay on hands ability to _[[spells/Heal|heal]]_ yourself as if you had used lay on hands, plus an additional 1d6 hit points.
  If this healing brings your hit point total above 0 hit points, you do not fall, and may continue to act. If you have no more uses of lay on hands this spell has no effect.

 #### Other
**Source:** APG
**Rating:** 4
**Potion:** Yes
