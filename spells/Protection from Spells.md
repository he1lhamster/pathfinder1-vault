**School:** abjuration 
_arcanist_ 8, _wizard_ 8, _sorcerer_ 8, _summoner_ 6, _psychic_ 8, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (diamond worth 500 gp), F (One 1,000 gp diamond per target. Each subject must carry the gem for the duration of the spell. If a subject loses the gem, the spell ceases to affect him.) {diamond worth 500 gp}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** up to one creature touched per four levels
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 10 min./level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
The subject gains a +8 _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ bonus on saving throws against spells and _[[universal monster rules/Spell-Like Abilities|spell-like abilities]]_ (but not against supernatural and extraordinary abilities).
