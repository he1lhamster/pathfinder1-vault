# <font style='color:orange'>Mage's Crawl Space</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _bard_ 2, _shaman_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a piece of clay) {a piece of clay}
 #### Effects
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 hour/level (D)
**Effect:** 5-foot sphere centered on yourself
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
When you cast this spell, you merge with an adjacent natural stone surface, forming a 5-foot pocket within. If the stone surface does not have enough volume to safely contain a 5-foot sphere of empty space, the spell fails. When created, there is sufficient air within the pocket for up to one _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ creature to survive for the spell's duration, including one additional Tiny or smaller creature, such as a familiar. At the end of the spell's duration, you are immediately expelled into the nearest available open space adjacent to the surface you merged with.

 #### Other
**Source:** Heroes Of The Darklands
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
