# <font style='color:blue'>Litany Of Escape</font> 
**School:** conjuration (teleportation) [language-dependent] 
_inquisitor_ 4, _antipaladin_ 3, _paladin_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 swift action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one willing creature that is grappled
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** no; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
With a powerful _[[spells/Prayer|prayer]]_, you call upon the servants of your god to whisk a friend out of a grapple. The target loses the _[[conditions/Grappled|grappled]]_ and _[[conditions/Pinned|pinned]]_ conditions and is teleported 10 feet. If there is no available space for the target to teleport to, the spell fails.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Combat
**Rating:** 4
**Potion:** Yes
