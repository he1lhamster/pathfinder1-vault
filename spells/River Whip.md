# <font style='color:red'>River Whip</font> 
**School:** conjuration (creation) [water] 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _witch_ 2, _magus_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Range:** 0 ft.
**Duration:** 1 minute/level or until discharged (see text) (D)
**Effect:** whip of water
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You create a coil of flowing fresh water, functioning a _[[items/Weapon/Whip|whip]]_ appropriate for your size, except you make a melee touch attack instead of a regular attack. Anything you strike with the _whip_ takes damage and is doused with 1 pint of water.
  A creature with the fire subtype takes an additional 1d6 points of damage. If the target is on fire, it gains a +2 bonus on its next saving throw to extinguish the flames.
  After you have successfully used the _whip_ to hit a creature a number of times equal to your caster level, the water is expended and the spell is discharged. The water created by this spell is otherwise identical to that produced by _[[spells/Create Water|create water]]_.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Class Guide
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
