# <font style='color:green'>Litany of Sloth</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [language-dependent, mind-affecting] 
_inquisitor_ 1, _antipaladin_ 1, _paladin_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 swift action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round
**Saving Throw:** no; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
With a litany against the wages of sloth, you _[[spells/Slow|slow]]_ the target's defenses. The target cannot make attacks of opportunity or cast spells defensively. While subject to this spell, the target cannot be the target of another spell that has the word "litany" in the title.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Combat
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
