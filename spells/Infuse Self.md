# <font style='color:blue'>Infuse Self</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _magus_ 3, _bard_ 3, _skald_ 3, _bloodrager_ 3, _alchemist_ 3, _investigator_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a piece of the creature whose form you plan to assume) {a piece of the creature whose form you plan to assume}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level (D)

 #### Description
When you cast this spell, you assume the form of a native outsider from the following list: _[[monsters/Aasimar|aasimar]]_, _[[monsters/Aphorite|aphorite]]_ (see page 224), _[[monsters/Duskwalker|duskwalker]]_ (see page 230), fetchlingB2, _[[monsters/Ganzi|ganzi]]_ (see page 234), ifritB2, oreadB2, shabtiB5, suliB3, sylphB2, _[[monsters/Tiefling|tiefling]]_, or undineB2. You gain a +2 size bonus to one physical ability score of your choice. If the race has _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ to one or more energy types, you gain _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Energy Resistance|energy resistance]]_ 5 to your choice of one of those types. If the race gains a racial bonus on saving throws or skills, you gain those bonuses. If the race has _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ or _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_, you gain those qualities.

 #### Other
**Source:** Planar Adventures
**Rating:** 4
**Potion:** Yes
