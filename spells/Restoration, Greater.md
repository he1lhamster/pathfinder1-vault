# <font style='color:blue'>Restoration, Greater</font> 
**School:** conjuration (healing) 
_shaman_ 7, _cleric_ 7, _oracle_ 7, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 minute
 **Components:** V, S, M (diamond dust 5,000 gp) {diamond dust 5,000 gp}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
This spell functions like lesser _[[spells/Restoration|restoration]]_, except that it dispels all permanent and temporary negative levels afflicting the healed creature. Greater _restoration_ also dispels all magical effects penalizing the creature's abilities, cures all temporary ability damage, and restores all points permanently drained from all ability scores. It also eliminates fatigue and exhaustion, and removes all forms of _[[spells/Insanity|insanity]]_, _[[spells/Confusion|confusion]]_, and similar mental effects.
