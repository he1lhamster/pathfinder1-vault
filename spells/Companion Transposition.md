# <font style='color:orange'>Companion Transposition</font> 
**School:** conjuration (teleportation) 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _witch_ 5, _shaman_ 5, _druid_ 5, _hunter_ 4, _ranger_ 4, _antipaladin_ 4, _paladin_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, F (a feather, tuft of fur, or similar memento from your target) {a feather, tuft of fur, or similar memento from your target}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** yourself and one animal companion, familiar, or spirit animal within range
**Range:** long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** none (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You trade places with the other target, teleporting as if you were both affected by _[[spells/Dimension Door|dimension door]]_. Both you and the other target arrive in a square you choose in the other's former space. If the targets are different sizes, they must appear in locations that cover previously occupied squares. As with _dimension door_, after casting this spell, you can't take any other actions until your next turn, and the other target is _[[conditions/Staggered|staggered]]_ until the end of its next turn due to the transposition.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Wilderness
**Rating:** 2
