# <font style='color:orange'>Touch Of Slumber</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _witch_ 6, _bard_ 6, _skald_ 6, _shaman_ 5, _druid_ 5, _hunter_ 5, _cleric_ 5, _oracle_ 5, _warpriest_ 5, _psychic_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a bit of seaweed) {a bit of seaweed}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** nonhostile creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions only against a creature with an attitude toward you of indifferent or better, and only if the target is not hostile toward you or your visible allies. A target touched by you (this touch usually requires a successful melee touch attack) must succeed at a Will saving throw or fall asleep for 2d6 hours. While asleep, the creature is _[[conditions/Helpless|helpless]]_. For 1 minute after the creature is affected, any loud noise or rough contact causes it to _[[spells/Awaken|awaken]]_ immediately. Thereafter, slapping or _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Wounding|wounding]]_ an affected creature awakens it, but normal noise does not. Awakening a creature is a standard action (this is an application of the aid another action).

 #### Other
**Source:** Magic Tactics Toolbox
**Rating:** 2
