# <font style='color:green'>Night Blindness</font> 
**School:** necromancy [curse] 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _witch_ 1, _bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, _bloodrager_ 1, _shaman_ 2, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, _antipaladin_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one living creature
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 day/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
Your target's _[[spells/Vision|vision]]_ becomes impaired as if the light level were one step lower, treating bright light as normal light, normal light as dim light, and dim light as _[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_. In addition, the subject gains no benefit from _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_, or the _[[universal monster rules/See in Darkness|see in darkness]]_ ability. The subject gains a +4 bonus on saving throws to resist the blind or _[[conditions/Dazzled|dazzled]]_ condition caused by bright light (such as _[[spells/Flare|flare]]_). Remove blindness/deafness dispels _[[spells/Night Blindness|night blindness]]_.

 #### Other
**Source:** Black Markets
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
