# <font style='color:orange'>Litany of Thunder</font> 
**School:** evocation [language-dependent, sonic] 
_inquisitor_ 5, _antipaladin_ 4, _paladin_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 swift action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You call down a thunderous boom upon your enemy. The target becomes _[[conditions/Deafened|deafened]]_ until the condition is removed, and is _[[conditions/Confused|confused]]_ for 1 round. While subject to this spell, the target cannot be the target of another spell that has the word "litany" in the title.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Combat
**Rating:** 2
