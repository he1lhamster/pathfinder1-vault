# <font style='color:red'>Track Ship</font> 
**School:** divination (scrying) [travel] 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, F (piece of ship) {piece of ship}
 #### Effects
**Range:** see text
**Duration:** 1 hour/level
**Effect:** magical icon
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (object); **Spell Resistance:** yes (object); 
 #### Description
In order to cast this spell, you must have a piece of the ship you _[[spells/Wish|wish]]_ to track. Merchants often _[[spells/Preserve|preserve]]_ slivers from their trade ships specifically for this purpose. You also need a _[[items/Mundane/Nautical chart|nautical chart]]_.
  When you cast this spell, an icon of the targeted ship appears on the _nautical chart_. The icon moves as the ship moves for the duration of this spell. If the ship is not within the area delineated by the chart, the spell fails. If the ship is reduced to 0 or fewer hit points, its icon changes from a ship to a skull and crossbones.

 #### Other
**Source:** Pirates Of The Inner Sea

**Potion:** Yes
