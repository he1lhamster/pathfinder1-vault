# <font style='color:blue'>Phantasmal Affliction</font> 
**School:** illusion (phantasm) [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 3, _psychic_ 3, _mesmerist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a drop of cod liver oil) {a drop of cod liver oil}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** living creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** see text
**Saving Throw:** Will disbelief, then Fortitude or Will negates (see text); **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You cause the creature to believe she has a _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Debilitating|debilitating]]_ affliction. The target can attempt a Will save to recognize the affliction as unreal. If that save fails, the creature suffers an imaginary affliction of your choice. Curse: The target believes she has been cursed. She takes a permanent -4 penalty on attack rolls, saves, ability checks, and skill checks for 1 hour per caster level. After her save to disbelieve, the target attempts a second Will save to negate this effect. Poison: The target believes she has been poisoned. Choose a physical ability score. Each round for 6 rounds, plus 1 round per 5 caster levels, the target takes 1d3 points of damage to the chosen ability score. Each turn, the creature can attempt a Fortitude save to negate the damage and end the ongoing damage. Wasting: The target believes she has contracted a wasting disease. Each day, the creature takes 1d4 points of Constitution damage and becomes _[[conditions/Fatigued|fatigued]]_. A successful Fortitude save prevents this damage. Two consecutive successful saves end the effect. Since the affliction exists entirely in the creature's mind, _[[spells/Phantasmal Affliction|phantasmal affliction]]_ is not affected by normal cures like _[[spells/Neutralize Poison|neutralize poison]]_ or _[[spells/Remove Disease|remove disease]]_, or other effects like _[[spells/Delay Poison|delay poison]]_ or the _[[spells/Heal|Heal]]_ skill. Ordinary immunities do not apply in this case (though a creature immune to the affliction receives a +4 bonus on the Will save to disbelieve the illusion). Constitution damage from the affliction can't kill the target. Instead, it causes the target to fall _[[conditions/Unconscious|unconscious]]_ like other forms of ability damage. _Phantasmal affliction_ is a spell effect and can be dispelled normally. Placebo effectOA counters and dispels _phantasmal affliction_.
