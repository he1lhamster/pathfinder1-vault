# <font style='color:orange'>Age Resistance, Lesser</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 4, _druid_ 4, _hunter_ 4, _alchemist_ 3, _investigator_ 3, _psychic_ 4, _occultist_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 24 hours

 #### Description
You ignore the physical detriments of being middle-aged. This spell does not cause you to look younger, nor does it prevent you from _[[conditions/Dying|dying]]_ of old age, but as long as the spell is in effect, you ignore the -1 penalties to Strength, Dexterity, and Constitution that accrue once you become middle-aged. You retain the agerelated bonuses to Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma while under the effects of this spell. Additional penalties that you accrue upon becoming old or venerable apply in full.
