# <font style='color:blue'>Grasping Tentacles</font> 
**School:** conjuration (creation) 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 3, _magus_ 3, _summoner_ 3, _bloodrager_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (octopus or squid tentacle) {octopus or squid tentacle}
 #### Effects
**Area:** 20-ft.-radius spread
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Black Tentacles|black tentacles]]_, except the tentacles blindly _[[spells/Grasp|grasp]]_ at the targets' eyes and ears, and tug at their hair, clothes, and equipment. Instead of grapple attempts, the tentacles attempt dirty trick combat maneuver checks (CMB = your caster level + 4 [the tentacles' Strength bonus] + 1 [the tentacles' size bonus]). Roll on the following table to determine the effect of a successful dirty trick combat maneuver check.
  
  <table><tbody><tr><th>d%</th><th>Effect</th></tr><tr><td>1-20</td><td>_[[conditions/Blinded|Blinded]]_. The tentacles suction over the victim's eyes.</td></tr><tr><td>21-40</td><td>_[[conditions/Deafened|Deafened]]_. The tentacles clap themselves around the victim's ears.</td></tr><tr><td>41-60</td><td>_[[conditions/Entangled|Entangled]]_. The tentacles weave around the victim's limbs.</td></tr><tr><td>61-80</td><td>_[[conditions/Shaken|Shaken]]_. The tentacles menace the victim from all sides. This is a _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_ effect.</td></tr><tr><td>81-100</td><td>_[[conditions/Sickened|Sickened]]_. The tentacles give off sickening vapors. This is a poison effect.</td></tr></tbody></table>
