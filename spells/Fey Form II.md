# <font style='color:blue'>Fey Form II</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_arcanist_ 6, _wizard_ 6, _sorcerer_ 6, _witch_ 6, _magus_ 6, _shaman_ 5, _druid_ 5, _hunter_ 5, _alchemist_ 5, _investigator_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a piece of the creature whose form you plan to assume) {a piece of the creature whose form you plan to assume}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)

 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Fey Form I|fey form I]]_, except it also allows you to assume the form of a Tiny or Large creature of the fey type. Your base speed can't increase above 90 feet this way. If the form you assume has any of the following abilities, you gain those abilities: _[[universal monster rules/Burrow|burrow]]_ speed 30 feet, _[[universal monster rules/Climb|climb]]_ speed 90 feet, fly speed 60 feet (good maneuverability), swim speed 60 feet, _[[universal monster rules/All-Around Vision|all-around vision]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Blindsense|blindsense]]_ 30 feet, _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 60 feet, _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Scent|scent]]_, _[[universal monster rules/See in Darkness|see in darkness]]_, abduct, _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Animated|animated]]_ hair, _[[universal monster rules/Bleed|bleed]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Blood Rage|blood rage]]_, boot stomp, _[[universal monster rules/Burn|burn]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Compression|compression]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Constrict|constrict]]_, crushing leap, DR 2/cold iron, _[[universal monster rules/Grab|grab]]_, heavy weapons, icewalking, kneecapper, nasal spray, no _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Shadow|shadow]]_, oversized weapons, poison, _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Putrid|putrid]]_ vomit, _[[universal monster rules/Rock Throwing|rock throwing]]_ (50 feet, 1d6 damage), _[[universal monster rules/Sound Mimicry|sound mimicry]]_, _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Trackless|trackless]]_ step, _[[universal monster rules/Trample|trample]]_, tree meld, _[[universal monster rules/Undersized Weapons|undersized weapons]]_, and woodland stride. If the creature has _[[universal monster rules/Immunity|immunity]]_ to mind-affecting effects or poison, you gain a +4 _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ bonus on saves against those effects. If the creature has any weaknesses, you gain them.
  **Tiny Fey**: If you assume this form, you gain a +6 size bonus to your Dexterity score and take a -2 penalty to your Strength score.
  **Large Fey**: If you assume this form, you gain a +4 size bonus to your Strength and Constitution scores and take a -2 penalty to your Dexterity score.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Wilderness
**Rating:** 4
