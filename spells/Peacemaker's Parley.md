# <font style='color:orange'>Peacemaker's Parley</font> 
**School:** enchantment (charm) 
_cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** swift action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Area:** creatures in a 20-foot radius spread
**Range:** close (25 feet + 5 feet/2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You can attempt to influence unfriendly or hostile creatures, altering their attitudes toward you. After casting, make a Diplomacy check against the creatures as a free action; you gain a +2 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ bonus on this check against creatures that failed their saves against the spell. Even if you favorably manipulate the attitude of the affected targets, they quickly revert to their previous attitude if threatened or treated poorly.

 #### Other
**Source:** Dwarves of Golarion

**Potion:** Yes
