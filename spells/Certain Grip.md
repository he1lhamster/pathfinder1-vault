# <font style='color:red'>Certain Grip</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _bloodrager_ 2, _druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, _alchemist_ 2, _investigator_ 2, _occultist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M/DF (a tiny ball of tar) {a tiny ball of tar}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
The subject's grip and footing become sure, granting a +4 competence bonus on _[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_ checks, on Acrobatics checks to balance, and to CMD against bull rush, drag, reposition, and _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_ attempts. While affected by this spell, the target is also immune to the disarm combat maneuver.
