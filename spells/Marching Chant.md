# <font style='color:red'>Marching Chant</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 minute
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature/level
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** concentration
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
You _[[spells/Invigorate|invigorate]]_ your allies, who can hustle (Core Rulebook 170) as long as you continue to sing or _[[spells/Chant|chant]]_ (which requires your concentration). This movement counts as a walk (not a hustle) for the purpose of accruing nonlethal damage and fatigue. You can concentrate on this spell and hustle at the same time.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Class Guide

**Potion:** Yes
