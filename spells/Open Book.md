# <font style='color:orange'>Open Book</font> 
**School:** divination [curse] 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 3, _bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _inquisitor_ 2, _antipaladin_ 2, _psychic_ 2, _mesmerist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a page torn from a book) {a page torn from a book}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** permanent
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You lay bare not only the mind of a target, but the target's history as well. The target takes a -2 penalty on saving throws against divinations, and Diplomacy checks to gather information about the target gain a bonus equal to half your caster level (maximum +10).

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Intrigue
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
