# <font style='color:green'>Fire Snake</font> 
**School:** evocation [fire] 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _magus_ 5, _druid_ 5, _hunter_ 5, _occultist_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a snake scale) {a snake scale}
 #### Effects
**Area:** see text
**Range:** 60 ft.
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Reflex half; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You create a sinuous line of flames that you may shape as desired. The _[[spells/Fire Snake|fire snake]]_ affects one 5-foot square per caster level, and each square must be adjacent to the previous square, starting with you. The _fire snake_ may not extend beyond its maximum range. Creatures in the path of the _fire snake_ take 1d6 points of fire damage per caster level (maximum 15d6).
