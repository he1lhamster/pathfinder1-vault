# <font style='color:green'>Walk Through Space</font> 
**School:** conjuration (teleportation) 
_arcanist_ 7, _wizard_ 7, _sorcerer_ 7, _witch_ 7, _magus_ 6, _summoner_ 6, _alchemist_ 6, _investigator_ 6, _psychic_ 7, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a wren's egg) 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 round/level

 #### Description
When under the effects of this spell, you can teleport up to 30 feet as a move action. You must end this movement in an unoccupied space that you can stand on within line of sight. Alternatively, you can spend a move action to teleport to a standing position from lying _[[conditions/Prone|prone]]_. Teleporting does not provoke attacks of opportunity.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Combat
**Rating:** 3
