# <font style='color:orange'>Cloud Of Seasickness</font> 
**School:** conjuration (creation) [poison] 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a piece of seaweed) {a piece of seaweed}
 #### Effects
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Effect:** cloud spreads in 20-ft. radius, 20 ft. high
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates; see text; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions like _[[spells/Stinking Cloud|stinking cloud]]_, except as noted above and that the vapors make creatures _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_ instead of _[[conditions/Nauseated|nauseated]]_. _[[spells/Cloud Of Seasickness|Cloud of seasickness]]_ can be made permanent with a _[[spells/Permanency|permanency]]_ spell (requiring a 9th-level caster and costing 2,500 gp). A permanent _cloud of seasickness_ dispersed by wind reforms in 10 minutes.

 #### Other
**Source:** AP 55
