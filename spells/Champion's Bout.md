# <font style='color:red'>Champion's Bout</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_bard_ 3, _skald_ 3, _cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** two creatures
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (see text); **Spell Resistance:** yes (see text); 
 #### Description
You allow two chosen champions to fight without interference. The two creatures must be willing in order for the spell to work. After you cast this spell, any creature attempting to aid or _[[feats/Hinder|hinder]]_ either of the two targets in any way must succeed at a Will save or lose its action instead (_[[universal monster rules/Spell Resistance|spell resistance]]_ applies). A creature that fails its Will save against _[[spells/Champion's Bout|champion's bout]]_ cannot attempt to interfere again. If a creature successfully interferes with the bout, the spell ends immediately.

 #### Other
**Source:** Inner Sea Races
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
