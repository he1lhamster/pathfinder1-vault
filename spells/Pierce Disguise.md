# <font style='color:orange'>Pierce Disguise</font> 
**School:** divination 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 3, _bard_ 3, _skald_ 3, _shaman_ 3, _psychic_ 3, _occultist_ 3, _medium_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 minute/level

 #### Description
You see through magical disguises (whether illusions or _[[spells/Polymorph|polymorph]]_ effects) of the same spell level or lower. This allows you to see the true forms of creatures magically disguised by such methods. The _[[spells/True Form|true form]]_ appears as a ghostly overlay on the creature's disguised appearance.
