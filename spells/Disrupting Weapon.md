# <font style='color:green'>Disrupting Weapon</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_cleric_ 5, _oracle_ 5, _warpriest_ 5, _inquisitor_ 5, _spiritualist_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one melee weapon
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless, object); see text; **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless, object); 
 #### Description
This spell makes a melee weapon _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Deadly|deadly]]_ to undead. Any undead creature with HD equal to or less than your caster level must succeed on a Will save or be destroyed utterly if struck in combat with this weapon. _[[universal monster rules/Spell Resistance|Spell resistance]]_ does not apply against the _[[spells/Destruction|destruction]]_ effect.

 #### Other
**Source:** PFRPG Core
**Rating:** 3
