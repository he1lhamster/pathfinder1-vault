# <font style='color:blue'>Polymorph, Greater</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_arcanist_ 7, _wizard_ 7, _sorcerer_ 7, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a piece of the creature whose form you choose) {a piece of the creature whose form you choose}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** living creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 min/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Polymorph|polymorph]]_ except that it allows the creature to take on the form of a dragon or plant creature. If you use this spell to cause the target to take on the form of an animal or magical beast, it functions as _[[spells/Beast Shape IV|beast shape IV]]_. If the form is that of an elemental, the spell functions as _[[spells/Elemental Body III|elemental body III]]_. If the form is that of a humanoid, the spell functions as _[[spells/Alter Self|alter self]]_. If the form is that of a plant, the spell functions as _[[spells/Plant Shape II|plant shape II]]_. If the form is that of a dragon, the spell functions as _[[spells/Form of the Dragon I|form of the dragon I]]_. The subject may choose to resume its normal form as a full-round action; doing so ends the spell.

 #### Other
