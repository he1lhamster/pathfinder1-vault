# <font style='color:red'>Hibernate</font> 
**School:** necromancy 
_druid_ 1, _hunter_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You place a willing subject into a cataleptic state. It remains aware of its surroundings but is _[[conditions/Paralyzed|paralyzed]]_, appearing dead unless observers make a DC 20 _[[spells/Heal|Heal]]_ check. _[[spells/Hibernate|Hibernate]]_ delays the effects of poison, disease, and _[[universal monster rules/Bleed|bleed]]_ effects for the spell's duration, and half of any hit point damage suffered by the subject is converted to nonlethal damage.
  This spell originated with the Green Faith, but has spread to all other druidic religions.

 #### Other
**Source:** Faction Guide

**Potion:** Yes
