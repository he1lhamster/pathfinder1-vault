# <font style='color:green'>Mental Barrier I</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_psychic_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 immediate action
 **Components:** V 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 round

 #### Description
You put a barrier of mental energy that protects you from _[[spells/Harm|harm]]_. This barrier grants you a +4 _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_ bonus to AC. In addition, you take half damage from _[[spells/Mind Thrust I|mind thrust I]]_ and II (reduced to one-quarter on a successful Will save).

 #### Other
**Source:** Occult Adventures
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
