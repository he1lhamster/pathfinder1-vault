 #### Effects
**Targets:** weapon touched

**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless, object); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless, object); 
 #### Description
_[[spells/Magic Weapon|Magic weapon]]_ gives a weapon a +1 enhancement bonus on attack and damage rolls. An enhancement bonus does not stack with a masterwork weapon's +1 bonus on attack rolls.
  You can't cast this spell on a natural weapon, such as an unarmed strike (instead, see _[[spells/Magic Fang|magic fang]]_). A _[[classes/Monk|monk]]_'s unarmed strike is considered a weapon, and thus it can be enhanced by this spell.
