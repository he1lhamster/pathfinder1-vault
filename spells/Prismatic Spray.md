# <font style='color:blue'>Prismatic Spray</font> 
**School:** evocation 
_arcanist_ 7, _wizard_ 7, _sorcerer_ 7, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Area:** cone-shaped burst
**Range:** 60 ft.
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** see text; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell causes seven shimmering, multicolored beams of light to spray from your hand. Each beam has a different power. Creatures in the area of the spell with 8 HD or less are automatically _[[conditions/Blinded|blinded]]_ for 2d4 rounds. Every creature in the area is randomly struck by one or more beams, which have additional effects.
