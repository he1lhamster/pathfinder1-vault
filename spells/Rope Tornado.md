# <font style='color:orange'>Rope Tornado</font> 
**School:** evocation 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 4, _magus_ 4, _shaman_ 4, _druid_ 4, _hunter_ 4, _occultist_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M 
 #### Effects
**Area:** 120-foot line
**Range:** feet (120)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Reflex negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
A spiraling twister of tornado-force wind surges forth from your palm, unbalancing those it rushes over. Creatures on the ground within the area are knocked _[[conditions/Prone|prone]]_. A flying creature within the spell's area must instead succeed at a DC 25 Fly check or plummet to the ground, taking the appropriate amount of falling damage. Creatures that are Huge or larger in size gain a +2 bonus on their Reflex saves, while creatures that are _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ or smaller in size take a –2 penalty.Additionally, the area is subjected to tornado-force winds, extinguishing all nonmagical flames, dispersing clouds, and scattering unattended objects.

 #### Other
**Source:** Wilderness Origins
