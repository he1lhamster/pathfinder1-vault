# <font style='color:green'>Heart Of The Mammoth</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 8, _wizard_ 8, _sorcerer_ 8, _witch_ 8, _druid_ 8, _cleric_ 8, _oracle_ 8, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, F (a fetish of mammoth hair soaked in dragon's blood) 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
The target creature becomes stronger, hardier, and imbued with the courage of the greatest megafauna of the tundra. The spell grants a +8 enhancement bonus to Strength and Constitution, _[[universal monster rules/Immunity|immunity]]_ to _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_, a +4 morale bonus to Will saves, and doubles the critical threat range of all its attacks against creatures of the dragon type (this does not stack with _[[feats/Improved Critical|Improved Critical]]_, _[[spells/Keen Edge|keen edge]]_, or similar effects).

 #### Other
**Source:** Dragonslayer's Handbook
**Rating:** 3
