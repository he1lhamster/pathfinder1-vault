# <font style='color:blue'>Slow</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _magus_ 3, _bard_ 3, _skald_ 3, _summoner_ 2, _unsummoner_ 3, _bloodrager_ 3, _psychic_ 3, _occultist_ 3, _spiritualist_ 3, _medium_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a drop of molasses) {a drop of molasses}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature/level, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
An affected creature moves and attacks at a drastically slowed rate. Creatures affected by this spell are _[[conditions/Staggered|staggered]]_ and can take only a single move action or standard action each turn, but not both (nor may it take full-round actions). Additionally, it takes a -1 penalty on attack rolls, AC, and Reflex saves. A slowed creature moves at half its normal speed (round down to the next 5-foot increment), which affects the creature's jumping distance as normal for decreased speed.
  Multiple _[[spells/Slow|slow]]_ effects don't stack. _Slow_ counters and dispels _[[spells/Haste|haste]]_.

 #### Other
