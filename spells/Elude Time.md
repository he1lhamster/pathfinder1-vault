# <font style='color:orange'>Elude Time</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_alchemist_ 5, _investigator_ 5, _psychic_ 7, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (powdered diamond, emerald, ruby, and sapphire dust worth 500 gp) {powdered diamond, emerald, ruby, and sapphire dust worth 500 gp}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** up to 1 minute/level; see text

 #### Description
You place yourself in a state of suspended animation, similar to _[[spells/Temporal Stasis|temporal stasis]]_. At the time of casting, you choose when the stasis will expire, up to the maximum duration of the spell.
  Until the duration ends, time ceases to flow for you, and all bodily functions cease. No force or effect can _[[spells/Harm|harm]]_ you until the spell expires or is removed, such as by a successful _[[spells/Dispel Magic|dispel magic]]_ spell.

 #### Other
**Source:** APG
**Rating:** 2
