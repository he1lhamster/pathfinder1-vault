# <font style='color:orange'>Wartrain Mount</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _druid_ 2, _hunter_ 1, _ranger_ 1, _inquisitor_ 1, _antipaladin_ 1, _paladin_ 1, _mesmerist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 minute
 **Components:** V, S, M (a swatch of black cloth) {a swatch of black cloth}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one indifferent or friendly animal
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 hour/level
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You instill the target animal with the combat _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Training|training]]_ general purpose (see the Handle Animal skill, Core Rulebook 98). This supersedes the animal's previous trained purpose and any tricks it knows. When the spell ends, it reverts to its previous trained purpose and known tricks.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Magic
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
