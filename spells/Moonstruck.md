# <font style='color:blue'>Moonstruck</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting, emotion] 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 4, _bloodrager_ 4, _druid_ 4, _hunter_ 4, _psychic_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of powdered moonstone) {a pinch of powdered moonstone}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one humanoid creature
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You invoke the mystical power of the moon to drive the target into a mad, bestial frenzy. If the target fails its save, it is _[[conditions/Dazed|dazed]]_ for 1 round, dropping held items as its nails and teeth elongate and sharpen. The target gains a bite attack and two claw attacks that deal damage appropriate for the creature's size, and for the remainder of the spell's duration the target behaves as if under simultaneous _[[spells/Rage|rage]]_ and _[[spells/Confusion|confusion]]_ spells, attacking with its natural weapons in preference to other actions. During the final round of the spell's duration, the target is again _dazed_ as it returns to its normal state.

 #### Other
**Source:** APG
**Rating:** 4
**Domain:** Insanity (4), Rage (6), Moon (4); **Patron:** Moon (8);
