# <font style='color:green'>Ray of Exhaustion</font> 
**School:** necromancy 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 3, _magus_ 3, _bloodrager_ 3, _mesmerist_ 3, _spiritualist_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a drop of sweat) {a drop of sweat}
 #### Effects
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 min./level
**Effect:** ray
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude partial; see text; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
A black ray projects from your pointing finger. You must succeed on a ranged touch attack with the ray to strike a target.
  The subject is immediately _[[conditions/Exhausted|exhausted]]_ for the spell's duration. A successful Fortitude save means the creature is only _[[conditions/Fatigued|fatigued]]_.
  A character that is already _fatigued_ instead becomes _exhausted_.
  This spell has no effect on a creature that is already _exhausted_.
  Unlike normal exhaustion or fatigue, the effect ends as soon as the spell's duration expires.
