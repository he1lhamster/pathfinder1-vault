# <font style='color:orange'>Hold Portal</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _psychic_ 1, _occultist_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one portal, up to 20 sq. ft./level
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 min./level (D)
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell magically holds shut a door, _[[spells/Gate|gate]]_, window, or shutter of wood, metal, or stone. The magic affects the portal just as if it were securely closed and normally locked. A _[[spells/Knock|knock]]_ spell or a successful _[[spells/Dispel Magic|dispel magic]]_ spell can negate a _[[spells/Hold Portal|hold portal]]_ spell.
  Add 5 to the normal DC for forcing open a portal affected by this spell.
