**School:** necromancy 
_witch_ 9, _shaman_ 8, _cleric_ 8, _oracle_ 8, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects

**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Will half; **Spell Resistance:** yes;
