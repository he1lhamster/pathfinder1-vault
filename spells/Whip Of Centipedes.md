# <font style='color:green'>Whip Of Centipedes</font> 
**School:** conjuration (summoning) 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _witch_ 5, _bard_ 5, _skald_ 5, _summoner_ 5, _unsummoner_ 5, _druid_ 5, _hunter_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M/DF (a square of red cloth) {a square of red cloth}
 #### Effects
**Range:** 0 ft.
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D (see text)
**Effect:** whiplike swarm
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Whip Of Spiders|whip of spiders]]_, except it summons centipedes, and any creature you strike with the _[[items/Weapon/Whip|whip]]_ takes swarm damage as if it were attacked by a centipede swarm (2d6 points of damage plus poison and _[[universal monster rules/Distraction|distraction]]_, Bestiary 43). If you transform the _whip_ into a centipede swarm, it has 10 hit points.
