 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Area:** cone-shaped emanation
**Range:** 60 ft.
**Duration:** concentration, up to 10 minutes/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions like _[[spells/Detect Evil|detect evil]]_, except that it detects only outsiders with the evil subtype, as well as the lingering effects caused by their gifts, presence, and spells. It can also detect clerics and paladins of fiendish deities, including Asmodeus, archdevils, daemonic harbingers, and demon lords.
