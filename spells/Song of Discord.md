# <font style='color:blue'>Song of Discord</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting, sonic] 
_bard_ 5, _skald_ 5, _psychic_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Area:** creatures within a 20-ft.-radius spread
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell causes those within the area to turn on each other rather than attack their foes. Each affected creature has a 50% chance to attack the nearest target each round. (Roll to determine each creature's behavior every round at the beginning of its turn.) A creature that does not attack its nearest neighbor is free to act normally for that round. Creatures forced by a _[[spells/Song of Discord|song of discord]]_ to attack their fellows employ all methods at their disposal, choosing their deadliest spells and most advantageous combat tactics. They do not, however, _[[spells/Harm|harm]]_ targets that have _[[monsters/Fallen|fallen]]_ _[[conditions/Unconscious|unconscious]]_.

 #### Other
**Source:** PFRPG Core
**Rating:** 4
**Domain:** Riot (7);
