# <font style='color:blue'>Alter Self</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _witch_ 2, _magus_ 2, _bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _summoner_ 2, _unsummoner_ 2, _shaman_ 2, _alchemist_ 2, _investigator_ 2, _psychic_ 2, _mesmerist_ 2, _medium_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a piece of the creature whose form you plan to assume) {a piece of the creature whose form you plan to assume}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 min./level (D)

 #### Description
When you cast this spell, you can assume the form of any Small or _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ creature of the humanoid type. If the form you assume has any of the following abilities, you gain the listed ability: _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 60 feet, _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Scent|scent]]_, and swim 30 feet.
  Small creature: If the form you take is that of a Small humanoid, you gain a +2 size bonus to your Dexterity.
  _Medium_ creature: If the form you take is that of a _Medium_ humanoid, you gain a +2 size bonus to your Strength.

 #### Other
**Source:** PFRPG Core

**Potion:** Yes
