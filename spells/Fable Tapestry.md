**School:** illusion (shadow) [shadow] 
_bard_ 5, _skald_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S, F (your kapenia) {your kapenia}
 #### Effects
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Effect:** 1 legendary character/4 caster levels
**Saving Throw:** Will disbelief; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
The spell weaves shadows and colors from your kapenia into the shape of legendary characters from Varisian folktales. This spell functions as _[[spells/Shadow Conjuration|shadow conjuration]]_ emulating _[[spells/Summon Monster I|summon monster I]]_, except as noted here. You can _[[universal monster rules/Summon|summon]]_ only one copy of the same character at the same time, even with multiple castings of this spell.
