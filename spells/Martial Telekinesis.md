# <font style='color:orange'>Martial Telekinesis</font> 
**School:** evocation 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _magus_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** see text
**Range:** long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Will none; see text; **Spell Resistance:** yes; see text; 
 #### Description
You mingle the power of your mind and body to exert force beyond your reach. This spell functions as the combat maneuver version of _[[spells/Telekinesis|telekinesis]]_, except as noted above. Alternatively, you can inflict a telekinetic unarmed strike with this spell, using your own unarmed strike damage and applying your primary spellcasting ability score modifier to the damage in place of your Strength modifier.

 #### Other
**Source:** Inner Sea Races
**Rating:** 2
