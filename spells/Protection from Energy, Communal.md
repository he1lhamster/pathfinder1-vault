 #### Effects
**Targets:** creatures touched

**Duration:** 10 min./level or until discharged
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
This spell functions like _[[spells/Protection from Energy|protection from energy]]_, except you divide the duration in 10-minute intervals among the creatures touched.
