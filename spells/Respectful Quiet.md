# <font style='color:red'>Respectful Quiet</font> 
**School:** illusion (glamer) 
_bard_ 3, _skald_ 3, _cleric_ 5, _oracle_ 5, _warpriest_ 5, _inquisitor_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** any number of creatures in a 30-ft.-radius burst
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
All targets are rendered silent, as per _[[spells/Silence|silence]]_. The _silence_ affects the entirety of each creature but does not extend beyond. However, the _silence_ is one-way-sounds from outside the creatures' spaces can still enter. Quieted creatures gain a +10 circumstance bonus on Stealth checks.

 #### Other
**Source:** Inner Sea Races
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
