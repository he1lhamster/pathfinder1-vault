# <font style='color:green'>Litany of Defense</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_inquisitor_ 2, _antipaladin_ 2, _paladin_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 swift action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 round
**Saving Throw:** no; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
Invoking this litany strengthens your defenses. Any enhancement bonus your armor has is doubled and you are immune to _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_. While subject to this spell, the target cannot be the target of another spell that has the word "litany" in the title.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Combat
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
