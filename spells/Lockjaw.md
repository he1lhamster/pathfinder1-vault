# <font style='color:orange'>Lockjaw</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, _ranger_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (sticky tree gum) {sticky tree gum}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
You give a creature the ability to use one of its _[[universal monster rules/Natural Attacks|natural attacks]]_ to firmly _[[universal monster rules/Attach|attach]]_ itself to an opponent. Choose one of the creature's _natural attacks_ (usually a claw or bite attack).
  The creature gains the _[[universal monster rules/Grab|grab]]_ ability with that natural attack, including the +4 bonus on combat maneuver checks to start or maintain a grapple. A creature with multiple _natural attacks_ can strike at its _[[conditions/Grappled|grappled]]_ opponent with its other _natural attacks_, but cannot attack any other creature.
