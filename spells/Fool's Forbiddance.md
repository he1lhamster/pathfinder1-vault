# <font style='color:green'>Fool's Forbiddance</font> 
**School:** abjuration [mind-affecting] 
_bard_ 6, _skald_ 6, _psychic_ 8, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a ring of keys) {a ring of keys}
 #### Effects
**Area:** 10-ft.-radius spherical emanation centered on you
**Range:** 10 ft.
**Duration:** concentration
**Saving Throw:** Will partial; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
Through your antics and performance, you create an area of _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Warding|warding]]_ that adversely affects all enemies that dare enter it. When an enemy creature enters the area it must make an immediate Will saving throw. If it fails, the creature is _[[conditions/Confused|confused]]_ as long as it is in the area and for 1 round after it leaves. If it succeeds on the saving throw, the creature is _[[conditions/Staggered|staggered]]_ as long as it is in the area and for 1 round after it leaves.

 #### Other
**Source:** APG
**Rating:** 3
