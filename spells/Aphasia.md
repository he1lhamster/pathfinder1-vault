# <font style='color:orange'>Aphasia</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _witch_ 1, _bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, _psychic_ 1, _mesmerist_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** close (25 ft.+ 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (see text); **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You render the target unable to understand any language, including spoken language, written language, sign language, gestures attempting to _[[monsters/Mimic|mimic]]_ a crude language, or even truespeech and _[[universal monster rules/Telepathy|telepathy]]_. The affected creature is unable to communicate, use _[[spells/Command|command]]_ words, cast spells with verbal components, or use any other abilities that requires language. At the end of each of its turns, the subject can attempt a new saving throw to end the effect. _[[spells/Tongues|Tongues]]_ counters and dispels _[[spells/Aphasia|aphasia]]_, and a creature with the _tongues_ spell active is immune to _aphasia_. Oracles with the _tongues_ curse ignore _aphasia_ in combat.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Intrigue
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
