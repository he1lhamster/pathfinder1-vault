# <font style='color:red'>Dazzling Blade, Mass</font> 
**School:** illusion (pattern) 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 swift action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** One metal weapon/level, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (see text); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless, object); 
 #### Description
This spell functions like _[[spells/Dazzling Blade|dazzling blade]]_, except that it affects multiple weapons. Each wielder of a _dazzling blade_ can _[[spells/Discharge|discharge]]_ the weapon's effect to attempt to blind a foe independently of the others.

 #### Other
**Source:** Rival Guide
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
