# <font style='color:green'>Guardian Of Faith</font> 
**School:** abjuration [see text] 
_cleric_ 4, _oracle_ 4, _warpriest_ 4, _paladin_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (parchment with a holy text written on it) {parchment with a holy text written on it}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one ally
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
The target gains the benefit of _[[spells/Shield Of Faith|shield of faith]]_ and your choice of _[[spells/Protection From Chaos|protection from chaos]]_, evil, good, or law. As a move action, the target can transfer this spell to a touched ally, who becomes the new target of the spell.
  The alignment descriptor of this spell matches the alignment descriptor of the protection spell you chose when casting it. For example, granting the target _[[spells/Protection From Evil|protection from evil]]_ gives this spell the good descriptor.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Class Guide
**Rating:** 3
