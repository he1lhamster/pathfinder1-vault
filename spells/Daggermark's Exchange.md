# <font style='color:red'>Daggermark's Exchange</font> 
**School:** transmutation [poison] 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, _cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, _alchemist_ 2, _investigator_ 2, _psychic_ 3, _occultist_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a dose of poison) {a dose of poison}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** instantaneous

 #### Description
You swallow a dose of poison with no effect and regurgitate a dose of a different poison (your choice) into the old container (or another you have on hand). Attempt a Craft (alchemy) check against the new poison's DC. If you succeed, you create a dose of your chosen poison. If you fail, the poison you regurgitate is inert. If you roll a natural 1, you expose yourself to the original poison (although you remain immune to that poison if you have venom _[[universal monster rules/Immunity|immunity]]_ or a similar class feature or ability). The new poison's price must be the same as or less than the original poison's price.

 #### Other
**Source:** Inner Sea Intrigue
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
