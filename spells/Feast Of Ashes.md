# <font style='color:red'>Feast Of Ashes</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_witch_ 2, _druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, _psychic_ 2, _occultist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of ash) {a pinch of ash}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft/2 levels)
**Duration:** 2 days/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You curse the target with a hunger no food can assuage. On a failed saving throw, the target begins to starve (see page 444 of the Core Rulebook). The effects of this spell cannot inflict more nonlethal damage than the target has hit points. No amount of eating can counter the effect and, further, even attempting to eat causes the target to become _[[conditions/Nauseated|nauseated]]_ for 1 round unless they make a DC 12 Fortitude save. The target still experiences the other normal effects of anything consumed (such as poisons or potions).
  _[[spells/Feast Of Ashes|Feast of ashes]]_ can be removed with a _[[spells/Break Enchantment|break enchantment]]_, _[[spells/Limited Wish|limited wish]]_, _[[spells/Miracle|miracle]]_, _[[spells/Remove Curse|remove curse]]_, or _[[spells/Wish|wish]]_ spell, but effects suffered as a result of starvation must be healed by normal means.
