 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
Your cape, cloak, or shawl stiffens and maintains its shape even if removed. A _[[spells/Sculpted Cape|sculpted cape]]_ has hardness 2 and a number of hit points equal to your caster level, and it can support 1 pound of weight per caster level. An unworn _sculpted cape_ can provide cover.
