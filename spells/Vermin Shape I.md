# <font style='color:orange'>Vermin Shape I</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 3, _magus_ 4, _bloodrager_ 4, _druid_ 3, _hunter_ 3, _alchemist_ 4, _investigator_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a piece of the creature whose form you plan to assume) {a piece of the creature whose form you plan to assume}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 minute/level

 #### Description
When you cast this spell, you assume the form of any Small or _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ creature of the vermin type. If the form you assume has any of the following abilities, you gain the listed ability: _[[universal monster rules/Climb|climb]]_ 30 feet, fly 30 feet (average maneuverability), swim 30 feet, _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 60 feet, _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Scent|scent]]_, and _[[feats/Lunge|lunge]]_. You don't gain full _[[universal monster rules/Immunity|immunity]]_ to mind-affecting effects, but you do gain a +2 _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ bonus on all saving throws against such effects.
  Small vermin: If you take the form of a Small vermin, you gain a +2 size bonus to your Dexterity and a +2 natural armor bonus.
  _Medium_ vermin: If you take the form of a _Medium_ vermin, you gain a +2 size bonus to your Strength and a +3 natural armor bonus.

 #### Other
