# <font style='color:orange'>Greensight</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _shaman_ 2, _druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a leaf) {a leaf}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
The target of this spell gains the ability to see up to 60 feet through thick plant matter as though it were transparent. Greenery, leaves, and vines-even lichen, moss, and slime-offer no concealment to the recipient's sight, though her _[[spells/Vision|vision]]_ still can be blocked by solid wood, such as trees or wooden structures. Undergrowth does not grant concealment to a creature against a target of the effects of _[[universal monster rules/Greensight|greensight]]_.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Wilderness
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
