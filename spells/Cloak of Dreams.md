# <font style='color:blue'>Cloak of Dreams</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 6, _wizard_ 6, _sorcerer_ 6, _witch_ 6, _bard_ 5, _skald_ 5, _shaman_ 7, _psychic_ 6, _mesmerist_ 5, _occultist_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S, M (a rose petal and a drop of perfume) {a rose petal and a drop of perfume}
 #### Effects
**Area:** 5-ft.-radius emanation centered on you
**Range:** 5 ft.
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You are surrounded by a soporific aroma that causes living creatures that begin their turn or end their movement within 5 feet of you to fall asleep for 1 minute. Creatures must save each time they begin their turn or end their movement within the _[[spells/Cloak of Dreams|cloak of dreams]]_, even if they have previously saved against the effect. Sleeping creatures are _[[conditions/Helpless|helpless]]_ but can be awakened with a standard action or after being wounded.
  Creatures with the _[[universal monster rules/Scent|scent]]_ special quality have a -4 penalty on their saves.
