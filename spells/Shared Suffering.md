# <font style='color:orange'>Shared Suffering</font> 
**School:** necromancy [evil] 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _witch_ 2, _inquisitor_ 2, _antipaladin_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, F (a silver dagger inlaid with onyx and jade worth 100 gp) {a silver dagger inlaid with onyx and jade worth 100 gp}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one living creature
**Range:** long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You infuse a _[[items/Weapon/Dagger|dagger]]_ with necromantic energy, then plunge the _dagger_ into your own body. You take 1d6 points of negative energy damage plus 1d6 points of damage for every 2 caster levels you possess beyond 1st (to a maximum of 5d6 at 9th level). The target takes an amount of damage equal to the damage you took this way, plus an amount equal to your Intelligence modifier (if you're a _[[classes/Witch|witch]]_ or _[[classes/Wizard|wizard]]_), Wisdom modifier (if you're an _[[classes/Inquisitor|inquisitor]]_), or Charisma modifier (if you're an _[[classes/Antipaladin|antipaladin]]_ or _[[classes/Sorcerer|sorcerer]]_). Damage from this spell cannot be divided by effects such as _[[spells/Shield Other|shield other]]_ or unwilling shieldAPG; it cuts through such protections completely.

 #### Other
**Source:** Champions Of Corruption
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
