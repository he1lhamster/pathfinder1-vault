# <font style='color:orange'>Prediction of Failure</font> 
**School:** divination [curse, fear, mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 8, _wizard_ 8, _sorcerer_ 8, _witch_ 8, _psychic_ 8, _occultist_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** permanent or 1 round/level (see text)
**Saving Throw:** Will partial; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You wrack the target's body and mind with the anguish and suffering of every _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Bitter|bitter]]_ failure it will ever experience, rendering it permanently _[[conditions/Shaken|shaken]]_ and _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_. A successful Will save reduces the duration to 1 round per level. If the target is a spellcaster, failing this saving throw means it also gains a random minor spellblight (see page 95).
