# <font style='color:green'>Necrostasis</font> 
**School:** necromancy 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 2, _shaman_ 2, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, _inquisitor_ 2, _spiritualist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M/DF (a finger bone) {a finger bone}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one undead creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You drain necromantic energy from an undead creature, inducing a sluggish stupor. The target becomes _[[conditions/Staggered|staggered]]_. For the duration of the spell, dark wisps of energy seep out of the target creature. If the target already has the _staggered_ condition (as a zombie does), it becomes _[[conditions/Dazed|dazed]]_ for 1 round instead.
