_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 3, _bloodrager_ 4, _shaman_ 3, _druid_ 3, _hunter_ 3, _ranger_ 3, _alchemist_ 4, _investigator_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M/DF (a bit of thistle) {a bit of thistle}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one living creature/level that is a reptile, has the dragon type, or has the reptilian subtype, and also has a natural armor bonus of at least +1
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 hour/level
**Saving Throw:** Fort negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
This spell functions like _[[spells/Scale Spikes|scale spikes]]_, except that the spikes _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Growing|growing]]_ out of _[[diseases/The Scales|the scales]]_ have an enhancement bonus on attack and damage rolls equal to +1 for every 4 caster levels (maximum +5). This bonus does not allow the spikes to bypass _[[universal monster rules/Damage Reduction|damage reduction]]_ aside from magic.
