# <font style='color:orange'>Blessing of Courage and Life</font> 
**School:** conjuration (healing) [emotion] 
_cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, _paladin_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one living creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (see below)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
With this _[[spells/Prayer|prayer]]_ you provide long-lasting succor to a wounded creature. For as long as the effect lasts, the target receives a +2 morale bonus on saving throws against _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_ and death effects.
  At any time while the spell is in effect, the target can choose to end the spell as a swift action for a burst of healing energy. The target loses the saving throw bonus, but is healed of 1d8 points of damage +1 point per caster level (maximum +10).

 #### Other
**Source:** APG
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
**Patron:** Death (4);
