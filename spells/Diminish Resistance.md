#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M/DF (a pinch of coarse sand) {a pinch of coarse sand}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You weaken a creature's _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ against one energy type you select: acid, cold, electricity, fire, or sonic. The creature's _resistance_ to that energy type decreases by 5 (minimum 0), plus an additional 5 for every 5 caster levels beyond 3rd (to a maximum of 20 at 18th level).
