# <font style='color:orange'>Secret Coffer</font> 
**School:** conjuration (summoning) 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 10 minutes
 **Components:** V, S, F (the chest and its replica) {the chest and its replica}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one small chest or coffer and up to 1 cu. ft. of goods
**Range:** see text
**Duration:** 60 days or until discharged
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions like _[[spells/Secret Chest|secret chest]]_, except the coffer is only 1 foot square and can contain only 1 cubic foot of goods, and the container can remain safely hidden on the Ethereal Plane for only 14 days before it risks becoming lost. The spell automatically fails if a living creature enters the coffer. The coffer used as a focus for _[[spells/Secret Coffer|secret coffer]]_ must cost at least 500 gp, and its replica costs 5 gp.

 #### Other
**Source:** Black Markets
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
