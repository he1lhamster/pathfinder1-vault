# <font style='color:red'>Horrifying Visage</font> 
**School:** necromancy (haunted) [emotion, fear, mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 3, _magus_ 3, _bard_ 3, _skald_ 3, _shaman_ 3, _cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, _inquisitor_ 3, _psychic_ 3, _spiritualist_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a live spider) {a live spider}
 #### Effects
**Area:** one 5-ft. cube/level (S)
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 day/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You channel residual feelings of terror into the minds of creatures within the area, causing those fears to manifest when confronted with a specific object. All creatures in the area must succeed at a Will save or acquire a _[[spells/Phobia|phobia]]_ (Pathfinder RPG GameMastery Guide 250) for the spell's duration. You choose one kind of creature, object, or image within the area of the spell when it is cast that triggers the _phobia_. The kind of creature, object, or image that triggers the _phobia_ must be named specifically. A creature type or subtype is not specific enough. The _phobia_ can be cured using traditional methods of treating _[[spells/Insanity|insanity]]_, and spells or effects that prevent _[[spells/Possession|possession]]_ or mind control suppress the _phobia_ for their duration. Each time the _phobia_ is triggered in an affected creature, the spell's haunt-like manifestation occurs during that round.

 #### Other
**Source:** Haunted Heroes Handbook

**Potion:** Yes
