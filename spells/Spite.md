# <font style='color:blue'>Spite</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_witch_ 4, _mesmerist_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S, M (rare inks worth 250 gp) {rare inks worth 250 gp}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 hour/level or until discharged (D)

 #### Description
Choose a single touch range spell of 4th level or lower with a casting time of 1 standard action or less. As part of the action of casting _[[spells/Spite|spite]]_, you cast the associated spell and bind it into a defensive ward in the form of a _[[items/Mundane/Tattoo|tattoo]]_, birthmark, or wart somewhere upon your body. The next time you are hit by a melee attack or a combat maneuver is used successfully against you, the stored spell is triggered against your foe. You do not need to succeed on a touch attack to affect the target, but in all other respects the spell is treated as though you had cast it normally. If the attacking creature is not a valid target for the spell, the stored spell is lost with no effect.
  You can have only one _spite_ spell in effect at a time; if you cast this spell a second time, the previous spell effect ends.

 #### Other

**Potion:** Yes
