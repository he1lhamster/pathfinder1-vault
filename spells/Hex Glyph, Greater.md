# <font style='color:red'>Hex Glyph, Greater</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_witch_ 5, _shaman_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 10 minutes
 **Components:** V, S, M (powdered diamond worth 400 gp) {powdered diamond worth 400 gp}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** object touched or up to 5 sq. ft./level
**Area:** object touched or up to 5 sq. ft./level
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** permanent until discharged (D)
**Saving Throw:** see text; **Spell Resistance:** no (object) and yes (see text); 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Hex Glyph|hex glyph]]_, except you can store a hex or a major hex (but not a grand hex). You trace the glyph with _[[items/Mundane/Incense|incense]]_, which must first be sprinkled with powdered diamond (as noted in the material line).
  _[[spells/Read Magic|Read magic]]_ allows its caster to _[[spells/Identify|identify]]_ a greater _hex glyph_ with a DC 16 Spellcraft check.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Class Guide
**Rating:** 1
