# <font style='color:blue'>Fleshworm Infestation</font> 
**School:** conjuration (summoning) [evil] 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 4, _cleric_ 4, _oracle_ 4, _warpriest_ 4, _inquisitor_ 4, _psychic_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude partial (see text); **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
With a touch, you cause an infestation of _[[curses/Ravenous|ravenous]]_ worms to manifest in the target's flesh. The target must make a Fortitude save every round. Failure means it takes 1d6 hit points of damage and 2 points of Dexterity damage, and is _[[conditions/Staggered|staggered]]_ for 1 round. If it makes the save, it takes no hit point or Dexterity damage and is only _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_ for 1 round rather than _staggered_. _[[spells/Fleshworm Infestation|Fleshworm infestation]]_ cannot be ended early by _[[spells/Remove Disease|remove disease]]_ or _[[spells/Heal|heal]]_, as the infestation starts anew if the current worms are slain. _[[spells/Protection From Evil|Protection from evil]]_ negates this spell's effects for as long as the two durations overlap.
  _[[spells/Dispel Evil|Dispel evil]]_ automatically ends a _fleshworm infestation_.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Magic
**Rating:** 4
