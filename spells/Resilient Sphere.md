# <font style='color:orange'>Resilient Sphere</font> 
**School:** evocation [force] 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _psychic_ 4, _occultist_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, F (a crystal sphere) {a crystal sphere}
 #### Effects
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 min./level (D)
**Effect:** 1-ft.-diameter/level sphere, centered around a creature
**Saving Throw:** Reflex negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
A globe of shimmering force encloses a creature, provided the creature is small enough to fit within the diameter of the sphere.
  The sphere contains its subject for the spell's duration. The sphere functions as a _[[spells/Wall Of Force|wall of force]]_, except that it can be negated by _[[spells/Dispel Magic|dispel magic]]_. A subject inside the sphere can breathe normally.
  The sphere cannot be physically moved either by people outside it or by the struggles of those within.

 #### Other
**Source:** PFRPG Core
**Rating:** 2
