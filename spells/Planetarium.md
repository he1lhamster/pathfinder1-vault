#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** concentration + 3 rounds
**Effect:** 15-ft.-radius spherical projection of night sky
**Saving Throw:** Will disbelief (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You project an image of the night sky based on your current location and the local time, allowing you to observe the heavens and all of its celestial bodies and features even during the daytime, indoors, or underground. Anyone within the _[[spells/Planetarium|planetarium]]_'s sphere can see the projection, though outside of the sphere the image becomes grainy and indistinct.
