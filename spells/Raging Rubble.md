# <font style='color:red'>Raging Rubble</font> 
**School:** transmutation [earth] 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 3, _bard_ 3, _skald_ 3, _bloodrager_ 3, _druid_ 3, _hunter_ 3, _cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** concentration + 2 rounds
**Effect:** one swarm of stones
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You animate an area of rubble, gravel, or other small stones, creating a dangerous, rolling area of debris. The _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Animated|animated]]_ rubble has a space of 10 feet and acts like a swarm, damaging (1d6 hit points) and _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Distracting|distracting]]_ (DC 12) anything within it. As a move action, you can direct the rubble to move up to 10 feet. If the rubble is attacked, treat it as a _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ _[[monsters/Animated Object|animated object]]_ with the young creature simple template and the swarm subtype.
