# <font style='color:blue'>Delayed Consumption</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_alchemist_ 5, _investigator_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 day/level or until discharged (D)

 #### Description
When you consume this extract, you quickly consume another extract of your choice-this second extract's effects do not come into effect until a later point. You must consume this second, companion extract on the round following _[[spells/Delayed Consumption|delayed consumption]]_ or waste the extract. The companion extract can be no higher than 4th level, and you must pay any costs associated with the companion extract when you consume it.
  At any point during the duration of this extract, you can cause the companion extract to take effect as an immediate action. You can only have one _delayed consumption_ in effect at one time. If a second is consumed, the first is dispelled without any effect.
