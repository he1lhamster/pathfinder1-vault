# <font style='color:orange'>Compel Hostility</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_witch_ 1, _bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, _summoner_ 1, _unsummoner_ 1, _hunter_ 1, _ranger_ 1, _cleric_ 1, _oracle_ 1, _warpriest_ 1, _inquisitor_ 1, _paladin_ 1, _psychic_ 1, _mesmerist_ 1, _medium_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a drop of your blood) {a drop of your blood}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** see text; **Spell Resistance:** see text; 
 #### Description
Whenever a creature you can see that threatens you makes an attack against one of your allies, as an immediate action, you can compel that creature to attack you instead. When you compel a creature to attack you, you must first overcome that creature's _[[universal monster rules/Spell Resistance|spell resistance]]_, and the creature can attempt a Will saving throw to ignore the compulsion.
  A _[[classes/Summoner|summoner]]_ casting this spell can choose his eidolon as the target of the spell.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Combat
