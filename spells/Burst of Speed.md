# <font style='color:green'>Burst of Speed</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_magus_ 3, _bloodrager_ 3, _hunter_ 3, _ranger_ 3, _inquisitor_ 3, _antipaladin_ 3, _paladin_ 3, _alchemist_ 3, _investigator_ 3, _psychic_ 3, _medium_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 swift action
 **Components:** V 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** see text

 #### Description
Until the end of your turn, you gain a +20-foot bonus to speed (or +10-foot bonus if you are wearing _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ or Heavy armor), your movement does not provoke attacks of opportunity, and you can move through the space of creatures that are larger than you are, but you cannot end your movement this round in a space occupied by a creature.

**Source:** Ultimate Combat
