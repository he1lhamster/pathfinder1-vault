# <font style='color:red'>Animal Purpose Training</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_witch_ 1, _bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _shaman_ 2, _druid_ 2, _hunter_ 1, _ranger_ 1, _inquisitor_ 1, _antipaladin_ 1, _paladin_ 1, _psychic_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 minute
 **Components:** V, S, M (a swatch of black cloth) {a swatch of black cloth}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one indifferent or friendly animal
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 hour/level
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You instill the target animal with a general purpose (see the Handle Animal skill), which can be any purpose except combat training-fighting, _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Guarding|guarding]]_, heavy labor, hunting, performance, or riding. This purpose supersedes the animal's previous trained purpose and any tricks it knows.
  When the spell ends, the animal reverts to its previous trained purpose and known tricks.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Class Guide
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
