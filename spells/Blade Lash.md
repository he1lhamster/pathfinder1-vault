# <font style='color:orange'>Blade Lash</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_magus_ 1, _bloodrager_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** your melee weapon
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
Your weapon elongates and becomes whiplike. As part of casting this spell, you can use this weapon to attempt a _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_ combat maneuver against one creature within 20 feet, and you gain a +10 bonus on your roll, after which the weapon returns to its previous form.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Class Guide
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
