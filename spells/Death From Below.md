# <font style='color:green'>Death From Below</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _bard_ 3, _skald_ 3, _bloodrager_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
You grant the target a _[[feats/Dodge|dodge]]_ bonus to its Armor Class against attacks from larger creatures. The bonus is equal to +1 for every size category the attacker is larger than the target of the spell, to a maximum of +1 per 3 caster levels. If the spell's target is a gnome, the maximum bonus is equal to +1 per 2 caster levels.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Race Guide
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
