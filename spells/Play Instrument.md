# <font style='color:red'>Play Instrument</font> 
**School:** divination 
_bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 10 minute/level

 #### Description
You may play one instrument of your choice as if you had 1 rank in the appropriate Perform skill. Instead of a conventional instrument, you may use an object as an improvised instrument, such as using a _[[items/Mundane/Barrel|barrel]]_ as a drum or a bow as a harp, playing it as if it were crafted to make music.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Magic
