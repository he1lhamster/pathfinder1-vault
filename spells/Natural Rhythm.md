# <font style='color:green'>Natural Rhythm</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a handful of pebbles dropped one by one onto the ground) {a handful of pebbles dropped one by one onto the ground}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
You enhance a creature's ability to _[[spells/Harm|harm]]_ an opponent based on the number of times it has already injured that opponent with a natural attack. Each time the creature successfully strikes an opponent with a natural attack, the subject gains a cumulative +1 bonus on damage rolls made against that opponent when making _[[universal monster rules/Natural Attacks|natural attacks]]_ (maximum +5 bonus). If an attack misses, the bonus to damage for all attacks resets to +0.

 #### Other
**Source:** APG
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
