# <font style='color:orange'>Fester</font> 
**School:** necromancy 
_witch_ 2, _inquisitor_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (rotted meat) {rotted meat}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one living creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level or 1 round; see text
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude partial; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
Necrotic energy permeates the target, blocking healing abilities.
  The subject gains _[[universal monster rules/Spell Resistance|spell resistance]]_ equal to 12 + your caster level against effects that restore hit points or grant temporary hit points. In addition, any healing provided by effects that ignore _spell resistance_ (such as _[[universal monster rules/Fast Healing|fast healing]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Regeneration|regeneration]]_, and some spells) are halved. If the target succeeds on a Fortitude saving throw, _[[spells/Fester|fester]]_ lasts only a single round.
