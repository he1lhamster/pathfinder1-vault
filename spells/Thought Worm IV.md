# <font style='color:blue'>Thought Worm IV</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_psychic_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will partial (see text); **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This functions as per _[[spells/Thought Worm III|thought worm III]]_, except that the penalty on Will saves is -6 and a target that succeeds at its Will save still takes a -3 penalty on Will saves against your mind-affecting spells and effects for the same duration. This spell can be undercast.

 #### Other
**Source:** Psychic Anthology
**Rating:** 4
