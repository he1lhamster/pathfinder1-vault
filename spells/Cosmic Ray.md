# <font style='color:green'>Cosmic Ray</font> 
**School:** evocation 
_arcanist_ 6, _wizard_ 6, _sorcerer_ 6, _magus_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a piece of meteorite) {a piece of meteorite}
 #### Effects
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous and 1 round/level (see text)
**Effect:** ray
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude partial; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You _[[feats/Fling|fling]]_ a ray of _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Deadly|deadly]]_ cosmic energy at the target creature. If you succeed at a ranged touch attack with the ray, the target takes 1d6 points of damage per caster level (maximum 20d6) and must make a Fortitude save. On a failed save, the target becomes _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_ for 1 round per caster level and emits _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Toxic|toxic]]_ cosmic energy for as long as it is _sickened_; any creature that comes within 5 feet of the affected target must succeed at a Fortitude save (DC = spell's DC) or become _sickened_ for 1 round per 2 caster levels.

 #### Other
**Source:** People Of The Stars
**Rating:** 3
