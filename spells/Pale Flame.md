# <font style='color:orange'>Pale Flame</font> 
**School:** evocation [fire] 
_shaman_ 2, _druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (caphorite shard) {caphorite shard}
 #### Effects
**Range:** 0 ft.
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)
**Effect:** flame in your palm
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as per _[[spells/Produce Flame|produce flame]]_, except that the flames never glow brighter than dim light, including any fires started by this spell. These flames cast light only half the distance of a _[[items/Mundane/Torch|torch]]_ and cannot be seen from more than 100 feet away. The flames deal 2d6 points of fire damage + 1 point per caster level (maximum +5). Against plants, this damage increases to 2d6 points + 2 points per caster level (maximum +10).

 #### Other
**Source:** Heroes Of The Darklands
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
