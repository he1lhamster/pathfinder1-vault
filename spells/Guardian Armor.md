# <font style='color:red'>Guardian Armor</font> 
**School:** conjuration (teleportation) 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _magus_ 1, _bloodrager_ 1, _cleric_ 1, _oracle_ 1, _warpriest_ 1, _inquisitor_ 1, _antipaladin_ 1, _paladin_ 1, _occultist_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 immediate action
 **Components:** V, S, F (armor worn) {armor worn}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one willing creature; see below
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell teleports the suit of armor you are wearing off of your body and onto an ally within range; the target must be the same size and general shape as you. This armor appears on the target's body fully formed and properly donned, granting the creature the immediate benefit of its protection. If the target of _[[spells/Guardian Armor|guardian armor]]_ was already wearing armor, the armor wearing it is removed from the target's body and falls undamaged into an adjacent square.
