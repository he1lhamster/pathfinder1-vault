# <font style='color:orange'>Hidden Speech</font> 
**School:** transmutation [language-dependent] 
_witch_ 2, _bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _inquisitor_ 3, _psychic_ 2, _mesmerist_ 2, _medium_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M/DF (a cipher written on a piece of parchment) {a cipher written on a piece of parchment}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you plus one creature/level, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You greatly enhance the ability of the subjects to communicate hidden messages to each other. Each target gains a +10 competence bonus on Bluff checks to send secret _[[spells/Message|message]]_ to other recipients. Others using opposed Sense Motive checks to decipher these messages receive a -5 penalty. All subjects affected by the spell must share a language to be able to pass messages.

 #### Other
**Source:** APG

**Potion:** Yes
