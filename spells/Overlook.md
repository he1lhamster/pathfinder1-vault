# <font style='color:orange'>Overlook</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_bard_ 3, _skald_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 minute/level

 #### Description
You enhance your natural ability to seem small and unimportant, causing those around you to _[[spells/Overlook|overlook]]_ you. You gain a +20 bonus on Stealth checks, which doesn't stack with bonuses from effects that actually make you sneakier, such as _[[spells/Invisibility|invisibility]]_ or _[[spells/Respectful Quiet|respectful quiet]]_ (see below); you do not need cover or concealment to hide; and you can hide while you are being observed. If a creature fails to notice you because of this spell, that creature can still remember seeing a halfling pass by, but _[[universal monster rules/Attach|attach]]_ no importance to the memory. You do not gain these benefits against creatures that are immune to compulsions or mind-affecting effects, and _[[universal monster rules/Spell Resistance|spell resistance]]_ applies. If you take an actively aggressive or extremely conspicuous action, creatures automatically notice you, and you gain no further benefits against those creatures for the remainder of the spell's duration.

 #### Other
**Source:** Inner Sea Races
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
