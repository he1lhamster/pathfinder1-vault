# <font style='color:green'>Breeze</font> 
**School:** evocation [air] 
_arcanist_ 0, _wizard_ 0, _sorcerer_ 0, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a miniature fan) {a miniature fan}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature or object
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 hour (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You create a light wind that blows against the target, from a direction of your choice. The _[[spells/Breeze|breeze]]_ grants the subject a +2 bonus on saves against very hot conditions, severe _[[universal monster rules/Heat|heat]]_, breath weapons, and saves against cloud vapors and gases (such as _[[spells/Cloudkill|cloudkill]]_, _[[spells/Stinking Cloud|stinking cloud]]_, and inhaled poisons). This spell does not function without air or _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Underwater|underwater]]_.
  You can only have one _breeze_ active at any one time. If you cast this spell while another casting is still in effect, the previous casting is dispelled.
