# <font style='color:orange'>Create Mindscape, Greater</font> 
**School:** illusion (phantasm) [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 7, _wizard_ 7, _sorcerer_ 7, _psychic_ 6, _mesmerist_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature/level
**Range:** long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 day/level
**Saving Throw:** Will disbelief; see text; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions like _[[spells/Create Mindscape|create mindscape]]_, except it can affect more creatures, the mindscape lasts longer, and you can choose the mindscape's magic trait. You can also direct a number of believable creatures at a time equal to your caster level. Greater _create mindscape_ can be made permanent with a _[[spells/Permanency|permanency]]_ spell by a caster of 15th level or higher at a cost of 17,500 gp. You and other creatures aware of a permanent mindscape can come and go using the _[[spells/Mindscape Door|mindscape door]]_ spell.
