# <font style='color:blue'>Reinvigorating Wind</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [air, mind-affecting] 
_witch_ 3, _bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _shaman_ 2, _druid_ 3, _hunter_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M/DF (a flower petal) {a flower petal}
 #### Effects
**Area:** cone-shaped burst
**Range:** 30 ft.
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
You exhale deeply, creating a gentle magical wind that invigorates any allies in the affected area, as follows. Any sleeping allies immediately wake up. _[[conditions/Fascinated|Fascinated]]_ allies are _[[conditions/Shaken|shaken]]_ free of the fascinate effect. _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|Flat-footed]]_ allies no longer count as _flat-footed_ even if they have not acted yet. The duration of effects that cause any allies to be _[[conditions/Confused|confused]]_, _[[conditions/Frightened|frightened]]_, _[[conditions/Paralyzed|paralyzed]]_, slowed, or _[[conditions/Stunned|stunned]]_ is decreased by 1d4 rounds (roll separately for each target). If the duration of any such effect is reduced to 0 rounds or fewer, the effect ends for that ally. Finally, any allies lying _[[conditions/Prone|prone]]_ may stand up as an immediate action, provoking attacks of opportunity as normal.

 #### Other
**Source:** Villain Codex
**Rating:** 4
**Potion:** Yes
