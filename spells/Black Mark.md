# <font style='color:red'>Black Mark</font> 
**School:** necromancy [curse, fear] 
_witch_ 7, _druid_ 7, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a flask of seawater) {a flask of seawater}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** permanent
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You mark the target with a black marking on its skin; the mark's exact appearance determined by you, but can be no larger than your hand. The _[[spells/Black Mark|black mark]]_ functions as a _[[spells/Mark of Justice|mark of justice]]_, and when the mark is activated, the target becomes _[[conditions/Shaken|shaken]]_ anytime it is on or in the water more than a 5 feet from shore. In addition, as long as the _black mark_ is active, the target is affected as if subject to _[[spells/Nature's Exile|nature's exile]]_ (Advanced Player's Guide 233), but all creatures with the aquatic or water subtype or with a swim speed are made hostile, even those not of the animal type, though non-aquatic animals are not.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Race Guide
**Rating:** 1
