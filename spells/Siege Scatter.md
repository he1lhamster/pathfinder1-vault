# <font style='color:green'>Siege Scatter</font> 
**School:** evocation [earth] 
_shaman_ 5, _druid_ 5, _hunter_ 4, _ranger_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Area:** 50-ft.-radius spread
**Range:** long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Reflex partial (see text); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
A roiling blast of rough rock and dirt scatters along the ground in the area, dealing 1d6 points of slashing damage per 2 caster levels (maximum 5d6) to all creatures on the ground in the area, even on a successful saving throw. Creatures in the area that fail the Reflex save fall _[[conditions/Prone|prone]]_ and are knocked to the nearest edge of the area, taking 1d6 points of bludgeoning damage for every 10 feet they travel this way. Creatures that did not move during the past round and those with the stability racial trait gain a +4 bonus on the saving throw.

 #### Other
**Source:** Inner Sea Races
**Rating:** 3
