#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one living creature
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
The affected creature suffers from a continuous _[[spells/Confusion|confusion]]_ effect, as the spell.
  _[[spells/Remove Curse|Remove curse]]_ does not remove _[[spells/Insanity|insanity]]_. Greater _[[spells/Restoration|restoration]]_, _[[spells/Heal|heal]]_, _[[spells/Limited Wish|limited wish]]_, _[[spells/Miracle|miracle]]_, or _[[spells/Wish|wish]]_ can restore the creature.
