# <font style='color:purple'>Good Hope</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting, emotion] 
_bard_ 3, _skald_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one living creature/level, no two of which may be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 min./level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
This spell instills powerful hope in the subjects. Each affected creature gains a +2 morale bonus on saving throws, attack rolls, ability checks, skill checks, and weapon damage rolls.
  _[[spells/Good Hope|Good hope]]_ counters and dispels _[[spells/Crushing Despair|crushing despair]]_.

 #### Other
**Source:** PFRPG Core
**Rating:** 5
**Potion:** Yes
