# <font style='color:orange'>Share Shape</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 4, _hunter_ 3, _ranger_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a piece of the creature whose form you plan to assume) {a piece of the creature whose form you plan to assume}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 hour/level (D)

 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Beast Shape II|beast shape II]]_, but you may only assume the form of an animal of a type identical to your animal companion or familiar. If your familiar or companion is not an animal, this spell has no effect.

**Source:** Animal Archive
