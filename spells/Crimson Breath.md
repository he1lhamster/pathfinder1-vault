# <font style='color:red'>Crimson Breath</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 4, _druid_ 4, _hunter_ 4, _cleric_ 4, _oracle_ 4, _warpriest_ 4, _alchemist_ 4, _investigator_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
When you cast this spell, your salivary glands transform and instantly fill with _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Potent|potent]]_ venom. Make a ranged touch attack against a single target in range; if you hit, the target is affected by a dose of breath of the mantis god (see page 158). The Fortitude save DC for this poison is the same as the spell's DC, not the standard save DC for the poison.

 #### Other
**Source:** Adventurer's Guide
