# <font style='color:red'>Starsight</font> 
**School:** divination 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _witch_ 1, _bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, _druid_ 1, _hunter_ 1, _ranger_ 1, _cleric_ 1, _oracle_ 1, _warpriest_ 1, _alchemist_ 1, _investigator_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level

 #### Description
You can observe the night sky and all of its celestial bodies as if it were a clear night, regardless of weather conditions that would otherwise block your view. Your _[[spells/Vision|vision]]_ penetrates any light pollution from nonmagical sources, though this spell doesn't function in _[[spells/Daylight|daylight]]_, indoors, or underground. You see through forest canopies and similar natural obstructions, but only for the purpose of stargazing. For the spell's duration, you gain a +2 insight bonus on Knowledge (geography) checks relating to the stars and planets and Survival checks to avoid getting lost.

 #### Other
