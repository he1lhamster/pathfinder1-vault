# <font style='color:red'>Life Channel</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one touched creature with negative energy affinity
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
When cast on a creature with _[[universal monster rules/Negative Energy Affinity|negative energy affinity]]_, the target is able to convert channeled positive energy into temporary hit points. When subject to an effect that heals hit points only to living creatures (such as _[[spells/Cure Light Wounds|cure light wounds]]_ or channel positive energy), the target gains a number of temporary hit points equal to half the number of hit points that the positive energy would normally _[[spells/Heal|heal]]_. These temporary hit points go away at the end of this spell's duration.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Race Guide
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
