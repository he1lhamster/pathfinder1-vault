# <font style='color:green'>Forced Repentance</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting, emotion] 
_inquisitor_ 4, _paladin_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one evil creature without the evil subtype
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You force an evil creature that does not have the evil subtype to momentarily reflect on its past actions and be overcome by grief and conscience. The target immediately drops _[[conditions/Prone|prone]]_ and begins to loudly _[[spells/Confess|confess]]_ all of its sins and transgressions to the caster for the duration of the spell. The spell immediately ends if you move out of line of sight or if the target is attacked.

 #### Other
**Source:** APG
**Rating:** 3
**Domain:** Redemption (4);
