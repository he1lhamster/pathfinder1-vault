# <font style='color:red'>Flickering Lights</font> 
**School:** evocation [darkness, light] 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _magus_ 2, _bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _shaman_ 2, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, _inquisitor_ 2, _occultist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S, M (a patch of white cloth and a patch of black cloth) {a patch of white cloth and a patch of black cloth}
 #### Effects
**Area:** contiguous area consisting of one 10-foot cube/level (S)
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You cause the illumination in the area to seem to flicker erratically, fluctuating between absolute _[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_ and _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Blinding|blinding]]_ brightness. The level of light in the area changes at the start of each creature's turn, as determined by rolling a percentile die and consulting the following table.
  
  <table><tbody><tr><th>d%</th><th>Illumination level</th></tr><tr><td>1-10</td><td>Supernatural _darkness_</td></tr><tr><td>11-25</td><td>_Darkness_</td></tr><tr><td>26-50</td><td>Dim light</td></tr><tr><td>51-90</td><td>Normal light</td></tr><tr><td>91-00</td><td>Bright light</td></tr></tbody></table>
  
  Even _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ can't see through supernatural _darkness_ (as _[[spells/Deeper Darkness|deeper darkness]]_). Bright light affects creatures with _[[universal monster rules/Light Blindness|light blindness]]_ or _[[universal monster rules/Light Sensitivity|light sensitivity]]_. For the purpose of superseding its effects with higher-level light or _darkness_ spells, _[[spells/Flickering Lights|flickering lights]]_ counts as a light spell when it increases the ambient light level and a _darkness_ spell when it decreases the ambient light level.
