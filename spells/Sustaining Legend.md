# <font style='color:red'>Sustaining Legend</font> 
**School:** conjuration (healing) 
_bard_ 5, _skald_ 5, _cleric_ 6, _oracle_ 6, _warpriest_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature/level, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
The rulers of Azlant are thought to have used this spell to amplify the mythic power of themselves and their most gifted lieutenants. You amplify the mythic power of the target creatures, _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Bolstering|bolstering]]_ them with healing energy each time they draw upon their mythic abilities. When a creature affected by this spell expends uses of mythic power for any ability, it immediately heals 2d8 points of damage + 1 point per caster level (maximum +25). In addition, the creature benefits from one of the following additional effects.
  
  * Remove any one of the following conditions: _[[conditions/Dazzled|dazzled]]_, _[[conditions/Fatigued|fatigued]]_, _[[conditions/Shaken|shaken]]_, _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_, or _[[conditions/Staggered|staggered]]_.
  * Reduce the _[[conditions/Dazed|dazed]]_ or _[[conditions/Stunned|stunned]]_ condition to _staggered_.
  * Reduce the _[[conditions/Exhausted|exhausted]]_ condition to _fatigued_.
  * Reduce the _[[conditions/Frightened|frightened]]_ condition to _shaken_, or the _[[conditions/Panicked|panicked]]_ condition to _frightened_.
  * Reduce the _[[conditions/Nauseated|nauseated]]_ condition to _sickened_.
  
  A target can benefit from the effects of _[[spells/Sustaining Legend|sustaining legend]]_ only once per round, even if the target expends multiple uses of mythic power during that time.

 #### Other
**Source:** Mythic Origins
**Rating:** 1
