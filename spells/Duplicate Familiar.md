# <font style='color:green'>Duplicate Familiar</font> 
**School:** conjuration (creation) 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _witch_ 5, _alchemist_ 4, _investigator_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (alchemically preserved mockingfey feathers), F (familiar) {alchemically preserved mockingfey feathers}{familiar}
 #### Effects
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level
**Effect:** temporary duplicate of familiar touched
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You create a duplicate of a familiar. The familiar's master can use the duplicate as if it were his familiar in all respects, though he doesn't gain the bonus special ability from more than one familiar at a time. When the spell's duration expires, the familiar duplicate shrivels into nothing, even if _[[conditions/Petrified|petrified]]_ or otherwise transformed.

 #### Other
**Source:** Familiar Folio
**Rating:** 3
