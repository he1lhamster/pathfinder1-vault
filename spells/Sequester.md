# <font style='color:red'>Sequester</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_arcanist_ 7, _wizard_ 7, _sorcerer_ 7, _summoner_ 5, _unsummoner_ 6, _psychic_ 7, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a basilisk eyelash and gum arabic) {a basilisk eyelash and gum arabic}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one willing creature or object (up to a 2-ft. cube/level) touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 day/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** none or Will negates (object); **Spell Resistance:** no or yes (object); 
 #### Description
When cast, this spell prevents _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_ spells from detecting or locating the target and also renders the affected target _[[conditions/Invisible|invisible]]_ (as the _[[spells/Invisibility|invisibility]]_ spell). The spell does not prevent the subject from being discovered through tactile means or through the use of devices. Creatures affected by _[[spells/Sequester|sequester]]_ become comatose and are effectively in a state of suspended animation until the spell ends.
  Note: The Will save prevents an attended or magical object from being sequestered. There is no save to see the sequestered creature or object or to detect it with a _divination_ spell.

 #### Other
**Source:** PFRPG Core
**Rating:** 1
