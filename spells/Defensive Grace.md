# <font style='color:green'>Defensive Grace</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_investigator_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)

 #### Description
Each round you can roll an inspiration die without expending a use of inspiration and gain the result as a _[[feats/Dodge|dodge]]_ bonus to AC for that round. You are immune to precision damage (including the extra damage from the _[[feats/Precise Strike|precise strike]]_ deed, sneak attack, studied combat, and studied strike).If a critical hit is confirmed against you, you can dismiss this spell as an immediate action to negate the critical and make the attack instead deal normal damage.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Class Origins
**Rating:** 3
