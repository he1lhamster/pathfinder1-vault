# <font style='color:blue'>Mind Thrust I</font> 
**School:** divination [mind-affecting] 
_psychic_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Will half; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You divine the most vulnerable portions of your opponent's mind and overload it with a glut of _[[classes/Psychic|psychic]]_ information. This attack deals 1d6 points of damage per caster level (maximum 5d6). The target receives a Will save for half damage. This attack has no effect on creatures without an Intelligence score.
