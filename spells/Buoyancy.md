# <font style='color:orange'>Buoyancy</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _witch_ 2, _bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _shaman_ 2, _psychic_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 immediate action
 **Components:** V 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one Medium or smaller object or creature/level, no two of which can be more than 20 ft. apart
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** up to 1 round/level (see text)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless) or Will negates (object); **Spell Resistance:** yes (object); 
 #### Description
The affected targets become incredibly buoyant, floating naturally on any water at least 1 foot deep.
  An affected creature that deliberately tries to submerge must succeed at a DC 20 Swim check every round to stay _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Underwater|underwater]]_. If a target spends at least 1 round on dry land, the spell ends for that creature only.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Class Guide
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
