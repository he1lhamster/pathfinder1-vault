# <font style='color:orange'>Arrow of Law</font> 
**School:** evocation [lawful] 
_cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, _paladin_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF (a holy symbol) {a holy symbol}
 #### Effects
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous (1 round); see text
**Effect:** arrow-shaped projectile of lawful energy
**Saving Throw:** Will partial (see text); **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You fire a shimmering arrow of pure order from your holy symbol at any one target in range as a ranged touch attack.
  A chaotic creature struck by an _[[spells/Arrow of Law|arrow of law]]_ takes 1d8 points of damage per two caster levels (maximum 5d8). A chaotic outsider instead takes 1d6 points of damage per caster level (maximum 10d6) and is _[[conditions/Dazed|dazed]]_ for 1 round. A successful Will save reduces the damage to half and negates the _[[spells/Daze|daze]]_ effect.
  This spell deals only half damage to creatures that are neither chaotic nor lawful, and they are not _dazed_. The arrow has no effect on lawful creatures.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Magic
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
