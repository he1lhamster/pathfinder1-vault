# <font style='color:blue'>Thoughtsense</font> 
**School:** divination [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _psychic_ 4, _mesmerist_ 4, _spiritualist_ 4, _medium_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, M (a bit of dried brain tissue) {a bit of dried brain tissue}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 minute/level

 #### Description
You automatically detect and locate conscious creatures within 60 feet, as if you possessed the _[[universal monster rules/Blindsight|blindsight]]_ ability. _[[spells/Nondetection|Nondetection]]_, _[[spells/Mind Blank|mind blank]]_, and similar effects can block this effect. _[[universal monster rules/Thoughtsense|Thoughtsense]]_ can distinguish between sentient (Intelligence 3 or greater) and non-sentient (Intelligence 1-2) creatures, but otherwise provides no information about the creatures it detects.

 #### Other
**Source:** Occult Adventures
**Rating:** 4
