# <font style='color:orange'>Touch of Fatigue</font> 
**School:** necromancy 
_arcanist_ 0, _wizard_ 0, _sorcerer_ 0, _witch_ 0, _shaman_ 0, _mesmerist_ 0, _occultist_ 0, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a drop of sweat) {a drop of sweat}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You channel negative energy through your touch, fatiguing the target. You must succeed on a touch attack to strike a target. The subject is immediately _[[conditions/Fatigued|fatigued]]_ for the spell's duration.
  This spell has no effect on a creature that is already _fatigued_.
  Unlike with normal fatigue, the effect ends as soon as the spell's duration expires.
