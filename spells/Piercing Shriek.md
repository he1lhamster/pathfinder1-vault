# <font style='color:blue'>Piercing Shriek</font> 
**School:** evocation [pain, sonic] 
_bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _psychic_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You emit an ear-splitting shriek which can be heard only by the target of this spell; all other observers merely see you screaming silently. The target suffers wracking pain from the scream, gaining the _[[conditions/Staggered|staggered]]_ condition. This spell has no effect on deaf creatures and cannot penetrate an area of _[[spells/Silence|silence]]_.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Magic
**Rating:** 4
**Potion:** Yes
