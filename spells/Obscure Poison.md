# <font style='color:red'>Obscure Poison</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _witch_ 1, _bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, _shaman_ 1, _druid_ 1, _hunter_ 1, _cleric_ 1, _oracle_ 1, _warpriest_ 1, _antipaladin_ 1, _alchemist_ 1, _investigator_ 1, _mesmerist_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** S, M (herbs used in antitoxins worth 10 gp) {herbs used in antitoxins worth 10 gp}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one dose of poison or one venomous creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 hour/level
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You make the touched poison difficult to detect or _[[spells/Identify|identify]]_. _[[spells/Detect Poison|Detect poison]]_ and similar effects detect an obscured poison only with a successful caster level check against a DC equal to 15 + your caster level (rolled secretly by the GM). Even if the poison is detected, the DC of Craft (alchemy) or Wisdom checks to _identify_ the poison is increased by 10. If cast upon a venomous creature, _[[spells/Obscure Poison|obscure poison]]_ disguises all of the creature's natural poisons in the same way.
