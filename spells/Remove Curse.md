# <font style='color:blue'>Remove Curse</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 3, _bard_ 3, _skald_ 3, _shaman_ 3, _cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, _inquisitor_ 3, _paladin_ 3, _alchemist_ 3, _investigator_ 3, _mesmerist_ 3, _spiritualist_ 4, _medium_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature or object touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
_[[spells/Remove Curse|Remove curse]]_ can remove all curses on an object or a creature. If the target is a creature, you must make a caster level check (1d20 + caster level) against the DC of each curse affecting the target.
  Success means that the curse is removed. _Remove curse_ does not remove the curse from a cursed _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_, weapon, or suit of armor, although a successful caster level check enables the creature afflicted with any such cursed item to remove and get rid of it.
  _Remove curse_ counters and dispels _[[spells/Bestow Curse|bestow curse]]_.
