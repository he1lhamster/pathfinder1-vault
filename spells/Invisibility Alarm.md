# <font style='color:red'>Invisibility Alarm</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, _hunter_ 1, _ranger_ 1, _inquisitor_ 1, _alchemist_ 1, _investigator_ 1, _psychic_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, F/DF (a tiny bell and a piece of very fine silver wire) {a tiny bell and a piece of very fine silver wire}
 #### Effects
**Area:** 20-ft.-radius emanation centered on a point in space
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Alarm|alarm]]_, except it is triggered only by _[[conditions/Invisible|invisible]]_ creatures entering or leaving the warded area.
  The spell has a different mental _alarm_ depending on whether the _invisible_ creature is entering or leaving the area. This spell does not aid you in _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Sensing|sensing]]_ or pinpointing the location of an _invisible_ creature within the spell's area.
  _[[spells/Invisibility Alarm|Invisibility alarm]]_ can be made permanent with a _[[spells/Permanency|permanency]]_ spell by a caster of 9th level or higher at a cost of 2,500 gp.

 #### Other
