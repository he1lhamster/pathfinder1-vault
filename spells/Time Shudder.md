**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _magus_ 2, _summoner_ 2, _unsummoner_ 2, _psychic_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a tiny hourglass) {a tiny hourglass}
 #### Effects
**Area:** 30-ft.-radius emanation centered on you
**Range:** 30 ft.
**Duration:** 1 round
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You cause a ripple in time that affects all creatures in the area.
  At the start of each creature's turn, there is a 50% chance the creature is hasted (as _[[spells/Haste|haste]]_); otherwise it is slowed (as _[[spells/Slow|slow]]_).
