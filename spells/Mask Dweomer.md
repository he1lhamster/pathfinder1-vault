# <font style='color:orange'>Mask Dweomer</font> 
**School:** illusion (glamer) 
_witch_ 1, _psychic_ 1, _occultist_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a piece of gauze) {a piece of gauze}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature or object touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 day/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** none; see text; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You _[[items/Mundane/Mask|mask]]_ and manipulate a spell's _[[spells/Magic Aura|magic aura]]_ to make it harder to detect. Select one spell effect on the target creature or object. You must have either cast this spell yourself or have perceived it by means such as _[[spells/Detect Magic|detect magic]]_ or _[[spells/Arcane Sight|arcane sight]]_.
  Both the desired spell effect and _[[spells/Mask Dweomer|mask dweomer]]_ are hidden from _detect magic_, although more powerful spells (such as _arcane sight_) pierce the deception if the caster succeeds on a Will save. _[[spells/Analyze Dweomer|Analyze dweomer]]_ automatically detects both _mask dweomer_ and any masked spell effects.

 #### Other
