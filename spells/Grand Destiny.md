# <font style='color:green'>Grand Destiny</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _bard_ 4, _skald_ 4, _summoner_ 5, _alchemist_ 5, _investigator_ 5, _mesmerist_ 5, _medium_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level or until discharged
**Effect:** one willing creature
**Saving Throw:** none (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
You inspire the target with the power to seize a _[[spells/Grand Destiny|grand destiny]]_. The target gains a +4 competence bonus that it can choose to apply before rolling any attack roll, caster level check, saving throw, or skill check. The spell ends once the bonus has been applied to two rolls. At 12th level and 15th level, the bonus can be applied an additional time before the spell is discharged. A subject can be under the effect of only one _grand destiny_ spell at a time.

 #### Other
**Source:** Arcane Anthology
**Rating:** 3
