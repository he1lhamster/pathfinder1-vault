# <font style='color:green'>Wing Bounty</font> 
**School:** conjuration (creation) 
_shaman_ 2, _druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Range:** feet (0)
**Duration:** 1 day/level
**Effect:** berries (2d4) + berries/level (2)
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
Your wings sprout a variety of colorful and flavorful berries. Each berry functions as if affected by _[[spells/Goodberry|goodberry]]_. You can choose for one affected berry to also be poisonous (as _[[poisons/Striped Toadstool|striped toadstool]]_); only you and druids of 5th level or higher can tell which is which without magic.

**Source:** Ultimate Wilderness
