# <font style='color:green'>Intellect Fortress III</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_psychic_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 immediate action
 **Components:** V 
 #### Effects
**Area:** 20-ft.-radius emanation centered on you
**Range:** 20 ft.
**Duration:** 1 round
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Intellect Fortress II|intellect fortress II]]_, but creatures within the area who succeed at a saving throw against a _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_ or emotion effect suffer no effects, even if there is usually a partial effect on a successful saving throw. This spell can be undercast.

 #### Other
**Source:** Occult Adventures
**Rating:** 3
