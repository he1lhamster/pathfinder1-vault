# <font style='color:orange'>Passing Fancy</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _witch_ 2, _bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _psychic_ 2, _mesmerist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one living creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 4d4 rounds (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
Taldor's infamous _[[monsters/Lion|Lion]]_ Blades imported this spell from the _[[classes/Ninja|ninja]]_ clans of Minkai, and both groups use it to great effect when maneuvering among the shallow upper classes. When you cast the spell, you dictate one specific topic or recent event and instill a magical obsession with that subject within the target. An affected creature becomes _[[conditions/Fascinated|fascinated]]_ by the topic, and becomes completely focused on discussing the event with anyone nearby, possibly wandering off or _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Distracting|distracting]]_ unaffected creatures. _[[spells/Passing Fancy|Passing fancy]]_ has no effect on a creature with an Intelligence of 2 or less. Because the target talks and interacts with its usual cadence, onlookers take a -10 penalty on Sense Motive checks to notice that anything is amiss with the target's behavior.

 #### Other
**Source:** Spymaster's Handbook
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
