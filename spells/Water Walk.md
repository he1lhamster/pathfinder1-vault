# <font style='color:orange'>Water Walk</font> 
**School:** transmutation [water] 
_witch_ 3, _shaman_ 3, _hunter_ 3, _ranger_ 3, _cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, _psychic_ 3, _medium_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one touched creature/level
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 10 min./level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
The transmuted creatures can tread on any liquid as if it were firm ground. Mud, oil, snow, quicksand, running water, ice, and even lava can be traversed easily, since the subjects' feet _[[feats/Hover|hover]]_ an inch or two above the surface. Creatures crossing molten lava still take damage from the _[[universal monster rules/Heat|heat]]_ because they are near it. The subjects can walk, run, charge, or otherwise move across the surface as if it were normal ground.
  If the spell is cast _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Underwater|underwater]]_ (or while the subjects are partially or wholly submerged in whatever liquid they are in), the subjects are borne toward the surface at 60 feet per round until they can stand on it.
