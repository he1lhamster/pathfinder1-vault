# <font style='color:orange'>Leshy Swarm</font> 
**School:** conjuration (summoning) 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _bard_ 3, _skald_ 3, _summoner_ 3, _druid_ 3, _hunter_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S, M, DF 
 #### Effects
**Range:** close (25 feet + 5 feet/2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Effect:** 10-foot-square swarm of leshys
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You _[[feats/Call Out|call out]]_ to nearby nature spirits and grant them temporary plant bodies. These creatures are similar to leshys, except they are Tiny and far more simpleminded. The swarm uses the statistics for a rat swarm, except it does not carry a disease or have _[[universal monster rules/Scent|scent]]_. The leshy swarm has _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ with a range of 60 feet and is immune to electricity, mind-affecting effects, _[[universal monster rules/Paralysis|paralysis]]_, poison, _[[spells/Polymorph|polymorph]]_, sleep effects, sonic, and stunning.You can direct the leshy swarm to move up to its speed along a path for your choice by spending a standard action to communicate your desires through the spell, or by spending a move action if you can speak to creatures that understand only Druidic and Sylvan. The leshys are _[[conditions/Confused|confused]]_ by their sudden _[[spells/Possession|possession]]_ of new bodies and assume that nearly any creature you direct them toward is a threat. They attack all creatures within their area except for you and other creatures with the plant type.
