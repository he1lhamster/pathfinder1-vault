# <font style='color:red'>Serenity</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [emotion, mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 6, _wizard_ 6, _sorcerer_ 6, _bard_ 4, _skald_ 4, _cleric_ 5, _oracle_ 5, _warpriest_ 5, _psychic_ 5, _mesmerist_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature/level, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You fill the targets' minds with feelings of tranquility. Those attempting to commit violence become stricken with wracking pain and take 3d6 points of nonlethal damage each round they attempt to _[[spells/Harm|harm]]_ another creature. If attacked, affected individuals can defend themselves and may participate in combat by using _[[feats/Combat Expertise|Combat Expertise]]_, fighting defensively, or taking the total defense action without triggering the spell's nonlethal damage.
