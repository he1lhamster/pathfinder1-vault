# <font style='color:orange'>Adhesive Spittle</font> 
**School:** conjuration (creation) 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _witch_ 1, _alchemist_ 1, _investigator_ 1, _psychic_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** 15 ft.
**Duration:** 1 round/level or until discharged (see text)
**Saving Throw:** Reflex partial; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
Once during this spell's duration, you can spit a viscous liquid as a standard action. This liquid functions as a _[[items/Mundane/Tanglefoot bag|tanglefoot bag]]_, except you do not have to make a successful attack roll to hit your target. The DCs to counteract this _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Adhesive|adhesive]]_ (to avoid being stuck to the floor, to fly, to break the _adhesive_, or to cast a spell) use the spell's DC rather than a _tanglefoot bag_'s normal DCs. The _adhesive_ persists for 2d4 rounds after you spit it.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Class Guide
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
