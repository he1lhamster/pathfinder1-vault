# <font style='color:red'>Undine's Curse</font> 
**School:** necromancy [curse, evil] 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _witch_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 hour/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
The target loses its body's natural ability to breathe automatically. As long as it remains conscious and is able to take physical actions, it keeps breathing and is able to function normally. If it is ever _[[conditions/Unconscious|unconscious]]_ (including sleeping) or unable to take physical actions, it stops breathing, must hold its breath, and might begin to suffocate. Creatures that do not have to breathe are immune to this spell.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Race Guide
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
