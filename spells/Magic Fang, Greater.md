# <font style='color:blue'>Magic Fang, Greater</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_summoner_ 3, _unsummoner_ 3, _druid_ 3, _hunter_ 3, _ranger_ 3, _spiritualist_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one living creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 hour/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
This spell functions like _[[spells/Magic Fang|magic fang]]_, except that the enhancement bonus on attack and damage rolls is +1 per four caster levels (maximum +5). This bonus does not allow a natural weapon or unarmed strike to bypass _[[universal monster rules/Damage Reduction|damage reduction]]_ aside from magic.
  Alternatively, you may imbue all of the creature's natural weapons with a +1 enhancement bonus (regardless of your caster level).
  Greater _magic fang_ can be made permanent with a _[[spells/Permanency|permanency]]_ spell.
