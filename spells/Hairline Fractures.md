# <font style='color:green'>Hairline Fractures</font> 
**School:** transmutation [earth] 
_witch_ 1, _shaman_ 1, _druid_ 1, _hunter_ 1, _cleric_ 1, _oracle_ 1, _warpriest_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a broken twig) {a broken twig}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** 5-foot-square section of stone or a creature with the  earth subtype
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates (object); **Spell Resistance:** yes (object); 
 #### Description
With a single touch, you create temporary _[[spells/Hairline Fractures|hairline fractures]]_ in a piece of stone or a creature with the earth subtype. If you cast this spell on a section of stone, you reduce its hardness to 5 and its hit points to 10/inch of thickness. If you cast this spell on a creature with the earth subtype, that creature takes a -2 penalty to AC for the spell's duration. _[[spells/Make Whole|Make whole]]_ reverses this spell's effect.

 #### Other
**Source:** Inner Sea Gods
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
