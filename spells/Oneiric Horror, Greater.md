# <font style='color:blue'>Oneiric Horror, Greater</font> 
**School:** illusion (phantasm) [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _bard_ 4, _skald_ 4, _psychic_ 4, _mesmerist_ 3, _medium_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one living creature
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates, Fortitude negates, see text; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Oneiric Horror|oneiric horror]]_ except each round the subject fails its Will save, it takes 1 point of Strength damage, and after the spell ends, the subject must succeed at a Fortitude save or be _[[conditions/Fatigued|fatigued]]_ for a number of minutes equal to the number of rounds spent under the spell's effect.

 #### Other
**Source:** Occult Adventures
**Rating:** 4
**Potion:** Yes
