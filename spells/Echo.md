# <font style='color:red'>Echo</font> 
**School:** illusion (figment) 
_witch_ 1, _bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, _druid_ 1, _hunter_ 1, _ranger_ 1, _psychic_ 1, _mesmerist_ 1, _occultist_ 1, _spiritualist_ 1, _medium_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** S, F (a conch shell) {a conch shell}
 #### Effects
**Area:** one 10-ft. cube/level (S)
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) or long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level); see text
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will disbelief (if interacted with); **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You cause a sound heard in the target area up to 1 round ago and lasting up to 1 round in duration to repeat at a regular interval. The original sound need not have come from the area, but it echoes from the target area at its original full volume. Any special effects of the sound are not duplicated by this spell. If the area is naturally _[[conditions/Prone|prone]]_ to echoes, such as a space surrounded on at least two sides by cliffs or high river banks, the spell's range is long. Otherwise, the spell's range is close.
  You can concentrate as a standard action to alter the _[[spells/Echo|echo]]_. You can change it to be any sound audible in the area within the last 1 round, move the apparent source of the sound within the area, or attempt a Bluff check to create a _[[universal monster rules/Distraction|distraction]]_ to hide with a bonus equal to your caster level.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Wilderness
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
