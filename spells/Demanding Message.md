# <font style='color:green'>Demanding Message</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [language-dependent, mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 4, _bard_ 3, _skald_ 3, _psychic_ 3, _mesmerist_ 3, _occultist_ 4, _medium_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, F (a piece of copper wire) {a piece of copper wire}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature/level
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level, then 1 hour/level or until completed; see text (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (see text); **Spell Resistance:** yes; see text; 
 #### Description
This spell initially functions as _[[spells/Message|message]]_ (allowing no save or _[[universal monster rules/Spell Resistance|spell resistance]]_). Once during the _message_ effect, you can concentrate as a standard action to issue a _[[spells/Suggestion|suggestion]]_ to one target as part of delivering a _message_. _Spell resistance_ and a Will save apply to the _suggestion_, and it lasts for 1 hour per level or until completed.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Intrigue
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
