# <font style='color:green'>Aspect of the Falcon</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_druid_ 1, _hunter_ 1, _ranger_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 minute/level

 #### Description
You take on an aspect of a falcon. Your eyes become wide and raptor-like, and you grow feathers on the sides of your head. You gain a +3 competence bonus on Perception checks, a +1 competence bonus on ranged attacks, and the critical multiplier for your bows and crossbows becomes 19-20/x3.
  This effect does not stack with any other effect that expands the threat range of a weapon, such as the _[[feats/Improved Critical|Improved Critical]]_ feat or a _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Keen|keen]]_ weapon.

 #### Other
**Source:** APG
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
