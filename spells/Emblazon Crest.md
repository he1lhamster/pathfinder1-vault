# <font style='color:red'>Emblazon Crest</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _magus_ 1, _paladin_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** S, F (an example of the crest or coat of arms) {an example of the crest or coat of arms}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 hour/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
This spell ensures the subject touched is always able to display her proper crest and coat of arms. Any _[[items/Mundane/Tabard|tabard]]_ or tunic worn and any _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_ carried by the subject while this spell is active can be made to display a coat of arms when the spell is cast. For example, even if the subject picks up a different _shield_, it still displays the subject's crest, regardless of the _shield_'s normal appearance.

 #### Other
**Source:** Knights Of The Inner Sea
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
