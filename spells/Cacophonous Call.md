# <font style='color:blue'>Cacophonous Call</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a scrap of sheet music) {a scrap of sheet music}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You fill your target's mind with a blaring cacophony of discordant sounds, making it hard for the target to act and concentrate.
  The creature gains the _[[conditions/Nauseated|nauseated]]_ condition for the duration of the spell if it fails its Will save.

 #### Other
**Source:** APG
**Rating:** 4
**Potion:** Yes
