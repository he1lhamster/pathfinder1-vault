# <font style='color:orange'>Litany of Weakness</font> 
**School:** necromancy [language-dependent] 
_inquisitor_ 1, _antipaladin_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 swift action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round
**Saving Throw:** no; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
Your litany proclaims your target weak, _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sapping|sapping]]_ its strength. The target is _[[conditions/Fatigued|fatigued]]_ for 1 round. While subject to this spell, the target cannot be the target of another spell that has the word "litany" in the title.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Combat
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
