# <font style='color:orange'>Hide From Undead, Greater</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, _inquisitor_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched/level (1)
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless) (see text); **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Hide from Undead|hide from undead]]_, except the spell does not immediately end if a warded creature attempts to turn or _[[spells/Command Undead|command undead]]_, channels positive energy, touches an undead creature, or attacks any creature. Instead, when the warded creature takes one of these actions, an intelligent undead creature can attempt another Will saving throw to negate the effects of the spell.

 #### Other
**Source:** People of the Wastes
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
