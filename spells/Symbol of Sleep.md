# <font style='color:red'>Symbol of Sleep</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _witch_ 5, _cleric_ 5, _oracle_ 5, _warpriest_ 5, _mesmerist_ 5, _occultist_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 10 minutes
 **Components:** V, S, M (mercury and phosphorus, plus powdered diamond and opal worth a total of 1,000 gp) {mercury and phosphorus, plus powdered diamond and opal worth a total of 1,000 gp}
 #### Effects
**Range:** 0 ft.; see text
**Duration:** see text
**Effect:** one symbol
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions like _[[spells/Symbol of Death|symbol of death]]_, except that all creatures of 10 HD or less within 60 feet of the _[[spells/Symbol of Sleep|symbol of sleep]]_ instead fall into a catatonic slumber for 3d6 x 10 minutes. Unlike with the sleep spell, sleeping creatures cannot be awakened by nonmagical means before this time expires.
  Unlike _symbol of death_, _symbol of sleep_ has no hit point limit; once triggered, a _symbol of sleep_ simply remains active for 10 minutes per caster level.
  Note: Magic traps such as _symbol of sleep_ are hard to detect and disable. A _[[classes/Rogue|rogue]]_ (only) can use the Perception skill to find a _symbol of sleep_ and Disable Device to thwart it. The DC in each case is 25 + spell level, or 30 for _symbol of sleep_.
