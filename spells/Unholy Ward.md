# <font style='color:orange'>Unholy Ward</font> 
**School:** abjuration [evil] 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _cleric_ 4, _oracle_ 4, _warpriest_ 4, _inquisitor_ 3, _antipaladin_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Fort negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
This spell grants you _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ to the holy powers of good-aligned deities and their servants. You resist 10 points of damage from holy sources (_[[spells/Divine Power|divine power]]_ damage from _[[spells/Flame Strike|flame strike]]_, additional damage from smite evil, channeled positive energy, etc.), provided they originate from a good-aligned source or a servant of a good-aligned god. You ignore a maximum of 10 points of damage per caster level, at which point the spell ends.

 #### Other
**Source:** Agents Of Evil
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
