# <font style='color:blue'>Destruction</font> 
**School:** necromancy [death] 
_witch_ 8, _shaman_ 8, _cleric_ 7, _oracle_ 7, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, F (holy or unholy symbol costing 500 gp) {holy or unholy symbol costing 500 gp}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude partial; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell instantly delivers 10 points of damage per caster level. If the spell slays the target, it consumes the remains utterly in holy (or _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Unholy|unholy]]_) fire (but not its equipment or possessions). If the target's Fortitude saving throw succeeds, it instead takes 10d6 points of damage. The only way to restore life to a character who has failed to save against this spell (and was slain) is to use _[[spells/True Resurrection|true resurrection]]_, a carefully worded _[[spells/Wish|wish]]_ spell followed by _[[spells/Resurrection|resurrection]]_, or _[[spells/Miracle|miracle]]_.
