# <font style='color:orange'>Fastidiousness</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, _cleric_ 1, _oracle_ 1, _warpriest_ 1, _paladin_ 1, _psychic_ 1, _mesmerist_ 1, _occultist_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 24 hours

 #### Description
While protected by fastidiousness, your person and your equipment stay clean and dry. Dust and moisture _[[spells/Slough|slough]]_ off immediately; even when you are completely soiled, your appearance reverts to a pristine state over the course of 1 minute. While this spell offers no protection against most attacks, staying constantly sanitary grants you a +2 circumstance bonus on Fortitude saving throws against inhaled and ingested diseases.Members of the _[[npcs/Prophet of Kalistrade|Prophet of Kalistrade]]_ prestige class can cast this spell, even though it is not a _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_ or enchantment.
