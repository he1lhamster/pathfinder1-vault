# <font style='color:red'>Forgetful Slumber</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 4, _bard_ 4, _skald_ 4, _psychic_ 4, _mesmerist_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S, M (a few drops of river water) {a few drops of river water}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one living creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell acts as the deeper slumber spell, but only affects one creature of 10 Hit Dice or fewer. In addition, a creature affected by this spell awakens with no knowledge of the events that led to the spell's casting. The target loses all memory from the 5 minutes prior to falling asleep. No effect short of a _[[spells/Miracle|miracle]]_ or _[[spells/Wish|wish]]_ can restore memories lost by this spell.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Race Guide
**Rating:** 1
