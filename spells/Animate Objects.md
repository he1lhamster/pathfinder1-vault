# <font style='color:blue'>Animate Objects</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_witch_ 6, _bard_ 6, _skald_ 6, _cleric_ 6, _oracle_ 6, _warpriest_ 6, _psychic_ 6, _occultist_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one Small object per caster level; see text
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You imbue inanimate objects with _[[feats/Mobility|mobility]]_ and a semblance of life. Each such _[[monsters/Animated Object|animated object]]_ then immediately attacks whomever or whatever you initially designate.
  An _animated object_ can be of any nonmagical material. You may animate one Small or smaller object or a corresponding number of larger objects as follows: A _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ object counts as two Small or smaller objects, a Large object as four, a Huge object as eight, a Gargantuan object as 16, and a Colossal object as 32. You can change the designated target or targets as a move action, as if directing an active spell. See the Pathfinder RPG Bestiary for the statistics of _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Animated|animated]]_ objects.
  This spell cannot affect objects carried or worn by a creature.
  _[[spells/Animate Objects|Animate objects]]_ can be made permanent with a _[[spells/Permanency|permanency]]_ spell.

 #### Other
**Source:** PFRPG Core
**Rating:** 4
**Permanency:** Yes
**Domain:** Chaos (6), Flotsam (6); **Bloodline:** Impossible (13);
