# <font style='color:orange'>Fiendish Wrath</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [emotion, mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 4, _bard_ 3, _skald_ 3, _summoner_ 3, _unsummoner_ 3, _bloodrager_ 3, _alchemist_ 3, _investigator_ 3, _psychic_ 4, _mesmerist_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one willing living creature per 3 levels, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** concentration + 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
Each affected creature flies into a combat frenzy. The target gains a +2 morale bonus to Strength and Constitution, and a +1 morale bonus on Will saves. If an affected creature strikes an enemy with two claw attacks or two unarmed strikes while in this frenzy, it can use the _[[universal monster rules/Rend|rend]]_ ability, dealing an additional amount of damage equal to its claw or unarmed strike damage plus 1-1/2 times its Strength bonus.

 #### Other
**Source:** Plane-Hopper's Handbook
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
