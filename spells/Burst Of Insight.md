# <font style='color:green'>Burst Of Insight</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_psychic_ 1, _mesmerist_ 1, _spiritualist_ 1, _medium_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 immediate action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You plumb the depths of your mind for insight, leaving you momentarily frazzled. When you are about to make a d20 roll based on Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma, you can cast this spell to gain a +8 enhancement bonus to Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma for that roll, but you are _[[conditions/Dazed|dazed]]_ for 1 round afterward.

 #### Other
**Source:** Occult Adventures
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
