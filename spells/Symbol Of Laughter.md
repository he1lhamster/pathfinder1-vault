# <font style='color:red'>Symbol Of Laughter</font> 
**School:** enchantment (charm) [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 4, _mesmerist_ 3, _occultist_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 10 minutes
 **Components:** V, S, M (mercury and phosphorus, plus powdered diamond and opal worth a total of 1,000 gp) {mercury and phosphorus, plus powdered diamond and opal worth a total of 1,000 gp}
 #### Effects
**Range:** 0 ft.; see text
**Duration:** see text
**Effect:** one symbol
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Symbol of Death|symbol of death]]_, except that all creatures within 60 feet are affected with uncontrollable laughter (as _[[spells/Hideous Laughter|hideous laughter]]_) for 1 round per caster level.
  Unlike _symbol of death_, _[[spells/Symbol Of Laughter|symbol of laughter]]_ has no hit point limit; once it is triggered, a _symbol of laughter_ remains active for 1 minute per caster level.
  _Symbol of laughter_ can be made permanent with a _[[spells/Permanency|permanency]]_ spell by a caster of 10th level or higher at a cost of 5,000 gp.
