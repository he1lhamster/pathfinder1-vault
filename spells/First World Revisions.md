# <font style='color:red'>First World Revisions</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 2, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, _antipaladin_ 3, _alchemist_ 2, _investigator_ 2, _psychic_ 2, _medium_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** willing wayang touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 24 hours (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
This spell functions as ancestral regressionARG, except as noted above and as follows. The _[[monsters/Wayang|wayang]]_ loses her _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ racial trait and gains the _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_ racial trait in its place. The alignment and personality of the _wayang_ are not affected by the _[[spells/Transformation|transformation]]_, but the spell conceals her alignment as per _[[spells/Undetectable Alignment|undetectable alignment]]_. Unlike _[[spells/Ancestral Regression|ancestral regression]]_, this spell grants the target a +20 bonus on Disguise checks to pass as a gnome; even though the _wayang_ appears as a gnomish analog of herself, the differences between gnomes and wayangs are great enough that she cannot be mistaken for a gnome by other wayangs who know her.

 #### Other
**Source:** Blood Of Shadows
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
