# <font style='color:orange'>Telepathic Bond</font> 
**School:** divination 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _witch_ 5, _inquisitor_ 5, _psychic_ 4, _mesmerist_ 4, _spiritualist_ 5, _medium_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (two eggshells from two different creatures) {two eggshells from two different creatures}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you plus one willing creature per three levels, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 10 min./level (D)
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You forge a _[[spells/Telepathic Bond|telepathic bond]]_ among yourself and a number of willing creatures, each of which must have an Intelligence score of 3 or higher. Each creature included in the link is linked to all the others. The creatures can communicate telepathically through the bond regardless of language. No special power or influence is established as a result of the bond. Once the bond is formed, it works over any distance (although not from one plane to another).
  If desired, you may leave yourself out of the _telepathic bond_ forged. This decision must be made at the time of casting.
  _Telepathic bond_ can be made permanent with a _[[spells/Permanency|permanency]]_ spell, though it only bonds two creatures per casting of _permanency_.

 #### Other
**Source:** PFRPG Core
**Rating:** 2
**Permanency:** Yes
**Domain:** Community (5), Language (5), Thought (5), Friendship (5), Aeon (5);
