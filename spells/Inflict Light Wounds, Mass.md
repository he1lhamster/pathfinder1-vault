# <font style='color:red'>Inflict Light Wounds, Mass</font> 
**School:** necromancy 
_witch_ 6, _shaman_ 5, _cleric_ 5, _oracle_ 5, _warpriest_ 5, _inquisitor_ 5, _occultist_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature/level, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Will half; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
Negative energy spreads out in all directions from the point of origin, dealing 1d8 points of damage + 1 point per caster level (maximum +25) to nearby living enemies.
  Like other inflict spells, mass _[[spells/Inflict Light Wounds|inflict light wounds]]_ cures undead in its area rather than damaging them. A _[[classes/Cleric|cleric]]_ capable of spontaneously casting inflict spells can also spontaneously cast mass inflict spells.

 #### Other
**Source:** PFRPG Core
**Rating:** 1
