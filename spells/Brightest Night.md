# <font style='color:orange'>Brightest Night</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _witch_ 1, _bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, _druid_ 1, _hunter_ 1, _cleric_ 1, _oracle_ 1, _warpriest_ 1, _paladin_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature, willing (1) + creature/3 levels, willing (1) no two of which can be more than 30 feet apart
**Range:** close (25 feet + 5 feet/2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 hour/level
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You grant _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_ to the affected creatures. Creatures that already have _low-light vision_ have their existing lowlight _[[spells/Vision|vision]]_ enhanced so that they can see four times as far in conditions of dim light. Creatures within 10 feet of someone under the effects of brightest night do not gain concealment from dim light.
