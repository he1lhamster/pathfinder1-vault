# <font style='color:orange'>Mantle Of Calm</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [emotion, mind-affecting] 
_shaman_ 3, _cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, _inquisitor_ 3, _paladin_ 3, _psychic_ 3, _mesmerist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** none and Will negates (see text); **Spell Resistance:** no and yes (see text); 
 #### Description
You surround yourself with a _[[spells/Mantle Of Calm|mantle of calm]]_ _[[spells/Serenity|serenity]]_. You take a -2 penalty on attack rolls, and opponents gain a +2 bonus on saving throws against spells you cast. Any creature affected by a _[[spells/Rage|rage]]_ effect (_[[classes/Barbarian|barbarian]]_'s _rage_, _[[classes/Bloodrager|bloodrager]]_'s bloodrage, _[[universal monster rules/Blood Rage|blood rage]]_ monster ability, _rage_ spell, _[[classes/Skald|skald]]_'s _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Inspired|inspired]]_ _rage_ raging song, and so on) that strikes you with a melee attack must attempt a Will saving throw, without the _rage_ effect's bonus to Will; failure means the _rage_ effect ends (as if it were dispelled or the creature voluntarily ended it, as appropriate). If you become affected by a _rage_ effect while this spell is active, this spell immediately ends. If your _rage_ effect comes from a _skald_'s raging song, then it ends only for you, and you cannot regain the effects of the song.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Class Guide
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
