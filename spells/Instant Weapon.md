# <font style='color:green'>Instant Weapon</font> 
**School:** conjuration (creation) [force] 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _magus_ 2, _bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _bloodrager_ 2, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, _inquisitor_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Range:** 0 ft.
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Effect:** melee weapon of opaque force

 #### Description
You create a melee weapon sized appropriately for you from opaque force. You are considered proficient with this weapon, which acts in all ways as a masterwork weapon typical of its type. The _[[spells/Instant Weapon|instant weapon]]_ has hardness 20 and the same number of hit points as a typical weapon of its type. As a force effect, it can strike and damage _[[universal monster rules/Incorporeal|incorporeal]]_ creatures. If the _instant weapon_ leaves your hand at any time, the spell ends at the beginning of your next turn.
