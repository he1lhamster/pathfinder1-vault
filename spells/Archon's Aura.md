# <font style='color:green'>Archon's Aura</font> 
**School:** evocation [good, lawful] 
_cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, _paladin_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Area:** 20-ft. radius centered on you
**Range:** 20 ft.
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You gain a powerful aura, similar to an _[[spells/Archon's Aura|archon's aura]]_ of menace.
  Any hostile creature within a 20-foot radius of you must make a Will save to resist the effects of this aura. If the creature fails, it takes a -2 penalty on attack rolls and saving throws and to Armor Class for the duration of this spell, or until it successfully hits you with an attack. A creature that has resisted or _[[conditions/Broken|broken]]_ the cannot be affected again by this particular casting of _archon's aura_.
