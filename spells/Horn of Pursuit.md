# <font style='color:red'>Horn of Pursuit</font> 
**School:** evocation [sonic] 
_bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, _hunter_ 1, _ranger_ 1, _inquisitor_ 1, _paladin_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** S 
 #### Effects
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 round
**Effect:** 3 peals of a horn
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You create the sound of a large hunting horn, blowing up to three notes as loud as a roaring dragon. These notes can be heard up to 2 miles away in typical outdoor conditions.
  You can make these sounds at any point during the spell's duration as a free action, and each sounding can be short or long, allowing you to send very simple coded messages.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Magic
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
