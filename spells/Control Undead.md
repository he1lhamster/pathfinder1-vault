# <font style='color:orange'>Control Undead</font> 
**School:** necromancy 
_arcanist_ 7, _wizard_ 7, _sorcerer_ 7, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a piece of bone and a piece of raw meat) {a piece of bone and a piece of raw meat}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** up to 2 HD/level of undead creatures, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 min./level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell enables you to _[[spells/Control Undead|control undead]]_ creatures for a short period of time. You _[[spells/Command|command]]_ them by voice and they understand you, no matter what language you speak. Even if vocal communication is impossible, the controlled undead do not attack you. At the end of the spell, the subjects revert to their normal behavior.
  Intelligent undead creatures remember that you controlled them, and they may seek revenge after the spell's effects end.
