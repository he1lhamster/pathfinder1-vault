# <font style='color:orange'>Unhallowed Blows</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _witch_ 1, _cleric_ 1, _oracle_ 1, _warpriest_ 1, _inquisitor_ 1, _antipaladin_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature/level, undead (1) no two of which can be more than 30 feet apart, or creature, undead (1) (see text)
**Range:** close (25 feet + 5 feet/2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
This spell empowers the _[[universal monster rules/Natural Attacks|natural attacks]]_ or unarmed strikes of undead creatures, granting a +1 enhancement bonus on attack and damage rolls made by the target with its unarmed strikes or attacks with one natural attack. This spell can cause an undead creature's unarmed strike to deal lethal damage, but in this case, the attack does not gain an enhancement bonus.Unhallowed blows can be made permanent with a _[[spells/Permanency|permanency]]_ spell. In this case, the caster must be at least 9th level and must _[[spells/Expend|expend]]_ 2,500 gp worth of diamond dust as a material component.

 #### Other
**Source:** People of the Wastes
**Rating:** 2
**Permanency:** Yes
**Potion:** Yes
