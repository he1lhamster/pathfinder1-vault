# <font style='color:green'>Rage</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting, emotion] 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 3, _bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _summoner_ 3, _unsummoner_ 3, _bloodrager_ 3, _alchemist_ 3, _investigator_ 3, _psychic_ 3, _mesmerist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one willing living creature per three levels, no two of which may be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** concentration + 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
Each affected creature gains a +2 morale bonus to Strength and Constitution, a +1 morale bonus on Will saves, and a -2 penalty to AC. The effect is otherwise identical with a _[[classes/Barbarian|barbarian]]_'s _[[spells/Rage|rage]]_ except that the subjects aren't _[[conditions/Fatigued|fatigued]]_ at the end of the _rage_.
