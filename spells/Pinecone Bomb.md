# <font style='color:orange'>Pinecone Bomb</font> 
**School:** evocation 
_witch_ 2, _shaman_ 2, _druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, _ranger_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M 
 #### Effects
**Area:** 10-foot radius spread
**Range:** close (25 feet + 5 feet/2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Reflex half; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
Created by Ghorus when he was working to create the ghorans, pinecone bomb infuses a small pinecone with power, causing it to _[[spells/Detonate|detonate]]_ with explosive force when thrown. You throw the pinecone at a target or point within range and it detonates immediately upon landing, dealing 1d6 points of piercing damage per 2 caster levels you have (maximum 5d6) to each creature caught in the spread.
