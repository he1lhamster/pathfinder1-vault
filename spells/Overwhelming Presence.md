# <font style='color:blue'>Overwhelming Presence</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [emotion, mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 9, _wizard_ 9, _sorcerer_ 9, _bard_ 6, _skald_ 6, _cleric_ 9, _oracle_ 9, _inquisitor_ 6, _psychic_ 9, _mesmerist_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a swan feather) {a swan feather}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature/level, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates, see text; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
Your presence inspires incredible awe in those nearby. A creature that fails a save against this spell falls to the ground and prostrates itself before you, believing it bows before a divine presence. A flying creature incapable of hovering must land immediately in order to prostrate itself. These creatures are considered to be _[[conditions/Helpless|helpless]]_ for the duration of the spell.
  Each round on its turn, a target of this spell may attempt a new saving throw to end the effect; this is a full-round action that does not provoke attacks of opportunity. A creature that recovers from this spell early after being affected by it for at least 1 round takes 1d6 points of Wisdom drain and is _[[conditions/Staggered|staggered]]_ for 1d4 rounds. A creature that makes the initial save to resist this spell is merely _staggered_ for 1 round.
