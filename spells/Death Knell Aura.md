# <font style='color:red'>Death Knell Aura</font> 
**School:** necromancy [death, evil] 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 4, _cleric_ 4, _oracle_ 4, _warpriest_ 4, _inquisitor_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Area:** 20-ft.-radius emanation, centered on you
**Range:** 20 ft.
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You gain a shimmering _[[monsters/Gray|gray]]_ aura that feeds on the souls of creatures who die within it. The aura sheds light as a _[[items/Mundane/Candle|candle]]_. If a creature at -1 or fewer hit points is within the aura at the start of its turn, it must save or die, granting you the benefits of _[[spells/Death Knell|death knell]]_.

 #### Other
**Source:** Horsemen Of The Apocalypse
**Rating:** 1
