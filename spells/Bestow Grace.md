# <font style='color:blue'>Bestow Grace</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_paladin_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one good creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** Will (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
With this spell you can bestow your divine _[[spells/Grace|grace]]_ on another good creature for a short amount of time, infusing that creature with a portion of your holy _[[spells/Virtue|virtue]]_. When you touch the subject, you grant that creature a _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ bonus to its saving throws equal to its Charisma bonus (if any) on all saving throws.
