# <font style='color:red'>Defensive Shock</font> 
**School:** evocation [electricity] 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _magus_ 2, _bloodrager_ 2, _alchemist_ 2, _investigator_ 2, _occultist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a hollow metal sphere) {a hollow metal sphere}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 minute/level or until discharged

 #### Description
Electrical energy floods your body, shocking the next creature that touches you. Any creature striking you with its body or a handheld weapon takes 1d6 points of electricity damage per two caster levels (maximum 6d6). If the attacker has _[[universal monster rules/Spell Resistance|spell resistance]]_, it applies against this damage. Each time the spell discharges, the number of damage dice it deals is halved (rounded down); when the spell's damage dice reach 0, the spell ends.
