# <font style='color:green'>Reflexive Barrier</font> 
**School:** evocation [force] 
_magus_ 3, _psychic_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 immediate action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round (see text)
**Saving Throw:** none (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** no (harmless); 
 #### Description
In the _[[spells/Blink|blink]]_ of an eye, you erect a deflective barrier of force to protect one target. The target gains a _[[spells/Deflection|deflection]]_ bonus to its AC equal to half your caster level. This bonus lasts until the beginning of your next turn. You can cast this spell as a reaction to an attack, but you must do so before the result of the attack is revealed.

 #### Other
**Source:** Psychic Anthology
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
