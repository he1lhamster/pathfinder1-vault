# <font style='color:green'>Insect Spies, Greater</font> 
**School:** divination 
_arcanist_ 7, _wizard_ 7, _sorcerer_ 7, _witch_ 7, _summoner_ 6, _unsummoner_ 6, _druid_ 6, _hunter_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S, M (a drop of honey) {a drop of honey}
 #### Effects
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level (D)
**Effect:** up to one insect spy/4 levels
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
As _[[spells/Insect Spies|insect spies]]_, but you can also borrow the senses of the summoned insects. As a move action, you can choose to receive sensory input from one of the insects, seeing what it sees and hearing what it hears. While doing so, you are treated as being blind and deaf. You can change to another insect, or return to your own senses, with another move action.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Intrigue
**Rating:** 3
