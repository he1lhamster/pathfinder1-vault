_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 4, _bard_ 4, _skald_ 4, _shaman_ 4, _psychic_ 4, _mesmerist_ 4, _occultist_ 4, _spiritualist_ 4, _medium_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)
**Effect:** magical sensor
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (object); **Spell Resistance:** yes (object); 
 #### Description
You plant a _[[spells/Scrying|scrying]]_ sensor on a touched creature, object, or point in space, allowing you to see and hear the creature, object, or point and its surroundings (approximately 10 feet in all directions). If the creature or object on moves, the sensor moves with it. Unlike other _scrying_ spells, _[[spells/Vicarious View|vicarious view]]_ doesn't allow magically or supernaturally enhanced senses to work through it.
