# <font style='color:red'>Stench Of Prey</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_shaman_ 3, _druid_ 3, _hunter_ 3, _ranger_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature (see text)
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates (see text); **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
The target exudes a strong odor that most predators recognize as the _[[universal monster rules/Scent|scent]]_ of _[[conditions/Helpless|helpless]]_ prey. The closest 1d4+1 predatory creatures within 30 feet must succeed at a Will save or start attacking the target (animals with the _scent_ ability are affected if within 60 feet). If the predator is an animal, it can be _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Called|called]]_ away from the target by a handler using the "down" trick (Core Rulebook 97) or any effect that suspends or dispels emotion or _[[spells/Rage|rage]]_ effects.
  This spell has no effect on constructs, undead, or creatures with the _[[universal monster rules/Unnatural Aura|unnatural aura]]_ ability or effects that repel animals.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Class Guide
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
