# <font style='color:green'>Permanent Image</font> 
**School:** illusion (figment) 
_arcanist_ 6, _wizard_ 6, _sorcerer_ 6, _bard_ 6, _skald_ 6, _psychic_ 6, _mesmerist_ 6, _occultist_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, F (a bit of fleece) {a bit of fleece}
 #### Effects
**Range:** long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
**Duration:** permanent (D)
**Effect:** figment that cannot extend beyond a 20-ft. cube + one 10-ft. cube/level (S)
**Saving Throw:** Will disbelief (if interacted with); **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions like _[[spells/Silent Image|silent image]]_, except that the figment includes visual, auditory, olfactory, and thermal elements, and the spell is permanent. By concentrating, you can move the image within the limits of the range, but it is static while you are not concentrating.

 #### Other
**Source:** PFRPG Core
**Rating:** 3
