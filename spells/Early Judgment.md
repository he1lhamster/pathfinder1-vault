# <font style='color:red'>Early Judgment</font> 
**School:** divination 
_witch_ 1, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, _inquisitor_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one humanoid creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1d4 rounds
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You show one creature the effect of its life so far and what it might expect when it passes under Pharasma's impartial _[[universal monster rules/Gaze|gaze]]_ at the end of its life. Depending on the creature's alignment and its adherence to its ethos, you can provide it a brief glimpse of the reward or punishment that waits for it when it dies by showing it a mental image of its destined plane in the Great Beyond. If the target is good-aligned, it is _[[conditions/Fascinated|fascinated]]_ for 1d4 rounds on a failed save. If the target is neutral-aligned, it is _[[conditions/Confused|confused]]_ for 1d4 rounds on a failed save. If the target is evil-aligned, it is _[[conditions/Shaken|shaken]]_ for 1d4 rounds on a failed save.

 #### Other
**Source:** Inner Sea Gods
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
