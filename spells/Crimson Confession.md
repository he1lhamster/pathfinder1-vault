# <font style='color:red'>Crimson Confession</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _witch_ 2, _psychic_ 2, _occultist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 10 minutes
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** object touched or up to 1 sq. ft.
**Area:** object touched or up to 1 sq. ft.
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** permanent and 1 hour/level (see text)
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You ward an object or an area with your personal rune or mark. You can choose for the writing to be visible or _[[conditions/Invisible|invisible]]_, and it doesn't _[[spells/Harm|harm]]_ the material upon which it is placed. A _[[spells/Detect Magic|detect magic]]_ spell causes an _invisible_ mark to glow and be visible. If any creature other than you touches the warded object or area and fails a Fortitude save, its skin turns bright red for 1 hour per level. This coloring cannot be washed away, but it can be dispelled.
  _[[spells/See Invisibility|See invisibility]]_, _[[spells/True Seeing|true seeing]]_, a _[[items/Wondrous Item/Gem of Seeing|gem of seeing]]_, or a _[[items/Wondrous Item/Robe of Eyes|robe of eyes]]_ likewise allows the user to see an _invisible_ mark. A _[[spells/Read Magic|read magic]]_ spell reveals the words, if any. The ward cannot be dispelled, but it can be removed by the caster or by an _[[spells/Erase|erase]]_ spell.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Class Guide
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
