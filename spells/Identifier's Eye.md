# <font style='color:red'>Identifier's Eye</font> 
**School:** divination 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _summoner_ 2, _unsummoner_ 2, _alchemist_ 2, _investigator_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched (1)
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 3 rounds + 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
This spell infuses a _[[spells/Spark|spark]]_ of magical energy into the veins of the target, allowing it to feel the presence of magic the way one might feel the thrum of vibrations in the air. For the spell's duration, the target gains the benefits of the spell _[[spells/Detect Magic|detect magic]]_ and also gains a +5 enhancement bonus on Spellcraft checks to _[[spells/Identify|identify]]_ the properties and _[[spells/Command|command]]_ words of magic items. Further, the target can attempt such checks untrained while under the effects of this spell. This ability does not allow the target to _identify_ artifacts.

 #### Other
**Source:** Potions and Poisons
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
