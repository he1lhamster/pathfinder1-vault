# <font style='color:orange'>Sealed Life, Greater</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_spiritualist_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. +5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Sealed Life|sealed life]]_, except it additionally provides the benefits of _[[spells/Death Ward|death ward]]_ and renders its targets immune to effects that extract or transfer its soul, such as _[[spells/Possession|possession]]_ and _[[spells/Trap the Soul|trap the soul]]_. This spell prevents a soul from returning to its body if it has already departed, until the spell's duration expires.

 #### Other
**Source:** Occult Adventures
**Rating:** 2
