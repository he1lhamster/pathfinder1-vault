# <font style='color:green'>Dimensional Lock</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_arcanist_ 8, _wizard_ 8, _sorcerer_ 8, _summoner_ 6, _cleric_ 8, _oracle_ 8, _psychic_ 8, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Area:** 20-ft.-radius emanation centered on a point in space
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 day/level
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You create a shimmering emerald barrier that completely blocks extradimensional travel. Forms of movement barred include _[[spells/Astral Projection|astral projection]]_, _[[spells/Dimension Door|dimension door]]_, _[[spells/Ethereal Jaunt|ethereal jaunt]]_, _[[spells/Etherealness|etherealness]]_, _[[spells/Gate|gate]]_, _[[spells/Maze|maze]]_, _[[spells/Plane Shift|plane shift]]_, _[[spells/Shadow Walk|shadow walk]]_, teleport, and similar _[[universal monster rules/Spell-Like Abilities|spell-like abilities]]_. Once _[[spells/Dimensional Lock|dimensional lock]]_ is in place, extradimensional travel into or out of the area is not possible.
  A _dimensional lock_ does not interfere with the movement of creatures already in ethereal or astral form when the spell is cast, nor does it block extradimensional perception or attack forms. Also, the spell does not prevent summoned creatures from disappearing at the end of a summoning spell.
