# <font style='color:orange'>Shield Speech</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, _druid_ 1, _hunter_ 1, _cleric_ 1, _oracle_ 1, _warpriest_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you + creature (1)
**Range:** feet (10)
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
You can securely communicate with one creature within range. So long as your target remains within 10 feet of you, you and it can speak freely to each other without _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_ of being overheard. Communications that involve _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_ speech cannot be spied on, such as with a _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_ spell, and observers of the conversation can tell you are communicating but cannot read lips or hear the conversation unless they are the target of the spell. If you or the target speak to any other creatures, they can hear you normally (only communication between you and the target is protected). The spell does not _shield_ writing, sign language, or any form of communication other than speaking.

 #### Other

**Potion:** Yes
