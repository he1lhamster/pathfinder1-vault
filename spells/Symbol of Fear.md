# <font style='color:red'>Symbol of Fear</font> 
**School:** necromancy [fear, mind-affecting, emotion] 
_arcanist_ 6, _wizard_ 6, _sorcerer_ 6, _witch_ 6, _cleric_ 6, _oracle_ 6, _warpriest_ 6, _occultist_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 10 minutes
 **Components:** V, S, M (mercury and phosphorus, plus powdered diamond and opal worth a total of 1,000 gp) {mercury and phosphorus, plus powdered diamond and opal worth a total of 1,000 gp}
 #### Effects
**Range:** 0 ft.; see text
**Duration:** see text
**Effect:** one symbol
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions like _[[spells/Symbol of Death|symbol of death]]_, except that all creatures within 60 feet of the _[[spells/Symbol of Fear|symbol of fear]]_ instead become _[[conditions/Panicked|panicked]]_ for 1 round per caster level.
  Note: Magic traps such as _symbol of fear_ are hard to detect and disable. A _[[classes/Rogue|rogue]]_ (only) can use the Perception skill to find a _symbol of fear_ and Disable Device to thwart it. The DC in each case is 25 + spell level, or 31 for _symbol of fear_.
