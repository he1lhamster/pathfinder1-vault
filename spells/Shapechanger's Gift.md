# <font style='color:red'>Shapechanger's Gift</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _magus_ 5, _alchemist_ 5, _investigator_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a piece of the creature whose form you plan to grant) {a piece of the creature whose form you plan to grant}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** willing living creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
You change the target into an animal (as per _[[spells/Beast Shape I|beast shape I]]_), humanoid (as per _[[spells/Alter Self|alter self]]_), or monstrous humanoid (as per _[[spells/Monstrous Physique I|monstrous physique I]]_). The chosen form can't have a fly speed. The subject's statistics change as per the appropriate spell except the creature's ability scores and natural armor do not change. The creature can change between its natural form and the chosen form at will as a standard action.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Wilderness
**Rating:** 1
