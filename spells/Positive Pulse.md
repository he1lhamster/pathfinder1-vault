# <font style='color:orange'>Positive Pulse</font> 
**School:** necromancy 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _witch_ 1, _summoner_ 1, _unsummoner_ 1, _shaman_ 1, _cleric_ 1, _oracle_ 1, _warpriest_ 1, _inquisitor_ 1, _paladin_ 1, _spiritualist_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Area:** 5-ft.-radius burst
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous plus 1 round (see text)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates or Will negates (harmless); see text; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell causes a wave of positive energy to spread from a point you designate, damaging foes vulnerable to positive energy and _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Bolstering|bolstering]]_ living creatures. Each creature damaged by positive energy in the area (such as undead) must succeed at a Will save or take 1d6 points of damage, plus 1 point for every 2 caster levels (maximum +5). Haunts manifesting in the spell's area also take this damage. This spell does not _[[spells/Heal|heal]]_ creatures healed by positive energy; such creatures instead gain a +2 bonus on saving throws against death effects, _[[universal monster rules/Energy Drain|energy drain]]_, negative energy, and effects directly caused by haunts. This bonus lasts for 1 round.

 #### Other
**Source:** Planar Adventures

**Potion:** Yes
