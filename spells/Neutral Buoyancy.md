#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a bag of air), DF {a bag of air}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
The target's _[[spells/Buoyancy|buoyancy]]_ becomes and stays neutral, regardless of how dense the target and its gear are. Among other effects, the target neither sinks nor rises when it doesn't attempt a Swim check, making it easier to stay put while performing demanding actions or when _[[conditions/Helpless|helpless]]_ or _[[conditions/Disabled|disabled]]_, and the target can swim up and down with equal ease.
