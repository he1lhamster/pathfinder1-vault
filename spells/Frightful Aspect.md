# <font style='color:green'>Frightful Aspect</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_arcanist_ 8, _wizard_ 8, _sorcerer_ 8, _witch_ 8, _druid_ 8, _cleric_ 8, _oracle_ 8, _psychic_ 8, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (the skin of a toad) {the skin of a toad}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 minute/level

 #### Description
You become a larger, awful version of yourself. You grow to size Large, and take on features that horrify your enemies. You gain the following abilities: a +6 size bonus to Strength, a +4 size bonus to Constitution, a +6 natural armor bonus, DR 10/magic, and _[[universal monster rules/Spell Resistance|spell resistance]]_ equal to 10 + half your caster level. You also emit an aura that emanates 30 feet from you. Enemy creatures within the aura are _[[conditions/Shaken|shaken]]_. Each time a creature _shaken_ by this aura hits you with a melee attack, that creature becomes _[[conditions/Frightened|frightened]]_ for 1d4 rounds, though at the end of that duration it is no longer affected by this aura. The aura's effect is a _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_ and mind-affecting effect.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Combat
**Rating:** 3
