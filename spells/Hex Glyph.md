# <font style='color:red'>Hex Glyph</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_witch_ 3, _shaman_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 10 minutes
 **Components:** V, S, M (powdered diamond worth 200 gp) {powdered diamond worth 200 gp}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** object touched or up to 5 sq. ft./level
**Area:** object touched or up to 5 sq. ft./level
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** permanent until discharged (D)
**Saving Throw:** see text; **Spell Resistance:** no (object) and yes (see text); 
 #### Description
This spell functions like the spell glyph version of _[[spells/Glyph Of Warding|glyph of warding]]_, except you can store a hex you know (but not a major hex or a grand hex) instead of a spell. If the hex has a target, it targets the intruder. If the hex has an area or an _[[universal monster rules/Amorphous|amorphous]]_ effect, the area or effect is centered on the intruder. The triggered hex uses this spell's caster level and DC.
  _[[spells/Read Magic|Read magic]]_ allows its caster to _[[spells/Identify|identify]]_ a _[[spells/Hex Glyph|hex glyph]]_ with a successful DC 13 Knowledge (arcana) check. Identifying the glyph does not _[[spells/Discharge|discharge]]_ it and indicates that it stores a hex, but does not indicate the effect of that hex.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Class Guide
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
