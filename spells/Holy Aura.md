# <font style='color:blue'>Holy Aura</font> 
**School:** abjuration [good] 
_cleric_ 8, _oracle_ 8, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, F (a tiny reliquary worth 500 gp) {a tiny reliquary worth 500 gp}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature/level in a 20-ft.-radius burst centered on you
**Range:** 20 ft.
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** see text; **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
A brilliant divine _[[items/Weapon/Radiance|radiance]]_ surrounds the subjects, protecting them from attacks, granting them _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ to spells cast by evil creatures, and causing evil creatures to become _[[conditions/Blinded|blinded]]_ when they strike the subjects. This abjuration has four effects.
  First, each warded creature gains a +4 _[[spells/Deflection|deflection]]_ bonus to AC and a +4 _resistance_ bonus on saves. Unlike _[[spells/Protection From Evil|protection from evil]]_, this benefit applies against all attacks, not just against attacks by evil creatures.
  Second, each warded creature gains _[[universal monster rules/Spell Resistance|spell resistance]]_ 25 against evil spells and spells cast by evil creatures.
  Third, the abjuration protects the recipient from _[[spells/Possession|possession]]_ and mental influence, just as _protection from evil_ does.
  Finally, if an evil creature succeeds on a melee attack against a creature warded by a _[[spells/Holy Aura|holy aura]]_, the offending attacker is _blinded_ (Fortitude save negates, as blindness/deafness, but against _holy aura_'s save DC).
