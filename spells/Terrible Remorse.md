# <font style='color:green'>Terrible Remorse</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [emotion, mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _bard_ 3, _skald_ 3, _cleric_ 4, _oracle_ 4, _warpriest_ 4, _inquisitor_ 3, _psychic_ 4, _mesmerist_ 3, _spiritualist_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** 1 living creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will partial (see text); **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You fill a target with such profound remorse that it begins to _[[spells/Harm|harm]]_ itself. Each round, the target must save or deal 1d8 points of damage + its Strength modifier to itself using an item held in its hand or with unarmed attacks. If the creature saves, it is _[[conditions/Staggered|staggered]]_ for 1 round and takes a -2 penalty to Armor Class, after which the spell ends.
