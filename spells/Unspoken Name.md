# <font style='color:orange'>Unspoken Name</font> 
**School:** divination 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, _inquisitor_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 minute
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 24 hours or until discharged

 #### Description
Prized among those who seek to monitor the attention paid to them, this spell is said to be used by Arazni to _[[spells/Identify|identify]]_ those followers who dare refer to her as "the Harlot Queen" so she might summarily dismiss them. When you cast this spell, designate a word, phrase, or name. If a creature within 1 mile of you uses that word, phrase, or name to refer to you, you immediately learn that creature's name, appearance, and location. This discharges the spell.

 #### Other
**Source:** AP 140
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
