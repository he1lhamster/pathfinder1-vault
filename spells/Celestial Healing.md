# <font style='color:red'>Celestial Healing</font> 
**School:** conjuration (healing) [good] 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _witch_ 1, _magus_ 1, _summoner_ 1, _bloodrager_ 1, _cleric_ 1, _oracle_ 1, _warpriest_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S, M (1 drop of blood from a good outsider or 1 dose of holy water) {1 drop of blood from a good outsider or 1 dose of holy water}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 round/2 levels
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
You anoint a wounded creature with the blood of an outsider with the good subtype (such as an angel) or _[[items/Mundane/Holy water|holy water]]_, giving it _[[universal monster rules/Fast Healing|fast healing]]_ 1. The target radiates the aura of a good creature for the duration of the spell and can sense the righteousness of the magic, though this has no long-term effect on the target's alignment. If the target has its own evil aura, this is not suppressed by _[[spells/Celestial Healing|celestial healing]]_, and can also be detected normally.

 #### Other
**Source:** Arcane Anthology
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
