# <font style='color:blue'>Brow Gasher</font> 
**School:** necromancy 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _magus_ 2, _bloodrager_ 2, _hunter_ 2, _ranger_ 2, _inquisitor_ 2, _occultist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Area:** one slashing melee weapon touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 round/level or until discharged
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless, object); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless, object); 
 #### Description
You imbue a slashing melee weapon with the ability to deal a gruesome head wound. When the target weapon hits a living creature, in addition to the normal effects of that hit, the wielder can _[[spells/Discharge|discharge]]_ this spell as a free action to open a gash on the target's forehead that deals _[[universal monster rules/Bleed|bleed]]_ damage equal to half your caster level. At the start of each of the target's turns, when it takes _bleed_ damage, it also takes a cumulative -1 penalty on all attack rolls. When that penalty reaches -3, the target also treats all targets as having concealment (20% miss chance). When the penalty reaches -5, the target is _[[conditions/Blinded|blinded]]_. Stopping the _bleed_ damage ends the effects this spell imposes on the bleeding creature. A target that is immune to _bleed_ damage is also immune to all this spell's effects.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Combat
**Rating:** 4
**Potion:** Yes
