# <font style='color:green'>Transmute Mud to Rock</font> 
**School:** transmutation [earth] 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _druid_ 5, _hunter_ 5, _occultist_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M/DF (sand, lime, and water) {sand, lime, and water}
 #### Effects
**Area:** up to two 10-ft. cubes/level (S)
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** permanent
**Saving Throw:** see text; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell permanently transforms normal mud or quicksand of any depth into soft stone (sandstone or a similar mineral).
  Any creature in the mud is allowed a Reflex save to escape before the area is hardened to stone.
  _[[spells/Transmute Mud to Rock|Transmute mud to rock]]_ counters and dispels _[[spells/Transmute Rock to Mud|transmute rock to mud]]_.

 #### Other
**Source:** PFRPG Core
**Rating:** 3
