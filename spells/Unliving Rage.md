# <font style='color:orange'>Unliving Rage</font> 
**School:** necromancy 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 3, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, _mesmerist_ 2, _spiritualist_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one willing undead creature per 3 levels, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** concentration + 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Rage|rage]]_, except it affects only undead creatures and bolsters them with necromantic energy rather than emotion. Each affected creature gains a +2 profane bonus to Strength and Charisma, a +1 profane bonus on Will saves, and a -2 penalty to AC. The effect is otherwise identical to a _[[classes/Barbarian|barbarian]]_'s _rage_.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Class Guide

**Potion:** Yes
