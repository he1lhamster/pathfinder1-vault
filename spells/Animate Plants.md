# <font style='color:green'>Animate Plants</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_shaman_ 7, _druid_ 7, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one Large plant per three caster levels or all plants within  range; see text
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level or 1 hour/level; see text
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You imbue inanimate plants with _[[feats/Mobility|mobility]]_ and a semblance of life. Each _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Animated|animated]]_ plant then immediately attacks whomever or whatever you initially designate as though it were an _[[monsters/Animated Object|animated object]]_ of the appropriate size category. You may animate one Large or smaller plant, or a number of larger plants as follows: a Huge plant counts as two Large or smaller plants, a Gargantuan plant as four, and a Colossal plant as eight. You can change the designated target or targets as a move action, as if directing an active spell.
  Use the statistics for _animated_ objects (see Pathfinder RPG Bestiary), except that plants smaller than Large don't have hardness.
  _[[spells/Animate Plants|Animate plants]]_ cannot affect plant creatures, nor does it affect nonliving vegetable material.
  _[[spells/Entangle|Entangle]]_: Alternatively, you may imbue all plants within range with a degree of _mobility_, which allows them to entwine around creatures in the area. This usage of the spell duplicates the effect of an _entangle_ spell. _[[universal monster rules/Spell Resistance|Spell resistance]]_ does not keep creatures from being _[[conditions/Entangled|entangled]]_. This effect lasts 1 hour per caster level.
