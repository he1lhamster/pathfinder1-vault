# <font style='color:red'>Wall Of Split Illumination</font> 
**School:** evocation [darkness, light] 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _magus_ 3, _bard_ 3, _skald_ 3, _shaman_ 3, _cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, _inquisitor_ 3, _occultist_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)
**Effect:** 10-ft.-high vertical sheet of illumination up to 5 ft. long/level
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
An immobile curtain of illumination springs into existence. When created, one side of the wall (designated by you) radiates bright light to a range of 60 feet away from that side while the other side radiates _[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_ to an equal distance. This effect alters the illumination level by up to two steps toward either bright light (the light side) or _darkness_ (the dark side). The wall also obstructs _[[spells/Vision|vision]]_ through it, regardless of which side of the wall the viewer is on.

 #### Other
**Source:** Blood Of Shadows
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
