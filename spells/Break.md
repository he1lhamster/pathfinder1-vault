# <font style='color:red'>Break</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _bloodrager_ 1, _psychic_ 1, _occultist_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a twig) {a twig}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one Medium or smaller object
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates (object); **Spell Resistance:** yes (object); 
 #### Description
You can attempt to break or at least damage any one _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ or smaller object within range. If the target fails its Fortitude saving throw, it gains the _[[conditions/Broken|broken]]_ condition. If cast on a _broken_ item, that item is destroyed on a failed save.

 #### Other
**Source:** APG
**Rating:** 1
**Potion:** Yes
