# <font style='color:orange'>Pernicious Poison</font> 
**School:** necromancy [poison] 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _witch_ 2, _druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, _antipaladin_ 2, _occultist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 10 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You weaken the target's defenses against poison. The target gains a -4 penalty on saves against poison, and poisons affecting the target continue for another 2 frequency increments (for example, _[[poisons/Black Adder Venom|black adder venom]]_ lasts 8 rounds instead of 6, and _[[poisons/Arsenic|arsenic]]_ lasts for 6 minutes instead of 4). Attempts to cure the poisoned target with skill or magic take a -4 penalty.
