# <font style='color:green'>Curative Distillation</font> 
**School:** conjuration (healing) 
_cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, _alchemist_ 2, _investigator_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (healing herb, alchemical remedy, or potion of a healing spell worth at least 50 gp) {healing herb, alchemical remedy, or potion of a healing spell worth at least 50 gp}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one living creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Will half (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
You concentrate the healing potential of the material component so that it's exceptionally _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Potent|potent]]_ and restorative. This spell restores a number of hit points equal to 1d8 + 1 point per caster level (maximum +10) to the target, and also grants the target the benefits of the material component. Roll the hit points restored separately when determining how many total hit points this spell and the material component restore.
