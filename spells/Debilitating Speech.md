# <font style='color:red'>Debilitating Speech</font> 
**School:** enchantment 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** see text
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature/level, living (1) no two of which can be more than 30 feet apart
**Area:** cone-shaped burst
**Range:** close (25 feet + 5 feet/2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
By speaking eloquently on the subject of your opponents' ineptitude in combat, past failures, personal hygiene, or other faults, you _[[items/Weapon/Sap|sap]]_ their will to fight.This spell can be cast as a standard action or as a full-round action. If cast as a standard action, it takes effect in 1d4 rounds following the spell's casting. If cast as a full-round action, it takes effect 1 round after its casting. Enemies with 4 Hit Dice or fewer are _[[conditions/Panicked|panicked]]_, enemies with 5–8 Hit Dice are _[[conditions/Cowering|cowering]]_, and enemies with more than 8 Hit Dice are _[[conditions/Shaken|shaken]]_.

 #### Other
**Source:** Disciple's Doctrine
**Rating:** 1
