# <font style='color:green'>Transmute Metal to Wood</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_druid_ 7, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Area:** all metal objects within a 40-ft.-radius burst
**Range:** long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes (object; see text); 
 #### Description
This spell enables you to change all metal objects within its area to wood. Weapons, armor, and other metal objects carried by creatures are affected as well. A magic object made of metal effectively has _[[universal monster rules/Spell Resistance|spell resistance]]_ equal to 20 + its caster level against this spell. Artifacts cannot be transmuted. Weapons converted from metal to wood take a -2 penalty on attack and damage rolls.
  The armor bonus of any armor converted from metal to wood is reduced by 2. Weapons changed by this spell splinter and break on any natural attack roll of 1 or 2, and armor changed by this spell loses an additional point of armor bonus every time it is struck with a natural attack roll of 19 or 20.
  Only _[[spells/Limited Wish|limited wish]]_, _[[spells/Miracle|miracle]]_, _[[spells/Wish|wish]]_, or similar magic can restore a transmuted object to its metallic state.

 #### Other
