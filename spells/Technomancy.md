# <font style='color:orange'>Technomancy</font> 
**School:** divination 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (oil stirred with a coil of wires) {oil stirred with a coil of wires}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** cone-shaped emanation
**Range:** 60 ft.
**Duration:** 3 rounds/level
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Detect Magic|detect magic]]_, except it detects the presence of technological objects instead of magical objects. The spell grants you the _[[feats/Technologist|Technologist]]_ feat for the purposes of attempting Knowledge (engineering) checks to _[[spells/Identify|identify]]_ the properties of technological items in your _[[spells/Possession|possession]]_. If you already possess this feat, you gain a +10 bonus on Knowledge (engineering) checks to _identify_ item properties.

 #### Other
**Source:** Technology Guide
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
