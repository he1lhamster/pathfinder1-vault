# <font style='color:green'>Find Fault</font> 
**School:** divination 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 3, _magus_ 3, _bard_ 3, _skald_ 3, _bloodrager_ 3, _shaman_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _inquisitor_ 3, _psychic_ 3, _medium_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** 1 creature
**Range:** medium (100 ft. plus 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You instantly learn many of the target's weaknesses, as if you had made an appropriate Knowledge skill check with a result equal to 20 + your caster level. Additionally, your first melee or ranged attack roll against the creature before the end of your next turn gains a +5 insight bonus. If the creature is polymorphed, disguised, or hidden by an illusion and you are not aware of it's _[[spells/True Form|true form]]_, this spell fails as if the target has _[[universal monster rules/Spell Resistance|spell resistance]]_ you failed to penetrate (the GM may have you attempt a caster level check to conceal the true reason for the spell's failure).
