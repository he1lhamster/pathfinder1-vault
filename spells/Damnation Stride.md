# <font style='color:red'>Damnation Stride</font> 
**School:** conjuration (teleportation) [fire] 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _witch_ 5, _summoner_ 4, _unsummoner_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you (teleportation) and creatures within a 10-foot-radius burst (burst of flame) (see text)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Reflex half, see text; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions like _[[spells/Dimension Door|dimension door]]_, except you leave behind a burst of fire. Choose one corner of your starting square. A 10-foot-radius burst of flame explodes from that corner the moment you leave, dealing 4d6 points of fire damage.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Race Guide
**Rating:** 1
