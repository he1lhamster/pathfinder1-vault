# <font style='color:red'>Symbol of Healing</font> 
**School:** conjuration (healing) 
_witch_ 4, _cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, _paladin_ 4, _occultist_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 10 minutes
 **Components:** V, S, M (mercury and phosphorous, plus powdered diamond and opal worth a total of 500 gp) {mercury and phosphorous, plus powdered diamond and opal worth a total of 500 gp}
 #### Effects
**Range:** 0 ft.; see text
**Duration:** see text
**Effect:** one symbol
**Saving Throw:** Will half (harmless) (see text); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless) (see text); 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Symbol of Death|symbol of death]]_, except all creatures within 60 feet of the _[[spells/Symbol of Healing|symbol of healing]]_ instead are bathed in positive energy and _[[spells/Heal|heal]]_ 2d8 points + 1 point of damage per caster level (maximum +15). Undead and other creatures harmed by positive energy instead take 2d8 points of damage + 1 point per caster level (maximum +15); a Will save reduces this damage by half. A creature can only be healed or harmed by the symbol once in any 24-hour period. Once triggered, the symbol remains active for 10 minutes per caster level.
  _Symbol of healing_ can be made permanent with a _[[spells/Permanency|permanency]]_ spell by a caster of 10th level or higher for the cost of 10,000 gp.
