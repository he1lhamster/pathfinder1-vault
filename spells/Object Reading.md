**School:** divination 
_psychic_ 2, _mesmerist_ 2, _occultist_ 1, _spiritualist_ 2, _medium_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one touched object
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** concentration, up to 1 round per level (D)
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You read the _[[classes/Psychic|psychic]]_ impressions left upon an object by emotionally or psychically charged events in the item's history, or the information imprinted by the _[[spells/Charge Object|charge object]]_ or _[[spells/Implant False Reading|implant false reading]]_ spells. T his spell returns the s ame i nformation a s the psychometry occult skill unlock (see page 196), but gives 1 piece of information when cast and requires 1 round of concentration per additional piece of information instead of 1 minute. You must still attempt an Appraise check to see how much information you gain. You gain a +10 competence bonus on the check.
