# <font style='color:purple'>Song of Discord, Greater</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) 
_bard_ 6, _skald_ 6, _psychic_ 8, _mesmerist_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Area:** creatures within a 20-foot-radius spread
**Range:** medium (100 feet + 10 feet/level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will partial; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Song of Discord|song of discord]]_ except that affected creatures automatically attack the nearest target each round. In addition, all affected creatures gain a +4 morale bonus to Strength for the duration of the spell. A creature that succeeds at the Will save reduces the effect's duration to 1 round.

 #### Other
**Source:** Chronicle of Legends
**Rating:** 5
