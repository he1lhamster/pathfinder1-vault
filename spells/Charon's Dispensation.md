# <font style='color:red'>Charon's Dispensation</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 4, _shaman_ 4, _cleric_ 4, _oracle_ 4, _warpriest_ 4, _inquisitor_ 4, _psychic_ 4, _mesmerist_ 4, _spiritualist_ 4, _medium_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (2 silver coins) {2 silver coins}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature/level
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
The target of this spell gains _[[universal monster rules/Immunity|immunity]]_ to the harmful effects of touching or drinking from the River Styx and a +4 profane bonus on saves against effects that alter or suppress memory (such as _[[spells/Modify Memory|modify memory]]_ or mindwipeOA). This does not grant the target the ability to breathe water, nor does it grant any protection against creatures or mundane hazards such as rapids. The spell has no power to restore memory to a creature already suffering from an existing fugue or _[[spells/Amnesia|amnesia]]_ state.

 #### Other
**Source:** Book of the Damned
**Rating:** 1
