# <font style='color:green'>Gravity Well</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _druid_ 6, _hunter_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a small lead weight) {a small lead weight}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You bring into being a localized region of increased gravity that crushes a creature to the ground. Affected targets weigh twice as much as normal, can move only at half speed, _[[spells/Jump|jump]]_ only half as far as normal, and can lift and carry only half as much as normal. Any flying target has its maneuverability worsened by two steps (from good to poor, for example; minimum clumsy) and plummets to the ground unless it succeeds at a DC 25 Fly check to remain in the air. _[[spells/Freedom of Movement|Freedom of movement]]_ negates the effects of this spell. A creature targeted by _[[spells/Gravity Well|gravity well]]_ behaves normally in the area of a _[[spells/Reverse Gravity|reverse gravity]]_ spell.

 #### Other
**Source:** Dragonslayer's Handbook
**Rating:** 3
