# <font style='color:blue'>Purified Calling</font> 
**School:** conjuration (healing) 
_summoner_ 4, _unsummoner_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a stick of incense) {a stick of incense}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 minute

 #### Description
Your eidolon is restored to full health when you _[[universal monster rules/Summon|summon]]_ it. Upon casting this spell, you must immediately begin the ritual to _summon_ your eidolon. Upon completion of the ritual, your eidolon appears at full hit points and without any damage or penalties to its ability scores, regardless of its previous condition. In addition, any temporary negative conditions affecting your eidolon are immediately removed.
  Permanent conditions and ability drain are not affected by this spell.

 #### Other
**Source:** APG
**Rating:** 4
