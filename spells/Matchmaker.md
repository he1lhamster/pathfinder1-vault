# <font style='color:orange'>Matchmaker</font> 
**School:** enchantment (charm) [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _witch_ 3, _bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _mesmerist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** S, M (a rose petal) {a rose petal}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** two living creatures
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 hour/level
**Saving Throw:** Will partial, see text; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You entice the target creatures to become romantically interested in one another. Each creature saves and applies _[[universal monster rules/Spell Resistance|spell resistance]]_ separately. Both must be affected for the spell to have an effect. If either creatures has a prior unfriendly or hostile attitude toward the other, it receives a +4 bonus on its saving throw. This spell doesn't override the targets' normal sexual preferences or other limitations. If romantic feelings are incompatible for this reason, the creature instead feels an intimate platonic bond with the other.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Intrigue
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
