# <font style='color:orange'>Titanic Anchoring</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_magus_ 3, _bloodrager_ 3, _hunter_ 3, _ranger_ 3, _cleric_ 3, _oracle_ 3, _warpriest_ 3, _antipaladin_ 3, _paladin_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a shaving of oak root) {a shaving of oak root}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 round/level

 #### Description
You become grounded and nearly immovable. You are immune to the _[[universal monster rules/Pull|pull]]_ and push special abilities, and all bull rush, grapple, overrun, reposition, and _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_ combat maneuvers made by creatures your size or smaller always fail. You gain a +10 bonus to your CMD against those combat maneuver checks made by creatures larger than you.

 #### Other
**Source:** AP 91
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
