# <font style='color:red'>Stave Off Corruption</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, _paladin_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (see text) {see text}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 day/2 caster levels
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
You ward the target to _[[spells/Slow|slow]]_ the advancement of its corruption. The target receives a +2 circumstance bonus on saving throws against the advancement of its corruption, as described in the catalyst section of each corruption. Multiple applications of this spell do not stack, nor does the bonus stack with morale, profane, or _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ bonuses on saving throws against the advancement of the target's corruption. The material components for this spell vary with the type of corruption. For example, _[[items/Mundane/Garlic|garlic]]_ is used for a vampirism corruption, a sprig of _[[items/Mundane/Wolfsbane|wolfsbane]]_ is used for a lycanthropy corruption, and so on. The GM can determine appropriate material components, but the components should cost at least 25 gp.
