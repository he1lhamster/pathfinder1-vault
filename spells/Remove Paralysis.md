# <font style='color:green'>Remove Paralysis</font> 
**School:** conjuration (healing) 
_shaman_ 2, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, _inquisitor_ 2, _paladin_ 2, _spiritualist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** up to four creatures, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
You can free one or more creatures from the effects of temporary _[[universal monster rules/Paralysis|paralysis]]_ or related magic, including spells and effects that cause a creature to gain the _[[conditions/Staggered|staggered]]_ condition. If the spell is cast on one creature, the _paralysis_ is negated. If cast on two creatures, each receives another save with a +4 _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ bonus against the effect that afflicts it. If cast on three or four creatures, each receives another save with a +2 _resistance_ bonus.
  The spell does not restore ability scores reduced by penalties, damage, or drain.

 #### Other
**Source:** PFRPG Core
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
**Domain:** Liberation (2);
