# <font style='color:blue'>Traveling Dream</font> 
**School:** divination (scrying) 
_druid_ 4, _hunter_ 4, _cleric_ 4, _oracle_ 4, _warpriest_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** minutes (10)
 **Components:** V, DF 
 #### Effects
**Range:** unlimited
**Duration:** 1 hour/level
**Effect:** magical sensor
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions like _[[spells/Arcane Eye|arcane eye]]_, except as noted above. Upon casting this spell, you fall asleep for its duration, creating an _[[conditions/Invisible|invisible]]_ magic sensor (_[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Called|called]]_ a dreamscryer) that is the exact size and shape of your body, is recognizable as you, and cannot pass through spaces that your body cannot enter. Unlike the _invisible_ sensor created by _arcane eye_, children of any race (no older than the human equivalent of 10) and animals can see your dreamscryer. Sleeping creatures can sense its presence and might incorporate your image into their dreams. Force effects and abjuration spells that ward out creatures (such as _[[spells/Magic Circle against Evil|magic circle against evil]]_) are effective barriers against a dreamscryer (regardless of your creature type, alignment, or other specifics). If you dismiss the spell or the dreamscryer is dispelled or destroyed, you _[[spells/Awaken|awaken]]_.
