# <font style='color:red'>Ooze Form III</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_arcanist_ 7, _wizard_ 7, _sorcerer_ 7, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a bit of the creature whose form you plan to assume) {a bit of the creature whose form you plan to assume}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)

 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Ooze Form II|ooze form II]]_ except you can also assume the form of a Huge ooze. You gain _[[universal monster rules/Blindsight|blindsight]]_ 60 feet and a +8 _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ bonus on saving throws against mind-affecting effects and poison as well.
  **Huge Ooze**: If you assume this form, you gain a +4 size bonus to your Strength, a +10 size bonus to your Constitution score, a -10 penalty to your Dexterity score, base speed 20 feet, _[[universal monster rules/Climb|climb]]_ speed 20 feet, swim speed 30 feet, a slam attack (2d6 plus 2d6 acid and _[[universal monster rules/Grab|grab]]_), and _[[universal monster rules/Constrict|constrict]]_ (2d6).

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Wilderness
**Rating:** 1
