# <font style='color:blue'>Microcosm</font> 
**School:** illusion (phantasm) [mind-affecting] 
_psychic_ 9, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one or more creatures
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** permanent
**Saving Throw:** Will partial (see text); **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You plunge the targets' minds into a veiled immersive mindscape (see page 235) of your own design. You can affect any number of creatures whose combined total Hit Dice do not exceed 30. A creature of 10 HD or fewer gets no saving throw, one of 11-15 HD escapes after 10 minutes per level you possess on a successful save, and a creature of 16 HD or more negates the effect on a successful save. Given time, the bodies of creatures whose minds are trapped in a _[[spells/Microcosm|microcosm]]_ can die of starvation and thirst without care. Creatures can neither escape from nor enter a _microcosm_, even via a _[[spells/Mindscape Door|mindscape door]]_. _Microcosm_ can be ended only by _[[spells/Miracle|miracle]]_ or _[[spells/Wish|wish]]_. Creatures inside a _microcosm_ can be affected by mind-affecting effects targeted against their real bodies. However, they perceive them as natural events within their inner mindscape. Multiple creatures affected by the same _microcosm_ can interact with one another within the mindscape.
