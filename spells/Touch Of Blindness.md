# <font style='color:orange'>Touch Of Blindness</font> 
**School:** necromancy [darkness, shadow] 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _witch_ 1, _bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, _shaman_ 1, _cleric_ 1, _oracle_ 1, _warpriest_ 1, _antipaladin_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature or creatures touched (up to one/level)
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 round/level (see text)
**Saving Throw:** Fortitude negates; **Spell Resistance:** Yes; 
 #### Description
A touch from your hand, which is engulfed in _[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_, disrupts a creature's _[[spells/Vision|vision]]_ by coating its eyes in supernatural _darkness_. Each touch causes the target to become _[[conditions/Blinded|blinded]]_ for 1 round unless it makes a successful Fortitude saving throw. You can use this melee touch attack up to one time per caster level. Any touch attack not used after 1 round per caster level is lost.

 #### Other
**Source:** Blood Of Shadows
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
