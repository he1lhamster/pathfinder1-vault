# <font style='color:red'>Summon Minor Monster</font> 
**School:** conjuration (summoning) 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _witch_ 1, _bard_ 1, _skald_ 1, _summoner_ 1, _unsummoner_ 1, _cleric_ 1, _oracle_ 1, _warpriest_ 1, _antipaladin_ 1, _psychic_ 1, _medium_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S, F/DF (a tiny bag and a small candle) {a tiny bag and a small candle}
 #### Effects
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Effect:** 1d3 summoned creatures
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Summon Monster I|summon monster I]]_, except you can _[[universal monster rules/Summon|summon]]_ 1d3 Tiny or smaller animals, such as bats, lizards, monkeys, rats, ravens, toads, or weasels. The summoned animals must all be the same type of creature. As with animals summoned with _summon monster I_, you may apply one alignment-appropriate template to these animals.
