# <font style='color:red'>Endothermic Touch</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _witch_ 1, _shaman_ 1, _druid_ 1, _hunter_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M/DF (a small bit of snakeskin) {a small bit of snakeskin}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one living creature that has the dragon type or the reptilian subtype
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Fort negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell slows the metabolism and other bodily functions of a creature for a short amount of time. The target is _[[conditions/Staggered|staggered]]_ and moves at half its normal speed (round down to the next 5-foot increment), but it can hold its breath for twice as long as normal.
