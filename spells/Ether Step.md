# <font style='color:green'>Ether Step</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 6, _wizard_ 6, _sorcerer_ 6, _magus_ 5, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 immediate action
 **Components:** S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** see text

 #### Description
You step into the Ethereal Plane long enough to _[[feats/Dodge|dodge]]_ a blow or confound a spell; you must cast _[[spells/Ether Step|ether step]]_ after you are targeted by an attack or effect but before you learn the results of the attack or effect. This spell functions as _[[spells/Ethereal Jaunt|ethereal jaunt]]_, except that you become ethereal until the beginning of your next turn. On the turn after you cast this spell and return to your starting point, you can take only move actions or free actions.

 #### Other
**Source:** Planar Adventures
**Rating:** 3
