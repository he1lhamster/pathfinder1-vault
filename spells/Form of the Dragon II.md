# <font style='color:blue'>Form of the Dragon II</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_arcanist_ 7, _wizard_ 7, _sorcerer_ 7, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a scale of the dragon type you plan to assume) {a scale of the dragon type you plan to assume}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 min./level (D)
**Saving Throw:** see below; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Form of the Dragon I|form of the dragon I]]_ except that it also allows you to assume the form of a Large chromatic or metallic dragon.
  You gain the following abilities: a +6 size bonus to Strength, a +4 size bonus to Constitution, a +6 natural armor bonus, fly 90 feet (poor), _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 60 feet, a _[[universal monster rules/Breath Weapon|breath weapon]]_, DR 5/magic, and _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ to one element. You also gain one bite (2d6), two claws (1d8), two wing attacks (1d6), and one tail slap attack (1d8). You can only use the _breath weapon_ twice per casting of this spell, and you must wait 1d4 rounds between uses. All breath weapons deal 8d8 points of damage and allow a Reflex save for half damage. Line breath weapons increase to 80-foot lines and cones increase to 40-foot cones.
