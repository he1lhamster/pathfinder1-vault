**School:** transmutation

#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M/DF (an old reed from a wind instrument) {an old reed from a wind instrument}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one incorporeal creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
The target coalesces into a semi-physical form for a short period of time. While subject to the spell, the _[[universal monster rules/Incorporeal|incorporeal]]_ creature takes half damage (50%) from nonmagical attack forms, and full damage from magic weapons, spells, spell-like effects, and supernatural effects.
