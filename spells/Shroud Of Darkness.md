# <font style='color:green'>Shroud Of Darkness</font> 
**School:** evocation [darkness] 
_arcanist_ 5, _wizard_ 5, _sorcerer_ 5, _bard_ 5, _skald_ 5, _shaman_ 5, _cleric_ 5, _oracle_ 5, _warpriest_ 5, _inquisitor_ 5, _antipaladin_ 4, _mesmerist_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M/DF (a pinch of black sand) {a pinch of black sand}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature/3 levels, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
With a dark incantation and a gesture of your hand over your eyes, you cause a _[[spells/Shroud Of Darkness|shroud of darkness]]_ to descend upon the targets of this spell. The subjects treat the light level as two steps lower than normal for the duration of this spell. Bright light becomes dim light, normal light becomes _[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_, and areas of dim light and _darkness_ become supernaturally dark (as _darkness_, but even creatures with _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ cannot see).

 #### Other
**Source:** Inner Sea Temples
**Rating:** 3
