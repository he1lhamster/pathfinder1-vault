# <font style='color:blue'>Magical Beast Shape</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_arcanist_ 7, _wizard_ 7, _sorcerer_ 7, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a piece of the creature whose form you plan to assume) {a piece of the creature whose form you plan to assume}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 min./level (D)

 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Beast Shape IV|beast shape IV]]_ except you can assume the form of only a magical beast of a size between Diminutive and Huge. If the form you assume has any of the following abilities, you gain those abilities: _[[universal monster rules/Burrow|burrow]]_ speed 60 feet, _[[universal monster rules/Climb|climb]]_ speed 90 feet, fly speed 120 feet (good maneuverability), swim speed 120 feet, _[[universal monster rules/Blindsense|blindsense]]_ 60 feet, _[[universal monster rules/Blindsight|blindsight]]_ 30 feet, _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 90 feet, _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Scent|scent]]_, _[[universal monster rules/See in Darkness|see in darkness]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Tremorsense|tremorsense]]_ 60 feet, _[[universal monster rules/Blood Drain|blood drain]]_, blood frenzy, _[[universal monster rules/Breath Weapon|breath weapon]]_ (if damage, up to 12d6 points), _[[universal monster rules/Constrict|constrict]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Fast Healing|fast healing]]_ 5, _[[universal monster rules/Ferocity|ferocity]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Grab|grab]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Hold Breath|hold breath]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Jet|jet]]_, _[[universal monster rules/No Breath|no breath]]_, poison, _[[universal monster rules/Pounce|pounce]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Powerful Charge|powerful charge]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Pull|pull]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Rake|rake]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Rend|rend]]_, roar, spikes, _[[universal monster rules/Trample|trample]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_, and web. If the creature has _[[universal monster rules/Immunity|immunity]]_ to poison, you gain a +8 _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ bonus on saves against poison. If the creature has _immunity_ or _resistance_ to any energy types, you gain _resistance_ 20 to those energy types. If the creature has _[[curses/Vulnerability|vulnerability]]_ to an energy type, you gain that _vulnerability_.
  **Diminutive Magical Beast**: If you assume this form, you gain a +10 size bonus to your Dexterity score and take a -4 penalty to your Strength score.
  **Huge Magical Beast**: If you assume this form, you gain a +8 size bonus to your Strength score, take a -4 penalty on your Dexterity score, a +2 size bonus to your Constitution score, and gain a +7 natural armor bonus.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Wilderness
**Rating:** 4
