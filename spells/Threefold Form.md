# <font style='color:red'>Threefold Form</font> 
**School:** divination 
_witch_ 9, _druid_ 9, _psychic_ 9, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, F (a green, three-pointed knot made of mithral worth 729 gp) {a green, three-pointed knot made of mithral worth 729 gp}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 9 rounds

 #### Description
You _[[universal monster rules/Pull|pull]]_ forth idealized versions of yourself at various ages from your mind, splitting into three copies of yourself. _[[spells/Threefold Form|Threefold form]]_ functions as _[[spells/Bilocation|bilocation]]_ (Pathfinder RPG Occult Adventures 158), except there are three of you instead of two, and each of the three bodies is under the effect of threefold aspectAPG with a different idealized age. Other than the differences from _[[spells/Threefold Aspect|threefold aspect]]_, each body shares all other effects with you, as normal for _bilocation_.

 #### Other
**Source:** Legacy Of The First World
