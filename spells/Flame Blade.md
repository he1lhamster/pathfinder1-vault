# <font style='color:orange'>Flame Blade</font> 
**School:** evocation [fire] 
_shaman_ 2, _druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, DF 
 #### Effects
**Range:** 0 ft.
**Duration:** 1 min./level (D)
**Effect:** sword-like beam
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
A 3-foot-long, blazing beam of red-hot fire springs forth from your hand. You wield this blade-like beam as if it were a _[[items/Weapon/Scimitar|scimitar]]_. Attacks with the _[[spells/Flame Blade|flame blade]]_ are melee touch attacks.
  The blade deals 1d8 points of fire damage + 1 point per two caster levels (maximum +10). Since the blade is immaterial, your Strength modifier does not apply to the damage. A _flame blade_ can ignite combustible materials such as parchment, straw, dry sticks, and cloth.
