# <font style='color:blue'>Dirge Of The Victorious Knights</font> 
**School:** illusion (shadow) 
_bard_ 6, _skald_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 round
 **Components:** V, S, F (a medal from a dead Hellknight or a copy of a Chelish opera script, either worth at least 100 gp) {a medal from a dead Hellknight or a copy of a Chelish opera script, either worth at least 100 gp}
 #### Effects
**Range:** 120 ft.
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Effect:** 120-ft. line, 10 ft. wide
**Saving Throw:** Reflex half; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
By performing part of the Chelish opera Victory of the Hellknights, you call forth spectral illusions of mounted Hellknights to _[[universal monster rules/Trample|trample]]_ your foes under the hooves of their _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Glorious|glorious]]_ steeds. The shadowy knights appear in an adjacent 10-foot square and ride forward in the direction you indicate, dealing 1d6 points of damage per caster level (maximum 20d6) to all creatures in their path. Half of this damage is cold damage, while half results directly from arcane power and is not subject to cold _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ or _[[universal monster rules/Immunity|immunity]]_. The knights cannot pass through force effects or barriers that block _[[universal monster rules/Incorporeal|incorporeal]]_ creatures or undead. I

 #### Other
**Source:** Adventurer's Guide
**Rating:** 4
