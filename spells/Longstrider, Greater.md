# <font style='color:green'>Longstrider, Greater</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_druid_ 3, _hunter_ 3, _ranger_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of dirt) {a pinch of dirt}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 hour/level (D)

 #### Description
This functions as _[[spells/Longstrider|longstrider]]_, except it gives you a +20-foot enhancement bonus to your base speed and a +10-foot enhancement bonus to your other modes of movement (_[[universal monster rules/Burrow|burrow]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Climb|climb]]_, fly, swim, and so on). It does not affect movement modes you do not actually have-for example, if you do not have a swim speed, this spell does not grant you a swim speed.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Class Guide
**Rating:** 3
**Potion:** Yes
