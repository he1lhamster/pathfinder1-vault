# <font style='color:blue'>Soothing Word</font> 
**School:** conjuration (healing) 
_witch_ 2, _druid_ 2, _hunter_ 2, _ranger_ 2, _cleric_ 2, _oracle_ 2, _warpriest_ 2, _inquisitor_ 2, _paladin_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** 1 creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
The target of this spell causes several conditions that he suffers from to be lessened in severity one step. If he is _[[conditions/Nauseated|nauseated]]_, he is instead _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_. If he is _[[conditions/Stunned|stunned]]_, he is instead _[[conditions/Dazed|dazed]]_. If he is _[[conditions/Exhausted|exhausted]]_, he is _[[conditions/Fatigued|fatigued]]_. If he is _[[conditions/Frightened|frightened]]_, he is _[[conditions/Shaken|shaken]]_. If he is _[[conditions/Paralyzed|paralyzed]]_, he is _[[conditions/Staggered|staggered]]_. The duration of the effect is unchanged; the effect is simply reduced to the lesser version. This spell has no effect on conditions not listed above.

 #### Other
**Source:** Condition Cards
**Rating:** 4
**Potion:** Yes
