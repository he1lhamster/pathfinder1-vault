# <font style='color:red'>Stabilize Powder</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _witch_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a few drops of liquor) {a few drops of liquor}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** 1 loaded firearm
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** instantaneous
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless, object); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless, object); 
 #### Description
Ammunition currently loaded in the target firearm is less _[[conditions/Prone|prone]]_ to misfire. Decrease the misfire range by 1 + 1 per five caster levels (maximum +5, minimum misfire 0) for the ammunition currently loaded into that firearm.

 #### Other
**Source:** Ultimate Combat
