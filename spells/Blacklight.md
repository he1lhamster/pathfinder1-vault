# <font style='color:blue'>Blacklight</font> 
**School:** evocation 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _cleric_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Area:** emanation, 20-foot radius
**Range:** close (25 feet + 5 feet/2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates or none (object); **Spell Resistance:** yes or no (object); 
 #### Description
You create an area of total _[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_. The _darkness_ is impenetrable even to _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_, but you can see normally within it. Creatures outside the spell's area, even you, cannot see through it.You can cast the spell on a point in space, but the effect is stationary unless cast on a mobile object. You can cast the spell on a creature, and the effect then radiates from the creature and moves as it moves. Unattended objects and points in space do not get saving throws or benefit from _[[universal monster rules/Spell Resistance|spell resistance]]_. Blacklight counters or dispels any light spell of equal or lower level. _[[spells/Daylight|Daylight]]_ counters or dispels blacklight.
