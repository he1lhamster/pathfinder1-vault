# <font style='color:red'>Symbol of Insanity</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_arcanist_ 8, _wizard_ 8, _sorcerer_ 8, _witch_ 8, _cleric_ 8, _oracle_ 8, _occultist_ 6, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 10 minutes
 **Components:** V, S, M (mercury and phosphorus, plus powdered diamond and opal worth a total of 5,000 gp) {mercury and phosphorus, plus powdered diamond and opal worth a total of 5,000 gp}
 #### Effects
**Range:** 0 ft.; see text
**Duration:** see text
**Effect:** one symbol
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions like _[[spells/Symbol of Death|symbol of death]]_, except that all creatures within the radius of the _[[spells/Symbol of Insanity|symbol of insanity]]_ instead become permanently insane (as the _[[spells/Insanity|insanity]]_ spell).
  Unlike _symbol of death_, _symbol of insanity_ has no hit point limit; once triggered, a _symbol of insanity_ simply remains active for 10 minutes per caster level.
  Note: Magic traps such as _symbol of insanity_ are hard to detect and disable. A _[[classes/Rogue|rogue]]_ (only) can use the Perception skill to find a _symbol of insanity_ and Disable Device to thwart it. The DC in each case is 25 + spell level, or 33 for _symbol of insanity_.
