# <font style='color:orange'>Keen Senses</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_druid_ 1, _hunter_ 1, _ranger_ 1, _alchemist_ 1, _investigator_ 1, _occultist_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, M/DF (a hawk's feather) 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** creature touched
**Range:** touch
**Duration:** 1 minute/level (D)
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
The subject gains a +2 competence bonus on Perception checks and gains _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_. Subjects that have _low-light vision_ double the distance they can see under the effects of this spell.

 #### Other
**Source:** APG
