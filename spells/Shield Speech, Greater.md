#### Casting
**Casting Time:** standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Area:** 30-foot emanation centered on you
**Range:** feet (30)
**Duration:** 10 minutes/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** yes (harmless); 
 #### Description
