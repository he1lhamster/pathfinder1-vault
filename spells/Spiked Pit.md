# <font style='color:blue'>Spiked Pit</font> 
**School:** conjuration (creation) 
_arcanist_ 3, _wizard_ 3, _sorcerer_ 3, _summoner_ 3, _unsummoner_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, F (miniature shovel costing 10 gp) {miniature shovel costing 10 gp}
 #### Effects
**Range:** medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)
**Duration:** 1 round + 1 round/level
**Effect:** 10-ft.-by-10-ft. hole, 10 ft. deep/2 levels
**Saving Throw:** Reflex negates; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Create Pit|create pit]]_, except that the pit is lined with wickedly sharp spikes along its bottom and walls and has a maximum depth of 50 feet. Creatures who fall into the pit take falling damage as normal, plus 2d6 points of piercing damage from the spikes. Any creature or object coming into contact with the spikes along the walls, such as a creature trying to _[[universal monster rules/Climb|climb]]_ out, or rope or other typical aids to climbing, takes 1d6 points of piercing damage each round they are in contact with the walls. For those willing to accept the damage incurred while climbing, the pit's walls have a _Climb_ DC of 20.
