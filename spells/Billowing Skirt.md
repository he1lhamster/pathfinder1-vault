# <font style='color:red'>Billowing Skirt</font> 
**School:** transmutation 
_arcanist_ 2, _wizard_ 2, _sorcerer_ 2, _witch_ 2, _bard_ 2, _skald_ 2, _shaman_ 2, _inquisitor_ 2, _occultist_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, F (a skirt, kilt, or gown) {a skirt, kilt, or gown}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 minute/level
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
A skirt, _[[items/Mundane/Kilt|kilt]]_, or gown you are wearing enables you to control your falls through the air. A creature wearing the enchanted item of clothing falls slowly as if affected by _[[spells/Feather Fall|feather fall]]_ and can attempt a DC 15 Fly skill check as a move action to _[[feats/Hover|hover]]_ in place for 1 round.

 #### Other
**Source:** Adventurer's Armory 2

**Potion:** Yes
