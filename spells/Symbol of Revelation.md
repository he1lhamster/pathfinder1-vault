# <font style='color:red'>Symbol of Revelation</font> 
**School:** divination 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _witch_ 4, _cleric_ 4, _oracle_ 4, _warpriest_ 4, _occultist_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 10 minutes
 **Components:** V, S, M (mercury and phosphorous, plus powdered diamond and opal worth a total of 1,000 gp) {mercury and phosphorous, plus powdered diamond and opal worth a total of 1,000 gp}
 #### Effects
**Range:** 0 ft.; see text
**Duration:** see text
**Effect:** one symbol
**Saving Throw:** none; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Symbol of Death|symbol of death]]_, except it is only activated by _[[conditions/Invisible|invisible]]_ creatures, creatures affected by an illusion (glamer) effect, creatures with the shapechanger subtype, or creatures that have magically changed their shape. These creatures are outlined by a pale light that functions like _[[spells/Faerie Fire|faerie fire]]_, except it lasts for 10 minutes per caster level; the symbol does not otherwise reveal their _[[spells/True Form|true form]]_. Once triggered, a _[[spells/Symbol of Revelation|symbol of revelation]]_ remains active for 10 minutes per caster level.
  _Symbol of revelation_ can be made permanent with a _[[spells/Permanency|permanency]]_ spell by a caster of 12th level or higher for the cost of 10,000 gp.
