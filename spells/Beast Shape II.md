# <font style='color:green'>Beast Shape II</font> 
**School:** transmutation (polymorph) 
_arcanist_ 4, _wizard_ 4, _sorcerer_ 4, _magus_ 4, _bloodrager_ 4, _alchemist_ 4, _investigator_ 4, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (a piece of the creature whose form you plan to assume) {a piece of the creature whose form you plan to assume}
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 min./level (D)

 #### Description
This spell functions as _[[spells/Beast Shape I|beast shape I]]_, except that it also allows you to assume the form of a Tiny or Large creature of the animal type. If the form you assume has any of the following abilities, you gain the listed ability: _[[universal monster rules/Climb|climb]]_ 60 feet, fly 60 feet (good maneuverability), swim 60 feet, _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 60 feet, _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Scent|scent]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Grab|grab]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Pounce|pounce]]_, and _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_.
  Tiny animal: If the form you take is that of a Tiny animal, you gain a +4 size bonus to your Dexterity, a -2 penalty to your Strength, and a +1 natural armor bonus.
  Small animal: If the form you take is that of a Small animal, you gain a +2 size bonus to your Dexterity and a +1 natural armor bonus.
  _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ animal: If the form you take is that of a _Medium_ animal, you gain a +2 size bonus to your Strength and a +2 natural armor bonus.
  Large animal: If the form you take is that of a Large animal, you gain a +4 size bonus to your Strength, a -2 penalty to your Dexterity, and a +4 natural armor bonus.
