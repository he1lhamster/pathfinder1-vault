# <font style='color:orange'>Thought Worm I</font> 
**School:** enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting] 
_psychic_ 2, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** one creature
**Range:** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Duration:** 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates; **Spell Resistance:** yes; 
 #### Description
You implant a minor _[[spells/Suggestion|suggestion]]_ within the target creature's psyche that makes it less inclined to resist mental intrusion. The target creature takes a -2 penalty on Will saves against all mind-affecting spells and effects for the duration of the spell.

 #### Other
**Source:** Psychic Anthology
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
