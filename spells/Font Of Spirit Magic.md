# <font style='color:orange'>Font Of Spirit Magic</font> 
**School:** conjuration (creation) 
_shaman_ 3, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S, M (gold dust worth 50 gp) {gold dust worth 50 gp}
 #### Effects
**Area:** 30-ft.-radius emanation centered on you
**Range:** 30 ft.
**Duration:** concentration + 1 round/level
**Saving Throw:** Will negates (harmless); **Spell Resistance:** no; 
 #### Description
You amplify the effect of magic associated with your spirit and your wandering spirit (any spells on the spirit magic spell lists for these spirits). When allies within the area cast these spells, they gain a +2 bonus on caster level checks and concentration checks, as well as to spell DCs. This bonus is a _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ bonus if you are good-aligned or a profane bonus if you are evil-aligned; if you are neither good nor evil, you can choose what type of bonus the spell grants when you cast it.

 #### Other
**Source:** Advanced Class Guide
**Rating:** 2
**Potion:** Yes
