# <font style='color:red'>Stunning Barrier</font> 
**School:** abjuration 
_arcanist_ 1, _wizard_ 1, _sorcerer_ 1, _cleric_ 1, _oracle_ 1, _warpriest_ 1, _inquisitor_ 1, _paladin_ 1, _psychic_ 1, 
#### Casting
**Casting Time:** 1 standard action
 **Components:** V, S 
 #### Effects
**Targets:** you
**Range:** personal
**Duration:** 1 round/level or until discharged
**Saving Throw:** none and Will negates (see text); **Spell Resistance:** no and yes (see text); 
 #### Description
You are closely surrounded by a barely visible magical field.
  The field provides a +1 _[[spells/Deflection|deflection]]_ bonus to AC and a +1 _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ bonus on saves. Any creature that strikes you with a melee attack is _[[conditions/Stunned|stunned]]_ for 1 round (Will negates). Once the field has _stunned_ an opponent, the spell is discharged.
