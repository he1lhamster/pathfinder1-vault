# Water Skinned

Your touch extinguishes small flames.

**Prerequisites:** _[[monsters/Undine|Undine]]_.

**Benefit:** As a standard action, you can extinguish a small nonmagical fire with a touch, affecting anything up to the size of a large campfire. This ability does not affect fires with a total area greater than 5 square feet. Touching the fire in this way does not _[[spells/Harm|harm]]_ you.

**Source** Advanced Race Guide
