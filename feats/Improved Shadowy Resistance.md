# Improved Shadowy Resistance

You have developed a _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ to the life-sapping environment of the _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Shadow|Shadow]]_ Plane.

**Prerequisites:** _[[monsters/Fetchling|Fetchling]]_, shadowy _resistance_ racial trait.

**Benefit:** You gain _resistance_ 5 against negative energy damage, do not lose hit points when you gain a negative level, and gain a +2 racial bonus on saving throws against death effects, _[[universal monster rules/Energy Drain|energy drain]]_, negative energy, and spells or _[[universal monster rules/Spell-Like Abilities|spell-like abilities]]_ of the necromancy school.

**Source** Blood Of Shadows
