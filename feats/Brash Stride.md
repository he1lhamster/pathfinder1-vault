# Brash Stride

You have studied the bold fighting style of Grand Prince Cyricas and emulate his intrepid tread through jungles and other dangerous lands.

**Prerequisites:** Dex 15, _[[feats/Acrobatic Steps|Acrobatic Steps]]_, _[[feats/Nimble Moves|Nimble Moves]]_.

**Benefit:** When you charge or run, you treat difficult terrain as normal terrain.

**Source** AP 132
