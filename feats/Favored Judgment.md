# Favored Judgment

Your judgment against a particular type of creature is particularly harsh.

**Prerequisites:** Wis 13, judgment class feature.

**Benefit:** Select a favored race from the _[[classes/Ranger|ranger]]_'s favored enemies chart. Any _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ or profane bonus you gain from a judgment is 1 higher for attacks you make against or take from creatures that match the selected favored enemy.

**Special:** You can gain this feat multiple times. Its effects do not stack. Each time you take the feat, it applies to a different favored enemy.

**Source** Ultimate Magic
