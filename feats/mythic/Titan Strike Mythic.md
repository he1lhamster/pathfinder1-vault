# Titan Strike (Mythic)

Your fists can fell _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Titanic|titanic]]_ foes.

**Prerequisites:** _[[feats/Improved Unarmed Strike|Improved Unarmed Strike]]_ (mythic).

**Benefit:** Your unarmed strike deals damage as if you were one size category larger. You also gain a +1 bonus for each size category that your target is larger than you on the following: bull rush, drag, grapple, overrun, sunder, and _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_ combat maneuver checks and the DC of your _[[feats/Stunning Fist|Stunning Fist]]_.

**Source** Mythic Adventures
