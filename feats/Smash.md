# Smash (Combat)

You overcome obstacles by _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Breaking|breaking]]_ them.

**Prerequisites:** _[[feats/Power Attack|Power Attack]]_, half-orc.

**Benefit:** When you attack an inanimate, unattended object, you ignore the first 5 points of its hardness. You also receive a +5 bonus on Strength checks made to _[[spells/Knock|knock]]_ down or break open doors.

**Special:** The benefits of this feat do not apply to sunder attempts or attacks against constructs, only attacks on inanimate, unattended objects.

**Source** Advanced Player's Guide
