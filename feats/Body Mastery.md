# Body Mastery

Meditation allows you to harden your body against physical attacks.

**Prerequisites:** Wis 19, _[[feats/Body Control|Body Control]]_, _[[feats/Meditation Master|Meditation Master]]_, character level 15th.

**Benefit:** For 24 hours after you meditate, you gain _[[universal monster rules/Damage Reduction|damage reduction]]_ 2/-.

**Source** Faiths & Philosophies
