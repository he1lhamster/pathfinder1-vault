# Prone Shooter (Combat)

While _[[conditions/Prone|prone]]_, you use the ground to stabilize your aim while using a crossbow or firearm.

**Prerequisites:** Base attack bonus +1.

**Benefit:** If you have been _prone_ since the end of your last turn, the penalty to your Armor Class against melee attacks made against you is reduced to -2. In addition, the bonus to your Armor Class against ranged attacks made against you is increased to +6.

**Source** Ultimate Combat
