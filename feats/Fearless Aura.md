# Fearless Aura

Your aura of courage becomes more _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Potent|potent]]_, as your steadfast resolve is also manifested by your allies.

**Prerequisites:** Aura of courage class feature, caster level 8th.

**Benefit:** Your aura of courage expands to a 20-foot-radius emanation. Allies within the aura are immune to _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_ effects.

**Normal:** The aura of courage affects each ally within 10 feet, and grants a +4 morale bonus on saving throws against _fear_ effects.

**Source** Ultimate Magic
