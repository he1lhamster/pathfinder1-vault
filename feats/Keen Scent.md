# Keen Scent

Your nose is as sensitive as that of a wild predator.

**Prerequisites:** Wis 13, half-orc or _[[monsters/Orc|orc]]_.

**Benefit:** You gain the _[[universal monster rules/Scent|scent]]_ special ability.
