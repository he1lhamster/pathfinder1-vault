# Catch Off-Guard (Combat)

Foes are surprised by your skilled use of unorthodox and improvised weapons.

**Benefit:** You do not suffer any penalties for using an improvised melee weapon. Unarmed opponents are _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ against any attacks you make with an improvised melee weapon.

**Normal:** You take a -4 penalty on attack rolls made with an improvised weapon.

**Source** PFRPG Core
