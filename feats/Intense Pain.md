# Intense Pain (Combat, Stare)

You deal additional damage when using your painful stare.

**Prerequisites:** _[[classes/Mesmerist|Mesmerist]]_ level 7th, painful stare class feature.

**Benefit:** When you use your painful stare ability to increase your own damage, you deal an additional 1d6 points of damage. This damage increases to 2d6 at _mesmerist_ level 12th and to 3d6 at _mesmerist_ level 18th. When you use painful stare to augment an ally's damage, the target of the painful stare takes 1 additional point of damage for every 4 _mesmerist_ levels you possess.

**Source** Occult Adventures
