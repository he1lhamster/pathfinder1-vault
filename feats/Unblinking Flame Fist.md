# Unblinking Flame Fist (Combat)

You can exploit the weaknesses in your opponents' defenses, like a flame seeking fuel to consume.

**Prerequisites:** Int 13; Wis 13; _[[feats/Combat Expertise|Combat Expertise]]_; _[[feats/Perfect Style|Perfect Style]]_; _[[feats/Stunning Fist|Stunning Fist]]_; _[[feats/Unblinking Flame Feint|Unblinking Flame Feint]]_; base attack bonus +13 or _[[classes/Monk|monk]]_ level 13th.

**Benefit:** When using _Perfect Style_, the save DC of your _stunning fist_ attack increases by 2 against _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ targets. When you spend 1 ki point to gain the benefits of _[[feats/Improved Feint|Improved Feint]]_ for 1 round, you can attempt a feint as part of your movement when charging.

**Source** Adventurer's Guide
