# Point Blank Master (Combat)

You are adept at firing ranged weapons in close quarters.

**Prerequisites:** _[[feats/Weapon Specialization|Weapon Specialization]]_ with selected ranged weapon.

**Benefit:** Choose one type of ranged weapon. You do not provoke attacks of opportunity when firing the selected weapon while threatened.

**Normal:** Using a ranged weapon while you are threatened provokes attacks of opportunity.

**Special:** Starting at 6th level, a _[[classes/Ranger|ranger]]_ with the archery combat style may select _[[feats/Point Blank Master|Point Blank Master]]_ as a combat style feat, but he must have _[[feats/Weapon Focus|Weapon Focus]]_ instead of _Weapon Specialization_ in the selected weapon.

**Source** Advanced Player's Guide
