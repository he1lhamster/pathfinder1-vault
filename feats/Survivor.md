# Survivor

Only the strong thrive in your homeland, and you are no weakling, even among your kin.

**Prerequisites:** Con 13, _[[feats/Diehard|Diehard]]_, _[[feats/Endurance|Endurance]]_.

**Benefit:** You gain a +5 bonus on all Constitution checks made to stabilize while _[[conditions/Dying|dying]]_. Once per day, if you are struck by a critical hit or sneak attack, you can spend an immediate action to negate the critical or the sneak attack damage, making the attack a normal hit.

**Source** Inner Sea World Guide
