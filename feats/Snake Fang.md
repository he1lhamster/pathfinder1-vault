# Snake Fang (Combat)

You can unleash attacks against an opponent that has dropped its _[[npcs/Guard|guard]]_.

**Prerequisites:** _[[feats/Combat Reflexes|Combat Reflexes]]_, _[[feats/Improved Unarmed Strike|Improved Unarmed Strike]]_, _[[feats/Snake Sidewind|Snake Sidewind]]_, _[[feats/Snake Style|Snake Style]]_, Acrobatics 6 ranks, Sense Motive 9 ranks.

**Benefit:** While using the _Snake Style_ feat, when an opponent's attack misses you, you can make an unarmed strike against that opponent as an attack of opportunity. If this attack of opportunity hits, you can spend an immediate action to make another unarmed strike against the same opponent.

**Source** Ultimate Combat
