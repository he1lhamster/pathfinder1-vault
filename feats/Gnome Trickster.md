# Gnome Trickster

Your arcane talents go beyond the illusory.

**Prerequisites:** Cha 13, gnome, gnome magic racial trait.

**Benefit:** In addition to your normal gnome _[[universal monster rules/Spell-Like Abilities|spell-like abilities]]_, you also gain the following _spell-like abilities_: 1/day-mage hand and _[[spells/Prestidigitation|prestidigitation]]_.

**Source** Advanced Player's Guide
