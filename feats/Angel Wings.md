# Angel Wings

Feathered wings sprout from your back.

**Prerequisites:** _[[feats/Angelic Blood|Angelic Blood]]_, _[[monsters/Aasimar|aasimar]]_, character level 10th.

**Benefit:** You gain a pair of gleaming feathered wings that grant a fly speed of 30 feet (average maneuverability) if wearing light armor or unencumbered, or 20 feet (poor maneuverability) with a _[[classes/Medium|medium]]_ or heavy load or _medium_ or heavy armor. Fly is a class skill for you.

**Source** Advanced Race Guide
