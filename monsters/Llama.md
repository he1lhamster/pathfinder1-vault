# Llama
This shaggy, hoofed quadruped sports an elongated neck and large, crooked teeth.
**Source** Animal Archive pg. 28
**XP** 200

N Medium animal
**Init** +1; **Senses** _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_; Perception +1

##### Defense

**AC** 11, touch 11, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 10 (+1 Dex)
**hp** 6 (1d8+2)
**Fort** +4, **Ref** +3, **Will** +1

##### Offense
**Speed** 40 ft.
**Melee** bite +1 (1d6+1)
**Special Attacks** spit (+1 ranged touch)

##### Statistics
**Str** 12, **Dex** 13, **Con** 15, **Int** 2, **Wis** 12, **Cha** 9
**Base Atk** +0; **CMB** +1; **CMD** 12 (16 vs. _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_)
**Feats** _[[feats/Endurance|Endurance]]_
**Skills** Acrobatics +5 (+9 when jumping); **Racial Modifiers** +4 Acrobatics when jumping

##### Ecology

**Environment** cold or temperate hills or mountains
**Organization** solitary, pair, or herd (3–15)
**Treasure** none

### Special Abilities
**Spit (Ex)** Once per hour, a _[[monsters/Llama|llama]]_ can regurgitate the contents of its stomach, spitting the foul material at a single target within 10 feet. The target must succeed at a DC 12 Fortitude save or be _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_ for 1d4 rounds. The save DC is Constitution-based.

##### Description

Perhaps llamas’ most distinctive feature is their long necks. _[[feats/Rival|Rival]]_ males violently bump their necks against one another during fights for dominance among the herd, but overall these social animals are remarkably tame.
