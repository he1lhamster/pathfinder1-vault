# Dark Ice Brownie
A pale bluish tiny elfin creature, less than two feet tall, scowls at you from beneath a dull _[[monsters/Gray|gray]]_ cloak dusted with light frost.
**Source** Carnival of Tears pg. 32

NE Tiny fey (cold)
**Init** +8; **Senses** _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_; Listen +8, Spot +8

##### Defense

**AC** 20, touch 16, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 16 (+4 Dex, +4 natural, +2 size)
**hp** 7 (1d6+1); _[[universal monster rules/Fast Healing|fast healing]]_ 3
**Fort** +1, **Ref** +6, **Will** +4
**Immune** cold; **SR** 16
**Weaknesses** fire _[[curses/Vulnerability|vulnerability]]_

##### Offense
**Speed** 20 ft.
**Melee** _[[items/Weapon/Short sword|short sword]]_ +6 (1d4-1/19-20) and claw +1 (1d3-1)
**Space** 2-1/2 ft., **Reach** 2-1/2 ft.
**Special Attacks** _[[spells/Frigid Touch|frigid touch]]_, frosty _[[spells/Grasp|grasp]]_
**_[[universal monster rules/Spell-Like Abilities|Spell-Like Abilities]]_** (CL 7th)
1/day - _[[spells/Confusion|confusion]]_ (DC 17), _[[spells/Continual Flame|continual flame]]_, _[[spells/Dancing Lights|dancing lights]]_, _[[spells/Dimension Door|dimension door]]_, _[[spells/Magic Circle against Good|magic circle against good]]_, _[[spells/Mirror Image|mirror image]]_, _[[spells/Ventriloquism|ventriloquism]]_ (DC 14)

##### Statistics
**Str** 9, **Dex** 18, **Con** 13, **Int** 14, **Wis** 14, **Cha** 16
**Base Atk** +0; **Grapple** -8
**Feats** _[[feats/Improved Initiative|Improved Initiative]]_, _[[feats/Weapon Finesse|Weapon Finesse]]_
**Skills** Bluff +7, Craft (leatherworking) +6, Craft (metalworking) +6, Hide +16 (+21 forest), Listen +8, Move Silently +8, Search +6, Spot +8
**Languages** Common, Halfling, Sylvan
**SQ** frostwalker

##### Ecology

**Environment** any cold
**Organization** gang (2-4) or band (5-12)
**Treasure** no coins; 50% good; 50% items

### Special Abilities

**_Frigid Touch_ (Su)** Once per day, a _[[monsters/Dark Ice Brownie|dark ice brownie]]_ may make a touch attack against a foe to deal 1d6 points of Dexterity damage.

**Frosty _Grasp_ (Su)** A _[[monsters/Frosty Chiseler|frosty chiseler]]_’s _[[universal monster rules/Natural Attacks|natural attacks]]_, as well as any weapons it wields, deal an additional 1d6 points of cold damage.

##### Description

Under the influence of the _[[monsters/Cold Rider|cold rider]]_, the frigid clutch of winter’s malice entered the hearts of Syntira’s fey and transformed them to ice. Now, empowered by the cold hate of the _[[classes/Witch|Witch]]_ Queen, her former companions are ready to exact a brutal _[[feats/Vengeance|vengeance]]_ against the people of Falcon’s Hollow.
