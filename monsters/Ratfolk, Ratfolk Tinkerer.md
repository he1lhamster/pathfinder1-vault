# Ratfolk, Ratfolk Tinkerer

**Source** Monster Codex pg. 178
**XP** 400
_[[monsters/Ratfolk|Ratfolk]]_ _[[classes/Rogue|rogue]]_ 2

N Small humanoid (_ratfolk_)
**Init** +7; **Senses** _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 60 ft.; Perception +6

##### Defense

**AC** 16, touch 14, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 13 (+2 armor, +3 Dex, +1 size)
**hp** 16 (2d8+4)
**Fort** +1, **Ref** +6, **Will** –1
**Defensive Abilities** evasion

##### Offense
**Speed** 20 ft.
**Melee** mwk _[[items/Weapon/Dagger|dagger]]_ +6 (1d3/19–20)
**Ranged** _[[items/Weapon/Light crossbow|light crossbow]]_ +5 (1d6/19–20)
**Special Attacks** sneak attack +1d6, swarming

### Tactics

**Before Combat** If he can, the tinkerer sets an ambush in an area with multiple entrances and exits.
 **During Combat** The tinkerer shares his space with allies to make sneak attacks. If he needs to escape, the tinkerer uses his potion of _[[spells/Pass without Trace|pass without trace]]_.

##### Statistics
**Str** 10, **Dex** 17, **Con** 13, **Int** 16, **Wis** 8, **Cha** 10
**Base Atk** +1; **CMB** +0; **CMD** 13
**Feats** _[[feats/Improved Initiative|Improved Initiative]]_, _[[feats/Weapon Finesse|Weapon Finesse]]_
**Skills** Acrobatics +8 (+4 when jumping), Appraise +8, _[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_ +5, Craft (alchemy) +5, Disable Device +11, Escape Artist +8, Knowledge (dungeoneering) +7, Knowledge (local) +7, Perception +6, Sleight of Hand +8, Stealth +12, Swim +5, Use Magic Device +7
**Languages** Common, Gnome, _[[monsters/Goblin|Goblin]]_, Undercommon
**SQ** _rogue_ talents (finesse _rogue_), trapfinding +1
**Combat Gear** potion of _[[spells/Cure Light Wounds|cure light wounds]]_, potion of _pass without trace_, acid, _[[classes/Alchemist|alchemist]]_’s fire, mwk bolts (5), _[[items/Mundane/Tanglefoot bag|tanglefoot bag]]_; **Other Gear** _[[items/Armor/Leather armor|leather armor]]_, _light crossbow_ with 10 bolts, mwk _dagger_, mwk thieves’ tools, 121 gp

##### Ecology

**Environment** warm deserts or urban

##### Description

_Ratfolk_ are adept at finding and disarming traps, which they scavenge to add to their warrens’ defenses.
