# Dragon (Imperial, Forest), Young Forest Dragon
This massive serpentine and wingless creature has jade scales and antlers, and sounds like _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Grinding|grinding]]_ stones as it stalks forth.
**Source** Bestiary 3 pg. 94
**XP** 9,600
CE Large dragon (earth)
**Init** +5; **Senses** _[[universal monster rules/Dragon Senses|dragon senses]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Tremorsense|tremorsense]]_ 60 ft.; Perception +15

##### Defense

**AC** 22, touch 10, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 21 (+1 Dex, +12 natural, –1 size)
**hp** 126 (11d12+55)
**Fort** +11, **Ref** +8, **Will** +8
**Immune** _[[universal monster rules/Paralysis|paralysis]]_, poison, sleep

##### Offense
**Speed** 40 ft., _[[universal monster rules/Burrow|burrow]]_ 20 ft., _[[universal monster rules/Climb|climb]]_ 30 ft., fly 200 ft. (poor)
**Melee** bite +17 (2d6+9), 2 claws +16 (1d8+6), gore +16 (1d8+9), tail slap +14 (1d8+9)
**Space** 10 ft., **Reach** 5 ft. (10 ft. with bite and gore)
**Special Attacks** _[[universal monster rules/Breath Weapon|breath weapon]]_ (40-ft. cone, 6d6 piercing damage, DC 19)
**_[[universal monster rules/Spell-Like Abilities|Spell-Like Abilities]]_** (CL 11th; concentration +12)
At will—_[[spells/Pass without Trace|pass without trace]]_
**Spells Known **(CL 1st; concentration +12)
1st (4/day)—_[[spells/Obscuring Mist|obscuring mist]]_, _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_
0 (at-will)—_[[spells/Ghost Sound|ghost sound]]_, _[[spells/Read Magic|read magic]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_, _[[spells/Touch of Fatigue|touch of fatigue]]_

##### Statistics
**Str** 23, **Dex** 12, **Con** 18, **Int** 12, **Wis** 13, **Cha** 12
**Base Atk** +11; **CMB** +18; **CMD** 29 (33 vs. _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_)
**Feats** _[[feats/Improved Initiative|Improved Initiative]]_, _[[feats/Multiattack|Multiattack]]_, _[[feats/Power Attack|Power Attack]]_, _[[feats/Skill Focus|Skill Focus]]_ (Stealth), _[[feats/Toughness|Toughness]]_, _[[feats/Weapon Focus|Weapon Focus]]_ (bite)
**Skills** Acrobatics +7 (+11 when jumping), Bluff +15, _Climb_ +28, Fly –5, Intimidate +15, Knowledge (arcana, nature) +9, Perception +15, Stealth +17, Survival +10
**Languages** Common, Draconic
**SQ** sound imitation, woodland stride

##### Ecology

**Environment** any forest
**Organization** solitary
**Treasure** triple

##### Description

Forest dragons, or dilung, are fickle and _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Malevolent|malevolent]]_ dragons that dwell in deep, rugged woodlands. While a forest dragon can fly, it prefers to stalk the earth, flying only to pursue objects of its wrath.
