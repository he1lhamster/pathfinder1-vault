# Rat, Dire Rat
This filthy rat is the size of a small _[[monsters/Dog|dog]]_. It has a coat of coarse fur, a long and scabby tail, and two glittering eyes.
**Source** Pathfinder RPG Bestiary pg. 232
**XP** 135

N Small animal
**Init** +3; **Senses** _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Scent|scent]]_; Perception +4

##### Defense

**AC** 14, touch 14, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 11 (+3 Dex, +1 size)
**hp** 5 (1d8+1)
**Fort** +3, **Ref** +5, **Will** +1

##### Offense
**Speed** 40 ft., _[[universal monster rules/Climb|climb]]_ 20 ft., swim 20 ft.
**Melee** bite +1 (1d4 plus disease)
**Special Attacks** disease

##### Statistics
**Str** 10, **Dex** 17, **Con** 13, **Int** 2, **Wis** 13, **Cha** 4
**Base Atk** +0; **CMB** –1; **CMD** 12 (16 vs. _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_)
**Feats** _[[feats/Skill Focus|Skill Focus]]_ (Perception)
**Skills** _Climb_ +11, Perception +4, Stealth +11, Swim +11; **Racial Modifiers** uses Dex to modify _Climb_ and Swim

##### Ecology

**Environment** any urban
**Organization** solitary or pack (2–20)
**Treasure** none

### Special Abilities

**Disease (Ex) **_[[diseases/Filth Fever|Filth fever]]_: Bite—injury; save Fort DC 11; onset 1d3 days; frequency 1/day; effect 1d3 Dex damage and 1d3 Con damage; cure 2 consecutive saves. The save DC is Constitution-based.

##### Description

Dire rats grow up to 2 feet long and weigh up to 25 pounds. They are common menaces in dungeons and city sewers alike.
