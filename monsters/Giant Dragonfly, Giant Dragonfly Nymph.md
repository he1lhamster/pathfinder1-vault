# Giant Dragonfly, Giant Dragonfly Nymph

**Source** Bestiary 2 pg. 105
**XP** 800

N Small vermin (aquatic)
**Init** +1; **Senses** _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 60 ft.; Perception +1

##### Defense

**AC** 15, touch 12, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 14 (+1 Dex, +3 natural, +1 size)
**hp** 32 (5d8+10)
**Fort** +6, **Ref** +2, **Will** +2
**Immune** mind-affecting effects

##### Offense
**Speed** 10 ft., swim 30 ft.
**Melee** bite +6 (2d6+3)
**Space** 5 ft., **Reach** 5 ft. (10 ft. with bite)
**Special Attacks** extending jaw

##### Statistics
**Str** 15, **Dex** 13, **Con** 14, **Int** —, **Wis** 12, **Cha** 5
**Base Atk** +3; **CMB** +4; **CMD** 15 (23 vs. _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_)
**Skills** Stealth +5 (+13 in shallow water) , Swim +10; **Racial Modifiers** +8 Stealth in shallow water

##### Ecology

**Environment** temperate or warm water
**Organization** solitary, pair, or brood (3–8)
**Treasure** incidental

### Special Abilities

**Extending Jaw (Ex)** A _[[monsters/Giant Dragonfly|giant dragonfly]]_ _[[monsters/Nymph|nymph]]_ can extend its jaws with surprising speed. Not only does this extend the _nymph_’s reach with its bite attack, but during the surprise round, a _nymph_ gains a +4 bonus on attack rolls with its bite.

##### Description

Giant dragonflies lay clutches of eggs in swampy terrain or areas of standing water. Their young, _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Called|called]]_ nymphs, voraciously eat carrion and small prey, _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Growing|growing]]_ and maturing rapidly until they sprout fully functional wings and become adult dragonflies.
