# Familiar, Ptarmigan
This small bird has a mix of brown and white feathers and makes a loud croaking sound.
**Source** Ultimate Wilderness pg. 203, Reign of Winter Player's Guide pg. 10
**XP** 65

N Tiny animal
**Init** +2; **Senses** _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_; Perception +2

##### Defense

**AC** 14, touch 14, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 12 (+2 Dex, +2 size)
**hp** 2 (1d8–2)
**Fort** +0, **Ref** +4, **Will** +2

##### Offense
**Speed** 20 ft., fly 40 ft. (poor)
**Melee** bite +4 (1d2–4)
**Space** 2-1/2 ft., **Reach** 0 ft.

##### Statistics
**Str** 2, **Dex** 14, **Con** 7, **Int** 2, **Wis** 14, **Cha** 5
**Base Atk** +0; **CMB** +0; **CMD** 6
**Feats** _[[feats/Weapon Finesse|Weapon Finesse]]_
**Skills** Stealth +14 (+18 in snow); **Racial Modifiers** +4 Stealth in snow

##### Ecology

**Environment** cold forests and plains
**Organization** solitary, pair, or flock (3–12)
**Treasure** none

##### Description

These grouses molt through the seasons, appearing all white in the spring and shifting to a mix of brown and white in winter months.

Ptarmigans are roughly a foot long from beak to tail and have a wingspan of twice that.
