# Ratfolk, Ratfolk Troubleshooter

**Source** Monster Codex pg. 178
**XP** 1,600
_[[monsters/Ratfolk|Ratfolk]]_ _[[classes/Rogue|rogue]]_ 6

N Small humanoid (_ratfolk_)
**Init** +4; **Senses** _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 60 ft.; Perception +12

##### Defense

**AC** 19, touch 15, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 15 (+4 armor, +4 Dex, +1 Size)
**hp** 42 (6d8+12)
**Fort** +5, **Ref** +10, **Will** +4
**Defensive Abilities** evasion, trap sense +2, uncanny dodge

##### Offense
**Speed** 20 ft.
**Melee** mwk _[[items/Weapon/Rapier|rapier]]_ +10 (1d4/18–20)
**Ranged** _[[items/Weapon/Hand crossbow|hand crossbow]]_ +9 (1d3/19–20)
**Special Attacks** sneak attack +3d6, swarming

### Tactics

**During Combat** After drinking her potion of _[[spells/Invisibility|invisibility]]_, the troubleshooter makes a sneak attack against her most vulnerable foe. If her enemies wear metal armor, she instead uses her wand of _[[spells/Shocking Grasp|shocking grasp]]_ against them.

##### Statistics
**Str** 11, **Dex** 18, **Con** 14, **Int** 12, **Wis** 12, **Cha** 8
**Base Atk** +4; **CMB** +3; **CMD** 17
**Feats** _[[feats/Deft Hands|Deft Hands]]_, _[[feats/Pack Rat|Pack Rat]]_, _[[feats/Weapon Finesse|Weapon Finesse]]_
**Skills** Acrobatics +13 (+9 when jumping), Appraise +7, Bluff +5, _[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_ +6, Craft (alchemy) +3, Diplomacy +5, Disable Device +20, Escape Artist +13, Knowledge (local) +10, Perception +12, Sense Motive +10, Sleight of Hand +6, Stealth +17, Use Magic Device +10
**Languages** Common, Undercommon
**SQ** _rogue_ talents (fast stealth, quick disable, trap spotter), trapfinding +3
**Combat Gear** _[[items/Wondrous Item/Elixir of Hiding|elixir of hiding]]_, potion of _[[spells/Cure Light Wounds|cure light wounds]]_, potions of _invisibility_ (2), wand of _shocking grasp_ (CL 4th, 12 charges), acid (2); **Other Gear** +1 studded leather, _hand crossbow_ with 10 bolts, mwk _rapier_, _[[items/Wondrous Item/Cloak of _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|Resistance]]_ +1|cloak of _resistance_ +1]]_, _[[items/Weapon/Grappling hook|grappling hook]]_, mwk thieves’ tools, _[[items/Mundane/Silk rope|silk rope]]_ (50 ft.), 314 gp

##### Ecology

**Environment** warm deserts or urban

##### Description

_Ratfolk_ are adept at finding and disarming traps, which they scavenge to add to their warrens’ defenses.
