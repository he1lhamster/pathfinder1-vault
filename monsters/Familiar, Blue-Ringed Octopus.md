# Familiar, Blue-Ringed Octopus

**Source** Ultimate Magic pg. 117
**XP** 200

N Tiny animal (aquatic)
**Init** +5; **Senses** _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_; Perception +1

##### Defense

**AC** 17, touch 17, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 12 (+5 Dex, +2 size)
**hp** 4 (1d8)
**Fort** +2, **Ref** +7, **Will** +1
**Defensive Abilities** _[[items/Mundane/Ink|ink]]_ cloud

##### Offense
**Speed** 20 ft., swim 30 ft., _[[universal monster rules/Jet|jet]]_ 60 ft.
**Melee** bite +7 (1d2–1 plus poison), tentacles +5 (_[[universal monster rules/Grab|grab]]_)
**Space** 2-1/2 ft., **Reach** 0 ft.
**Special Attacks** poison

##### Statistics
**Str** 8, **Dex** 21, **Con** 10, **Int** 2, **Wis** 13, **Cha** 3
**Base Atk** +0; **CMB** +3 (+7 grapple); **CMD** 12 (can’t be tripped)
**Feats** _[[feats/Multiattack|Multiattack]]_, _[[feats/Weapon Finesse|Weapon Finesse]]_
**Skills** Escape Artist +15, Stealth +25, Swim +13; **Racial Modifiers** +10 Escape Artist, +8 Stealth

##### Ecology

**Environment** temperate or cold aquatic
**Organization** solitary
**Treasure** none

### Special Abilities

**_Ink_ Cloud (Ex) **While within water, an _[[monsters/Octopus|octopus]]_ can emit a 5-foot-radius sphere of _ink_ once per minute as a swift action. This _ink_ provides total concealment and persists for 1 minute.

**_Jet_ (Ex) **The _octopus_ can _jet_ 60 feet in a straight line as a full-round action. This does not provoke attacks of opportunity.

**Poison (Ex) **Bite—injury; save Fort DC 10; frequency 1/round for 6 rounds; effect 1 Str; cure 1 save.

##### Description

Blue-ringed octopuses are aquatic animals capable of a surprising amount of _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Cunning|cunning]]_. They cannot survive out of water.
