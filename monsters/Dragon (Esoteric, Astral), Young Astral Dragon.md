# Dragon (Esoteric, Astral), Young Astral Dragon
This majestic dragon seems to shimmer as if surrounded by thousands of eddies of astral thought.
**Source** Bestiary 5 pg. 88
**XP** 6,400

N Large dragon (extraplanar)
**Init** +6; **Senses** _[[universal monster rules/Dragon Senses|dragon senses]]_, _[[spells/See Invisibility|see invisibility]]_; Perception +16
**Aura** mental static (30 ft., DC 16)

##### Defense

**AC** 21, touch 11, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 19 (+2 Dex, +10 natural, –1 size)
**hp** 95 (10d12+30)
**Fort** +10, **Ref** +9, **Will** +10; +4 vs. _[[classes/Psychic|psychic]]_ spells
**Defensive Abilities** _[[universal monster rules/Psychic Resilience|psychic resilience]]_; **Immune** _[[universal monster rules/Paralysis|paralysis]]_, sleep; **SR** 20

##### Offense
**Speed** 40 ft., fly 200 ft. (poor)
**Melee** bite +15 (2d6+7), 2 claws +14 (1d8+5), 2 wings +12 (1d6+2), tail slap +12 (1d8+7)
**Space** 10 ft., **Reach** 5 ft. (10 ft. with bite)
**Special Attacks** _[[universal monster rules/Breath Weapon|breath weapon]]_ (80-ft. line, DC 18, 6d6 force)
**_[[universal monster rules/Psychic Magic|Psychic Magic]]_** (CL 10th; concentration +13)
5 PE—_[[spells/Deja Vu|deja vu]]_ (1 PE), _[[spells/Telekinetic Projectile|telekinetic projectile]]_ (0 PE)
**_Psychic_ Spells Known** (CL 1st; concentration +4)
 1st (4/day)— _[[spells/Burst Of Insight|burst of insight]]_, _[[spells/Sundering Shards|sundering shards]]_ (DC 14)
0 (at will)—_[[spells/Daze|daze]]_ (DC 13), _[[spells/Detect Magic|detect magic]]_, _[[spells/Mage Hand|mage hand]]_, open/close

##### Statistics
**Str** 21, **Dex** 14, **Con** 16, **Int** 17, **Wis** 16, **Cha** 12
**Base Atk** +10; **CMB** +16; **CMD** 28 (32 vs. _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_)
**Feats** _[[feats/Flyby Attack|Flyby Attack]]_, _[[feats/Improved Initiative|Improved Initiative]]_, _[[feats/Multiattack|Multiattack]]_, _[[feats/Power Attack|Power Attack]]_, _[[feats/Weapon Focus|Weapon Focus]]_ (bite)
**Skills** Appraise +16, Diplomacy +14, Fly +9, Intimidate +14, Knowledge (arcana, history, planes) +16, Perception +16, Sense Motive +16
**Languages** Celestial, Common, Draconic

##### Description

Reserved and haughty, these dragons roam the Astral Plane, seeking to expand their knowledge of its esoteric secrets. They hunt to eat, and use _psychic magic_ to fight one another.
