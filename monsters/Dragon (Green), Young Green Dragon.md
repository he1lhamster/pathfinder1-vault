# Dragon (Green), Young Green Dragon
Scales the color of emeralds armor this ferocious dragon. A single sharp horn protrudes from the end of its toothy snout.
**Source** Pathfinder RPG Bestiary pg. 96
**XP** 4,800

LE Large dragon (air)
**Init** +1; **Senses** _[[universal monster rules/Dragon Senses|dragon senses]]_; Perception +15

##### Defense

**AC** 20, touch 10, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 19 (+1 Dex, +10 natural, –1 size)
**hp** 85 (9d12+27)
**Fort** +9, **Ref** +7, **Will** +9
**Immune** acid, _[[universal monster rules/Paralysis|paralysis]]_, sleep

##### Offense
**Speed** 40 ft., fly 200 ft. (poor), swim 40 ft.
**Melee** bite +13 (2d6+7), 2 claws +13 (1d8+5), 2 wings +8 (1d6+2), tail slap +8 (1d8+7)
**Space** 10 ft., **Reach** 5 ft. (10 ft. with bite)
**Special Attacks** _[[universal monster rules/Breath Weapon|breath weapon]]_ (40-ft. cone, DC 17, 6d6 acid)
**_[[universal monster rules/Spell-Like Abilities|Spell-Like Abilities]]_** (CL 9th)
At will—_[[spells/Entangle|entangle]]_ (DC 12)

##### Statistics
**Str** 21, **Dex** 12, **Con** 17, **Int** 12, **Wis** 13, **Cha** 12
**Base Atk** +9; **CMB** +15; **CMD** 26 (30 vs. _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_)
**Feats** _[[feats/Alertness|Alertness]]_, _[[feats/Cleave|Cleave]]_, _[[feats/Great Cleave|Great Cleave]]_, _[[feats/Iron Will|Iron Will]]_, _[[feats/Power Attack|Power Attack]]_
**Skills** Fly +7, Knowledge (nature) +13, Perception +15, Spellcraft +13, Stealth +9, Survival +13, Swim +25
**Languages** Common, Draconic
**SQ** _[[universal monster rules/Water Breathing|water breathing]]_, woodland stride

##### Ecology

**Environment** temperate forests

##### Description

Green dragons dwell in the ancient forests of the world, prowling under towering canopies in search of prey. Of all the chromatic dragons, green dragons are perhaps the easiest to deal with diplomatically.
