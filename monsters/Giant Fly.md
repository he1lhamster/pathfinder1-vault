# Giant Fly
Bristling with coarse hairs, this enormous fly’s legs twitch just before it launches into the air on buzzing wings.
**Source** Bestiary 2 pg. 124, Pathfinder #8: Seven Days to the Grave pg. 84
**XP** 400

N Medium vermin
**Init** +3; **Senses** _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 60 ft.; Perception +2

##### Defense

**AC** 13, touch 13, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 10 (+3 Dex)
**hp** 15 (2d8+6)
**Fort** +6, **Ref** +3, **Will** –2
**Immune** disease, mind-affecting effects

##### Offense
**Speed** 20 ft., _[[universal monster rules/Climb|climb]]_ 20 ft., fly 60 ft. (good)
**Melee** bite +2 (1d6+1 plus disease)

##### Statistics
**Str** 12, **Dex** 17, **Con** 16, **Int** —, **Wis** 7, **Cha** 2
**Base Atk** +1; **CMB** +2; **CMD** 15 (21 vs. _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_)
**Skills** _Climb_ +9, Fly +7, Perception +2; **Racial Modifiers** +4 Perception

##### Ecology

**Environment** any temperate or tropical
**Organization** solitary, pair, or swarm (3–12)
**Treasure** none

### Special Abilities

**Disease (Ex) **_[[diseases/Filth Fever|Filth Fever]]_: Bite—injury; save Fortitude DC 14; onset 1d3 days; frequency 1/day; effect 1d3 Dex damage and 1d3 Con damage; cure 2 consecutive saves. Some flies might carry other diseases, at the GM’s discretion. The save DC is Constitution-based.

##### Description

Much like their tiny cousins, giant flies feed upon carrion. Wholly monstrous, these disgusting creatures have been known to sometimes attack still-living foes, particularly when they are hungry or living creatures disturb their meals. Some species of _[[monsters/Giant Fly|giant fly]]_ bear their larva live, ejecting piles of undulating giant maggots from their engorged abdomens rather than laying eggs in decaying corpses.
