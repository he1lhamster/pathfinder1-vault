# Familiar, Lemming
This small, plump rodent has a calico mix of color and a short tail.
**Source** Ultimate Wilderness pg. 200, Reign of Winter Player's Guide pg. 10
**XP** 50

N Diminutive animal
**Init** +1; **Senses** _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_; Perception +1

##### Defense

**AC** 15, touch 15, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 14 (+1 Dex, +4 size)
**hp** 2 (1d8–2)
**Fort** +2, **Ref** +3, **Will** +1

##### Offense
**Speed** 15 ft.
**Melee** bite –1 (1d2–5)
**Space** 1 ft., **Reach** 0 ft.

##### Statistics
**Str** 1, **Dex** 12, **Con** 6, **Int** 2, **Wis** 13, **Cha** 4

**Feats** _[[feats/Great Fortitude|Great Fortitude]]_
**Skills** Stealth +17

##### Ecology

**Environment** cold plains
**Organization** solitary, pair, or slice (3–30)
**Treasure** none

##### Description

These herbivores scurry about colder regions chewing on bulbs, grasses, roots, and shoots. They do not _[[spells/Hibernate|hibernate]]_, but they do _[[universal monster rules/Burrow|burrow]]_ for shelter and warmth.

Lemmings grow to be as large as 6 inches from their noses to their short tails.
