# Shadowfire Creature, Shadowfire Fire Elemental (Large)
_[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Burning|Burning]]_ with a wispy blue-and-purple flame, this ghostly creature has a roughly humanoid shape and stands nearly ten feet tall.
**Source** The Emerald Spire Superdungeon pg. 157
**XP** 3,200

N Large outsider (elemental, extraplanar, fire)
**Init** +10; **Senses** _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 60 ft., _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_; Perception +11

##### Defense

**AC** 20, touch 16, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 13 (+6 Dex, +1 dodge, +4 natural, –1 size)
**hp** 60 (8d10+16)
**Fort** +8, **Ref** +12, **Will** +4
**Defensive Abilities** fire absorption, _[[universal monster rules/Incorporeal|incorporeal]]_ step, _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Shadow|shadow]]_ _[[spells/Blend|blend]]_ (20% miss chance except in bright light); **DR** 5/—; **Immune** cold, elemental traits, fire; **Resist** electricity 10; **SR** 13

##### Offense
**Speed** 50 ft.
**Melee** 2 slams +13 (1d8+3 plus _shadow_ touch and _[[universal monster rules/Burn|burn]]_)
**Space** 10 ft., **Reach** 10 ft.
**Special Attacks** _burn_ (1d8 fire, DC 16), _shadow_ touch (1d6 negative energy)
**_[[universal monster rules/Spell-Like Abilities|Spell-Like Abilities]]_** (CL 8; concentration +10)
1/day—_[[spells/Shadow Step|shadow step]]_

##### Statistics
**Str** 16, **Dex** 23, **Con** 14, **Int** 6, **Wis** 11, **Cha** 15
**Base Atk** +8; **CMB** +12; **CMD** 29
**Feats** _Dodge_, _[[feats/Improved Initiative|Improved Initiative]]_, _[[feats/Iron Will|Iron Will]]_, _[[feats/Mobility|Mobility]]_, _[[feats/Spring Attack|Spring Attack]]_, _[[feats/Weapon Finesse|Weapon Finesse]]_
**Skills** Acrobatics +15 (+23 when jumping), _[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_ +10, Escape Artist +13, Intimidate +11, Knowledge (planes) +5, Perception +11; **Racial Modifiers** +8 Acrobatics when jumping
**Languages** Ignan

##### Ecology

**Environment** any (Plane of Fire)
**Organization** solitary, pair, or gang (3–8)
**Treasure** none

##### Description

Created through rituals that suffused elemental fire with the dark corruption of _shadow_, shadowfire creatures were spawned from the evil cult led by Yarrix (see page 103). Cruelly transformed from their true forms by their ancient creators, they flicker between existence and nothingness, reality and _[[monsters/Oblivion|oblivion]]_, feeding on pain and flame and the screams of those who dare stand against them.
