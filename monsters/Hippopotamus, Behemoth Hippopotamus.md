# Hippopotamus, Behemoth Hippopotamus
This immense behemoth of a hippo is larger than an _[[monsters/Elephant|elephant]]_. Its teeth are like scimitars, and it moves with an indomitable gait.
**Source** Bestiary 2 pg. 157
**XP** 9,600

N Huge animal
**Init** +3; **Senses** _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_; Perception +12

##### Defense

**AC** 23, touch 7, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 23 (–1 Dex, +16 natural, –2 size)
**hp** 133 (14d8+70)
**Fort** +14 (+16 vs. nonmagical disease), **Ref** +8, **Will** +7
**Defensive Abilities** sweat

##### Offense
**Speed** 50 ft.
**Melee** bite +17 (4d8+13/19–20 plus _[[universal monster rules/Grab|grab]]_)
**Space** 15 ft., **Reach** 15 ft.
**Special Attacks** _[[universal monster rules/Capsize|capsize]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Trample|trample]]_ (2d6+13, DC 26)

##### Statistics
**Str** 29, **Dex** 8, **Con** 20, **Int** 2, **Wis** 13, **Cha** 5
**Base Atk** +10; **CMB** +21 (+25 grapple); **CMD** 30 (34 vs. _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_)
**Feats** _[[feats/Diehard|Diehard]]_, _[[feats/Endurance|Endurance]]_, _[[feats/Improved Critical|Improved Critical]]_ (bite), _[[feats/Improved Initiative|Improved Initiative]]_, _[[feats/Iron Will|Iron Will]]_, _[[feats/Power Attack|Power Attack]]_, _[[feats/Skill Focus|Skill Focus]]_ (Perception)
**Skills** Perception +12, Stealth +2 (+12 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Underwater|underwater]]_), Swim +13; **Racial Modifiers** +10 Stealth _underwater_
**SQ** _[[universal monster rules/Hold Breath|hold breath]]_

##### Ecology

**Environment** warm rivers
**Organization** solitary or bloat (2–8)
**Treasure** none

##### Description

The behemoth _[[monsters/Hippopotamus|hippopotamus]]_ is a true monster. Standing taller than an _elephant_, this animal has few enemies in the natural world—even dinosaurs and dire crocodiles avoid fights with them. Making them even more dangerous is the fact that they are practically carnivores—while not above eating plant matter, they devour carrion or foolish creatures that come too close.
