# Familiar, Rabbit
This small, furry creature has long ears and oversized feet.
**Source** Ultimate Wilderness pg. 204, Animal Archive pg. 31
**XP** 65

N Tiny animal
**Init** +3; **Senses** _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_; Perception +1

##### Defense

**AC** 15, touch 15, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 12 (+3 Dex, +2 size)
**hp** 3 (1d8–1)
**Fort** +1, **Ref** +5, **Will** +1

##### Offense
**Speed** 50 ft.
**Melee** bite –2 (1d3–4)
**Space** 2-1/2 ft., **Reach** 0 ft.

##### Statistics
**Str** 3, **Dex** 16, **Con** 9, **Int** 2, **Wis** 12, **Cha** 5
**Base Atk** +0; **CMB** +1; **CMD** 7
**Feats** Run
**Skills** Acrobatics +0 (+8 when jumping), Stealth +15

##### Ecology

**Environment** temperate plains
**Organization** solitary, pair, or down (3–16)
**Treasure** none

##### Description

Rabbits are herbivores with short tails, long ears for _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Sensing|sensing]]_ predators, and powerful hind legs that allow them to leap quickly to safety when threatened.

An average rabbit is between 8 and 20 inches long and weighs 1 to 4 pounds.
