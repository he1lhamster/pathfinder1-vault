Bastion of the Inheritor

Magical properties

Description

This +1 rallying heavy steel shield prominently displays the sword emblem of the god Iomedae, and functions as a holy symbol for her worshipers. When using this shield, the wearer reduces the extra damage she takes from anarchic weapons, smite good, smite law, and unholy weapons by her tier, to a minimum of 0. When its wearer uses the smite evil class feature, she can expend one use of mythic power to extend the deflection bonus to AC she gains against her target to all allies in a 10-foot-radius burst centered on her. This bonus lasts for 1 minute or until the smite evil ends, whichever comes first.

Crafting cost: 11170 gp
