Stirge Dart

Properties

Type: WeaponPrice: 4501 gpWeight: 0.5 lbs.Slot: Slotless

Weapon properties

Proficiency group: SimpleType: RangedDamage: 1d4Critical: x2Enhancement bonus: +1Range: 20 ft.Damage type: P

Magical properties

Caster level: 15Aura: strong necromancy, strong transmutation

Description

When thrown, this +1 dart animates and attacks a single target as a dancing weapon for 4 rounds. When a stirge dart hits, instead of dealing normal damage, the dart drains blood from the target, dealing 1 point of Constitution damage. The dart falls to the ground harmlessly once it stops attacking. A stirge dart can be used only once before it loses all of its magical properties.

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 2251 gp

Craft Magic Arms and Armor, animate objects, bleed
