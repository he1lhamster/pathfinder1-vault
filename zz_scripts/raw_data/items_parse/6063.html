Pale Lavender Ellipsoid Ioun Stone (Cracked)

Properties

Type: Wondrous ItemPrice: 2500 gpWeight: –Slot: Slotless

Magical properties

Caster level: 12Aura: strong abjuration

Description

This stone absorbs spells of 1st level or lower. After absorbing 5 spell levels, the stone burns out and turns dull gray.

Resonance: If the bearer is holding the wayfinder, he can use the stone's absorption ability as an immediate action instead of a readied action.

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 1250 gp

Craft Wondrous Item, creator must be 12th level
