Staff of Spirit-Talking

Properties

Type: StaffPrice: 39050 gpWeight: 5 lbs.Slot: Slotless

Magical properties

Caster level: 11Aura: moderate divination

Description

A Mwangi staff of spirit-talking is made of ebony or other dark wood, with the bones of numerous small animals dangling from it. It allows the use of the following spells:

  • speak with animals (1 charge)
  • speak with plants (1 charge)
  • command plants (1 charge)
  • commune with nature (2 charges)
  • stone tell (2 charges)

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 19525 gp

Craft Staff, command plants, commune with nature, speak with animals, speak with plants, stone tell

Sources

  • Sargava, the Lost Colony
