Dune Boat

Properties

Type: Wondrous ItemPrice: 9000 gpWeight: 6 lbs.Slot: Slotless

Magical properties

Caster level: 8Aura: moderate transmutation

Description

Fashioned from bound reeds in traditional Osirian style, a dune boat is a wondrous variant of the more common folding boat. Like that item, a dune boat appears as a wooden box about 12 inches long, 6 inches wide, and 6 inches deep when inactive, save that each surface is emblazoned with Ancient Osiriani hieroglyphs. A dune boat functions identically to a folding boat, save that it can move across sandy terrain as if it were moving across water. In boat form, it can hold 6 people comfortably, while the ship form carries 20 with ease.

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 4500 gp

Craft Wondrous Item, fabricate, shifting sand, creator must have 2 ranks in Craft (ships)
