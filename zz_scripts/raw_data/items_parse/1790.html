Field Medic's Breastplate

Properties

Type: ArmorPrice: 52900 gpWeight: 30 lbs.Slot: Armor

Armor properties

Type: MediumArmor bonus: 6Enhancement bonus: +1Maximum dexterity: 3Armor check penalty: -4Arcane spell failure chance: 25

Magical properties

Caster level: 7Aura: moderate abjuration, faint conjuration

Description

This battered yet lovingly polished +1 breastplate is marked by three chipped glass circles set over the heart, each of a different color. Three times per day, the wearer may tap a circle to activate it, enveloping her in a shimmering aura that protects her from harm. The wearer gains DR 10/— and is protected if she were under the effects of a globe of invulnerability spell. The effect ends as soon as the wearer makes an offensive action or casts an offensive spell, or after 1 minute. In addition, whenever the wearer casts a cure spell or administers a cure potion while under this effect, she may add one additional die to the total amount of damage healed.

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 26450 gp

Craft Magic Arms and Armor, cure light wounds, sanctuary
