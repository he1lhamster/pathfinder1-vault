Description

An icon of order is a +2 heavy steel shield that typically displays the sword-and-sunburst holy symbol of Iomedae on a white field. The wielder of an icon of order gains a +2 sacred bonus on saves against spells and effects with the chaotic descriptor. Once per day, the wielder can cast protection from chaos as a swift action.

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 13170 gp

Craft Magic Arms and Armor, Quicken Spell, protection from chaos
