Stalker's Fang (Shoanti)

Magical properties

Description

The following suit of armor is generally available only to members of the indicated race or ethnicity.

The Shoanti craft this +1 keen klar from the skull of a horned spirestalker. Its enhancement bonus applies to attack rolls and damage rolls for attacks made with the blade, as well as to its shield bonus to AC. If the wielder confirms a critical hit against a creature that is denied its Dexterity bonus to AC, the target takes an additional 1d6 points of bleed damage.

Crafting cost: 5962 gp
