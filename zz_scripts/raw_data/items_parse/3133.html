Hammertail

Properties

Type: WeaponPrice: 10305 gpWeight: 2 lbs.Slot: Slotless

Weapon properties

Proficiency group: ExoticType: RangedDamage: 1d4Critical: x2Enhancement bonus: +1Range: 10 ft.Damage type: BSpecial: Nonlethal, Trip

Magical properties

Caster level: 10Aura: moderate transmutation

Description

This +1 bolas, crafted from strands of jungle vine woven into strong cord and weighted with rocks, is favored by the charauka. Ranged trip attacks with a hammertail are made with a +4 enhancement bonus on the CMB check. In addition, if an attack with a hammertail misses its target, the weapon returns as if it had the returning property.

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 5305 gp

Craft Magic Arms and Armor, entangle, telekinesis
