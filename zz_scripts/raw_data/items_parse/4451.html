Goblin Fire Drum (Greater)

Properties

Type: Wondrous ItemPrice: 4500 gpWeight: 1 lb.Slot: Slotless

Magical properties

Caster level: 3Aura: faint transmutation

Description

This small crude-looking drum is attached to a strap long enough to loop around the wearer's neck. A successful DC 12 Perform (percussion) check activates the drum, which enhances any nearby natural or magical fire as long as the bearer spends a move action each round to continue playing. All fires (including fire spells and effects) within 30 feet of the bearer deal an additional 1 point of fire damage (or 2 points for the greater version).

All unattended flammable alchemical objects such as tindertwigs, flasks of oil or alchemist's fire, and fireworks within this range have a 10% chance of spontaneously igniting or exploding (as appropriate to the item) when the drum activates. This chance increases by 5% each round and is checked at the start of the performer's turn as long as the playing continues.

A greater goblin fire drum has all the above abilities and can create pyrotechnics once per day.

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 2250 gp

Craft Wondrous Item, pyrotechnics, spark (Advanced Player's Guide)
