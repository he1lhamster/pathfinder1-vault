Peacekeeping Pistol

Properties

Type: WeaponPrice: 11700 gpWeight: 4 lbs.Slot: Slotless

Weapon properties

Proficiency group: ExoticType: RangedDamage: 1d8Critical: x4Enhancement bonus: +1Range: 20 ft.Damage type: B and P

Magical properties

Caster level: 5Aura: faint enchantment

Description

Crafted in the forges of Alkenstar, this +1 merciful pistol is ornamented with filigree of angelic creatures along the barrel, and houses intricate brass gears. Whenever the wielder scores a critical hit while using this weapon to deal nonlethal damage, the target must succeed at a DC 12 Will saving throw or be dazed for 1 round. As a swift action, a wielder with the grit class feature can spend 1 grit point to increase the DC of this save by 4.

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 6500 gp

Craft Magic Arms and Armor, cure light wounds, daze monster
