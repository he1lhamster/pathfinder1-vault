Skyplate Armor

Properties

Type: ArmorPrice: 30300 gpWeight: 20 lbs.Slot: Armor

Armor properties

Type: MediumArmor bonus: 6Enhancement bonus: +1Maximum dexterity: 3Armor check penalty: -5Arcane spell failure chance: 25

Magical properties

Caster level: 11Aura: moderate transmutation

Description

This suit of +1 steel lamellar weighs less than normal steel lamellar (though it is still considered medium armor). Skyplate armor grants its wearer the ability to use wind walk once per day. Furthermore, while clad in skyplate armor, the wearer gains a +4 resistance bonus on all saving throws against electricity effects, wind effects, and all effects associated with magical or mundane weather.

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 15300 gp

Craft Magic Arms and Armor, resist energy, wind walk
