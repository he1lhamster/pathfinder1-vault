Red Crusader's Quill

Properties

Type: WeaponPrice: 25320 gpWeight: 2 lbs.Slot: Slotless

Weapon properties

Proficiency group: MartialType: One-HandedDamage: 1d6Critical: 18-20/x2Enhancement bonus: +3Damage type: P

Magical properties

Caster level: 10Aura: moderate necromancy, moderate transmutation

Description

An artful rapier with a lotus petal guard, this magnificent weapon dedicated to Arazni's former incarnation is useful to warriors and artists alike.

The tip of this narrow +3 rapier can be used as a mundane writing instrument, producing any color of ink the wielder desires. Once per day as a free action when the wielder confirms a critical hit against a creature with the Red Crusader's quill, she can inscribe an arcane mark on the creature. Once per month, the wielder can instead inscribe a mark of justice; this mark ends immediately if the wielder inscribes another mark of justice with the Red Crusader's quill.

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 12820 gp

Craft Magic Arms and Armor, arcane mark, mark of justice, creator must worship Arazni
