Heavy crossbow

Properties

Type: WeaponPrice: 50 gpWeight: 8 lbs.Slot: Slotless

Weapon properties

Proficiency group: SimpleType: RangedDamage: 1d10Critical: 19-20/x2Range: 120 ft.Damage type: PWeapon groups: Crossbows

Description

You draw a heavy crossbow back by turning a small winch. Loading a heavy crossbow is a full-round action that provokes attacks of opportunity. A heavy crossbow fires crossbow bolts.

Normally, operating a heavy crossbow requires two hands. However, you can shoot, but not load, a heavy crossbow with one hand at a –4 penalty on attack rolls. You can shoot a heavy crossbow with each hand, but you take a penalty on attack rolls as if attacking with two one-handed weapons. This penalty is cumulative with the penalty for one-handed firing.
