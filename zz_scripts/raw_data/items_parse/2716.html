Rod of Metamagic, Intensified (normal)

Properties

Type: RodPrice: 11000 gpWeight: 5 lbs.Slot: Slotless

Magical properties

Caster level: 17Aura: strong universal

Description

The wielder can cast up to three spells per day that are intensified as though using the Intensified Spell feat (Advanced Player's Guide).

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 5500 gp
