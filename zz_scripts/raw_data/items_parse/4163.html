Earth Child Faceguard

Properties

Type: Wondrous ItemPrice: 10000 gpWeight: –Slot: Head

Magical properties

Caster level: 10Aura: moderate transmutation

Description

Decorated with the emblems of the Earth Child cadre, an earth child faceguard is designed to refine the wearer's focus when fighting giants. The wearer gains the benefits of Earth Child Style (Pathfinder RPG Ultimate Combat 99), regardless of whether she meets the prerequisites. If the wearer does not possess the defensive training racial trait, the dodge bonus to AC against giants is reduced to +2. If the wearer already possesses the Earth Child Style feat, she can enter the earth child stance as a free action.

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 5000 gp

Craft Wondrous Item, Earth Child Style, know the enemy
