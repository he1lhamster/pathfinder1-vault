Ring of Summoning Affinity (Daemon)

Properties

Type: RingPrice: 10600 gpWeight: –Slot: Ring

Magical properties

Caster level: 5Aura: faint conjuration

Description

This ring is carved from bone. If the wearer can cast summon monster spells, the wearer adds cacodaemon to the 3rd-level list of monsters she can summon with those spells, adds ceustodaemon to the 5th-level list, adds hydrodaemon to the 6th-level list, adds luekodaemon and piscodaemon to the 7th-level list, adds derghodaemon and meladaemon to the 8th level list, and adds thanadaemon to the 9thlevel list. Also, once per day on command the wearer can use this ring to summon a cacodaemon, as if by the summon monster III spell.

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 5300 gp

Forge Ring, planar ally or planar binding, creator must be neutral evil
