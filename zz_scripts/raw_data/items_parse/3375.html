Traitor's Blade

Properties

Type: WeaponPrice: 50302 gpWeight: 1 lb.Slot: Slotless

Weapon properties

Proficiency group: SimpleType: LightDamage: 1d4Critical: 19-20/x2Enhancement bonus: +2Range: 10 ft.Damage type: P and S

Magical properties

Caster level: 14Aura: strong enchantment, strong necromancy

Description

This +2 wounding (Ultimate Equipment 149) dagger allows the wielder to use charm monster (DC 18) three times per day. When the wielder hits a creature affected by the dagger's charm effect, the dagger deals an additional 2d6 points of damage to that creature, even if the creature is no longer charmed because of the hostile action. All other creatures charmed by the blade that see this act of betrayal become sickened for 1d4 rounds, but are immediately freed from the charm effect.

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 25302 gp

Craft Magic Arms and Armor, bleed, charm monster, inflict moderate wounds
