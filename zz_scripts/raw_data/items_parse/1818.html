Gunner's Buckler

Properties

Type: ShieldPrice: 10155 gpWeight: 2 lbs.Slot: Shield

Armor properties

Type: ShieldArmor bonus: 1Enhancement bonus: +1Maximum dexterity: –Armor check penalty: -1Arcane spell failure chance: 5Special material: Mithral

Magical properties

Caster level: 3Aura: faint abjuration

Description

The top edge of this +1 mithral buckler features a notched gun rest. A character wearing a gunner's buckler who uses a firearm while threatened by an opponent in melee does not provoke attacks of opportunity, but takes a –4 penalty on all ranged attack rolls in any round in which he makes a ranged attack while threatened in melee.

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 5155 gp

Craft Magic Arms and Armor, cat's grace, shield
