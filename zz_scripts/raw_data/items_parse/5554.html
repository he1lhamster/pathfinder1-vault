Snapleaf

Properties

Type: Wondrous ItemPrice: 750 gpWeight: –Slot: Slotless

Magical properties

Caster level: 5Aura: faint transmutation

Description

This crystalline carving looks like a hand-sized tree leaf. It is usually worn around the neck on a strap so it is within easy reach, but can be affixed to a belt or clothing just like a badge. While falling, the bearer can snap and destroy the snapleaf as an immediate action to gain the benefits of feather fall and invisibility. The duration of the two effects work independently; ending one early does not affect the other. The item cannot be activated to provide just one of these two effects; they are always activated simultaneously.

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 375 gp

Craft Wondrous Item, feather fall, invisibility
