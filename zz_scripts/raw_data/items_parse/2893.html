Staff of Illumination

Properties

Type: StaffPrice: 51500 gpWeight: 5 lbs.Slot: Slotless

Magical properties

Caster level: 15Aura: strong evocation

Description

This staff is usually sheathed in a layer of silver and decorated with gold and amber sunbursts. A staff of illumination allows use of the following spells:

  • Dancing lights (1 charge)
  • Flare (1 charge)
  • Daylight (2 charges)
  • Sunburst (3 charges)

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 25750 gp

Craft Staff, dancing lights, daylight, flare, sunburst
