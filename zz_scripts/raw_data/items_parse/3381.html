Trident of the Storm Captain

Properties

Type: WeaponPrice: 109665 gpWeight: 4 lbs.Slot: Slotless

Weapon properties

Proficiency group: MartialType: One-HandedDamage: 1d8Critical: x2Enhancement bonus: +4Range: 10 ft.Damage type: PSpecial: Brace

Magical properties

Caster level: 13Aura: strong evocation, strong transmutation

Description

This +4 shock trident is carved from fish-bone and its points are shark teeth. The wielder gains a +4 competence bonus on Profession (sailor) and Swim checks as long as it is held in hand. Once per day, the wielder can utter a command word to create one of the following effects: vortex (Pathfinder RPG Advanced Player's Guide 254) or control weather.

If the wielder uses a polymorph or supernatural effect to take the shape of a creature with a natural bite attack, the trident of the storm captain vanishes and the wielder's bite attack gains a +4 enhancement bonus until the shapechanging effect ends and the trident reappears. The wielder cannot use the trident's other abilities while shapechanged.

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 54990 gp

Craft Magic Arms and Armor, call lightning, control weather, greater magic fang, vortex
