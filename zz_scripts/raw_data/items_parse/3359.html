Talmandor Blade

Properties

Type: WeaponPrice: 58615 gpWeight: 2 lbs.Slot: Slotless

Weapon properties

Proficiency group: MartialType: One-HandedDamage: 1d8Critical: 19-20/x2Enhancement bonus: +2Damage type: SSpecial material: Mithral

Magical properties

Caster level: 7Aura: moderate abjuration, moderate conjuration, moderate evocation

Description

This +2 holy mithral longsword continually sheds light as the spell. While wielded, the Talmandor Blade grants the wielder low-light vision (or darkvision 60 feet, if the wielder already has low-light vision) and a +4 bonus on Perception checks. Additionally, the sword grants a +4 sacred bonus on saving throws against paralysis and petrification spells and effects.

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 29465 gp

Craft Magic Arms and Armor, darkvision, holy smite, stone to flesh, creator must be good
