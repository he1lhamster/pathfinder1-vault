Dark Blue Rhomboid Ioun Stone (Flawed)

Properties

Type: Wondrous ItemPrice: 300 gpWeight: –Slot: Slotless

Magical properties

Caster level: 12Aura: strong transmutation

Description

This stone grants a +2 competence bonus on Perception checks and a –1 penalty to initiative checks.

Resonance: The wearer no longer takes penalties on Perception checks for being distracted or asleep; while asleep, the wearer can remain open-eyed and fully aware of his environment, though he is still helpless against undetected threats until he actually wakes up.

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 150 gp

Craft Wondrous Item, creator must be 12th level
