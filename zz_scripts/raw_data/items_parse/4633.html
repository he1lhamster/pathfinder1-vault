Herald Seed

Properties

Type: Wondrous ItemPrice: 8000 gpWeight: –Slot: Slotless

Magical properties

Caster level: 12Aura: strong divination

Description

Twice per day, when thrown into the air as a standard action, this palm-sized, tufted, purple seed drifts at a rate of 60 feet per round toward a spot clearly visualized by the thrower. It can be grabbed out of the air with a successful combat maneuver check against a CMD of 20, which ends its flight. The seed flies for up to 12 miles (taking 2 hours) before it settles to the ground. A gathlain thrower can choose to speak a message of up to 25 words when throwing the seed; in this case, the seed repeats the message in the thrower's voice when its flight ends.

If the thrower speaks a command word as part of the action to throw it, the herald seed flies back to where it was last thrown, even if the thrower can't visualize the location.

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 4000 gp

Craft Wondrous Item, commune with nature, message
