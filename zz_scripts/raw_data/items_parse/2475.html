Ring of Natural Attunement (Kami)

Properties

Type: RingPrice: 7800 gpWeight: –Slot: Ring

Magical properties

Caster level: 5Aura: faint conjuration

Description

This ring carved from a single piece of cherry wood bears an inscription written in Tien. If the wearer can cast summon nature's ally spells, the wearer adds shikigami to the 3rd-level list of monsters he can summon with those spells, adds kodama to the 5th-level list, adds zuishin to the 7th-level list, and adds toshigami to the 9th-level list. Also, once per day on command the wearer can use this ring to summon a shikigami, as if by the summon nature's ally III spell.

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 3900 gp

Forge Ring, commune with nature, summon nature's ally III
