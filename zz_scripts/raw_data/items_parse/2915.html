Staff of Speaking

Properties

Type: StaffPrice: 57200 gpWeight: 5 lbs.Slot: Slotless

Magical properties

Caster level: 13Aura: strong enchantment

Description

When the wielder of this hollow wooden staff speaks or shouts, it reverberates with the sound of her voice, lending a booming insistence to her words. It allows the use of the following spells:

  • Suggestion (1 charge)
  • Tongues (1 charge)
  • Denounce (2 charges; Advanced Player's Guide)
  • Glibness (2 charges)
  • Mass suggestion (4 charges)

Crafting requirements

Crafting cost: 28600 gp

Craft Staff, denounce, glibness, mass suggestion, suggestion, tongues
