# Staff of Necromancy

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 82000 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 13 **Aura:** strong necromancy

### Description

This staff is made from ebony or other dark wood and carved with images of bones and skulls mingled with strange spidery runes. It allows use of the following spells:

* _[[spells/Cause Fear|Cause fear]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Ghoul touch|Ghoul touch]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Halt Undead|Halt undead]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Enervation|Enervation]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Waves of Fatigue|Waves of fatigue]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Circle Of Death|Circle of death]]_ (3 charges)
