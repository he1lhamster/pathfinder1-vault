# Staff of Many Rays

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 57200 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 11 **Aura:** moderate evocation, moderate necromancy

### Description

The elaborate swirls and delicate flecks of shining metal adorning the head of this pale wooden shaft make it seem almost as much a long mace as a staff. It allows use of the following spells:

* _[[spells/Ray Of Enfeeblement|Ray of enfeeblement]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Ray of Exhaustion|Ray of exhaustion]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Scorching Ray|Scorching ray]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Enervation|Enervation]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Disintegrate|Disintegrate]]_ (3 charges)
