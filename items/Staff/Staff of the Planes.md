# Staff of the Planes

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 63960 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 13 **Aura:** strong conjuration

### Description

This obsidian staff twinkles as if _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Reflecting|reflecting]]_ the star-filled sky at night. The staff allows use of the following spells.

* _[[spells/Planar Adaptation|Planar adaptation]]_ (1 charge; Advanced Player's Guide)
* _[[spells/Planar Binding|Planar binding]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Plane Shift|Plane shift]]_ (5 charges)
