# Staff of Aspects

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 37600 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d6/1d6 **Critical:** x2/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1/+1 **Damage type:** B **Special:** Double, _[[classes/Monk|Monk]]_

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This stout staff is made of ash. Four faces of animals—a bear, a falcon, a stag, and a _[[monsters/Wolf|wolf]]_—are carved at its top. The aspect staff functions as a +1/+1 _[[items/Weapon/Quarterstaff|quarterstaff]]_ and allows the use of the following spells:

* _[[spells/Aspect of the Bear|Aspect of the bear]]_ (1 charge; Advanced Player's Guide)
* _[[spells/Aspect of the Falcon|Aspect of the falcon]]_ (1 charge; Advanced Player's Guide)
* _[[spells/Aspect of the Stag|Aspect of the stag]]_ (2 charges; Advanced Player's Guide)
* _[[spells/Aspect of the Wolf|Aspect of the wolf]]_ (3 charges; Advanced Player's Guide)
