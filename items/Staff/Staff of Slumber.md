# Staff of Slumber

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 81766 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate enchantment, moderate illusion, moderate necromancy

### Description

Smoky spirals adorn the length of this staff, and have an almost hypnotic effect. The staff allows use of the following spells:

* _[[spells/Deep Slumber|Deep slumber]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Ray of Exhaustion|Ray of exhaustion]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Dream|Dream]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Nightmare|Nightmare]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Waves of Fatigue|Waves of fatigue]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Symbol of Sleep|Symbol of sleep]]_ (3 charges)
