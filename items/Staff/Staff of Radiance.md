# Staff of Radiance

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 23200 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

This silver-tipped willow staff constantly emits light, as if from a light spell—the _[[items/Weapon/Radiance|radiance]]_ is emitted from the staff's metallic end and flickers as if with flame. The staff's wielder can deactivate or activate this glowing light as a swift action. In addition, a _[[items/Staff/Staff of Radiance|staff of radiance]]_ allows use of the following spells:

* _[[spells/Glitterdust|Glitterdust]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Daylight|Daylight]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Searing Light|Searing light]]_ (2 charges)
