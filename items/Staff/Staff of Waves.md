# Staff of Waves

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 90300 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d6/1d6 **Critical:** x2/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1/+0 **Damage type:** B **Special:** Double, _[[classes/Monk|Monk]]_

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 13 **Aura:** strong necromancy

### Description

This staff is made of entwined coral and topped with a brilliant sapphire. The staff functions as a +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Quenching|quenching]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Quarterstaff|quarterstaff]]_ above the waves and a +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Impact|impact]]_ _quarterstaff_ below, and attacks with the _[[items/Staff/Staff of Waves|staff of waves]]_ take no penalties for being bludgeoning melee attacks _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Underwater|underwater]]_. It allows use of the following spells:

* _[[spells/Hydraulic Torrent|Hydraulic torrent]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Slipstream|Slipstream]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Wave Form|Wave form]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Ride The Waves|Ride the waves]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Vortex|Vortex]]_ (3 charges)
