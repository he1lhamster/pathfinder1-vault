# Staff of Traps

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 56925 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate abjuration

### Description

The tip of this dark wooden shaft holds a piece of hard, chalklike stone between black, clawlike protrusions. The staff allows use of the following spells:

* _[[spells/Alarm|Alarm]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Magic Mouth|Magic mouth]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Phantom Trap|Phantom trap]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Explosive Runes|Explosive runes]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Fire Trap|Fire trap]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Sepia Snake Sigil|Sepia snake sigil]]_ (2 charges)
