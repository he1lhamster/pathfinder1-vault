# Staff of Spiders

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 30200 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d6/1d6 **Critical:** x2/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1/+1 **Damage type:** B **Special:** Double, _[[classes/Monk|Monk]]_

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate conjuration, moderate transmutation

### Description

This staff is constructed from thousands of carefully prepared spiderwebs packed around a base and layered to make a shaft just as hard as oak, if not harder. A _[[items/Staff/Staff of Spiders|staff of spiders]]_ functions as a +1/+1 _[[items/Weapon/Quarterstaff|quarterstaff]]_. It allows the use of the following spells:

* _[[spells/Spider Climb|Spider climb]]_ (1 charge)
* Web (1 charge)
* _[[universal monster rules/Summon|Summon]]_ spider (as _[[spells/Summon Monster III|summon monster III]]_, but only summons 1d3 giant spiders; 2 charges)
