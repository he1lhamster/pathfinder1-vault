# Staff of Tricks

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 8800 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate illusion

### Description

This thin staff is made of stiff wood that has been wrapped in colorful bands of cloth and topped with a number of feathers, bells, and other decorative baubles. The staff allows use of the following spells:

* _[[spells/Ghost Sound|Ghost sound]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Mage Hand|Mage hand]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Prestidigitation|Prestidigitation]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Silent Image|Silent image]]_ (2 charges)
