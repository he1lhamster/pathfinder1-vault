### Description

This stout staff is made of wood and capped with steel. Elongated carvings of smiling beasts adorn its length. The _[[items/Staff/Staff of Curchanus|staff of Curchanus]]_ functions as a +1/+1 _[[items/Weapon/Quarterstaff|quarterstaff]]_ and allows the use of the following spells.

* _[[spells/Charm Animal|Charm animal]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Longstrider|Longstrider]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Monkey Fish|Monkey fish]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Speak with Animals|Speak with animals]]_ (1 charge)
* Greater _longstrider_ (2 charges)
