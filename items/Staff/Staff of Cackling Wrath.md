# Staff of Cackling Wrath

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 56700 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate enchantment, moderate necromancy, moderate transmutation

### Description

The thorns embedded in this gnarled length of wood make it rather tricky for the unwary to _[[spells/Grasp|grasp]]_. It allows use of the following spells:

* Blindness/deafness (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Charm Person|Charm person]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Hold Person|Hold person]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Inflict Moderate Wounds|Inflict moderate wounds]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Baleful Polymorph|Baleful polymorph]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Bestow Curse|Bestow curse]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Vampiric Touch|Vampiric touch]]_ (2 charges)
