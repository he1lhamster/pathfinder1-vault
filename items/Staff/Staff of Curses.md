# Staff of Curses

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 43500 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d6/1d6 **Critical:** x2/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1/+1 **Damage type:** B **Special:** Double, _[[classes/Monk|Monk]]_

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This gnarled wooden staff is bedecked with _[[conditions/Broken|broken]]_ charms and burnt fetishes that were once used to ward against bad luck, but now are in shambles. The staff allows use of the following spells:

* Blindness/deafness (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Ill Omen|Ill omen]]_ (1 charge; Advanced Player's Guide)
* _[[spells/Baleful Polymorph|Baleful polymorph]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Bestow Curse|Bestow curse]]_ (2 charges)
