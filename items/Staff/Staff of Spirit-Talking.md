### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 39050 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

* _[[spells/Speak with Animals|speak with animals]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Speak with Plants|speak with plants]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Command Plants|command plants]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Commune with Nature|commune with nature]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Stone Tell|stone tell]]_ (2 charges)
