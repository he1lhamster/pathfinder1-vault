# Staff of Revelations

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 51008 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

The forked top of this walnut shaft resembles a combination of a _[[items/Mundane/Dowsing rod|dowsing rod]]_ and the curled horns of a beast, and a delicate gemstone hovers between its enfolding arms. The staff allows use of the following spells:

* _[[spells/Augury|Augury]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Speak with Dead|Speak with dead]]_ (1 charge)
* _Divination_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Commune|Commune]]_ (3 charges)
