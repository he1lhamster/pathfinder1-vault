# Staff of Bolstering

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 41600 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

The top of this rosewood shaft narrows and then expands again, bound by a number of metal rings. It allows use of the following spells:

* _[[spells/Bear's Endurance|Bear's endurance]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Bull's Strength|Bull's strength]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Cat's Grace|Cat's grace]]_ (1 charge)
* Greater _[[spells/Magic Weapon|magic weapon]]_ (1 charge)
