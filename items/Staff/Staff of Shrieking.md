# Staff of Shrieking

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 36000 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

Holes in this metal shaft cause it to emit an eerie moan when swung through the air. It allows use of the following spells:

* _[[spells/Shatter|Shatter]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Sound Burst|Sound burst]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Shout|Shout]]_ (2 charges)
