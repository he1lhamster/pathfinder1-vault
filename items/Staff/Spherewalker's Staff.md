# Spherewalker's Staff

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 43400 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate conjuration

### Description

This slender darkwood staff is topped either with a star-shaped crystal or a butterfly-like silver head. _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|Sacred]]_ to the faith of Desna, a _[[items/Staff/Spherewalker's Staff|spherewalker's staff]]_ allows use of the following spells:

* _[[spells/Ant Haul|ant haul]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Water Walk|water walk]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Air Walk|air walk]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Dimension Door|dimension door]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Freedom of Movement|freedom of movement]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Plane Shift|plane shift]]_ (3 charges)
