# Animate Staff

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 49800 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 11 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

Created from a number of iron rods, copper gears, and silver screws, this staff constantly shifts and changes. The staff allows use of the following spells:

* _[[spells/Make Whole|Make whole]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Mending|Mending]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Animate Objects|Animate objects]]_ (2 charges)
