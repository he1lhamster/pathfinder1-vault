# Staff of Obstacles

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 52850 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate abjuration, moderate conjuration, moderate evocation

### Description

Dark and solid, this worn staff appears to be carved of rough stone, yet feels and weighs like light wood. It allows use of the following spells:

* _[[spells/Arcane Lock|Arcane lock]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Grease|Grease]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Wind Wall|Wind wall]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Wall Of Fire|Wall of fire]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Wall Of Ice|Wall of ice]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Wall Of Stone|Wall of stone]]_ (3 charges)
