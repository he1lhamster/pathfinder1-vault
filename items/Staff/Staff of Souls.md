# Staff of Souls

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 32800 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_, moderate necromancy

### Description

This _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Ominous|ominous]]_ staff is made of twisting wood and topped with a small skull. It allows use of the following spells:

* _[[spells/Deathwatch|Deathwatch]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Detect Undead|Detect undead]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Gentle Repose|Gentle repose]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Death Ward|Death ward]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Speak with Dead|Speak with dead]]_ (2 charges)
