# Staff of Hunting

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 51000 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 13 **Aura:** strong conjuration, strong _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_, strong necromancy, strong transmutation

### Description

This length of aged ivory is carved with numerous hunting scenes which seem to animate and tell a story when the staff is rotated. It allows the use of the following spells:

* _[[spells/Acute Senses|Acute senses]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Bard's Escape|Bard's escape]]_ (4 charges)
* _[[spells/Locate Creature|Locate creature]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Unwilling Shield|Unwilling shield]]_ (3 charges)
