# Staff of Performance

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 48800 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate enchantment, moderate illusion, moderate transmutation

### Description

Soon after falling into the hands of a new owner, this piece of finely varnished cedar develops ornamental brass keys, strings, or other decorations that make it look like a stylized version of its owner's favorite type of musical instrument. It allows use of the following spells:

* _[[spells/Calm Emotions|Calm emotions]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Glibness|Glibness]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Hypnotic Pattern|Hypnotic pattern]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Pyrotechnics|Pyrotechnics]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Enthrall|Enthrall]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Major Image|Major image]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Sculpt Sound|Sculpt sound]]_ (2 charges)
