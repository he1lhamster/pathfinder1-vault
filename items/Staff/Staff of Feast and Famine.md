# Staff of Feast and Famine

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 20800 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

Leaves sprout from this wooden staff, some green and some black. When the wielder uses the staff, he plucks one of the leaves: a green leaf to provide sustenance or a black leaf to cause hunger or thirst. The staff allows the use of the following spells:

* _[[spells/Create Water|Create water]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Goodberry|Goodberry]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Purify Food and Drink|Purify food and drink]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Cup Of Dust|Cup of dust]]_ (2 charges; Advanced Player's Guide)
* _[[spells/Feast Of Ashes|Feast of ashes]]_ (2 charges; Advanced Player's Guide)
