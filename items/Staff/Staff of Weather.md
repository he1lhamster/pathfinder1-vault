# Staff of Weather

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 84066 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 13 **Aura:** strong conjuration, strong evocation, strong transmutation

### Description

The carved surface of this solid oaken beam always depicts a scene with the opposite qualities of the current weather (rainy on dry days, dry on rainy days, and so on). It allows use of the following spells:

* _[[spells/Fog Cloud|Fog cloud]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Gust Of Wind|Gust of wind]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Wind Wall|Wind wall]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Ice Storm|Ice storm]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Sleet Storm|Sleet storm]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Control Weather|Control weather]]_ (3 charges)
