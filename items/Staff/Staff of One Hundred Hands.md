# Staff of One Hundred Hands

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 180200 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 17 **Aura:** strong evocation

### Description

Sculpted into the shape of dozens of grasping, distorted hands, this staff appears to be carved entirely from one piece of clear crystal. When held, the hands that form the staff distort to resemble the gloves or hands of the person holding it. It allows the use of the following spells:

* _[[spells/Forceful Hand|Forceful hand]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Interposing Hand|Interposing hand]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Clenched Fist|Clenched fist]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Grasping Hand|Grasping hand]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Crushing Hand|Crushing hand]]_ (3 charges)
