# Staff of the Master (Necromancy)

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 15200 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d6/1d6 **Critical:** x2/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1/+1 **Damage type:** B **Special:** Double, _[[classes/Monk|Monk]]_

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint necromancy

### Description

Often given as gifts to apprentices upon reaching the rank of master, these staves come in eight different varieties, one each for every school of magic. This particular staff is for the school of necromancy. Aside from acting as a +1/+1 _[[items/Weapon/Quarterstaff|quarterstaff]]_, this staff allows use of the following spells:

* _[[spells/Ray Of Enfeeblement|ray of enfeeblement]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Spectral Hand|spectral hand]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Vampiric Touch|vampiric touch]]_ (2 charges)
