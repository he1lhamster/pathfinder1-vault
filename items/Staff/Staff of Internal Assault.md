# Staff of Internal Assault

### Properties

**Type:** Staff **Price:** 20800 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate conjuration, moderate transmutation

### Description

Crumbling bark continuously flakes from this solid, aged oak staff without diminishing its size. It allows the use of the following spells:

* _[[spells/Calcific Touch|Calcific touch]]_ (2 charges)
* _[[spells/Corrosive Touch|Corrosive touch]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Touch Injection|Touch injection]]_ (2 charges)
