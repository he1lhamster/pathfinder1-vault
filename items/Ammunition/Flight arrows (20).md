# Flight arrows (20)

### Properties

**Type:** Ammunition **Price:** 2 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

These arrows have light shafts and special fletching to give them greater range. A _[[universal monster rules/Flight|flight]]_ arrow's range increment is 20 feet greater with longbows and 10 feet greater with shortbows. They deal damage as if one size category smaller.
