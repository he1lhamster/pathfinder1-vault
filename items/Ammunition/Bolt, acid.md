# Bolt, acid

### Properties

**Type:** Ammunition **Price:** 40 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

These metal crossbow bolts have a glass section in the middle filled with acid. On a hit, an acid bolt deals 1d4 points of acid damage in addition to its normal damage. Acid bolts do not cause splash damage.
