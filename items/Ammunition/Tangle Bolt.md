# Tangle Bolt

### Properties

**Type:** Ammunition **Price:** 226 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 12 **Aura:** strong conjuration

### Description

Often etched with images of spider webs or swampy vines, this sickly green +1 seeking bolt transforms into _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sticky|sticky]]_ goo when it hits, entangling its target and becoming tough and resilient upon exposure to air. In addition to the bolt's damage, the target is _[[conditions/Entangled|entangled]]_ as if it had been hit by a _[[items/Mundane/Tanglefoot bag|tanglefoot bag]]_.
