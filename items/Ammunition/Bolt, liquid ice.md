# Bolt, liquid ice

### Properties

**Type:** Ammunition **Price:** 40 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

These metal crossbow bolts have a glass section in the middle filled with _[[items/Mundane/Liquid ice|liquid ice]]_. On a hit, it deals 1d4 points of cold damage in addition to its normal damage. _Liquid ice_ bolts do not cause splash damage.
