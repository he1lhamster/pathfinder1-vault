# Bamboo shaft arrows

### Properties

**Type:** Ammunition **Price:** 1 gp **Weight:** 0.5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

These foot-long arrows are used as ammunition for arrow tubes. As an improvised melee weapon, they deal damage equivalent to that of a _[[items/Weapon/Spiked gauntlet|spiked gauntlet]]_. They come in a bamboo tube that holds 10 shafts.
