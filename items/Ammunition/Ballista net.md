# Ballista net

### Properties

**Type:** Ammunition **Price:** 75 gp **Weight:** 15 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This ballista bolt is specially equipped with a _[[items/Mundane/Net|net]]_ which unfurls as the bolt flies toward its target. Unlike a standard ballista bolt, a _[[items/Ammunition/Ballista net|ballista net]]_ requires only a touch attack to hit. If a _ballista net_ hits, it deals no damage, but causes the target to become _[[conditions/Entangled|entangled]]_. An _entangled_ creature takes a –2 penalty on attack rolls and a –4 penalty to Dexterity, can move at only half speed, and cannot charge or run.
