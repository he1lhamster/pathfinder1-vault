# Dustburst Bullet

### Properties

**Type:** Ammunition **Price:** 196 gp **Weight:** 0.5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint conjuration

### Description

This gritty and irregular +1 _[[items/Weapon/Sling|sling]]_ bullet explodes into a cloud of choking dust when it successfully strikes its target. This cloud fills a 5-foot cube and follows the target for 1d6 rounds before dispersing. Any creature ending its turn in the cloud must make a successful DC 13 Fortitude save or be _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_ and _[[conditions/Blinded|blinded]]_ for 1 round.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 98 gp
