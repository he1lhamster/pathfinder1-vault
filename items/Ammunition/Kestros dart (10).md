# Kestros dart (10)

### Properties

**Type:** Ammunition **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This _[[items/Weapon/Dart|dart]]_, meant to be thrown using a _[[items/Weapon/Kestros|kestros]]_, consists of a heavy metal point about 9 inches long attached to a shaft of wood about 12 inches long, fletched with feathers for stability. If used as a melee weapon, it is a light improvised weapon (–4 penalty on attack rolls) and deals damage as a _[[items/Weapon/Dagger|dagger]]_ of its size (critical multiplier ×2). _Kestros_ darts come in a quiver that holds 10 darts.
