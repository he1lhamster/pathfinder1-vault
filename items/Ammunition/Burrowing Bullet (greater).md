# Burrowing Bullet (greater)

### Properties

**Type:** Ammunition **Price:** 3447 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** moderate necromancy

### Description

This +1 _[[items/Ammunition/Firearm bullet|firearm bullet]]_ deals normal damage, but when it hits a living creature, it burrows into the creature's flesh, causing wracking pain until removed or until the bullet burrows its way out of the creature. While these bullets _[[universal monster rules/Burrow|burrow]]_, the creature is _[[conditions/Staggered|staggered]]_. This effect lasts for 1d3 rounds or until the bullet is removed with a DC 15 _[[spells/Heal|Heal]]_ check made as a standard action. Greater burrowing bullets take longer to pass though the bodies of living creatures (the _staggered_ effect lasts 1d3+2 rounds) and are harder to remove (DC 20 _Heal_ check as a standard action).
