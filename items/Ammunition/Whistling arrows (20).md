# Whistling arrows (20)

### Properties

**Type:** Ammunition **Price:** 2 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

These arrows come with specially designed grooves and fletching that cause them to emit a loud keening sound audible within 500 feet of their _[[universal monster rules/Flight|flight]]_ path. They are sold in quantities of 20.
