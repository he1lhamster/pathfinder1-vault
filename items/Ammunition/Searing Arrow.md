# Searing Arrow

### Properties

**Type:** Ammunition **Price:** 1516 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

This +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Flaming|flaming]]_ arrow continues to _[[universal monster rules/Burn|burn]]_ its target each round, dealing 1d6 points of fire damage on the attacker's turn for the next 3 rounds. Removing the arrow requires a successful DC 10 _[[spells/Heal|Heal]]_ check and prevents any further damage from the arrow (the DC increases to 15 if the target tries to remove the arrow from himself). Removing the arrow destroys it, and it burns up once the 3 rounds pass.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 758 gp
