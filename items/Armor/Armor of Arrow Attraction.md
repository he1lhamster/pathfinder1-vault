### Description

Magical analysis indicates that this armor is a normal suit of +3 _[[items/Armor/Full plate|full plate]]_. The armor works normally with regard to melee attacks but actually attracts ranged weapons. The wearer takes a –15 penalty to AC against ranged weapons. The true nature of the armor does not reveal itself until the character is fired upon in earnest.
