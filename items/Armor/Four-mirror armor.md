# Four-mirror armor

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 125 gp **Weight:** 45 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ **Armor bonus:** 6 **Maximum dexterity:** 2 **Armor check penalty:** -5 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 30

### Description

This armor consists of four plates harnessed together with leather shoulder straps. Two round plates protect your front and back, while two smaller rectangular plates cover the sides of the torso. _[[items/Armor/Four-mirror armor|Four-mirror armor]]_ is worn over _[[items/Armor/Chainmail|chainmail]]_ to provide added protection, and comes with a spiked helmet with a _chainmail_ hood.
