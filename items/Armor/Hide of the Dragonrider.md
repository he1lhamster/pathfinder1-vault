# Hide of the Dragonrider

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 18480 gp **Weight:** 25 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ **Armor bonus:** 4 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Maximum dexterity:** 4 **Armor check penalty:** -3 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 20 **Special material:** Dragonhide

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate abjuration, moderate enchantment

### Description

Crafted from the scaled hide of a black dragon and lined with _[[monsters/Wolliped|wolliped]]_ fur for warmth, this suit of +1 _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Bolstering|bolstering]]_ (Ultimate Equipment) black dragonhide armor was designed to protect and aid dragonriders of the Dragon Legion in their battles against the evil dragons of the Drakelands. _[[items/Armor/Hide of the Dragonrider|Hide of the dragonrider]]_ grants its wearer a +5 competence bonus on Ride checks, and the wearer does not take an armor penalty from the armor on Ride checks. In addition, when mounted, the wearer can transfer some or all of the armor's armor bonus (including its enhancement bonus) to his _[[spells/Mount|mount]]_ as a bonus that stacks with all others. As a free action at the start of his turn, the wearer chooses how to allocate the armor's armor bonus, and that allocation lasts until the wearer's next turn. Once per day, the wearer can imbue _hide of the dragonrider_ with _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ against one type of energy, as the _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Energy Resistance|energy resistance]]_ armor special ability. This _resistance_ lasts for 10 minutes. _Hide of the dragonrider_ functions as a cold weather outfit, granting its wearer a +5 circumstance bonus on Fortitude saves against the effects of cold weather.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 9405 gp

_[[feats/Craft Magic Arms and Armor|Craft Magic Arms and Armor]]_, _[[spells/Heroism|heroism]]_, _[[spells/Resist Energy|resist energy]]_, _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_ or _[[spells/Shield Of Faith|shield of faith]]_, creator must have 5 ranks in the Ride skill
