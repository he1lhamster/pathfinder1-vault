# Breastplate

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 200 gp **Weight:** 30 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ **Armor bonus:** 6 **Maximum dexterity:** 3 **Armor check penalty:** -4 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 25

### Description

A _[[items/Armor/Breastplate|breastplate]]_ protects a wearer's torso with a single piece of sculpted metal, similar to the core piece of a suit of _[[items/Armor/Full plate|full plate]]_. Despite its sturdiness, its inflexibility and open back make it inferior to complete suits of metal armor, but still an improvement over most non-metal armors.
