# Iadaran Dress Uniform

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 2625 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Light **Armor bonus:** 3 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Maximum dexterity:** 5 **Armor check penalty:** -1 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 15 **Special material:** Mithral

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

This finely crafted +1 mithral _[[items/Armor/Parade armor|parade armor]]_ from Kyonin is as light and comfortable as regular clothing. An _[[items/Armor/Iadaran Dress Uniform|Iadaran dress uniform]]_ counts as a _[[items/Mundane/Courtier's outfit|courtier's outfit]]_ (including appropriate _[[items/Mundane/Jewelry|jewelry]]_) when the wearer is interacting with nobles or courtiers, in addition to providing a +2 circumstance bonus on Diplomacy and Intimidate checks to influence people from the elven home nation. Further, the wearer also gains a +2 competence bonus on Perception and Sense Motive checks to intuit that a creature is concealing its true beliefs, feelings, or motives.
