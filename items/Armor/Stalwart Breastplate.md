### Properties

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ **Armor bonus:** 6 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Maximum dexterity:** 3 **Armor check penalty:** -4 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 25

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 13 **Aura:** strong abjuration

### Description

The burden of this weighty +1 light _[[universal monster rules/Fortification|fortification]]_ _[[items/Armor/Breastplate|breastplate]]_ gives it an armor check penalty of –5. As an immediate action, the wearer can negate the effects of a successful combat maneuver made to bull rush, overrun, or _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_ her. As a swift action, the wearer can _[[spells/Expend|expend]]_ one use of mythic power to gain _[[universal monster rules/Immunity|immunity]]_ to ability damage, ability drain, critical hits, and sneak attacks. This effect lasts for 1 round per mythic tier the wearer possesses.
