# Mountain pattern

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 250 gp **Weight:** 40 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ **Armor bonus:** 6 **Maximum dexterity:** 3 **Armor check penalty:** -4 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 30

### Description

This _medium_ armor consists of hundreds of small, interlocking pieces of steel shaped to resemble an ancient symbol for the word "mountain." The mail is then riveted to a cloth or leather backing. It is worn like a mail coat and covers your torso, shoulders, and thighs.
