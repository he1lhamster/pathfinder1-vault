# Wooden armor

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 20 gp **Weight:** 25 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Light **Armor bonus:** 3 **Maximum dexterity:** 3 **Armor check penalty:** -1 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 15

### Description

This suit of _[[items/Armor/Leather armor|leather armor]]_ has plates of fire-treated wood sewn over vital areas. Though not as effective as metal armor, it offers better protection than leather alone. Unlike metal armor, the wood is slightly buoyant, and the armor check penalty for swimming in this armor is 0.
