### Description

This blue-black +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Defiant|defiant]]_ _[[items/Armor/Chainmail|chainmail]]_ has a metal badge depicting Calistria's three-dagger symbol fused to it. Each of these suits of armor is crafted with a particular type of foe in mind for the _defiant_ property, as appropriate for the _[[feats/Vengeance|vengeance]]_ the original crafter sought.
