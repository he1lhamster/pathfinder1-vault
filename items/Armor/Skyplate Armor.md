# Skyplate Armor

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 30300 gp **Weight:** 20 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ **Armor bonus:** 6 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Maximum dexterity:** 3 **Armor check penalty:** -5 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 25

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 11 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This suit of +1 steel lamellar weighs less than normal steel lamellar (though it is still considered _medium_ armor). _[[items/Armor/Skyplate Armor|Skyplate armor]]_ grants its wearer the ability to use _[[spells/Wind Walk|wind walk]]_ once per day. Furthermore, while clad in _skyplate armor_, the wearer gains a +4 _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ bonus on all saving throws against electricity effects, wind effects, and all effects associated with magical or mundane weather.
