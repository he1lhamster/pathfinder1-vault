# Mammoth Hide

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 14665 gp **Weight:** 25 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ **Armor bonus:** 4 **Enhancement bonus:** +3 **Maximum dexterity:** 4 **Armor check penalty:** -3 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 20

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 11 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

Made of tattered _[[items/Armor/Mammoth Hide|mammoth hide]]_ and bone, this +3 _[[items/Armor/Hide armor|hide armor]]_ is similar to _[[items/Armor/Rhino Hide|rhino hide]]_, but is fashioned in the harsh lands of the Realm of the Mammoth Lords using beasts common to that region. In addition to granting a +3 enhancement bonus to AC, it has a –1 armor check penalty and grants the wearer 4d6 additional points of damage on the first successful charge attack she makes in a round, including a mounted charge.
