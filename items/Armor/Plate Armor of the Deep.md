# Plate Armor of the Deep

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 24650 gp **Weight:** 50 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Heavy **Armor bonus:** 9 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Maximum dexterity:** 1 **Armor check penalty:** -6 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 35

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 11 **Aura:** moderate abjuration

### Description

This +1 _[[items/Armor/Full plate|full plate]]_ is decorated with a wave and fish motif and often has tiny fins and scales worked into the design. Although the armor remains as heavy and bulky as normal _full plate_, the wearer of _[[items/Armor/Plate Armor of the Deep|plate armor of the deep]]_ is treated as unarmored for purposes of Swim checks. The wearer can breathe _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Underwater|underwater]]_ and can converse with any waterbreathing creature with a language.
