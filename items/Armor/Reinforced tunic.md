### Description

Sometimes _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Called|called]]_ a leine, this belted tunic has thick cords woven through it that cover vital areas. A _[[items/Armor/Reinforced tunic|reinforced tunic]]_'s armor bonus is increased by 2 against attack rolls made to confirm critical hits against the wearer.
