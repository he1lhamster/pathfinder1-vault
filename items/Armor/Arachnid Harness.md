# Arachnid Harness

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 6160 gp **Weight:** 15 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Light **Armor bonus:** 2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Maximum dexterity:** 6 **Armor check penalty:** 0 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 10

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 4 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

This formfitting black +1 leather features eight ridged support ribs along the back. On _[[spells/Command|command]]_, the _[[items/Armor/Arachnid Harness|arachnid harness]]_ sprouts eight enormous spidery legs from these ribs. These legs allow the armor's wearer to attempt _[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_ checks at full speed without needing to use her hands or feet. Additionally, while the legs are deployed, the armor's wearer doesn't lose her Dexterity bonus to AC while climbing, and need not succeed at a _Climb_ check to avoid falling if she takes damage while climbing.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 3160 gp

_[[feats/Craft Magic Arms and Armor|Craft Magic Arms and Armor]]_, _[[spells/Spider Climb|spider climb]]_
