# Relic of Kazavon, Armor of Skulls

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** – **Weight:** 30 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ **Armor bonus:** 6 **Enhancement bonus:** +4 **Maximum dexterity:** 3 **Armor check penalty:** -4 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 25

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 20 **Aura:** strong abjuration, strong transmutation

### Description

Crafted from the skull of the dragon Kazavon, the Armor of Skulls is a Large intelligent +4 greater electricity _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ _[[items/Armor/Breastplate|breastplate]]_ that grants its wearer a +4 enhancement bonus to Strength and Constitution. Once per day, the wearer can unleash either a bolt or burst of electricity. The bolt is a 120-foot line of electricity that deals 24d8 points of electricity damage (Reflex DC 37 for half). The burst deals 6d8 points of electricity damage to all creatures within a 30-foot-radius burst (Reflex DC 37 for half).

### _[[spells/Destruction|Destruction]]_

One who has never killed another creature must bind the Armor of Skulls in gold chains (worth 1,000,000 gp), then slowly _[[spells/Shatter|shatter]]_ the armor by shortening the chain by one link every day for 1,000 days.
