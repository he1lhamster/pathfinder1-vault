# Buccaneer's Breastplate

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 23850 gp **Weight:** 30 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ **Armor bonus:** 6 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Maximum dexterity:** 3 **Armor check penalty:** -4 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 25

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This +1 _[[items/Armor/Breastplate|breastplate]]_ is made of shining amber metal and decorated with faint motifs of the waves and water. It allows the wearer to continually utilize the effects of the spell _[[spells/Water Walk|water walk]]_. If a creature puts on the armor while _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Underwater|underwater]]_, the wearer is borne toward the surface at a rate of 60 feet per round.
