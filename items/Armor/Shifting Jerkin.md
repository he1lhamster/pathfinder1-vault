# Shifting Jerkin

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 5965 gp **Weight:** 8 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Light **Armor bonus:** 2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Maximum dexterity:** 4 **Armor check penalty:** 0 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 5

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint illusion, faint transmutation

### Description

This +1 _[[items/Armor/Lamellar cuirass|lamellar cuirass]]_ grants its wearer shapeshifting. On _[[spells/Command|command]]_, the wearer of a _[[items/Armor/Shifting Jerkin|shifting jerkin]]_ can change her appearance at will as per _[[spells/Disguise Self|disguise self]]_. If the wearer has a talents class feature (_[[classes/Investigator|investigator]]_ talents, _[[classes/Ninja|ninja]]_ tricks, _[[classes/Rogue|rogue]]_ talents, or _[[classes/Slayer|slayer]]_ talents), once per day she can spend 1 hour to swap out one talent for another for which she qualifies. She can't swap a talent that is a prerequisites for other talents or feats she has.

**Crafting cost:** 3065 gp
