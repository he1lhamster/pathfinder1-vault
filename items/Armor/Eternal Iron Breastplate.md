### Description

This +1 _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Stanching|stanching]]_ _[[items/Armor/Breastplate|breastplate]]_ is battle-scarred but polished with great care, and has a small sword-and-mountain symbol of Gorum hammered just below the gorget.
