# Legion Armor

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 13400 gp **Weight:** 35 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Heavy **Armor bonus:** 7 **Enhancement bonus:** +2 **Maximum dexterity:** 1 **Armor check penalty:** -6 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 35

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

This gilded suit of +2 _[[items/Armor/Banded mail|banded mail]]_ is decorated with images of celestial beings holding their weapons high. _[[items/Armor/Legion Armor|Legion armor]]_ is typically worn by high-ranking angels and archons directly combating the forces of evil on the Material Plane.

Once per day as a standard action, the wearer of a suit of _legion armor_ can cast the spell _[[spells/Spiritual Ally|spiritual ally]]_ with a duration of 1 minute. This ally typically takes on the appearance of an imposing angelic warrior. At any time while the armor's _spiritual ally_ is conjured, the wearer of a suit of _legion armor_ can dismiss the effect as a standard action, causing the _spiritual ally_ to explode with a thunderous choir of voices and blaring trumpets. This effect causes each evil-aligned creatures within 100 feet to become _[[conditions/Shaken|shaken]]_ unless it succeeds at a DC 20 Will save.
