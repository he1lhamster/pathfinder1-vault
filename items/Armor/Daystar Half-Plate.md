# Daystar Half-Plate

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 81250 gp **Weight:** 50 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Heavy **Armor bonus:** 8 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Maximum dexterity:** 0 **Armor check penalty:** -7 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 40

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 15 **Aura:** strong evocation

### Description

This glowing +1 _[[items/Armor/Half-plate|half-plate]]_ is decorated with various images of the sun and depictions of nature. At will, the wearer can _[[spells/Command|command]]_ the armor to shine with a _[[spells/Daylight|daylight]]_ spell for 10 minutes. Once per day, the wearer can _command_ it to shine as per the _[[spells/Sunburst|sunburst]]_ spell. The _sunburst_ is centered on the wearer and the wearer is immune to its effects.

**Crafting cost:** 41000 gp
