# Ekujae Jungle Armor

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 3900 gp **Weight:** 20 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Light **Armor bonus:** 3 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Maximum dexterity:** 5 **Armor check penalty:** 0 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 15

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint abjuration, faint transmutation

### Description

Made of densely compacted jungle leaves by the Ekujae jungle elves of the Mwangi, this +1 _[[items/Armor/Leaf armor|leaf armor]]_ has been delicately inscribed with elven script. Prized by druids who venture out of the jungle, this armor counts as a plant for the purposes of an _[[spells/Entangle|entangle]]_ spell, allowing the spell to originate from the position of the wearer.

_[[items/Armor/Ekujae Jungle Armor|Ekujae jungle armor]]_ is uniquely suited to jungle terrain, keeping out the elements and granting the wearer a +5 competence bonus on Stealth checks in such terrain. This armor also allows the wearer to ignore the effects of a hot climate as if under the effects of _[[spells/Endure Elements|endure elements]]_.
