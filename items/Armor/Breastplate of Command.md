# Breastplate of Command

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 25400 gp **Weight:** 30 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ **Armor bonus:** 6 **Enhancement bonus:** +2 **Maximum dexterity:** 3 **Armor check penalty:** -4 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 25

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 15 **Aura:** strong enchantment

### Description

This +2 _[[items/Armor/Breastplate|breastplate]]_ typically bears the image of a rampant grizzly bear or other impressive predator. It bestows a commanding aura upon its wearer. The wearer gains a +2 competence bonus on all Charisma checks, including Charisma-based skill checks. The wearer also gains a +2 competence bonus to his _[[feats/Leadership|Leadership]]_ score. Friendly troops within 360 feet of the user become braver than normal, gaining a +2 _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ bonus on saving throws against _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_. Since the effect arises in great part from the distinctiveness of the armor, it does not function if the wearer hides or conceals herself in any way.
