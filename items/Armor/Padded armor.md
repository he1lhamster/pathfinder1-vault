# Padded armor

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 10 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Light **Armor bonus:** 1 **Maximum dexterity:** 8 **Armor check penalty:** 0 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 5

### Description

More than simple clothing, _[[items/Armor/Padded armor|padded armor]]_ combines heavy, _[[items/Armor/Quilted cloth|quilted cloth]]_ and layers of densely packed stuffing to create a cheap and basic protection. It is typically worn by those not intending to face lethal combat or those who _[[spells/Wish|wish]]_ their maneuverability to be impacted as little as possible.
