# Chainmail

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 150 gp **Weight:** 40 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ **Armor bonus:** 6 **Maximum dexterity:** 2 **Armor check penalty:** -5 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 30

### Description

Unlike a _[[items/Armor/Chain shirt|chain shirt]]_, which covers only the chest, _[[items/Armor/Chainmail|chainmail]]_ protects the wearer with a complete mesh of chain links that cover the torso and arms, and extends below the waist. Multiple interconnected pieces offer additional protection over vital areas. The suit includes gauntlets.
