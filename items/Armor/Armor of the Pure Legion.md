# Armor of the Pure Legion

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 18300 gp **Weight:** 30 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ **Armor bonus:** 6 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Maximum dexterity:** 3 **Armor check penalty:** -5 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 25

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 15 **Aura:** strong abjuration

### Description

The plates of this suit of +1 steel lamellar armor are etched with the complete Laws of Man from the secular nation of Rahadoum. Suits of _[[items/Armor/Armor of the Pure Legion|armor of the Pure Legion]]_ are created for high-ranking members in the eponymous organization and grant protection against the two most common scourges of Rahadoum: divine magic and desert _[[universal monster rules/Heat|heat]]_. Such armor grants its wearer _[[universal monster rules/Spell Resistance|spell resistance]]_ against divine spells equal to 5 + the wearer's base attack bonus. It also protects the wearer from _heat_ dangers as if she were affected by a continuous _[[spells/Endure Elements|endure elements]]_ spell. Additionally, once per day when the wearer of a suit of _armor of the Pure Legion_ fails a Will save against a mindeffecting spell cast by a divine spellcaster, she can attempt another save as a free action on the following round to end the spell.
