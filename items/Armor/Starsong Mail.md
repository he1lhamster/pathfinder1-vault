# Starsong Mail

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 3460 gp **Weight:** 40 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ **Armor bonus:** 6 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Maximum dexterity:** 3 **Armor check penalty:** -5 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 30

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint conjuration, faint transmutation

### Description

This +1 _[[items/Armor/Chainmail|chainmail]]_ is usually a metallic midnight blue and may sport a badge in the shape of a butterfly, moth, or _[[items/Weapon/Starknife|starknife]]_. Once per day, the wearer can use _[[spells/Expeditious Retreat|expeditious retreat]]_. If the armor's wearer sleeps in or next to the armor, the armor automatically casts _[[spells/Dream Feast|dream feast]]_ once per night on the wearer or the last creature who wore it.
