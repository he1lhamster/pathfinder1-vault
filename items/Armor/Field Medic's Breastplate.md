# Field Medic's Breastplate

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 52900 gp **Weight:** 30 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ **Armor bonus:** 6 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Maximum dexterity:** 3 **Armor check penalty:** -4 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 25

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate abjuration, faint conjuration

### Description

This battered yet lovingly polished +1 _[[items/Armor/Breastplate|breastplate]]_ is marked by three chipped glass circles set over the heart, each of a different color. Three times per day, the wearer may tap a circle to activate it, enveloping her in a shimmering aura that protects her from _[[spells/Harm|harm]]_. The wearer gains DR 10/— and is protected if she were under the effects of a _[[spells/Globe Of Invulnerability|globe of invulnerability]]_ spell. The effect ends as soon as the wearer makes an offensive action or casts an offensive spell, or after 1 minute. In addition, whenever the wearer casts a cure spell or administers a cure potion while under this effect, she may add one additional die to the total amount of damage healed.
