# Raiment of the Shining Crusade

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 20750 gp **Weight:** 50 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Heavy **Armor bonus:** 8 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Maximum dexterity:** 0 **Armor check penalty:** -7 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 40

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate abjuration, moderate evocation

### Description

This +1 _[[items/Armor/Half-plate|half-plate]]_ is emblazoned with holy script and religious iconography. _[[items/Armor/Raiment of the Shining Crusade|Raiment of the shining crusade]]_ was first crafted by the holy warriors serving under General Arnisant. The wearer of _raiment of the shining crusade_ gains a +2 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ bonus on saving throws against channeled negative energy and to resist negative levels. When worn by a character with the smite evil class feature, the armor sheds light like a _[[items/Mundane/Torch|torch]]_ at all times.

A wearer with the smite evil class feature can _[[spells/Expend|expend]]_ one use of this ability as an immediate action to create a defensive ward around an adjacent creature, granting the target a _[[spells/Deflection|deflection]]_ bonus to AC and a _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ bonus on saves equal to the wearer's Charisma bonus against attacks and effects originating from creatures with an evil alignment. This effect lasts for 1 round.
