# Banded Mail of Luck

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 18900 gp **Weight:** 35 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Heavy **Armor bonus:** 7 **Enhancement bonus:** +3 **Maximum dexterity:** 1 **Armor check penalty:** -6 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 35

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 12 **Aura:** strong enchantment

### Description

Ten 100-gp gems adorn this +3 _[[items/Armor/Banded mail|banded mail]]_. Once per week, the armor allows its wearer to require that an attack roll made against him be rerolled. He must take whatever consequences come from the second roll. The wearer's player must decide whether to have the attack roll rerolled before damage is rolled.
