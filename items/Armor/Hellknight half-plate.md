# Hellknight half-plate

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 850 gp **Weight:** 50 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Heavy **Armor bonus:** 8 **Maximum dexterity:** 0 **Armor check penalty:** -7 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 40

### Description

The armor worn by a _[[npcs/Hellknight Signifer|Hellknight signifer]]_ is a vestment of armored plates, chain, and dark fabric. It functions as _[[items/Armor/Half-plate|half-plate]]_, but it can be donned and removed as though it were _[[classes/Medium|medium]]_ armor. Any spellcaster who wears this armor is treated as if he were wearing _[[items/Armor/Hellknight plate|Hellknight plate]]_ for the purposes of any feats and effects that require one to be wearing _Hellknight plate_. This armor includes a _[[items/Mundane/Signifer mask|signifer mask]]_, though signifer masks can be purchased separately.
