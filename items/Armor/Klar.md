# Klar

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 12 gp **Weight:** 6 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _[[spells/Shield|Shield]]_ **Armor bonus:** 1 **Maximum dexterity:** 0 **Armor check penalty:** -1 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 5

### Description

The traditional form of this tribal weapon is a short blade bound to the skull of a large horned lizard, but a skilled smith can craft one entirely out of metal. A traditional _[[items/Armor/Klar|klar]]_ counts as a _[[items/Shield/Light wooden shield|light wooden shield]]_ with armor spikes; a metal _klar_ counts as a _[[items/Shield/Light steel shield|light steel shield]]_ with armor spikes.
