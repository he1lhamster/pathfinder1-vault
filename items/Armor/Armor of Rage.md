# Armor of Rage

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 25400 gp **Weight:** 50 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Heavy **Armor bonus:** 9 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Maximum dexterity:** 1 **Armor check penalty:** -6 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 35

##### Magical properties

**Cursed**

**Caster level:** 16 **Aura:** strong necromancy

### Description

This armor is similar in appearance to armor of _[[spells/Command|command]]_ and functions as a suit of +1 _[[items/Armor/Full plate|full plate]]_. However, when it is worn, the armor causes the character to take a –4 penalty to Charisma. All unfriendly creatures within 300 feet gain a +1 morale bonus on attack rolls against her. The effect is not noticeable to the wearer or those affected. In other words, the wearer does not immediately notice that donning the armor is the cause of her problems, nor do foes understand the reason for the depth of their enmity.
