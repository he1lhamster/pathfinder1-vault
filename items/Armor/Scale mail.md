# Scale mail

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 50 gp **Weight:** 30 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ **Armor bonus:** 5 **Maximum dexterity:** 3 **Armor check penalty:** -4 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 25

### Description

_[[items/Armor/Scale mail|Scale mail]]_ is made up of dozens of small, overlapping metal plates. Similar to both _[[items/Armor/Splint mail|splint mail]]_ and _[[items/Armor/Banded mail|banded mail]]_, scalemail has a flexible arrangement of scales in an attempt to avoid hindering the wearer's _[[feats/Mobility|mobility]]_, but at the expense of omitting additional protective layers of armor. A suit of _scale mail_ includes gauntlets.
