# Gray maiden plate

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 1500 gp **Weight:** 50 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Heavy **Armor bonus:** 9 **Maximum dexterity:** 1 **Armor check penalty:** -6 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 35

### Description

The _[[monsters/Gray|Gray]]_ Maidens' distinctive armor is one of their most iconic identifiers. _[[items/Armor/Gray maiden plate|Gray Maiden plate]]_ is mechanically identical to _[[items/Armor/Full plate|full plate]]_, and any effect that functions with _full plate_ functions with _Gray Maiden plate_. Certain options for _Gray_ Maidens function only when they wear _Gray Maiden plate_.
