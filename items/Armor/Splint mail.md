# Splint mail

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 200 gp **Weight:** 45 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Heavy **Armor bonus:** 7 **Maximum dexterity:** 0 **Armor check penalty:** -7 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 40

### Description

_[[items/Armor/Splint mail|Splint mail]]_ is made up of overlapping layers of metal strips attached to a backing of leather or sturdy fabric. These splints are of greater size and durability than those that compose a suit of _[[items/Armor/Scale mail|scale mail]]_, improving the protection they afford the wearer, but at the cost of flexibility. A suit of _splint mail_ includes gauntlets.
