# Martial arts training gear

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 45 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Light **Armor bonus:** 1 **Maximum dexterity:** 8 **Armor check penalty:** 0 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 5

### Description

This armor consists of several sections of curved padding held together by an assembly of leather straps. Meant to be worn during martial arts sparring contests, the armor absorbs blows from _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Training|training]]_ weapons and unarmed strikes. The wearer reduces nonlethal damage caused by physical attacks, including _[[universal monster rules/Natural Attacks|natural attacks]]_, by 2 points of damage. The wearer also ignores the first point of lethal bludgeoning damage from _natural attacks_.
