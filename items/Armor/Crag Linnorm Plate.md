# Crag Linnorm Plate

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 41300 gp **Weight:** 50 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Heavy **Armor bonus:** 9 **Enhancement bonus:** +4 **Maximum dexterity:** 1 **Armor check penalty:** -6 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 35 **Special material:** Dragonhide

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 11 **Aura:** moderate necromancy

### Description

This specialized suit of _[[items/Armor/Full plate|full plate]]_ is made from the tanned hide of a linnorm and acts as +4 _[[items/Armor/Dragonhide Plate|dragonhide plate]]_. If a creature is slain while wearing such armor, its killer is subject to a curse. The cursed creature must succeed at a DC 17 Will save or gain _[[curses/Vulnerability|vulnerability]]_ to fire until the curse is removed. This curse can be removed only by a _[[spells/Break Enchantment|break enchantment]]_, _[[spells/Miracle|miracle]]_, or _[[spells/Wish|wish]]_ spell. This particular suit of armor is made from the hide of a crag linnorm, though others exist, each with a different curse effect similar to the curse of the associated linnorm.

Once per day as a swift action, the wear of this armor can _[[universal monster rules/Summon|summon]]_ forth the uncanny movement powers of a linnorm, gaining the benefits of _[[spells/Freedom of Movement|freedom of movement]]_ for 10 minutes.
