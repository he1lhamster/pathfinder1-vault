# Hellknight plate

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 2000 gp **Weight:** 50 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Heavy **Armor bonus:** 9 **Maximum dexterity:** 1 **Armor check penalty:** -5 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 35

### Description

These distinctive suits of armor are a special type of masterwork _[[items/Armor/Full plate|full plate]]_ that grant additional effects when worn by a character with levels in the _[[npcs/Hellknight|Hellknight]]_ prestige class. Apart from its distinctive look (each order of Hellknights has its own style of armor), a suit of _[[items/Armor/Hellknight plate|Hellknight plate]]_ functions the same as a suit of masterwork _full plate_.
