# Invincible Armor

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 137650 gp **Weight:** 50 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Heavy **Armor bonus:** 9 **Enhancement bonus:** +2 **Maximum dexterity:** 1 **Armor check penalty:** -6 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 35

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 13 **Aura:** strong abjuration

### Description

_[[items/Armor/Invincible Armor|Invincible armor]]_ shifts between three different appearances, but always weighs the same and protects as +2 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Impervious|impervious]]_ moderate _[[universal monster rules/Fortification|fortification]]_ _[[items/Armor/Full plate|full plate]]_. As an immediate action, the wearer can change the armor into heavy plate that blocks slashing attacks (DR 10/bludgeoning or piercing), thickly layered _[[items/Armor/Chainmail|chainmail]]_ that blocks piercing attacks (DR 10/ bludgeoning or slashing), or overlapping metal scales that block bludgeoning attacks (DR 10/piercing or slashing). In addition, _invincible armor_ provides the wearer with 5 points of _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ to acid, cold, electricity, fire, and sonic, and is itself unharmed by damage from any of these energy types.
