# Leather armor

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 10 gp **Weight:** 15 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Light **Armor bonus:** 2 **Maximum dexterity:** 6 **Armor check penalty:** 0 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 10

### Description

_[[items/Armor/Leather armor|Leather armor]]_ is made up of multiple overlapping pieces of leather, boiled to increase their natural _[[feats/Toughness|toughness]]_ and then deliberately stitched together. Although not as sturdy as metal armor, the flexibility it allows wearers makes it among the most widely used types of armor.
