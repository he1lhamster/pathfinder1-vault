# Giant-Hide Armor (ogre)

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 39165 gp **Weight:** 25 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ **Armor bonus:** 4 **Enhancement bonus:** +3 **Maximum dexterity:** 4 **Armor check penalty:** -3 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 20

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 15 **Aura:** strong transmutation

### Description

This drooping, many-folded suit of +3 _[[items/Armor/Hide armor|hide armor]]_ is made from the tanned skin of an actual giant. Once per day on _[[spells/Command|command]]_, the wearer can grow to match the size of the appropriate giant, as if using _[[spells/Giant Form I|giant form I]]_ (_[[monsters/Ogre|ogre]]_, hill _[[monsters/Giant, Stone Giant|giant, stone giant]]_, fire giant, frost giant, or _[[monsters/Troll|troll]]_) or _[[spells/Giant Form II|giant form II]]_ (cloud giant or storm giant). This _[[spells/Transformation|transformation]]_ lasts up to 15 minutes, and ends when the wearer commands.

Most good folk consider wearing the skins of intelligent creatures to be abhorrent. Giants gain a +2 morale bonus on attack and damage rolls against the wearer, or +4 if the wearer's armor is made from the same kind of giant as the attacker.
