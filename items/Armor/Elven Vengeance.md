# Elven Vengeance

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 16150 gp **Weight:** 20 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Light **Armor bonus:** 6 **Enhancement bonus:** +2 **Maximum dexterity:** 4 **Armor check penalty:** -2 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 30

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 12 **Aura:** strong abjuration

### Description

This set of +2 _[[items/Armor/Elven Chain|elven chain]]_ grants its wearer a +4 _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ bonus on saving throws against spells, _[[universal monster rules/Spell-Like Abilities|spell-like abilities]]_, and other effects created by worshipers and minions of a specific demon lord, chosen when the armor is created. Against such foes, the armor's enhancement bonus increases to +4. The first suit of this armor was created quite recently by Perelir for her close friend Malindil as part of the _[[classes/Ranger|ranger]]_'s vengeful crusade against Abraxas; since then, many other suits focused against other powerful demons (particularly the nascent demon lord _[[monsters/Treerazer|Treerazer]]_) have been crafted.
