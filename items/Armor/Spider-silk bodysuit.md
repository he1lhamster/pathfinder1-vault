# Spider-silk bodysuit

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 850 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Light **Armor bonus:** 3 **Maximum dexterity:** 6 **Armor check penalty:** -1 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 10

### Description

This exotic form-fitting garment is woven entirely from alchemically treated silk harvested from giant spiders or other arachnid creatures. If you have the _[[monsters/Drow|drow]]_'s weapon familiarity racial trait, you treat the _[[items/Armor/Spider-silk bodysuit|spider-silk bodysuit]]_'s maximum Dexterity bonus as 2 higher.
