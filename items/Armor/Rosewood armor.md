# Rosewood armor

### Properties

**Type:** Armor **Price:** 50 gp **Weight:** 15 lbs. **Slot:** Armor

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Light **Armor bonus:** 2 **Maximum dexterity:** 6 **Armor check penalty:** 0 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 10

### Description

This suit of _[[items/Armor/Leather armor|leather armor]]_ is wrapped in special rose vines. Treat this as _leather armor_ with armor spikes. The vines must be watered with at least 1 gallon of water each day or they wither and die, turning the armor into normal _leather armor_.
