#  Stanching

**Source** _Ultimate Equipment pg. 122_
**Aura** moderate transmutation **CL** 7th
**Slot** armor quality; **Price** +1 bonus; **Weight** —

### Description

A _stanching _suit of armor helps stop the flow of b lood from the wearer’s wounds, automatically tightening like a tourniquet in the appropriate places while also magically reducing the severity of the wound. _Stanching _armor reduces hit point damage, ability damage, or ability drain by an amount equal to its enhancement bonus if the damage or drain was from a bleed effect. The wearer also adds the armor’s enhancement bonus to Constitution checks to become stable.

### Construction

**Requirements** Craft Magic Arms and Armor, _stabilize_, _cure critical wounds _or _lesser restoration_; **Price** +1 bonus
