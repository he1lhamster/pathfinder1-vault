#  Radiant

**Source** _Ultimate Equipment pg. 121_
**Aura** moderate evocation **CL** 6th
**Slot** armor/shield quality; **Price** +7,500 gp; **Weight** —

### Description

Armor and shields with the _radiant _special ability shine as brightly as a torch when worn. This radiance can be suppressed or resumed on command. Designs usually feature bright colors and a brilliant sheen even when not illuminated. Once per day, the wearer can command the armor or shield to brighten to the strength of a _daylight _spell for 1 hour or until commanded to dim.

### Construction

**Requirements** Craft Magic Arms and Armor, _daylight_; **Price** +3,750 gp
