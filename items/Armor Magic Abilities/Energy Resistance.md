#  Energy Resistance

**Source** _Ultimate Equipment pg. 117_, _PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 463_
**Aura** faint abjuration **CL** 3rd
**Slot** armor/shield quality; **Price** +18,000 gp; **Weight** —

### Description

A suit of armor or a shield with this special ability protects against one type of energy (acid, cold, electricity, fire, or sonic) and is designed with patterns depicting the element it protects against. The armor absorbs the first 10 points of energy damage per attack that the wearer would normally take (similar to the _resist energy _spell).

### Construction

**Requirements** Craft Magic Arms and Armor, _resist energy_; **Price** +9,000 gp
