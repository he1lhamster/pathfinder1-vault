# Spell component pouch

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

A spellcaster with a _[[items/Mundane/Spell component pouch|spell component pouch]]_ is assumed to have all the material components and focuses needed for spellcasting, except for components that have a specific cost, divine focuses, and focuses that wouldn't fit in a pouch. Most spell component pouches are waterproof and can be strung onto a belt or _[[items/Mundane/Bandolier|bandolier]]_.
