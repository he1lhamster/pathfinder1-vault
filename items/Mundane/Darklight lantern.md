# Darklight lantern

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 20 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This lantern does not _[[universal monster rules/Burn|burn]]_ oil, but instead burns shadowcloy (see below). When shadowcloy is used as its fuel, this lantern creates a strange, hazy _[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_ that decreases the light level for 30 feet around it by one step . Unlike when shadowcloy is thrown at a single target, this haze does not decrease natural _darkness_ to supernatural _darkness_. One _[[items/Mundane/Flask|flask]]_ of shadowcloy fuels a _[[items/Mundane/Darklight lantern|darklight lantern]]_ for 1 minute.
