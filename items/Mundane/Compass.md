# Compass

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 10 gp **Weight:** 0.5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

An ordinary _[[items/Mundane/Compass|compass]]_ that points to the magnetic north _[[items/Mundane/Pole|pole]]_ grants you a +2 circumstance bonus on Survival checks made to avoid becoming lost. You can also use it to grant the same bonus on Knowledge (dungeoneering) checks made to navigate underground.
