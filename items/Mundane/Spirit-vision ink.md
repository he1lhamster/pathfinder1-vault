# Spirit-vision ink

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 25 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Originally developed by kami-revering priests to write _[[spells/Prayer|prayer]]_ scrolls, this pale _[[items/Mundane/Ink|ink]]_ is now often used by ninjas to mark locations or leave messages. Once _[[items/Mundane/Spirit-vision ink|spirit-vision ink]]_ dries, it becomes completely _[[conditions/Invisible|invisible]]_. To _invisible_ creatures, however, writing in _spirit-vision ink_ shimmers with a rich azure hue.
