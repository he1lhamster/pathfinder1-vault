# Harrow deck

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 100 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This is a traditional fortune-telling deck of _[[items/Mundane/Cards|cards]]_ used by soothsayers and seers. Some harrow decks are elaborately illustrated, but most are parchment or paper _cards_ with hand-painted images. Harrow decks are often handed down through generations and treated with utmost care by their users as a result.
