# Ink of stolen secrets

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 50 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This alchemically concocted black _[[items/Mundane/Ink|ink]]_ can be attuned to a standard sheet of parchment. Anything inscribed using the _ink_ (anywhere on the same plane of existence) appears on the attuned parchment in a sparkling blue script that lasts 1 hour. There is enough _ink_ in one _[[items/Mundane/Vial|vial]]_ to fill one sheet of parchment. The _ink_ cannot duplicate any written magical effects, such as a _[[spells/Symbol of Death|symbol of death]]_ (as per the spell of the same name). Crafting a _vial_ of _[[items/Mundane/Ink of stolen secrets|ink of stolen secrets]]_ requires a successful DC 15 Craft (alchemy) check.
