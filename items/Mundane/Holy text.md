# Holy text

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 50.5 gp **Weight:** 10.5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This is the religious text of a particular religion, with a title, text, and detailed rites specific to that faith. The cheap variety is a small, light book with no illustrations, printed or written on thin paper, and with a cover made of canvas or thin leather. A typical _[[items/Mundane/Holy text|holy text]]_ for an experienced adventuring priest is durable, with a leather cover, chapter illustrations, quality paper; it costs 25 gp and weighs 2 pounds. The most expensive versions have detailed illustrations, gilded initials and border decorations, covers made of fine leather, wood, or even metal, and are often oversized and difficult to carry around.
