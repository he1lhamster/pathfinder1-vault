# Furs

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 12 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

The most basic of cold-weather gear, animal _[[items/Mundane/Furs|furs]]_ serve to keep their wearers warm. Wearing enough fur to cover the body provides a +2 bonus on Fortitude saves to resist cold weather. This does not stack with any bonuses gained from the Survival skill. _Furs_ may supplement another outfit.
