# Gloomstick

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 25 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This variant _[[items/Mundane/Sunrod|sunrod]]_ was developed by _[[monsters/Fetchling|fetchling]]_ alchemists. When twisted as a standard action, a _[[items/Mundane/Gloomstick|gloomstick]]_ begins drawing in light from the surrounding area, reducing the light level by one step (to a minimum of dim light) in a 20-foot radius. A _gloomstick_ remains active for 1 minute before crumbling to dust.

* Adventurer's Armory 2
