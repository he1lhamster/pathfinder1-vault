# Anesthetic wine

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 50 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This alchemical anesthetic, developed from ancient recipes for Sarenite _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ wine, grants a powerful _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ to pain. For 1 hour after drinking _[[items/Mundane/Anesthetic wine|anesthetic wine]]_, you gain a +5 bonus on saving throws against pain effects, and when you would take nonlethal damage, reduce the amount of damage taken by 1.

* Adventurer's Armory 2
