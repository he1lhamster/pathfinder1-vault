# Bloodblock

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 25 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This gooey, pinkish substance helps treat wounds. Using a dose gives you a +5 alchemical bonus on _[[spells/Heal|Heal]]_ checks for providing first aid, treating wounds made by _[[items/Mundane/Caltrops|caltrops]]_ or similar objects, or treating _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Deadly|deadly]]_ wounds. A dose of _[[items/Mundane/Bloodblock|bloodblock]]_ ends a _[[universal monster rules/Bleed|bleed]]_ effect as if you had made a DC 15 _Heal_ check. When treating _deadly_ wounds, using a dose of _bloodblock_ counts as one use of a _[[items/Mundane/Healer's kit|healer's kit]]_ (and grants the +5 bonus stated above).
