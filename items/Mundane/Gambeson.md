# Gambeson

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 1 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This padded cloth jacket is often worn alone or with mail or plate armor and is favored by hunters, guards, and soldiers. A _[[items/Mundane/Gambeson|gambeson]]_ fitted with leather loops to more easily _[[universal monster rules/Attach|attach]]_ armor is _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Called|called]]_ an arming doublet. Although sufficient to protect you against casual scrapes, it is not as thick as _[[items/Armor/Padded armor|padded armor]]_.
