# Flash powder

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 50 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This coarse _[[monsters/Gray|gray]]_ _[[items/Mundane/Powder|powder]]_ ignites and burns almost instantly if exposed to flame, significant friction, or even simple force such as _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Throwing|throwing]]_ it against a floor (a standard action). Creatures within a 10-foot-radius burst are _[[conditions/Blinded|blinded]]_ for 1 round (Fortitude DC 13 negates). Crafting this item is a DC 20 Craft (alchemy) check.
