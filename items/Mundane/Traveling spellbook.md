# Traveling spellbook

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 10 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

A _[[items/Mundane/Traveling spellbook|traveling spellbook]]_ is less cumbersome than a normal one, but holds fewer spells. It has 50 pages. Some wizards prefer to travel with a smaller _[[items/Mundane/Spellbook|spellbook]]_, omitting specialized spells that are rarely used on adventures. Others hide a _traveling spellbook_ containing their most important spells, just in case.
