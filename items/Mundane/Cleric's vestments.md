# Cleric's vestments

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 6 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

These ceremonial clothes are worn when performing priestly functions within a church or temple, and aren't well suited to adventuring. A _[[items/Mundane/Cleric's vestments|cleric's vestments]]_ typically include a cassock, stole, and surplice. The colors usually correspond to the deity worshiped, and while some priests may choose elaborate embroidery with ecclesiastical themes, others find merit in humble simplicity.
