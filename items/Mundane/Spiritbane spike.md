# Spiritbane spike

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 300 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

When you slash this alchemically treated _[[items/Mundane/Iron spike|iron spike]]_ against your flesh—a move action that deals 1d6 points of damage— the spike extends and becomes a _[[items/Weapon/Short sword|short sword]]_ with the _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Ghost Touch|ghost touch]]_ weapon special ability. The spike reverts to its old form after 10 minutes, corroded and useless. Should a _[[items/Mundane/Spiritbane spike|spiritbane spike]]_ rematerialize within a corporeal body, it is shunted to the nearest empty space without effect. Crafting this item requires a successful DC 25 Craft (alchemy) check.
