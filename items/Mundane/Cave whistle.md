# Cave whistle

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 20 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Fashioned from the spongy stem of a rare mushroom, this _[[items/Mundane/Whistle|whistle]]_ produces a sound that only kobolds and dragons can hear. Kobolds often create complicated codes made up of notes that they use to coordinate ambushes and raids.
