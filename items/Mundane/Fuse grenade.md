# Fuse grenade

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 100 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This hollow _[[items/Mundane/Clay|clay]]_ container holds a small charge of black power and a slow-burning fuse. Lighting the fuse is a move action; 1d3 rounds later the grenade explodes, dealing 2d6 points of bludgeoning damage and 1d6 points of fire damage in a 10-foot-radius burst (Reflex DC 15 halves). You throw a _[[items/Mundane/Fuse grenade|fuse grenade]]_ as if it were a splash weapon. Crafting this item is a DC 25 Craft (alchemy) check.
