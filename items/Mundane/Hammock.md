# Hammock

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 0.1 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This _[[items/Mundane/Blanket|blanket]]_ or _[[items/Mundane/Net|net]]_ is attached to strong ropes, allowing you to hang it from a heavy branch or two trees and sleep above the ground. Climbing into or out of a _[[items/Mundane/Hammock|hammock]]_ is a full-round action. A successful DC 5 Dexterity check reduces this to a move action.
