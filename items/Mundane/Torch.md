# Torch

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 0.01 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

A _[[items/Mundane/Torch|torch]]_ burns for 1 hour, shedding normal light in a 20-foot radius and increasing the light level by one step for an additional 20 feet beyond that area (_[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_ becomes dim light and dim light becomes normal light). A _torch_ does not increase the light level in normal light or bright light. If a _torch_ is used in combat, treat it as a one-handed improvised weapon that deals bludgeoning damage equal to that of a _[[items/Weapon/Gauntlet|gauntlet]]_ of its size, plus 1 point of fire damage.
