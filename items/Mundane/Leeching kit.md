# Leeching kit

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This sturdy ceramic jar has a heavy lid with a few tiny holes to let air in. It is normally half-filled with water and contains four adult leeches, each about 6 inches long. A _[[items/Mundane/Leeching kit|leeching kit]]_ grants you a +2 circumstance bonus on _[[spells/Heal|Heal]]_ checks to treat poison. Used for medicinal _[[feats/Bloodletting|bloodletting]]_, a leech can survive for 6 months between feedings.
