# Shinobi shozoku

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 50 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This close-fitting bodysuit of soft, dark fabric is favored by _[[classes/Ninja|ninja]]_ and assassins. When worn, it grants a +2 circumstance bonus on Stealth checks. Wearing armor with a base armor bonus higher than +1 negates this bonus, as does additional heavy clothing or equipment worn under the _[[items/Mundane/Shinobi shozoku|shinobi shozoku]]_ (at the GM's discretion).

* Adventurer's Armory 2
