# Sneezing powder

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 60 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This coarse yellowish-red _[[items/Mundane/Powder|powder]]_ is a splash weapon that causes uncontrollable sneezing for 1d4+1 rounds. Anyone standing in the square of _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Impact|impact]]_ must succeed at a DC 12 Fortitude save to resist the _powder_, while those in adjacent squares must make DC 8 Fortitude saves. Creatures affected by _[[items/Mundane/Sneezing powder|sneezing powder]]_ must make a DC 10 Fortitude save every round for the duration or be _[[conditions/Staggered|staggered]]_ until their next turn. This is a poison effect. Crafting this item is a DC 25 Craft (alchemy) check.
