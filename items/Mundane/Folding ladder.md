# Folding ladder

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 2 gp **Weight:** 16 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

The hook, _[[items/Mundane/Pole|pole]]_, and rungs of this 10-foot _[[items/Mundane/Ladder|ladder]]_ are hinged, allowing you to fold it into a 3-foot-by-1-foot-by-1-foot bundle. _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Folding|Folding]]_ or unfolding it is a standard action.
