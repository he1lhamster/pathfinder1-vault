# Blight tonic

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 150 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

_[[items/Mundane/Blight tonic|Blight tonic]]_ increases the potency of a creature's disease attack transmitted by physical contact, such as a dire rat's _[[diseases/Filth Fever|filth fever]]_. The next time a creature must save against the drinker's disease save DC, the DC for the initial infection increases by +2. The tonic lasts for 10 minutes or until the next time a creature must save against the drinker's disease DC, whichever comes first.
