# Ghost ink

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 25 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Pale blue when wet, _[[items/Mundane/Ghost ink|ghost ink]]_ dries to near-transparency 1 minute after application. _Ghost ink_ is used to subtly mark trails and locations. The pigment glows red under the light shed by fire beetle glands and sunrods; otherwise, a successful DC 25 Perception check is required to notice a mark in _ghost ink_. One _[[items/Mundane/Vial|vial]]_ of _ghost ink_ is sufficient for writing a page's worth of characters.
