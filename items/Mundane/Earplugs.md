# Earplugs

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 0.03 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Made of waxed cotton or cork, _[[items/Mundane/Earplugs|earplugs]]_ give you a +2 circumstance bonus on saves against effects that require hearing, but also cause a –5 penalty on hearing-based Perception checks.
