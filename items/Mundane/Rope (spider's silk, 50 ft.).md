# Rope (spider's silk, 50 ft.)

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 100 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Rare to virtually nonexistent on the surface world, spider's _[[items/Mundane/Silk rope|silk rope]]_ is woven of strands of silk from monstrous spiders by _[[monsters/Goblin|goblin]]_ slaves in the employ of the _[[monsters/Drow|drow]]_. As such it is commonly used by the dark elves, though shorter spider's _silk rope_ scraps (generally no more than 10 feet long) frequently appear among goblins. Spider's _silk rope_ has 6 hit points and can be burst with a DC 25 Strength check.
