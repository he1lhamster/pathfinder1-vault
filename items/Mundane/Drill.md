# Drill

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 0.5 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

A _[[items/Mundane/Drill|drill]]_ can create a 1-inch-diameter hole in stone, wood, or metal as a standard action. Harder materials wear down or break the _drill_ more quickly. Hearing the sound of drilling requires a DC 15 Perception check.
