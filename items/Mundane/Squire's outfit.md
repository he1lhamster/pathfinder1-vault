# Squire's outfit

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 8 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This outfit includes heavy woolen pants, a tunic, leather boots, a belt, sturdy gloves, a cap, and a heavy _[[items/Mundane/Tabard|tabard]]_ bearing the colors or sigil of a noble house or organization. Many organizations provide new squires this outfit for free, with the expectation they wear it during their duties.
