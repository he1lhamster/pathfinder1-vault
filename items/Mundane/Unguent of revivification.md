# Unguent of revivification

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 50 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

The preservation of dead flesh is important in undead-friendly Geb. Geb's elite commonly use this alchemical ointment as a cheaper alternative to _[[spells/Gentle Repose|gentle repose]]_ to give their undead flesh the blush of life. A single dose staves off the decomposition of dead flesh for 1d6 days. It cannot reverse decay that is already present and has no effect on the time limit for raising creatures from the dead.
