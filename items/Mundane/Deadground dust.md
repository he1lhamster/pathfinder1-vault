# Deadground dust

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 70 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Concocted from samples of elemental earth drained of vitality, this fine _[[items/Mundane/Powder|powder]]_ damages creatures with the earth subtype, as well as constructs made from earth, stone, or precious metals (brass, bronze, copper, gold, mithral, or silver), like _[[items/Mundane/Holy water|holy water]]_ damages undead. _[[items/Mundane/Deadground dust|Deadground dust]]_ can also be applied to a stone door to reduce its break DC by 10 (multiple doses don't further lower the DC), and it affects devices and mechanisms made from precious metals or stone like _[[items/Mundane/Rusting powder|rusting powder]]_ does. Crafting this item requires a successful DC 25 Craft (alchemy) check.
