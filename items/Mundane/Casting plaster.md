# Casting plaster

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 0.5 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This white, dry _[[items/Mundane/Powder|powder]]_ mixes with water to form a paste, which hardens over the course of an hour to create a solid material. It can be used to make casts of footprints or carvings, fill in gaps or cracks in walls, or (if applied over a cloth wrapping) create a cast for a _[[conditions/Broken|broken]]_ bone. Hardened plaster has hardness 1 and 5 hit points per inch of thickness. A 5-pound _[[items/Mundane/Clay|clay]]_ pot of plaster can cover about 5 square feet of flat space to a depth of 1 inch, five _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ forearm or lower-leg casts, two _Medium_ full-arm or full-leg casts, or twice as many casts for a Small creature.
