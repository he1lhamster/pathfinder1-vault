# Caltrops, cold iron

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 2 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Cold iron _[[items/Mundane/Caltrops|caltrops]]_ function in all ways as ordinary _caltrops_ (Pathfinder RPG Core Rulebook 155) except that they are made of cold iron, and affect creatures with DR/cold iron accordingly.
