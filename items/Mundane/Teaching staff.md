# Teaching staff

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 75 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Carved from heartwood and bearing ornate symbols along its length, this staff is used to reveal critical or uplifting information to other members of the faith, usually during a sermon. When activated, all creatures of the _[[classes/Cleric|cleric]]_'s religion gain a _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ (if positive energy) or profane (if negative energy) bonus on Knowledge (religion) checks for 1 minute; this bonus is equal to the number of dice of the _cleric_'s channel energy ability.
