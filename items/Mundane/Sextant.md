# Sextant

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 500 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

A _[[items/Mundane/Sextant|sextant]]_ is used to determine your latitude. Using a _sextant_ with an _[[items/Mundane/Astrolabe|astrolabe]]_ gives you a +4 circumstance bonus on Survival checks made to prevent getting lost.
