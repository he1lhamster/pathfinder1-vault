# The Formulae of Master Gebr

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 6615 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

These four cloth-bound books are neatly printed—as with a printing press or some other form of mechanical transcription—with a title page, a table of contents, and a useful cross-referenced index. They have been treated well and are clearly reference works, rather than laboratory notes; the writer was obviously a most disciplined and focused inventor. Protection The cover is treated with _[[poisons/Tears of Death|tears of death]]_ poison (contact; save Fort DC 22; onset 1 minute; frequency 1/minute for 6 minutes; effect 1d6 Con damage and _[[conditions/Paralyzed|paralyzed]]_ for 1 minute).
