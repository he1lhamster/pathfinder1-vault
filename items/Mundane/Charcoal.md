# Charcoal

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 0.5 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Sticks of _[[items/Mundane/Charcoal|charcoal]]_ are useful for marking floors and walls, writing on paper or parchment, and making rubbings of engravings or other markings. In a pinch, they can even be burned to stay warm. A good-quality rubbing generally takes 1 minute per sheet of paper.
