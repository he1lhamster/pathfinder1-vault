# Acid

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 10 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

You can throw a _[[items/Mundane/Flask|flask]]_ of acid as a splash weapon. Treat this attack as a ranged touch attack with a range increment of 10 feet. A direct hit deals 1d6 points of acid damage. Every creature within 5 feet of the point where the acid hits takes 1 point of acid damage from the splash.
