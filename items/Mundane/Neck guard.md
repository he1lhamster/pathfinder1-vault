# Neck guard

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 10 gp **Weight:** 0.25 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Made from hardened leather reinforced with a band of metal, this collar protects the wearer against _[[monsters/Vampire|vampire]]_ bites when worn around the throat. It provides a +1 armor bonus to AC against _vampire_ bites or similar attacks that specifically target the wearer's throat. Unlike most armor bonuses, the _[[items/Mundane/Neck guard|neck guard]]_'s +1 bonus stacks with the armor bonus of light or _[[classes/Medium|medium]]_ armor, but it provides no additional bonus when worn with heavy armor.
