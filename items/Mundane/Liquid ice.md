# Liquid ice

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 40 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Also known as "_[[classes/Alchemist|alchemist]]_'s ice," this sealed jar contains crystalline blue fluid that immediately starts to hiss and evaporate once opened. During the 1d6 rounds after it is opened but before it evaporates completely, you can use it to _[[universal monster rules/Freeze|freeze]]_ a liquid or to coat an object in a thin layer of ice. You can also throw _[[items/Mundane/Liquid ice|liquid ice]]_ as a splash weapon. A direct hit deals 1d6 points of cold damage; creatures within 5 feet of where it hits take 1 point of cold damage from the splash. Crafting this item is a DC 25 Craft (alchemy) check.
