# Troll styptic

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 100 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

_[[items/Mundane/Troll styptic|Troll styptic]]_ is used as a field treatment for wounds and bleeding, particularly where magical healing is not available. When applied directly to wounds, this jar of paste grants a living creature _[[universal monster rules/Fast Healing|fast healing]]_ 2 for 2d4 rounds. While active, the styptic also closes any open wounds the subject receives, _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Negating|negating]]_ any _[[universal monster rules/Bleed|bleed]]_ effects that would affect the target. This is a painful cure, and the target must succeed at a DC 15 Fortitude save or be _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_ for the styptic's duration.
