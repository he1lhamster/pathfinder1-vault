# Hollowed pommel

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Depending on the weapon, a hollow pommel may hold something as large as a _[[items/Mundane/Flask|flask]]_ or as small as a rolled piece of paper. Detecting a hollow pommel requires a DC 15 Perception check.
