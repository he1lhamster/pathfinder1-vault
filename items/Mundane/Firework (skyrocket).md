# Firework (skyrocket)

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 50 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

When lit, this footlong wooden tube begins to shake and emit a handful of white sparks, shedding light as a _[[items/Mundane/Torch|torch]]_. One round later it takes _[[universal monster rules/Flight|flight]]_, moving at a speed of 90 feet for 1d6 rounds before loudly exploding in a burst of light and sound, dealing 2d6 points of fire damage in a 10-foot-radius burst (Reflex DC 15 halves). Anyone who takes damage from the explosion is either _[[conditions/Blinded|blinded]]_ or _[[conditions/Deafened|deafened]]_ (50% chance of either) for 1 round. Crafting this item is a DC 25 Craft (alchemy) check.
