# War spirit pouch

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 50 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This tiny bundle of _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ herbs and bones supposedly attracts the attention of helpful battle-spirits. By crushing the pouch as a standard action, an _[[monsters/Orc|orc]]_ (or a creature from a suitably warlike culture) gains 1d4+1 temporary hit points. These temporary hit points go away after 10 minutes. A creature can only benefit from 1 spirit pouch at a time. Once used, the spirit pouch is destroyed.
