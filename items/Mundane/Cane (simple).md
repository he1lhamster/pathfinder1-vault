# Cane (simple)

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 2 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

A cane is a walking stick fashioned from wood. A cane sports either a metal cap or a curved top that's easy to grip. Simple canes are no more than a sturdy branch, while high quality canes are made from fine materials and bear intricate carvings or adornments.
