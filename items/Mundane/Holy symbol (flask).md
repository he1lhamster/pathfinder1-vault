# Holy symbol (flask)

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 10 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This version of a standard holy symbol is oversized and has a watertight compartment that can hold 1 pint (the same volume as a _[[items/Mundane/Flask|flask]]_ of _[[items/Mundane/Holy water|holy water]]_). Adventurers normally use the compartment to hold _holy water_ or alcohol. A holy symbol _flask_ is not meant for you to throw at enemies and is not fragile like a _flask_ or _[[items/Mundane/Vial|vial]]_. Like using _holy water_ on _[[universal monster rules/Incorporeal|incorporeal]]_ opponent, you unstopper the _flask_ and pour the contents onto an adjacent target. Detecting the compartment requires a successful DC 10 Perception check.
