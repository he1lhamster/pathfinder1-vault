# Dung grenade

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 100 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

The casing of this modified _[[items/Mundane/Fuse grenade|fuse grenade]]_ is partially filled with animal dung or guano. You can throw a _[[items/Mundane/Dung grenade|dung grenade]]_ as a splash weapon with a range increment of 10 feet. Any creature directly hit by a _dung grenade_ or in its splash radius takes 1d6 points of fire damage and is covered with the grenade's malodorous contents, becoming _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_ for 1d4 rounds. A target can attempt a DC 15 Reflex saving throw to halve the damage and the duration of the _sickened_ condition. Crafting a _dung grenade_ requires a successful DC 25 Craft (alchemy) check.
