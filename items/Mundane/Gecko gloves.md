# Gecko gloves

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 300 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

The palms and fingers of these leather gloves are covered in small hairlike bristles, resembling those found on the feet of geckos, that aid you when climbing. _[[items/Mundane/Gecko gloves|Gecko gloves]]_ grant you a +1 circumstance bonus on _[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_ checks. They also enable you to _climb_ with only one hand; doing so imposes a –5 penalty on your _Climb_ checks.
