# Chalkboard

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 1 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

A wooden frame approximately the same size as a large book surrounds this thinly sliced piece of polished black stone. Rubbing a damp cloth over the slate erases anything scribed with _[[items/Mundane/Chalk|chalk]]_ on its surface.
