# Minkaian ceremonial tea

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 900 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This tea is extremely rare and expensive, requiring several years to grow and prepare. A geisha _[[classes/Bard|bard]]_ (Pathfinder RPG Ultimate Magic 27) who uses this tea as part of her tea _[[spells/Ceremony|ceremony]]_ to inspire courage increases the bonuses granted by 1. A lotus geisha _bard_ (Pathfinder Player Companion: Dragon Empires Primer 22) who drinks this tea increases the bonus granted by her enrapturing performance ability by 1; this effect lasts for 24 hours.
