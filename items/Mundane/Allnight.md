# Allnight

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 75 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This treated wafer dissolves into a chalky paste when placed under the tongue and then gives the imbiber a _[[spells/Jolt|jolt]]_ of restless energy. It eliminates the effects of fatigue for the next 8 hours; when the drug's effect ends, the user is _[[conditions/Exhausted|exhausted]]_. _[[items/Mundane/Allnight|Allnight]]_ makes its users jittery and unable to focus; they suffer a –2 penalty on all skill checks until its effects wear off.
