# Waterproof lantern

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Meant to shelter its flame from both water and wind, this lantern treats severe winds as strong winds, windstorms as severe winds, and hurricanes as windstorms for the purpose of determining whether they extinguish its flame. It holds enough air to _[[universal monster rules/Burn|burn]]_ _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Underwater|underwater]]_ for 5 rounds before going dark.
