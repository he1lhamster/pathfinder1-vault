# Candle

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 0.01 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

A _[[items/Mundane/Candle|candle]]_ dimly illuminates a small area, increasing the light level in a 5-foot radius by one step (_[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_ becomes dim light and dim light becomes normal light). A _candle_ cannot increase the light level above normal light. A _candle_ burns for 1 hour.
