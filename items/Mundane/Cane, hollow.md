# Cane, hollow

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 1 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

A hollow cane has a false top that conceals a small compartment within the cane. The compartment is large enough to house a potion or _[[items/Mundane/Vial|vial]]_. Spotting the secret compartment of a hollow cane requires a successful DC 18 Perception check.
