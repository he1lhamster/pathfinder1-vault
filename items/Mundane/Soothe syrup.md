# Soothe syrup

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 25 gp **Weight:** 0.5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This sweet and wholesome-tasting blue liquid creates a sense of warmth and _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Comfort|comfort]]_. _[[items/Mundane/Soothe syrup|Soothe syrup]]_ coats your stomach and makes it much more difficult for you to succumb to queasiness. For 1 hour after drinking _soothe syrup_, you gain a +5 alchemical bonus on saving throws made to resist effects that would make you _[[conditions/Nauseated|nauseated]]_ or _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_.
