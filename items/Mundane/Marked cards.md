# Marked cards

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 1 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Whether bent, colored, or scratched, _[[items/Mundane/Marked cards|marked cards]]_ allow an informed user to know what's on the front of the card by viewing the mark on the back. Realizing that the _[[items/Mundane/Cards|cards]]_ are marked requires a DC 25 Perception check or DC 20 Profession (_[[npcs/Gambler|gambler]]_) check. The listed price is for a common paper deck.
