# Tobacco

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 1 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

These crushed and shredded leaves range in color from peppery red to black; users can either smoke or chew them. _[[items/Mundane/Tobacco|Tobacco]]_ users experience a certain level of calm and are more easily able to shrug off hunger pangs. _Tobacco_ is addictive (Fort DC 10 to resist), and long-term users suffer Constitution damage.
