# Shovel (common)

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 2 gp **Weight:** 8 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This tool lets you dig a pit at a rate of 2 cubic feet per minute. If a shovel is used in combat, treat it as a one-handed improvised weapon that deals bludgeoning damage equal to that of a _[[items/Weapon/Club|club]]_ of its size.

_[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Folding|Folding]]_ Shovel: A _folding_ shovel is a full-sized shovel with a sturdy sectional handle that folds into pieces no longer than the shovel blade, allowing it to fit in a smaller space such as a backpack. Converting the shovel from its folded state to usable (or vice versa) is 2 full-round actions.
