# Sparring gear

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 35 gp **Weight:** 15 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This array of thick, heavily lined pads, including a quilted helmet, rigid shin guards, and a wraparound rib _[[npcs/Guard|guard]]_, protects you during hand-to-hand _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Training|training]]_. _[[items/Mundane/Sparring gear|Sparring gear]]_ cannot be worn with armor, but grants DR 2/— against nonlethal damage. Fighting schools often customize their _sparring gear_ to feature prestigious colors or symbols.
