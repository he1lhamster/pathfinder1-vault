# False manacles

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 65 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

These manacles are nearly indistinguishable from standard manacles upon inspection (Perception DC 25). A wearer who knows the location of the secret catch can open them as a standard action; otherwise they act like masterwork manacles. Some appear to be of common quality, though _[[items/Mundane/False manacles|false manacles]]_ are always masterwork.
