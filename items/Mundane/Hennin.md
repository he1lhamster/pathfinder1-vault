# Hennin

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 55 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This high conical headpiece ends in a tip that usually has a piece of silk or similar diaphanous material dangling from it. Women of noble birth sometimes wear a _[[items/Mundane/Hennin|hennin]]_ to formal or social occasions as a signal of their eligibility, and give the silk part of the headpiece as a token to suitors they favor.
