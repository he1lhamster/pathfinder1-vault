# Stretcher

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 1 gp **Weight:** 10 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

A _[[items/Mundane/Stretcher|stretcher]]_ allows two people to share the burden of carrying one heavy object. You can also use it to drag a load you couldn't carry on your own. A _stretcher_ holds up to 300 pounds.
