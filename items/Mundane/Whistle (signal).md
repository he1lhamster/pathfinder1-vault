# Whistle (signal)

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 0.8 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

With a DC 5 Perform (wind instruments) check you can use a _[[items/Mundane/Whistle|whistle]]_ to signal the same sorts of situations as signal horns. A _whistle_'s piercing report can be clearly heard (Perception DC 0) up to a quarter-mile away. For each quarter-mile beyond, Perception checks to hear a _whistle_ suffer a –2 penalty.

Silent whistles that only animals and other creatures with _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Keen|keen]]_ hearing can hear also exist.
