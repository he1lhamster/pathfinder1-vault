# Linguist's codex (standard)

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 50 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This leather-bound reference tome contains several alphabets used in the various languages of Golarion, along with extensively cross-referenced and indexed words, phrases, and idioms. As long as you speak at least three languages, consulting a standard linguist's codex grants you a +2 circumstance bonus on Linguistics checks when communicating in a language you do not speak.

An expanded linguist's codex has far more phrases and _[[spells/Guidance|guidance]]_, including instructions to pronounce the sounds in a correct and pleasing manner. These codices provide a +2 circumstance bonus on Diplomacy checks when communicating in a language you do not speak.
