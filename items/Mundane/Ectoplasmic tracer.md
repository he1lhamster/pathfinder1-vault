# Ectoplasmic tracer

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 100 gp **Weight:** 7 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This small canister has a hand pump, allowing it to forcefully eject the semi-ethereal green concoction held within. Spraying a creature with _[[items/Mundane/Ectoplasmic tracer|ectoplasmic tracer]]_ is a ranged touch attack with a range increment of 10 feet. A single canister holds enough _ectoplasmic tracer_ to coat one _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ or smaller _[[universal monster rules/Incorporeal|incorporeal]]_ creature. When coated with _ectoplasmic tracer_, an _incorporeal_ creature leaves behind a trail of vibrant mucus that allows it to be tracked by mundane means. Calculate the Survival DC to track an _incorporeal_ creature covered in _ectoplasmic tracer_ as though you were following tracks left on soft ground. Once discharged, _ectoplasmic tracer_ dissolves after 1d4 days. _Ectoplasmic tracer_ has no effect when sprayed on a corporeal creature. Crafting this item requires a successful DC 20 Craft (alchemy) check.
