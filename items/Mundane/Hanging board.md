# Hanging board

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 75 gp **Weight:** 7 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This wooden board bears a number of narrow, thin holes large enough to hold by the fingertips. The board mounts to a wall, allowing climbers to practice their technique and improve their grip by hanging from the board. Using a _[[items/Mundane/Hanging board|hanging board]]_ to improve your grip requires 1 hour of practice and a DC 18 _[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_ check. If you succeed at the check, you gain a +10 circumstance bonus on _Climb_ checks to catch yourself while falling and a +5 bonus to catch a falling character while climbing. This bonus lasts for 24 hours.

### Sources

* Adventurer's Armory 2
