# Liquid breeze

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 30 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Developed in ancient times to ease travels across the harsh deserts of Kelesh, _[[items/Mundane/Liquid breeze|liquid breeze]]_ is a common ware in markets catering to travelers, adventurers, and explorers. This soothing liquid grants the benefits of _[[spells/Endure Elements|endure elements]]_ for 8 hours, but only against hot environments. This effect is nonmagical and can't be dispelled.

* Adventurer's Armory 2
