# Collapsible plank

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 0.4 gp **Weight:** 10 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Hinged in two places, collapsible planks can hold 250 pounds before _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Breaking|breaking]]_. The 10-foot-long plank folds down into a 3-foot-by-1-foot-by-6-inch bundle. _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Folding|Folding]]_ or unfolding it is a standard action.
