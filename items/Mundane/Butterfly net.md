# Butterfly net

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

One end of this 6-foot-long _[[items/Mundane/Pole|pole]]_ supports a wide metal hoop covered by a thin mesh _[[items/Mundane/Net|net]]_. You can use this to _[[spells/Sift|sift]]_ objects from material capable of passing through the _net_, such as sand or water. You can also use a _[[items/Mundane/Butterfly net|butterfly net]]_ to capture Fine or Diminutive creatures as if it were a _net_ (weapon), though you don't need to re-fold the _butterfly net_ if you miss, and you use the handle of the _butterfly net_ like you would the rope of a _net_ weapon.
