# Banner

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 10.5 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This is a _[[items/Mundane/Banner|banner]]_, _[[items/Mundane/Flag|flag]]_, or pennant. You tie it to a _[[items/Mundane/Pole|pole]]_, _[[items/Weapon/Lance|lance]]_, or polearm. Most are woven, dyed, or painted with a pattern or symbol, such as a _[[npcs/Knight|knight]]_'s crest or a country's _flag_. A simple _banner_ with one field color and a simple insignia such as a weapon or _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_ costs 1 gp. A _banner_ with two to four field colors and a complex insignia such as a _[[monsters/Lion|lion]]_ or dragon costs 5 gp. A detailed _banner_ with four or more field colors and a very complex insignia, such as a heraldic device with eight or more sections, costs 20 gp.
