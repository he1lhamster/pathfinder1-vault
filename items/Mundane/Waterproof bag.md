# Waterproof bag

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 0.5 gp **Weight:** 0.5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This leather _[[items/Mundane/Sack|sack]]_ sealed with tar or pitch keeps delicate items from being ruined by water. Items kept inside remain relatively dry, making the bag ideal for carrying maps, scrolls, spellbooks, and the like, although the bag is not _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Impervious|impervious]]_ and can only be completely immersed for 10 rounds before enough water seeps in to ruin such items.
