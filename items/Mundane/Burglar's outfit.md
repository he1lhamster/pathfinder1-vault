# Burglar's outfit

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This outfit consists of fitted pants, a shirt, a hooded _[[items/Mundane/Reversible cloak|reversible cloak]]_, soft leather boots, and a face _[[items/Mundane/Mask|mask]]_, all in dark or neutral colors. The outfit's few buttons and rivets are wrapped in dull, dark cloth to avoid jingling or _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Reflecting|reflecting]]_ light. A number of loops and shallow pockets are also worked into the outfit, providing ample spaces for stashing small tools or weapons.
