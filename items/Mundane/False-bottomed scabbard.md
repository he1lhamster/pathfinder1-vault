# False-bottomed scabbard

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 45 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

The space at the bottom of this sword scabbard is just large enough to hold a potion _[[items/Mundane/Flask|flask]]_. Some are accessed from the outside; others open on the inside with a secret catch at the top end. Finding the secret compartment requires a DC 25 Perception check, or DC 20 if you can compare the sword's length to that of the scabbard.
