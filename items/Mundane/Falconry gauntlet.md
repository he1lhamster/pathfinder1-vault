# Falconry gauntlet

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 10 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This _[[items/Weapon/Gauntlet|gauntlet]]_, made from layers of thick leather, is worn on a _[[npcs/Falconer|falconer]]_'s off hand to give a falcon a place to stand before and after the hunt. The _gauntlet_ also has a tassel and a ring to _[[universal monster rules/Attach|attach]]_ to a falcon's tether.
