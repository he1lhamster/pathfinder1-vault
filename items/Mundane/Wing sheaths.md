# Wing sheaths

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 10 gp **Weight:** 10 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

_[[items/Mundane/Wing sheaths|Wing sheaths]]_ are protective coverings created from thick fabric or canvas. The sheaths are designed to tightly cover a pair of wings to keep them hidden beneath a cloak or loose-fitting clothing. Noticing hidden wings bound within _wing sheaths_ requires a successful DC 20 Perception check. Donning or removing _wing sheaths_ requires 1 minute, or a full-round action with assistance. _Wing sheaths_ can be burst with a successful DC 22 Strength check.
