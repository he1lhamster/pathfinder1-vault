# Blackfire clay

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 20 gp **Weight:** 10 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This pliant black _[[items/Mundane/Clay|clay]]_ is always pleasantly warm to the touch. Working _[[items/Mundane/Blackfire clay|blackfire clay]]_ in your hands for a full round causes the _clay_ to grow much warmer, granting you a +4 alchemical bonus on saving throws made to resist cold weather. You can combine five blocks of _clay_ over the course of a minute to produce a source of warmth equivalent to a small campfire, allowing you to _[[universal monster rules/Heat|heat]]_ a campsite and cook food.

_Blackfire clay_ only produces _heat_, never light, smoke, or odor. The _clay_ emits warmth for 1 hour before hardening into an unusable lump. A newly created brick of _blackfire clay_ is composed of 10 blocks.
