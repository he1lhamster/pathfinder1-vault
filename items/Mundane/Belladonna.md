# Belladonna

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 2 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Also known as _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Deadly|deadly]]_ nightshade, this plant, with its distinctive greenish-purple leaves and dull black berries, has served as a cosmetic and medicine for longer than memory despite its toxicity. It can also induce vivid but usually unpleasant hallucinations, particularly ones dealing with _[[universal monster rules/Flight|flight]]_. Herbalists and others trained to use it safely believe it has numerous applications, including pain relief.
