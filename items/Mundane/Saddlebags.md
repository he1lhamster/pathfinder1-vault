# Saddlebags

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 4 gp **Weight:** 8 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

These sturdy, weatherproof bags are draped over a saddle to add extra carrying capacity. Each side of a saddlebag can typically carry 20 pound of items that can fit in the bags. _[[items/Mundane/Saddlebags|Saddlebags]]_ do not increase the amount of weight a _[[spells/Mount|mount]]_ can carry; they merely give riders a place to stow their equipment.
