# Pesh

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 20 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

The milk of this vibrantly green cactus, when mixed with resins and other ingredients, congeals into _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sticky|sticky]]_, black chunks with an exceedingly sour taste. Though _[[items/Mundane/Pesh|pesh]]_ comes in several different varieties, refined _pesh_ is both the most _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Potent|potent]]_ and expensive type. Taking refined _pesh_ gives a person 15 temporary hit points for 1 hour, a +2 morale bonus on saves versus _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_ effects for 1 day, and a –4 penalty to Dexterity and Wisdom for 1 day. _Pesh_ is addictive (Fortitude DC 10 to resist), and long-term users suffer Dexterity and Wisdom damage.
