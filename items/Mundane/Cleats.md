# Cleats

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

_[[items/Mundane/Cleats|Cleats]]_ reduce the penalty for walking over _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Slick|slick]]_ surfaces by 50%. For example, walking across ice normally costs 2 squares of movement (1 square plus a 1 square penalty) for every square of movement, but with _cleats_ it costs only 1.5 squares for every square. _Cleats_ cause damage to any type of finished flooring. They may supplement another outfit.
