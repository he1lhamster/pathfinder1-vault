# Mithral horseshoes

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 550 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

These four thin horseshoes _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_ a _[[monsters/Horse|horse]]_'s feet as well as steel, allowing it to gallop with confidence, but _[[items/Mundane/Mithral horseshoes|mithral horseshoes]]_ are far lighter than steel. Horses shod with _mithral horseshoes_ treat their hoof attacks as if they were silver for the purposes of overcoming _[[universal monster rules/Damage Reduction|damage reduction]]_.
