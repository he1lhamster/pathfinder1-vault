# Whistle

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 0.8 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Also known as a _[[items/Mundane/Signal whistle|signal whistle]]_, a _[[items/Mundane/Whistle|whistle]]_'s piercing report can be clearly heard (DC 0) up to half a mile away. With a DC 5 Perform (wind instruments) check you can use it to signal the same sorts of situations as signal horns (see page 8). For each additional quarter mile, Perception checks to hear a _whistle_ suffer a –2 penalty.
