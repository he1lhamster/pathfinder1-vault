# Firework (paper candle)

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 1 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This finger-sized explosive detonates noisily 1 round after lighting. Anyone in the same square as a paper _[[items/Mundane/Candle|candle]]_ when it explodes must make a DC 15 Fortitude save or be _[[conditions/Dazzled|dazzled]]_ for 1d4 rounds. Crafting this item is a DC 20 Craft (alchemy) check.
