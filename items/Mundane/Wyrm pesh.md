# Wyrm pesh

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 500 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This rare and expensive variant of standard Garundi _[[items/Mundane/Pesh|pesh]]_ is produced by introducing a _[[items/Mundane/Vial|vial]]_ of _[[items/Mundane/Dragon's blood|dragon's blood]]_ into a dose of _pesh_ during the refining process, and has a noticeable reddish tint. While the drug provides a more _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Potent|potent]]_ high and fewer immediate penalties, it is highly addictive, and unless a user has a steady supply of _dragon's blood_, the cost to her wellbeing can be severe. For more information on drugs and addictions, see page 236 of the Pathfinder RPG GameMastery Guide.

##### _[[items/Mundane/Wyrm pesh|Wyrm Pesh]]_

**Type** ingested or inhaled; **Addiction** severe, Fortitude DC 24

**Price** 500 gp

**Effect** 1 hour; 25 temporary hit points

**Effect** 1 day; +2 alchemical bonus on saves against sleep and _[[universal monster rules/Paralysis|paralysis]]_ effects

**Damage** 1d2 Wis
