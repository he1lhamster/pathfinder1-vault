# Sunrod

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 2 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This 1-foot-long, gold-tipped iron rod glows brightly when struck (a standard action). It sheds normal light in a 30-foot radius and increases the light level by one step for an additional 30 feet beyond that area (_[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_ becomes dim light and dim light becomes normal light). A _[[items/Mundane/Sunrod|sunrod]]_ does not increase the light level in normal light or bright light. It glows for 6 hours, after which the gold tip is burned out and worthless.
