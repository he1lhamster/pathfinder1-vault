# Balancing pole

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 0.8 gp **Weight:** 12 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This flexible _[[items/Mundane/Pole|pole]]_ can be anywhere from 15 to 30 feet in length. When used properly, it helps you keep your balance when crossing narrow walkways. Using a _[[items/Mundane/Balancing pole|balancing pole]]_ grants you a +1 circumstance bonus on Acrobatics checks made to traverse a narrow surface.
