# Magnifying glass

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 100 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This simple lens allows a closer look at small objects. It is also useful as a substitute for a _[[items/Mundane/Flint and steel|flint and steel]]_ when starting fires. Lighting a fire with a _[[items/Mundane/Magnifying glass|magnifying glass]]_ requires bright light, such as sunlight to focus, tinder to ignite, and at least a fullround action. A _magnifying glass_ grants a +2 circumstance bonus on Appraise checks involving any item that is small or highly detailed.
