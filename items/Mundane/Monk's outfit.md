# Monk's outfit

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This simple outfit includes sandals, loose breeches, and a loose shirt, and is bound together with sashes. The outfit is designed to give you maximum _[[feats/Mobility|mobility]]_, and it's made of high-quality fabric. You can conceal small weapons in pockets hidden in the folds, and the sashes are strong enough to serve as short ropes.
