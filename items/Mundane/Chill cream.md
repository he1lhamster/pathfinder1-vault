# Chill cream

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 15 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sticky|sticky]]_ light blue paste grows pleasantly cool to the touch whenever it is exposed to light. Spreading a dose on your face and hands takes a full-round action and grants you a +2 alchemical bonus on saves to resist hot weather. In areas of bright light this bonus increases to +4, while in areas of dim light or _[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_ it is negated. A dose of _[[items/Mundane/Chill cream|chill cream]]_ lasts for 1 hour after being applied. A newly crafted _[[items/Mundane/Bottle|bottle]]_ of _chill cream_ holds 5 doses.
