# Bedroll

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 0.1 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This consists of two woolen sheets sewn together along the bottom and one side to create a bag for sleeping in. Some have cloth straps along the open side so the _[[items/Mundane/Bedroll|bedroll]]_ can be tied closed while you are sleeping. It can be rolled and tied into a tight coil for storage or transport. Most people use a _[[items/Mundane/Blanket|blanket]]_ with the _bedroll_ to stay warm or provide a ground cushion.
