# Hourglass (1 minute)

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 20 gp **Weight:** 0.5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

The standard _[[items/Mundane/Hourglass|hourglass]]_ takes 1 hour to empty the sand from the upper chamber; smaller ones that mark time more precisely are also readily available, in 1-minute and 6-second sizes. Hourglasses tracking more than an hour are rare.
