# Horse whip

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

A short _[[items/Weapon/Whip|whip]]_ with a leather loop at the end, this _whip_ can be felt through a _[[monsters/Horse|horse]]_'s armor but does not deal damage to the _horse_. A rider who uses a _[[items/Mundane/Horse whip|horse whip]]_ while using the Ride skill to spur her _[[spells/Mount|mount]]_ does not deal damage to the _mount_, as opposed to the 1d3 points of damage normally dealt by this action. Using a _horse whip_ does not negate the fatigue that spurring a _mount_ can cause.
