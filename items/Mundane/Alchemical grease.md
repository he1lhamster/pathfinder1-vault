# Alchemical grease

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Each pot of this _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Slick|slick]]_ black goo has sufficient contents to cover one _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ creature or two Small ones. If you coat yourself in _[[items/Mundane/Alchemical grease|alchemical grease]]_, you gain a +5 alchemical bonus on Escape Artist checks, on combat maneuver checks made to escape a grapple, and to your CMD to avoid being _[[conditions/Grappled|grappled]]_; this lasts 4 hours or until you wash it off.
