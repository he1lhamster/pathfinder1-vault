# Spear-thrower

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 1 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This is little more than a handle with a cup, loop, or spur to hold the butt of a _[[items/Weapon/Dart|dart]]_, _[[items/Weapon/Javelin|javelin]]_, or _[[items/Weapon/Shortspear|shortspear]]_. Using a _[[items/Mundane/Spear-thrower|spear-thrower]]_ to throw such a weapon doubles the projectile's range increment. Setting a weapon into a _spear-thrower_ is a move action; by taking the _[[feats/Rapid Reload|Rapid Reload]]_ (_spear-thrower_) feat, you reduce this to a free action.
