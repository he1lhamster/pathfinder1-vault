# Poh armor

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 500 gp **Weight:** 65 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Named after a ferocious unicorn-tiger hybrid, this suit of _[[items/Armor/Scale mail|scale mail]]_ barding bears a _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Vicious|vicious]]_ horn on the forehead plate and metal _[[feats/Tiger Claws|tiger claws]]_ protruding from the poitrel. The armor is heavily reinforced to protect a _[[monsters/Horse|horse]]_'s chest and neck. A _horse_ wearing _[[items/Mundane/Poh armor|poh armor]]_ gains a gore attack as a secondary natural attack that deals 1d8 points of damage. Additionally, a _horse_ wearing _poh armor_ deals 2d6 points of damage to any foe it knocks _[[conditions/Prone|prone]]_ when using an overrun combat maneuver. _Poh armor_ otherwise functions like barding.

The price and weight listed is for _poh armor_ for a Large _[[spells/Mount|mount]]_. _Poh armor_ for a _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ _mount_ costs 250 gp and weighs 35 pounds. _Poh armor_ slows a _mount_ with a base speed of 40 feet to 30 feet, 50 feet to 35 feet, or 60 feet to 40 feet.
