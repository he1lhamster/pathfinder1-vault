# Celestial censer

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 50 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This _[[feats/Blessed|blessed]]_ _[[items/Mundane/Thurible|thurible]]_ holds up to 10 pieces of _[[items/Mundane/Incense|incense]]_, and burns at a rate of 1 stick per hour. If a _[[items/Mundane/Smokestick|smokestick]]_ is added to the _incense_ in the _[[items/Mundane/Censer|censer]]_ while it burns, creatures with the evil subtype are _[[conditions/Dazzled|dazzled]]_ for as long as they remain within the area of the resulting smoke.
