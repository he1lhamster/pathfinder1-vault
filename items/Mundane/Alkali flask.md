# Alkali flask

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 15 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This _[[items/Mundane/Flask|flask]]_ of caustic liquid reacts with an ooze's natural acids. You can throw an _[[items/Mundane/Alkali flask|alkali flask]]_ as a splash weapon with a range increment of 10 feet. Against non-ooze creatures, an _alkali flask_ functions as a normal _flask_ of acid. Against oozes and other acid-based creatures, the _alkali flask_ inflicts double damage. Crafting this item is a DC 20 Craft (alchemy) check.
