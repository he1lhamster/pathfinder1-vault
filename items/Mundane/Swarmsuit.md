# Swarmsuit

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 20 gp **Weight:** 10 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

These heavy and overlapping layers of clothing, coupled with a wide _[[items/Mundane/Hat|hat]]_ outfitted with a dense, veil-like netting around its brim, makes it all but impossible for Diminutive and Fine creatures to make physical contact with your body. Wearing a _[[items/Mundane/Swarmsuit|swarmsuit]]_ cuts your speed in half, but gives you DR 10/— against swarms of Fine creatures and DR 5/— against swarms of Diminutive creatures.
