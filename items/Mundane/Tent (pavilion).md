# Tent (pavilion)

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 100 gp **Weight:** 50 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Tents come in a variety of sizes and accommodate between one and 10 people. A small _[[items/Mundane/Tent|tent]]_ holds one _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ creature and takes 20 minutes to assemble, a _medium_ _tent_ holds two creatures and takes 30 minutes, a large _tent_ holds four creatures and takes 45 minutes, and a pavilion holds 10 creatures and takes 90 minutes (two Small creatures count as a _Medium_ creature, and one Large creature counts as two _Medium_ creatures). Pavilion tents are large enough to accommodate a small fire in the center. Packing up a _tent_ takes half as long as assembling it.
