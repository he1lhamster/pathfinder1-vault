# Nautical chart

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 25 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Nautical charts map waterways, showing the depth of water and height of coastlines, as well as currents, harbors, navigational hazards, reefs, and tides. A _[[items/Mundane/Nautical chart|nautical chart]]_ grants a +4 circumstance bonus on Profession (sailor) checks to navigate in the area detailed by the chart.
