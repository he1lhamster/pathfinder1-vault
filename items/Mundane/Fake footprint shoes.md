# Fake footprint shoes

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

These oversized shoes are worn over normal footwear. They create footprints or bootprints that look like those left by another creature. Each pair creates a specific type of footprint, such as _[[monsters/Tiger|tiger]]_, _[[monsters/Orc|orc]]_, _[[monsters/Goblin|goblin]]_, or _[[monsters/Ogre|ogre]]_. When walking with these shoes, you may make a Survival check; the check result is the Survival DC for anyone trying to determine whether the tracks are what they appear to be.
