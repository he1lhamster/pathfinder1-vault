# Bottled lightning

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 40 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Electricity crackles along a metal filament inside this small glass _[[items/Mundane/Bottle|bottle]]_. You can open the _bottle_ as a standard action, unleashing a small bolt of lightning toward an enemy within 20 feet of you. This is a ranged touch attack that deals 1d8 points of electricity damage. Any creature in a line between you and the target (including the target) takes 1 point of sonic damage from the terrific clap of thunder the bolt generates (Reflex DC 15 negates). Crafting _[[items/Mundane/Bottled lightning|bottled lightning]]_ is a DC 25 Craft (alchemy) check.
