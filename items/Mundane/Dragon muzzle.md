# Dragon muzzle

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 20 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This large leather muzzle is reinforced with steel chains, and is specifically shaped for a dragon's snout. The muzzle is also treated with one type of _[[items/Mundane/Elemental proofing|elemental proofing]]_ (see below) to prevent it from being destroyed by the _[[spells/Dragon's Breath|dragon's breath]]_ weapon. The muzzle has hardness 10, 40 hp, and a break DC of 30. A _[[items/Mundane/Dragon muzzle|dragon muzzle]]_ can be fitted on a dragon using the rules to tie up a _[[conditions/Pinned|pinned]]_ creature. A standard _dragon muzzle_ can be resized to fit dragons from size Tiny to Large. For larger versions, the weight increases by a factor of 4 and the price doubles for each size increase.
