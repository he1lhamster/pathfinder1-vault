# Caltrops, vicious

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 50 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

These specially modified _[[items/Mundane/Caltrops|caltrops]]_ have clusters of needle-sharp spines that are slightly longer and more brittle than those on normal _caltrops_. Like standard _caltrops_, a 2-pound bag of _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Vicious|vicious]]_ _caltrops_ covers a 5-foot-square area. _Vicious_ _caltrops_ work just like normal _caltrops_, save that they have an attack bonus of +4, do 1d2 points of damage, and require a DC 20 _[[spells/Heal|Heal]]_ check to fully treat.
