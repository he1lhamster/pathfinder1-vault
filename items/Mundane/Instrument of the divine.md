# Instrument of the divine

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 200 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This masterwork musical instrument may be of any type (lyre, harp, drum, and so on). As a focus, it transforms faith into powerful music. Once activated, a member of the _[[classes/Cleric|cleric]]_'s faith can draw upon the power in the instrument to add a _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ (if positive energy) or profane (if negative energy) bonus on his next Perform check; this bonus is equal to the number of dice of the _cleric_'s channel energy ability. The bonus persists for the entirety of the performance.
