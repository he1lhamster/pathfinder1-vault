# Alchemist's kindness

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 1 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Favored by young rakes and other well-to-do inebriates, this crystalline _[[items/Mundane/Powder|powder]]_ resembles salt. Mixed with water, it makes a fizzing cocktail that eliminates the effects of a hangover within 10 minutes of drinking it.
