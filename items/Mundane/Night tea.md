# Night tea

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 0.1 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This frothy tea made from the bone-white, triangular leaves of the _[[items/Mundane/Night tea|night tea]]_ plant renders a humanoid female sterile if drunk every day. In high doses, it may be able to end a pregnancy.
