# Compact spellbook

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 50 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

The need to be able to record and travel with dozens or even hundreds of spells often forces elven wizards to seek lighter spellbooks. Compact spellbooks hold only 70 pages of spells, but they weigh significantly less than an ordinary _[[items/Mundane/Spellbook|spellbook]]_.
