# Elemental breath

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 80 gp **Weight:** 0.5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This _[[items/Mundane/Vial|vial]]_ at first seems to be empty, but when it's opened, a rush of wind issues forth from within. When _[[items/Mundane/Elemental breath|elemental breath]]_ is inhaled (as drinking a potion) by an air-breathing creature, that creature does not need to breath air for 10 rounds. A _vial_ of _elemental breath_ must be inhaled on the round it was opened to gain this effect. If it is not, the item is wasted.
