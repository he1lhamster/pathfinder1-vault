# Portable altar

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 250 gp **Weight:** 40 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

A _[[items/Mundane/Portable altar|portable altar]]_ is typically made of wood and is ornately carved and decorated with the iconography of a specific religion. The top of the altar lifts up, and items such as candles, _[[items/Mundane/Incense|incense]]_, and oil can be stored within. These altars are modest, but give traveling priests a center from which to say their daily prayers, perform minor rituals, or preach to an assembled crowd.
