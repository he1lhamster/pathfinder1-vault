# Patchwork cloak

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 0.5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

These cloaks are popular among traveling bards, and each _[[items/Mundane/Patchwork cloak|patchwork cloak]]_ is unique. The patches represent where the traveler has been, and often the performer uses them to recall specific stories from his repertoire. When a _[[classes/Bard|bard]]_ retires or dies, he often bequeaths his cloak to a young entertainer he mentored or admired.
