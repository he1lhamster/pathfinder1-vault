# Stove can

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 10 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This little metal container holds a waxy fuel that creates enough _[[universal monster rules/Heat|heat]]_ to cook with but only as much light as a _[[items/Mundane/Candle|candle]]_. To snuff the flame, close the metal lid on the container; it cools enough that it can be carried in a few minutes. Each lasts 5 hours (enough to cook about 10 meals).
