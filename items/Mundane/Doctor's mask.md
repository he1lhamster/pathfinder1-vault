# Doctor's mask

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 50 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This protective _[[items/Mundane/Mask|mask]]_ gives you a +1 circumstance bonus on Fortitude saves made against airborne toxins and scent-based effects. In Korvosa, it is a minor crime to wear a _[[items/Mundane/Doctor's mask|doctor's mask]]_ in public if you are not a _[[npcs/Healer|healer]]_ or physician.
