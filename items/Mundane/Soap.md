# Soap

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 0.01 gp **Weight:** 0.5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Most _[[items/Mundane/Soap|soap]]_ is made from animal fat and lye derived from ashes. You can use this thick block of _soap_ to scrub clothes, pots, linens, adventurers, or anything else that might be dirty. A bar of _soap_ has approximately 50 uses.
