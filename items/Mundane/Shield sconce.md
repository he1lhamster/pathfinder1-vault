# Shield sconce

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 1 gp **Weight:** 0.5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This metal frame holds a _[[items/Mundane/Torch|torch]]_ and is designed to be strapped to the front of a light, heavy, or _[[items/Shield/Tower shield|tower shield]]_, allowing you to carry a _torch_ without giving up your _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_ or occupying your weapon hand.
