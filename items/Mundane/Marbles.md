# Marbles

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 0.1 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

_[[items/Mundane/Marbles|Marbles]]_ can be used to _[[spells/Slow|slow]]_ opponents. One 2-pound bag of _marbles_ covers an area 5 feet square. A creature entering a square with _marbles_ scattered on it must succeed at a DC 10 Reflex save or fall _[[conditions/Prone|prone]]_ (the creature's stability bonus to _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_ applies to this save). Any creature moving at half speed or slower can pick its way through a square of _marbles_ with no trouble.
