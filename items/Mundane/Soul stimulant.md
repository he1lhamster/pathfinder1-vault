# Soul stimulant

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 300 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This soothing elixir was created to counter the energy-draining effects of vampires, wights, and similar horrible creatures. If you have a negative level (whether temporary or permanent), you can drink a dose of _[[items/Mundane/Soul stimulant|soul stimulant]]_, _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Negating|negating]]_ the negative level's penalty for 12 hours. You can only benefit from 1 dose of _soul stimulant_ at a time, though you can continue to take a dose every 12 hours to stave off the negative level's effects.
