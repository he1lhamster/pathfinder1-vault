# Flask thrower

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 25 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This staff has a cradle at the end, designed to hold alchemical weapons such as acid. A _[[items/Mundane/Flask thrower|flask thrower]]_ significantly extends the range of thrown substances that deal splash damage, such as acid, _[[items/Mundane/Alchemist's fire|alchemist's fire]]_, or _[[items/Mundane/Holy water|holy water]]_, as well as that of tools such as tanglefoot bags, thunderstones, or _[[items/Mundane/Caltrops|caltrops]]_.
