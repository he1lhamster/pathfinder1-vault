# Heated gloves

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 2 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

These thick, fur-lined gloves have several internal pockets containing segmented packets of alchemical reagents that produce _[[universal monster rules/Heat|heat]]_ for 4 hours when _[[conditions/Broken|broken]]_ and mixed. The wearer can massage the gloves to activate these _heat_ packets as a full-round action. While the _heat_ packets last, the wearer ignores the Strength and Dexterity penalties from the _[[conditions/Fatigued|fatigued]]_ condition when performing _[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_, Disable Device, and Sleight of Hand checks. Replacement _heat_ packets cost 1 gp, and the _heat_ packets in the gloves can be replaced with 1 minute of uninterrupted work.
