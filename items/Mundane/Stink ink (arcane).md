# Stink ink (arcane)

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 0 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Most common among _[[monsters/Ratfolk|ratfolk]]_ alchemists, arcane stink _[[items/Mundane/Ink|ink]]_ is used to inscribe formulae or spells into formula books and spellbooks. Like normal stink _ink_, the arcane version can only be read by creatures with the _[[universal monster rules/Scent|scent]]_ ability (although _[[spells/Read Magic|read magic]]_ works normally on spells and arcane formulae inscribed with stink _ink_). Using arcane stink _ink_ to inscribe a spell or formula into a book costs +10% of the normal amount .
