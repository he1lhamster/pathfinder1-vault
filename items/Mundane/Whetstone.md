# Whetstone

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 0.02 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

A _[[items/Mundane/Whetstone|whetstone]]_ allows you to sharpen a blade by sliding it against the stone at a precise angle. Honing a blade with a _whetstone_ requires about 15 minutes of work and grants the weapon a +1 bonus on your damage roll the first time you hit with it. This only works on nonmagical blades.
