# Ambrosia (vial)

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 100 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Upon _[[feats/Consumption|consumption]]_, this heavenly elixir, brewed from _[[items/Mundane/Holy water|holy water]]_ and _[[feats/Blessed|blessed]]_ herbs, grants a +2 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ bonus on saving throws against negative energy, _[[universal monster rules/Energy Drain|energy drain]]_, and death effects for 1 hour, including saves to remove negative levels. _[[items/Wondrous Item/Ambrosia|Ambrosia]]_ affects undead and evil outsiders as _holy water_.
