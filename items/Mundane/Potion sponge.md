# Potion sponge

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 2 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This egg-sized sponge is covered in a layer of waterproof edible wax, designed to absorb 1 dose of a potion. Chewing a _[[items/Mundane/Potion sponge|potion sponge]]_ and swallowing its liquid contents is a full-round action. A creature of at least Large size can swallow the sponge in its entirely; other creatures must spit out the sponge once it's depleted (a free action). Unlike a potion that is drunk from a _[[items/Mundane/Vial|vial]]_, a _potion sponge_ can be used _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Underwater|underwater]]_. A potion can be poured from a _vial_ into a sponge potion (or squeezed from a sponge into a _vial_) as a full-round action. The _potion sponge_ is immune to attacks that specifically target crystal, glass, ceramic, or porcelain, such as _[[spells/Shatter|shatter]]_. It otherwise works like a potion _vial_.
