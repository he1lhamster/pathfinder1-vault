# Twitch tonic

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 45 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This thick syrup is extracted from _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Bitter|bitter]]_ herbs and enhanced by alchemy to ward against sleep, _[[universal monster rules/Paralysis|paralysis]]_, and the _[[conditions/Staggered|staggered]]_ condition. If you drink _[[items/Mundane/Twitch tonic|twitch tonic]]_, you gain a +2 alchemical bonus on saving throws made against these effects for the next hour. If you drink _twitch tonic_ while suffering from any of these effects, you may immediately roll another saving throw against the effect (with the +2 bonus); you may gain this particular benefit only once per day. You can administer _twitch tonic_ to an _[[conditions/Unconscious|unconscious]]_ or _[[conditions/Paralyzed|paralyzed]]_ creature as a full-round action, similar to administering a potion.
