# Darkflare

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 1 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This alchemical _[[spells/Flare|flare]]_ explodes in black sparks 1 round after lighting. Any creature with _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ in the same square as a _[[items/Mundane/Darkflare|darkflare]]_ when it explodes must succeed at a DC 15 Fortitude save or lose its _darkvision_ for 1d10 rounds. Affected creatures can still use other _[[spells/Vision|vision]]_ (such as normal _vision_ and _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_) with no penalty.
