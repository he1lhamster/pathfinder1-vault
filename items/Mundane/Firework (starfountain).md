# Firework (starfountain)

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 500 gp **Weight:** 100 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This tree stump-sized bundle of tubes immediately begins to emit arcs of multi-colored sparks when lit. Starting 1d6 rounds after lighting, it loudly releases brightly colored streaks of tiny _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Flaming|flaming]]_ particles for 4 rounds. Outdoors, these particles soar far up into the sky before dispersing. Indoors or in a closed area, the ricocheting fireworks deal 1d6 points of fire damage each round in a 20-foot-radius spread (Reflex DC 15 halves). Creatures who fail their save are _[[conditions/Blinded|blinded]]_ for 1d4 rounds and _[[conditions/Deafened|deafened]]_ for 1 hour. Crafting this item is a DC 25 Craft (alchemy) check.
