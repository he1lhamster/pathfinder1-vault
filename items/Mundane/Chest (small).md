# Chest (small)

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 2 gp **Weight:** 25 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

The common wooden chest comes in several sizes, including small (2 cubic feet, 1 hit point, break DC 17), _[[classes/Medium|medium]]_ (4 cubic feet, 15 hp, break DC 23), large (6 cubic feet, 30 hit points, break DC 29), and huge (8 cubic feet, 50 hit points, break DC 35). Most include a simple inset lock.
