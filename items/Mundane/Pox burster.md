# Pox burster

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 50 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

A _[[items/Mundane/Pox burster|pox burster]]_ is an alchemically preserved animal bladder or gourd that has been filled with _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Toxic|toxic]]_, rotting materials. You can throw a _pox burster_ as a splash weapon. Treat this attack as a ranged touch attack with a range increment of 10 feet. A direct hit forces a target to immediately make a DC 13 Fortitude save or contract _[[diseases/Filth Fever|filth fever]]_. Every space adjacent to the target square of the _pox burster_ is covered in disease-causing filth. For the next minute, any creature that is injured while in one of these spaces must also make a DC 9 Fortitude save or contract _filth fever_.
