# Elemental flux (flask)

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 20 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This _[[items/Mundane/Flask|flask]]_ of alchemical _[[items/Mundane/Powder|powder]]_ reacts to the elemental power in _[[monsters/Suli|suli]]_ blood. Adding at least 1 hit point worth of _suli_ blood to it creates an alchemical splash weapon. The splash weapon works like a _flask_ of acid, except the damage is a random energy type (acid, cold, electricity, or fire). A DC 10 Craft (alchemy) check identifies what energy type the activated _flask_ has. The activated flux retains its power for 24 hours before becoming inert material. The Craft (alchemy) DC to create this is 20.
