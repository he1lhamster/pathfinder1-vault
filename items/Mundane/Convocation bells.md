# Convocation bells

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 100 gp **Weight:** 10 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This set of simple brass bells is held in a wheeled stand that allows _[[items/Mundane/Bell|bell]]_ ringers to easily move the _[[feats/Ensemble|ensemble]]_. The bells are carefully constructed and ordered to enable ringers to perform the melodies of songs or hymns of a particular religion.
