# Sunblock kohl

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 10 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Made from ground antimony, this sooty cosmetic is used to line the eyes. It grants the wearer a +2 circumstance bonus on saves against light-based effects that would dazzle or blind him for 8 hours. A container of kohl holds 10 applications.
