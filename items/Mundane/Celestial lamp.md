# Celestial lamp

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 300 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This polished lantern contains a _[[spells/Continual Flame|continual flame]]_ and sheds light as a common _[[items/Mundane/Lamp|lamp]]_. If its font of consecrated crystal and metalwork is filled with _[[items/Mundane/Holy water|holy water]]_, the _lamp_'s light is sanctified for 24 hours, adding a +1 bonus to the save DC of channeled positive energy or energy channeled to _[[spells/Harm|harm]]_ evil outsiders within a 30-foot radius.
