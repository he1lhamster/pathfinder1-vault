# Wolfsbane

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 0.5 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

The _[[spells/Root|root]]_ of this tall plant with blue flowers is _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Toxic|toxic]]_ (see the page 560 of the Pathfinder RPG Core Rulebook), but herbalists use it in low doses to reduce pain and regulate the heart. Folklore says it can help a victim of lycanthropy throw off the curse.
