# Sparkle smoke

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 25 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

When inhaled, this _[[items/Mundane/Incense|incense]]_ causes creatures to see dazzling auras around light sources, drawing their attention to such illumination as the moon or stars and away from other nearby creatures. Any creature inhaling _[[items/Mundane/Sparkle smoke|sparkle smoke]]_ that fails a DC 15 Fortitude saving throw takes a –4 penalty on Perception checks to notice creatures smaller than itself in areas of at least dim light for 1 hour. Crafting a dose of _sparkle smoke_ requires a successful DC 15 Craft (alchemy) check.
