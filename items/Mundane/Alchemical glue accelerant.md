# Alchemical glue accelerant

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 25 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This tiny dropper contains a liquid that speeds up the cure time of _[[items/Mundane/Alchemical glue|alchemical glue]]_. Applying the accelerant is a standard action. Accelerated glue is tacky after 1 round and fully cured in 1 minute. The dropper contains 20 doses; each dose is sufficient to cure one small application of _alchemical glue_. The entire dropper must be used to cure a full square foot of glue.
