# Signal horn

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 1 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Sounding a horn requires a DC 10 Perform (wind instruments) check and can convey concepts such as "Attack!", "Help!", "Advance!", "Retreat!", "Fire!", and "_[[spells/Alarm|Alarm]]_!" The report of a _[[items/Mundane/Signal horn|signal horn]]_ can be clearly heard (Perception DC 0) up to a half-mile distant. For each quarter-mile beyond, Perception checks to hear the horn take a –1 penalty.
