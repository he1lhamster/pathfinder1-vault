# Meditation tea

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 30 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

Drinking this cloudy tea has a soothing effect that clarifies your thoughts. For 10 minutes after drinking the tea, you gain a +2 alchemical bonus against mind-affecting effects. If you drink _[[items/Mundane/Meditation tea|meditation tea]]_ while suffering from a mind-affecting affect, you may immediately roll another saving throw (with the +2 bonus) against the effect; you may gain this particular benefit only once per day.
