# Flayleaf

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 20 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

These narrow, rust-colored leaves produce a mildly _[[spells/Hallucinogenic Smoke|hallucinogenic smoke]]_ that also serves as powerful sedative. Users are immune to pain for 4 hours after smoking _[[items/Mundane/Flayleaf|flayleaf]]_, but during this time they take a –5 penalty on saves against mind-altering effects.
