# Sledge

### Properties

**Type:** Mundane **Price:** 1 gp **Weight:** 10 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

### Description

This heavy, two-handed _[[items/Mundane/Hammer|hammer]]_ is normally used in carpentry and other construction tasks, and is also useful for driving in _[[items/Mundane/Tent|tent]]_ stakes to help secure campsites in areas with strong winds. If used in combat, treat it as an improvised _[[items/Weapon/Earth breaker|earth breaker]]_ (see page 27).
