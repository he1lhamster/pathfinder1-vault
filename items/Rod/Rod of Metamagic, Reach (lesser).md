# Rod of Metamagic, Reach (lesser)

### Properties

**Type:** Rod **Price:** 3000 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 17 **Aura:** strong universal

### Description

The wielder can cast up to three spells per day with a onestep increase in their range category (such as from close to _[[classes/Medium|medium]]_, or _medium_ to long) as though using the _[[feats/Reach Spell|Reach Spell]]_ feat (Advanced Player's Guide).

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Rod|Craft Rod]]_, _Reach Spell_
