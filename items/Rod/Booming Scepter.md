# Booming Scepter

### Properties

**Type:** Rod **Price:** 34000 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** B

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

This bejeweled scepter constantly emits a low hum that amplifies the wielder's voice. A _[[items/Rod/Booming Scepter|booming scepter]]_ acts as a +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Thundering|thundering]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Light mace|light mace]]_ that grants the wielder a +5 competence bonus on Intimidate checks and the ability to use _[[spells/Shout|shout]]_, as per the spell, once per day. When a _booming scepter_ is held in both hands, the wielder's voice carries far enough to be heard by all creatures within 1 mile, regardless of ambient noise or intervening obstacles, although this feature cannot penetrate the area of a _[[spells/Silence|silence]]_ spell.
