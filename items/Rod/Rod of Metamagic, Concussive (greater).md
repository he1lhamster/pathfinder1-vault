# Rod of Metamagic, Concussive (greater)

### Properties

**Type:** Rod **Price:** 73000 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 17 **Aura:** strong universal

### Description

Up to three times per day the wielder can cast a spell with the sonic descriptor that causes those who take damage from that spell to take a –2 penalty on attack rolls, saving throws, skill checks, and ability checks for a number of rounds equal to the actual spell level of the spell cast as though using the _[[feats/Concussive Spell|Concussive Spell]]_ feat (Ultimate Magic).

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 36500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Rod|Craft Rod]]_, _Concussive Spell_
