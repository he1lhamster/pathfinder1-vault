# Rod of Metamagic, Eclipsed (normal)

### Properties

**Type:** Rod **Price:** 5500 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 17 **Aura:** strong universal

### Description

The wielder can cast up to three spells per day that alter how the spell raises or lowers the illumination level in their affected areas as though using the _[[feats/Eclipsed Spell|Eclipsed Spell]]_ feat (see page 25).

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 2750 gp
