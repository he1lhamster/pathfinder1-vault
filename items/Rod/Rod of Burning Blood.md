# Rod of Burning Blood

### Properties

**Type:** Rod **Price:** 11050 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** B

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

This rod of blackened iron is inset with rubies and functions as a +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Cunning|cunning]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Light mace|light mace]]_. _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Unholy|Unholy]]_ water poured over the rod is absorbed, giving the rod 1 charge per _[[items/Mundane/Vial|vial]]_. When the rod strikes a creature, the wielder can _[[spells/Expend|expend]]_ 1 charge from the rod to spray the creature with _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Burning|burning]]_ blood from the weapon's head. This _burning_ blood deals 1d6 points of damage (half fire, half _unholy_).

Alternatively, the wielder can _expend_ 5 charges at once to create a spray of _burning_ blood in a 15-foot cone. Creatures in the area take 5d4 points of damage (half fire, half _unholy_; Reflex DC 17 half). The rod can hold at most 10 charges at any one time. Charging the rod with _unholy_ water is a standard action that provokes attacks of opportunity.
