# Rod of the Wayang

### Properties

**Type:** Rod **Price:** 12000 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

This rod is lacquered _[[universal monster rules/Jet|jet]]_ black, covered with bright white runes, and capped with crown of jagged smoky quartz. When the rod's wielder casts a spell of the _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Shadow|shadow]]_ subschool, she increases the DC of that spell's saving throw by 1. Furthermore, once per day on _[[spells/Command|command]]_, wielder can treat positive and negative energy effects as if she were an undead creature, taking damage from positive energy and healing damage from negative energy. This ability lasts for 1 minute unless dismissed as a free action.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 6000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Rod|Craft Rod]]_, _[[spells/Shadow Conjuration|shadow conjuration]]_ greater, _[[spells/Shadow Evocation|shadow evocation]]_ greater
