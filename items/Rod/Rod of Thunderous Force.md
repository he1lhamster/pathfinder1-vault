# Rod of Thunderous Force

### Properties

**Type:** Rod **Price:** 5400 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

This mahogany rod is etched with the image of whirling storm clouds. Once per day on _[[spells/Command|command]]_, the wielder can slam the rod against the ground, releasing a concussive wave of sonic and force energy. This creates a 20-foot radius burst centered on the wielder. While it causes no damage, those within the burst must succeed at a DC 19 Fortitude save or fall _[[conditions/Prone|prone]]_ and be _[[conditions/Deafened|deafened]]_ for 1d4 rounds. The wielder is unaffected by this blast. This effect is a force and sonic effect.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 2700 gp

_[[feats/Craft Rod|Craft Rod]]_, blindness/deafness, _[[spells/Wall Of Force|wall of force]]_
