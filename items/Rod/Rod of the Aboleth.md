# Rod of the Aboleth

### Properties

**Type:** Rod **Price:** 29000 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** B

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

The _[[items/Rod/Rod of the Aboleth|rod of the aboleth]]_ acts as a +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Corrosive|corrosive]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Light mace|light mace]]_. One end of the rod has three eye-shaped rubies imbedded in it, while the other end comes to a sharp point. When exposed to moisture, the surface of the rod takes on a slimy sheen that doesn't affect the wielder's grip. This rod has the following powers.

* Once per day as a free action, when the rod hits a living creature, the target must succeed at a DC 18 Fortitude saving throw or take 1d4 points of Constitution damage.
* Once per day as a free action, when the rod hits a living creature, the creature must succeed at a DC 13 Will saving throw or be affected as though targeted with a _[[spells/Aboleth's Lung|aboleth's lung]]_ spell (Advanced Race Guide).
* On _[[spells/Command|command]]_ for 1 minute per day, the wielder may gain a swim speed of 60 feet for 1 minute. This time need not be continuous, and can be spent in 1-round increments.
