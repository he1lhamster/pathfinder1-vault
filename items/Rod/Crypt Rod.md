# Crypt Rod

### Properties

**Type:** Rod **Price:** 8500 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** B

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

This 3-foot-long iron rod has a flanged, conical head and strikes as a +1 _[[items/Weapon/Light mace|light mace]]_. On _[[spells/Command|command]]_ when held in hand, the rod points in the direction of the closest grave, tomb, or cairn within 500 feet. If the wielder concentrates on a specific type or subtype of creature, the rod identifies only gravesites that hold the remains of that type of creature. The rod can search for the remains of a specific individual if the wielder carries an item that the individual owned in life.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 4250 gp
