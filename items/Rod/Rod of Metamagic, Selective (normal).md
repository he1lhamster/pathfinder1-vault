# Rod of Metamagic, Selective (normal)

### Properties

**Type:** Rod **Price:** 11000 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 17 **Aura:** strong universal

### Description

The wielder can cast up to three spells per day as though using the _[[feats/Selective Spell|Selective Spell]]_ feat (Advanced Player's Guide). For each spell, he can select up to four creatures of his choice within the area that are unaffected.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 5500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Rod|Craft Rod]]_, _Selective Spell_
