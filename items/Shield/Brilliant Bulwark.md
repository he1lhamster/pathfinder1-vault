### Description

This +1 _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Blinding|blinding]]_ _[[items/Armor/Buckler|buckler]]_ is made from a lustrous glass that is as hard as steel and reflects light like a _[[items/Mundane/Mirror|mirror]]_. The _shield_ glows as a _[[items/Mundane/Torch|torch]]_, though this light can be suppressed or resumed on _[[spells/Command|command]]_. The _shield_ can be commanded to shine light as per _[[spells/Daylight|daylight]]_ for up to 10 minutes each day. This duration need not be consecutive, but it must be used in 1-minute increments. The wielder can also _[[spells/Expend|expend]]_ this duration while activating the _buckler_'s _blinding_ ability to increase the save DC by 1 for every 2 minutes of _daylight_ duration expended. The _shield_ must sit in natural bright light for at least 1 hour after its 10 minutes of _daylight_ is expended before the ability can be used again.
