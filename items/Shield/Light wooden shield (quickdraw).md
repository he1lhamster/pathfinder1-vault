# Light wooden shield (quickdraw)

### Properties

**Type:** _[[spells/Shield|Shield]]_ **Price:** 53 gp **Weight:** 6 lbs. **Slot:** _Shield_

##### Armor properties

**Type:** Light **Armor bonus:** 1 **Maximum dexterity:** 0 **Armor check penalty:** -2 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 5

### Description

A wooden quickdraw _shield_ is essentially the same as a steel quickdraw _shield_, except it responds differently to some spells and effects (such as _[[spells/Rusting Grasp|rusting grasp]]_). A _[[classes/Druid|druid]]_ can use a heavy wooden quickdraw _shield_, but not a steel quickdraw _shield_.
