# Mwangi Spirit Shield

### Properties

**Type:** _[[spells/Shield|Shield]]_ **Price:** 19393 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** _Shield_

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _Shield_ **Armor bonus:** 1 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Maximum dexterity:** – **Armor check penalty:** -1 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 5

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 15 **Aura:** strong transmutation

### Description

This large wood-and-hide _shield_ is similar to other Mwangi shields, but the totem animal painted on the _shield_'s face seems to move when the _shield_ is carried. Crafted by Mwangi shamans to grant its wielder the power of _[[monsters/Tribal Totem|tribal totem]]_ spirits, a _[[items/Shield/Mwangi Spirit Shield|Mwangi spirit shield]]_ functions as a +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Ghost Touch|ghost touch]]_ _[[items/Shield/Light wooden shield|light wooden shield]]_. Once per day on _[[spells/Command|command]]_, the _shield_ can grant its bearer _[[spells/Bull's Strength|bull's strength]]_ for 1 minute.

Other versions of the _Mwangi spirit shield_ exist; some are known to grant _[[spells/Cat's Grace|cat's grace]]_ or _[[spells/Bear's Endurance|bear's endurance]]_ to their wielders instead of _bull's strength_.
