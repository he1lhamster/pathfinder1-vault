# Phalanx Shield

### Properties

**Type:** _[[spells/Shield|Shield]]_ **Price:** 14080 gp **Weight:** 30 lbs. **Slot:** _Shield_

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _Shield_ **Armor bonus:** 4 **Enhancement bonus:** +2 **Maximum dexterity:** 2 **Armor check penalty:** -10 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 50 **Special material:** Adamantine

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

Once per day as a standard action, the wielder of this +2 adamantine _[[items/Shield/Tower shield|tower shield]]_ can conjure two spiritual allies (as per the spell). These conjured allies last for 1 minute and can neither attack nor move beyond spaces that are adjacent to the wielder of the _[[items/Shield/Phalanx Shield|phalanx shield]]_. Both allies gain a +4 enhancement bonus to AC and have the _[[feats/Shield Wall|Shield Wall]]_ teamwork feat.
