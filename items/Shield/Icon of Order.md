### Description

An _[[items/Shield/Icon of Order|icon of order]]_ is a +2 _[[items/Shield/Heavy steel shield|heavy steel shield]]_ that typically displays the sword-and-sunburst holy symbol of Iomedae on a white field. The wielder of an _icon of order_ gains a +2 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ bonus on saves against spells and effects with the chaotic descriptor. Once per day, the wielder can cast _[[spells/Protection From Chaos|protection from chaos]]_ as a swift action.
