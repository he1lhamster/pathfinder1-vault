# Breach Barrier

### Properties

**Type:** _[[spells/Shield|Shield]]_ **Price:** 2370 gp **Weight:** 15 lbs. **Slot:** _Shield_

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _Shield_ **Armor bonus:** 2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Maximum dexterity:** – **Armor check penalty:** -2 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 15

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint abjuration

### Description

This +1 _[[items/Shield/Heavy steel shield|heavy steel shield]]_ is often stylized by its dwarven crafters to look like a door or archway. Once per day after uttering a _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word, the wielder can press this _shield_ against a portal to secure it as per the _[[spells/Hold Portal|hold portal]]_ spell. The _shield_ remains adhered to the portal for 3 minutes, after which the _shield_ comes free. The wielder can remove the _shield_ from her arm as a move action, leaving the _shield_ attached to the portal for the remainder of the duration. Before the end of the duration, the wielder can _[[universal monster rules/Pull|pull]]_ the _shield_ from the portal, automatically ending the effect.
