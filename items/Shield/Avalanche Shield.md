**Type:** _Shield_ **Armor bonus:** 2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Maximum dexterity:** – **Armor check penalty:** -2 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 15

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 12 **Aura:** strong evocation

### Description

The front of this +1 _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Bashing|bashing]]_ _[[items/Shield/Heavy steel shield|heavy steel shield]]_ looks as though it is crafted from hundreds of bits of rubble and larger stones. When used to make a _shield_ bash attack, stones fall from _shield_ and cling to the target of the _shield_ bash. The target must succeed at a DC 14 Fortitude saving throw or be _[[conditions/Entangled|entangled]]_ for 1d4 rounds. Once per day on _[[spells/Command|command]]_, the target's wielder can choose to petrify the target of a _shield_ bash instead. The target must succeed at a DC 16 Fortitude saving throw or become _[[conditions/Petrified|petrified]]_ for 1 minute.
