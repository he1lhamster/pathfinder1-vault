# Spined Shield

### Properties

**Type:** _[[spells/Shield|Shield]]_ **Price:** 5580 gp **Weight:** 15 lbs. **Slot:** _Shield_

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _Shield_ **Armor bonus:** 2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Maximum dexterity:** – **Armor check penalty:** -2 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 15

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 6 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

This +1 _[[items/Shield/Heavy steel shield|heavy steel shield]]_ is covered in spines. It acts as a normal spiked _shield_. On _[[spells/Command|command]]_ up to three times per day, the _shield_'s wearer can fire one of the _shield_'s spines. A fired spine has a +1 enhancement bonus, a range increment of 120 feet, and deals 1d10 points of damage (19–20/×2). Fired spines _[[spells/Regenerate|regenerate]]_ each day.
