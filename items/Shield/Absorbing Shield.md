# Absorbing Shield

### Properties

**Type:** _[[spells/Shield|Shield]]_ **Price:** 50170 gp **Weight:** 15 lbs. **Slot:** _Shield_

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _Shield_ **Armor bonus:** 2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Maximum dexterity:** – **Armor check penalty:** -2 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 15

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 17 **Aura:** strong transmutation

### Description

This +1 _[[items/Shield/Heavy steel shield|heavy steel shield]]_ is made of metal, but its color is a flat black that seems to absorb light. Once every 2 days, on _[[spells/Command|command]]_, it can _[[spells/Disintegrate|disintegrate]]_ an object that it touches, as the spell but requiring a melee touch attack. This effect only functions as an attack—it can't be activated to target a creature or weapon as it strikes the _shield_.
