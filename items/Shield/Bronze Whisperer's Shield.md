### Properties

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint evocation, faint transmutation

### Description

This +1 _[[items/Shield/Light steel shield|light steel shield]]_ is decorated with bronze gears and vaguely resembles a feminine metal _[[items/Mundane/Mask|mask]]_ with a rune on its forehead. As a standard action, the wearer can _[[spells/Command|command]]_ it to noisily transform into a _[[items/Armor/Buckler|buckler]]_ or back into its light _shield_ form. Once per day, the wearer can use it to deliver a _[[spells/Shocking Grasp|shocking grasp]]_.
