# Lion's Shield

### Properties

**Type:** _[[spells/Shield|Shield]]_ **Price:** 9170 gp **Weight:** 15 lbs. **Slot:** _Shield_

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _Shield_ **Armor bonus:** 2 **Enhancement bonus:** +2 **Maximum dexterity:** – **Armor check penalty:** -2 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 15

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate conjuration

### Description

This +2 _[[items/Shield/Heavy steel shield|heavy steel shield]]_ is fashioned to appear to be a roaring _[[monsters/Lion|lion]]_'s head. Three times per day as a free action, the _lion_'s head can be commanded to attack (independently of the _shield_ wearer), biting with the wielder's base attack bonus (including multiple attacks, if the wielder has them) and dealing 2d6 points of damage. This attack is in addition to any actions performed by the wielder.
