# Elysian Shield

### Properties

**Type:** _[[spells/Shield|Shield]]_ **Price:** 52620 gp **Weight:** 45 lbs. **Slot:** _Shield_

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _Shield_ **Armor bonus:** 4 **Enhancement bonus:** +2 **Maximum dexterity:** 2 **Armor check penalty:** -10 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 50

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 13 **Aura:** strong conjuration

### Description

This +2 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Ghost Touch|ghost touch]]_ _[[items/Shield/Tower shield|tower shield]]_ is engraved with funerary runes depicting the _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Peaceful|peaceful]]_ rest of the contented dead. The wielder of an _[[items/Shield/Elysian Shield|elysian shield]]_ is immune to the create spawn ability of undead, and once per day as an immediate action can negate a single _[[universal monster rules/Energy Drain|energy drain]]_, ability drain, or ability damage attack by an undead creature. In addition, once per day as a standard action, the wielder can release a wave of positive energy that panics undead, as the _[[feats/Turn Undead|Turn Undead]]_ feat (Will DC 20 negates).
