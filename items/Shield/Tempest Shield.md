### Description

The face of this +1 _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Bashing|bashing]]_ _[[items/Shield/Heavy steel shield|heavy steel shield]]_ swirls with the image of swirling clouds, driving rain, or falling snow. When used to make a _shield_ bash attack, the _shield_'s wielder can make a bull rush maneuver as a free action against her target without provoking an attack of opportunity from the creature being bull rushed. Once per day on _[[spells/Command|command]]_, the wielder can cause the _[[items/Shield/Tempest Shield|tempest shield]]_ to emit a s tream of high-powered air as the _[[spells/Gust Of Wind|gust of wind]]_ spell.
