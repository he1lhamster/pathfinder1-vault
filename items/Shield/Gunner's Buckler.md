# Gunner's Buckler

### Properties

**Type:** _[[spells/Shield|Shield]]_ **Price:** 10155 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** _Shield_

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _Shield_ **Armor bonus:** 1 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Maximum dexterity:** – **Armor check penalty:** -1 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 5 **Special material:** Mithral

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint abjuration

### Description

The top edge of this +1 mithral _[[items/Armor/Buckler|buckler]]_ features a notched gun rest. A character wearing a _[[items/Shield/Gunner's Buckler|gunner's buckler]]_ who uses a firearm while threatened by an opponent in melee does not provoke attacks of opportunity, but takes a –4 penalty on all ranged attack rolls in any round in which he makes a ranged attack while threatened in melee.
