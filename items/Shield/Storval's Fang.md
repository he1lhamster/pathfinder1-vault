# Storval's Fang

### Properties

**Type:** _[[spells/Shield|Shield]]_ **Price:** 38312 gp **Weight:** 6 lbs. **Slot:** _Shield_

##### Armor properties

**Type:** _Shield_ **Armor bonus:** 1 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Maximum dexterity:** – **Armor check penalty:** -1 **Arcane spell failure chance:** 5

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 6 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This metal +1 _[[items/Armor/Klar|klar]]_ is shaped like the head of a storm _[[monsters/Roc|roc]]_, whose sharp feathers serve as the _klar_'s spikes, and it hums and crackles like a distant stormy sky. When used to make a _shield_ bash attack, a _[[items/Shield/Storval's Fang|Storval's fang]]_ deals an additional 1d6 points of sonic damage. In addition, the wielder gains electricity _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ 5. When the wielder confirms a critical hit with a _Storval's fang_, it unleashes a thunderclap, deafening the target of the critical hit for 1 minute unless that target succeeds at a DC 16 Fortitude saving throw.

If the wielder of a _Storval's fang_ also wields a Storval's thunder and uses the _[[feats/Thunder And Fang|Thunder and Fang]]_ feat, each weapon deals an additional 1d6 points of electricity damage, the DCs to negate their critical hit effects increase by 2, and a creature that fails such a saving throw is also pushed back 10 feet and knocked _[[conditions/Prone|prone]]_.
