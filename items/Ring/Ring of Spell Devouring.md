# Ring of Spell Devouring

### Properties

**Type:** Ring **Price:** 100000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Ring

##### Magical properties

**Cursed**

**Caster level:** 13 **Aura:** strong abjuration

### Description

This delicate ring appears to be a _[[items/Ring/Ring of Spell Storing|ring of spell storing]]_ or a _[[items/Ring/Ring of Spell Turning|ring of spell turning]]_, but instead of aiding casting or counterspelling, the ring hinders such efforts. Each time the wearer casts an arcane spell, he suffers a 25% spell failure chance. Furthermore, the wearer of the ring takes a –4 penalty on Spellcraft checks to _[[spells/Identify|identify]]_ a spell when attempting to counterspell. The ring can only be removed by a _[[spells/Remove Curse|remove curse]]_ spell or a similar effect.
