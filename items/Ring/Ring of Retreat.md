# Ring of Retreat

### Properties

**Type:** Ring **Price:** 12000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Ring

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 12 **Aura:** strong conjuration

### Description

Each of these silver-and-gemstone rings is attuned to a specific aiudara, or elf _[[spells/Gate|gate]]_. The ring must be created at the aiudara in question, and its gemstone always matches the _gate_'s portal-key. On _[[spells/Command|command]]_ once per week, the ring teleports its wearer back to that _gate_, as per _[[spells/Word of Recall|word of recall]]_. Theoretically, a _[[items/Ring/Ring of Retreat|ring of retreat]]_ could be crafted instead to transport the wearer to a specific site of import, such as a temple or _[[feats/Stronghold|stronghold]]_, but to date, these rings have been crafted only with elf gates in mind.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 6000 gp

_[[feats/Forge Ring|Forge Ring]]_, _word of recall_
