# Ring of Rat Fangs

### Properties

**Type:** Ring **Price:** 5000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Ring

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

This bone ring is the shape of an elongated rat biting its own tail. The wearer gains a bite attack as a primary natural attack. This attack deals 1d4 points of piercing damage for a _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ wearer or 1d3 points of damage for a Small wearer. After about a week of wearing the ring, the wearer's appearance becomes more ratlike, though she still looks like a member of her original race (for example, a human might gain prominent teeth).

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 2500 gp

_[[feats/Forge Ring|Forge Ring]]_, _[[spells/Magic Fang|magic fang]]_, _[[spells/Polymorph|polymorph]]_
