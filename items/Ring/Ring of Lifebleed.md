# Ring of Lifebleed

### Properties

**Type:** Ring **Price:** 21000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Ring

##### Magical properties

**Cursed**

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate necromancy

### Description

Before being worn, this ring appears to be a _[[items/Ring/Ring of Regeneration|ring of regeneration]]_. Once it is worn in combat, however, the true nature of the ring becomes apparent. Each time the wearer is hit with a melee or ranged weapon attack, he takes an additional 1d4 points of damage. This ring cannot be removed until the wearer dies or by way of a _[[spells/Remove Curse|remove curse]]_, _[[spells/Wish|wish]]_, or _[[spells/Miracle|miracle]]_ spell.
