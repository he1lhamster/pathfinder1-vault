# Ring of Heroes

### Properties

**Type:** Ring **Price:** 8000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Ring

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate universal

### Description

Sculpted in the shape of two noble looking bronze lions charging toward a centrally placed garnet, this ring grants the wearer the _[[feats/Luck Of Heroes|Luck of Heroes]]_ feat. The ring must be worn for 24 hours before it takes effect. The wearer can also call upon the ring's power to gain a hero point that must be used immediately, although this causes the ring to lose its powers permanently.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 4000 gp

_[[feats/Forge Ring|Forge Ring]]_, _Luck of Heroes_
