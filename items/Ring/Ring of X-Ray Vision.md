# Ring of X-Ray Vision

### Properties

**Type:** Ring **Price:** 25000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Ring

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 6 **Aura:** moderate _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

On _[[spells/Command|command]]_, this ring gives its wearer the ability to see into and through solid matter. _[[spells/Vision|Vision]]_ range is 20 feet, with the viewer seeing as if he were looking at something in normal light even if there is no illumination. X-ray _vision_ can penetrate 1 foot of stone, 1 inch of common metal, or up to 3 feet of wood or dirt. Thicker substances or a thin sheet of lead blocks the _vision_.

Using the ring is _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Exhausting|exhausting]]_, causing the wearer 1 point of Constitution damage per minute after the first 10 minutes of use in a single day. The ring must be used in 1-minute increments.
