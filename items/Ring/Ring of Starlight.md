# Ring of Starlight

### Properties

**Type:** Ring **Price:** 1600 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Ring

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 1 **Aura:** faint evocation

### Description

This smooth mithral ring glimmers with light, and a close look reveals the reflection of a starry night sky within the silvery metal. The wearer of a _[[items/Ring/Ring of Starlight|ring of starlight]]_ can cause portions of her body or items in her _[[spells/Possession|possession]]_ to glow with colorful light, as per _[[spells/Faerie Fire|faerie fire]]_, by waving the hand adorned with the ring over the item or body part as a swift action. This glow lasts for 1 hour, until dismissed as a free action, or until the ring is removed. This duration need not be used all at once, but must be used in 10-minute increments. (Each time an increment of duration is used, the wearer can choose the portion of her body or item in her _possession_ that glows.) In addition, wearing the ring allows the caster to use _[[spells/Dancing Lights|dancing lights]]_ on _[[spells/Command|command]]_ three times per day as a standard action.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 800 gp

_[[feats/Forge Ring|Forge Ring]]_, _dancing lights_, _faerie fire_
