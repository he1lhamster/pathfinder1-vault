# Dungeon Ring (prisoner's)

### Properties

**Type:** Ring **Price:** 250 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Ring

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

A jailer's dungeon ring is worked gold, set with carnelians. The ring is magically attuned to one or more iron prisoner's dungeon rings. When the wearer of a jailer's ring places a prisoner's ring on a subject, the prisoner cannot remove the ring without the use of a _[[spells/Remove Curse|remove curse]]_, _[[spells/Limited Wish|limited wish]]_, _[[spells/Miracle|miracle]]_, or _[[spells/Wish|wish]]_ spell. The wearer of the jailer's ring can also remove a linked prisoner's ring at any time.

The jailer is aware of any wearer of a linked prisoner's ring as per a constant _[[spells/Status|status]]_ spell. Additionally, all wearers of linked prisoner's rings count as familiar to the jailer for the purposes of spells such as _[[spells/Scrying|scrying]]_ and teleport.

**Crafting cost:** 125 gp
