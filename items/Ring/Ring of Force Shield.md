# Ring of Force Shield

### Properties

**Type:** Ring **Price:** 8500 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Ring

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

This ring generates a shield-sized (and shield-shaped) _[[spells/Wall Of Force|wall of force]]_ that stays with the ring and can be wielded by the wearer as if it were a heavy _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_ (+2 AC). This special creation has no armor check penalty or arcane spell failure chance since it is weightless and encumbrance-free. It can be activated and deactivated at will as a free action.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 4250 gp

_[[feats/Forge Ring|Forge Ring]]_, _wall of force_
