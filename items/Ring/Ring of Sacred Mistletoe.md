# Ring of Sacred Mistletoe

### Properties

**Type:** Ring **Price:** 6000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Ring

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 1 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

This tiny ring of living _[[items/Mundane/Mistletoe|mistletoe]]_ coils about the wearer's finger of its own accord. The wearer gains a +2 competence bonus on Handle Animal, Knowledge (nature), and wild _[[feats/Empathy|empathy]]_ checks, and a wearer with the woodland stride ability can move effortlessly, without _[[spells/Harm|harm]]_ or impediment, even through magically created or manipulated plants. This does not allow the wearer to move through solid objects or creatures.

Furthermore, the wearer can use _[[spells/Shillelagh|shillelagh]]_ once per day.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 3000 gp

_[[feats/Forge Ring|Forge Ring]]_, _[[spells/Pass without Trace|pass without trace]]_, _shillelagh_
