# Ring of Clumsiness

### Properties

**Type:** Ring **Price:** 2200 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Ring

##### Magical properties

**Cursed**

**Caster level:** 15 **Aura:** strong transmutation

### Description

This ring operates exactly like a _[[items/Ring/Ring of Feather Falling|ring of feather falling]]_. However, it also makes the wearer clumsy. She takes a –4 penalty to Dexterity and has a 20% chance of spell failure when trying to cast any arcane spell that has a somatic component. (This chance of spell failure stacks with other arcane spell failure chances.)
