# Band of Triumph

### Properties

**Type:** Ring **Price:** 50000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Ring

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 13 **Aura:** strong enchantment, strong evocation

### Description

This white-gold ring bears the relief image of a roaring _[[monsters/Lion|lion]]_. Once per day on _[[spells/Command|command]]_, the wearer can point the ring and mentally designate one or more creatures or objects in a 60-foot cone as enemies, after which the ring roars triumphantly. Selected targets in the area are affected by _[[spells/Shout|shout]]_ (Fortitude DC 20). All other objects in the area are unharmed, and all other creatures in the area receive the benefits of the inspire courage bardic performance as performed by a 7th-level _[[classes/Bard|bard]]_ for 3 rounds. When activating the ring, the wearer can _[[spells/Expend|expend]]_ 1 round of her bardic performance ability to use her own _bard_ level to calculate the performance's effect.

In addition, the ring grants the wearer a +4 enhancement bonus to Charisma; treat this as a temporary ability bonus for the first 24 hours the ring is worn.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 25000 gp

_[[feats/Forge Ring|Forge Ring]]_, _[[spells/Eagle's Splendor|eagle's splendor]]_, _[[spells/Good Hope|good hope]]_, _shout_
