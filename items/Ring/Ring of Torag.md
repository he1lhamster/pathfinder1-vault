# Ring of Torag

### Properties

**Type:** Ring **Price:** 1200 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Ring

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint abjuration

### Description

This simple golden ring has a large red gemstone set into it that sparkles with an inner fire. The wearer of the ring gains fire _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ 10 against the first fire attack that hits him that day. This protection renews itself every morning at dawn. In addition, the wearer receives a +1 _resistance_ bonus on saves made against fire spells and effects. The ring must be worn for 24 hours to have any effect.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 600 gp

_[[feats/Forge Ring|Forge Ring]]_, _[[spells/Resist Energy|resist energy]]_
