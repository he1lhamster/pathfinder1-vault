# Ring of Summoning Affinity (Agathion)

### Properties

**Type:** Ring **Price:** 7700 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Ring

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint conjuration

### Description

This ring is forged from electrum and inlaid with runes. If the wearer can cast _[[universal monster rules/Summon|summon]]_ monster spells, the wearer adds silvanshee to the 3rd-level list of monsters she can _summon_ with those spells, adds vulpinal to the 5th-level list, adds avoral to the 7th-level list, and adds leonal to the 8th-level list. Also, once per day on _[[spells/Command|command]]_ the wearer can use this ring to _summon_ a silvanshee, as if by the _[[spells/Summon Monster III|summon monster III]]_ spell.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 3850 gp
