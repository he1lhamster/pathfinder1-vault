# Ring of Truth

### Properties

**Type:** Ring **Price:** 8000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Ring

##### Magical properties

**Cursed**

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate enchantment

### Description

Deceptively pleasant-looking, a _[[items/Ring/Ring of Truth|ring of truth]]_ bears images of _[[feats/Childlike|childlike]]_ angels and broadly smiling divine creatures holding onto links of a heavy chain. The wearer of this cursed ring is rendered unable to tell a deliberate lie, in either speech or writing. The wearer may simply omit the truth or choose not to communicate, but even then must succeed at a DC 20 Will saving throw to avoid _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Answering|answering]]_ a direct question truthfully. The wearer cannot remove the ring unless the magic of the curse is negated.

### Crafting requirements

_[[items/Ring/Ring of Mind Shielding|ring of mind shielding]]_, _[[items/Ring/Ring of X-Ray Vision|ring of x-ray vision]]_
