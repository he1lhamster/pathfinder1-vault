# Minotaur Belt

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 11000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Belt

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 4 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

This belt grants its wearer a +2 enhancement bonus to Strength, and the ability to ignore difficult terrain while charging or making an overrun, bull rush, or _[[universal monster rules/Trample|trample]]_ attempt. Treat the enhancement bonus to Strength as a temporary ability bonus for the first 24 hours the belt is worn.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 5500 gp

Craft Wondrous Items, _[[spells/Bull's Strength|bull's strength]]_, _[[spells/Feather Step|feather step]]_ (Advanced Player's Guide)
