# Necklace of Bloody Incisors

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 1850 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Neck

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

This thin iron chain holds up to 10 extracted teeth of common predatory animals strung along it. As a move action, the wearer can place up to three teeth into a thrown splash weapon or an _[[classes/Alchemist|alchemist]]_'s bomb. When such a splash weapon or bomb hits a target, it deals an additional 1d4 points of slashing damage per tooth used. Each tooth used in such a splash weapon or bomb also increases its splash damage by 2 points of slashing damage per tooth. Once all of the necklace's teeth are used in this way, the necklace's magic is expended. A newly purchased _[[items/Wondrous Item/Necklace of Bloody Incisors|necklace of bloody incisors]]_ contains 10 extracted teeth.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 925 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, greater _[[spells/Magic Fang|magic fang]]_
