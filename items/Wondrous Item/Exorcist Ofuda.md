# Exorcist Ofuda

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 650 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint abjuration

### Description

This paper slip covered with holy writings can be used to protect against lingering evil. A creature holding an _[[items/Wondrous Item/Exorcist Ofuda|exorcist ofuda]]_ gains a +3 bonus on Perception checks to notice a haunt when the haunt's effects manifest. Additionally, as a standard action the _exorcist ofuda_ can be triggered to grant all allies within 30 feet a +2 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ bonus on saving throws and a +2 _[[spells/Deflection|deflection]]_ bonus to AC against a haunt's effects for 1 round. Triggering the ofuda this way destroys it in a gout of flame.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 325 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Protection From Evil|protection from evil]]_
