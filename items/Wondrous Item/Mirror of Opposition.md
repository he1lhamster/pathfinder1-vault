# Mirror of Opposition

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 92000 gp **Weight:** 45 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 15 **Aura:** strong necromancy

### Description

This item resembles a normal _[[items/Mundane/Mirror|mirror]]_ about 4 feet long and 3 feet wide. It can be hung or placed on a surface and then activated by speaking a _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word. The same _command_ word deactivates the _mirror_. If a creature sees its reflection in the _mirror_'s surface, an exact duplicate of that creature comes into being. This opposite immediately attacks the original. The duplicate has all the possessions and powers of its original (including magic). Upon the defeat or _[[spells/Destruction|destruction]]_ of either the duplicate or the original, the duplicate and its items disappear completely. The _mirror_ functions up to four times per day. Destroying the _mirror_ (hardness 1, 5 hit points) causes all of the duplicates to immediately _[[spells/Vanish|vanish]]_.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 46000 gp
