# Insistent Doorknocker

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 5000 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This iron _[[monsters/Gargoyle|gargoyle]]_ face holds an iron ring in its mouth. Once per day, when the flat back of the _gargoyle_ face is placed against a wall, floor, or ceiling, it fuses into place, creating a usable door and penetrating the space beyond up to 1 foot as if using _[[spells/Passwall|passwall]]_. Speaking the _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word again ends the _passwall_ effect, returns the surface to its previous doorless shape, and releases the doorknocker from the surface.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 2500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _passwall_
