# Efreeti Horns

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 24000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Headband

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

The wearer of _[[items/Wondrous Item/Efreeti Horns|efreeti horns]]_ gains a +4 enhancement bonus to Wisdom. In addition, the wearer can spend 1 ki point as a full-round action to gain the effects of _[[spells/Enlarge Person|enlarge person]]_ (self only) or _[[spells/Reduce Person|reduce person]]_ (self only) for 1 minute. These effects apply to the wearer regardless of her actual creature type.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 12000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _enlarge person_, _[[spells/Owl's Wisdom|owl's wisdom]]_, _reduce person_

* Adventurer's Guide
