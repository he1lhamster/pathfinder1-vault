# Cauldron of Flying

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 40000 gp **Weight:** 100 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This iron pot is large enough for two _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ humanoids to stand in comfortably. Upon _[[spells/Command|command]]_, the _[[items/Mundane/Cauldron|cauldron]]_ and up to 500 pounds of additional weight can fly as if using _[[spells/Overland Flight|overland flight]]_. The _cauldron_ moves under the direction of the person who spoke the activating _command_ word. It can carry up to double its capacity, but doing so reduces its speed to 30 feet. It can _[[feats/Hover|hover]]_ in place without the need for a Fly skill check. The _cauldron_ provides partial cover for anyone standing in it. It has a hardness of 10 and 60 hit points.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 20000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _overland flight_
