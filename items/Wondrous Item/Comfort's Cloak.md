# Comfort's Cloak

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 15600 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Shoulders

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint abjuration, faint necromancy

### Description

A favored cloak of rangers, this cloak is a patchwork of hides and fur. _[[items/Wondrous Item/Comfort's Cloak|Comfort's cloak]]_ grants a constant _[[spells/Endure Elements|endure elements]]_ effect to the wearer, and grants a +4 competence bonus to saves against disease, _[[universal monster rules/Energy Drain|energy drain]]_, effects that cause fatigue or exhaustion, and poisons. Furthermore, while the wearer is resting he regains twice his Hit Dice in hit points for 8 hours of rest instead of the typical 1 hit point per Hit Die. When taking a full 24 hours of rest, the character regains 5 times his Hit Dice in hit points instead of twice his Hit Dice in hit points.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 7800 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _endure elements_
