# Ohalia's Horn

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 55000 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 15 **Aura:** strong conjuration

### Description

The _[[items/Mundane/Bell|bell]]_ of this slightly curved horn resembles the head of a roaring lioness. The horn functions in many ways like a _[[items/Wondrous Item/Horn of Valhalla|horn of valhalla]]_, but while held in one hand, the wielder gains the benefits of _[[spells/Deathwatch|deathwatch]]_. Once per year when the wielder sounds the horn, she can _[[universal monster rules/Summon|summon]]_ one _[[monsters/Sleipnir|sleipnir]]_ with a saddle for 1 hour in place of summoning the barbarians. The _sleipnir_ willingly serves as a _[[spells/Mount|mount]]_ or combatant.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 22500 gp
