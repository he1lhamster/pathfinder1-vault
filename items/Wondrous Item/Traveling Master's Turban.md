# Traveling Master's Turban

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 4000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Head

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

This turban consists of 30 feet of cloth held in place by a _[[items/Mundane/Brooch|brooch]]_. The owner can _[[spells/Command|command]]_ the turban to tie or untie itself as a move action. When the turban is tied on the wearer's head, the wearer gains the benefits of _[[spells/Endure Elements|endure elements]]_. Once per day when the turban is untied, the owner can use the fabric to perform a _[[spells/Rope Trick|rope trick]]_ (as per the spell _rope trick_). In addition, the owner can spend 1 ki point as a swift action to cast _[[spells/Animate Rope|animate rope]]_.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 2000 gp
