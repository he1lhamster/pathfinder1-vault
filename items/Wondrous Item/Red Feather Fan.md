# Red Feather Fan

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 5000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 6 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

This fan is made of copper fashioned into a dozen small feathers mounted on a ring-shaped bone handle. Once per day, the bearer can hold it and speak a _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word to counterspell a _[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_ effect (as if using _[[spells/Daylight|daylight]]_) or counterspell a light effect (as if using _[[spells/Deeper Darkness|deeper darkness]]_). If activated in an area of overlapping light and _darkness_ effects, the fan randomly counterspells one of them, leaving the other intact.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 2500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _daylight_, _deeper darkness_
