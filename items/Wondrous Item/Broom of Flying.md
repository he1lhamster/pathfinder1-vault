# Broom of Flying

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 17000 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This broom is able to fly through the air as if affected by an _[[spells/Overland Flight|overland flight]]_ spell (+4 on Fly skill checks) for up to 9 hours per day (_[[universal monster rules/Split|split]]_ up as its owner desires). The broom can carry 200 pounds and fly at a speed of 40 feet, or up to 400 pounds at a speed at 30 feet. In addition, the broom can travel alone to any destination named by the owner as long as she has a good idea of the location and layout of that destination. It flies to its owner from as far away as 900 feet when she speaks the _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word. The _[[items/Wondrous Item/Broom of Flying|broom of flying]]_ has a speed of 40 feet when it has no rider.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 8500 gp
