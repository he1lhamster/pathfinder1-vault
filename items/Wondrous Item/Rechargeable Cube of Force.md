# Rechargeable Cube of Force

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 70000 gp **Weight:** 0.5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

This cube functions like a _[[items/Wondrous Item/Cube of Force|cube of force]]_ except that it can be recharged by casting a _[[spells/Wall Of Force|wall of force]]_ spell into it. Each spell cast in this manner adds 2 charges to the cube. The cube cannot hold more than 36 charges.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 35000 gp
