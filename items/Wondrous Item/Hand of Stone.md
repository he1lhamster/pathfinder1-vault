# Hand of Stone

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 27000 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Neck

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

This mummified dwarf hand hangs on a steel chain around a character's neck, bringing the wearer more in tune with the earth beneath her feet. If the wearer spends a standard action concentrating on the hand, she gains _[[universal monster rules/Tremorsense|tremorsense]]_ out to a range of 30 feet until the start of her next turn. This ability only allows her to sense creatures and objects that are in contact with earth or stone.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 13500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, clairvoyance/clairaudience
