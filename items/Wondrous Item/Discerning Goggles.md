# Discerning Goggles

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 13500 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Eyes

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

This eyewear consists of two thick, gold-rimmed glass lenses encased in a leather band. When worn over the eyes, a pair of _[[items/Wondrous Item/Discerning Goggles|discerning goggles]]_ allows you to determine creatures' alignments with a single look. Good creatures appear to faintly glow blue and evil creatures to faintly glow red. Neutral-aligned creatures appear normal, as do creatures under the effect of an _[[spells/Undetectable Alignment|undetectable alignment]]_ spell or similar effect. These goggles function only during times when you normally would possess sight; for example, they do not work in _[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_ unless you possess _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 6750 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Detect Good|detect good]]_, _[[spells/Detect Evil|detect evil]]_
