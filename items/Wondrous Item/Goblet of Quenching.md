# Goblet of Quenching

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 180 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 1 **Aura:** faint conjuration

### Description

At your _[[spells/Command|command]]_, this simple _[[items/Mundane/Clay|clay]]_ goblet fills itself with cool, fresh drinking water. The goblet can create up to 2 gallons of water per day. The water evaporates normally if left exposed, and is completely normal water; you can store it, use it to create _[[items/Mundane/Holy water|holy water]]_, and so on.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 90 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Create Water|create water]]_

* Legacy of Fire Player's Guide
