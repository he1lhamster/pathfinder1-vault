# Clamor Box

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 2000 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate illusion

### Description

This delicate tin music box can produce a great volume of noise at a specific time determined by its owner. Four _[[spells/Command|command]]_ words determine what sorts of sounds it is to play (battle, fire, _[[spells/Massacre|massacre]]_, or riot), and a fifth determines the delay (anywhere from 1 round to 20 minutes) until the box begins to produce the desired sound. The noise from the box is as loud as 40 people. The sound persists for 2d6 rounds.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Ghost Sound|ghost sound]]_
