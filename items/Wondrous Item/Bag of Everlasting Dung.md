# Bag of Everlasting Dung

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 500 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint conjuration

### Description

This leather bag exudes a pungent _[[universal monster rules/Scent|scent]]_ when opened. Each day it provides enough dried dung to fuel a small fire for 8 hours or fertilize a 20-foot-square patch of land.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 250 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Create Food and Water|create food and water]]_
