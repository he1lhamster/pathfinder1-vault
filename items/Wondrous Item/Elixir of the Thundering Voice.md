# Elixir of the Thundering Voice

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 250 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

This light _[[monsters/Gray|gray]]_ elixir makes the user's voice fiercer and louder, and gives it an inhuman quality that rattles those he talks to, granting the user a +10 competence bonus on Intimidate checks for 1 hour.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 125 gp
