# Laurel Wreath

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 19000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Head

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint enchantment

### Description

This crown of laurel leaves inspires its wearer to achieve mighty deeds. She gains a +4 morale bonus on saving throws against mind-affecting effects. If she succeeds at a saving throw against a harmful mind-affecting effect, the surge of confidence gives her a +2 morale bonus on attack rolls, skill checks, and weapon damage rolls for 1 minute. If she fails such a saving throw, she is instead _[[conditions/Shaken|shaken]]_ for 1 minute, though this condition doesn't stack with other _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_ effects to make her _[[conditions/Frightened|frightened]]_ or _[[conditions/Panicked|panicked]]_.

A mythic creature wearing the wreath gains 2 temporary hit points per tier at the start of each day. These temporary hit points are restored whenever the wearer rests 8 hours or otherwise gains the equivalent of doing so.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 9500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, Mythic Crafter, false hope, _[[spells/Good Hope|good hope]]_
