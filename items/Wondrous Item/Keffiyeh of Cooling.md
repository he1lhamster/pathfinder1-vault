# Keffiyeh of Cooling

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 2100 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Head

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** faint abjuration

### Description

This keffiyeh remains bright white, no matter how many sandstorms it has been in. It protects you from hot environments as if you had _[[spells/Endure Elements|endure elements]]_ cast upon you. It provides no magical protection against cold environments.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1050 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _endure elements_
