# Mattock of the Titans

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 23348 gp **Weight:** 120 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x3 **Enhancement bonus:** +3 **Damage type:** B **Special material:** Adamantine

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 16 **Aura:** strong transmutation

### Description

This digging tool is 10 feet long. Any creature of at least Huge size can use it to loosen or tumble earth or earthen ramparts (a 10-foot cube every 10 minutes). It also smashes rock (a 10- foot cube per hour). If used as a weapon, it is the equivalent of a Gargantuan +3 adamantine _[[items/Weapon/Warhammer|warhammer]]_, dealing 4d6 points of base damage.
