# Dust of Darkness

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 600 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint evocation

### Description

This shimmering black dust causes one creature to be coated in a layer of _[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_ for 1 minute. In normal light, the creature gains concealment (20% miss chance). In dim light, the creature gains total concealment (50% miss chance). It has no effect in areas of bright light or _darkness_ (though in bright light, a viewer can make a DC 20 Perception check to notice the target is slightly shadowed). Creatures with _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ see through the dust's _darkness_ as if it were normal _darkness_. The dust dispels and counters the outlining effects of _[[spells/Glitterdust|glitterdust]]_, but doesn't affect the spell's _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Blinding|blinding]]_ effects. _Glitterdust_ dispels and counters the effects of the _[[items/Wondrous Item/Dust of Darkness|dust of darkness]]_.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 300 gp
