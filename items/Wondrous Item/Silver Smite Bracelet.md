# Silver Smite Bracelet

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 16000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Wrists

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This heavy silver bracelet is etched with icons of purity, fidelity, chastity, and honor, and glows with a soft white light whenever its owner prays. The wearer of this bracelet treats her _[[classes/Paladin|paladin]]_ level as 4 higher than normal for the purpose of her smite evil class feature.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 8000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Align Weapon|align weapon]]_, creator must be a _paladin_
