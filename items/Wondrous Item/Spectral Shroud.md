# Spectral Shroud

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 26000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Chest

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** moderate necromancy

### Description

This thin, bleached cloth covers the wearer's entire torso. Some also cover the wearer's face, but don't interfere with the wearer's _[[spells/Vision|vision]]_. The morbid burial shroud grants the wearer some affinity with the spectral dead. The wearer can discern _[[conditions/Invisible|invisible]]_ or ethereal creatures as though using _[[spells/See Invisibility|see invisibility]]_. Once per day, the wearer can become _[[universal monster rules/Incorporeal|incorporeal]]_ for 10 rounds and gain a fly speed equal to half his base speed with perfect maneuverability. The wearer can't attack while _incorporeal_, but can dismiss the effect as a move action.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 13000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Hide from Undead|hide from undead]]_, _see invisibility_
