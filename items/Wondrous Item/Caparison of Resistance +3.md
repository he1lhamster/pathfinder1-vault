# Caparison of Resistance +3

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 24300 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Shoulders

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint abjuration

### Description

This garment is intended for a _[[monsters/Horse|horse]]_ or similar quadruped, but it can change size and shape to fit any four-legged creature ranging from Small to Huge in size. When worn, a _[[items/Mundane/Caparison|caparison]]_ of _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ offers the wearer magic protection in the form of a +1 to +5 _resistance_ bonus on all saving throws (Fortitude, Reflex, and Will). This bonus on saving throws also applies to a single rider who is mounted on the creature (in the case of multiple riders, the _resistance_ bonus is not shared at all and applies only to the _[[spells/Mount|mount]]_).

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 12150 gp
