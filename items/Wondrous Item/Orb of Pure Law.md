# Orb of Pure Law

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 60000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

This featureless _[[monsters/Gray|gray]]_ stone is perfectly smooth and flawlessly polished. The bearer of an _[[items/Wondrous Item/Orb of Pure Law|orb of pure law]]_ gains a +4 _[[spells/Deflection|deflection]]_ bonus to AC against attacks from chaotic creatures and a +4 _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ bonus on saving throws against _[[spells/Polymorph|polymorph]]_ effects, effects with the chaotic descriptor, and effects that inflict the _[[conditions/Confused|confused]]_ condition.

When held in hand, the bearer of the orb may use _[[spells/Detect Chaos|detect chaos]]_ and _[[spells/Detect Law|detect law]]_ at will. A lawful creature may hold the orb in hand and _[[spells/Command|command]]_ it to invoke _[[spells/Dispel Chaos|dispel chaos]]_ and _[[spells/Arrow of Law|arrow of law]]_ each once per day.

The orb bestows one permanent negative level on any chaotic creature holding or carrying it. The negative level remains as long as the creature has the orb and disappears immediately when the orb is no longer held or carried. This negative level cannot be overcome in any way (including by _[[spells/Restoration|restoration]]_ spells) while the bearer has the orb.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 30000 gp
