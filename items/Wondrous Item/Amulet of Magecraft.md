# Amulet of Magecraft

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 20000 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Neck

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate universal

### Description

A universalist _[[classes/Wizard|wizard]]_ who selects the amulet as his bonded object may choose one school of spells each day when he prepares spells. He then can use the necklace to spontaneously convert any prepared _wizard_ spell of that school into any other _wizard_ spell of that school he knows— though the desired spell must be of the same level or lower than the prepared spell. For example, if the _wizard_ chooses evocation when he prepares his spells that morning, until the next time he prepares spells, he can spontaneously convert a prepared _[[spells/Fireball|fireball]]_ into any other evocation _wizard_ spell of 3rd level or lower that he knows.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 10000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Permanency|permanency]]_, creator must be a universalist
