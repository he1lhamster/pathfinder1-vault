# Shoes of Lightning Leaping

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 10500 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Feet

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 6 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

These short black shoes fit tightly around the wearer's feet and bear designs of leaping arcs of lightning and wild winds. Once per day on _[[spells/Command|command]]_, a creature wearing these boots can transform into lightning and move up to 50 feet in a straight line, re-materializing at the far end of the _[[spells/Lightning Bolt|lightning bolt]]_. Creatures in the path of the lightning take 6d6 points of electricity damage and objects take damage as per the _lightning bolt_ spell. If the lightning strikes an object or barrier and fails to burst through it, the creature wearing the boots appears next to the impacted object and is _[[conditions/Staggered|staggered]]_ for 1 round. Movement while in lightning form does not provoke attacks of opportunity.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 5250 gp
