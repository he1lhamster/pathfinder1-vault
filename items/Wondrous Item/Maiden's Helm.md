# Maiden's Helm

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 3500 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Head

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 6 **Aura:** moderate enchantment

### Description

A _[[items/Wondrous Item/Maiden's Helm|maiden's helm]]_ appears as the distinctive plate and plumed helm worn by the _[[monsters/Gray|Gray]]_ Maidens. The helm imparts enhanced power and gravitas to the wearer's voice, granting a +5 competence bonus on Intimidate checks. The wearer of a _maiden's helm_ can use _[[spells/Command|command]]_ three times per day as a spell-like ability.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1750 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Cause Fear|cause fear]]_, _command_
