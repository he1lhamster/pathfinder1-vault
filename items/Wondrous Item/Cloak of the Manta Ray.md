# Cloak of the Manta Ray

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 7200 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Shoulders

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This cloak appears to be made of leather until the wearer enters salt water. At that time, the _[[items/Wondrous Item/Cloak of the Manta Ray|cloak of the manta ray]]_ adheres to the individual, and he appears nearly identical to a manta ray (as the _[[spells/Beast Shape II|beast shape II]]_ spell, except that it allows only manta ray form). He gains a +3 natural armor bonus, the ability to breathe _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Underwater|underwater]]_, and a swim speed of 60 feet, like a real manta ray.

The cloak also allows the wearer to attack with a sting ray's tail spine, dealing 1d6 points of damage. This attack can be used in addition to any other attack the character has, using his highest melee attack bonus. The wearer can release his arms from the cloak without sacrificing _underwater_ movement if so desired.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 3600 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _beast shape II_, _[[universal monster rules/Water Breathing|water breathing]]_
