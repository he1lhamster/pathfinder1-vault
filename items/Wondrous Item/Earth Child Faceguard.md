# Earth Child Faceguard

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 10000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Head

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

Decorated with the emblems of the Earth Child cadre, an _[[items/Wondrous Item/Earth Child Faceguard|earth child faceguard]]_ is designed to refine the wearer's focus when fighting giants. The wearer gains the benefits of _[[feats/Earth Child Style|Earth Child Style]]_ (Pathfinder RPG Ultimate Combat 99), regardless of whether she meets the prerequisites. If the wearer does not possess the defensive _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Training|training]]_ racial trait, the _[[feats/Dodge|dodge]]_ bonus to AC against giants is reduced to +2. If the wearer already possesses the _Earth Child Style_ feat, she can enter the earth child stance as a free action.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 5000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _Earth Child Style_, _[[spells/Know The Enemy|know the enemy]]_
