# Bracelet of Second Chances

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 15750 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Wrists

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate conjuration

### Description

This bracelet is adorned with seven coral beads carved in intricate designs. When a critical hit or sneak attack is confirmed on the wearer, as an immediate action before damage is rolled, he can choose to convert the critical hit into a normal hit. Each time the wearer negates a critical hit or sneak attack in this manner, one bead shatters. When all seven are used up, the bracelet itself crumbles into _[[items/Mundane/Powder|powder]]_.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 7875 gp
