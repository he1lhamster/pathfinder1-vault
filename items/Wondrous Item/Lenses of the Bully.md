# Lenses of the Bully

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 12500 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Eyes

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint abjuration

### Description

Commonly used by thugs, these dark-tinted lenses make the wearer appear threatening. The wearer gains a +2 competence bonus on Intimidate checks. Intelligent creatures find the wearer hard to read; the wearer is treated as if affected by a constant _[[spells/Undetectable Alignment|undetectable alignment]]_ spell, making her true motives difficult to ascertain.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 6250 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _undetectable alignment_
