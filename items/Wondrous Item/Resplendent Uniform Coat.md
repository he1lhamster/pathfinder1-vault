# Resplendent Uniform Coat

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 7000 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Chest

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint enchantment

### Description

This woolen long coat is typically worn over light armor by military commanders. A _[[classes/Cavalier|cavalier]]_ with the tactician ability may use that ability one additional time per day, and his tactician ability affects allies within 60 feet instead of 30 feet.

**Crafting cost:** 3500 gp
