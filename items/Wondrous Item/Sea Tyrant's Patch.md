# Sea Tyrant's Patch

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 70000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Eyes

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 15 **Aura:** strong enchantment

### Description

This ornate silk _[[items/Mundane/Eye patch|eye patch]]_ is embroidered with gold thread and studded with pearls in a pattern representing a whirlpool. The wearer of this item, which is a favorite patch of ruthless or particularly driven pirate captains, is constantly affected as if subject to the _[[spells/Touch of the Sea|touch of the sea]]_ (Advanced Player's Guide) and _[[universal monster rules/Water Breathing|water breathing]]_ spells. Furthermore, once per day on _[[spells/Command|command]]_, the wearer can use mass _[[spells/Charm Monster|charm monster]]_, as per the spell.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 35000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, mass _charm monster_, _touch of the sea_, _water breathing_
