# Campfire Bead

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 720 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 1 **Aura:** faint evocation

### Description

This tiny glass bead transforms itself into a small (2-foot-tall) pile of _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Burning|burning]]_ logs whenever its _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word is spoken. The fire burns for 8 hours or until extinguished, at which point it turns back into a bead. The owner of the item must wait twice as long as the fire burned before he can order the bead to become a campfire again.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 360 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Produce Flame|produce flame]]_
