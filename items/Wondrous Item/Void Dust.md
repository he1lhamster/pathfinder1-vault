# Void Dust

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 4500 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 11 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

Infused with the essence of _[[spells/Destruction|destruction]]_, a pinch of _[[items/Wondrous Item/Void Dust|void dust]]_ unmakes force effects. If sprinkled on or thrown at a force effect, it acts as a _[[spells/Dispel Magic|dispel magic]]_, unless the force effect is immune to _dispel magic_, in which case it acts like _[[spells/Disintegrate|disintegrate]]_. _Void dust_ can be sprinkled on an adjacent force effect or thrown up to 10 feet. No attack roll is required. A single pinch affects only a single force effect. A found container holds three pinches.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 2250 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _disintegrate_
