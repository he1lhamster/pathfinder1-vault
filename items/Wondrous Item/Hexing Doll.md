# Hexing Doll

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 2500 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint enchantment

### Description

This malevolent-looking doll is made out of cheap rags and embroidery thread and is stuffed with straw or sawdust. A creature holding or carrying the doll takes a –4 penalty on saving throws against _[[classes/Witch|witch]]_ hexes. Alternatively, if the doll is stuffed with the hair of a creature, any _witch_ holding the doll increases the DC of her hexes by +2 when using the hexes against that creature. A doll used in this way does not cause the _witch_ to take a penalty on her saving throws.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1250 gp
