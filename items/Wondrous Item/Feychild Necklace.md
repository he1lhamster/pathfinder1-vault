# Feychild Necklace

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 6000 gp **Weight:** 0.5 lbs. **Slot:** Neck

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

This necklace is set with a bloodstone sculpted to look like a scintillating green eye. It gives the wearer a +4 competence bonus on Charisma checks and Charisma-based checks made to interact with gnomes, a +2 competence bonus on similar checks when dealing with beings of the fey creature type, and a –2 penalty on similar checks when interacting with anyone else. The wearer can read, speak, and understand the Gnome language. If the wearer is not a gnome, she gains _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_, and may cast _[[spells/Dancing Lights|dancing lights]]_, _[[spells/Ghost Sound|ghost sound]]_, _[[spells/Prestidigitation|prestidigitation]]_, and _[[spells/Speak with Animals|speak with animals]]_ each once per day as a spell-like ability.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 3000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, creator must be a gnome
