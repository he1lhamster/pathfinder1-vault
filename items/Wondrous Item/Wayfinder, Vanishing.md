# Wayfinder, Vanishing

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 8000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint illusion

### Description

Vanishing wayfinders are crafted of ivory chased with gold and silver. In addition to the usual functions of a _[[items/Wondrous Item/Wayfinder|wayfinder]]_ (including the ability to create light), the bearer of a vanishing _wayfinder_ can press a hidden catch as a standard action to become _[[conditions/Invisible|invisible]]_ (as the spell _[[spells/Invisibility|invisibility]]_) for as long as she concentrates, up to a maximum duration of 5 minutes per day. These minutes need not be consecutive, but each activation uses 1 minute of duration, even if the user becomes visible by making an attack or by some other means before that minute is up.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 4000 gp
