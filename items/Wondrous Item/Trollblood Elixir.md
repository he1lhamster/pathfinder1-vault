# Trollblood Elixir

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 4550 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 13 **Aura:** strong conjuration

### Description

This rancid, bubbling tonic is mud-colored and reeks of sweat, gore, and decay. Upon drinking a dose of _[[items/Wondrous Item/Trollblood Elixir|trollblood elixir]]_, the imbiber gains _[[universal monster rules/Fast Healing|fast healing]]_ 5. This _fast healing_ is even capable of reattaching a severed limb that is pressed against its stump for 1 round, as long as the limb has been severed for no more than 1 hour and is essentially intact. The effects of _trollblood elixir_ last 1 minute; during this time, the imbiber is _[[conditions/Nauseated|nauseated]]_. Each round, the imbiber can choose to prematurely end the elixir's effects by succeeding at a DC 10 Fortitude save as a full-round action. _Trollblood elixir_ does not affect objects or nonliving creatures such as constructs and undead.
