# Swarmbane Clasp

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 3000 gp **Weight:** 0.5 lbs. **Slot:** Neck

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate abjuration

### Description

An ancient fossilized insect lies trapped within this ornate amber clasp, impaled by the long golden pin that fastens the wearer's cloak. The wearer's weapons, unarmed attacks, and _[[universal monster rules/Natural Attacks|natural attacks]]_ deal full damage to swarms, regardless of the swarm's _[[universal monster rules/Immunity|immunity]]_ to weapon damage (if any, although _[[universal monster rules/Damage Reduction|damage reduction]]_ applies as normal). If the wearer is damaged by a swarm, she automatically succeeds on her saving throw against the swarm's _[[universal monster rules/Distraction|distraction]]_ ability.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Repel Vermin|repel vermin]]_
