# Book of the Loremaster

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 15000 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

This digest-sized book contains a seemingly random collection of words, phrases, and strange mnemonic aids. Three times each day, a _[[classes/Bard|bard]]_ can consult it while using the lore master class feature in order to gain a +5 competence bonus when taking 10 or taking 20 on a Knowledge check.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 7500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, clairaudience/clairvoyance
