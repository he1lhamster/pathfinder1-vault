# Potion of Poison

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 27000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Cursed**

**Caster level:** 12 **Aura:** strong conjuration

### Description

This potion has lost its beneficial abilities and become a _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Potent|potent]]_ poison. This poison deals 1d3 Constitution damage per round for 6 rounds. A poisoned creature can make a DC 14 Fortitude save each round to negate the damage and end the affliction.

### Crafting requirements

any potion
