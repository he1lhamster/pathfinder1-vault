# Belt of Thunderous Charging

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 10000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Belt

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

An engraving of a charging _[[monsters/Rhinoceros|rhinoceros]]_ decorates this thick leather belt. The belt grants its wearer a +2 enhancement bonus to Strength. Treat this as a temporary ability bonus for the first 24 hours the belt is worn.

The belt magnifies the wearer's momentum whenever she charges, granting her a +2 bonus on bull rush and overrun maneuvers. Furthermore, when the wearer makes a charge attack, her melee weapons and natural weapons deal damage as if they were one size category larger than they actually are.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 5000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Bull's Strength|bull's strength]]_, _[[spells/Lead Blades|lead blades]]_ (Advanced Player's Guide)
