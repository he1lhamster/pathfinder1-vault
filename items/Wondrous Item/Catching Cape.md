# Catching Cape

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 200 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Shoulders

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 1 **Aura:** faint abjuration

### Description

This silvery cape furls and ripples like a _[[items/Mundane/Flag|flag]]_ with every step its owner takes. As a swift action, the wearer can order the cape to transform into a faint sphere of force that surrounds him on all sides. The field grants the wearer concealment (20% miss chance) against ranged attacks and lasts for 1 minute or until the wearer is missed by a ranged attack due to the power of the cloak, whichever comes first. When the field is used, all magic is lost from the cloak, leaving only a mundane silvery garment.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 100 gp
