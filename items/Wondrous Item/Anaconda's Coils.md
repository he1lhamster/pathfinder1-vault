# Anaconda's Coils

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 18500 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Belt

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This snakeskin belt's buckle is shaped like a serpent's head. The wearer gains a +2 enhancement bonus to Strength and a +2 competence bonus on grapple combat maneuver checks. Treat the enhancement bonus to Strength as temporary ability bonus for the first 24 hours the belt is worn. In addition, the belt grants the wearer the _[[universal monster rules/Constrict|constrict]]_ ability (Bestiary 298) for 1d6 points of damage plus the wearer's Strength modifier.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 9250 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Beast Shape I|beast shape I]]_, _[[spells/Bull's Strength|bull's strength]]_
