# Cloak of Fangs

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 2800 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Shoulders

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

Wearing this animal-hair cloak grants a +1 _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ bonus on saving throws. Furthermore, the wearer can force its teeth to grow rapidly up to five times per day as a swift action. The oversized teeth last for 1 round, during which time the wearer can make a bite attack. Treat this attack as a primary natural attack that deals 1d6 points of damage (or 1d4 if the wearer is Small). If the wearer already has a bite attack, the damage of that bite attack increases by one step (Bestiary 302).

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1250 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Alter Self|alter self]]_, _resistance_
