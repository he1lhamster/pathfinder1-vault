# Tremor Bar (Kobold)

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 70000 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 15 **Aura:** strong evocation

### Description

The following wondrous item is generally available only to members of the indicated race or ethnicity.

This mithral _[[items/Mundane/Crowbar|crowbar]]_ can coax stone to shift aside with alarming ease. Five times per day, when a _[[monsters/Kobold|kobold]]_ wielder pries at a section of stone and speaks a _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word, the touched rock reshapes as if with _[[spells/Stone Shape|stone shape]]_.

Additionally, once per day a _kobold_ wielder can throw the _crowbar_ like a thrown splash weapon at a spot of earth, metal, or stone to unleash an _[[spells/Earthquake|earthquake]]_ on the spot where the _crowbar_ lands.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 35000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _earthquake_, _stone shape_, creator must be a _kobold_

* Inner Sea Races
