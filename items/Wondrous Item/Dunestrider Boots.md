# Dunestrider Boots

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 3000 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Feet

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

These boots are cracked and weathered from the desert sun. When you wear _[[items/Wondrous Item/Dunestrider Boots|dunestrider boots]]_, you may walk on sand as if it were hardpacked earth. Overland movement across sandy desert is treated as plains. Even loose sand and quicksand are not difficult terrain for you, allowing you to cross them at normal speed.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Expeditious Retreat|expeditious retreat]]_, _[[spells/Longstrider|longstrider]]_
