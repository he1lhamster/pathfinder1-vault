# Drums of Panic

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 30000 gp **Weight:** 10 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate necromancy

### Description

These drums are kettle drums (hemispheres about 1-1/2 feet in diameter on stands). They come in pairs and are unremarkable in appearance. If both of the pair are sounded, all creatures within 120 feet (with the exception of those within a 20-foot-radius safe zone around the drums) are affected as by a _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_ spell (Will DC 16 partial). _[[items/Wondrous Item/Drums of Panic|Drums of panic]]_ can be used once per day.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 15000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _fear_
