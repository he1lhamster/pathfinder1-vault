# Censer of Conjuring Air Elementals

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 90000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 13 **Aura:** moderate conjuration

### Description

This 6-inch-wide, perforated metallic vessel resembles a _[[items/Mundane/Thurible|thurible]]_ found in a place of worship. The _[[items/Mundane/Censer|censer]]_ must be filled with _[[items/Mundane/Incense|incense]]_ to function. On _[[spells/Command|command]]_ as a full-round action, the bearer may _[[universal monster rules/Summon|summon]]_ an air elemental. If the _censer_ is filled with _[[items/Wondrous Item/Incense of Meditation|incense of meditation]]_, it conjures a greater air elemental as if using _[[spells/Summon Monster VII|summon monster VII]]_. If filled with any other _incense_, it conjures a Large air elemental as if using _[[spells/Summon Monster V|summon monster V]]_. Conjuring an elemental consumes any normal _incense_ in the _censer_, or one-fourth of a block of _incense of meditation_. The _censer_ cannot _summon_ another elemental until the previous one is dismissed, killed, or the duration of its summoning expires.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 45000 gp
