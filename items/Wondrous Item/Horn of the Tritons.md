# Horn of the Tritons

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 15000 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate conjuration

### Description

This device is a large, intricately inscribed and decorated conch shell that can be blown once per day (except by a _[[monsters/Triton|triton]]_, who can sound it three times per day). Blowing the horn activates any one of the following functions:

* Calm rough waters in a 1-mile radius. Alternatively, this functions as a _[[spells/Dismissal|dismissal]]_ spell against a targeted water elemental (DC 19 negates).
* _[[universal monster rules/Summon|Summon]]_ 1d4+1 sharks or 1d3 advanced sharks as if using _[[spells/Summon Nature's Ally V|summon nature's ally V]]_.
* Create a _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_ effect (DC 16 negates) in a 500-foot radius that only affects creatures with the aquatic or _[[universal monster rules/Amphibious|amphibious]]_ subtype which have Intelligence scores of 1 or 2.
