# Crown of Swords

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 6000 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Head

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint evocation

### Description

This _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Radiant|radiant]]_ crown of steel is bedecked with miniature mithral swords. A _[[items/Wondrous Item/Crown of Swords|crown of swords]]_ can be used up to 10 times per day. When struck in combat, the wearer may spend one use as an immediate action to create a longsword-shaped _[[spells/Spiritual Weapon|spiritual weapon]]_ that then attacks her attacker. On the wearer's next turn, she may spend one additional use each round to continue attacking that target with the _spiritual weapon_; the _spiritual weapon_ cannot be redirected and disappears if the target is killed or moves out of range. Multiple spiritual weapons may be created (even attacking the same target) if the wearer is attacked in subsequent rounds.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 3000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _spiritual weapon_
