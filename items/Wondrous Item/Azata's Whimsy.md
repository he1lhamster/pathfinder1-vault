# Azata's Whimsy

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 8000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Neck

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate enchantment

### Description

Any time the wearer of an _[[items/Wondrous Item/Azata's Whimsy|azata's whimsy]]_ would become _[[conditions/Confused|confused]]_, her random actions are modified as follows:

* Act normally: No changes.
* _[[spells/Babble|Babble]]_: The wearer's babbling is beautiful and inspiring, functioning as a 7th-level _[[classes/Bard|bard]]_'s inspire courage bardic performance during each round this result is rolled.
* Damage self: The damage is nonlethal.
* Attack nearest creature: The wearer gains a +1 insight bonus on attack rolls against enemies and takes a –4 penalty against allies.
