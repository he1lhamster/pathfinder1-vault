# Beneficial Bandolier

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 1000 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Belt

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This _[[items/Mundane/Bandolier|bandolier]]_ is made of finely tanned leather. It has slots for up to 200 rounds of ammunition. Pellets and black _[[items/Mundane/Powder|powder]]_ are kept in tiny individual pouches, and bullets in small loops. The _bandolier_ alters itself as needed to accommodate both. There are also places on the _[[items/Wondrous Item/Beneficial Bandolier|beneficial bandolier]]_ for a _[[items/Ammunition/Gunsmith's kit|gunsmith's kit]]_ and a _[[items/Mundane/Powder horn|powder horn]]_. Regardless of what quantities of these items are placed within the _beneficial bandolier_, its weight does not change.

As a swift action, the wearer can _[[spells/Command|command]]_ a single round of ammunition from the _beneficial bandolier_ to teleport into a firearm of the appropriate type that he is wielding.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 500 gp
