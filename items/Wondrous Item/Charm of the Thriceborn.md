# Charm of the Thriceborn

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 2400 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

A creature carrying this knot-shaped charm for 3 full days becomes its owner until she loses _[[spells/Possession|possession]]_ of it. A creature can only be the owner of a single _[[items/Wondrous Item/Charm of the Thriceborn|charm of the thriceborn]]_. At the start of each day, the charm's owner rolls 3d20 and keeps their results as her charmed dice. Once during that day, instead of rolling a d20, the charm's owner can instead use the result of one of the three charmed dice, randomly selected (roll 1d3 to choose).

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1200 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Threefold Sight|threefold sight]]_
