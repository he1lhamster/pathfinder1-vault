# Teleporting Climbing Rig

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 7600 gp **Weight:** 7 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate conjuration

### Description

This mithral _[[items/Weapon/Grappling hook|grappling hook]]_ is decorated with runic engravings and attached to 50 feet of _[[items/Mundane/Silk rope|silk rope]]_, with the other end of the rope tied around a thick iron ring. Three times per day, the wielder of this item may speak the item's _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word while holding the iron ring. The _grappling hook_ then teleports to any designated location within 50 feet to which the user has both line of sight and line of effect. The _grappling hook_ does not teleport if the _silk rope_ is held at any point other than the iron ring. If the designated location does not possess a means for the rig's hook to _[[universal monster rules/Attach|attach]]_, the _grappling hook_ falls to the ground after teleporting.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 3800 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Dimension Door|dimension door]]_
