# Chaos Emerald

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 25000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 19 **Aura:** strong abjuration

### Description

This lustrous green gemstone is uncut and about the size of a human fist. The gem grants its bearer the use of the following abilities.

* _[[spells/Entropic Shield|entropic shield]]_ (1 charge)
* lesser _[[spells/Confusion|confusion]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Magic Circle against Law|magic circle against law]]_ (1 charge)
* _[[spells/Chaos Hammer|chaos hammer]]_ (2 charges)
