# Merciful Vambraces

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 8000 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Wrists

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate conjuration

### Description

These vambraces are constructed of polished steel. When a _[[classes/Paladin|paladin]]_ wears these vambraces, engravings of the holy symbol of a _paladin_'s deity and other religious iconography mystically appear upon the metal's surface. Once per day, when the _paladin_ uses her lay on hands ability, she can choose to apply an additional mercy to that use of lay on hands that she did not already select. She must be of sufficient level to use that mercy.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 4000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Cleanse|cleanse]]_ (Advanced Player's Guide)
