# Brazier of Conjuring Fire Elementals

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 90000 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 11 **Aura:** moderate conjuration

### Description

This device appears to be a normal container for holding _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Burning|burning]]_ coals. The brazier must be filled with coals or wood and a fire lit within it to function. On _[[spells/Command|command]]_ as a full-round action, the bearer may _[[universal monster rules/Summon|summon]]_ a fire elemental. If 1 pound of brimstone (sulfur) is _burning_ in the brazier, it conjures a Huge fire elemental as if using _[[spells/Summon Monster VI|summon monster VI]]_. Otherwise, the brazier conjures a Large fire elemental as if using _[[spells/Summon Monster V|summon monster V]]_. Conjuring an elemental extinguishes the fire in the brazier. The brazier cannot _summon_ another elemental until the previous one is dismissed, killed, or the duration of its summoning expires.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 45000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _summon monster V_, _summon monster VI_
