# Headband of Deathless Devotion

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 6400 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Headband

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 1 **Aura:** faint enchantment

### Description

This white headband, often painted or embroidered with calligraphy or a stylized pictogram such as a _[[spells/Sunburst|sunburst]]_, helps focus the wearer's resolve and absolute _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Determination|determination]]_ to survive and succeed. The wearer gains a +2 morale bonus on stabilization checks and on saving throws against ongoing effects with recurring saves, such as diseases, poisons, or compulsions such as _[[spells/Dominate Person|dominate person]]_ or _[[spells/Hold Person|hold person]]_.

If the wearer is a _[[classes/Samurai|samurai]]_, he treats his resolve ability as if he were two class levels higher.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 3200 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Heroism|heroism]]_, stabilize
