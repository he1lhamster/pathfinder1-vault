# Amulet of Water Parting

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 10000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Neck

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 11 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This _[[items/Wondrous Item/Amulet of Natural Armor +2|amulet of natural armor +2]]_ is carved from blackwood and often takes abstract artistic forms that incorporate motifs of waves or moving water. When worn, an _[[items/Wondrous Item/Amulet of Water Parting|amulet of water parting]]_ confers additional protection from damaging water effects, providing a +2 _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ bonus on saving throws against spells or effects with the water descriptor as well as a +2 _[[spells/Deflection|deflection]]_ bonus to AC against any such spells or effects that require an attack roll.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 5000 gp
