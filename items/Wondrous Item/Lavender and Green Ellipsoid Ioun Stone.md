# Lavender and Green Ellipsoid Ioun Stone

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 40000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 12 **Aura:** strong abjuration

### Description

Absorbs spells of 8th level or lower. After absorbing 50 spell levels, the stone burns out and turns dull _[[monsters/Gray|gray]]_, forever useless.

**Resonance:** If the bearer is holding the _[[items/Wondrous Item/Wayfinder|wayfinder]]_, he can use the stone's absorption ability as an immediate action instead of a readied action.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 20000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, creator must be 12th level
