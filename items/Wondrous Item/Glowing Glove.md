# Glowing Glove

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 2000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Hands

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 1 **Aura:** faint universal

### Description

The wearer of this slender white leather glove may, as a standard action, press it against any surface or object and cause a luminous handprint to appear. This print glows any color the wearer wishes, sheds light as a _[[items/Mundane/Candle|candle]]_, and is easily visible from a distance of up to 60 feet. Such handprints last for 1 day before fading away. The glowing handprint matches the wearer's hand in terms of size, position of fingers, and so on.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1000 gp
