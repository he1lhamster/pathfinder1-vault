# Monk's Robe

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 13000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Body

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

When worn, this simple brown robe confers great ability in unarmed combat. If the wearer has levels in _[[classes/Monk|monk]]_, her AC and unarmed damage are treated as a _monk_ of 5 levels higher. If donned by a character with the _[[feats/Stunning Fist|Stunning Fist]]_ feat, the robe lets her make one additional stunning attack per day. If the character is not a _monk_, she gains the AC and unarmed damage of a 5th-level _monk_ (although she does not add her Wisdom bonus to her AC). This AC bonus functions just like the _monk_'s AC bonus.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 6500 gp
