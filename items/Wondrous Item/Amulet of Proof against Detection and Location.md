# Amulet of Proof against Detection and Location

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 35000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Neck

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate abjuration

### Description

This silver amulet protects the wearer from _[[spells/Scrying|scrying]]_ and magical location just as a _[[spells/Nondetection|nondetection]]_ spell does. If a _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_ spell is attempted against the wearer, the caster of the _divination_ must succeed on a caster level check (1d20 + caster level) against a DC of 19 (as if the wearer had cast _nondetection_ on herself).

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 17500 gp
