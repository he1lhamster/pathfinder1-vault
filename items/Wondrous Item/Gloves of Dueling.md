# Gloves of Dueling

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 15000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Hands

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

These supple gloves grant the wearer a +4 bonus to her CMD against disarm attacks, attempts to sunder her wielded weapons, and effects that cause her to lose her grip on her weapons (such as _[[spells/Grease|grease]]_). The wearer doesn't drop held weapons when _[[conditions/Panicked|panicked]]_ or _[[conditions/Stunned|stunned]]_. If the wearer has the weapon _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Training|training]]_ class feature and is using an appropriate weapon, her weapon _training_ bonus increases by +2.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 7500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, greater _[[spells/Magic Weapon|magic weapon]]_
