# Wind Fan

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 5500 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint evocation

### Description

A _[[items/Wondrous Item/Wind Fan|wind fan]]_ appears to be nothing more than a wood and papyrus or cloth instrument with which to create a cooling _[[spells/Breeze|breeze]]_. By uttering the _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word, its possessor causes the fan to duplicate a _[[spells/Gust Of Wind|gust of wind]]_ spell. The fan can be used once per day with no risk. If it is used more frequently, there is a 20% cumulative chance per usage during that day that the device tears into useless, nonmagical tatters.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 2750 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _gust of wind_
