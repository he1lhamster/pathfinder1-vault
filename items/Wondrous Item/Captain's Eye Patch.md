# Captain's Eye Patch

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 46000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Eyes

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 11 **Aura:** moderate _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

This black velvet _[[items/Mundane/Eye patch|eye patch]]_ is attached to an onyx chain that is worn around the head. The _eye patch_ grants its wearer _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ with a range of 60 feet. Additionally, as a standard action, the wearer can speak the _eye patch_'s _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word to see through powerful magical _[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_, such as an area of _[[spells/Deeper Darkness|deeper darkness]]_, for up to 11 minutes per day. This duration need not be used all at once, but it must be used in 1-minute increments. Ending this effect is a free action.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 23000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _darkvision_, _[[spells/True Seeing|true seeing]]_
