# Vial of Reckless Courage

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 5000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

This small glass _[[items/Mundane/Vial|vial]]_ contains an effervescent blue liquid. When imbibed, a _[[items/Wondrous Item/Vial of Reckless Courage|vial of reckless courage]]_ instills a sense of bravery in the drinker, granting a +4 enhancement bonus on saving throws against _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_ effects. If the drinker is under a _fear_ effect, she can attempt a new saving throw with a +2 bonus. The drinker becomes eager to rush into danger, and as a result, all of her modes of movement (including _[[universal monster rules/Burrow|burrow]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Climb|climb]]_, fly, and swim speeds) increase by 30 feet, to a maximum of double her normal speed in any given form of movement, for 5 rounds.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 2500 gp
