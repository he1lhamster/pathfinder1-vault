# Dyso's Travel Canopy

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 750 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** faint evocation

### Description

This 5-foot-square cloth canopy is brightly colored with gold fringe around the edges, something a slightly foppish sultan might use while resting on the seashore. When you speak the _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word, the canopy unfolds and floats 3 feet above your head, positioning itself to keep you shaded. The travel canopy cannot hold any weight. It reverts to its 1-foot-square folded form at your _command_, or automatically when it is _[[feats/Out Of The Sun|out of the sun]]_ for more than 1 minute.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 375 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Floating Disk|floating disk]]_
