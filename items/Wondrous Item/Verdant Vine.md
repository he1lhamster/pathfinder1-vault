# Verdant Vine

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 6000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Wrists

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint conjuration

### Description

Consisting of a vine with five green berries, a _[[items/Wondrous Item/Verdant Vine|verdant vine]]_ is worn tied around the wrist. Once per day on _[[spells/Command|command]]_, the wearer can pinch a berry off the vine, and throw it up to 30 feet away, like a splash weapon. Viciously thorny vines erupt in a 20-foot radius from the point of _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Impact|impact]]_, even if the berry misses its target. Creatures within the area must succeed at a DC 16 Reflex saving throw or become _[[conditions/Entangled|entangled]]_, as the spell _[[spells/Entangle|entangle]]_, as the vines coil around the creature's legs. The vines persist, but at the start of the affected creature's turn, it gains a new saving throw against the effect. If the affected creature fails the saving throw, it also takes 1d4 points of damage.

A DC 20 Strength check, made as a standard action, allows an affected creature to break free and end the effect. Failing the check causes the vines to _[[universal monster rules/Constrict|constrict]]_, dealing 1d4 points of damage.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 3000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _entangle_, _[[spells/Plant Growth|plant growth]]_
