# Druid's Vestment

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 3750 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Body

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This light garment is worn over normal clothing or armor. Most such vestments are green, embroidered with elaborate plant or animal motifs. When this item is worn by a character with the wild shape ability, the character can use that ability one additional time each day.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1375 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Polymorph|polymorph]]_ or wild shape ability
