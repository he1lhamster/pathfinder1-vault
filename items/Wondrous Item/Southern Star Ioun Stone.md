# Southern Star Ioun Stone

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 8000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 12 **Aura:** strong transmutation

### Description

This brilliant, deep violet crystal is perfectly cut in the shape of a five-pointed star. The gem grants its bearer a swim speed of 30 feet. When inserted into a _[[items/Wondrous Item/Wayfinder|wayfinder]]_, it allows the bearer to breathe water for up to 10 minutes per day (in 1-minute increments).

**_[[spells/Revelation|Revelation]]_:** Can breath water for 10 minutes a day.

**Crafting cost:** 4000 gp
