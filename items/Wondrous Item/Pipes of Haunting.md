# Pipes of Haunting

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 6000 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 4 **Aura:** faint necromancy

### Description

This magic item appears to be a small set of pan pipes. When played by a person who succeeds on a DC 15 Perform (wind instruments) check, the pipes create an eerie, spellbinding tune. Those within 30 feet who hear the tune must succeed at a DC 13 Will save or become _[[conditions/Frightened|frightened]]_ for 4 rounds. Creatures with 6 or more Hit Dice are unaffected. _[[items/Wondrous Item/Pipes of Haunting|Pipes of haunting]]_ can be sounded twice a day.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 3000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Scare|scare]]_
