# Miser's Mask

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 3000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Head

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 1 **Aura:** faint _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

This wooden _[[items/Mundane/Mask|mask]]_ depicts the sunken face of a miser with an upturned nose. The _mask_ grants the wearer the _[[universal monster rules/Scent|scent]]_ ability (Bestiary 304), but she can use it only to sense coins and gems. Each type and quality of gem has its own unique aura to the wearer, granting her a +5 competence bonus on Appraise checks made to ascertain the value of gems.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Bloodhound|bloodhound]]_ (Advanced Player's Guide), the creator must have 4 ranks in Appraise
