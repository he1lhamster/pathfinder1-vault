# Monocle of Unveiled Auras

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 12700 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Eyes

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

The wearer of this crystal monocle gains the ability to use _[[spells/Detect Chaos|detect chaos]]_, _[[spells/Detect Evil|detect evil]]_, _[[spells/Detect Good|detect good]]_, _[[spells/Detect Magic|detect magic]]_, or _[[spells/Detect Law|detect law]]_, each at will for as long as he concentrates, but only one at a time. Any caster level checks to penetrate magical protections receive a +5 bonus, and use the higher of the item's caster level or the wearer's character level. The wearer can _[[spells/Expend|expend]]_ one use of mythic power to gain the benefits of _[[spells/Arcane Sight|arcane sight]]_ and _[[spells/See Invisibility|see invisibility]]_ for 1 hour, or until the monocle is removed.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 6350 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, Mythic Crafter, _arcane sight_, _see invisibility_, and any one of the following: _detect chaos_, _detect evil_, _detect good_, _detect law_, or _detect magic_
