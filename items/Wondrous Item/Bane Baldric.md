# Bane Baldric

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 10000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Chest

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate conjuration

### Description

This ornate sash of embroidered velvet stretches across the chest from shoulder to waist. If the wearer is an _[[classes/Inquisitor|inquisitor]]_, she is treated as five levels higher when using her _[[spells/Bane|bane]]_ and greater _bane_ abilities. If the wearer is not an _inquisitor_, she gains the _bane_ ability of a 5th-level _inquisitor_, but must first attune a light or one-handed melee weapon to the baldric by hanging it from the cloth for 24 hours, and can only use the _bane_ ability with the attuned weapon. Attuning a new weapon to the baldric ends the attunement for the previous weapon.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 5000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Summon Monster I|summon monster I]]_, creator must be an _inquisitor_
