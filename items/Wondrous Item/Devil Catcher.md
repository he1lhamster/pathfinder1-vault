# Devil Catcher

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 30320 gp **Weight:** 6 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate abjuration, moderate _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

This +1 _[[items/Mundane/Net|net]]_ is made from fine silvery cords rather than simple rope. The _net_ prevents extradimensional travel, affecting any creature _[[conditions/Entangled|entangled]]_ by it as per _[[spells/Dimensional Anchor|dimensional anchor]]_ for as long as it remains _entangled_. Additionally, any _[[spells/Invisibility|invisibility]]_ effects targeting a creature _entangled_ by the _net_ are suppressed so long as it remains _entangled_. Devils take a –4 penalty on Strength checks and Escape Artist checks to free themselves from a _[[items/Wondrous Item/Devil Catcher|devil catcher]]_.
