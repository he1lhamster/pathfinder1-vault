# Stormlord's Helm

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 35000 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Head

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** strong transmutation

### Description

Two large horns jut from the sides of this iron helm, each of them carved with runes representing storms, thunder, and lightning. The runes give the wearer the ability to control the elements and bring forth a great storm. Once per day, the wearer can call upon the magic of the helm to call a thunderstorm, as if casting the _[[spells/Control Weather|control weather]]_ spell. Like that spell, it takes 10 minutes to call the weather and an additional 10 minutes for the thunderstorm to manifest. Once it does, the wearer can call lighting (as the spell) for 10 minutes or until the wearer dismisses the thunderstorm with a standard action.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 17500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Call Lightning|call lightning]]_, _control weather_
