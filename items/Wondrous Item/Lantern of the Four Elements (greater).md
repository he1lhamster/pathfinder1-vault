# Lantern of the Four Elements (greater)

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 6500 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

This bronze lantern has a square base and four glass panels, each of which represents one of the fundamental elements in the color, shape, and quality of the light that passes through it. The lantern shines light as per the light spell, and as long as the bearer carries it the lantern protects her from _[[universal monster rules/Heat|heat]]_ and cold as per _[[spells/Endure Elements|endure elements]]_. (The bearer can turn the lantern's light off or on as a standard action.)

In addition, while carrying the lantern, the bearer gains a +2 competence bonus on Spellcraft checks to _[[spells/Identify|identify]]_ spells with the air, earth, fire, or water descriptor and a +2 competence bonus on Knowledge (planes) checks to _identify_ creatures that are native to the Elemental Planes.

The greater version of this item also allows the bearer to use certain _[[universal monster rules/Spell-Like Abilities|spell-like abilities]]_. As a standard action, the bearer of a greater lantern of the four elements can press one of the lantern's four panels to produce an effect: _[[spells/Levitate|levitate]]_ for the air panel, _[[spells/Resist Energy|resist energy]]_ (fire only) for the fire panel, _[[spells/Air Bubble|air bubble]]_ for the water panel, and _[[spells/Spider Climb|spider climb]]_ for the earth panel. Each panel can grant its effect only once per day.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 3250 gp
