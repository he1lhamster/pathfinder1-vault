# Eye Orb

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 80000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

If a blind creature holds this orb, that creature can see with _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ with a range of 120 feet, but also gains _[[universal monster rules/Light Sensitivity|light sensitivity]]_ (Pathfinder RPG Bestiary 301). If a sighted creature holds this orb and holds his eyes shut for 1 minute, he gains the _[[conditions/Blinded|blinded]]_ condition until he opens his eyes (as a free action), but can see through the orb as if he were a blind creature.

By expending one use of mythic power, a creature using the _[[items/Wondrous Item/Eye Orb|eye orb]]_ can see as though using _[[spells/True Seeing|true seeing]]_, and loses _light sensitivity_. These effects last for 1 minute or until he stops using the orb to see, whichever comes first.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 40000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, Mythic Crafter, _darkvision_, _true seeing_
