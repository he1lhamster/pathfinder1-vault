# Poisoner's Jacket (Lesser)

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 12000 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Chest

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint conjuration, faint necromancy

### Description

Three times per day, the wearer of this dark jacket can create a _[[items/Mundane/Vial|vial]]_ containing one dose of any poison from the Core Rulebook that costs 300 gp or less. The jacket's poison always looks like a murky amber fluid. This poison becomes inert after 1 hour.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 6000 gp
