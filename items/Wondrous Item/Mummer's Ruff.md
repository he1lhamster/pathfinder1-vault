# Mummer's Ruff

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 3500 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Neck

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint illusion

### Description

Usually fashioned from brightly colored silk, a _[[items/Wondrous Item/Mummer's Ruff|mummer's ruff]]_ is a favored accoutrement of performers and con artists alike. The wearer of a _mummer's ruff_ gains a +10 bonus on Bluff skill checks when attempting to imitate another's voice. In addition, once per day, the wearer of the _mummer's ruff_ can throw his voice as if using _[[spells/Ventriloquism|ventriloquism]]_ for up to 5 minutes.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1750 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Sculpt Sound|sculpt sound]]_, _ventriloquism_
