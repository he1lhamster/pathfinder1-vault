# Bookplate of Recall

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 1000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 13 **Aura:** strong conjuration

### Description

This metal bookplate is inscribed with mystical words in Draconic, leaving space for a single written name. When the bookplate is glued to the inside cover of a book, the named individual may speak the title of the book to _[[universal monster rules/Summon|summon]]_ it as if using _[[spells/Instant Summons|instant summons]]_. This ability functions once per day.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _instant summons_
