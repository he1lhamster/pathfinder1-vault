# Anchoring Belt

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 60000 gp **Weight:** 10 lbs. **Slot:** Belt

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate abjuration

### Description

This belt looks like nothing more than iron chains connecting large pieces of slate. It grants its wearer a +5 inherent bonus to combat maneuver defense against bull rush, drag, grapple, overrun, reposition, and _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_ combat maneuvers and a +5 inherent bonus on saving throws against any effect that attempts to move the wearer with physical force or energy. Furthermore, if the wearer is a mythic creature and targeted by an effect that would teleport her, she can choose whether or not she wishes to be teleported.

As a standard action, the wearer can _[[spells/Expend|expend]]_ one use of mythic power to create a 30-foot-radius aura that affects all enemy creatures within it as though they were subject to a _[[spells/Dimensional Anchor|dimensional anchor]]_ spell. This aura lasts for a number of rounds equal to the wearer's mythic tier.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 30000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, Mythic Crafter, _[[spells/Bull's Strength|bull's strength]]_, _dimensional anchor_
