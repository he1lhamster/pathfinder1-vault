# Sash of Flowing Water

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 25000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Belt

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate abjuration

### Description

If this belt's wearer has at least one hand free (holding nothing), he can attempt to deflect a successful melee attack with an immediate action. To do so, he rolls an unarmed strike against the opponent, including any applicable modifiers such as a favored enemy bonus or the penalty for _[[feats/Power Attack|Power Attack]]_. If his attack roll is greater than the opponent's attack roll, the attack is negated without effect. The unarmed strike itself causes no damage and does not count as a successful attack against the opponent. The opponent does not need to be within the wearer's reach to use this ability. Whether the attack succeeds or not, the wearer takes a –4 penalty on attack rolls until the end of his next turn.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 12500 gp
