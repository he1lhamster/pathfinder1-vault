# Mountainshaper's Girdle

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 25000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Belt

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This wide belt grants the wearer a +4 enhancement bonus to Strength. In addition, the wearer can spend 1 ki point to use _[[spells/Stone Shape|stone shape]]_. When he uses this ability, he can instead choose to shape metal, but he can affect only one-tenth as much material. To shape a usable weapon out of metal or stone, the wearer must succeed at a Craft (weapons) check. Items created in this way can never be of masterwork quality.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 12500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Bear's Endurance|bear's endurance]]_, _stone shape_
