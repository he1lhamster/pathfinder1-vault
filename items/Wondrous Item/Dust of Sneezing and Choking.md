# Dust of Sneezing and Choking

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 1800 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Cursed**

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate conjuration

### Description

This fine dust appears to be _[[items/Wondrous Item/Dust of Appearance|dust of appearance]]_. If cast into the air, it causes those within a 20-foot spread to fall into fits of sneezing and coughing. Those who fail a DC 15 Fortitude save take 3d6 points of Constitution damage immediately. Those who succeed on this saving throw are nonetheless _[[conditions/Disabled|disabled]]_ by choking (treat as _[[conditions/Stunned|stunned]]_) for 5d4 rounds.
