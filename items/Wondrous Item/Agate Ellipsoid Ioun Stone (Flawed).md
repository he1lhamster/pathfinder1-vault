# Agate Ellipsoid Ioun Stone (Flawed)

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 800 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 12 **Aura:** strong _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

This stone functions as a normal _[[items/Wondrous Item/Agate Ellipsoid Ioun Stone|agate ellipsoid ioun stone]]_, but you lose any abilities to reroll ability checks, skill checks, or saving throws for 24 hours after equipping it.

**Resonance:** Increases the caster level of _divination_ spells by 1.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 400 gp
