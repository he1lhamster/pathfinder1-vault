# Vambraces of Defense

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 6000 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Wrists

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint abjuration

### Description

These polished steel vambraces grant a +1 _[[spells/Deflection|deflection]]_ bonus to AC. Once per day, as an immediate action, if the wearer has at least one hand free, when he would normally be hit with an attack from a ranged weapon, he may deflect it with the bracers, taking no damage from it, as if he had the _[[feats/Deflect Arrows|Deflect Arrows]]_ feat.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 3000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_
