# Poisoner's Jacket (Greater)

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 58000 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Chest

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 15 **Aura:** strong conjuration, strong necromancy

### Description

This jacket has the abilities of a lesser poisoner's jacket, and once per day the wearer can create a _[[items/Mundane/Vial|vial]]_ that reproduces the effect of any poison in the Core Rulebook that costs 4,000 gp or less. The jacket's poison always looks like a murky lime fluid. This poison becomes inert after 1 hour.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 29000 gp
