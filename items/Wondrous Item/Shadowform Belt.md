# Shadowform Belt

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 110000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Belt

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 17 **Aura:** strong transmutation

### Description

When wrapped around a wearer's waist, this belt grants a +6 enhancement bonus to Dexterity. Treat this as a temporary ability bonus for the first 24 hours the belt is worn. Furthermore, as a swift action, its wearer can become _[[universal monster rules/Incorporeal|incorporeal]]_. While _incorporeal_, he does not gain a _[[spells/Deflection|deflection]]_ bonus to AC based on his Charisma, but his natural armor bonus is converted to _deflection_ bonus. The wearer can remain _incorporeal_ for up to 10 rounds per day. The rounds need not be consecutive.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 55000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Cat's Grace|cat's grace]]_, _[[spells/Etherealness|etherealness]]_
