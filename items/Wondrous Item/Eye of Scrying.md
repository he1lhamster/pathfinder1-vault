# Eye of Scrying

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 6000 gp **Weight:** 7 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

This bloodshot eye is larger than a grown man's fist. Its vivid green iris suggests strange depths, and is unnerving to look at for too long. Once per day you may use it to cast clairaudiance/clairvoyance. If you can cast _[[spells/Scrying|scrying]]_, you may use the eye as your focus object for the spell.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 3000 gp
