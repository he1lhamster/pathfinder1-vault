# Inquisitor's Bastion Vambraces

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 4000 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Wrists

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint abjuration

### Description

Constructed of blackened steel, these vambraces augment the protection granted to the _[[classes/Inquisitor|inquisitor]]_ by the protection judgment. While under that judgment's effects, the _inquisitor_ gains a _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ bonus both to CMD and on checks made to cast spells defensively. This bonus is equal to the _sacred_ bonus the judgement grants to AC.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 2000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Shield Of Faith|shield of faith]]_, creator must be an _inquisitor_ with access to the protection judgment
