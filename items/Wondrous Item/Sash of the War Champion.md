# Sash of the War Champion

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 4000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Chest

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate abjuration

### Description

This bright red strip of cloth, stitched with images of a cheering crowd _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Throwing|throwing]]_ garlands toward a chariot, fits across the wearer's shoulders and then diagonally down his chest to reach his opposite hip. The wearer treats his _[[classes/Fighter|fighter]]_ level as 4 higher than normal for the purpose of the armor _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Training|training]]_ and bravery class features.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 2000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Cat's Grace|cat's grace]]_, _[[spells/Remove Fear|remove fear]]_
