# Erinyes Company Cloak

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 22000 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Shoulders

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This crimson cloak is trimmed with feathers taken from erinyes devils. Three times per day on _[[spells/Command|command]]_, the cloak transforms into a pair of black-feathered wings that grant the wearer a fly speed of 30 feet (average maneuverability) for 10 minutes. If the wearer is wearing _[[items/Armor/Gray maiden plate|Gray Maiden plate]]_, its armor check penalty does not apply to the wearer's Fly checks. In addition, the wearer gains a +2 _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ bonus on all saving throws.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 11000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, fly, _resistance_
