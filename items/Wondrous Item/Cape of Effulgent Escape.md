# Cape of Effulgent Escape

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 14000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Shoulders

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint evocation, faint illusion

### Description

This shining white cloak is trimmed with golden sunbursts and continuously radiates light (as per the light spell, but only in a 10-foot radius). Twice per day, the wearer can _[[spells/Command|command]]_ the cape to erupt in a burst of light, _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Blinding|blinding]]_ all creatures other than the wearer within 20 feet for 1d4 rounds (Reflex DC 14 negates). After triggering this burst, the wearer becomes _[[conditions/Invisible|invisible]]_ (as the _[[spells/Invisibility|invisibility]]_ spell, with a duration of 1 minute) and the cape's light is extinguished as long as the wearer is _invisible_.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 7000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Daylight|daylight]]_, _invisibility_
