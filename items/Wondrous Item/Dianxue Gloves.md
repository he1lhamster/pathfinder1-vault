# Dianxue Gloves

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 3000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Hands

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

Thought to have been developed by disciples of Soong Bai, the famous promulgator of acupuncture, these gloves grant their wearer powerful skills in the arts of healing—and harming—living beings. The wearer of a pair of _[[items/Wondrous Item/Dianxue Gloves|dianxue gloves]]_ gains a +4 competence bonus on _[[spells/Heal|Heal]]_ checks to provide first aid, long-term care, and to treat _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Deadly|deadly]]_ wounds for living, humanoid creatures. In addition, the wearer gains a +2 competence bonus on rolls to confirm critical hits with unarmed strikes or with natural weapons against living humanoid creatures.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Status|status]]_, _[[spells/True Strike|true strike]]_, creator must have 5 ranks in _Heal_
