# Cauldron of Plenty

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 15000 gp **Weight:** 25 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 12 **Aura:** strong conjuration

### Description

This enchanted iron pot can produce nourishing, simple fare sufficient to feed up to 36 people per day upon _[[spells/Command|command]]_. Once per week it can also be commanded to create a _[[spells/Heroes' Feast|heroes' feast]]_ for up to 12 people. The _[[items/Mundane/Cauldron|cauldron]]_ needs neither fire nor ingredients to produce food; it does so instantly upon _command_.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 7500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Create Food and Water|create food and water]]_, _heroes' feast_
