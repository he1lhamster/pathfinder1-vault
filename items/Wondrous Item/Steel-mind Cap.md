# Steel-mind Cap

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 33600 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Head

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate abjuration

### Description

Ruined scraps of various constructs were repurposed and combined to form this ornate iron helmet. Though the helmet is not intelligent, it has a brute mechanical _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Cunning|cunning]]_ that allows it to assert control whenever the wearer's mind is overwhelmed. The helmet takes control of the wearer's body anytime he is _[[conditions/Dazed|dazed]]_ or _[[conditions/Stunned|stunned]]_. Whenever the wearer would be _dazed_, he is instead _[[conditions/Confused|confused]]_. Whenever the wearer would be _stunned_, he is instead _confused_ and _[[conditions/Staggered|staggered]]_.

Time spent under the cap's influence counts against the duration of the _[[spells/Daze|daze]]_ or stun effect. The cap functions for up to 5 rounds per day. These rounds do not need to be consecutive. The cap stops asserting control over its wearer when the duration of the _daze_ or stun effect ends, or when the cap's 5 rounds of duration are expended.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 16800 gp
