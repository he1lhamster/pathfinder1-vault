# Amulet of Spell Cunning

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 10000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Neck

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This silver locket only has power when used as a _[[classes/Wizard|wizard]]_'s bonded object. It allows a _wizard_ to prepare an additional 3 levels of spells per day.

**Crafting cost:** 5000 gp
