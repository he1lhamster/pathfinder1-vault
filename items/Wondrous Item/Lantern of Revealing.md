# Lantern of Revealing

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 30000 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint evocation

### Description

This lantern operates as a normal _[[items/Mundane/Hooded lantern|hooded lantern]]_. While it is lit, it also reveals all _[[conditions/Invisible|invisible]]_ creatures and objects within 25 feet of it, just like the spell _[[spells/Invisibility Purge|invisibility purge]]_.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 15000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _invisibility purge_
