# Shoes of the Firewalker

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 21000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Feet

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 6 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

These low-cut shoes of polished red leather feature golden eyelets and stitching in a fiery pattern. They grant the wearer fire _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ 10 and allow the wearer to walk atop lava and magma as if subject to the _[[spells/Water Walk|water walk]]_ spell.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 10500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Resist Energy|resist energy]]_, _water walk_
