# Dust of Hex Hiding

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 250 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint illusion

### Description

This translucent dust warps harmful magical auras, making it difficult to discern cursed items from beneficial ones. Sprinkling the dust over a cursed magic item shields it from _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_ magic for 5 days. When identifying the item, a creature must exceed the item's DC by 15 (instead of by 10) to determine whether the item is cursed. If a creature activates the curse while the item is under this effect, treat the curse's caster level as 2 higher for the purpose of resisting spells such as _[[spells/Remove Curse|remove curse]]_ and _[[spells/Break Enchantment|break enchantment]]_.

The dust can also be scattered over a magical glyph, rune, or symbol to increase the DC to find and disable the trap by 5 for 5 days.
