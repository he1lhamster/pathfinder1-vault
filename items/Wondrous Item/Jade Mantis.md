# Jade Mantis

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 9450 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 11 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

A tiny emerald statuette, the _[[items/Wondrous Item/Jade Mantis|jade mantis]]_ is part of the broader collection of figurines of wondrous power (often referred to across Golarion as jeweled life). When activated, the statuette transforms into a giant praying mantis under your _[[spells/Command|command]]_. You may call upon it three times per week for up to 1 hour per use.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 4725 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Animate Objects|animate objects]]_
