# Liberator's Key

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 2000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

This tiny, unremarkable silver key can open nearly any set of shackles. When the key is inserted as a standard action into the _[[spells/Keyhole|keyhole]]_ of locked leg irons, manacles, or shackles, the lock unlocks provided that its Disable Device DC is 30 or lower. Shackles whose lock has a Disable Device DC of 15 or lower are instantly and silently _[[conditions/Broken|broken]]_ into fragments.

A _[[items/Wondrous Item/Liberator's Key|liberator's key]]_ can be used 10 times, after which it crumbles to dust.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Knock|knock]]_, _[[spells/Shatter|shatter]]_
