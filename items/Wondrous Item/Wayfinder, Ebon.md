# Wayfinder, Ebon

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 18000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint evocation, faint transmutation

### Description

Favored by Pathfinders exploring dark and dangerous locales, the exterior of this _[[items/Wondrous Item/Wayfinder|wayfinder]]_ is constructed of panels of darkwood with ebony inlays. The panels slide apart to reveal platinum meshwork capable of holding one or two ioun stones. An ebon _wayfinder_ grants the bearer _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ with a range of 60 feet, and with a _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word it can create _[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_ (as per the spell) once per day, centered on itself.

When one ioun stone is slotted into the device, its _darkness_ ability no longer functions. When a second stone is placed in it, the _darkvision_ ability no longer functions.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 9000 gp
