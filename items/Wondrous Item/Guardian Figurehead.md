# Guardian Figurehead

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 1350 gp **Weight:** 50 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint abjuration

### Description

This elegantly carved figurehead typically depicts a fantastic magical creature, divine figure, or local hero, and it is either attached to the prow of a sailing vessel or carved into the hull of the ship. Once per day, the figurehead can be activated by speaking its _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word as a swift action, awakening its protective power for 1 minute. While the figurehead is active, all ranged attacks originating from anywhere other than the ship have a 20% miss chance against the vessel and anyone aboard it. This ability has no effect on melee attacks, rays, or ranged attacks that do not require an attack roll to hit (such as _[[spells/Magic Missile|magic missile]]_).

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 675 gp
