# Oil of Silence

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 250 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint illusion

### Description

When applied to a one-handed or two-handed firearm, this strange grayish oil renders that firearm silent for 1 hour. Five vials of _[[items/Wondrous Item/Oil of Silence|oil of silence]]_ can be used in conjunction to _[[spells/Silence|silence]]_ a Large firearm siege engine, and 10 can be used to _silence_ a Huge firearm siege engine. The oil does not work on firearm siege engines that are larger than Huge.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 125 gp
