# Gunman's Duster

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 36000 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Body

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 12 **Aura:** strong abjuration

### Description

This long, loose-fitting coat grants a +4 armor bonus to AC and a +2 luck bonus to the wearer's touch AC against firearm attacks. If the wearer is a _[[classes/Gunslinger|gunslinger]]_ or has the _[[feats/Amateur Gunslinger|Amateur Gunslinger]]_ feat (see Ultimate Combat), she begins each day with 1 additional grit point. This extra point does not increase her maximum grit total. When the wearer uses the _gunslinger_'s _[[feats/Dodge|dodge]]_ deed, the deed's _dodge_ bonuses to AC increase by +1. If the wearer is not a _gunslinger_, she may use the _gunslinger_'s _dodge_ deed once per day.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 18000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _Amateur Gunslinger_ or creator must be a _gunslinger_, _[[spells/Bullet Shield|bullet shield]]_
