# Grappler's Mask

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 5000 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Head

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 1 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

This _[[items/Mundane/Mask|mask]]_ is fashioned from dark leather decorated with geometrical patterns that emphasize the wearer's fearsomeness. It covers the wearer's face, but leaves his mouth and eyes uncovered. The wearer may attempt to bull rush and grapple without provoking attacks of opportunity.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 2500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Bull's Strength|bull's strength]]_, _[[spells/Cat's Grace|cat's grace]]_, _[[spells/Expeditious Retreat|expeditious retreat]]_
