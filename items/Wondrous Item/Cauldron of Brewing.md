# Cauldron of Brewing

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 3000 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

A _[[items/Wondrous Item/Cauldron of Brewing|cauldron of brewing]]_ looks like a fine cooking pot with four stout legs. The _[[items/Mundane/Cauldron|cauldron]]_ is capable of heating any liquid placed in it to a precise temperature (anywhere from just above room temperature to hot enough to boil salt water) and maintaining it indefinitely while still remaining only slightly warm to the touch on the outside. A _cauldron of brewing_ provides a +5 competence bonus on Craft (alchemy) skill checks.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, creator must be trained in Craft (Alchemy)
