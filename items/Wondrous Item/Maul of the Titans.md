# Maul of the Titans

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 25305 gp **Weight:** 160 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d10 **Critical:** x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +3 **Damage type:** B

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 15 **Aura:** strong evocation

### Description

This mallet is 8 feet long. If used as a weapon, it is the equivalent of a +3 _[[items/Weapon/Greatclub|greatclub]]_ and deals triple damage against inanimate objects. The wielder must have a Strength of at least 18 to wield it properly. Otherwise, she takes a –4 penalty on attack rolls.
