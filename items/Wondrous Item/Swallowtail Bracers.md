# Swallowtail Bracers

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 27000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Wrists

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate abjuration

### Description

These delicate sliver bracers are decorated with blue butterflies. While worn, _[[items/Wondrous Item/Swallowtail Bracers|swallowtail bracers]]_ grant the wearer a +1 luck bonus on all saving throws, ability checks, and skill checks. If the wearer worships Desna, the bracers grant an additional boon: Once per day, by speaking a _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word and the name and/or title of the intended recipient, the wearer may cast _[[spells/Dream|dream]]_. Such a wearer is also immune to _[[spells/Nightmare|nightmare]]_, and when the wearer is targeted by that spell, the effects reflect back upon the caster.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 13500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Divine Favor|divine favor]]_, _dream_, creator must worship Desna

* Pathfinder #75: Demon's Heresy
