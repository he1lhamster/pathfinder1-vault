# Stone of Weight (Loadstone)

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 8000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Cursed**

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

This dark, polished stone reduces the possessor's base land speed by half. Once picked up, the stone cannot be disposed of by any nonmagical means—if it is thrown away or smashed, it reappears somewhere on the possessor's person. If a _[[spells/Remove Curse|remove curse]]_ spell is cast upon a loadstone, the item may be discarded normally and no longer haunts the individual.
