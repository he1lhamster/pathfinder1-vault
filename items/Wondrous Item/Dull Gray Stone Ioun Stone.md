# Dull Gray Stone Ioun Stone

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 25 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 12 **Aura:** faint universal

### Description

These are ioun stones that have been burned out or otherwise rendered all but powerless. They retain the ability to float and orbit, and are useful as the target of spells such as _[[spells/Continual Flame|continual flame]]_, _[[spells/Daylight|daylight]]_, and _[[spells/Silence|silence]]_, allowing you to keep your hands free. They may be any shape (cabochon, disk, ellipsoid, and so on).

**Resonance:** _[[spells/Read Magic|Read magic]]_ 1/day for 10 minutes.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 12.5 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, creator must be 12th level
