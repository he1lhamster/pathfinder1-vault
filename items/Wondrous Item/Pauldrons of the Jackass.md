# Pauldrons of the Jackass

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 13000 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Shoulders

##### Magical properties

**Cursed**

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

These steel pauldrons seem to be emblazoned with stampeding horses until they are worn, and then the horses _[[universal monster rules/Change Shape|change shape]]_ to that of dumfounded donkeys. The wearer of these pauldrons takes a –4 penalty to Dexterity and reduces all speeds by 10 feet. A successful _[[spells/Remove Curse|remove curse]]_ spell is required before this item can be removed.

### Crafting requirements

any pauldrons
