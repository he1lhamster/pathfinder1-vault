# Charlatan's Cape

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 45000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Shoulders

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 13 **Aura:** strong evocation, strong illusion

### Description

This garish cloak of red velvet is embroidered with gold-threaded patterns, typically in shapes of sinisterly masked harlequins or other imagery of sinister revelry. The cloak acts like a cloak of the mountebank, but its _[[spells/Dimension Door|dimension door]]_ power can be used three times per day. Furthermore, once per day the wearer of the cloak can use _[[spells/Ethereal Jaunt|ethereal jaunt]]_, as per the spell.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 22500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _dimension door_, _ethereal jaunt_
