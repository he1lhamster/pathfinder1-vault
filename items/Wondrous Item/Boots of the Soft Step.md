# Boots of the Soft Step

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 1000 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Feet

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

These bronze-riveted boots have thick leather soles that compress and expand as you walk. This reduces the force of your footfalls, making you more difficult to detect with _[[universal monster rules/Tremorsense|tremorsense]]_. If you are walking at half speed, a creature with _tremorsense_ can only sense you at half the usual distance. If you walk at one-quarter speed or slower, a creature with _tremorsense_ cannot detect you at all.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Feather Fall|feather fall]]_
