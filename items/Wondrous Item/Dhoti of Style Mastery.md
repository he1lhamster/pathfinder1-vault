# Dhoti of Style Mastery

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 5400 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Body

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint necromancy

### Description

When the wearer enters a stance from a style feat while wearing a _[[items/Wondrous Item/Dhoti of Style Mastery|dhoti of style mastery]]_, he gains 1d8+3 temporary hit points plus an additional 1d8 temporary hit points for each feat he has that lists that style feat as a prerequisite (maximum 3d8+3 temporary hit points). These temporary hit points last as long as the wearer maintains the stance during that combat, but _[[spells/Vanish|vanish]]_ when the combat concludes. The wearer cannot gain temporary hit points in this way more than once per day.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 2700 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, any style feat, _[[spells/False Life|false life]]_
