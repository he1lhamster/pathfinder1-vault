# Cackling Hag's Blouse

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 6000 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Chest

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint enchantment

### Description

This loose-fitting blouse is adorned with grotesque fetishes and trophies, granting the wearer a +2 competence bonus on Intimidate checks. If the wearer is a _[[classes/Witch|witch]]_, she gains the cackle hex. If the wearer already has the cackle hex, twice per day she can use her cackle ability as a swift action instead of a move action.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 3000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Hideous Laughter|hideous laughter]]_ or the cackle hex, creator must have 5 ranks in Intimidate
