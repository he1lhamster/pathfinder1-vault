# Iron Cobra Gauntlet

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 8000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Hands

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This elbow-length scaled metal _[[items/Weapon/Gauntlet|gauntlet]]_ flares at the wrist like a cobra's hood. Up to three times per day, the wearer way transform the _gauntlet_ into an _[[monsters/Iron Cobra|iron cobra]]_ (Bestiary 182), which crawls from the wearer's wrist and obeys his commands, leaving behind a simple bracelet of linked scales. Whoever wears the bracelet is the _iron cobra_'s master. The _iron cobra_ can remain _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Animated|animated]]_ for a total of 1 hour per day.

If the snake is within hearing range of the master and commanded to revert to _gauntlet_ form (a standard action), it travels to its master as fast as possible, crawls onto its master's arm, and as a free action reverts to its inactive shape. If the snake's duration expires before it can reach its master, it reverts to _gauntlet_ form, but automatically melds with the bracelet if the two touch.

If slain in cobra form, the snake reverts to _gauntlet_ form and cannot be used again for 24 hours. If destroyed in _gauntlet_ form, the item is destroyed. The wearer can refill the _iron cobra_'s poison reservoir in snake form or _gauntlet_ form.
