# Glarecutter Goggles

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 2000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Eyes

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint conjuration

### Description

These dark lenses of smoked glass are set in a thin leather headband and _[[items/Mundane/Filter|filter]]_ the light reaching your eyes. You gain a +5 _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ bonus on Fortitude saving throws against sight- and light-based hazards or attacks, such as desert glare, blindness, _[[universal monster rules/Gaze|gaze]]_ attacks, and so on.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, remove blindness/deafness, _resistance_
