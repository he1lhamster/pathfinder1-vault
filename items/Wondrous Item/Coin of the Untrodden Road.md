# Coin of the Untrodden Road

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 6000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 6 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

When tossed into the air, this tarnished copper coin brings forth potential lost by life's decisions. If a human, half-elf, or half-orc flips the coin and catches it, she takes a –2 penalty to the ability score modified by her +2 racial ability score bonus, but gains a +2 inherent bonus to a random other ability score. The bonus and penalty last for 1 hour. _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Negating|Negating]]_ the penalty automatically negates the bonus as well. The coin can be used three times per day.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 3000 gp
