# Northern Star Ioun Stone

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 10000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 12 **Aura:** strong transmutation

### Description

This pearlescent crystal is perfectly cut in the shape of a five-pointed star. It grants the bearer a +5 competence bonus on Acrobatics and _[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_ checks. When inserted into a _[[items/Wondrous Item/Wayfinder|wayfinder]]_, it grants the bearer a +4 bonus on Reflex saves to avoid falling.

**Resonance:** +4 bonus on Reflex Saves to avoid falling.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 5000 gp
