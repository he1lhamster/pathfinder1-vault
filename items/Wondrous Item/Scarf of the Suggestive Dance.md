# Scarf of the Suggestive Dance

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 3000 gp **Weight:** 0.5 lbs. **Slot:** Belt

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 1 **Aura:** faint enchantment

### Description

This silk _[[items/Mundane/Scarf|scarf]]_ is embroidered with all the colors of a flame and tasseled in tiny beads. When wrapped around the hips, it grants the wearer a +5 competence bonus on Perform (dance) checks. The _scarf_ adds +1 to the saving throw DC of the wearer's fascinate and _[[spells/Suggestion|suggestion]]_ bardic performances.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 950 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Hypnotism|hypnotism]]_

* Murder's Mark
