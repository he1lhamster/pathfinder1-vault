# Ioun Torch

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 75 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 12 **Aura:** strong universal

### Description

This item is merely a burned out, dull _[[monsters/Gray|gray]]_ ioun stone with a _[[spells/Continual Flame|continual flame]]_ spell cast upon it. It retains the ability to float and orbit, and allows the bearer to carry light and still have his hands free. It may be in any crystalline shape common to ioun stones (ellipsoid, prism, sphere, and so on).

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 62.5 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _continual flame_, creator must be 12th level
