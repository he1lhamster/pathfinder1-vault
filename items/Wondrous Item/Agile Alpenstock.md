# Agile Alpenstock

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 2000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

This gnarled wooden walking stick aids a creature's travel, granting a +5 foot enhancement bonus to speed and a +2 enhancement bonus to CMD against _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_ attempts and on saving throws to avoid being tripped. These bonuses apply as long as the _[[items/Wondrous Item/Agile Alpenstock|agile alpenstock]]_ is held in hand. It can be wielded in combat as a _[[items/Weapon/Quarterstaff|quarterstaff]]_.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1000 gp
