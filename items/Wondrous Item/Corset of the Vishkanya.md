# Corset of the Vishkanya

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 3000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Body

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

This black _[[items/Mundane/Corset|corset]]_ fits neatly beneath armor. As a free action, the wearer can compress herself to fit through tight spaces as though affected by a _[[spells/Squeeze|squeeze]]_ spell (Advanced Race Guide) for up to 10 rounds per day. While using the _corset_'s magic, she gains a +5 bonus on Escape Artist checks.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Grease|grease]]_, _squeeze_
