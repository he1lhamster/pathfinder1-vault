# Giant Fist Gauntlets

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 20000 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Hands

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

As a swift action, the wearer of these rough leather gauntlets can expand her hands to twice normal size and harden to the consistency of hardwood. This _[[spells/Transformation|transformation]]_ lasts for up to 20 rounds per day, though they need not be consecutive rounds. If the wearer hits with an unarmed strike or natural attack using her enlarged hands, she may attempt to bull rush her opponent as a free action.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 10000 gp
