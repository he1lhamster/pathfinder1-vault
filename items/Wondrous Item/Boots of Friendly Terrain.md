# Boots of Friendly Terrain

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 2400 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Feet

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

_[[items/Wondrous Item/Boots of Friendly Terrain|Boots of friendly terrain]]_ turn inhospitable territory into a perfect hunting grounds for any _[[classes/Ranger|ranger]]_ who wears them, so much so that his footprints _[[spells/Vanish|vanish]]_ behind him with each step he takes. Each pair of these supple leather boots corresponds to a specific sort of environment as defined by the _ranger_'s favored terrain class feature (jungles, plains, and so on). A _ranger_ wearing the boots can treat the corresponding environment of the boots as one of his favored terrains, granting him a +2 bonus. If he already has that particular environment as one of his favored terrains, that terrain's bonus increases by 2.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1200 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Pass without Trace|pass without trace]]_, creator must be a _ranger_
