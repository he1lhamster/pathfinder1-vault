# Cloak of the Duskwalker

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 10000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Shoulders

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint illusion

### Description

This dark, velvety cloak billows with a dramatic flourish in its wearer's wake. The cloak grants its wearer _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_. Once per day, on _[[spells/Command|command]]_, the wearer can surround herself with _[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_, as the _darkness_ spell. While this _darkness_ is active, the cloak also grants the wearer _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 60 feet as well as _low-light vision_.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 5000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _darkness_, _darkvision_
