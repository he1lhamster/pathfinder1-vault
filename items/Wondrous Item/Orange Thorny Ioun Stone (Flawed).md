# Orange Thorny Ioun Stone (Flawed)

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 6900 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 12 **Aura:** strong transmutation

### Description

The flawed version of a thorny ioun stone of any type functions as the normal version, but the wearer also takes a -2 penalty to CMD against the corresponding type of combat maneuver check.

**Resonance:** Grants the bearer the benefit of either the _[[feats/Acrobatic|Acrobatic]]_ or the _[[feats/Athletic|Athletic]]_ feat. The feat varies by individual ioun stone, and each is equally likely (50% chance).

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 3450 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, creator must be 12th level
