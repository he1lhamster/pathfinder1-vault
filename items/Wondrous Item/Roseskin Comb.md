# Roseskin Comb

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 37000 gp **Weight:** 0.5 lbs. **Slot:** Head

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

A green copper comb holds this pink-hued alabaster headpiece in its wearer's hair, subtly infusing her skin with strength. In addition to providing an air of loveliness to its wearer, a _[[items/Wondrous Item/Roseskin Comb|roseskin comb]]_ grants a +2 enhancement bonus to its wearer's natural armor. In addition, three times per day as a standard action, the wearer of a _roseskin comb_ can _[[spells/Command|command]]_ barbs to protrude from her skin, as per _[[spells/Thorn Body|thorn body]]_, for 7 rounds.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 18500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Barkskin|barkskin]]_, _thorn body_
