# Jar of Dragon's Teeth

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** – **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 20 **Aura:** strong transmutation

### Description

Each time this jar gains a new user it produces 2d4 dragon teeth. When one of these teeth is swallowed, it grants the user the following powers for 1 full week or until it is dismissed as a standard action that does not provoke attacks of opportunity.

* The user is immediately polymorphed into a dragon, as if subject to _[[spells/Form of the Dragon I|form of the dragon I]]_, II, or III, depending on the user's Hit Dice. If the creature swallowing the tooth has 12 HD or fewer, it is affected as per _form of the dragon I_. Those with 13 to 18 HD are affected as per _[[spells/Form of the Dragon II|form of the dragon II]]_, and those 19 or more Hit Dice are affected as per _[[spells/Form of the Dragon III|form of the dragon III]]_. The type of dragon is chosen by the user based on its alignment.
