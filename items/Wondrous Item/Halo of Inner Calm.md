# Halo of Inner Calm

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 16000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Head

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 15 **Aura:** strong abjuration

### Description

This silvery ring looks like a halo. When worn by a _[[monsters/Tiefling|tiefling]]_ it hovers just above his head, though it still uses the head magic item slot. It helps to calm the baser emotions and the _[[spells/Dark Whispers|dark whispers]]_ that plague tieflings, granting the wearer a +4 _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ bonus on saving throws against all spells with the emotion descriptor. When worn by a _tiefling_ of a good alignment, it provides _[[universal monster rules/Spell Resistance|spell resistance]]_ 13 against spells with the evil descriptor and a +2 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ bonus on saving throws.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 8000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Holy Aura|holy aura]]_, creator must be a _tiefling_ of good alignment
