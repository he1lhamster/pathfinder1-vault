# Scarlet and Green Cabochon Ioun Stone (Flawed)

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 8000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 12 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This stone grants the wearer the _[[feats/Endurance|Endurance]]_ feat. Anything that would make the wearer _[[conditions/Fatigued|fatigued]]_ instead makes him _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_. Anything that would make the wearer _[[conditions/Exhausted|exhausted]]_ instead makes him _[[conditions/Nauseated|nauseated]]_.

**Resonance:** +5 bonus on Constitution checks made to stabilize.
