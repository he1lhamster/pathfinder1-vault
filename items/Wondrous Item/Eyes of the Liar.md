# Eyes of the Liar

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 16000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Eyes

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

These smoked crystal lenses hide the wearer's thoughts and intents from the prying minds of others. They grant the wearer a +5 competence bonus on Bluff checks and _[[universal monster rules/Immunity|immunity]]_ to _[[spells/Detect Thoughts|detect thoughts]]_. Both lenses must be worn for the magic item to function.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 8000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Eagle's Splendor|eagle's splendor]]_, _[[spells/Nondetection|nondetection]]_
