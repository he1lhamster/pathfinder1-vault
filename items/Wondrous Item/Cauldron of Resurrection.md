# Cauldron of Resurrection

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 33000 gp **Weight:** 35 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 13 **Aura:** strong conjuration

### Description

The mouth of this heavy, dark iron _[[items/Mundane/Cauldron|cauldron]]_ is shaped like a monstrous maw and is large enough to accommodate a single _[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_ creature. When the _cauldron_ is filled with pure water and certain rare and _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ herbs, and a deceased creature is placed inside it, the _cauldron_ can restore the creature to life as if with a _[[spells/Raise Dead|raise dead]]_ or _[[spells/Resurrection|resurrection]]_ spell. The spell effect depends on the components used: _raise dead_ costs 5,000 gp worth of materials, while _resurrection_ costs 10,000 gp worth of materials. The _cauldron_ is usable once per day.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 16500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _resurrection_
