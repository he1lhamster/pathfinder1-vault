# Cloak of the Night Sky

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 2500 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Shoulders

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint abjuration

### Description

This dark hooded cloak is decorated with embroidered comets, moons, and stars along its edge. If the wearer sleeps while wearing the cloak, it protects him from hot and cold environments, as _[[spells/Endure Elements|endure elements]]_, and the wearer can sleep in _[[classes/Medium|medium]]_ armor without becoming _[[conditions/Fatigued|fatigued]]_. The wearer can use _[[spells/Know Direction|know direction]]_ once per day.

If Desna is the wearer's patron, as a standard action the wearer can cause additional celestial bodies to appear on the cloak so that they match the current configuration in the sky above the wearer. The wearer can use _[[spells/Longstrider|longstrider]]_ once per day. Once per day, the wearer can throw a tiny, white-burning meteor that acts as a _[[spells/Flare|flare]]_ effect. Some priests of Desna claim to have cloaks of this type that also allow them to travel from _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Shadow|shadow]]_ to _shadow_ when under a starry night sky.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1250 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Disguise Self|disguise self]]_, _endure elements_, _flare_, _know direction_, lesser _[[spells/Restoration|restoration]]_, _longstrider_
