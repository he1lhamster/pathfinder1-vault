# Rope of Entanglement

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 21000 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 12 **Aura:** strong transmutation

### Description

A _[[items/Wondrous Item/Rope of Entanglement|rope of entanglement]]_ looks just like any other hempen rope about 30 feet long. Upon _[[spells/Command|command]]_, the rope lashes forward 20 feet or upward 10 feet to _[[spells/Entangle|entangle]]_ a victim. An _[[conditions/Entangled|entangled]]_ creature can break free with a DC 20 Strength check or a DC 20 Escape Artist check.

A _rope of entanglement_ has AC 22, 12 hit points, hardness 10, and _[[universal monster rules/Damage Reduction|damage reduction]]_ 5/slashing. The rope repairs damage to itself at a rate of 1 point per 5 minutes, but if a _rope of entanglement_ is severed (all 12 hit points lost to damage), it is destroyed.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 10500 gp
