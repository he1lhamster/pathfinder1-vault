# Throne of the Sky-Father

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 8000 gp **Weight:** 1000 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate abjuration, moderate transmutation

### Description

This altar takes the form of a throne sized for a Large humanoid. It's carved from mountain stone, and its surface is inscribed with prayers to Gozreh in Thassilonian and to Ioz'om in Giant. The _[[items/Wondrous Item/Throne of the Sky-Father|Throne of the Sky-Father]]_ projects a _[[spells/Magic Circle against Law|magic circle against law]]_ that affects creatures within 50 feet of the throne. A character within this area can speak a _[[spells/Prayer|prayer]]_ to Gozreh to gain a temporary blessing from the altar (treat this as speaking a _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word to activate a magic item). Praying at the altar grants a +4 _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ bonus on saving throws against weather effects and a +2 circumstance bonus on Acrobatics, _[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_, and Fly checks. This blessing ends after 24 hours (or earlier, if dispelled or ended by the actions of the worshiper). The altar can provide its blessing only once per creature per day.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 4000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Control Winds|control winds]]_, _[[spells/Guidance|guidance]]_, _magic circle against law_, _[[spells/Stone Shape|stone shape]]_, creator must worship Gozreh
