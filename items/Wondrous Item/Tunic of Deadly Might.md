# Tunic of Deadly Might

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 6000 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Chest

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint evocation

### Description

This tunic is crafted from golden thread, and has silver-hemmed sleeves. The wearer gains a +2 bonus to Combat Maneuver Defense against disarm and sunder combat maneuvers. If the wearer is a _[[classes/Samurai|samurai]]_, twice per day as a swift action he can apply the effects of the _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Thundering|thundering]]_ weapon special ability (Core Rulebook 472) to a successful critical hit against the target of his challenge.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 3000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, blindness/deafness, _[[spells/Mage Armor|mage armor]]_

* Ultimate Equipment
