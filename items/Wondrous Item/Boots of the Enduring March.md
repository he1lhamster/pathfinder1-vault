# Boots of the Enduring March

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 1500 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Feet

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 2 **Aura:** faint conjuration

### Description

These travel-stained boots of stout brown leather enable the wearer to traverse great distances without tiring. When making a forced march (Core Rulebook 171), for each hour of marching beyond 8 hours, the wearer needs only to make a Constitution check (DC 10, +1 per extra hour) instead of the normal check. Furthermore, if the wearer fails the check, she only takes 1d4 points of nonlethal damage and does not becomes _[[conditions/Fatigued|fatigued]]_ the first time she takes nonlethal damage in this way.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 750 gp
