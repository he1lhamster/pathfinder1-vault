# Bracers of Defenselessness

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 25000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Wrists

##### Magical properties

**Cursed**

**Caster level:** 16 **Aura:** strong conjuration

### Description

These bejeweled and shining bracers initially appear to be _[[items/Wondrous Item/Bracers of Armor +5|bracers of armor +5]]_ and actually serve as such until the wearer is attacked in anger by an enemy with a Challenge Rating equal to or greater than her level. At that moment and thereafter, the bracers bestow a –5 penalty to AC. Once their curse is activated, _[[items/Wondrous Item/Bracers of Defenselessness|bracers of defenselessness]]_ can be removed only by means of a _[[spells/Remove Curse|remove curse]]_ spell.
