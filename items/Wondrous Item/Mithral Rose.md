# Mithral Rose

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 9000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

This seemingly delicate silver rose is rigid and nearly _[[feats/Unbreakable|unbreakable]]_. When wearing the rose openly on his chest, the wearer treats all of his _[[universal monster rules/Natural Attacks|natural attacks]]_, unarmed attacks, and weapon attacks as if they were made using silver weapons. The wearer can _[[spells/Expend|expend]]_ one use of mythic power when making a melee attack against an enemy that possesses DR/silver to negate that DR for a number of rounds equal to the wearer's mythic tier.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 4500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Magic Arms and Armor|Craft Magic Arms and Armor]]_, Mythic Crafter, _[[spells/Magic Weapon|magic weapon]]_
