# Spellguard Bracers

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 5000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Wrists

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 4 **Aura:** faint abjuration

### Description

Typically made of leather dyed blue and decorated with star-shaped studs, these bracers are used by spellcasters who practice their art in the _[[universal monster rules/Heat|heat]]_ of combat. The bracers grant the wearer a +2 bonus on concentration checks made to cast defensively. Three times per day, the wearer can choose to roll a concentration check to cast defensively twice and take the better result.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 2500 gp
