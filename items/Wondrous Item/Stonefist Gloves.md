# Stonefist Gloves

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 10000 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Hands

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

These heavy, stone gloves feel surprisingly supple to anyone who puts them on. While a creature wears _[[items/Wondrous Item/Stonefist Gloves|stonefist gloves]]_, his unarmed strike damage is treated as if he were one size category larger, and bypasses hardness of 8 or lower. When a mythic creature uses these gloves to make a sunder combat maneuver, he adds half his tier as a bonus on the combat maneuver check.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 5000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Magic Arms and Armor|Craft Magic Arms and Armor]]_, Mythic Crafter, _[[spells/Stoneskin|stoneskin]]_
