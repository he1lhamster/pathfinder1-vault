# Mask of Giants (Lesser)

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 30000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Head

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 6 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This wooden _[[items/Mundane/Mask|mask]]_ depicts a leering humanoid with an oversized nose and ears. If the wearer has the wild shape class feature, the _mask_ allows her to use wild shape to take the form of a humanoid with the giant subtype. The forms allowed by a lesser _mask_ of giants are _[[monsters/Ogre|ogre]]_, _[[monsters/Troll|troll]]_, fire giant, frost giant, and stone giant. If the form has any of the following abilities, the wearer gains the listed ability: _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 60 feet, _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Scent|scent]]_. In giant form, the wearer gains a +4 size bonus to Strength, a –2 penalty to Dexterity, and a +1 natural armor bonus.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 15000 gp
