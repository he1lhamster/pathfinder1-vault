# Mask of Ugliness

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 8000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Head

##### Magical properties

**Cursed**

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This strange, featureless _[[items/Mundane/Mask|mask]]_ seems to have the powers of a _[[items/Wondrous Item/Headband of Alluring Charisma +4|headband of alluring charisma +4]]_, but instead of granting a bonus to Charisma, the _mask_ melds to the wearer's face, making him seem uglier and his words come off as crude and insulting. The wearer takes a –4 penalty to Bluff and Diplomacy checks but gains a +2 bonus to Intimidate checks.
