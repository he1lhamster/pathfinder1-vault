# Journeyman Book of Rul Thaven

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 2195 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate universal

### Description

This medium-sized book has a creased spine and supple _[[monsters/Gray|gray]]_ leather covers. The leather has rubbed away in certain spots, tiny pinhole burns pock the surface, and a brass lock holds the book shut. A woman's portrait has been bound into the front cover, and text covers almost all of the image except her face: text in circles, with arrows pointing to new circles with suppositions. Underneath the woman's chin are the words "Diabolist _[[spells/Sacrifice|sacrifice]]_?"

**Opposition:** Illusion, transmutation
