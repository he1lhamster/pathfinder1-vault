# Manteau of the Mouse

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 12500 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Chest

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** strong transmutation

### Description

This soft fur _[[items/Mundane/Vest|vest]]_ comes in a variety of _[[spells/Shades|shades]]_ of brown, black, and white. Any creature larger than the wearer takes a –1, –2, –4, or –8 penalty on Perception checks to notice the wearer, depending on whether it is one, two, three, or four or more size categories larger than the wearer, respectively. In addition, the wearer can use _[[spells/Beast Shape III|beast shape III]]_ once per day to transform into a mouse.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 6250 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _beast shape III_
