# Belt of Stoneskin

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 60000 gp **Weight:** 10 lbs. **Slot:** Belt

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate abjuration

### Description

Every 24 hours, this belt's wearer gains DR 10/adamantine until the belt absorbs 100 points of damage, at which point the belt becomes useless for the remainder of the 24-hour period. When first worn, or after each time this belt is taken off, it must worn for 24 consecutive hours in order for its magic to take effect again.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 30000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Stoneskin|stoneskin]]_, creator must be an _[[monsters/Oread|oread]]_ or a creature with the earth subtype
