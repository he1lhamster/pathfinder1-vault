# Vest of Stable Mutation

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 20000 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Chest

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate abjuration

### Description

This leather _[[items/Mundane/Vest|vest]]_ is fitted with a number of pockets that are useful for storing the ingredients necessary for _[[spells/Alchemical Tinkering|alchemical tinkering]]_. The main ability of this _vest_, however, is to lessen the _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Debilitating|debilitating]]_ effects of an _[[classes/Alchemist|alchemist]]_'s mutagen. While under the effect of any type of mutagen, the wearer takes no penalty to mental ability scores from that mutagen.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 10000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Eagle's Splendor|eagle's splendor]]_, _[[spells/Fox's Cunning|fox's cunning]]_, _[[spells/Owl's Wisdom|owl's wisdom]]_, creator must be an _alchemist_
