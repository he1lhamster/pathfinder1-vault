# Scrap Collector's Strap

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 500 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

When this leather strap is tied around the grip of a _[[conditions/Broken|broken]]_ weapon, the user can ignore the effects of the _broken_ condition on that weapon. If the weapon loses its remaining hit points, it is destroyed as normal. Tying or untying the strap is a full-round action.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 250 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Mending|mending]]_
