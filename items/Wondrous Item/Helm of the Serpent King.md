# Helm of the Serpent King

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 96000 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Head

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

This helm is made of large, alchemically hardened snake scales, and is crafted in the shape of a stylized serpent mouth. Its wearer stares out through the serpent's eyes, and sees all things cast in a yellow tint. When worn, it grants the following _[[universal monster rules/Spell-Like Abilities|spell-like abilities]]_ to the wearer:

* _[[spells/Hypnotism|Hypnotism]]_ (3/day)
* _[[spells/Summon Nature's Ally III|Summon nature's ally III]]_ (constrictor snake only, 3/day)
* _[[spells/Beast Shape III|Beast shape III]]_ (snake form only, 1/day)
* Poison (1/day)
