# Robe of Eyes

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 120000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Body

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 11 **Aura:** moderate _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

This valuable garment appears to be a normal robe until it is put on. Its wearer is able to see in all directions at the same moment due to scores of visible, magical eye-like patterns that adorn the robe. She also gains 120-foot _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_.

The _[[items/Wondrous Item/Robe of Eyes|robe of eyes]]_ sees all forms of _[[conditions/Invisible|invisible]]_ or ethereal creatures or objects within 120 feet. The wearer of a _robe of eyes_ gains a +10 competence bonus on Perception checks. She retains her Dexterity bonus to AC even when _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_, and can't be flanked. She is not able to avert or close her eyes when confronted by a creature with a _[[universal monster rules/Gaze|gaze]]_ attack.

A light or _[[spells/Continual Flame|continual flame]]_ spell cast directly on a _robe of eyes_ causes the wearer to be _[[conditions/Blinded|blinded]]_ for 1d3 minutes. A _[[spells/Daylight|daylight]]_ spell blinds the wearer for 2d4 minutes.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 60000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/True Seeing|true seeing]]_
