# Oath Breaker's Brand

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 2500 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate necromancy

### Description

When this iron medallion is placed between the palms of two persons willingly entering into an agreement via handshake, the accord is enforced by the _[[spells/Brand|brand]]_'s enchantment. If either person breaks the word of the agreement, a hideous mark appears on the top of the unfaithful creature's hand. The _brand_ marks her as an oath breaker throughout the River Kingdoms, imposing upon her a –8 penalty on Diplomacy and Bluff checks attempted anywhere in the region as long as the _brand_ is visible. This mark lasts for 1 year or until it is removed by _[[spells/Break Enchantment|break enchantment]]_, _[[spells/Limited Wish|limited wish]]_, _[[spells/Miracle|miracle]]_, _[[spells/Remove Curse|remove curse]]_, or _[[spells/Wish|wish]]_, whichever comes first.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1250 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Mark of Justice|mark of justice]]_
