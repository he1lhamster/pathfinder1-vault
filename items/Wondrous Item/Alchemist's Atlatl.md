# Alchemist's Atlatl

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 1500 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 1 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

This wooden _[[items/Weapon/Atlatl|atlatl]]_ resembles a handheld, lever-controlled catapult. One end sports a shaft covered in carvings of alchemical formulae, and the other is a roughly carved bowl. The user can place an alchemical splash weapon, such as a _[[items/Mundane/Flask|flask]]_ of acid or _[[items/Mundane/Alchemist's fire|alchemist's fire]]_, in the bowl. This is a move action that provokes an attack of opportunity. When the splash weapon is launched from the _atlatl_, its range is doubled. Magic items (including an _[[classes/Alchemist|alchemist]]_'s bombs) aren't affected by an _[[items/Wondrous Item/Alchemist's Atlatl|alchemist's atlatl]]_.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 750 gp
