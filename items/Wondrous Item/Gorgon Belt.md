# Gorgon Belt

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 23000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Belt

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This belt grants its wearer a +4 enhancement bonus to Strength, and the ability to ignore difficult terrain while charging or making an overrun, bull rush, or _[[universal monster rules/Trample|trample]]_ attempt. Furthermore, once per day on _[[spells/Command|command]]_, its wearer can spew a 60-foot cone of poisonous green gas as a _[[universal monster rules/Breath Weapon|breath weapon]]_. Any creature caught in the area of the gas can attempt a DC 18 saving throw to resist its effect, but if the creature fails the saving throw, it is _[[conditions/Paralyzed|paralyzed]]_ for 1d4 rounds. At the end of its turn, a creature _[[conditions/Petrified|petrified]]_ by this _breath weapon_ can attempt a new save to end the _[[universal monster rules/Paralysis|paralysis]]_ effect. Treat the enhancement bonus to Strength as a temporary ability bonus for the first 24 hours the belt is worn.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 11500 gp

Craft Wondrous Items, _[[spells/Bull's Strength|bull's strength]]_, _[[spells/Feather Step|feather step]]_ (Advanced Player's Guide), poison
