# Iron Rope

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 750 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 4 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

When the end of this _[[monsters/Gray|gray]]_ hemp rope is held and the _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word is uttered, it is irrevocably transmuted into solid iron. The transmuted rope loses all magic and has the hardness and hit points of a normal inch-thick iron bar. When crafted, the rope is 25 feet long, but can be cut up to three times and each section of rope can be commanded to harden individually.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 375 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Animate Rope|animate rope]]_
