# Hexing Runes

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 10000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Neck

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate abjuration

### Description

These runes may be carved from wood or cast in silver and are worn on a leather cord. When a _[[classes/Witch|witch]]_ prepares her spells, she may also choose to use the _[[items/Wondrous Item/Hexing Runes|hexing runes]]_ to augment one hex she can use. The save DC of this hex increases by 1 for 24 hours. Additionally, once per day when she speaks the _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word, the wearer gains the benefits of the ward hex as if cast by an 8th level _witch_.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 5000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[feats/Spell Focus|Spell Focus]]_ (abjuration), _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_, creator must be able to use the ward hex
