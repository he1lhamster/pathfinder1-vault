# Headband of Fortune's Favor

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 7700 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Headband

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint enchantment, faint evocation

### Description

This multicolored headband with beaded tassels tips luck in the wearer's favor. The wearer gains a +1 luck bonus on saving throws.

Any effect that gives the wearer a luck bonus (such as a _[[spells/Divine Favor|divine favor]]_ or _[[spells/Prayer|prayer]]_ spell) or explicitly increases the wearer's good luck (such as a _[[classes/Witch|witch]]_'s fortune hex, the Luck domain's _[[spells/Bit Of Luck|bit of luck]]_ domain power, and so on) lasts for 1 round longer than normal on the wearer. Abilities without a time-based duration (such as carrying a stone of good luck) are unaffected by the headband.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 3850 gp
