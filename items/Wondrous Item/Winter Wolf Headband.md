# Winter Wolf Headband

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 32000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Headband

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

Crafted of pale bone or silver, this headband features a snarling _[[monsters/Wolf|wolf]]_'s head centered on the wearer's forehead. The wearer gains cold _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ 10. Once per day, the wearer may transform into a Large white-furred _wolf_ for 11 minutes. In addition, once per day the wearer may breathe a 30-foot _[[spells/Cone of Cold|cone of cold]]_ that deals 7d6 points of cold damage (Reflex DC 16 for half). The wearer may use this _[[universal monster rules/Breath Weapon|breath weapon]]_ even when transformed.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 16000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Beast Shape II|beast shape II]]_, _[[spells/Dragon's Breath|dragon's breath]]_ (Advanced Player's Guide), _[[spells/Resist Energy|resist energy]]_
