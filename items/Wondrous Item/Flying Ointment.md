# Flying Ointment

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 2250 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This preparation of herbs includes _[[items/Mundane/Belladonna|belladonna]]_, mandrake, and foxglove (also known as "_[[classes/Witch|witch]]_'s glove"), all carefully measured and mixed into a base of rendered fats. When rubbed over the skin, the ointment grants the subject the ability to fly (as the _[[spells/Overland Flight|overland flight]]_ spell) for up to 9 hours.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1125 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _overland flight_
