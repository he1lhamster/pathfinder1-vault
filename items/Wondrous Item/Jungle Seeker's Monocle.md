# Jungle Seeker's Monocle

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 12400 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint conjuration

### Description

The lens of this classical monocle is made of carefully shaped Shory glass. By looking through the lens, a wearer of a _[[items/Wondrous Item/Jungle Seeker's Monocle|jungle seeker's monocle]]_ can find hidden creatures even in the thickest of jungles. The wearer can use Perception to detect the presence of other living creatures up to 3d6 ×10 feet away in any forest, treating it as if it were a sparse forest (Pathfinder RPG Core Rulebook 426). The monocle also grants a +2 competence bonus on Perception checks to notice creatures using Stealth, regardless whether the wearer is in a forest or elsewhere.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 6200 gp

Create Wondrous Item, _[[spells/Acute Senses|acute senses]]_
