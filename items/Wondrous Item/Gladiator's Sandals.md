# Gladiator's Sandals

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 6000 gp **Weight:** 0.5 lbs. **Slot:** Feet

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

These sand-worn leather sandals are designed to lace around the calf and are favored by pit fighters and those who make careers out of performing in the ring. When the wearer acts during a surprise round and attempts any combat maneuver check, she gains a +4 circumstance bonus on that check. If the check is successful, the wearer can also attempt a performance combat check (Pathfinder RPG Ultimate Combat 153) with a +10 circumstance bonus to affect the crowd's attitude.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 3000 gp
