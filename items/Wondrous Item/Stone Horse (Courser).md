# Stone Horse (Courser)

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 10000 gp **Weight:** 6000 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 14 **Aura:** strong transmutation

### Description

This item is a roughly hewn _[[spells/Statue|statue]]_ of a _[[monsters/Horse|horse]]_, carved from some type of hard stone. A _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word brings the steed to life, enabling it to carry a burden and even to attack as if it were a real _horse_ of the appropriate kind. A stone _horse_ is combat-trained.

A stone _horse_ can carry 1,000 pounds tirelessly and never needs to rest or eat. Damage dealt to it can be repaired either by feeding it gems (1 hp for every 50 gp value of the gem) or by casting _[[spells/Stone to Flesh|stone to flesh]]_, then using healing spells until it reaches its maximum hit points, at which time it automatically returns to its stone form.

There are two sorts of stone horses.

Courser: This item has the statistics of a _horse_ with hardness 10.

Destrier: This item has the statistics of a heavy _horse_ with hardness 10.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 5000 gp
