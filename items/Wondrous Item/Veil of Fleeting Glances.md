# Veil of Fleeting Glances

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 9000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Head

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

In addition to hiding the face, this fine lace _[[spells/Veil|veil]]_ protects the wearer against _[[universal monster rules/Gaze|gaze]]_ attacks. The wearer has a 50% chance to avoid making a saving throw against a _gaze_ attack even when not averting her eyes. If the wearer averts her eyes, she rolls twice (once for the _veil_, once for averting her eyes) and uses the more favorable result.

Against patterns, visual figments, and other vision-based effects and attacks that cannot affect blind creatures, the _veil_ grants the wearer a +4 circumstance bonus on saving throws. The _veil_ does not otherwise impede the wearer's _[[spells/Vision|vision]]_ in any way.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 4500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, blindness/deafness, _[[spells/True Strike|true strike]]_
