# Handstraps of Roofjumping

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 1500 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Hands

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

These long cloth strips are intricately embroidered with urban scenes. When wrapped around your hands (like a boxer's or _[[monsters/Mummy|mummy]]_'s wraps) you gain a +1 competence bonus to Balance, _[[spells/Jump|Jump]]_, and Tumble checks, and a +1 competence bonus to Reflex saves made to catch a ledge or arrest a fall.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 750 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Cat's Grace|cat's grace]]_, _jump_
