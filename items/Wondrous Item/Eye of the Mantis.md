# Eye of the Mantis

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 2000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

This 1-inch red marble _[[spells/Statue|statue]]_ depicts a tiny praying mantis. When the _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word is uttered, the mantis comes to life and moves as its owner instructs, as per a sensor eye from a _[[spells/Prying Eyes|prying eyes]]_ spell. The mantis remains _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Animated|animated]]_ for up to 9 hours or until it returns to the bearer and replays what it _[[items/Mundane/Saw|saw]]_, at which point it reverts to _statue_ form for 1 day. If the mantis is destroyed while animate, it reverts to _statue_ form.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _prying eyes_
