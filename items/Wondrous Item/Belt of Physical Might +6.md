# Belt of Physical Might +6

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 90000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Belt

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 12 **Aura:** strong transmutation

### Description

This belt grants the wearer an enhancement bonus to two physical ability scores (Strength, Dexterity, or Constitution) of +2, +4, or +6. Treat this as a temporary ability bonus for the first 24 hours the belt is worn. These bonuses are chosen when the belt is created and cannot be changed.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 45000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Bear's Endurance|bear's endurance]]_, _[[spells/Bull's Strength|bull's strength]]_, and/or _[[spells/Cat's Grace|cat's grace]]_
