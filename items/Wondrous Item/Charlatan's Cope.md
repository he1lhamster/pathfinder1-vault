# Charlatan's Cope

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 4800 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Body

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint illusion

### Description

This plush hooded robe is finely trimmed with gold accents. When the wearer casts a spell, the robe scintillates with color and produces ostentatious magical effects, such as bright lights and loud hums, increasing the DC of Spellcraft checks to _[[spells/Identify|identify]]_ the spell by 10. In addition, once per day as a move action, the wearer can trigger these effects with a dramatic flourish; each creature within 30 feet that can see the wearer must succeed at a DC 13 Will save or be _[[conditions/Blinded|blinded]]_ for 1d4+1 rounds.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 2400 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Ghost Sound|ghost sound]]_, _[[spells/Pyrotechnics|pyrotechnics]]_, _[[spells/Silent Image|silent image]]_
