# Scabbard of Honing

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 500 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

This leather scabbard resizes itself to fit any axe, sword, _[[items/Weapon/Dagger|dagger]]_, or similar item normally stored in a scabbard. Sheathing a nonmagical weapon in the scabbard for 1 minute hones its edge as if it had been sharpened with a _[[items/Mundane/Whetstone|whetstone]]_ (page 75).

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 250 gp
