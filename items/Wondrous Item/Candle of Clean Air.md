# Candle of Clean Air

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 7500 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate abjuration

### Description

This clean-smelling white _[[items/Mundane/Candle|candle]]_ is inscribed with glyphs portraying various sweet smelling flowers. When lit, the _candle_ repels smoke, mist, cloud, and other gas effects in a 5-foot radius. These effects do not penetrate the area around the _candle_ if they are of spell level 3 or lower. Against more powerful cloud effects (including effects not based on spells, such as dragon breath or a _[[monsters/Gorgon|gorgon]]_'s _[[universal monster rules/Breath Weapon|breath weapon]]_), the _candle_ grants creatures in the area a +4 _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ bonus on saving throws against these effects. Creatures in the area gain a +4 _[[spells/Deflection|deflection]]_ bonus to AC against attacks from creatures with the air subtype.

The _candle_ burns for up to 5 minutes, in increments of 1 minute. Once all 5 minutes are used, the _candle_ is destroyed.
