# Wings of Flying

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 54000 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Shoulders

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

A pair of these wings might appear to be nothing more than a plain cloak of old, black cloth, or they could be as elegant as a long cape of blue feathers. When the wearer speaks the _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word, the cloak turns into a pair of bat or bird wings that empower her to fly with a speed of 60 feet (average maneuverability), also granting a +5 competence bonus on Fly skill checks.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 27000 gp
