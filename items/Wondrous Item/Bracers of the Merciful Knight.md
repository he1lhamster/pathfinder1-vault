# Bracers of the Merciful Knight

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 15600 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Wrists

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate conjuration

### Description

These golden bracers are engraved with images of celestial creatures. When worn by a _[[classes/Paladin|paladin]]_, he is considered four levels higher for the purposes of determining the uses per day and healing provided by his lay on hands class feature. Additionally, once per day, the wearer can infuse a use of lay on hands with additional power, providing relief as a lesser _[[spells/Restoration|restoration]]_ spell.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 7800 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Cure Serious Wounds|cure serious wounds]]_, lesser _restoration_
