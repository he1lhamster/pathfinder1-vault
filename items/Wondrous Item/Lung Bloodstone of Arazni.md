# Lung Bloodstone of Arazni

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** – **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 20 **Aura:** strong abjuration, strong necromancy

### Description

This _[[items/Wondrous Item/Canopic Jar|canopic jar]]_ containing Arazni's preserved lungs echoes with Arazni's sense of duty and the lingering power of the dead god Aroden. The bearer of the _[[items/Wondrous Item/Lung Bloodstone of Arazni|Lung Bloodstone of Arazni]]_ gains _[[feats/Improved Iron Will|Improved Iron Will]]_ as a bonus feat. Any _[[classes/Cleric|cleric]]_ who bears the jar is granted the Knowledge domain as an additional domain, with the associated granted powers and domain spells, for as long as she bears it. If the bearer is not a _cleric_, she instead gains the lore keeper ability of the Knowledge domain, treating her character level as her _cleric_ level.

### _[[spells/Destruction|Destruction]]_

Together, the tyrant Geb and the _[[monsters/Lich|lich]]_ Arazni can destroy the Bloodstone, _[[spells/Binding|binding]]_ the organ within the withered form of the _[[monsters/Fallen|fallen]]_ hero as part of a 24-hour-long ritual.
