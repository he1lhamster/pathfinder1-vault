# Incense of Dulled Senses

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 1500 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate abjuration

### Description

This _[[monsters/Gray|gray]]_ _[[items/Mundane/Incense|incense]]_ smells of cold granite. When burned, the smoke from the _incense_ spreads to fill a space equal to ten 10-foot cubes. This smoke remains stationary unless moved by wind. If inhaled by a creature with _[[universal monster rules/Blindsense|blindsense]]_, _[[universal monster rules/Scent|scent]]_, or _[[universal monster rules/Tremorsense|tremorsense]]_, the creature must attempt a DC 20 Fortitude save. If it fails the save, it does not benefit from its _blindsense_, _tremorsense_, or _scent_ abilities for 10 minutes. After 10 minutes the smoke from the _incense_ dissipates and loses all magical properties.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 750 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, blindness/deafness
