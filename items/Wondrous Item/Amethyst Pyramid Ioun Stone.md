# Amethyst Pyramid Ioun Stone

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 8000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 12 **Aura:** strong abjuration

### Description

This stone grants you the ability to pass undetected before undead as if under the effects of a _[[spells/Hide from Undead|hide from undead]]_ spell. Unintelligent undead automatically can't see you, and intelligent undead receive a single DC 12 Will save to notice you despite the stone's effects. If you attempt to channel positive energy, turn or _[[spells/Command Undead|command undead]]_, touch an undead creature, or attack any creature (even with a spell), the stone's effects are negated for 1 hour.

**Resonance:** +2 circumstance bonus on Stealth checks against undead.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 4000 gp
