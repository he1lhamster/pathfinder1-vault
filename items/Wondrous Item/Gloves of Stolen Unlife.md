# Gloves of Stolen Unlife

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 3040 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Hands

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint necromancy

### Description

These pale _[[monsters/Gray|gray]]_ gauntlets feature cabochons of banded black onyx that slowly shift to white when the gloves are activated. Three times per day, the wearer can activate the gloves as a swift action after successfully affecting an undead creature with an offensive single-target spell. Doing so grants the wearer a number of temporary hit points equal to 10 + the Hit Dice of the affected undead (maximum +10), which last a number of minutes equal to the level of the spell cast. This effect ends immediately if either glove is removed.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1520 gp
