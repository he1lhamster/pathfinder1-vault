# Headband of Seduction

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 40000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Headband

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint enchantment, faint transmutation

### Description

This plain white band makes the wearer seem more attractive. The wearer gains a +2 competence bonus on Charisma-based checks with other members of his creature type. If the wearer is of the humanoid type, he gains a +5 competence bonus on creatures of his subtype instead. Once per day, the wearer can attempt to affect a creature as the spell _[[spells/Unnatural Lust|unnatural lust]]_ (Ultimate Magic) but with a duration of 10 minutes.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 20000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _unnatural lust_
