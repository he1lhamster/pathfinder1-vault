# Gloves of Reconnaissance

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 2000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Hands

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

Each of these fingerless worked leather gloves look as though they have seen heavy use, and often bear the stains or _[[universal monster rules/Scent|scent]]_ of grass or wet mud. On _[[spells/Command|command]]_, the wearer can use the gloves to see and hear though solid material no more than 5 feet thick by placing both hands on that material. The wearer can use the gloves once per day for up to 1 minute. The wearer must wear the gloves continuously for 24 hours before activating his ability.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, clairaudience/clairvoyance
