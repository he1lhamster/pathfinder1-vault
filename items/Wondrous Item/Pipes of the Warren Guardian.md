# Pipes of the Warren Guardian

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 6000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint conjuration

### Description

These finely crafted yellow-ivory pipes are bound in bronze and decorated with bits of amber. If the possessor learns the proper tune, once per day he can use these pipes to _[[universal monster rules/Summon|summon]]_ 1d4+1 dire rats as if using _[[spells/Summon Monster III|summon monster III]]_. The summoned dire rats also have the swarming racial trait, and count as _[[monsters/Ratfolk|ratfolk]]_ for the purposes of the _ratfolk_'s swarming racial trait.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 3000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _summon monster III_
