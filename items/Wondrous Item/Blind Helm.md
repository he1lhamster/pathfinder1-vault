# Blind Helm

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 85000 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Head

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 13 **Aura:** strong _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

This plain steel helm completely covers the face, lacking even eyeholes or a slit to peer out of. Its wearer is _[[conditions/Blinded|blinded]]_ by the helm, but gains _[[universal monster rules/Blindsense|blindsense]]_ with a range of 60 feet. As a swift action, the wearer can _[[spells/Expend|expend]]_ one use of mythic power to gain _[[universal monster rules/Blindsight|blindsight]]_ with a range of 60 feet for 1 minute, or instead _expend_ two uses of mythic power to gain _blindsight_ with a range of 120 feet for 1 minute. The wearer can _expend_ three uses of mythic power to gain the effects of truesight for 1 minute.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 42500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, Mythic Crafter, _[[spells/Echolocation|echolocation]]_, _[[spells/True Seeing|true seeing]]_
