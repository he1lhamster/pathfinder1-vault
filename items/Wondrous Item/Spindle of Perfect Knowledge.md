# Spindle of Perfect Knowledge

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** – **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 20 **Aura:** strong transmutation

### Description

The _[[items/Wondrous Item/Spindle of Perfect Knowledge|spindle of perfect knowledge]]_ is large, bright, and flawless marquise-cut diamond ioun stone the size of a halfling's fist. The gemstone would be a treasure in itself if it lacked magic, but it is also a powerful ioun stone. The _spindle of perfect knowledge_ functions like a _[[items/Wondrous Item/Headband of Mental Superiority +4|headband of mental superiority +4]]_ and grants 5 ranks in two random Knowledge skills. The wearer can communicate and understand languages as if using _[[spells/Comprehend Languages|comprehend languages]]_, _[[spells/Tongues|tongues]]_, and _[[universal monster rules/Telepathy|telepathy]]_ with a range of 100 feet.

### _[[spells/Destruction|Destruction]]_

The _spindle of perfect knowledge_ is destroyed if worn by a mindless creature for an entire year.
