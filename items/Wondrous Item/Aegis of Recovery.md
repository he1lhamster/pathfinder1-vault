# Aegis of Recovery

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 1500 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Neck

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint abjuration, faint conjuration

### Description

An _[[items/Wondrous Item/Aegis of Recovery|aegis of recovery]]_ grants its wearer a +2 _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ bonus on all saving throws made to recover from a continuing effect, such as ongoing poison damage, daily saving throws against a disease, a _[[spells/Hold Person|hold person]]_ spell, or lingering damage from an attack. This bonus does not help against initial saving throws against such effects. Should the wearer ever drop below 0 hit points, the _[[items/Shield/Aegis|aegis]]_ heals the wearer for 2d8+3 points of damage and then crumbles to dust.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 750 gp
