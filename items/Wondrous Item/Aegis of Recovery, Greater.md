# Aegis of Recovery, Greater

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 3750 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Neck

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate abjuration, moderate conjuration

### Description

This _[[items/Shield/Aegis|aegis]]_ has a thick medallion that is typically carved with the image of bared fangs or a poisoned _[[items/Weapon/Dagger|dagger]]_. It functions as an _[[items/Wondrous Item/Aegis of Recovery|aegis of recovery]]_, except it grants the wearer a +5 _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ bonus on all saving throws to recover from a continuing effect, such as ongoing poison damage, daily saving throws against a disease, a _[[spells/Hold Person|hold person]]_ spell, or lingering damage from an attack. Should the wearer drop below 0 hit points, the greater _aegis of recovery_ heals the wearer for 5d8+7 points of damage and then crumbles to dust.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1875 gp
