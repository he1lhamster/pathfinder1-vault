# Torc of Innocuous Gems

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 1800 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Neck

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate illusion

### Description

This simple platinum necklace has five sockets for gems. An ioun stone placed in a socket registers as nonmagical, as per _[[spells/Magic Aura|magic aura]]_, and confers no benefit upon the wearer. With a _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word, the wearer can release all ioun stones held within the necklace, _[[spells/Sending|sending]]_ them into orbit around her head and gaining their full benefits.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 900 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _magic aura_
