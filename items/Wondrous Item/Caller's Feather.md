# Caller's Feather

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 2000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Head

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 15 **Aura:** strong conjuration

### Description

This magnificent feather, plucked from the wing of a powerful erinyes or angel, allows the wearer to call stronger outsiders to her service. The wearer can use the feather to call 2 more HD worth of creatures when casting a _[[spells/Planar Binding|planar binding]]_ or _[[spells/Planar Ally|planar ally]]_ spell (including lesser and greater versions) or when casting _[[spells/Gate|gate]]_. Alternatively, when casting one of these spells, the spellcaster can _[[spells/Expend|expend]]_ this feather to _[[universal monster rules/Summon|summon]]_ a single outsider with up to 2 more Hit Dice than the spell would normally allow. For example, he may _summon_ a trumpet archon with _planar ally_, or a pit fiend with greater _planar binding_. The wearer gains a +2 insight bonus on all Charisma checks and Charisma-based skill checks to influence the creature _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Called|called]]_. Once used, the feather crumbles to mundane dust.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _planar ally_ or _planar binding_
