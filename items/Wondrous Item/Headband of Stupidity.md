# Headband of Stupidity

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 8000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Headband

##### Magical properties

**Cursed**

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** strong transmutation

### Description

This headband appears to be a headband of vast intellect +4, but instead of granting a +4 bonus to Intelligence and bonuses to associated skills, the wearer take a –4 penalty to Intelligence and a further –2 penalty on all Knowledge checks. The wearer is not aware of the _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Debilitating|debilitating]]_ effects of the headband; even the stupidest statement or misguided insight seems intelligent to him while he is wearing it.

### Crafting requirements

headband of vast intellect +4
