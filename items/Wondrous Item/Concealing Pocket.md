# Concealing Pocket

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 1000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint illusion

### Description

This 4-inch-square cloth pocket has no powers unless it is sewn onto a garment (which takes 1d4 minutes). Once sewn, it changes its appearance to match the garment. Any magic item inside it cannot be magically detected, as if protected by _[[spells/Magic Aura|magic aura]]_. Hiding an item in the pocket gives the garment's wearer a +5 competence bonus on Sleight of Hand checks to conceal the item. The pocket can be removed from the garment with a DC 10 _[[spells/Heal|Heal]]_ check or an appropriate Craft check such as Craft (cloth) or Craft (leather); failing this roll by 5 or more gives the pocket the _[[conditions/Broken|broken]]_ condition.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _magic aura_
