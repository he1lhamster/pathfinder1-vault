# Nightdrops

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 250 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

This narrow _[[items/Mundane/Vial|vial]]_ holds 5 doses of viscous liquid. When dripped onto a willing creature's eyes, it gives the creature _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_ for 1 hour. The affected creature takes a –2 penalty on saves against light-based spells and effects and gains the _[[universal monster rules/Light Sensitivity|light sensitivity]]_ weakness. A dose affects one creature, regardless of how many eyes it has.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 125 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Beast Shape I|beast shape I]]_
