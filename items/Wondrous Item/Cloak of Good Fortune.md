# Cloak of Good Fortune

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 12500 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Shoulders

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

This elegant cloak has simple prayers to kami stitched into its lining. The wearer gains a +1 luck bonus on attack rolls, and once per day, the wearer can spend 10 minutes reciting prayers from the cloak's lining to pose a single question to a nearby spirit, as per _[[spells/Commune|commune]]_.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 6250 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _commune_, _[[spells/Divine Favor|divine favor]]_

* Disciple's Doctrine
