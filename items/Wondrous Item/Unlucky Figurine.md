# Unlucky Figurine

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 4000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Cursed**

**Caster level:** 11 **Aura:** moderate enchantment

### Description

This figurine appears to be a figurine of wondrous power, but instead of having the ability to animate and serve its owner, it curses that owner with bad luck. While in _[[spells/Possession|possession]]_ of this figurine, the owner takes a –2 penalty to AC and CMD, and a –2 penalty on attack rolls, skill checks, ability checks, and saving throws.

### Crafting requirements

any
