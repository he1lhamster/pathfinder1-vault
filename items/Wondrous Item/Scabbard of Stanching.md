# Scabbard of Stanching

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 5000 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint conjuration

### Description

This fine, red leather sheath is decorated with gold filigree. It fits any bladed slashing weapon. While worn, a _[[items/Wondrous Item/Scabbard of Stanching|scabbard of stanching]]_ protects against _[[universal monster rules/Bleed|bleed]]_ damage. Anytime the wearer suffers a _bleed_ effect, the scabbard automatically stanches it. It has no effect on _bleed_ damage from effects that require a DC 16 or higher caster level or _[[spells/Heal|Heal]]_ check.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 2500 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Cure Light Wounds|cure light wounds]]_
