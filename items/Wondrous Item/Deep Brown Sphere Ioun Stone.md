# Deep Brown Sphere Ioun Stone

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 10000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 12 **Aura:** strong _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

This deep brown ioun stone is streaked with amber and looks much like a _[[monsters/Tiger|tiger]]_'s eye gem save for its perfectly spherical shape. While using this stone, you know at all times exactly how many feet you are under or above sea level.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 5000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Discern Location|discern location]]_, creator must be 12th level
