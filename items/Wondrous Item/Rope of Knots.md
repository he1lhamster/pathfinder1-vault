# Rope of Knots

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 6000 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This rope is 100 feet long and can perform all the functions of a _[[items/Wondrous Item/Rope of Climbing|rope of climbing]]_. Furthermore, upon _[[spells/Command|command]]_ the _[[items/Wondrous Item/Rope of Knots|rope of knots]]_ snakes out and knots itself into a rope structure. The rope can tie itself into any structure which can be created out of 100 feet of rope. Possible structures include (but are not limited to) a sturdy rope bridge up to 25 feet long, a 10-foot-square _[[items/Mundane/Net|net]]_ or _[[items/Mundane/Hammock|hammock]]_, or a 40-foot-tall rope _[[items/Mundane/Ladder|ladder]]_. The rope takes up to 10 rounds to create a complex structure or half that time to return itself to a simple coil. The rope has hardness 1 and 20 hit points. It repairs damage to itself at a rate of 2 hit points every 10 minutes.

### Crafting requirements
