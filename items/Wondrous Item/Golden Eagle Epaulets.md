# Golden Eagle Epaulets

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 4500 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Shoulders

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint abjuration

### Description

These decorative golden braids allow the wearer to cast _[[spells/Bless|bless]]_ and _[[spells/Sanctuary|sanctuary]]_ each once per day. They also grant a +2 competence bonus on Diplomacy checks and checks for one other skill, depending on what branch of the _[[monsters/Eagle|Eagle]]_ Knights the epaulets are attuned to (Perception for the Steel Falcons, Ride for the Golden Legion, Swim for the _[[monsters/Gray|Gray]]_ Corsairs, or Stealth for the Twilight Talons).

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 2250 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _bless_, _[[spells/Guidance|guidance]]_, _sanctuary_
