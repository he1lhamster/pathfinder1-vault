# Bracers of Celestial Intervention

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 16000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Wrists

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 13 **Aura:** strong conjuration

### Description

Prayers and engravings of celestial beings cover these golden bracers. A _[[classes/Paladin|paladin]]_ can _[[spells/Expend|expend]]_ one or more of her uses of smite evil into these bracers as a standard action to call forth agathions, angels, or archons to aid her. This acts as a _[[universal monster rules/Summon|summon]]_ monster spell of a spell level equal to the number of smite evil uses the _paladin_ expends. Outsiders summoned with the bracers must be from the list detailed in the _summon_ monster spell or from the expanded _summon_ monster list on pages 28–29.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 8000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Summon Monster VII|summon monster VII]]_, creator must be lawful good
