# Tome of Forgotten Epithets

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 108000 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 15 **Aura:** strong abjuration

### Description

The cover of this hefty, leather-bound volume has an ornate spine adorned with a stylized eye worked in metal and jewels hinges and is sealed shut by a strap with a brass scarab fastener. When first created, a _[[items/Wondrous Item/Tome of Forgotten Epithets|tome of forgotten epithets]]_ has 50 pages. Each page can hold the name of a creature, along with a short description that distinguishes it from other creatures with the same name. The wielder of a _tome of forgotten epithets_ can speak a _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word to cause the _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Burning|burning]]_ eye on the cover to close, protecting those named within as per _[[spells/Mind Blank|mind blank]]_ for 24 hours and sealing the book shut for this duration. Afterward, the eye on the cover reopens, and any pages that held names _[[spells/Vanish|vanish]]_.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 54000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _mind blank_, _[[spells/Secret Page|secret page]]_
