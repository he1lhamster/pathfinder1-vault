# Plague Mask

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 7500 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Head

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint necromancy

### Description

This stylized bird _[[items/Mundane/Mask|mask]]_ consists of a pair of goggles attached to a ceramic beak that covers the nose and mouth. The goggles are not magic and can be replaced with other goggles or eye slot items. It grants the wearer a +4 _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|resistance]]_ bonus on saving throws against disease. Once per day on _[[spells/Command|command]]_, the user can _[[spells/Remove Disease|remove disease]]_ (as the spell) on a single creature touched.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 3750 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _remove disease_
