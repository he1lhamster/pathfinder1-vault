# Robe of Arcane Heritage

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 16000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Body

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate necromancy

### Description

These elegant, dark purple robes are usually decorated with gold stitching suggesting a particular _[[classes/Sorcerer|sorcerer]]_ bloodline, though some might indicate a family tree. When a _sorcerer_ dons a _[[items/Wondrous Item/Robe of Arcane Heritage|robe of arcane heritage]]_, the stitching pulls itself apart and reweaves to match her particular _sorcerer_ bloodline. The wearer treats her _sorcerer_ level as 4 higher than normal for the purpose of determining what bloodline powers she can use and their effects.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 8000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _[[spells/Speak with Dead|speak with dead]]_, creator must be a _sorcerer_
