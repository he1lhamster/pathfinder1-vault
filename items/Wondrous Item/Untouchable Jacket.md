# Untouchable Jacket

### Properties

**Type:** Wondrous Item **Price:** 18000 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Body

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate abjuration

### Description

This black jacket is marked with the insignia of the Blackjackets. Once per day, the wearer can speak a _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word to gain _[[universal monster rules/Spell Resistance|spell resistance]]_ 20 for 3 rounds. This _spell resistance_ ends if the wearer successfully resists a spell as a result of it failing to penetrate his _spell resistance_. The first time each day that a spell has no effect on the wearer because of _spell resistance_ or a successful saving throw, the jacket becomes charged with abjuration magic and its insignia glows faintly. The wearer can touch the glowing insignia as a standard action to cast _[[spells/Dispel Magic|dispel magic]]_ from the jacket, expending the charge. The charge fades with no effect if unused for 1 hour.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 9000 gp

_[[feats/Craft Wondrous Item|Craft Wondrous Item]]_, _dispel magic_, _spell resistance_
