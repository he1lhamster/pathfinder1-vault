#  Redeemed

**Source** _Champions of Purity pg. 30_
**Aura** strong abjuration and evocation [good] **CL** 12th
**Slot** weapon quality; **Price** +3 bonus; **Weight** —

### Description

A _redeemed _weapon was once a corrupt _unholy _weapon. The
taint of evil has been purged from it, however, and the weapon
is now in all ways a _holy _weapon, dealing an additional 2d6
points of damage against all creatures of evil alignment. In
addition, the wielder gains a sacred bonus on saving throws
equal to the weapon’s enhancement bonus against the spells
and abilities of evil outsiders and a +5 competence bonus on
Knowledge (planes) checks to identify evil outsiders and their
special powers or vulnerabilities. When the _redeemed _weapon
special ability is added to an _unholy _weapon, the weapon loses
the _unholy _property and gains the _redeemed _property, and the
crafter must pay the price of adding a +1 enhancement bonus
to the weapon.

### Construction

**Requirements** Craft Magic Arms and Armor, _consecrate_, _holy smite_, creator must be good; **Price** —
