#  Speed

**Source** _Ultimate Equipment pg. 148_, _PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 469_
**Aura** moderate transmutation **CL** 7th
**Slot** weapon quality; **Price** +3 bonus; **Weight** —

### Description

When making a full-attack action, the wielder of a speed weapon may make one extra attack with it. The attack uses the wielder’s full base attack bonus, plus any modifiers appropriate to the situation. (This benefit is not cumulative with similar effects, such as a _haste _spell.)

### Construction

**Requirements** Craft Magic Arms and Armor, _haste_; **Price** —
