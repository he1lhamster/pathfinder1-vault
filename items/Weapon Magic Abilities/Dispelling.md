#  Dispelling

**Source** _Ultimate Equipment pg. 140_
**Aura** strong abjuration **CL** 10th
**Slot** weapon quality; **Price** +1 bonus; **Weight** —

### Description

A _dispelling _weapon functions like a _spell storing _weapon, but it may only store _dispel magic_; however, the caster level check to dispel gains an additional bonus equal to the weapon’s enhancement bonus. This bonus also applies to a magus’s dispelling strike arcana or a barbarian’s spell sunder or sunder enchantment combat maneuver check.

### Construction

**Requirements** Craft Magic Arms and Armor, _dispel magic_, creator must be a caster of at least 10th level; **Price** —
