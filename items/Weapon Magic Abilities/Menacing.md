#  Menacing

**Source** _Ultimate Equipment pg. 145_, _Advanced Player's Guide pg. 288_
**Aura** moderate illusion **CL** 10th
**Slot** weapon quality; **Price** +1 bonus; **Weight** —

### Description

This special ability can only be placed on melee weapons. A _menacing _weapon helps allies deal with flanked foes. When the wielder is adjacent to a creature that is being flanked by an ally, the flanking bonus on attack rolls for all flanking allies increases by +2. This ability works even if the wielder is not one of the characters flanking the creature.

### Construction

**Requirements** Craft Magic Arms and Armor, _phantasmal killer_; **Price** —
