#  Vicious

**Source** _Ultimate Equipment pg. 149_, _PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 469_
**Aura** moderate necromancy **CL** 9th
**Slot** weapon quality; **Price** +1 bonus; **Weight** —

### Description

This special ability can only be placed on melee weapons. When a _vicious _weapon strikes an opponent, it creates a flash of disruptive energy that resonates between the opponent and the wielder. This energy deals an extra 2d6 points of damage to the opponent and 1d6 points of damage to the wielder.

**Requirements** Craft Magic Arms and Armor, _enervation_; **Price** —
