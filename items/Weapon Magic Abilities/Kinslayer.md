#  Kinslayer

**Source** _Quests and Campaigns pg. 30_
**Aura** moderate conjuration **CL** 8th
**Slot** none; **Price** +1 bonus; **Weight** —

### Description

A _kinslayer _weapon is enhanced with enmity against all those
born into the wielder’s familial line. Against creatures related to
the wielder, this weapon’s enhancement bonus is +2 better than
its actual bonus. It also deals an extra 2d6 points of damage
against such creatures. This extra damage is not multiplied on
a critical hit.

### Construction

**Requirements** Craft Magic Arms and Armor, _detect relations_; **Price** —
