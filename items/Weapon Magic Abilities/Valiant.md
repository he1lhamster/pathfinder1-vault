#  Valiant

**Source** _Ultimate Equipment pg. 149_
**Aura** faint divination **CL** 5th
**Slot** weapon quality; **Price** +1 bonus; **Weight** —

### Description

This special ability can only be placed on melee weapons. When a cavalier wielding a _valiant _weapon targets a foe with his challenge ability, the _valiant _weapon deals an additional 1d6 points of damage against that foe. The wielder receives a +2 bonus on combat maneuver checks made to disarm or sunder a challenged foe’s weapon as well as a +4 bonus to his CMD against disarm and sunder attacks from that foe.

### Construction

**Requirements** Craft Magic Arms and Armor, _true strike_; **Price** —
