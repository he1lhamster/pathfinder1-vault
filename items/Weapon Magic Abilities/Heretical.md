#  Heretical

**Source** _Quests and Campaigns pg. 30_
**Aura** moderate evocation **CL** 9th
**Slot** none; **Price** +2 bonus; **Weight** —

### Description

A _heretical _weapon functions as a
fervent weapon, but when wielded
against a member of the religion to
which it is attuned, the weapon’s
enhancement bonus is +2 better
than its actual bonus. It also deals an
extra 2d6 points of damage against
followers of the designated religion.
This extra damage is not multiplied on
a critical hit.

### Construction

**Requirements** Craft Magic Arms and Armor, _align weapon_, _weapon of awe_APG, creator must be a member of the attuned religion; **Price** —
