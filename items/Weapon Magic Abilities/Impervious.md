#  Impervious

**Source** _Ultimate Equipment pg. 144_
**Aura** moderate transmutation **CL** 7th
**Slot** weapon quality; **Price** +3,000 gp; **Weight** —

### Description

An _impervious _weapon is warded from damage and decay. A metallic weapon cannot rust and a wooden weapon cannot rot or warp, even by magical or supernatural means. An _impervious_ weapon gains double the normal bonus to its hardness and hit points for each point of its enhancement bonus. The break DC for an _impervious _weapon and the wielder’s combat maneuver defense against sunder maneuvers against the _impervious_ weapon each gain a bonus equal to twice the weapon’s enhancement bonus.

### Construction

**Requirements** Craft Magic Arms and Armor, _fabricate_, _make whole_; **Price** +1,500 gp
