# Dagger

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 2 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Range:** 10 ft. **Damage type:** P and S **Weapon groups:** Light Blades

### Description

A _[[items/Weapon/Dagger|dagger]]_ is a fighting knife. Its blade is usually less than 1 foot long, and can be curved or straight, with one edge or two. _Dagger_ designs vary widely, and can have many different names (such as a thrusting _dagger_ being _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Called|called]]_ a "dirk"). You get a +2 bonus on Sleight of Hand skill checks made to conceal a _dagger_ on your body.
