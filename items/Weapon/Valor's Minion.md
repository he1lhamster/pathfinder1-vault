# Valor's Minion

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 41335 gp **Weight:** 6 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d10 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +2 **Damage type:** S

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 15 **Aura:** strong evocation

### Description

The blade of this +2 holy _[[items/Weapon/Bastard sword|bastard sword]]_ always appears lustrous and polished, with a hilt wrapped in alternating colors of deep black and royal purple. Effective on its own, a _[[items/Weapon/Valor's Minion|valor's minion]]_ is even more _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Potent|potent]]_ in the hands of those who dedicate their efforts to vanquishing a single foe. Whenever a wielder uses either the challenge or smite evil class feature, she can _[[spells/Sacrifice|sacrifice]]_ one additional use of that class feature to treat the smite or challenge as if she were three class levels higher.
