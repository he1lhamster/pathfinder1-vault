# Lantern staff

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 15 gp **Weight:** 9 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** x3 **Damage type:** B **Weapon groups:** Hammers

### Description

This long metal staff has reservoirs for _[[items/Mundane/Lamp|lamp]]_ oil, and a lantern-like structure at its head. The _[[items/Weapon/Lantern staff|lantern staff]]_ is fueled as and provides light as a _[[items/Mundane/Hooded lantern|hooded lantern]]_. While lit, attacks with the _lantern staff_ deal 1 point of fire damage in addition to the normal damage. Any effects that apply to a _[[items/Weapon/Quarterstaff|quarterstaff]]_, except those that require it be used as a double weapon, also apply to a _lantern staff_.
