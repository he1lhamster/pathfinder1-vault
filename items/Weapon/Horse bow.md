# Horse bow

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 150 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Ranged **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x3 **Range:** 110 ft. **Damage type:** P **Weapon groups:** Bows

### Description

The _[[items/Weapon/Horse bow|horse bow]]_ functions like a _[[items/Weapon/Composite longbow|composite longbow]]_, but it is designed to be used on horseback. A rider using a _horse bow_ can make ranged attacks while mounted; furthermore, she does not take a penalty to attack rolls when her _[[spells/Mount|mount]]_ is taking a double move action, and she takes only a –4 penalty on attack rolls when her _mount_ is running. The price of this item is for a default _horse bow_; each point of Strength bonus granted by the _horse bow_ adds 150 gp to its cost.
