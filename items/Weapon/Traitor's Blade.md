# Traitor's Blade

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 50302 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +2 **Range:** 10 ft. **Damage type:** P and S

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 14 **Aura:** strong enchantment, strong necromancy

### Description

This +2 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Wounding|wounding]]_ (Ultimate Equipment 149) _[[items/Weapon/Dagger|dagger]]_ allows the wielder to use _[[spells/Charm Monster|charm monster]]_ (DC 18) three times per day. When the wielder hits a creature affected by the _dagger_'s charm effect, the _dagger_ deals an additional 2d6 points of damage to that creature, even if the creature is no longer charmed because of the hostile action. All other creatures charmed by the blade that see this act of betrayal become _[[conditions/Sickened|sickened]]_ for 1d4 rounds, but are immediately freed from the charm effect.
