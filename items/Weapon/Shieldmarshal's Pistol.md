### Description

This +1 _[[items/Weapon/Double-barreled pistol|double-barreled pistol]]_ contains a single grit feat from the following list: _[[feats/Deft Shootist Deed|Deft Shootist Deed]]_, _[[feats/Fabulist|Fabulist]]_ (Pathfinder Player Companion: Champions of Balance 19), _[[feats/Gun Twirling|Gun Twirling]]_ (Champions of Balance 19), _[[feats/Leaping Shot Deed|Leaping Shot Deed]]_, _[[feats/No Name|No Name]]_, and _[[feats/Ricochet Shot Deed|Ricochet Shot Deed]]_. Anyone who wields or carries the _[[items/Weapon/Shieldmarshal's Pistol|shieldmarshal's pistol]]_ and meets the feat's prerequisites gains the feat's benefit. The wielder can't use the _[[items/Weapon/Pistol|pistol]]_'s feat to meet prerequisites.
