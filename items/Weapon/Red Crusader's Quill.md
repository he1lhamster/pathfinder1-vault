# Red Crusader's Quill

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 25320 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** 18-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +3 **Damage type:** P

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate necromancy, moderate transmutation

### Description

An artful _[[items/Weapon/Rapier|rapier]]_ with a lotus petal _[[npcs/Guard|guard]]_, this magnificent weapon dedicated to Arazni's former incarnation is useful to warriors and artists alike.

The tip of this narrow +3 _rapier_ can be used as a mundane writing instrument, producing any color of _[[items/Mundane/Ink|ink]]_ the wielder desires. Once per day as a free action when the wielder confirms a critical hit against a creature with the _[[items/Weapon/Red Crusader's Quill|Red Crusader's quill]]_, she can inscribe an _[[spells/Arcane Mark|arcane mark]]_ on the creature. Once per month, the wielder can instead inscribe a _[[spells/Mark of Justice|mark of justice]]_; this mark ends immediately if the wielder inscribes another _mark of justice_ with the _Red Crusader's quill_.
