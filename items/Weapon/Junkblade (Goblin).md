# Junkblade (Goblin)

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 9928 gp **Weight:** 0.5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** S **Special:** Fragile

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

The following weapon is generally available only to members of the indicated race or ethnicity.

This foul-smelling +1 animal-bane _[[items/Weapon/Dogslicer|dogslicer]]_ looks like it was crafted from an assortment of oddities chosen at random from a scrap heap. On _[[spells/Command|command]]_, the wielder can cause the blade to break apart into various pieces of junk that are not obviously a weapon. A second _command_ word causes the junk to reform into a weapon.
