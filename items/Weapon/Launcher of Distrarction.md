# Launcher of Distrarction

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 10550 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Ranged **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Range:** 30 ft. **Damage type:** P

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 6 **Aura:** moderate illusion

### Description

Twice per day, as a swift action when the wielder of this +1 _[[items/Weapon/Heavy wrist launcher|heavy wrist launcher]]_ makes an attack with the weapon, he can have the launcher make no sound, and can instead create a false sound of a crossbow being fired from another location within 40 feet of him. In addition, for 6 rounds, any auras upon the wielder that would normally be revealed by _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_ spells, such as detect spells, are redirected to the same location as the crossbow sound.

**Crafting cost:** 5550 gp
