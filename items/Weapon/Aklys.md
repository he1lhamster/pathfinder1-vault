# Aklys

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** x2 **Range:** 20 ft. **Damage type:** B **Weapon groups:** Hammers **Special:** Performance, _[[universal monster rules/Trip|Trip]]_

### Description

The _[[items/Weapon/Aklys|aklys]]_ is a hooked _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Throwing|throwing]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Club|club]]_ with a 20-foot-long cord, usually of woven leather; you can retrieve the _aklys_ after _throwing_ it as a move action. The hook allows you to make _trip_ attacks at range. Some aklyses have holes drilled through them and _[[items/Mundane/Whistle|whistle]]_ when flung.
