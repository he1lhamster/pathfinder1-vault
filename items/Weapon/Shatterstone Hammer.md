# Shatterstone Hammer

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 26712 gp **Weight:** 15 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x3 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** B

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** strong transmutation

### Description

The head of this +1 _[[items/Weapon/Warhammer|warhammer]]_ is made from magically treated stone that is as hard as metal. A _[[items/Weapon/Shatterstone Hammer|shatterstone hammer]]_ deals double damage against unattended earthen and stone objects, and gains the benefit of being a _[[spells/Bane|bane]]_ weapon when used against creatures of the earth subtype and constructs made of _[[items/Mundane/Clay|clay]]_ or stone. Once per day on a successful attack made with the _[[items/Mundane/Hammer|hammer]]_, the wielder can cause the target to be slowed (as the _[[spells/Slow|slow]]_ spell). A DC 14 Will save negates this effect.
