# Frost Brand

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 54475 gp **Weight:** 8 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Light **Damage:** 2d6 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +3 **Damage type:** S

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 14 **Aura:** strong evocation

### Description

This +3 frost _[[items/Weapon/Greatsword|greatsword]]_ sheds light as a _[[items/Mundane/Torch|torch]]_ when the temperature drops below 0° F. At such times it cannot be _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Concealed|concealed]]_ when drawn, nor can its light be shut off. Its wielder is protected from fire; the sword absorbs the first 10 points of fire damage each round that the wielder would otherwise take.

A _[[items/Weapon/Frost Brand|frost brand]]_ extinguishes all nonmagical fires in a 20-foot radius. As a standard action, it can also dispel lasting fire spells, but not instantaneous effects. You must succeed at a dispel check (1d20 +14) against each spell to dispel it. The DC to dispel such spells is 11 + the caster level of the fire spell.
