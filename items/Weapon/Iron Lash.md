# Iron Lash

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 90335 gp **Weight:** 6 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d10 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +2 **Damage type:** S **Special material:** Cold Iron

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 15 **Aura:** strong necromancy

### Description

This +2 cold iron _[[items/Weapon/Bastard sword|bastard sword]]_ becomes a +2 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Unholy|unholy]]_ cold iron _bastard sword_ in the hands of an _[[classes/Antipaladin|antipaladin]]_ sworn to the demon lord Nocticula. In the hands of a good creature, _[[items/Weapon/Iron Lash|Iron Lash]]_ bestows one permanent negative level as though it were _unholy_ while granting none of the associated benefits of the _unholy_ special ability.

Once per day, the wielder of this sword can cast _[[spells/Finger Of Death|finger of death]]_ with a range of 60 feet, dealing 150 points of damage on a successful hit. If the target succeeds at a DC 20 Fortitude saving throw, the attack instead deals 3d6+15 points of damage. This is a death effect.
