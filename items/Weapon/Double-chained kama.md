# Double-chained kama

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 8 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d6/1d6 **Critical:** x2/x2 **Damage type:** S **Weapon groups:** Double **Special:** Double, _[[classes/Monk|Monk]]_, Reach, _[[universal monster rules/Trip|Trip]]_

### Description

This weapon comprises a pair of _[[items/Weapon/Kama|kama]]_ connected with an 8-foot length of chain. The wielder can attack as if armed with a single _kama_ in each hand or extend the chain to make a single reach attack. By swinging the chain, the wielder can _[[items/Weapon/Whip|whip]]_ the _kama_ about to disarm or _trip_ opponents. Furthermore, if one of the weapons is dropped, the wielder can retrieve it as a free action by pulling on the chain.
