# Kinbonded Bow

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 3875 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Ranged **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x3 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Range:** 100 ft. **Damage type:** P

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

This stout +1 _[[items/Weapon/Longbow|longbow]]_ is engraved with images of leaping deer. Designed to prevent inaccuracy while hunting in a group, the bow is imbued with magic that allows the wielder to accurately shoot past an ally. At will, the wielder can spend 1 minute attuning the bow to a touched ally. Thereafter, that ally no longer provides soft cover against ranged attacks with the _[[items/Weapon/Kinbonded Bow|kinbonded bow]]_ (Core Rulebook 196). Only one ally can be attuned to the bow at a time.

**Crafting cost:** 2125 gp
