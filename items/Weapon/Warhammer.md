# Warhammer

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 12 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x3 **Damage type:** B **Weapon groups:** Hammers

### Description

This weapon consists of a wooden haft and a heavy, metal head. The head may be single (like a carpenter's _[[items/Mundane/Hammer|hammer]]_) or double (like a sledgehammer). The haft is long enough that you may wield it one- or two-handed. Though heavy and relatively _[[spells/Slow|slow]]_ to wield, warhammers are capable of delivering immense blows, crushing armor and flesh alike.
