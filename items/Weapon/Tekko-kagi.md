# Tekko-kagi

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 2 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d3 **Critical:** x2 **Damage type:** P **Weapon groups:** Close **Special:** Disarm

### Description

Also known as an iron claw, this device consists of a fanlike structure of three or more 10-inch blades secured to a sturdy handle strapped to the forearm of the off hand. It can be used as an offensive weapon or defensively like a _[[items/Armor/Buckler|buckler]]_, or used to disarm an opponent without provoking an attack of opportunity. It provides its owner with a +2 circumstance bonus on attempts to disarm or sunder swords or other slender-bladed weapons.
