# War razor

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 8 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Damage type:** S **Weapon groups:** Light Blades

### Description

To all appearances, a _[[items/Weapon/War razor|war razor]]_ is an oversized razor or flip knife. Since the razor folds into the handle, no sheath is required, making the weapon easy to hide and thus granting the wielder a +2 bonus on Sleight of Hand checks to conceal the weapon on her body.
