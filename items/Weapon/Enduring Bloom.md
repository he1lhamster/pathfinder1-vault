# Enduring Bloom

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 3848 gp **Weight:** 6 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** B and P

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint conjuration, faint enchantment

### Description

This +1 _[[items/Weapon/Morningstar|morningstar]]_ has a rose design on the handle and the spikes on its head resemble thorns. Once per day, the wielder can use _[[spells/Bless|bless]]_. Additionally, once per day when the wielder is reduced to 0 hit points or fewer, the weapon automatically casts _[[spells/Cure Light Wounds|cure light wounds]]_ on the wielder at the start of her next turn, so long as it remains in the same square as the wielder.
