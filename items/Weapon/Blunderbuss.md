# Blunderbuss

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 2000 gp **Weight:** 8 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x2 **Range:** 10 ft. **Damage type:** B and P

### Description

This weapon fires pellets or a bullet from its trumpet-shaped _[[items/Mundane/Barrel|barrel]]_, making it an effective fowling weapon or closefighting personal defense weapon. The _[[items/Weapon/Blunderbuss|blunderbuss]]_ fires in a 15-foot cone when firing pellets, and has a 10-foot range increment when firing a bullet. A _blunderbuss_ uses a bullet or pellets and a single dose of black _[[items/Mundane/Powder|powder]]_ or a single alchemical cartridge as ammunition. This is an early firearm.
