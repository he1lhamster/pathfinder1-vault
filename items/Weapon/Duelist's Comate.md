### Description

This +2 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Countering|countering]]_ _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Dueling|dueling]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Rapier|rapier]]_ is particularly well _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Balanced|balanced]]_, and features a stout steel ball on the pommel. Three times per day, the wielder can make a surprise melee attack with the pommel as a swift action, as if proficient with the attack and at her normal attack bonus. The attack deals 1d6+2 points of bludgeoning damage, and the critical multiplier is 20/×2.
