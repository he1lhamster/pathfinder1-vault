# Greatclub

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 8 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d10 **Critical:** x2 **Damage type:** B **Weapon groups:** Hammers

### Description

This larger, bulkier version of the common _[[items/Weapon/Club|club]]_ is heavy enough that you can't wield it with one hand. It may be ornate and carved, reinforced with metal, or a simple branch from a tree. Like simple clubs, greatclubs have many names, such as cudgels, bludgeons, shillelaghs, and more.
