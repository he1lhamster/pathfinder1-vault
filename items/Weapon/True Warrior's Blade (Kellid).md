# True Warrior's Blade (Kellid)

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 50350 gp **Weight:** 8 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Light **Damage:** 2d6 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +3 **Damage type:** S

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate enchantment

### Description

The following weapon is generally available only to members of the indicated race or ethnicity.

This +3 _[[items/Weapon/Greatsword|greatsword]]_ appears to be roughly forged and has scrawls of Hallit engraved about the hilt. The wielder gains extra power against arcane spellcasters, such as witches. The blade counts as a _[[spells/Bane|bane]]_ weapon against any creature that has the ability to cast arcane spells (_[[universal monster rules/Spell-Like Abilities|spell-like abilities]]_ don't count, but a dragon's ability to cast as a _[[classes/Sorcerer|sorcerer]]_ does, for example).
