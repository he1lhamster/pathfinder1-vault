# Earth breaker

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 40 gp **Weight:** 14 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 2d6 **Critical:** x3 **Damage type:** B **Weapon groups:** Hammers

### Description

The crude metal of this massive _[[items/Mundane/Hammer|hammer]]_'s head ends in multiple blunt spikes that help concentrate the force of a powerful swing down into a smaller, more focused area, allowing it to _[[feats/Smash|smash]]_ through armor with greater ease. Due to the tremendous size and weight of its head, an _[[items/Weapon/Earth breaker|earth breaker]]_ is poorly _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Balanced|balanced]]_, and requires two hands to properly lift and control.
