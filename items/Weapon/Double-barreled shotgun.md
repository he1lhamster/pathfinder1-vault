# Double-barreled shotgun

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 7000 gp **Weight:** 15 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x2 **Range:** 20 ft. **Damage type:** B and P **Weapon groups:** Firearms

### Description

This twin-barreled _[[items/Weapon/Shotgun|shotgun]]_ can be shot one _[[items/Mundane/Barrel|barrel]]_ at a time, or both together as one attack. A double shot that fires bullets is inaccurate, and takes a –4 penalty on both attacks. A double shot that fires bullets targets only a single creature and increases the damage of each _barrel_ to 2d6 points (Small) or 2d8 points (_[[classes/Medium|Medium]]_) for a total of 4d6 or 4d8 points of damage. A doublebarreled _shotgun_ uses metal cartridges (loaded with either a bullet or pellets) as ammunition. This is an advanced firearm.
