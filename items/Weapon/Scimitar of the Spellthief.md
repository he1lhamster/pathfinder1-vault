# Scimitar of the Spellthief

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 75815 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** 18-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** S

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 13 **Aura:** strong _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

This curved blade of golden metal, a +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Keen|keen]]_ heartseeking _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Spellstealing|spellstealing]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Scimitar|scimitar]]_, is the _[[spells/Bane|bane]]_ of spellcasters. Inscribed with flowing magical script that purports to reveal the inner secrets of magic, the _[[items/Weapon/Scimitar of the Spellthief|scimitar of the spellthief]]_ grants the wielder a +2 bonus on Spellcraft and Use Magic Device checks. The _scimitar_ also reveals magical auras by touch; this functions as _[[spells/Detect Magic|detect magic]]_ but affects only a single object or creature at a time, which must be touched with the blade while the wielder concentrates on detection, and the magical auras revealed are clearly visible to all.
