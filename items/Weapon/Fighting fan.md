### Properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** x3 **Damage type:** P **Weapon groups:** Close **Special:** _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Distracting|Distracting]]_, _[[classes/Monk|Monk]]_

### Description

Made to resemble a courtier's fan, this device consists of silk paper stretched and folded over a _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Folding|folding]]_ frame of hardened steel. _Folding_ fans are typically wielded in pairs using a technique that distracts the opponent. The fans sport sharp tips, which can be poisoned. For a list of appropriate poisons, see Poisons on page 110.
