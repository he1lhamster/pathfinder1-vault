# Seven-branched sword

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 50 gp **Weight:** 7 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d10 **Critical:** x3 **Damage type:** S **Weapon groups:** Heavy Blades **Special:** Disarm, _[[classes/Monk|Monk]]_

### Description

This unusual sword has a straight 2-foot-long blade with six shorter L-shaped blades protruding from it, three on each side in a _[[conditions/Staggered|staggered]]_ pattern. The shorter blades can be used to snag opponents' clothing or armor, or can target weapons in order to disarm them. To snag armor or clothing, the attacker makes a _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_ attempt. If successful, the victim doesn't fall _[[conditions/Prone|prone]]_, but instead is snagged and stumbles forward, leaving the victim _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ for the remainder of the round.
