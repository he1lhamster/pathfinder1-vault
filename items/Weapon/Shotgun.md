# Shotgun

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 5000 gp **Weight:** 12 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x2 **Range:** 20 ft. **Damage type:** B and P **Weapon groups:** Firearms

### Description

This advanced version of the _[[items/Weapon/Blunderbuss|blunderbuss]]_ shoots in a 30-foot cone when firing pellets, and has a 20-foot range increment when firing a bullet (often _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Called|called]]_ a slug). Shotguns can either be simple and functional, or art pieces in their own right, with engravings and inlay on the stocks and breeches. Those used by the upper class are often emblazoned with images of game animals, family crests, or elaborate maker's marks.

A _[[items/Weapon/Shotgun|shotgun]]_ uses metal cartridges (loaded with either a bullet or pellets) as ammunition. This is an advanced firearm.
