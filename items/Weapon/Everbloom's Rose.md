# Everbloom's Rose

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 3000 gp **Weight:** 6 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** B and P

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint abjuration

### Description

Often created by evangelistic clerics of Milani, an _[[items/Weapon/Everbloom's Rose|Everbloom's rose]]_ is a +1 _[[items/Weapon/Morningstar|morningstar]]_. Once per day, the wielder of an _Everbloom's rose_ can cast _[[spells/Remove Fear|remove fear]]_ on a creature touched by the weapon. If the subject succeeds at a saving throw against a _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_ effect while under effects of this spell, the subject gains a +1 morale bonus on attack and damage rolls against the source of the _fear_ effect for 1 round. If the subject is immune to _fear_ effects, she gains no bonuses from this effect.
