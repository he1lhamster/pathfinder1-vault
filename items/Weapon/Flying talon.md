# Flying talon

### Description

This weapon consists of a three-pronged, barbed hook attached to a length of chain. Due to the weapon's unwieldiness, you cannot make attacks of opportunity with a _[[items/Weapon/Flying talon|flying talon]]_ and do not threaten any squares with it.
