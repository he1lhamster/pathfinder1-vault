# Ripsaw glaive, gnome

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 30 gp **Weight:** 12 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d10 **Critical:** x3 **Damage type:** S **Weapon groups:** _[[items/Mundane/Pole|Pole]]_ Arms **Special:** Reach

### Description

The blade of this _[[items/Weapon/Glaive|glaive]]_ is serrated and mounted on an axle, around which a heavy cord is wrapped. When the cord is pulled as a move action, the blade spins rapidly for a number of rounds equal to your Strength bonus. While the blade is spinning, you gain a +2 bonus on damage rolls with the weapon. When the blade is not spinning, treat this weapon as a _glaive_.
