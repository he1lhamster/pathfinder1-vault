# Tetsubo

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 20 gp **Weight:** 10 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d10 **Critical:** x4 **Damage type:** B **Weapon groups:** Hammers

### Description

A slight improvement over the _[[items/Weapon/Greatclub|greatclub]]_, this weapon consists of a long, slender length of wood or metal whose upper half is shod with a thick sheet of studded iron. The iron gives added weight and strength to the _[[items/Weapon/Club|club]]_, while the studs are designed to crack helmets and armor.
