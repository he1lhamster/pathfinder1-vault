# Noriznigath

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** – **Weight:** 10 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +6 **Damage type:** S

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 20 **Aura:** strong evocation

### Description

The favored blade of the Storm _[[npcs/King|King]]_, _[[items/Weapon/Noriznigath|Noriznigath]]_ is named for the first nascent demon lord it was used to behead, and is a Large +6 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Unholy|unholy]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Longsword|longsword]]_ —as with all slashing weapons, _Noriznigath_ becomes a _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Vorpal|vorpal]]_ weapon in the hands of a balor. The body of a creature decapitated by _Noriznigath_ is disintegrated if the decapitation results in death, and heals the wielder of 100 points of damage.

### _[[spells/Destruction|Destruction]]_

_Noriznigath_ melts into blood and mist if its namesake, the long-dead nascent demon lord _Noriznigath_, is restored to life.
