# Trident of the Storm Captain

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 109665 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +4 **Range:** 10 ft. **Damage type:** P **Special:** Brace

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 13 **Aura:** strong evocation, strong transmutation

### Description

This +4 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Shock|shock]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Trident|trident]]_ is carved from fish-bone and its points are _[[monsters/Shark|shark]]_ teeth. The wielder gains a +4 competence bonus on Profession (sailor) and Swim checks as long as it is held in hand. Once per day, the wielder can utter a _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word to create one of the following effects: _[[spells/Vortex|vortex]]_ (Pathfinder RPG Advanced Player's Guide 254) or _[[spells/Control Weather|control weather]]_.

If the wielder uses a _[[spells/Polymorph|polymorph]]_ or supernatural effect to take the shape of a creature with a natural bite attack, the _[[items/Weapon/Trident of the Storm Captain|trident of the storm captain]]_ vanishes and the wielder's bite attack gains a +4 enhancement bonus until the shapechanging effect ends and the _trident_ reappears. The wielder cannot use the _trident_'s other abilities while shapechanged.
