# Battle ladder, gnome

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 20 gp **Weight:** 8 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d6/1d6 **Critical:** x2/x2 **Damage type:** B **Special:** Double, _[[universal monster rules/Trip|Trip]]_

### Description

Gnomes use these narrow, reinforced, 4-foot ladders as weapons. Fighting with a battle _[[items/Mundane/Ladder|ladder]]_ is like fighting with a _[[items/Weapon/Quarterstaff|quarterstaff]]_, except that the spaces in the _ladder_ can be used to _[[spells/Entangle|entangle]]_ limbs, granting the _ladder_ the _trip_ weapon feature.
