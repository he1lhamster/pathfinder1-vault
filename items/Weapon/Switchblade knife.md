# Switchblade knife

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Range:** 10 ft. **Damage type:** P **Weapon groups:** Light Blades, Thrown

### Description

This knife has a springloaded blade hidden inside the handle. It can be designed to look like another type of object (a DC 15 Perception check reveals the deception). If held in your hand, you can activate it as a free action. This weapon is treated as a _[[items/Weapon/Dagger|dagger]]_.
