# Kyoketsu shoge

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 6 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** x2 **Range:** 20 ft. **Damage type:** P and S **Weapon groups:** Flails **Special:** Disarm, Grapple, _[[classes/Monk|Monk]]_, Reach

### Description

This weapon consists of a foot-long double-edged blade, with another smaller, curved hook-blade sticking out the side like a rooster's spur. The blade is strung to a large iron hoop with a 10-foot length of rope. The blade can be used as an off-handed melee weapon or thrown like a _[[items/Weapon/Dagger|dagger]]_, while the rope and circlet can be whipped around and swung at opponents as a bludgeoning reach weapon.
