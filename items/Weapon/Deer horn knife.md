# Deer horn knife

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 10 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** x3 **Range:** 20 ft. **Damage type:** P **Weapon groups:** Light Blades **Special:** Blocking, _[[classes/Monk|Monk]]_

### Description

This flat, metal weapon consists of two joined crescent-shaped blades overlapping to form a hollow center with four projecting points. The blades can differ in length. One side of the center is wrapped to serve as a grip while the other acts as a _[[npcs/Guard|guard]]_. Deer horn knives are traditionally used in pairs.
