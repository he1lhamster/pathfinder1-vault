# Boarding axe

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 6 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** x3 **Damage type:** P and S **Weapon groups:** Axes

### Description

A _[[items/Weapon/Boarding axe|boarding axe]]_ is similar to a _[[items/Weapon/Handaxe|handaxe]]_, but features a spike that allows the weapon to do either slashing or piercing damage. When in hand, a _boarding axe_ grants a +2 circumstance bonus on _[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_ checks to scale wooden or other penetrable surfaces, such as ice.
