### Description

This massive _[[items/Weapon/Warhammer|warhammer]]_ is even heavier than its size would normally warrant. A creature wielding the _[[items/Mundane/Hammer|hammer]]_ takes a –2 penalty on attack rolls and a +4 bonus on damage rolls, as well as a –10 penalty to speed. Once picked up, this weapon must be used as the creature's primary weapon until subject to a successful _[[spells/Remove Curse|remove curse]]_ spell.
