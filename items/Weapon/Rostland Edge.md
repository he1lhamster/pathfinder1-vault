### Description

Crafted to defend against enemies seeking to damage it rather than its wielder, a _[[items/Weapon/Rostland Edge|Rostland edge]]_ is a +2 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Countering|countering]]_ _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Impervious|impervious]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Aldori _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Dueling|dueling]]_ sword|Aldori _dueling_ sword]]_. If the weapon is ever destroyed, it explodes with tremendous force, dealing 10d6 points of fire damage to every creature within a 20-foot-radius spread (Reflex DC 14 half).
