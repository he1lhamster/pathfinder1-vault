# Elven thornblade

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 60 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** 18-20/x2 **Damage type:** P and S **Weapon groups:** Heavy Blades

### Description

This blade is suited for thrusts and parries, making it especially capable of inflicting deep, penetrating wounds. A wielder with _[[feats/Weapon Finesse|Weapon Finesse]]_ can apply her Dexterity modifier to attack rolls with an _[[items/Weapon/Elven thornblade|elven thornblade]]_ instead of her Strength modifier. An _elven thornblade_ grants no additional benefit when wielded two-handed. In addition, attacks with an _elven thornblade_ gain a +2 bonus on attack rolls made to confirm critical hits.
