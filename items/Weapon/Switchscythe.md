# Switchscythe

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 18 gp **Weight:** 10 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 2d4 **Critical:** x4 **Damage type:** P and S **Weapon groups:** Heavy Blades **Special:** _[[universal monster rules/Trip|Trip]]_

### Description

This _[[items/Weapon/Scythe|scythe]]_ has a spring-loaded blade that folds down into the weapon's handle. Opening the _scythe_ blade is a swift action, and closing it again is a standard action. You can use Bluff or Sleight of Hand to treat a closed _[[items/Weapon/Switchscythe|switchscythe]]_ as a hidden weapon; the apparatus can be disguised with 1 minute of work, which grants a +5 bonus on such checks. This disguise is ruined when the weapon is opened and must be reapplied to hide the weapon again. Any effects that apply to a _scythe_ also apply to a _switchscythe_.
