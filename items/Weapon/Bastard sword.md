# Bastard sword

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 35 gp **Weight:** 6 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d10 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Damage type:** S **Weapon groups:** Heavy Blades

### Description

A _[[items/Weapon/Bastard sword|bastard sword]]_ is about 4 feet in length, making it too large to use in one hand without special _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Training|training]]_; thus, it is an exotic weapon. You can use a _bastard sword_ two-handed as a martial weapon.
