### Description

Created by the druids of the Green Faith, this +1 mithral _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Spell Storing|spell storing]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Sickle|sickle]]_ serves as a divine focus for druids. It can only store spells on the _[[classes/Druid|druid]]_ spell list or from the Air, Earth, Fire, or Water domains or nature bond domain lists.
