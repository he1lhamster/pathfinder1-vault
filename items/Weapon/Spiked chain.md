# Spiked chain

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 25 gp **Weight:** 10 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 2d4 **Critical:** x2 **Damage type:** P **Weapon groups:** Flails **Special:** Disarm, _[[universal monster rules/Trip|Trip]]_

### Description

A _[[items/Weapon/Spiked chain|spiked chain]]_ is about 4 feet in length and covered in wicked barbs. Some have metal hoops to use as handgrips. You can use the _[[feats/Weapon Finesse|Weapon Finesse]]_ feat to apply your Dexterity modifier instead of your Strength modifier to attack rolls with a _spiked chain_ sized for you, even though it isn't a light weapon.
