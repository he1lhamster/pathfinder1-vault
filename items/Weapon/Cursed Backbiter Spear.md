# Cursed Backbiter Spear

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 8301 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +2 **Range:** 20 ft. **Damage type:** P

##### Magical properties

**Cursed**

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

This is a +2 _[[items/Weapon/Shortspear|shortspear]]_, but each time it is used in melee against a foe and the attack roll is a natural 1, it damages its wielder instead of her intended target. When the curse takes effect, the _[[items/Weapon/Spear|spear]]_ curls around to strike its wielder in the back, automatically dealing the damage to the wielder. The curse even functions when the _spear_ is hurled, and in such a case the damage to the hurler is doubled.
