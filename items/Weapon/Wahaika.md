# Wahaika

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 3 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** x2 **Range:** 10 ft. **Damage type:** B **Weapon groups:** Hammers **Special:** Disarm

### Description

This short and broad Stone Age _[[items/Weapon/Club|club]]_ is made of hardened wood or bone. It has a notch on one side that is used for catching weapons. If you are proficient with the weapon, you can use the notch in the _[[items/Weapon/Wahaika|wahaika]]_ to disarm your foes. Otherwise, treat this weapon as a _club_. Feats and abilities that affect clubs apply to the _wahaika_.
