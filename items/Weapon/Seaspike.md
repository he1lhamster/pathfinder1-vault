**Caster level:** 12 **Aura:** strong conjuration, strong transmutation

### Description

This elegant, short-hafted _[[items/Weapon/Spear|spear]]_ has a razor-sharp head carved from a single piece of ivory. A twisting ring of ivory and jade tentacles hovers and slowly rotates just beneath the spearhead. Along the walnut haft sparkle tiny chips of peridot, each the size of a pinhead. _[[items/Weapon/Seaspike|Seaspike]]_ is a +3 aberration _[[spells/Bane|bane]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Shortspear|shortspear]]_ with three special properties.

First, the wielder of _Seaspike_ can cast _[[spells/Fire Shield|fire shield]]_ (chill) on herself three times per day as a 7th-level _[[classes/Sorcerer|sorcerer]]_. The chill _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_ takes the appearance of luminous blue and green flames that swirl around the wielder as if _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Underwater|underwater]]_.

Second, _Seaspike_ grants its wielder a swim speed of 30 feet when held. If the wielder already has a swim speed, _Seaspike_ increases the swim speed by 10 feet.

Third, _Seaspike_ is treated as a light weapon for purposes of the _[[feats/Weapon Finesse|Weapon Finesse]]_ feat and other similar abilities.
