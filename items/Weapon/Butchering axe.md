# Butchering axe

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 65 gp **Weight:** 25 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 3d6 **Critical:** x3 **Damage type:** S **Weapon groups:** Axes

### Description

A creation of the _[[monsters/Orc|orc]]_ smiths of Belkzen, this axe has an oversized head bristling with spikes and a long, thick haft that only barely counterbalances its weight. If your Strength is less than 19 (or 17 for a Small or smaller _[[items/Weapon/Butchering axe|butchering axe]]_), you take a –2 penalty on attacks with it, as you're unable to maneuver its daunting size and weight.
