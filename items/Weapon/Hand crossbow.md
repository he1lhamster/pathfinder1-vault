# Hand crossbow

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 100 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Ranged **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Range:** 30 ft. **Damage type:** P **Weapon groups:** Crossbows

### Description

You can draw a _[[items/Weapon/Hand crossbow|hand crossbow]]_ back by hand. Loading a _hand crossbow_ is a move action that provokes attacks of opportunity. A _hand crossbow_ fires standard crossbow bolts, like those used by light and heavy crossbows.

You can shoot, but not load, a _hand crossbow_ with one hand at no penalty. You can shoot a _hand crossbow_ with each hand, but you take a penalty on attack rolls as if attacking with two light weapons.
