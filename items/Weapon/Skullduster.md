### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 104508 gp **Weight:** 6 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +2 **Damage type:** B and P

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 14 **Aura:** strong abjuration, strong conjuration

### Description

This +2 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Disruption|disruption]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Morningstar|morningstar]]_'s metal head resembles the face of a half-orc. The weapon can cast _[[spells/Remove Paralysis|remove paralysis]]_ three times per day. _[[items/Weapon/Skullduster|Skullduster]]_ rarely speaks, and then usually only to utter grim predictions. The weapon craves the _[[spells/Destruction|destruction]]_ of undead creatures, and it uses _[[spells/Death Ward|death ward]]_ at will so long as its owner diligently pursues this goal. If _Skullduster_'s owner ever parleys with an undead creature, the weapon withholds any further aid, grumbles constantly in _[[monsters/Orc|Orc]]_ about treachery, and seeks a new owner.
