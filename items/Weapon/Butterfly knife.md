# Butterfly knife

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Damage type:** P and S **Weapon groups:** Light Blades

### Description

This weapon has a blade _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Concealed|concealed]]_ between the two halves of the handle that can be brought to bear quickly. If you are proficient with it (or have the _[[feats/Quick Draw|Quick Draw]]_ feat), you may open it as a free action; a nonproficient user must spend a move action to open it. Otherwise, treat it as a _[[items/Weapon/Dagger|dagger]]_. When it's closed, you gain a +2 bonus on Sleight of Hand checks made to conceal it on your body.
