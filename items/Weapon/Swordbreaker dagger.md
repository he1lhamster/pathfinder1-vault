# Swordbreaker dagger

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 10 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** x2 **Damage type:** S **Weapon groups:** Light Blades **Special:** Disarm

### Description

You can use this _[[items/Weapon/Dagger|dagger]]_ to disarm or sunder enemy blades by catching them in the grooves on the back of the blade and using your weapon as a lever. If you are proficient with this weapon, add a +4 bonus on combat maneuver checks to disarm or sunder bladed weapons while using it.
