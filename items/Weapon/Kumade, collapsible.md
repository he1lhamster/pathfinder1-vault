# Kumade, collapsible

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 10 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** x3 **Damage type:** P **Weapon groups:** Axes **Special:** Grapple

### Description

This weapon acts as a _[[items/Weapon/Kumade|kumade]]_, but the handle consists of bamboo sections held together by a taut rope running through the center. Releasing the rope is a standard action that provokes attacks of opportunity and causes the weapon to collapse into a bundle that fits into a backpack. Reassembling the collapsible _kumade_ requires a full-round action that provokes attacks of opportunity.
