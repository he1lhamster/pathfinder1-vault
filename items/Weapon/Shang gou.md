# Shang gou

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 6 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** x2 **Damage type:** S **Weapon groups:** _[[classes/Monk|Monk]]_ **Special:** Disarm, _Monk_, _[[universal monster rules/Trip|Trip]]_

### Description

Forged in pairs, these solid metal blades end in curved hooks, while the hand guards are hammered into outward-facing spiked crescents. Both the crescent and the tip of the hilt are sharpened for combat. They can be wielded as single weapons or with _[[feats/Two-Weapon Fighting|two-weapon fighting]]_ styles. These weapons gain the disarm special feature when they are used individually. A pair of _[[items/Weapon/Shang gou|shang gou]]_ can also be formed into a single one-handed weapon to gain the _trip_ special feature.
