# Leng Flail (greater)

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 50315 gp **Weight:** 10 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d10 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** B **Special:** Disarm, _[[universal monster rules/Trip|Trip]]_

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 6 **Aura:** moderate enchantment, moderate transmutation

### Description

The immense and inscrutable Leng spiders are known to construct makeshift flails by linking nearby debris, such as rocks or skulls, with their own extruded web fibers. Although this organic webbing typically degrades over time, skillful alchemy can _[[spells/Preserve|preserve]]_ one of these weapons indefinitely.

A lesser Leng flail is a +1 _[[items/Weapon/Heavy flail|heavy flail]]_ that allows its wielder to use _[[spells/Spider Climb|spider climb]]_ at will. Much less common are greater Leng flails, which are +2 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Throwing|throwing]]_ heavy flails that have the same _spider climb_ ability of the lesser flail. In addition, when the wielder of a greater Leng flail confirms a critical hit against a creature, the target suffers horrific hallucinations for 1d4 rounds, becoming _[[conditions/Confused|confused]]_ (Will DC 19 negates). This is a mind-affecting effect.
