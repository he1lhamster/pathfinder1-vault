# Dragonbreath Bow

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 50375 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Ranged **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x3 **Enhancement bonus:** +2 **Range:** 100 ft. **Damage type:** P

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 14 **Aura:** strong evocation

### Description

This +2 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Flaming Burst|flaming burst]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Longbow|longbow]]_ 's arrow rest is shaped like a dragon's head with its mouth agape—as if about to unleash its _[[universal monster rules/Breath Weapon|breath weapon]]_. A mythic wielder can _[[spells/Expend|expend]]_ one use of mythic power when drawing back the bow to create an arrow of pure flame. This _[[spells/Flame Arrow|flame arrow]]_ acts as a ranged touch attack that can target only creatures within the first range increment of the bow. When it hits its target, this _flame arrow_ deals 3d10+2 points of fire damage. This damage stacks with the bow's _flaming burst_ special ability.
