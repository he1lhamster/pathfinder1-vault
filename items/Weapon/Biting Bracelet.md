### Description

This +1 _[[items/Weapon/Heavy wrist launcher|heavy wrist launcher]]_ appears to be a lavish, gold bracelet. This functions like the _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Glamered|glamered]]_ weapon special ability (Pathfinder RPG Ultimate Equipment 142), except a _[[items/Weapon/Biting Bracelet|biting bracelet]]_ is able to only assume this one specific form, and can revert to its innocuous appearance 1 round after being used as a weapon.
