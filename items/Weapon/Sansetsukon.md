# Sansetsukon

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 8 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d10 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Damage type:** B **Weapon groups:** Flails **Special:** Blocking, Disarm, _[[classes/Monk|Monk]]_

### Description

Also _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Called|called]]_ a three-section staff, this weapon is divided into three 2-footlong segments chained together, allowing the wielder greater flexibility than a normal staff and allowing the weapon to be swung rapidly to create a defensive block, wrap and catch weapons, or harness momentum via a whipping motion for powerful strikes.
