# Battle poi

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** x2 **Damage type:** None **Weapon groups:** Flails

### Description

This pair of arm-length chains has handles at one end and heavy fuel-soaked _[[items/Mundane/Torch|torch]]_ heads at the other. The weight of the poi is insufficient to deal physical damage, but the _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Burning|burning]]_ fuel deals fire damage. If you are proficient in _[[items/Weapon/Battle poi|battle poi]]_, you are treated as if you have the _[[feats/Two-Weapon Fighting|Two-Weapon Fighting]]_ feat for the purposes of making poi attacks. Poi can be extinguished by spending a full round action smothering them in sand or submerging them in water.
