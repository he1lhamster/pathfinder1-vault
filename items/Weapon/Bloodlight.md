# Bloodlight

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 3970 gp **Weight:** 8 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Light **Damage:** 2d6 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** S

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint enchantment

### Description

This wicked-looking +1 _[[items/Weapon/Greatsword|greatsword]]_ is engraved with glowing Ulfen runes that spell out a Gorumite battle song. When wielded, the blade sheds light as a _[[items/Mundane/Candle|candle]]_. Once per day, the wielder can use _[[spells/Rage|rage]]_ (self only). The runes shine with a murderous red light, equivalent to a _[[items/Mundane/Torch|torch]]_, whenever the _rage_ ability is active and for 1 round whenever the weapon is used to deal damage to a living creature.

**Crafting cost:** 2160 gp
