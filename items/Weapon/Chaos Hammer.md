# Chaos Hammer

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 38312 gp **Weight:** 10 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x3 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** B

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

This oversized _[[items/Mundane/Hammer|hammer]]_ crackles with chaotic energy. Once per day as a standard action, the wielder of this +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Anarchic|anarchic]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Warhammer|warhammer]]_ can strike the ground to create the effects of the _[[spells/Chaos Hammer|chaos hammer]]_ spell (Will DC 16, CL 9th) that bursts out from the _warhammer_'s wielder. A mythic creature wielding this weapon can _[[spells/Expend|expend]]_ one use of mythic power to activate this ability without using up its daily use.
