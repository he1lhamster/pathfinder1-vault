# Elven branched spear

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 20 gp **Weight:** 10 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x3 **Damage type:** P **Weapon groups:** Spears **Special:** Brace, Reach

### Description

Behind this long _[[items/Mundane/Pole|pole]]_ weapon's spearhead, several short branches project from the shaft at irregular intervals, each angled forward and tipped with a smaller leaf like blade. When you make attacks of opportunity provoked by movement, you gain a +2 bonus on attack rolls. You can use the _[[feats/Weapon Finesse|Weapon Finesse]]_ feat to apply your Dexterity modifier instead of your Strength modifier on attack rolls with an _[[items/Weapon/Elven branched spear|elven branched spear]]_ sized for you, even though it isn't a light weapon. Elves treat elven branched spears as martial weapons.
