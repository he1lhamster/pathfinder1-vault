# Staff of the Hooded Cobra

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 42400 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d6/1d6 **Critical:** x2/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +3/+0 **Damage type:** B **Special material:** Darkwood/Darkwood **Special:** Double, _[[classes/Monk|Monk]]_

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate abjuration, moderate necromancy

### Description

Carved from a single piece of darkwood and topped by the head of a hooded cobra, this staff functions as a +3 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Defending|defending]]_ darkwood _[[items/Weapon/Quarterstaff|quarterstaff]]_. Additionally, once per day when the wielder hits a creature with the staff, she can cause the carved cobra head to bite her target. The attack deals an additional 1d6 points of damage and injects the target with a _[[feats/Virulent Poison|virulent poison]]_ (injury; save Fort DC 20; frequency 1/round for 6 rounds; effect 1d2 Str and 1d2 Con damage; cure 2 consecutive saves).
