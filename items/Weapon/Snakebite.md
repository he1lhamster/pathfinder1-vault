##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate necromancy, moderate transmutation

### Description

This +2 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Injecting|injecting]]_ _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Wounding|wounding]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Rapier|rapier]]_ is prized among the cults of Ydersius. This sword increases the save DC of any poison applied to its blade by 1. If the wielder worships Ydersius, she can utter a silent _[[spells/Prayer|prayer]]_ to the god as a swift action up to three times per day. Each _prayer_ creates 1 dose of _[[poisons/Purple Worm Poison|purple worm poison]]_ (Pathfinder RPG Core Rulebook 560) in the sword's pommel reservoir.
