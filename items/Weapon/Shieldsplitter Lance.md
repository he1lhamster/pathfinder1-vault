# Shieldsplitter Lance

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 18310 gp **Weight:** 10 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x3 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** P **Special:** Reach

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

This +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Keen|keen]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Lance|lance]]_ has the ability to penetrate and destroy shields. Whenever its wielder uses it to successfully strike an opponent who carries a _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_, the opponent's _shield_ takes the same damage as the opponent. If the wielder uses the _lance_ to make a sunder attack against a _shield_, it bypasses the _shield_'s hardness.
