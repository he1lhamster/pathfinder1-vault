# Bayonet

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** x2 **Damage type:** P **Weapon groups:** Light Blades

### Description

Bayonets are close combat weapons designed to fit into the grooves or muzzles of crossbows and firearms. They allow you to make melee attacks with these weapons but render them temporarily useless as ranged weapons. Attaching or removing a _[[items/Weapon/Bayonet|bayonet]]_ is a move action.
