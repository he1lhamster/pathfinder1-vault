### Description

Like a _[[items/Weapon/Glaive|glaive]]_, the _[[items/Weapon/Fauchard|fauchard]]_ is a curved blade at the end of a _pole_. However, the cutting edge of a _fauchard_ is along the concave side, like that of a _[[items/Weapon/Sickle|sickle]]_ or _[[items/Weapon/Scythe|scythe]]_.
