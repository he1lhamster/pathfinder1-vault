# Rapier

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 20 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** 18-20/x2 **Damage type:** P **Weapon groups:** Light Blades

### Description

You can use the _[[feats/Weapon Finesse|Weapon Finesse]]_ feat to apply your Dexterity modifier instead of your Strength modifier to attack rolls with a _[[items/Weapon/Rapier|rapier]]_ sized for you, even though it isn't a light weapon. You can't wield a _rapier_ in two hands in order to apply 1-1/2 times your Strength modifier to its damage.
