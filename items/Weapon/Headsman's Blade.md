# Headsman's Blade

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 13850 gp **Weight:** 8 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Light **Damage:** 2d6 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** S

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate necromancy, moderate transmutation

### Description

This scalpel-sharp _[[items/Weapon/Greatsword|greatsword]]_ has a simple black handle and crosspiece, and is made of unusually dark steel etched with an organic wave pattern. Once per day, the wielder of this +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Keen|keen]]_ _greatsword_ can attempt to assassinate a target, per the _[[classes/Slayer|slayer]]_ advanced talent of the same name. If the wielder is not a _slayer_, he must spend a full-round action studying the target before attempting the assassination, and the target's DC to avoid death is 15. In the hands of a _slayer_, the sword's enhancement bonus on attack rolls is 2 better against a studied target, and the DC to avoid death from an assassination attempt increases by 2. Additionally, if the assassination attempt is successful, the target is decapitated instead of merely slain.
