# Shard of Cocytus

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 16302 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Range:** 10 ft. **Damage type:** P and S

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

Appearing as a bloodstained icicle with a worn leather handle, this +1 frost _[[items/Weapon/Dagger|dagger]]_ is _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Impervious|impervious]]_ to both cold and fire. Once per day, the _dagger_ can blast forth a ray of hellfrost at any target within 30 feet as a ranged touch attack, dealing 4d6 points of damage. This hellfrost deals half cold damage and half damage from _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Unholy|unholy]]_ energy. Evil creatures and creatures with the evil subtype take no damage from this _unholy_ energy; good creatures and those with the good subtype take double the usual damage. _[[spells/Protection From Evil|Protection from evil]]_ and similar effects protect against the damage from the ray's _unholy_ energy. If the _[[items/Weapon/Shard of Cocytus|shard of Cocytus]]_ is used to deliver a coup de _[[spells/Grace|grace]]_ against a good creature, it recovers its ray of hellfrost ability for the day.
