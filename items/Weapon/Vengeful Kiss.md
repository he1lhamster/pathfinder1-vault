# Vengeful Kiss

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 11501 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d3 **Critical:** x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** S **Special:** Disarm, Nonlethal, Reach, _[[universal monster rules/Trip|Trip]]_

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint illusion, faint necromancy, faint transmutation

### Description

This +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Deadly|deadly]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Whip|whip]]_ is made of braided black and yellow leather cords, and is as strong as a _[[items/Mundane/Silk rope|silk rope]]_. The bearer of a _[[items/Weapon/Vengeful Kiss|vengeful kiss]]_ can activate the weapon as a standard action to make it stretch up to 50 feet long or retract to normal _whip_ length (although it cannot be used to attack when greater than its normal length). Once per day, a creature wielding the _whip_ can use _[[spells/Invigorate|invigorate]]_.
