# Mace of Terror

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 38552 gp **Weight:** 8 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +2 **Damage type:** B

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 13 **Aura:** strong necromancy

### Description

This weapon usually appears to be a particularly frighteninglooking iron or steel mace. On _[[spells/Command|command]]_, this +2 _[[items/Weapon/Heavy mace|heavy mace]]_ causes the wielder's clothes and appearance to transform into an illusion of darkest horror, such that living creatures in a 30- foot cone become _[[conditions/Panicked|panicked]]_ as if by a _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_ spell (Will DC 16 partial). Those who fail this save take a –2 morale penalty on saving throws, and they flee from the wielder. The wielder may use this ability up to three times per day.
