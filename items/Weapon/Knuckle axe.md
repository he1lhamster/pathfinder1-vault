# Knuckle axe

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 9 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** x3 **Damage type:** S **Weapon groups:** Axes **Special:** _[[classes/Monk|Monk]]_, Performance

### Description

Knuckle axes, usually used in pairs, resemble axe blades mounted on _[[items/Weapon/Brass knuckles|brass knuckles]]_. You may hold, but not wield, a weapon or other object in a hand wearing a _[[items/Weapon/Knuckle axe|knuckle axe]]_. Unlike when wearing _brass knuckles_, you cannot use a hand wearing a _knuckle axe_ for spellcasting.
