# Boomerang

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 3 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Ranged **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** x2 **Range:** 30 ft. **Damage type:** B **Weapon groups:** Thrown

### Description

The _[[items/Weapon/Boomerang|boomerang]]_ is primarily a hunting tool, but it is not uncommon for these versatile thrown weapons to be carried into battle. A _boomerang_ is made of wood and is flat, with a long haft, a pronounced forward curve, and a sharp backward bend at the head. A hunting _boomerang_ does not fly in a returning path.
