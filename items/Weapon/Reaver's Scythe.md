# Reaver's Scythe

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 80318 gp **Weight:** 10 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 2d4 **Critical:** x4 **Enhancement bonus:** +2 **Damage type:** P and S **Special:** _[[universal monster rules/Trip|Trip]]_

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 12 **Aura:** moderate necromancy

### Description

The black blade of this +2 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Keen|keen]]_ _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Unholy|unholy]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Scythe|scythe]]_ reflects faint images of the souls of those it has claimed. Whenever the _scythe_ is used to drop a creature to –1 hit points or less, the creature must immediately make a DC 14 Will save or be instantly slain. If the _[[items/Weapon/Reaver's Scythe|reaver's scythe]]_ kills a creature, either through damage or because the creature failed its Will save, the wielder immediately receives 1 hero point. This hero point must be used within 1 hour or it is lost. The wielder cannot gain another hero point from the _scythe_ until the first is used or lost.
