# Katana, double walking stick

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 50 gp **Weight:** 6 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Damage type:** S

### Description

This single case conceals a pair of matched fighting swords perfectly _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Balanced|balanced]]_ to be wielded as a pair. Despite their name, the blades more closely resemble the shorter _[[items/Weapon/Wakizashi|wakizashi]]_. When drawn, the blades use the statistics listed on the table. When the blades are _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Concealed|concealed]]_ in their case, this weapon can be used as a _[[items/Weapon/Quarterstaff|quarterstaff]]_.
