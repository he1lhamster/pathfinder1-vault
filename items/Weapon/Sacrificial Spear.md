# Sacrificial Spear

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 1802 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x3 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Range:** 20 ft. **Damage type:** P

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint evocation

### Description

An ashen shaft with a steel blade, this finely crafted masterwork _[[items/Weapon/Shortspear|shortspear]]_ holds a removable shard of carnelian at its base. By crushing the gem as a move action, the _[[items/Weapon/Spear|spear]]_ explodes as per the spell _[[spells/Fireball|fireball]]_. This detonation is centered on the _spear_ and deals 5d6 points of fire damage to all creatures within a 20-foot-radius spread. The _spear_'s bearer or allies are not immune to this damage (unless they have _[[universal monster rules/Immunity|immunity]]_ to fire from another source) and the weapon is destroyed.

A _[[items/Weapon/Sacrificial Spear|sacrificial spear]]_ can be detonated at a range by spending a full-round action to throw the _spear_ and crush the gem. If the attack roll hits the target, the _fireball_ is centered on the target, otherwise refer to the thrown weapons chart to determine where the _spear_ lands. Each _spear_ has a specific shard attuned to it at the time of its creation, which can be removed or reaffixed to its _spear_ as a standard action.
