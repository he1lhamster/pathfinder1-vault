### Description

This +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Flaming|flaming]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Light mace|light mace]]_ has an iron shaft and a polished ruby-red stone head, similar to a gemstone. Sometimes a diabolical eye or face is visible for a moment within the stone. The stone is suitable for use as a _[[spells/Scrying|scrying]]_ device for spells such as _scrying_. Once per day as a swift action, the wielder can transform the mace's flames to hellfire for 1 minute. This hellfire deals half fire damage and half damage from _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Unholy|unholy]]_ energy. Evil creatures and creatures with the evil subtype take no damage from this _unholy_ energy; good creatures and those with the good subtype take double the usual damage from it. _[[spells/Protection From Evil|Protection from evil]]_ and similar effects protect against the damage from _unholy_ energy.
