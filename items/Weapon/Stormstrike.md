# Stormstrike

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 9315 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Range:** 10 ft. **Damage type:** P **Special:** Brace

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

This +1 _[[items/Weapon/Trident|trident]]_ has a green-blue metal head, a _[[items/Mundane/Pole|pole]]_ made of driftwood, and a sheaf of white human hair tied at the joint. The wielder of _[[items/Weapon/Stormstrike|stormstrike]]_ can speak one _[[spells/Command|command]]_ word to use it as a frost weapon (which turns its tines blue-white), or a second _command_ word to use it as a _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Shock|shock]]_ weapon (which turns its tines sea-green). Activating one of these abilities deactivates the other.
