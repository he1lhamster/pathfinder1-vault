# Greataxe

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 20 gp **Weight:** 12 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d12 **Critical:** x3 **Damage type:** S **Weapon groups:** Axes

### Description

This two-handed battle axe is heavy enough that you can't wield it with one hand. The head may have one blade or two, and may be "bearded" (meaning hooked or trailing at the bottom) to increase cleaving power and help _[[universal monster rules/Pull|pull]]_ down enemy shields. The haft is usually 3 to 4 feet long.
