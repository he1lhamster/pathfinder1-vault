# Tenacious Atlatl Dart

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 29101 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Ranged **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Range:** 20 ft. **Damage type:** P

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate abjuration

### Description

This +1 returning _[[items/Ammunition/Atlatl dart|atlatl dart]]_ is not destroyed when used as ammunition for an _[[items/Weapon/Atlatl|atlatl]]_. Its returning special ability functions even if it is fired as ammunition. Like all _atlatl_ darts, a _[[items/Weapon/Tenacious _Atlatl_ Dart|tenacious _atlatl_ dart]]_ can be thrown; if it is thrown, it is treated in all ways as a _[[items/Weapon/Javelin|javelin]]_. Two times per day, when the _[[items/Weapon/Dart|dart]]_ successfully strikes a foe who is benefiting from an abjuration spell or ability, the _atlatl_ automatically attempts a dispel check against that effect, as _[[spells/Dispel Magic|dispel magic]]_, before returning to the wielder as normal.
