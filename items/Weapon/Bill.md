# Bill

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 11 gp **Weight:** 11 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x3 **Damage type:** S **Weapon groups:** _[[items/Mundane/Pole|Pole]]_ Arms **Special:** Brace, Disarm, Reach

### Description

The blade of this polearm is a curved or hooked chopping blade with a spiked projection on the back of the blade. The spike is commonly used to block and parry. When fighting defensively or with full defense, this weapon gives you a +1 _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_ bonus to AC. A mounted opponent hit by a _[[items/Weapon/Bill|bill]]_ takes a –1 penalty on his Ride check to stay mounted.
