**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d6/1d6 **Critical:** x2/x2 **Damage type:** P and S **Weapon groups:** Double **Special:** Double, _[[universal monster rules/Trip|Trip]]_

### Description

This _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Vicious|vicious]]_ weapon is often used in gladiatorial events due to its versatility. The body of the weapon is a standard short _[[items/Weapon/Spear|spear]]_, primarily used for thrusting. The butt of the _spear_ is fitted with a length of chain, often spiked, with a hook at the end. The hook and chain may be used to make _trip_ attacks. If your _trip_ attack fails by 10 or more, you may drop the _[[items/Weapon/Chain spear|chain spear]]_ to avoid being knocked _[[conditions/Prone|prone]]_.
