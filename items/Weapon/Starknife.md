# Starknife

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 24 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** x3 **Range:** 20 ft. **Damage type:** P **Weapon groups:** Light Blades

### Description

From a central metal ring, four tapering metal blades extend from this weapon like points on a _[[items/Mundane/Compass|compass]]_ rose. By gripping the crossbar that runs through the weapon's open middle, a wielder can strike with it as a _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Deadly|deadly]]_ melee weapon. Alternatively, by gripping it by the outer rim, a _[[items/Weapon/Starknife|starknife]]_'s light, aerodynamic design allows it to be thrown short distances in a manner similar to a _[[items/Weapon/Chakram|chakram]]_.
