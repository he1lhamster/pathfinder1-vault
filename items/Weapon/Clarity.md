### Description

_[[items/Weapon/Clarity|Clarity]]_ is a +1 holy _[[items/Weapon/Scimitar|scimitar]]_ crafted to vanquish the evil creatures of the night. Though she can't speak, her empathic communications clearly indicate a protective mindset. _Clarity_ enjoys giving warm, encouraging energy to her wielder as long as that wielder opposes evil and gives his best effort. To _[[spells/Preserve|preserve]]_ her wielder's valor in fights, she can cast _[[spells/Remove Fear|remove fear]]_ up to three times per day, affecting only her wielder.
