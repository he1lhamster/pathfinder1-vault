# Dwarfbond Hammer

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 25312 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x3 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** B

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

In the hands of a dwarf, this +1 _[[items/Weapon/Warhammer|warhammer]]_ functions as a +2 returning _warhammer_. It can be hurled with a 20-foot range increment. When hurled, a _[[items/Weapon/Dwarfbond Hammer|dwarfbond hammer]]_ deals an extra 1d8 points of damage against creatures of the giant subtype.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 12812 gp

_[[feats/Craft Magic Arms and Armor|Craft Magic Arms and Armor]]_, creator must be a dwarf of at least 7th level
