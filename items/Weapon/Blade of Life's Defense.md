# Blade of Life's Defense

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 170 gp **Weight:** 6 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Damage type:** S **Special:** _[[universal monster rules/Trip|Trip]]_

### Description

This _[[items/Weapon/Khopesh|khopesh]]_ has a silver blade polished to an almost _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Blinding|blinding]]_ sheen and etched with motifs of vines and leaves. Blades of life's defense can be bought in Anuli's Sword Alley, and are becoming more popular as rumblings of an open war with Geb continue to grow ever louder. Each blade is imbued with a shimmer of nonmagical might against undead that grants the blade's bearer a +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ bonus on saving throws against the abilities of undead. It is otherwise treated as a standard _khopesh_.
