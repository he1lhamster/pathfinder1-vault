# Dueling dagger

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 12 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Range:** 10 ft. **Damage type:** P and S **Weapon groups:** Light Blades

### Description

This _[[items/Weapon/Dagger|dagger]]_ is used in the off hand to block attacks. When you fight defensively or use _[[feats/Combat Expertise|Combat Expertise]]_ while wielding a _[[items/Weapon/Dueling dagger|dueling dagger]]_, your bonus to AC increases by 1. This benefit applies even if you did not attack with the _dueling dagger_. If you aren't proficient with a _dueling dagger_, you treat it as a _dagger_ and gain no special benefits. Any effects that apply to daggers apply to _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Dueling|dueling]]_ daggers.
