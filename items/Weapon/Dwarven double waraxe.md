# Dwarven double waraxe

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 60 gp **Weight:** 12 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d10 **Critical:** x3 **Damage type:** S **Weapon groups:** Axes

### Description

This hefty waraxe is similar to the common _[[items/Weapon/Dwarven waraxe|dwarven waraxe]]_, but its recurved blade spans forward and back from its head like a _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Deadly|deadly]]_ butterfly. A _[[items/Weapon/Dwarven double waraxe|dwarven double waraxe]]_ grants a +1 bonus on all attack rolls after the first when using _[[feats/Cleave|Cleave]]_ or _[[feats/Great Cleave|Great Cleave]]_.
