# Fighter's Fork

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 7315 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Range:** 10 ft. **Damage type:** P **Special:** Brace

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint transmutation

### Description

This steel-tined +1 _[[items/Weapon/Trident|trident]]_ can be commanded to lengthen or shorten its haft (or return to its normal length) as a swift action. It is a two-handed reach weapon when extended but cannot be thrown. If shortened, it can be wielded as a light weapon or hurled as a _[[items/Weapon/Javelin|javelin]]_. Feats or class abilities that apply to tridents apply to a _[[items/Weapon/Fighter's Fork|fighter's fork]]_ in any form.
