### Properties

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x3 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** S

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate necromancy

### Description

This +1 giant-bane _[[items/Weapon/Battleaxe|battleaxe]]_ feels unusually heavy, as if it were made from something far heavier than mere wood and steel. Once per day its wearer can, as a swift action after a successful attack roll, invoke a maximized _[[spells/Ray Of Enfeeblement|ray of enfeeblement]]_ on the creature struck (11 point Strength penalty, 7 rounds, Fortitude DC 16 half).
