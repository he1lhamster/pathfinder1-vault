# Alchemist's Aspergillum

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 2805 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** B

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint abjuration

### Description

This +1 _[[items/Weapon/Battle aspergillum|battle aspergillum]]_ has a dappled surface and a hollow interior, and it's made from a corrosion-resistant alloy that allows it to contain certain alchemical weapons. If the aspergillum is filled with one of these alchemical weapons, treat the contents as _[[items/Mundane/Holy water|holy water]]_, except that it deals the corresponding energy damage: acid (acid or _[[items/Mundane/Alkali flask|alkali flask]]_), cold (_[[items/Mundane/Liquid ice|liquid ice]]_), electricity (_[[items/Mundane/Bottled lightning|bottled lightning]]_), fire (_[[items/Mundane/Alchemist's fire|alchemist's fire]]_), or sonic (_[[items/Mundane/Burst jar|burst jar]]_). If the wielder is an _[[classes/Alchemist|alchemist]]_, the aspergillum can function in this way an additional number of attacks equal to his Intelligence bonus before it needs to be refilled.
