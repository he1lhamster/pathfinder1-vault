# Talonstrike Sword

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 33835 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d10 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +2 **Damage type:** S

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate abjuration

### Description

These mithral +2 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Defending|defending]]_ bastard swords are favored blades of the _[[monsters/Eagle|Eagle]]_ Knights. Easily recognized by its _eagle_'s-head pommel and golden quillons crafted like upswept _eagle_ wings, a _[[items/Weapon/Talonstrike Sword|talonstrike sword]]_'s _defending_ special ability also protects the wielder's _[[spells/Mount|mount]]_. Once per day when the wielder confirms a critical hit with a _talonstrike sword_, the wielder can cast _[[spells/Dimensional Anchor|dimensional anchor]]_ on the target as a free action.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 18435 gp

_[[feats/Craft Magic Arms and Armor|Craft Magic Arms and Armor]]_, _dimensional anchor_, _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_ or _[[spells/Shield Of Faith|shield of faith]]_
