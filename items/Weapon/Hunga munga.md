**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Ranged **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** x2 **Range:** 15 ft. **Damage type:** P **Weapon groups:** Light Blades

### Description

Also _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Called|called]]_ a mambele, danisco, goleyo, njiga, or any of many lesser-known names, this three-bladed _[[items/Weapon/Dagger|dagger]]_ is intended for _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Throwing|throwing]]_ but can be used as a melee weapon. Though the basic three-blade design is most common, hunga mungas can take a wide variety of forms.
