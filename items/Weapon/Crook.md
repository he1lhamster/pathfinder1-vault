This common herder's tool is a wooden _pole_ with a hooked end. When using a _[[items/Weapon/Crook|crook]]_, you can ignore the penalty for not having two hands free when making a combat maneuver check to _[[npcs/Initiate|initiate]]_ a grapple against a creature you threaten.
