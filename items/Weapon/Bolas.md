# Bolas

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Ranged **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** x2 **Range:** 10 ft. **Damage type:** B **Weapon groups:** Thrown **Special:** Nonlethal, _[[universal monster rules/Trip|Trip]]_

### Description

A _[[items/Weapon/Bolas|bolas]]_ is a pair of wooden, stone, or metal weights connected by a thin rope or cord. You can whirl and throw this weapon to make a _[[feats/Ranged Trip|ranged trip]]_ attack against an opponent. You can't be tripped during your own _trip_ attempt when using a _bolas_.
