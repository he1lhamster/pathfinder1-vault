# Rending Claw Blade

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 10305 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** B and S

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 11 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

This set of +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Keen|keen]]_ claw blades is most effective when the wearer has multiple claw attacks per round. When making a full attack with claws, if the wielder hits the same target with this weapon and a different claw attack or set of claw blades, he deals an extra 1d4 damage plus 1-1/2 times his Strength bonus. The wearer can deal this extra damage no more than once per round. This counts as a _[[universal monster rules/Rend|rend]]_ attack and does not stack with other abilities that grant _rend_ attacks.
