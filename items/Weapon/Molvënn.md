# Molvënn

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 63312 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x3 **Enhancement bonus:** +3 **Damage type:** B

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 15 **Aura:** strong necromancy, strong transmutation

### Description

This heavy _[[items/Weapon/Warhammer|warhammer]]_ is capped with steel spikes on both ends of its massive head, and a leather thong prevents it from falling from the wielder's hand. _[[items/Weapon/Molvënn|Molvënn]]_ is a +3 _warhammer_ that deals both piercing and bludgeoning damage. Yet although it is already a formidable weapon, its true potential is only realized in the hands of a _[[classes/Barbarian|barbarian]]_.

Wielded by a _barbarian_, _Molvënn_ becomes a +5 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Thundering|thundering]]_ _warhammer_. The _barbarian_ gains an additional 1 hit point per _barbarian_ level when raging and can add twice her Strength modifier (instead of half again her Strength modifier) to damage rolls while wielding _Molvënn_ two-handed. Additionally, while _Molvënn_'s wielder is raging, she cannot be disarmed, nor can _Molvënn_ be sundered.
