# Gloom Blade

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 8810 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** P

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 13 **Aura:** strong evocation

### Description

As black as coal, this _[[items/Weapon/Short sword|short sword]]_ grows more _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Potent|potent]]_ the farther it is kept from light. It acts as a +1 _short sword_ when in dim light. In _[[spells/Darkness|darkness]]_, it acts as +2 _short sword_. When surrounded by supernatural _darkness_, such as in an area of _[[spells/Deeper Darkness|deeper darkness]]_, it acts a +2 _short sword_ and bestows the benefit of the _[[feats/Blind-Fight|Blind-Fight]]_ feat on its wielder. In _[[spells/Daylight|daylight]]_ or bright illumination, the sword temporarily loses all its magical enhancement bonuses and acts as a masterwork weapon, though it resumes its magical functions once it is no longer in the bright light.
