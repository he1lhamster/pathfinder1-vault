# Hammer of Enemies

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 54212 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x3 **Enhancement bonus:** +3 **Damage type:** B

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate conjuration

### Description

This special +3 _[[spells/Bane|bane]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Warhammer|warhammer]]_ seeks only to crush the enemy of its wielder in battle. The _[[items/Mundane/Hammer|hammer]]_ can speak Common, constantly mutters, curses when it is not engaged in battle, and sings deeply of its love of carnage when it is being used in a fight. The _[[items/Weapon/Hammer of Enemies|hammer of enemies]]_ is always eager to point out potential enemies within its _[[spells/Vision|vision]]_. Because of its bloodlust it does not bestow negative levels on creatures that do not share its alignment—it is willing to fight for anyone. Additionally, three times per day, the _hammer of enemies_ can change the creature type of its _bane_ ability. A creature type remains until a new one is chosen.
