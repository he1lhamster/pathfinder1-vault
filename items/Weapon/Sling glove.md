# Sling glove

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Ranged **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** x2 **Range:** 50 ft. **Damage type:** B **Weapon groups:** Thrown

### Description

This curved, scooped-shaped, 2-foot-long wicker _[[items/Mundane/Basket|basket]]_ attaches to your wrist, allowing you to throw fist-sized stones at great speed merely by whipping your arm forward. Loading a _[[items/Weapon/Sling glove|sling glove]]_ is a free action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity so long as you have a free hand. You take –4 penalty when _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Throwing|throwing]]_ a stone at an adjacent target and cannot use the weapon to attack creatures in your space.
