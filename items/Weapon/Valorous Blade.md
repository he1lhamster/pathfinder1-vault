### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 20315 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate enchantment

### Description

Once per day within 1 minute of using this +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Valiant|valiant]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Scimitar|scimitar]]_ to kill or _[[spells/Knock|knock]]_ _[[conditions/Unconscious|unconscious]]_ a foe, as a standard action, the wielder can inspire all allies within 60 feet who can see her. Those allies gain a +2 morale bonus on attack rolls, skill checks, and saving throws for 10 minutes.
