# Crowcaller

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 29110 gp **Weight:** 10 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d12 **Critical:** x3 **Enhancement bonus:** +2 **Damage type:** S

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** faint necromancy

### Description

The Ulfen _[[npcs/Guard|Guard]]_ favor a single-bladed _[[items/Weapon/Battleaxe|battleaxe]]_ as their weapon of choice. An elite few are chosen to bear a _[[items/Weapon/Crowcaller|crowcaller]]_, a magically enhanced version that not only strikes down assassins, but also helps track the assassins' masters. A _crowcaller_ is a +2 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Keen|keen]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Greataxe|greataxe]]_. Once per day, if the axe is laid across the chest of a foe it has slain, it compels the corpse to answer five questions as per the _[[spells/Speak with Dead|speak with dead]]_ spell.
