# Mace of Smiting (lesser)

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 16012 gp **Weight:** 8 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** B **Special material:** Adamantine

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

A lesser mace of smiting is a +1 adamantine _[[items/Weapon/Heavy mace|heavy mace]]_ with a +3 enhancement bonus against constructs. When it deals a successful critical hit to a construct, it deals ×4 damage rather than ×2. A critical hit dealt to an outsider deals ×3 damage rather than ×2.

A mace of smiting is a +3 adamantine _heavy mace_ has a +5 enhancement bonus against constructs. When it deals a successful critical hit to a construct, it completely destroys the construct (no saving throw). A critical hit dealt to an outsider deals ×4 damage rather than ×2.
