# Skirmishing Spear

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 19502 gp **Weight:** 6 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x3 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Range:** 20 ft. **Damage type:** P

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate conjuration

### Description

When thrown, this +1 returning _[[items/Weapon/Spear|spear]]_ is considered a one-handed weapon and gains a +2 bonus on the attack roll. If the _spear_ hits when thrown, the wielder can, as an immediate action, _[[spells/Expend|expend]]_ one use of mythic power to teleport to an unoccupied space adjacent to the creature struck. This effect is treated as _[[spells/Dimension Door|dimension door]]_, except the wielder can act after teleporting. The _spear_ comes back to its wielder's hand when he appears. If he hadn't taken a move action prior to _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Throwing|throwing]]_ the _spear_, he can continue a full attack with the _spear_.
