# Berserking Sword

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 8350 gp **Weight:** 8 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 2d6 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +2 **Damage type:** S

##### Magical properties

**Cursed**

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

This sword appears to be a +2 _[[items/Weapon/Greatsword|greatsword]]_. However, whenever it is used in battle, its wielder goes berserk (gaining all the benefits and drawbacks of the _[[classes/Barbarian|barbarian]]_'s _[[spells/Rage|rage]]_ ability). He attacks the nearest creature and continues to fight until _[[conditions/Unconscious|unconscious]]_ or dead or until no living thing remains within 30 feet. Although many see this sword as a cursed object, others see it as a boon.
