# Blade boot

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 25 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** x2 **Damage type:** P

### Description

Blade boots come with a spring-mounted knife that pops out when triggered with the right combination of toe presses. You can use a _[[items/Weapon/Blade boot|blade boot]]_ as an off-hand weapon. Releasing the knife is a swift action; rearming it is a full-round action. When the blade is extended, you treat normal terrain as difficult and difficult terrain as impassable.
