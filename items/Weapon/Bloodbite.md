# Bloodbite

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 32350 gp **Weight:** 8 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Light **Damage:** 2d6 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +2 **Damage type:** S

##### Magical properties

**Cursed**

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

The grip of this _[[items/Weapon/Greatsword|greatsword]]_ has a series of deep notches, and its jagged blade is a deep crimson. A _[[items/Weapon/Bloodbite|bloodbite]]_ is awakened by bloodlust, and its appetite must be sated. When the wielder makes a successful attack with the _bloodbite_, it inflicts a wound that deals 1d4 points of _[[universal monster rules/Bleed|bleed]]_ damage (in addition to its normal damage), but it also deals 1d4 points of _bleed_ damage to the wielder as the hilt grows teeth that dig into the wielder's hands. Once used, the _bloodbite_ must be used as its wielder's primary melee weapon, and it can be discarded only after the owner benefits from a _[[spells/Remove Curse|remove curse]]_ spell or similar effect.
