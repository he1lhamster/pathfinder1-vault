# Shotel

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 30 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x3 **Damage type:** P **Weapon groups:** Heavy Blades **Special:** Performance

### Description

The _[[items/Weapon/Shotel|shotel]]_ is a downward-curving sword designed to reach over or around an opponent's _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_. You gain a +1 bonus on attack rolls with a _shotel_ against opponents using bucklers, light shields, or heavy shields. The curve of a _shotel_'s blade varies from sword to sword, and some models are double-edged, while others are single-edged, with the back edge able to be braced against a _shield_ for additional power. Shotels are particularly useful for hooking mounted combatants and ripping them from their steeds.
