# Rabbit's Blade

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 22307 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +2 **Damage type:** P

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate evocation

### Description

The blade of this unassuming _[[items/Weapon/Longsword|longsword]]_ is permanently _[[conditions/Broken|broken]]_, snapped at an angle halfway along the blade. In _[[spells/Spite|spite]]_ of this, the sword can be wielded as a +2 _[[items/Weapon/Short sword|short sword]]_ without penalty. As long as the wielder of a _[[items/Weapon/Rabbit's Blade|rabbit's blade]]_ is threatening two or more foes and he has no allies within 15 feet, he gains the use of the _[[feats/Cleave|Cleave]]_ feat. If he is threatening three or more foes and has no allies within 30 feet, the wielder gains the use of the _[[feats/Great Cleave|Great Cleave]]_ feat instead. Each threatened foe must have Hit Dice equal to or greater than 1/2 the wielder's character level in order to count toward this weapon's ability.
