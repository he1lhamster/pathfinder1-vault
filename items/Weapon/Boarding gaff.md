# Boarding gaff

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 8 gp **Weight:** 8 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d6/1d6 **Critical:** x2/x2 **Damage type:** P and S **Weapon groups:** Double **Special:** Double, Reach, _[[universal monster rules/Trip|Trip]]_

### Description

A _[[items/Weapon/Boarding gaff|boarding gaff]]_, or gaff hook, is a long _[[items/Mundane/Pole|pole]]_ with a hook for spearing fish. Pirates often add extra, heavier hooks to both ends of boarding gaffs to assist with boarding actions, giving the weapon its common name. A _boarding gaff_ can also be used for fishing.
