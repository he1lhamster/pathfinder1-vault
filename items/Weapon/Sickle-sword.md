# Sickle-sword

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 20 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Damage type:** S **Weapon groups:** Heavy Blades **Special:** _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Distracting|Distracting]]_

### Description

Favored by those residents of Irrisen with no talent for witchcraft, this exotic blade curves multiple times along its 3-1/2 feet. The _[[items/Weapon/Sickle-sword|sickle-sword]]_'s hilt is long and curved, and its blade bears a small secondary grip partway up its length. These allow a wielder with a free hand to rapidly twist the sword in unpredictable ways. When wielding a _sickle-sword_ in one hand and nothing in the other, you can spend a swift action to gain a +2 bonus on your next attack roll before the end of your turn.
