# Dire bolas

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 20 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Ranged **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** x2 **Range:** 10 ft. **Damage type:** B **Special:** Nonlethal, _[[universal monster rules/Trip|Trip]]_

### Description

This specialized _[[items/Weapon/Bolas|bolas]]_ can be used to perform _[[feats/Ranged Trip|ranged trip]]_ combat maneuvers against creatures far larger than the wielder. It consists of a pair of metal weights connected by a _[[items/Mundane/Silk rope|silk rope]]_ 20 feet long. Because of the great size and weight of the _bolas_, you need both hands to swing it around your head, and considerable time to get the weights up to speed. Making a ranged attack with it is a full-round action. You can use a _[[items/Weapon/Dire bolas|dire bolas]]_ to _trip_ a creature whose size is up to three size categories larger than yours.
