# Pepperbox

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 3000 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x4 **Range:** 20 ft. **Damage type:** B and P

### Description

This _[[items/Weapon/Pistol|pistol]]_ has six barrels instead of one. The entire _[[items/Mundane/Barrel|barrel]]_ housing can be quickly rotated by hand between shots (a free action requiring one free hand), allowing all six bullets to be fired before the weapon must be reloaded. Each _barrel_ of a _[[items/Weapon/Pepperbox|pepperbox]]_ uses either a bullet and 1 dose of black _[[items/Mundane/Powder|powder]]_ or a single alchemical cartridge as ammunition. This is an early firearm.
