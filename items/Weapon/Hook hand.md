# Hook hand

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 10 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** x2 **Damage type:** S **Special:** Disarm

### Description

Pirates who lose a hand in the course of their adventures sometimes _[[universal monster rules/Attach|attach]]_ hooks to their wrists. Hook hands can't be disarmed. They generally don't interfere with day-to-day activities, but can't be used for tasks that would require an actual hand (such as completing somatic components, drawing arrows, or holding a weapon with both hands).
