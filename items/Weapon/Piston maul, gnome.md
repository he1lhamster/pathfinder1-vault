# Piston maul, gnome

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 70 gp **Weight:** 15 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d10 **Critical:** x2 **Damage type:** B

### Description

An alchemically fired piston in the head of this two-handed _[[items/Mundane/Hammer|hammer]]_ strikes targets with astounding force. Successful sunder attacks made with a piston maul deal an additional 4 points of damage. A piston maul requires a _[[items/Mundane/Thunderstone|thunderstone]]_ to function; otherwise, it functions as a _[[items/Weapon/Greatclub|greatclub]]_. Inserting a _thunderstone_ into the weapon's compartment is a standard action, and powers the weapon for 24 hours, after which the _thunderstone_ is consumed.
