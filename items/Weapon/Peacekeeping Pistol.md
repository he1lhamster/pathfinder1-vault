# Peacekeeping Pistol

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 11700 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Ranged **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x4 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Range:** 20 ft. **Damage type:** B and P

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint enchantment

### Description

Crafted in the forges of Alkenstar, this +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Merciful|merciful]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Pistol|pistol]]_ is ornamented with filigree of angelic creatures along the _[[items/Mundane/Barrel|barrel]]_, and houses intricate brass gears. Whenever the wielder scores a critical hit while using this weapon to deal nonlethal damage, the target must succeed at a DC 12 Will saving throw or be _[[conditions/Dazed|dazed]]_ for 1 round. As a swift action, a wielder with the grit class feature can spend 1 grit point to increase the DC of this save by 4.
