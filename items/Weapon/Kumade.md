# Kumade

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** x3 **Damage type:** P **Weapon groups:** Axes **Special:** Grapple

### Description

Adapted from a garden _[[universal monster rules/Rake|rake]]_, the _[[items/Weapon/Kumade|kumade]]_ consists of a wood or bamboo _[[items/Mundane/Pole|pole]]_ topped with a metal claw. The _rake_ head doubles as a _[[items/Weapon/Grappling hook|grappling hook]]_. When a _kumade_ is secured to a surface, such as a rafter or window sill, its handle can be climbed as a 5-foot length of rope.
