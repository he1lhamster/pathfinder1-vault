# Talmandor Blade

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 58615 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +2 **Damage type:** S **Special material:** Mithral

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 7 **Aura:** moderate abjuration, moderate conjuration, moderate evocation

### Description

This +2 holy mithral _[[items/Weapon/Longsword|longsword]]_ continually sheds light as the spell. While wielded, the _[[items/Weapon/Talmandor Blade|Talmandor Blade]]_ grants the wielder _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_ (or _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 60 feet, if the wielder already has _low-light vision_) and a +4 bonus on Perception checks. Additionally, the sword grants a +4 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ bonus on saving throws against _[[universal monster rules/Paralysis|paralysis]]_ and petrification spells and effects.
