# Broken-back seax

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 40 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d10 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Damage type:** P and S **Weapon groups:** Light Blades

### Description

Used in Avistan since before Earthfall, seaxes are straight, single-edged blades. The _[[items/Weapon/Broken-back seax|broken-back seax]]_ is the size of a _[[items/Weapon/Short sword|short sword]]_, and has a broad blade with a distinctive trapezoidal shape. If your Strength score is less than 15, you take a –2 penalty on attack rolls with a _broken-back seax_, as you're unable to put enough force into the blows to land them effectively.
