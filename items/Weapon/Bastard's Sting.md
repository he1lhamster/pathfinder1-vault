# Bastard's Sting

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 123035 gp **Weight:** 6 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d10 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +2 **Damage type:** S **Special material:** Adamantine

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 18 **Aura:** strong necromancy

### Description

This +2 adamantine _[[items/Weapon/Bastard sword|bastard sword]]_ becomes a +5 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Unholy|unholy]]_ adamantine _bastard sword_ in the hands of an _[[classes/Antipaladin|antipaladin]]_.

While wielded by an _antipaladin_, a _[[items/Weapon/Bastard's Sting|bastard's sting]]_ deals 2d6 points of negative energy damage to all living creatures that start their turn adjacent to the wielder. The _antipaladin_ also gains _[[universal monster rules/Fast Healing|fast healing]]_ 5 as long as at least one living creature takes damage from this negative energy. This negative energy has no effect on undead. The sword also enables the _antipaladin_ to use _[[spells/Unholy Blight|unholy blight]]_ at will on _[[spells/Command|command]]_ at the class level of the _antipaladin_. Paladins take a –2 saving throw penalty against this effect.
