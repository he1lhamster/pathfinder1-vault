# Underwater light crossbow

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 70 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Ranged **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Range:** 80 ft. **Damage type:** P **Weapon groups:** Crossbows

### Description

An _[[items/Weapon/Underwater light crossbow|underwater light crossbow]]_ functions like its normal counterpart above water, and can be used _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Underwater|underwater]]_. When fired _underwater_, the crossbow has a range increment of 20 feet. Anyone proficient with a normal _[[items/Weapon/Light crossbow|light crossbow]]_ can use an _underwater light crossbow_.
