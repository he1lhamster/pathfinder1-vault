# Seraphic Pistol

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 37550 gp **Weight:** 5 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** Ranged **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x4 **Enhancement bonus:** +2 **Range:** 20 ft. **Damage type:** B and P

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 6 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

The gold-washed _[[items/Mundane/Barrel|barrel]]_ of this elegant +2 _[[items/Weapon/Double-barreled pistol|double-barreled pistol]]_ always catches the light, and its maple grip feels pleasantly warm to the touch. When its wielder successfully bypasses an evil creature's _[[universal monster rules/Damage Reduction|damage reduction]]_ with a single attack, the creature's skin cracks into fissures of golden light. This light negates the creature's DR for the _[[classes/Gunslinger|gunslinger]]_ and her allies, fading away after 1 minute.
