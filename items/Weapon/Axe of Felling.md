# Axe of Felling

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 11330 gp **Weight:** 8 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d10 **Critical:** x3 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** S

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

Born from dwarven forges, these +1 giant-bane dwarven waraxes are crafted in the likeness of the favored weapon of _[[npcs/King|King]]_ Harral, the last _king_ of the Pious Kingdom of Saggorak. According to legend, before he and his comrades starved to death during the _[[monsters/Orc|orc]]_ siege of Saggorak, _King_ Harral challenged and defeated an _[[monsters/Ettin|ettin]]_ in single combat by hacking its legs from beneath its body with his axe. An _[[items/Weapon/Axe of Felling|axe of felling]]_ possesses the _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_ weapon feature and can be used to _trip_ opponents up to two size categories larger than its wielder.
