##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

The stone handle of this +3 adamantine _[[items/Weapon/Warhammer|warhammer]]_ is carved in the form of a stately dwarven woman. The wielder of _[[items/Weapon/Hearthhammer|Hearthhammer]]_ gains a +5 competence bonus on Craft (armor, _[[items/Mundane/Jewelry|jewelry]]_, stonemasonry, traps, and weapon) checks. If the wielder is a humanoid with the dwarf subtype, this bonus increases to +10.
