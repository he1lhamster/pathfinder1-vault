# Perfectionist Shavtoosh

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 4461 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d3 **Critical:** x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** S **Special:** Disarm, Nonlethal, Reach, _[[universal monster rules/Trip|Trip]]_

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint conjuration, faint _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

This long piece of cloth is so fine that it can pass through a finger ring. Delicately woven with multiple colors to spell out Iroran prayers, it is usually worn as a shawl or belt, or wrapped around the chest and shoulders—it does not use a magic item body slot. If wrapped around at least one limb, it grants the wearer the _[[feats/Improved Unarmed Strike|Improved Unarmed Strike]]_ feat. It may also be wielded as a +1 _[[items/Weapon/Whip|whip]]_. The wearer can meditate on the shawl's _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Sacred|sacred]]_ mantras to use _[[spells/Cure Light Wounds|cure light wounds]]_ and _[[spells/Know The Enemy|know the enemy]]_ each once per day.
