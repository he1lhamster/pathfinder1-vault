# Windreader's Bow

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 54400 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Ranged **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x3 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Range:** 110 ft. **Damage type:** P

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate _[[spells/Divination|divination]]_

### Description

Whenever an arrow fired from this +1 adaptive seeking _[[items/Weapon/Composite longbow|composite longbow]]_ damages the wielder's favored enemy or studied target (as per the _[[classes/Ranger|ranger]]_ and _[[classes/Slayer|slayer]]_ class features, respectively), in addition to its normal effects, it also deals 2 points of _[[universal monster rules/Bleed|bleed]]_ damage. As long as the _bleed_ effect continues, the wielder can sense that foe's approximate direction and distance twice per day by smelling the wind as a standard action.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 27400 gp
