# Sea-knife

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 8 gp **Weight:** 1 lb. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Damage type:** S

### Description

A _[[items/Weapon/Sea-knife|sea-knife]]_ is a long-handled weapon with a short blade. It is designed to be strapped to the ankle or foot of the wielder, pointing downward and jutting out beyond the wearer's leg. Donning or removing a _sea-knife_ is a full-round action. The wearer cannot use a leg with a _sea-knife_ strapped to it for walking or running. A _sea-knife_ can be used as a light melee weapon when the wielder is swimming, flying, or _[[conditions/Prone|prone]]_. This allows the wielder to use a two-handed weapon, or wield a weapon with one hand and carry a _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_, and still make off-hand attacks with the _sea-knife_. Attacks made with a _sea-knife_ take a –2 circumstance penalty on attack rolls in addition to all other attack penalties.
