# Khopesh

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 20 gp **Weight:** 8 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Damage type:** S **Weapon groups:** Heavy Blades **Special:** _[[universal monster rules/Trip|Trip]]_

### Description

This heavy blade has a convex curve near the end, making its overall shape similar to that of a _[[items/Weapon/Battleaxe|battleaxe]]_. A typical _[[items/Weapon/Khopesh|khopesh]]_ is 20 to 24 inches in length. Its curved shape allows the wielder to hook around defenses and _trip_ foes. The elegant shape of a _khopesh_ leads some artisans to cover them in ornate decorations.
