# Smuggler's Sling

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 5300 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Ranged **Damage:** 1d4 **Critical:** x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Range:** 50 ft. **Damage type:** B

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint conjuration

### Description

Smuggler's slings are favored in Chu Ye, where they are employed by _[[spells/Freedom|freedom]]_ fighters sneaking contraband weapons past oni _[[items/Mundane/Slave|slave]]_ masters. The wielder of this +1 _[[items/Weapon/Sling|sling]]_ gains a +5 competence bonus on Sleight of Hand checks to conceal the _sling_. Up to five times per day, the wearer can load the _sling_ with a conjured bullet as a swift action. A conjured bullet counts as magical for the purposes of overcoming _[[universal monster rules/Damage Reduction|damage reduction]]_ and attacking _[[universal monster rules/Incorporeal|incorporeal]]_ creatures, but is otherwise a standard _sling_ bullet. This bullet must be fired by the end of the round or it vanishes.
