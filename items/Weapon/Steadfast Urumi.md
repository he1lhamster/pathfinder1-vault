# Steadfast Urumi

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 18830 gp **Weight:** 6 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** 18-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** S **Special:** _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Distracting|Distracting]]_

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 8 **Aura:** moderate abjuration, moderate evocation

### Description

This weapon consists of a flexible blade attached to a handle by a fine coil of steel, and is favored by tieflings and other outcasts in Jalmeray. This +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Mighty Cleaving|mighty cleaving]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Urumi|urumi]]_ grants its wielder a _[[spells/Deflection|deflection]]_ bonus to AC equal to the number of enemies within her reach with the _urumi_ (maximum +4). The wielder also gains a +5 competence bonus on Acrobatics checks to move through a threatened area.
