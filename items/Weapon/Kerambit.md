# Kerambit

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 2 gp **Weight:** – **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Light **Damage:** 1d3 **Critical:** x3 **Damage type:** S **Weapon groups:** Light Blades

### Description

This small, curved _[[universal monster rules/Pull|pull]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Dagger|dagger]]_ has a metal loop at the base of its handle, allowing it to be secured with a pinky or worn on a string tied in the hair. While relatively small, the curved blade can create brutal wounds. Easily _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Concealed|concealed]]_ (you gain a +2 bonus on Sleight of Hand checks made to conceal a _[[items/Weapon/Kerambit|kerambit]]_ on your body), it is a favorite weapon of _[[classes/Ninja|ninja]]_ and assassins.
