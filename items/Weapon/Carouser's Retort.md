# Carouser's Retort

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 3060 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** 18-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** P

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 3 **Aura:** faint abjuration, faint transmutation

### Description

This simple but finely crafted +1 _[[items/Weapon/Rapier|rapier]]_ has a hilt in the shape of a tankard, open on one end for the wielder's hand. Once per day, the wielder can use _[[spells/Remove Fear|remove fear]]_; if the wielder ever becomes _[[conditions/Frightened|frightened]]_ or _[[conditions/Panicked|panicked]]_ and this ability has not already been expended for the day, the weapon automatically activates this ability on the wielder's next turn. Once per day, the wielder can turn up to 8 gallons of water (fresh, salt, or contaminated) into weak beer that is safe to drink.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 1690 gp
