# Boar spear

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 5 gp **Weight:** 8 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x2 **Damage type:** P **Weapon groups:** Spears **Special:** Brace

### Description

This _[[items/Weapon/Spear|spear]]_ has a metal crossbar to protect you against onrushing attackers. If you ready a _[[items/Weapon/Boar spear|boar spear]]_ against a charge and your attack hits, you gain a +2 _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_ bonus to your AC against that creature until your next turn.
