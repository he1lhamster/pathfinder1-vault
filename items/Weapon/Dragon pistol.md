# Dragon pistol

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 1000 gp **Weight:** 3 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** x4 **Range:** 20 ft. **Damage type:** B and P **Weapon groups:** Firearms

### Description

Like a miniature _[[items/Weapon/Blunderbuss|blunderbuss]]_, the _[[items/Weapon/Dragon pistol|dragon pistol]]_ fires pellets or a bullet from its flared _[[items/Mundane/Barrel|barrel]]_. The _dragon pistol_ fires in a 15-foot cone when firing pellets, and has a 10-foot range increment when firing a bullet. A _dragon pistol_ uses either a bullet or group of pellets and 1 dose of black _[[items/Mundane/Powder|powder]]_, or a single alchemical cartridge (with either bullets or pellets) as ammunition. This is an early firearm.
