# Spear of the Honored Ancestors

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 15302 gp **Weight:** 6 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** x3 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Range:** 20 ft. **Damage type:** P

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 9 **Aura:** moderate conjuration

### Description

When the wielder of this glimmering +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Ghost Touch|ghost touch]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Spear|spear]]_ enters a _[[spells/Rage|rage]]_, she can drum the _spear_ against a _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_ or the ground as a swift action. If she does, colorful lights dance around the _spear_ and the wielder can choose to gain the benefit of _[[spells/Guidance|guidance]]_, grant the touched _shield_ the _ghost touch_ special ability, or grant the _spear_ the returning special ability. The chosen benefit lasts for the duration of the _rage_.
