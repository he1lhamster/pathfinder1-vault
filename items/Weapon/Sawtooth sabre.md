# Sawtooth sabre

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 35 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Exotic **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d8 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Damage type:** S **Weapon groups:** Heavy Blades

### Description

Crafted to evoke the barbed talons of the Mantis God himself, a _[[items/Weapon/Sawtooth sabre|sawtooth sabre]]_ may be used as a Martial Weapon (in which case it functions identically to a _[[items/Weapon/Longsword|longsword]]_). If you have the _[[feats/Exotic Weapon Proficiency|Exotic Weapon Proficiency]]_ (_sawtooth sabre_) feat, for the purpose of _[[feats/Two-Weapon Fighting|two-weapon fighting]]_ you can treat it as a light melee weapon; for all other purposes, it is a one-handed melee weapon.
