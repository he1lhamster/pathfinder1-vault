# Helicyon

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** – **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d6/1d6 **Critical:** x2/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +5/+0 **Damage type:** B **Special:** Double, _[[classes/Monk|Monk]]_

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 28 **Aura:** overwhelming universal

### Description

_[[items/Weapon/Helicyon|Helicyon]]_ is a +5 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Anchoring|anchoring]]_ _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Ghost Touch|ghost touch]]_ holy _[[items/Weapon/Quarterstaff|quarterstaff]]_. _Helicyon_ can also appear as a walking stick and can assume its _[[spells/True Form|true form]]_ as a free action. Any time _Helicyon_ deals damage, the creature struck must attempt a DC 25 Will saving throw. If it fails, the target becomes overwrought with remorse for events that took place in the course of its existence, even if it is denied, never had, or lost the capacity to feel emotion, as per _[[spells/Overwhelming Grief|overwhelming grief]]_. This effect affects only creatures with Intelligence 3 or greater, even if they are normally immune to mind-affecting effects. Once affected or after a successful saving throw, the target becomes immune to this ability for 24 hours. As a standard action, Andoletta can _[[universal monster rules/Summon|summon]]_ _Helicyon_ to her hand from any distance, even across _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Planar|planar]]_ boundaries.

### _[[spells/Destruction|Destruction]]_

_Helicyon_ must be _[[conditions/Broken|broken]]_ in the bare hands of an archon whose CR is 20 or greater, while that being renounces Heaven and swears allegiance to an evil master or cause.
