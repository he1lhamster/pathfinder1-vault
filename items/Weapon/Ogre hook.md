### Description

This large _[[items/Weapon/Crook|crook]]_ of sharpened metal makes it easy to _trip_ and catch fleeing opponents. Invented by ogres, most _[[monsters/Ogre|ogre]]_ hooks are Large and crudely fashioned—often from metal torn from previous victims' weapons and armor. Despite their poor workmanship, the rarity and peril involved in getting _ogre_ hooks make such items quite valuable.
