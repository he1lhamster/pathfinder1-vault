### Description

This humble weapon appears to be little more than a crudely sharpened stake or makeshift _[[items/Weapon/Spear|spear]]_, but in truth it is a far more formidable weapon. In addition to being a +2 _[[npcs/Undead Bane|undead bane]]_ _spear_, a _[[items/Weapon/Heartswood Spear|heartswood spear]]_ deals bludgeoning damage when it is swung as a staff. When employed in such a manner, it also possesses the _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Disruption|disruption]]_ property.
