# Serpent-Necked Flail

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 32315 gp **Weight:** 10 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d10 **Critical:** 19-20/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +1 **Damage type:** B **Special:** Disarm, _[[universal monster rules/Trip|Trip]]_

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 10 **Aura:** moderate transmutation

### Description

The head of this weapon resembles a knot of entwined serpents. In this form, it can be used as a masterwork _[[items/Weapon/Heavy mace|heavy mace]]_. Upon _[[spells/Command|command]]_, the head separates into three individual snakes joined to the weapon's handle by their tails, and the weapon acts as a +1 speed _[[items/Weapon/Heavy flail|heavy flail]]_. Speaking the _command_ word again makes the weapon revert to its mace form.
