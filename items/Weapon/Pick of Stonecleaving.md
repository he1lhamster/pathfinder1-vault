##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 5 **Aura:** faint evocation

### Description

When this +1 adamantine _[[items/Weapon/Heavy pick|heavy pick]]_ is used to damage unattended objects, it deals an additional 2d6 points of damage. If the wielder expends one use of mythic power when making such an attack, the pick bypasses the object's hardness and deals 4d6 additional damage instead of 2d6.
