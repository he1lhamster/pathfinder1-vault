# Baston fighting stick

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 10 gp **Weight:** 2 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** One-Handed **Damage:** 1d6 **Critical:** x2 **Damage type:** B

### Description

These weapons are treated sticks of wood, either left bare or covered in elaborate decorative designs. Baston fighting sticks vary in length from 2 to 4 feet, with thicknesses varying between 1 to 3 inches. In most applications, these weapons are similar to clubs or quarterstaffs, but in the hands of a trained expert, they truly shine. A creature with the _[[feats/Combat Reflexes|Combat Reflexes]]_ feat treats the weapon as having the blocking and performance weapon special features (Pathfinder RPG Ultimate Equipment 22).
