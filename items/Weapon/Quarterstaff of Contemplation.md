# Quarterstaff of Contemplation

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 34800 gp **Weight:** 4 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Simple **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d6/1d6 **Critical:** x2/x2 **Enhancement bonus:** +2/+0 **Damage type:** B **Special:** Double, _[[classes/Monk|Monk]]_

##### Magical properties

**Caster level:** 12 **Aura:** strong transmutation

### Description

This wooden _[[items/Mundane/Pole|pole]]_ is plain, save that green dragon scales adorn to its length. Treated as a +2 _[[items/Weapon/Quarterstaff|quarterstaff]]_, it is a favorite of Irori's wandering followers. In the hands of a worshiper of Irori, a _[[items/Weapon/Quarterstaff of Contemplation|quarterstaff of contemplation]]_ gains the _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Ki Intensifying|ki intensifying]]_ weapon special ability.

If the wielder is a follower of Irori and possesses the wholeness of body class feature, he can spend a full-round action to meditate using the staff as a focus. Treat this as using wholeness of body at the cost of 1 ki point instead of 2.

### Crafting requirements

**Crafting cost:** 17550 gp

_[[feats/Craft Magic Arms and Armor|Craft Magic Arms and Armor]]_, creator must be a 7th-level _monk_
