### Description

This ivory +1 _[[items/Weapon/Blowgun|blowgun]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Dart|dart]]_ emits a _[[spells/Silence|silence]]_ effect that envelops the space of the creature or object it strikes, up to a maximum radius of 20 feet.
