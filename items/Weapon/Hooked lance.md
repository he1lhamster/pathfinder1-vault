### Description

This slender, 10-foot-long polearm has an added hook protruding from the head that is used to snag the clothing and armor of opponents. A _[[items/Weapon/Hooked lance|hooked lance]]_, unlike a _[[items/Weapon/Lance|lance]]_, can't be wielded in one hand while mounted and doesn't deal extra damage if used while mounted.
