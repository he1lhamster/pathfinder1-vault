# Bec de corbin

### Properties

**Type:** Weapon **Price:** 15 gp **Weight:** 12 lbs. **Slot:** Slotless

##### Weapon properties

**Proficiency group:** Martial **Type:** Two-Handed **Damage:** 1d10 **Critical:** x3 **Damage type:** B and P **Weapon groups:** _[[items/Mundane/Pole|Pole]]_ Arms **Special:** Brace, Reach

### Description

The _[[items/Weapon/Bec de corbin|bec de corbin]]_ is a polearm very similar to the _[[items/Weapon/Lucerne hammer|lucerne hammer]]_, but the _[[items/Mundane/Hammer|hammer]]_ head is slightly blunter and the spike is stouter and more hooked. You primarily attack with the spike. You gain a +2 bonus on combat maneuver checks to sunder _[[classes/Medium|medium]]_ or heavy armor with a _bec de corbin_.
