# Cautious Hunter

**Source** NPC Codex pg. 101
**XP** 6,400
Half-orc _[[classes/Monk|monk]]_ 10

LE Medium humanoid (human, _[[monsters/Orc|orc]]_)
**Init** +5; **Senses** _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 60 ft., _[[spells/See Invisibility|see invisibility]]_; Perception +12

##### Defense

**AC** 21, touch 17, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 19 (+2 armor, +1 Dex, +1 _[[feats/Dodge|dodge]]_, +2 _monk_, +2 natural, +3 Wis)
**hp** 73 (10d8+25)
**Fort** +9, **Ref** +8, **Will** +10; +2 vs. enchantments
**Defensive Abilities** improved evasion, _orc_ _[[universal monster rules/Ferocity|ferocity]]_; **Immune** disease

##### Offense
**Speed** 60 ft.
**Melee** unarmed strike +11/+6 (1d10+4) or mwk _[[items/Weapon/Falchion|falchion]]_ +12/+7 (2d4+6/18–20) or unarmed strike flurry of blows +12/+12/+7/+7 (1d10+4)
**Ranged** _[[items/Weapon/Heavy crossbow|heavy crossbow]]_ +8 (1d10/19–20)
**Special Attacks** flurry of blows, _[[feats/Stunning Fist|stunning fist]]_ (10/day, DC 18)

### Tactics

**Before Combat **The _monk_ drinks potions of _[[spells/Barkskin|barkskin]]_ and _see invisibility_ and ambushes foes whenever possible.
**During Combat **The _monk_ charges, using _[[feats/Vital Strike|Vital Strike]]_ in conjunction with _Stunning Fist_. Against _[[conditions/Stunned|stunned]]_ opponents, she employs her _falchion_ and _[[feats/Power Attack|Power Attack]]_. If surrounded or adjacent to a particularly powerful combatant, she uses _[[feats/Improved Bull Rush|Improved Bull Rush]]_ and then _[[feats/Lunge|Lunge]]_, pushing her enemies away and then forcing them to close again.
**Base Statistics **Without _barkskin_ and _see invisibility_, the _monk_’s statistics are **Senses **no _see invisibility_; **AC **19, touch 17, _flat-footed_ 17.

##### Statistics
**Str** 18, **Dex** 12, **Con** 14, **Int** 10, **Wis** 16, **Cha** 8
**Base Atk** +7; **CMB** +14 (+17 bull rush); **CMD** 28 (30 vs. bull rush)
**Feats** _Dodge_, _[[feats/Extra Ki|Extra Ki]]_, _Improved Bull Rush_, _[[feats/Improved Initiative|Improved Initiative]]_, _[[feats/Improved Unarmed Strike|Improved Unarmed Strike]]_, _Lunge_, _[[monsters/Medusa|Medusa]]_’s Wrath, _Power Attack_, _[[feats/Scorpion Style|Scorpion Style]]_, _Stunning Fist_, _Vital Strike_
**Skills** Acrobatics +6 (+28 when jumping), _[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_ +12, Intimidate +9, Knowledge (history) +4, Perception +12, Sense Motive +16, Stealth +19, Survival +8, Swim +8
**Languages** Common, _Orc_
**SQ** fast movement, high _[[spells/Jump|jump]]_, ki pool (10 points, lawful, magic), maneuver _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Training|training]]_, _orc_ blood, purity of body, _[[spells/Slow|slow]]_ fall 50 ft., weapon familiarity, wholeness of body
**Combat Gear** potions of _barkskin_ (2), potions of _[[spells/Cure Moderate Wounds|cure moderate wounds]]_ (2), potion of _see invisibility_; **Other Gear** _heavy crossbow_ with 20 bolts, masterwork _falchion_, _[[items/Wondrous Item/Belt of Giant Strength +2|belt of giant strength +2]]_, _[[items/Wondrous Item/Bracers of Armor +2|bracers of armor +2]]_, _[[items/Wondrous Item/Cloak of Elvenkind|cloak of elvenkind]]_, masterwork manacles, 195 gp

These monks often serve as enforcers and bounty hunters for monastic orders or lawful lords, tracking down those in need of punishment.
