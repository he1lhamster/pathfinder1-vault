**Boon** Vikings can provide fairly safe sea passage to any port within a 1-week sail and can grant a +2 circumstance bonus on the PCs’ Survival checks during that voyage.

Vikings are riders of the waves, marauding plunderers from the frozen northern lands who are always ready to pillage with axe and _[[items/Weapon/Spear|spear]]_ and blazing _[[items/Mundane/Torch|torch]]_. They are boastful and proud, shouting battle cries to their savage gods for the glory of combat. Vikings can serve as elite marines or mobile _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Shock|shock]]_ troops. A lone viking may travel with a minstrel to chronicle his journeys (CR 8) or with a first mate and captain (CR 12), while five vikings might serve as honor _[[npcs/Guard|guard]]_ to a chieftain (CR 13).
