N Medium humanoid
**Init** +5; **Senses** Perception +16

**Defensive Abilities** evasion

**Speed** 30 ft.
**Melee** mwk _[[items/Weapon/Rapier|rapier]]_ +18/+13/+8 (1d6+2/18–20) or mwk _[[items/Weapon/Sap|sap]]_ +18/+13/+8 (1d6+2 nonlethal)
**Ranged** +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Merciful|merciful]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Composite longbow|composite longbow]]_ +18/+13/+8 (1d8+3/×3 plus 1d6 nonlethal) or +1 _merciful_ _composite longbow_ +16/+16/+11/+6 (1d8+3/×3 plus 1d6 nonlethal)

**_Ranger_ Spells Prepared **(CL 9th; concentration +10)

1st—_[[spells/Delay Poison|delay poison]]_, _[[spells/Entangle|entangle]]_ (DC 12), _[[spells/Longstrider|longstrider]]_

##### Statistics
**Str** 14, **Dex** 20, **Con** 14, **Int** 10, **Wis** 13, **Cha** 8
**Base Atk** +12; **CMB** +14; **CMD** 29
**Feats** _[[feats/Deadly Aim|Deadly Aim]]_, _[[feats/Endurance|Endurance]]_, _[[feats/Improved Precise Shot|Improved Precise Shot]]_, _[[feats/Improved Vital Strike|Improved Vital Strike]]_, _[[feats/Manyshot|Manyshot]]_, _[[feats/Pinpoint Targeting|Pinpoint Targeting]]_, Point Blank Shot, _[[feats/Precise Shot|Precise Shot]]_, _[[feats/Rapid Shot|Rapid Shot]]_, _[[feats/Vital Strike|Vital Strike]]_, _[[feats/Weapon Finesse|Weapon Finesse]]_
**Skills** _[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_ +10, Craft (traps) +10, Handle Animal +14, _[[spells/Heal|Heal]]_ +5, Intimidate +14, Knowledge (geography) +5, Knowledge (local) +1, Knowledge (nature) +5, Linguistics +2, Perception +16, Ride +12, Stealth +24, Survival +16 (+22 to follow tracks), Swim +6
**Languages** Common, Elven, Halfling
**SQ** camouflage, favored terrain (urban +4, underground +2), _[[classes/Hunter|hunter]]_’s bond (_[[monsters/Tiger|tiger]]_ animal companion), swift tracker, track +6, wild _[[feats/Empathy|empathy]]_ +11, woodland stride
**Combat Gear** wand of _[[spells/Cure Light Wounds|cure light wounds]]_ (CL 1, 50 charges), wand of _[[spells/Speak with Animals|speak with animals]]_ (CL 1, 50 charges), tanglefoot bags (2); **Other Gear** +1 _[[items/Armor/Chain shirt|chain shirt]]_, +1 _[[items/Armor/Buckler|buckler]]_, +1 _merciful_ _composite longbow_ (+2 Str) with 20 arrows, masterwork _rapier_, masterwork _sap_, _[[items/Wondrous Item/Belt of Incredible Dexterity +2|belt of incredible dexterity +2]]_, _[[items/Wondrous Item/Cloak of Elvenkind|cloak of elvenkind]]_, _[[poisons/Blue Whinnis|blue whinnis]]_ (6 doses), _[[poisons/Purple Worm Poison|purple worm poison]]_ (1 dose), 11 gp

**Boon** A _[[npcs/Bounty Hunter|bounty hunter]]_ can arrange to kidnap an NPC of 10th level or less for the PCs at half his usual fee.

Bounty hunters rarely hunt animals or beasts. Instead, they make not only sport but a lucrative business of capturing humanoid targets at the behest of wealthy patrons. Bounty hunters often work alone, but may lead a press gang of a sellsword, torturer, and two slavers (CR 12).
