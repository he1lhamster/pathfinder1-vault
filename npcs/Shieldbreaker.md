Dwarf _[[classes/Fighter|fighter]]_ 12

LN Medium humanoid (dwarf)
**Init** +3; **Senses** Perception +12

##### Defense

**AC** 24, touch 14, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 20 (+10 armor, +3 Dex, +1 _[[feats/Dodge|dodge]]_)
**hp** 134 (12d10+64)
**Fort** +14, **Ref** +8, **Will** +7; +2 vs. poison, spells, and _[[universal monster rules/Spell-Like Abilities|spell-like abilities]]_
**Defensive Abilities** bravery +3, defensive _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Training|training]]_ (+4 _dodge_ bonus to AC vs. giants)

##### Offense
**Speed** 30 ft.
**Melee** +1 adamantine _[[items/Weapon/Guisarme|guisarme]]_ +21/+16/+11 (2d4+9/×3) or masterwork _[[items/Weapon/Spiked gauntlet|spiked gauntlet]]_ +17/+12/+7 (1d4+5)
**Ranged** _[[items/Weapon/Javelin|javelin]]_ +15 (1d6+6)
**Space** 5 ft., **Reach** 5 ft. (10 ft. with _guisarme_)
**Special Attacks** +1 on attack rolls against goblinoid and _[[monsters/Orc|orc]]_ humanoids, weapon _training_ (polearms +2, thrown +1)

### Tactics

**Before Combat **The _fighter_ drinks his potions of bear’s _[[feats/Endurance|endurance]]_ and cat’s _[[spells/Grace|grace]]_, plus his potion of _[[spells/Enlarge Person|enlarge person]]_ if he has time.
**During Combat **The _fighter_ gets many foes within reach, then makes a _[[feats/Whirlwind Attack|Whirlwind Attack]]_ to hit them all, using _[[feats/Lunge|Lunge]]_ if he can get a few more. He tries to sunder shields to get a free attack with _[[feats/Greater Sunder|Greater Sunder]]_.
**Base Statistics **Without bear’s _endurance_ and cat’s _grace_, the _fighter_’s statistics are **Init **+0; **AC **22, touch 12, _flat-footed_ 20; **hp **110; **Fort **+12, **Ref **+6; **Ranged **_javelin_ +13 (1d6+5); **Dex **13, **Con **16; **CMB **+16 (+20 sunder); **CMD **28 (32 vs. bull rush, sunder, or _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_); **Skills **Acrobatics +0 (+5 when jumping).
