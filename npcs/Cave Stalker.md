# Cave Stalker

**Source** NPC Codex pg. 146
**XP** 1,600
Dwarf _[[classes/Rogue|rogue]]_ 6

LN Medium humanoid (dwarf)
**Init** +3; **Senses** _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 60 ft., Perception +11

##### Defense

**AC** 17, touch 13, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 14 (+4 armor, +3 Dex)
**hp** 54 (6d8+24)
**Fort** +5, **Ref** +8, **Will** +4; +2 vs. poison, spells, and _[[universal monster rules/Spell-Like Abilities|spell-like abilities]]_
**Defensive Abilities** defensive _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Training|training]]_ (+4 _[[feats/Dodge|dodge]]_ bonus to AC vs. giants), evasion, trap sense +2, uncanny _dodge_

##### Offense
**Speed** 20 ft.
**Melee** mwk _[[items/Weapon/Short sword|short sword]]_ +6 (1d6+1/19–20)
**Ranged** +1 _[[items/Weapon/Light crossbow|light crossbow]]_ +9 (1d8+1/19–20)
**Special Attacks** +1 on attack rolls against goblinoid and _[[monsters/Orc|orc]]_ humanoids, sneak attack +3d6

### Tactics

**Before Combat **The _rogue_ prefers to find an elevated spot so she can shoot opponents while they waste time climbing to her.
**During Combat **The _rogue_ uses _[[feats/Rapid Shot|Rapid Shot]]_, making the most of Point Blank Shot and her _darkvision_.

##### Statistics
**Str** 13, **Dex** 16, **Con** 16, **Int** 10, **Wis** 14, **Cha** 6
**Base Atk** +4; **CMB** +5; **CMD** 18 (22 vs. bull rush or _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_)
**Feats** _[[feats/Point-Blank Shot|Point-Blank Shot]]_, _[[feats/Precise Shot|Precise Shot]]_, _[[feats/Rapid Reload|Rapid Reload]]_, _Rapid Shot_, _[[feats/Weapon Focus|Weapon Focus]]_ (_light crossbow_)
**Skills** Acrobatics +12 (+8 when jumping), _[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_ +12, Escape Artist +12, Knowledge (dungeoneering) +9, Perception +11 (+13 to notice unusual stonework), Stealth +12, Survival +8, Swim +10
**Languages** Common, Dwarven
**SQ** _rogue_ talents (combat trick, resiliency, weapon _training_), trapfinding +3
**Combat Gear** potion of _[[spells/Cure Moderate Wounds|cure moderate wounds]]_, potions of _[[spells/Spider Climb|spider climb]]_ (2), _[[classes/Alchemist|alchemist]]_’s fire (2); **Other Gear** +1 studded leather, +1 _light crossbow_ with 20 bolts, masterwork _short sword_, climber’s kit, 308 gp

The _[[npcs/Cave Stalker|cave stalker]]_ is an experienced spelunker and treasure _[[classes/Hunter|hunter]]_.
