**Boon** A _[[npcs/Pirate Captain|pirate captain]]_ can arrange safe passage to virtually anywhere in the world reachable by ship.

Pirate captains are the _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Deadly|deadly]]_ mistresses of pirate bands, villainous cutthroats who have cursed, looted, ravaged, _[[feats/Betrayed|betrayed]]_, and carved their way to the top. These steelhanded stingrays lead their crews by sheer force of will. Pirate captains make excellent _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Champion|champion]]_ pit fighters and _deadly_ assassins. A _pirate captain_ is usually found with a first mate and a dozen shipmates (CR 12).
