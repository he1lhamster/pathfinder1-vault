# Town Watcher

**Source** NPC Codex pg. 128
**XP** 400
Halfling _[[classes/Ranger|ranger]]_ 2

LN Small humanoid (halfling)
**Init** +3; **Senses** Perception +8

##### Defense

**AC** 17, touch 14, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 14 (+3 armor, +3 Dex, +1 size)
**hp** 15 (2d10)
**Fort** +4, **Ref** +7, **Will** +2; +2 vs. _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_

##### Offense
**Speed** 20 ft.
**Melee** _[[items/Weapon/Short sword|short sword]]_ +4 (1d4+1/19–20)
**Ranged** mwk _[[items/Weapon/Shortbow|shortbow]]_ +7 (1d4/×3)
**Special Attacks** favored enemy (fey +2)

### Tactics

**During Combat **The _ranger_ shoots at opponents who attempt to _[[spells/Harm|harm]]_ innocents or his allies. If trying to capture someone alive, he uses a _[[items/Mundane/Tanglefoot bag|tanglefoot bag]]_. If trying to disperse a hostile crowd, he throws a _[[items/Mundane/Thunderstone|thunderstone]]_.

##### Statistics
**Str** 12, **Dex** 17, **Con** 10, **Int** 13, **Wis** 12, **Cha** 10
**Base Atk** +2; **CMB** +2; **CMD** 15
**Feats** _[[feats/Point-Blank Shot|Point-Blank Shot]]_, _[[feats/Precise Shot|Precise Shot]]_
**Skills** Acrobatics +7 (+3 when jumping), _[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_ +8, Diplomacy +1, Intimidate +4, Knowledge (geography, nature) +5, Knowledge (local) +2, Perception +8, Stealth +12, Survival +6, Swim +5
**Languages** Common, Halfling
**SQ** track +1, wild _[[feats/Empathy|empathy]]_ +2
**Combat Gear** potion of _[[spells/Cure Light Wounds|cure light wounds]]_, tanglefoot bags (2), _thunderstone_; **Other Gear** masterwork studded leather, masterwork _shortbow_ with 20 arrows, _short sword_, 84 gp

The _[[npcs/Town Watcher|town watcher]]_ protects a settlement from hostile creatures.
