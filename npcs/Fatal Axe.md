# Fatal Axe

**Source** NPC Codex pg. 211
**XP** 204,800
Dwarf _[[classes/Rogue|rogue]]_ 10/assassin 10

NE Medium humanoid (dwarf)
**Init** +0; **Senses** _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 60 ft.; Perception +25

##### Defense

**AC** 24, touch 13, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 24 (+9 armor, +2 _[[spells/Deflection|deflection]]_, +1 insight, +2 natural)
**hp** 183 (10d8+10d8+90)
**Fort** +12, **Ref** +14, **Will** +12; +2 vs. spells and _[[universal monster rules/Spell-Like Abilities|spell-like abilities]]_
**Defensive Abilities** defensive _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Training|training]]_ (+4 _[[feats/Dodge|dodge]]_ bonus to AC vs. giants), evasion, improved uncanny _dodge_, trap sense +3, +5 vs. poison

##### Offense
**Speed** 25 ft.
**Melee** +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Flaming|flaming]]_ frost _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Shock|shock]]_ _[[items/Weapon/Handaxe|handaxe]]_ +20/+15/+10 (1d6+5/19–20/×3 plus 1d6 cold and 1d6 electricity and 1d6 fire)
**Ranged** +1 returning _shock_ _[[items/Weapon/Throwing axe|throwing axe]]_ +15 (1d6+5/×3 plus 1d6 electricity)
**Special Attacks** +1 on attack rolls against goblinoid and _[[monsters/Orc|orc]]_ humanoids, angel of death 1/day, death attack (DC 26), _[[feats/Quiet Death|quiet death]]_, sneak attack +10d6, swift death 1/day, true death (DC 25)

### Tactics

**Before Combat **The assassin uses Stealth or Disguise to get close to her prey so she can study and strike with her death attack.
**During Combat **Using her fast stealth and _[[items/Wondrous Item/Boots of Speed|boots of speed]]_ to rush into the fray, the assassin attacks the most threatening target using _[[spells/Bleeding Strike|bleeding strike]]_. Next, she moves out of melee to soften foes with _throwing axe_ attacks before reentering melee.
