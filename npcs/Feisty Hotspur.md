# Feisty Hotspur

**Source** NPC Codex pg. 217
**XP** 19,200
Halfling _[[classes/Rogue|rogue]]_ 9/duelist 4

NE Small humanoid (halfling)
**Init** +7; **Senses** Perception +19

##### Defense

**AC** 26, touch 19, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 19 (+6 armor, +1 deflection, +6 Dex, +1 _[[feats/Dodge|dodge]]_, +1 natural, +1 size)
**hp** 101 (9d8+4d10+35)
**Fort** +9, **Ref** +18, **Will** +10; +2 vs. _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_
**Defensive Abilities** canny defense +2, enhanced _[[feats/Mobility|mobility]]_, evasion, _[[spells/Grace|grace]]_ +2, improved uncanny _dodge_, parry, trap sense +3

##### Offense
**Speed** 20 ft.
**Melee** +1 _[[items/Weapon/Rapier|rapier]]_ +18/+13 (1d4/18–20)
**Special Attacks** sneak attack +5d6, _[[feats/Precise Strike|precise strike]]_ +4

### Tactics

**During Combat **The duelist drinks her potion of _[[spells/Haste|haste]]_. She attempts to disarm opponents, then makes sneak attacks even if she must feint to do so.

##### Statistics
**Str** 8, **Dex** 20, **Con** 15, **Int** 14, **Wis** 12, **Cha** 12
**Base Atk** +10; **CMB** +14 (+16 to disarm); **CMD** 25 (27 vs. disarm)
**Feats** _[[feats/Agile Maneuvers|Agile Maneuvers]]_, _[[feats/Combat Expertise|Combat Expertise]]_, _[[feats/Combat Reflexes|Combat Reflexes]]_, _Dodge_, _[[feats/Greater Feint|Greater Feint]]_, _[[feats/Improved Disarm|Improved Disarm]]_, _[[feats/Improved Feint|Improved Feint]]_, _[[feats/Iron Will|Iron Will]]_, _Mobility_, _[[feats/Weapon Finesse|Weapon Finesse]]_, _[[feats/Weapon Focus|Weapon Focus]]_ (_rapier_)
**Skills** Acrobatics +23 (+19 when jumping), Bluff +17, _[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_ +1, Diplomacy +17, Disguise +17, Intimidate +17, Knowledge (dungeoneering, local) +10, Knowledge (nobility) +7, Perception +19, Perform (comedy) +5, Perform (sing) +6, Sleight of Hand +13, Stealth +25
**Languages** Common, Halfling
**SQ** improved reaction +2, _rogue_ talents (combat trick, finesse _rogue_, surprise attack, weapon _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Training|training]]_), trapfinding +4
**Combat Gear** potions of _[[spells/Cure Serious Wounds|cure serious wounds]]_ (2), potion of _haste_; **Other Gear** +2 mithral _[[items/Armor/Chain shirt|chain shirt]]_, +1 _rapier_, _[[items/Wondrous Item/Amulet of Natural Armor +1|amulet of natural armor +1]]_, _[[items/Wondrous Item/Belt of Physical Might +2|belt of physical might +2]]_ (Str, Con), _[[items/Wondrous Item/Cloak of _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|Resistance]]_ +2|cloak of _resistance_ +2]]_, _[[items/Ring/Ring of Protection +1|ring of protection +1]]_, 180 gp
