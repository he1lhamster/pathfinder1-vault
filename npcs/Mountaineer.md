**Base Statistics **Without bull’s strength, _longstrider_, and _resist energy_, the _ranger_’s statistics are **Resist **none; **Speed **20 ft.; **Melee **+1 _warhammer_ +7/+2 (1d8+4/×3), mwk heavy _shield_ +7/+2 (1d4+3 plus bull rush); **Str **16; **CMB **+10; **CMD **22 (26 vs. bull rush or _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_); **Skills **_[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_ +9, Swim +7.
