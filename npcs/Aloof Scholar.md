**Init** +6; **Senses** Perception +13

##### Offense

**Special Attacks** bardic performance 25 rounds/day (move action; countersong, _[[universal monster rules/Distraction|distraction]]_, fascinate, inspire competence +3, inspire courage +2, _[[spells/Suggestion|suggestion]]_)

3rd (2/day)—_[[spells/Glibness|glibness]]_, _[[spells/Scrying|scrying]]_ (DC 16)

1st (5/day)—_[[spells/Charm Person|charm person]]_ (DC 16), _[[spells/Comprehend Languages|comprehend languages]]_, _[[spells/Detect Secret Doors|detect secret doors]]_, _[[spells/Silent Image|silent image]]_ (DC 14), _[[spells/Unseen Servant|unseen servant]]_
0 (at will)—_[[spells/Dancing Lights|dancing lights]]_, _[[spells/Detect Magic|detect magic]]_, _[[spells/Know Direction|know direction]]_, _[[spells/Mage Hand|mage hand]]_, _[[spells/Message|message]]_, open/close

### Tactics

**During Combat **The _[[npcs/Pathfinder Chronicler|Pathfinder chronicler]]_ casts _blur_, then casts _[[spells/Haste|haste]]_ from her scroll. Preferring to stay out of melee, she uses bardic performance to aid her allies, and casts _daze monster_ or _charm person_ on enemies who come close. If threatened, she withdraws, drinks her potion of _[[spells/Invisibility|invisibility]]_, and makes her escape.

**Str** 8, **Dex** 14, **Con** 14, **Int** 12, **Wis** 12, **Cha** 16
**Base Atk** +6; **CMB** +5; **CMD** 19
**Feats** _Dodge_, _[[feats/Extra Performance|Extra Performance]]_, _[[feats/Greater Spell Focus|Greater Spell Focus]]_ (enchantment), _[[feats/Improved Initiative|Improved Initiative]]_, _[[feats/Mobility|Mobility]]_, _[[feats/Spell Focus|Spell Focus]]_ (enchantment)
**Skills** Knowledge (arcana, local, nobility, religion) +17, Knowledge (history) +13, Linguistics +7, Perception +13, Perform (keyboard instruments) +7, Perform (oratory) +15, Profession (scribe) +9, Spellcraft +12 (+14 to _[[spells/Identify|identify]]_ magic item properties), Stealth +12
**Languages** Celestial, Common, Draconic, Dwarven
**SQ** bardic knowledge +4, deep pockets (200 gp), elven magic, lore master 1/day, master scribe, pathfinding, versatile performance (keyboard, oratory)
**Combat Gear** potion of _invisibility_, scroll of _[[spells/Cure Serious Wounds|cure serious wounds]]_, scroll of _[[spells/Disguise Self|disguise self]]_, scroll of _haste_, scroll of _identify_, scroll of _[[spells/See Invisibility|see invisibility]]_, scroll of _[[spells/Speak with Animals|speak with animals]]_, scroll of _[[spells/Tiny Hut|tiny hut]]_, wand of _[[spells/Cure Light Wounds|cure light wounds]]_ (20 charges); **Other Gear** +1 _[[items/Armor/Leather armor|leather armor]]_, masterwork _dagger_, _[[items/Wondrous Item/Amulet of Natural Armor +1|amulet of natural armor +1]]_, _[[items/Wondrous Item/Cloak of _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|Resistance]]_ +1|cloak of _resistance_ +1]]_, _[[items/Ring/Ring of Protection +1|ring of protection +1]]_, silver _[[items/Mundane/Mirror|mirror]]_ (worth 1,000 gp), 63 gp

Often lost in their own thoughts, sometimes these scholars forget others are around them. They’re usually solitary, but have to go out exploring in search of information—or pay adventurers to retell their stories.
