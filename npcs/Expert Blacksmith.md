# Expert Blacksmith

**Source** NPC Codex pg. 262
**XP** 1,200
Dwarf expert 6

N Medium humanoid (dwarf)
**Init** +0; **Senses** _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 60 ft.; Perception +0

##### Defense

**AC** 12, touch 10, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 12 (+2 armor)
**hp** 39 (6d8+12)
**Fort** +4, **Ref** +2, **Will** +5; +2 vs. poison, spells, and _[[universal monster rules/Spell-Like Abilities|spell-like abilities]]_
**Defensive Abilities** defensive _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Training|training]]_ (+4 _[[feats/Dodge|dodge]]_ bonus to AC vs. giants)

##### Offense
**Speed** 20 ft.
**Melee** +1 _[[items/Weapon/Warhammer|warhammer]]_ +6 (1d8+2/×3) or mwk cold iron _warhammer_ +6 (1d8+1/×3)
**Special Attacks** +1 on attack rolls against goblinoid and _[[monsters/Orc|orc]]_ humanoids

### Tactics

**Before Combat** If she has at least 4 minutes to prepare to defend her shop, the expert dons her masterwork _[[items/Armor/Breastplate|breastplate]]_.
**During Combat** The expert uses one of her warhammers in melee, but calls for help and tries to avoid combat.

##### Statistics
**Str** 13, **Dex** 10, **Con** 14, **Int** 12, **Wis** 11, **Cha** 6
**Base Atk** +4; **CMB** +5; **CMD** 15 (19 vs. bull rush or _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_)
**Feats** _[[feats/Craft Magic Arms and Armor|Craft Magic Arms and Armor]]_, _[[feats/Master Craftsman|Master Craftsman]]_ (Craft [weapons]), _[[feats/Skill Focus|Skill Focus]]_ (Craft [armor, weapons])
**Skills** Appraise +10 (+12 to assess nonmagical metals or gemstones), Craft (armor, blacksmithing) +10, Craft (weapons) +15, Knowledge (history) +8, Knowledge (local) +9, Linguistics +7, Perform (sing) +7, Sense Motive +9
**Languages** Common, Draconic, Dwarven, Giant, Gnome, Halfling
**Combat Gear** oil of _[[spells/Mending|mending]]_ (5), potions of _[[spells/Cure Light Wounds|cure light wounds]]_ (2), potions of _[[spells/Endure Elements|endure elements]]_ (2), acid (2), _[[classes/Alchemist|alchemist]]_’s fire (2), _[[items/Mundane/Thunderstone|thunderstone]]_; **Other Gear** _[[items/Armor/Leather armor|leather armor]]_, +1 _warhammer_, masterwork cold iron _warhammer_, masterwork _breastplate_ (not worn), feather token (fan), _[[items/Mundane/Block and tackle|block and tackle]]_, chain (20 ft.), masterwork artisan’s tools, 1,211 gp

A blacksmith enjoys talking about metal almost as much as working it. She is proud that her skill allows her to craft everything from mundane items to magic weapons.
