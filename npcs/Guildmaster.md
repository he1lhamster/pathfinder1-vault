# Guildmaster

**Source** NPC Codex pg. 152
**XP** 19,200
Dwarf _[[classes/Rogue|rogue]]_ 13

LE Medium humanoid (dwarf)
**Init** +4; **Senses** _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 60 ft.; Perception +18

##### Defense

**AC** 23, touch 16, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 18 (+5 armor, +1 _[[spells/Deflection|deflection]]_, +4 Dex, +1 _[[feats/Dodge|dodge]]_, +1 natural, +1 _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_)
**hp** 114 (13d8+52)
**Fort** +7, **Ref** +13, **Will** +9; +2 vs. poison, spells, and _[[universal monster rules/Spell-Like Abilities|spell-like abilities]]_
**Defensive Abilities** defensive _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Training|training]]_ (+4 _dodge_ bonus to AC vs. giants), improved evasion, improved uncanny _dodge_, trap sense +4

##### Offense
**Speed** 20 ft.
**Melee** +1 _[[items/Weapon/Short sword|short sword]]_ +12/+7 (1d6+1/17–20), +1 _short sword_ +12/+7 (1d6+1/17–20) or +1 _short sword_ +14/+9 (1d6+1/17–20)
**Ranged** _[[items/Weapon/Dagger|dagger]]_ +13/+8 (1d4/19–20)
**Special Attacks** +1 on attack rolls against goblinoid and _[[monsters/Orc|orc]]_ humanoids, sneak attack +7d6
**_Spell-Like Abilities_** (CL 13th; concentration +14)
3/day—_[[spells/Detect Magic|detect magic]]_

### Tactics

**During Combat **The _rogue_ uses flanking and sneak attacks as often as possible. He relies on his resiliency and defensive roll _rogue_ talents to stay alive against dangerous opponents.

##### Statistics
**Str** 10, **Dex** 18, **Con** 14, **Int** 13, **Wis** 14, **Cha** 12
**Base Atk** +9; **CMB** +9; **CMD** 25 (29 vs. bull rush or _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_)
**Feats** _Dodge_, _[[feats/Improved Critical|Improved Critical]]_ (_short sword_), _[[feats/Improved Two-Weapon Fighting|Improved Two-Weapon Fighting]]_, _[[feats/Iron Will|Iron Will]]_, _[[feats/Toughness|Toughness]]_, _[[feats/Two-Weapon Defense|Two-Weapon Defense]]_, _[[feats/Two-Weapon Fighting|Two-Weapon Fighting]]_, _[[feats/Weapon Finesse|Weapon Finesse]]_
**Skills** Acrobatics +20 (+16 when jumping), Bluff +17, _[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_ +8, Disable Device +19, Disguise +9, Knowledge (arcana) +6, Knowledge (dungeoneering) +5, Knowledge (history) +2, Knowledge (local) +14, Perception +18 (+20 to notice unusual stonework), Sense Motive +18, Sleight of Hand +12, Stealth +20, Swim +8, Use Magic Device +9
**SQ** _rogue_ talents (bleeding attack +7, combat trick, defensive roll, improved evasion, minor magic, resiliency), trapfinding +6
**Combat Gear** potion of _[[spells/Cure Serious Wounds|cure serious wounds]]_, potion of _[[spells/Invisibility|invisibility]]_, _[[items/Mundane/Antitoxin|antitoxin]]_ (2); **Other Gear** +2 studded leather, +1 short swords (2), daggers (4), _[[items/Wondrous Item/Amulet of Natural Armor +1|amulet of natural armor +1]]_, _[[items/Wondrous Item/Belt of Physical Might +2|belt of physical might +2]]_ (Dex, Con), _[[items/Wondrous Item/Cloak of _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|Resistance]]_ +1|cloak of _resistance_ +1]]_, _[[items/Ring/Ring of Protection +1|ring of protection +1]]_, masterwork thieves’ tools, 2,047 gp

The _[[npcs/Guildmaster|guildmaster]]_ manipulates crime and politics—often the same thing—to create wealth and stability for himself and his devoted thieves.
