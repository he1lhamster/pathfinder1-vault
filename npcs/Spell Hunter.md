# Spell Hunter

**Source** NPC Codex pg. 83
**XP** 2,400
Dwarf _[[classes/Fighter|fighter]]_ 7

LE Medium humanoid (dwarf)
**Init** +2; **Senses** Perception +5

##### Defense

**AC** 22, touch 12, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 20 (+10 armor, +2 Dex)
**hp** 64 (7d10+21)
**Fort** +9, **Ref** +5, **Will** +5; +2 vs. poison, spells, and _[[universal monster rules/Spell-Like Abilities|spell-like abilities]]_
**Defensive Abilities** bravery +2, defensive _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Training|training]]_ (+4 _[[feats/Dodge|dodge]]_ bonus to AC vs. giants)

##### Offense
**Speed** 20 ft.
**Melee** mwk cold iron _[[items/Weapon/Dwarven waraxe|dwarven waraxe]]_ +14/+9 (1d10+9/×3)
**Ranged** mwk _[[items/Weapon/Throwing axe|throwing axe]]_ +11 (1d6+5) or mwk _[[items/Weapon/Light crossbow|light crossbow]]_ +10 (1d8/19–20)
**Special Attacks** +1 on attack rolls against goblinoid and _[[monsters/Orc|orc]]_ humanoids, weapon _training_ (axes +1)

### Tactics

**Before Combat **The _fighter_ drinks her potion of bull’s strength.
**During Combat **When fighting with a group, the _fighter_ uses bull rush to grant attacks of opportunity to her allies. She uses the combination of _[[feats/Step Up|Step Up]]_ and _[[feats/Disruptive|Disruptive]]_ to interrupt spellcasting.
**Base Statistics **Without bull’s strength, the _fighter_’s statistics are **Melee **mwk cold iron _dwarven waraxe_ +12/+7 (1d10+6/×3); **Ranged **mwk _throwing axe_ +11 (1d6+3); **Str **15; **CMB **+9 (+13 bull rush); **CMD **21 (27 vs. bull rush, 25 vs. _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_); **Skills **_[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_ +8.

##### Statistics
**Str** 19, **Dex** 14, **Con** 16, **Int** 10, **Wis** 14, **Cha** 6
**Base Atk** +7; **CMB** +12 (+16 bull rush); **CMD** 23 (29 vs. bull rush, 27 vs. _trip_)
**Feats** _[[feats/Blind-Fight|Blind-Fight]]_, _Disruptive_, _[[feats/Greater Bull Rush|Greater Bull Rush]]_, _[[feats/Improved Bull Rush|Improved Bull Rush]]_, _[[feats/Power Attack|Power Attack]]_, _Step Up_, _[[feats/Weapon Focus|Weapon Focus]]_ (_dwarven waraxe_), _[[feats/Weapon Specialization|Weapon Specialization]]_ (_dwarven waraxe_)
**Skills** _Climb_ +10, Intimidate +8, Perception +5 (+7 to notice unusual stonework), Survival +10
**Languages** Common, Dwarven
**SQ** armor _training_ 2
**Combat Gear** oil of _[[spells/Magic Weapon|magic weapon]]_ (2), potion of aid, potion of bull’s strength, potions of _[[spells/Cure Moderate Wounds|cure moderate wounds]]_ (2); **Other Gear** +1 _[[items/Armor/Full plate|full plate]]_, masterwork cold iron _dwarven waraxe_, masterwork _light crossbow_ with 20 bolts, masterwork _throwing axe_, _[[items/Wondrous Item/Cloak of _[[universal monster rules/Resistance|Resistance]]_ +1|cloak of _resistance_ +1]]_, 46 gp

Spell hunters specialize in locking down and then murdering enemy spellcasters.
