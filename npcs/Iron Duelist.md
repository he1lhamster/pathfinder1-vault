**Source** NPC Codex pg. 218

Dwarf _[[classes/Fighter|fighter]]_ 10/duelist 7

N Medium humanoid (dwarf)
**Init** +8; **Senses** _[[spells/Darkvision|darkvision]]_ 60 ft.; Perception +11

##### Defense

**AC** 24, touch 15, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 20 (+8 armor, +1 _[[spells/Deflection|deflection]]_, +3 Dex, +1 _[[feats/Dodge|dodge]]_, +1 natural)
**hp** 159 (10d10+7d10+61)
**Fort** +14, **Ref** +13, **Will** +10; +2 vs. poison, spells, and _[[universal monster rules/Spell-Like Abilities|spell-like abilities]]_
**Defensive Abilities** bravery +3, canny defense +1, defensive _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Training|training]]_ (+4 _dodge_ bonus to AC vs. giants), elaborate defense +2, enhanced _[[feats/Mobility|mobility]]_, _[[spells/Grace|grace]]_ +2, parry

##### Offense
**Speed** 20 ft.
**Melee** +1 _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Flaming|flaming]]_ frost _[[items/Weapon/Light pick|light pick]]_ +27/+22/+17/+12 (1d4+10/19–20/×4 plus 1d6 cold and 1d6 fire) or mwk _[[items/Weapon/Punching dagger|punching dagger]]_ +24/+19/+14/+9 (1d4+6/×3)
**Ranged** +1 _[[items/Weapon/Light crossbow|light crossbow]]_ +20 (1d8+1/19–20)
**Special Attacks** +1 on attack rolls against goblinoid and _[[monsters/Orc|orc]]_ humanoids, _[[feats/Acrobatic|acrobatic]]_ charge, _[[feats/Precise Strike|precise strike]]_ +7, riposte, weapon _training_ (axes +2, light blades +1)

### Tactics

**During Combat **The duelist drinks his potion of _[[spells/Invisibility|invisibility]]_, and gets into a tactical position. He uses _[[feats/Spring Attack|Spring Attack]]_, _[[feats/Lunge|Lunge]]_, and _[[feats/Improved Vital Strike|Improved Vital strike]]_ to make hit-and-run attacks.

**Str** 21, **Dex** 14, **Con** 16, **Int** 13, **Wis** 12, **Cha** 6
**Base Atk** +17; **CMB** +22; **CMD** 36 (40 vs. bull rush or _[[universal monster rules/Trip|trip]]_)
**Feats** _[[feats/Combat Expertise|Combat Expertise]]_, _[[feats/Combat Reflexes|Combat Reflexes]]_, _[[feats/Critical Focus|Critical Focus]]_, _Dodge_, _[[feats/Greater Weapon Focus|Greater Weapon Focus]]_ (_light pick_), _[[feats/Improved Critical|Improved Critical]]_ (_light pick_), _[[feats/Improved Initiative|Improved Initiative]]_, _Improved Vital Strike_, _[[feats/Iron Will|Iron Will]]_, _Lunge_, _Mobility_, _Spring Attack_, _[[feats/Vital Strike|Vital Strike]]_, _[[feats/Weapon Finesse|Weapon Finesse]]_, _[[feats/Weapon Focus|Weapon Focus]]_ (_light pick_), _[[feats/Weapon Specialization|Weapon Specialization]]_ (_light pick_)
**Skills** Acrobatics +7 (+3 when jumping), Appraise +6 (+8 to assess nonmagical metals or gemstones), _[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_ +13, Craft (weapons) +9, Diplomacy +3, Disable Device +7, Knowledge (dungeoneering, engineering) +9, Knowledge (history) +6, Perception +11 (+13 to detect unusual stonework), Perform (percussion) +0, Stealth +7, Survival +6, Swim +9

**SQ** armor _training_ 2, improved reaction +2
