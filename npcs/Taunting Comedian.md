**Init** +3; **Senses** Perception +1

##### Defense

**AC** 18, touch 13, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 15 (+5 armor, +3 Dex)
**hp** 25 (4d8+4)
**Fort** +2, **Ref** +8, **Will** +4; +2 vs. _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_, +4 vs. bardic performance, language-dependent, and sonic

**Speed** 20 ft.

1st (4/day)—_[[spells/Animate Rope|animate rope]]_, _[[spells/Cure Light Wounds|cure light wounds]]_ (DC 15), lesser _[[spells/Confusion|confusion]]_ (DC 15), _[[spells/Ventriloquism|ventriloquism]]_ (DC 15)
0 (at will)—_[[spells/Daze|daze]]_ (DC 14), _[[spells/Detect Magic|detect magic]]_, _[[spells/Flare|flare]]_ (DC 14), _[[spells/Ghost Sound|ghost sound]]_ (DC 14), _[[spells/Mending|mending]]_, _[[spells/Read Magic|read magic]]_

### Tactics

**During Combat **The _bard_ hides using _ventriloquism_, and snares enemies with _animate rope_.

##### Statistics
**Str** 11, **Dex** 16, **Con** 10, **Int** 12, **Wis** 8, **Cha** 18
**Base Atk** +3; **CMB** +3; **CMD** 16
**Feats** _[[feats/Arcane Strike|Arcane Strike]]_, _[[feats/Weapon Finesse|Weapon Finesse]]_
**Skills** Acrobatics +11 (+7 when jumping), Bluff +11, _[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_ +1, Diplomacy +8, Intimidate +9, Knowledge (local) +10, Perception +1, Perform (comedy) +11, Sense Motive +4, Spellcraft +6, Stealth +7, Use Magic Device +10
**Languages** Common, Halfling
**SQ** bardic knowledge +2, versatile performance (comedy)
**Combat Gear** potions of _[[spells/Cure Moderate Wounds|cure moderate wounds]]_ (2), tanglefoot bags (2); **Other Gear** +1 _[[items/Armor/Chain shirt|chain shirt]]_, masterwork _short sword_, _sling_ with 20 bullets, _[[items/Mundane/Everburning torch|everburning torch]]_, 30 gp
