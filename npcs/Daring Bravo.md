# Daring Bravo

**Source** NPC Codex pg. 80
**XP** 400
Gnome _[[classes/Fighter|fighter]]_ 2

CN Small humanoid (gnome)
**Init** +2; **Senses** _[[universal monster rules/Low-Light Vision|low-light vision]]_; Perception +2

##### Defense

**AC** 18, touch 14, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 15 (+3 armor, +2 Dex, +1 dodge, +1 _[[spells/Shield|shield]]_, +1 size)
**hp** 21 (2d10+6)
**Fort** +5, **Ref** +2, **Will** +0; +2 vs. illusions, +1 vs. _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_
**Defensive Abilities** bravery +1, defensive _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Training|training]]_ (+4 _dodge_ bonus to AC vs. giants)

##### Offense
**Speed** 20 ft.
**Melee** mwk _[[items/Weapon/Rapier|rapier]]_ +6 (1d4+1/18–20) or _[[items/Weapon/Dagger|dagger]]_ +5 (1d3+1/18–20)
**Ranged** _[[items/Weapon/Composite longbow|composite longbow]]_ +5 (1d6+1/×3)
**Special Attacks** +1 on attack rolls against goblinoid and reptilian humanoids

### Tactics

**During Combat **The _fighter_ shifts to maintain an advantageous position. If facing a similarly mobile foe, she resorts to her _[[items/Mundane/Tanglefoot bag|tanglefoot bag]]_.

##### Statistics
**Str** 12, **Dex** 15, **Con** 14, **Int** 13, **Wis** 10, **Cha** 10
**Base Atk** +2; **CMB** +2; **CMD** 15
**Feats** _Dodge_, _[[feats/Mobility|Mobility]]_, _[[feats/Weapon Finesse|Weapon Finesse]]_
**Skills** Acrobatics +3 (–1 when jumping), Intimidate +4, Knowledge (nobility) +2, Perception +2, Stealth +7
**Languages** Common, Gnome, Halfling, Sylvan
**Combat Gear** _tanglefoot bag_; **Other Gear** studded leather, masterwork _[[items/Armor/Buckler|buckler]]_, _composite longbow_ with 20 arrows, _dagger_, masterwork _rapier_, wineskin, 16 gp

These small, quick warriors are also clever and brave. _[[spells/They Know|They know]]_ when to strike fast and hard—and when to run.
