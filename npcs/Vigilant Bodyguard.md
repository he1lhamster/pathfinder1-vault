# Vigilant Bodyguard

**Source** NPC Codex pg. 97
**XP** 600
Human _[[classes/Monk|monk]]_ 3

LN Medium humanoid (human)
**Init** +5; **Senses** Perception +10

##### Defense

**AC** 20, touch 16, _[[conditions/Flat-Footed|flat-footed]]_ 18 (+4 armor, +1 Dex, +1 _[[feats/Dodge|dodge]]_, +4 Wis)
**hp** 20 (3d8+3)
**Fort** +4, **Ref** +4, **Will** +7; +2 vs. enchantments
**Defensive Abilities** evasion

##### Offense
**Speed** 40 ft.
**Melee** unarmed strike +5 (1d6+2) or _[[items/Weapon/Nunchaku|nunchaku]]_ +4 (1d6+2) or unarmed strike flurry of blows +3/+3 (1d6+2)
**Ranged** shuriken +3 (1d2+2)
**Special Attacks** flurry of blows, _[[feats/Stunning Fist|stunning fist]]_ (3/day, DC 15)

### Tactics

**Before Combat **The _monk_ drinks her potion of owl’s wisdom to enhance her AC and Perception, as well as a potion of _[[spells/Mage Armor|mage armor]]_.
**During Combat **The _monk_ uses a flurry of blows to make disarm attempts with her _nunchaku_. If protecting someone, she uses _Stunning Fist_ before withdrawing with her charge.
**Base Statistics **Without owl’s wisdom and _mage armor_, the _monk_’s statistics are **Senses **Perception +8; **AC **15, touch 14, _flat-footed_ 13; **Wis **15; **CMD **18; **Skills **Perception +8, Sense Motive +8, Survival +3.

##### Statistics
**Str** 14, **Dex** 13, **Con** 12, **Int** 10, **Wis** 19, **Cha** 8
**Base Atk** +2; **CMB** +5; **CMD** 20
**Feats** _[[feats/Combat Reflexes|Combat Reflexes]]_, _Dodge_, _[[feats/Improved Initiative|Improved Initiative]]_, _[[feats/Improved Unarmed Strike|Improved Unarmed Strike]]_, _Stunning Fist_, _[[feats/Weapon Focus|Weapon Focus]]_ (unarmed strike)
**Skills** Acrobatics +7 (+11 when jumping), _[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_ +6, Intimidate +5, Perception +10, Sense Motive +10, Stealth +7, Survival +5, Swim +6
**SQ** fast movement, maneuver _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Training|training]]_
**Combat Gear** potions of _[[spells/Cure Light Wounds|cure light wounds]]_ (3), potion of _mage armor_, potion of _[[spells/Magic Weapon|magic weapon]]_, potion of owl’s wisdom, smokesticks (3); **Other Gear** _[[items/Weapon/Dagger|dagger]]_, _nunchaku_, shuriken (20), _[[items/Wondrous Item/Bracers of Armor +1|bracers of armor +1]]_, 30 gp

Some _monk_ orders hire out _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Vigilant|vigilant]]_ bodyguards to princelings, merchants, and priests with compatible interests and philosophies.
