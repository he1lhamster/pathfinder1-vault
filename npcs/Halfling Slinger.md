##### Defense

**Fort** +11, **Ref** +11, **Will** +9; +4 vs. _[[universal monster rules/Fear|fear]]_
**Defensive Abilities** bravery +2

##### Offense
**Speed** 20 ft.
**Melee** +1 _[[items/Weapon/Halfling _[[items/Weapon/Sling|sling]]_ staff|halfling _sling_ staff]]_ +18/+13 (1d4+9/19–20) or _[[items/Weapon/Short sword|short sword]]_ +14/+9 (1d4+4/19–20)
**Ranged** +1 _halfling _sling_ staff_ +22 (1d6+8/19–20/×3)
**Special Attacks** weapon _[[items/Weapon Magic Abilities/Training|training]]_ (thrown +2, light blades +1)

### Tactics

**Before Combat **The _fighter_ drinks his potions of _[[spells/Bull's Strength|bull's strength]]_ and _[[spells/Heroism|heroism]]_.
**During Combat **If fighting elves, the _fighter_ stays back from the fight, wielding his _sling_ staff until he's out of elf-bane bullets. Otherwise, he rides into the fray, _[[items/Armor Magic Abilities/Bashing|bashing]]_ foes and hurling bulletswith his _sling_ staff while using _[[feats/Deadly Aim|Deadly Aim]]_ and _[[feats/Vital Strike|Vital Strike]]_.
**Base Statistics** Without _bull's strength_ and _heroism_, the _fighter_'s statistics are **Senses **Perception +3; **Fort **+9, **Ref **+9, **Will **+7; **Melee **+1 _halfling _sling_ staff_ +14/+9 (1d4+6/19–20) or _short sword_ +12/+7 (1d4+2/19–20); **Ranged **+1 _halfling _sling_ staff_ +20 (1d6+6/19–20/×3); **Str **12; **CMB **+9; **CMD **24; **Skills **Acrobatics +5 (+1 when jumping), _[[universal monster rules/Climb|Climb]]_ +1, Perception +3, Ride +15, Survival +13.
