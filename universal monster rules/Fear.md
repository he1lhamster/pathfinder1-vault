# Fear (Su or Sp)
Fear attacks can have various effects.Fear Aura (Su) The use of this ability is a free action. The aura can freeze an opponent (as in the case of a mummy's despair) or function like the fear spell. Other effects are possible. A fear aura is an area effect. The descriptive text gives the size and kind of the area.Fear Cone (Sp) and Ray (Su) These effects usually work like the fear spell.If a fear effect allows a saving throw, it is a Will save (DC 10 + 1/2 fearsome creature's racial HD + creature's Cha modifier; the exact DC is given in the creature's descriptive text). All fear attacks are mind-affecting fear effects.

"_Format:_ fear aura (30 ft., DC 17); Location: Aura._Format:_ fear cone (50 ft., DC 19); Location: Special Attacks."
