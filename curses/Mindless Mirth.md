# Mindless Mirth
 *Type:* Curse
*Save: * Will 16 
*Effect: * Whenever an initiative check is required, the victim must also succeed at a DC 16 Will save or collapse to the floor in gales of laughter (as hideous laughter). The episode lasts 2d4–1 rounds.
